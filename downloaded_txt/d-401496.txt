about legendary marine 



welcome to legendary marine. 






when it comes to all things boating, legendary marine is 100 percent committed to earning your valued business and becoming your exceptional, full-service provider. throughout our entire operation, including all seven dealership locations, our national and regional award-winning sales and service teams eagerly await the opportunity to deliver a truly remarkable experience for you. from the moment of first contact, we want your experience with legendary marine to be truly remarkable as we work together to maximize your boating investment, activity… and fun!






whether you are in the market to purchase a new boat, locate a premium used boat or yacht, or need professional guidance and help in selling your current boat, our veteran sales team is ready and fully prepared to assist you however needed. our staff of highly knowledgeable sales consultants, yacht brokers and managers bring years of industry experience to bear, along with a lengthy roster of highly satisfied customers. with a combined csi (customer service index) rating of 94%, we work diligently each and every day to deliver an outstanding and rewarding experience for every customer we have the opportunity to serve. please contact any of our dealerships’ sales departments today and let us know how we can help you achieve your boating goals!
