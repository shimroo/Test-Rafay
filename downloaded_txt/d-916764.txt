tenerife holiday rentals - pearly grey self catering apartments
 



pearly grey is a hotel resort with self-catering apartments located in callao salvaje along the stunning south coast of tenerife. at whichever time of year you decide to stay with us, you can enjoy the warm, sunny weather that the south of tenerife is known for. whether you’re looking for a family hotel or a quiet resort, pearly grey has it all. 














studio apartmentour studio apartments are located on the ground floor in the main hotel and along the s-level. all apartments have a fully equipped kitchen. some of our studio apartments have a double bed and some have two single beds.
find out moreone bedroom apartmentour one bedroom are located in the main hotel. all have a fully equipped kitchen and a private bathroom. some of our apartments have a double bed and some have two single beds.find out moreone bedroom sea view apartmentour one bedroom sea-view apartments are located in the main hotel and the balcony faces the atlantic ocean. all apartments have a fully equipped kitchen and a private bathroom. some of our apartments have a double bed and some have two single beds.
find out morecliff top suite one bedroomour self catering cliff top suites are situated overlooking the atlantic ocean. we have a selection of one bedroom suites and all have balconies with unrivalled views of the ocean and the neighbouring island of la gomera.find out moretwo bedroom apartmentour 2 bedroom 1 bathroom apartments are located in the main hotel. the master bedroom has a double bed and the second bedroom has two single beds. all have a fully equipped kitchen and a balcony.
