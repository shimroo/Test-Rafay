we are currently closed but will reopen at a new location in 2025.the 40/40 club is named after one of baseball’s most prestigious achievements. a benchmark for power and speed, the 40/40 club includes players who hit 40 home runs and stole 40 bases in one season. barry bonds (1996), alex rodriguez (1998), jose canseco (1988), alfonso soriano (2006), ronald acuna jr. (2023), and shohei ohtani (2024) are the only six to hold this title.
