add the joy & magic of puppets into your life!



in the heart of the beast puppet and mask theatre (hobt) is a community of artists working within our larger community to create magic together. our mission is to nurture creative empowerment through the joy and magic of puppetry performance and education. attend an and join in the magic yourself!

in the heart of the beast puppet and mask theatre (hobt) is a minneapolis-saint paul area arts nonprofit that provides a variety of enriching art classes and programming focused on the joy and magic of puppetry. we serve people of all kinds and all ages, including children, tweens, teens and adults. attend one of our free and low-cost arts events, bring our art activities to you, or learn how you can support our mission.
