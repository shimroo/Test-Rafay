							material processing						

columbus equipment company is dedicated to providing robust and dependable equipment to various industries, including forestry, aggregate, composting, mulching, topsoil processing, recycling, and waste management. we offer a wide range of powerful machinery from renowned brands such as terex cbi, astec, komatsu, cmi, terex ecotec, fae, and rotobec. our team of experts specializing in material processing works closely with customers throughout the midwest region, enhancing their operational efficiency and driving profitability.
