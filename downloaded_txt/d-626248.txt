john muir wrote that “everybody needs beauty as well as bread.” 



in our vancouver workshop, the craftspeople at beauty and bread agree. 



we build furniture that’s as durable as it is stylish: beds, dressers, tables, writing desks, and more. it’s quality furniture built sustainably and made to last for generations.we ship anywhere in the united states. and local delivery is always free.we’ll create your hardwood furniture from scratch, utilizing traditional woodworking methods. 



view our products. 











handmade furniture is a financial investment. 



the process begins when we hand-select and hew environmentally sourced walnut, white oak, cherry, ash, and maple. the process ends some 60+ hours later after we’ve transformed that gorgeous wood into furniture that’s useful and genuinely, authentically beautiful. 



view more about us.
