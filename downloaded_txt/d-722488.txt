what's this form? how to read your assessment change notice. 

every year at the end of february, taxpayers will receive a form like this — form 1019 — explaining the changes in the assessed and taxable value of your property.				read on...







board of commissioners approve 2025 committee assignments 

the cass county board of commissioners on thursday approved committee, board and commission assignments for the 2025 calendar year.				read on...







market one announces event to help employers navigate compliance changes 

market one is partnering with hr collaborative and three local chambers to bring employers a free event titled mitigating risk: hr 101 + compliance changes.				read on...











cass county land bank secures funding to demolish vacant church building 

thanks to a grant secured by the cass county land bank authority, the church of cassopolis was able to solve a decade-old problem.				read on...







cass county animal shelter to host rabies vaccination clinics 

cass county animal control with bergman veterinary small animal clinic will host two rabies clinics at cass county animal control, 323 m-62 n, cassopolis.				read on...







cass county board of commissioners appoints chair, vice chair 

a familiar face will be holding the gavel at board of commissioner meetings this year, as commissioner jeremiah jones was to serve as board chair again.				read on...











cass county seniors could be eligible for home modification support 

region iv area agency on aging will soon offer more support for low-income older adults and people with disabilities in southwest michigan.				read on...







sba offers disaster relief to farmers affected by 2024 frost 

economic injury disaster loans are available for small businesses with economic losses due to the frost and freeze that occurred on jan. 15 through march 21, 2024.				read on...
