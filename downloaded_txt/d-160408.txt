stan richards school of advertising & public relations















the stan richards school of advertising & public relations is a world-class program for students interested in the advertising and public relations industries at the undergraduate, master's or ph.d. levels. 
we have consistently been ranked as one of the top advertising programs over the past 20 years by employers and competitors, and our school is home to one of the largest undergraduate and graduate student populations of any university.

mission and goals of the stan richards school
generate top-tier research and creative work that contributes to the overarching theories that explain success and failure in the practice of advertising and public relations.attract and retain the best student, staff and faculty talent.maintain strong, positive relations with key communities served by the school.cultivate graduate students with skills in critical thinking, quantitative reasoning, creative problem solving, active listening, efficient communication, evidence-based strategies and ethical decision-making.
