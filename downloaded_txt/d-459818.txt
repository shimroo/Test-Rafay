a century of excellence


george courey inc. has been a key player in the wholesale of high-quality linens and
                                    fabrics for over 100 years and counting. today, we supply a vast network of
                                    customers in hospitality, healthcare and retail, with a range of linens from
                                    top-of-the-line luxury to budget-conscious quality. looking towards the future, we
                                    are committed to creating products that both meet and exceed the evolving needs of
