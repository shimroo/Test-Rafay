with the jesters university league season now complete, we’re excited to announce that the university of toronto men and the mcgill women have secured the regular season titles.

2025 weir team ontario men's veterans doubles championships recap






the 2025 weir team ontario men's veterans doubles championships delivered another incredible weekend of high-level doubles squash. with 40 teams competing, this event once again proved to be one of the most anticipated tournaments of the year.
read more →


























2025 osdl & jesters ontario junior doubles squash championships recap






the 2025 osdl & jesters ontario junior doubles squash championships wrapped up another exciting edition, bringing together nearly 80 junior players for a weekend of competitive and fun doubles squash at the granite club in toronto. the enthusiasm and energy from participants, families, and supporters made this event a huge success once again.

brock university set to host oua championships at white oaks






brock university will play host for the 2025 oua squash championships, taking place february 7-9 at the club at white oaks. eight eligible schools from across ontario, who all participate in our jesters university league, will be coming together to play for the oua title.

squash ontario is hitting the road this summer for squash fest 2025 – road to the olympics! as we gear up for squash’s debut in the 2028 la olympics, we’re bringing the excitement to communities across ontario with our mini court summer tour.
