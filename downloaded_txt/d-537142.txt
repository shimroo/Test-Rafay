box office

our new box office system is now live.
we’re thrilled to share that we're now back up and running with our brand new box office system.
if you had previously registered an account with us when booking tickets online, this will no longer be active. you will need to create a new account on our website next time you book a ticket online. you can use the same email address as you have previously done.

if you’re having any issues booking tickets please contact us on boxoffice@jermynstreettheatre.co.uk and we’d be happy to help.
