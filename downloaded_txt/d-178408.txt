			our story: making eggs better		

at egg beaters, we’re passionate about one thing: making it as easy as possible for you to eat more eggs. so we always start with the real thing – farm-fresh, all-natural eggs – but we remove the egg yolks, where the fat and cholesterol are, so you can feel good about using them as a wholesome ingredient in every meal of the day. simply put, egg beaters make eggs better.
