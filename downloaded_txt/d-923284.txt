 about the james r. connor university center

welcome to the james r. connor university center (uc). the uc is the heart of the uw-whitewater campus, providing opportunities for students to enhance their college experience through a wide variety of activities and services.

the uc is home to warhawk alley, roberta's art gallery, information services, uc building operations, uc live, student activities & involvement, the hawkcard office and numerous lounges and dining locations. 

whatever your need, the uc is here to get u connected!
