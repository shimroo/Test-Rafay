33 years & 327+ miles of georgia trails














how we entered this pathsince 1991, the path foundation has been transforming the landscape of the greater metropolitan atlanta area. by skillfully combining philanthropic, public and other funding sources, path has turned abandoned railroad corridors into beautiful linear green spaces, linked neighborhoods to bustling commercial centers and provided alternative modes of transportation for commuters, joggers, walkers, cyclists and people of all ages.
