elegance in stock














manhattan flagship now open



led by the third generation, twin brothers bruno and pietro bacchi introduce artefacto manhattan at 149 madison avenue where the brand’s 50-year legacy of excellence and operations endures and delivers fully furnished homes in two weeks. "it is a great honor that we continue building and evolving the company my grandfather started," says pietro. "we manufacture 100% of our collections, giving us complete control over the quality and designs we produce," adds bruno.
