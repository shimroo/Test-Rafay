slalom shop - luxury yachts & boats for sale - lewisville & graford tx 



welcome to slalom shop boats & yachts 



once again, named top 100 boat dealer in north america 2018! serving boating enthusiasts since 1977, slalom shop boats and yachts, with 2 premier locations in north texas, is one of texas’ top boat dealerships. slalom shop boats and yachts has a staff with over 200 years of combined marine experience. each of our factory trained technicians has been certified by the manufacturers we represent. we are proud to represent some of the best in the industry, cobalt boats, supra boats, and moomba wake-surf boats. we have also recently added barletta pontoons to the family. one of the hottest boats on the market today!we are a marine industry “certified dealership” and have been awarded the distinctive “top 100 u.s. dealers award” by boating industry magazine!slalom shop serves all of north texas. we offer both new and pre-owned boats, yachts, powerboats, fishing boats, pleasure boats, recreational boats, ski boats, deck boats, jet boats, runabouts, wakeboard boats, surf boats, and more from popular brands including sea ray, barletta, centurion, cobalt, chaparral, moomba, supra, and more!we provide attractive boat financing and loans, boat brokerage, boat parts, boat service, and repair. we have it all, even one of the best pro shops in the us carrying all the latest watersports gear and apparel!our dedicated sales team will provide you with personal and professional guidance in selecting the right boat and gear for your family.stop in at our flagship store on i-35 and garden ridge (exit 454b) on lake lewisville or the possum kingdom store by the harbor resort. we can accommodate all your boating needs! or, simply call one of our locations today:eagle point marina, lake lewisville (corporate showroom): (972) 627-4068the harbor marina, lake possum kingdom: sales (940) 329-2133
