guidance for back yard flock owners 

defend the flock; usda animal and plant health inspection services' education program offers free tools and resources highlighting best biosecurity practices to help your birds stay safe and protected from disease.				read on...







survey for potential enhancements to hains park 

the parks & recreation commission is seeking your input on potential enhancements to hains park (rogers lake). if you are an old lyme resident, please complete the following short survey. thank you in advance for your time and input!				read on...







stay informed with “notify me” 

old lyme residents are encouraged to sign-up and create a website account which will allow you to personalize subscriptions and alert notifications, plus keep track of forms you have filled out online.				read on...
