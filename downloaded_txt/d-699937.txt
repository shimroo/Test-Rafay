kramer's kampers inc.
in business since 1966, kramer's kampers has been owned and operated by the kramer family for more than 50 years. putting families in their own camping vehicle lance, forest river, palomino, salem fsx, salem cruise lite, salem, salem hyper-lyte, salem hemisphere is just about all we know how to do. we do the right thing, and we think we do it pretty well. you'll like the way we do business. stop by today! we offer a complete service department to handle all your travel trailer, fifth wheel, truck campers repair needs. financing is available.
kramer's kampers is conveniently located near the areas of zion, kenosha (wi), mchenry, waukegan, buffalo grove, northern chicago.
