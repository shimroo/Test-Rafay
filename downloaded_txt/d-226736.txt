the boston theological interreligious consortium



the bti consortium connects students, faculty, and staff from 11 interreligious theological and religious studies institutions. we facilitate cross-registration, library access, certificate programs, field education, events, and student-led conferences to share resources and build collaborative community across difference.














learn more about the bti consortium member schools



































































































































































can’t find what you’re looking for?



whether you are just beginning your journey toward graduate education, a currently enrolled student at a bti member school, or a bti alum, don’t hesitate to reach out.
