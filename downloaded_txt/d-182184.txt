welcome to kaufman county, texaswelcome to kaufman county, texas, founded in 1848. located adjacent to dallas county on the east, kaufman county is home to more than 145,000 people, 16 cities, four independent school districts, two colleges, and thousands of thriving businesses. kaufman county has recently been named by the us census bureau as the fastest-growing county over 100,000 in the nation.
