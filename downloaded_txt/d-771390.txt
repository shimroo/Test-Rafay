welcome home
salem is a place where the traditions of the past meet the present. it's a place where faith, fellowship, and family are more than taglines in mission statements. it's a place that many call their 'spiritual home'. we’d love for salem to be your home as well. join us friday, july 12th until friday, july 19th!campmeeting 2024




























our story
camp meetings, originating in kentucky in 1800, are a distinctly american contribution to the history of protestantism. salem, one of the south's oldest camp meetings, has been held every year since 1828 except during the civil war. as such, salem is one of the oldest thriving camp meetings in the nation.learn more
