providence roller derby (prd) was founded in 2004 and is a founding member of the women’s flat track derby association (wftda). prd is committed to fostering a positive, inclusive, and supportive environment for participating and engaging in the sport of flat track roller derby.prd is a 501(c)(3) non-profit charitable organization recognized by the irs and the state of ri. we are self-governed and dedicated to making a positive impact in the lives of our skaters and community by participating in local events and activities while working to increase recognition of flat-track roller derby.
