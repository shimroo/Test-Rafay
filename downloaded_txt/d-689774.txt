the sons of union veterans of the civil war is a patriotic society, whose members are direct descendants, either through paternal or maternal lines, of soldiers, sailors or marines who were regularly mustered and served honorably or were honorably discharged from the service during the war of rebellion, 1861--1865.  associates (those without direct lineage from union veterans) who hold to the principles and support the objects of the organization are also welcome. the national organization of the suvcw oversees the operation of 31 departments including the department of massachusetts.welcome to the official website of the department of massachusetts.
