a personal approach to better hearing.














listening is what we do best. we listen to your concerns, your problems, your family's perspectives. 



audible difference® provides state-of-the-art audiological care by appointment in madison, wisconsin. we also provide audiology services to area retirement centers.













audiologist veronica h. heide, au.d., will evaluate your hearing and auditory system with advanced diagnostic equipment. based on the results, she will then work with you to develop your plan, with a range of options and strategies tailored to your specific hearing, balance, tinnitus, and noise control issues.
