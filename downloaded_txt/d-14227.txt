this website is addressed to lovers of english
  poetry in general, and to teachers and students of english  all over the world.
  i hope that you will find a lot of interesting material here.
  
 poems by blake and other romantics  is selected poetry by
 william blake (1757-1827), william wordsworth (1770-1850),  samuel taylor coleridge (1772-1834) & gordon
  lord byron (1788-1824), which i have analysed and set to music.  in my programme romantic poems set to music, i present my work in the form of 
  music and recitals  in "candle-light lectures" of a most entertaining kind.
 
  to coleridge's kubla khan i have dedicated a website of its own, with  comprehensive text 
  analysis, graphic illustrations and a symphony playable in midi format.
  
  i have given performances and lectures on english poetry set to music in further teacher training (regierungsbezirk muenster, 
  nordrhein-westfalen, germany). i work as a college teacher. i am a winner of the international music 
  contest  a song for peace, rome 1999-2002   > links
juergen matthias schroeder, duelmen (d) 2002
