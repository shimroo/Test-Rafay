about hovertechnics


   hovertechnics was originally founded in the state of michigan in 1984 as a family-owned corporation. our sole business is the design, engineering, manufacturing and marketing of hovercraft, hovercraft trailers, related accessories and replacement parts.

in 2019, we moved manufacturing to our factory in indiana, which has many advantages. indiana is known as the crossroads of america since it is the hub of major highways, waterways and railroads in the united states. this central location expedites shipping to our customers in the u.s. and abroad. in addition, the wabash river and the varying midwestern weather provide ideal conditions for hovercraft testing and training.
