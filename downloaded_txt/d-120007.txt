public health update: bird flu, respiratory illness, and norovirus 


the amherst public health department is closely monitoring several illnesses currently circulating in our community: bird flu (h5n1), covid-19, other respiratory illnesses, and norovirus.						read on...







preparing for winter weather 


as winter approaches, the town of amherst reminds residents how to prepare for inclement weather including snow removal responsibilities and staying informed about parking bans.						read on...







housing production plan: scenario workshop 


join us on tuesday, february 18 from 4 to 6 pm in the town room at town hall to help amherst plan for future housing scenarios!						read on...







fire chief lindsay stromgren swearing-in ceremony 


on friday, january 31, 2025, the town of amherst hosted a swearing-in ceremony to mark lindsay stromgrenâ€™s appointment as chief of the amherst fire department.						read on...







winterfest 2025 


february 15 - february 23
get ready to celebrate the magic of winter during amherst recreation's annual winterfest!						read on...







amherst college makes $2.5 million contribution to the town of amherst 


on february 6, 2025, amherst college announced they are making a $2.5 million contribution to the town of amherst over the next three years as part of a broader commitment to the community beyond its campus.						read on...







town urges caution on ice, skate at your own risk 


the town of amherst conservation department is urging the community to exercise caution on frozen ponds and bodies of water. skate at your own risk.						read on...







charter review committee seeks public input 


the town council has appointed a charter review committee to review and propose changes to the town's charter. this message from committee members shares more about the process and how to provide feedback.						read on...







notice of property inspections 


the town of amherst in undergoing a cyclical inspection review required by the commonwealth of massachusetts [mgl chapter 59]. inspectors will be working throughout town from april 2024 through december 2025.						read on...
