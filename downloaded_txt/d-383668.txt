raleigh’s neighborhood bistro since 1995




























located in raleigh's historic five points, bloomsbury bistro is a casual neighborhood restaurant serving french inspired american cuisine. led by chef owner stephen koster, the menu rotates with the availability of fresh local ingredients and offers a notable wine list. cozy up to the bar for an impromptu lunch or night out. plan a romantic dinner in the dining room or host an event in one of our private event spaces.
