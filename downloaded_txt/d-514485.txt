about us











capital rice co., ltd. exported quality thai rice to all parts of the world. today, is well recognized for both the quality of its product and the reliability of its service. we continue to take pride in fulfilling an essential role in thailand’s on going economic grownth.capital rice is a pioneer in the use of the silo storage system to provide a consistent supply of premium quality rice. the principal warehouse covers spacious areas for container loading under roof protection, and a wharf which can accommodate ocean- going vessels for direct speedy loading.
