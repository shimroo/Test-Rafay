our name is what we are: hasofer is hebrew for the scribe.
sta"m is the acronym in both english and hebrew for scrolls (megillot), tefillin
      and mezuzot.
      throughout the ages and continuing today the sofer writes sta"m longhand
      with a kulmus, quill pen, on specially prepared klaf, parchment.
      look for our sofer holding his kulmus in the background of the page. that way you know you're on the
      original hasofer web site:
      your trusted source on the web for kashrut and value in israeli-made
      tefillin, mezuzot, megillot and jewish gifts.
