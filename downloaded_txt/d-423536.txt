your florida home builder
since 1962 the deltona corporation has been building new homes and creating some of florida's most successful communities. we build new homes with the needs of the home owner in mind. you can find your desired lifestyle in our single family homes or multi-family homes in ocala, st. augustine, north port, sunny hills or citrus springs.
we only use the most durable products to withstand florida's weather for years to come. the skilled craftsmanship in our homes will give you peace of mind knowing we always go above and beyond. "just good" homes are not good enough for us – or you!

live the florida lifestyle
from our west coast florida homes to our northeastern florida neighborhoods, deltona is here to build your dream home. we help you build a world class home made for your family. visit our design studios to see what we have to offer or walk our model homes in citrus springs, north port, ocala, sunny hills and st. augustine. our home designs for single family homes or community residences are built with customer satisfaction in mind.




the best florida real estate
we build homes and communities in some of florida's best cities. our sarasota & sunny hills, florida homes offer sandy beaches, excellent fishing and stress-free living. for those interested in moving to central florida, live next-door to the sunshine state's large nature preserves, world-renown theme parks and top-class equestrian centers. in northeastern florida, residents can enjoy golf resort living year-round in world golf village or delve into the history of the nation's oldest city in st. augustine.
