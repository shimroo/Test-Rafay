2025 spring primary 


the 2025 spring primary will be held on tuesday, february 18, 2025. find information about your ballot, polling places, and deadlines.						read on...







property taxes past due 


property taxes were considered past due as of february 1.						read on...







clerk of circuit court warns walworth county residents of jury duty scam 


walworth county clerk of circuit court michele jacobs is alerting residents about a telephone scam that recently defrauded a local woman of $2,000.						read on...







new office hours 


walworth county’s office hours changed to 8 am to 4:30 pm, monday through friday, effective february 3, 2025.						read on...







walworth county and united way announce sixth round of funding for local nonprofits 


walworth county has earmarked $400,000 for the united way to distribute to local 501(c)(3) nonprofit and 501(c)19 veterans service agencies in 2025 and 2026. applications are now available for the latest round of funding.						read on...







participants wanted for the next chapter 


the aging and disability resource center seeks supporting caregivers who are navigating life after loss and are ready to find new connections with those in similar situations.						read on...







forage management workshop 


dairy farmers in the region are invited to join uw-madison division of extension educators for an educational workshop on forage management on thursday february 27, from 10 am to 3 pm, at the community engagement center in whitewater.						read on...







adrc hosts mind over matter workshop 


the adrc's mind over matter workshop is a small, community-based program geared to women aged 50 or older.						read on...
