2025 tangible personal property worksheets 


march 15, 2025, is the deadline date to report tangible personal property or to return 2025 property worksheets to the butler county appraiser's office to avoid filing penalties (k.s.a. 79-1422).						read on...






poll site open hours change 


the hours for all future elections in 2025 and going forward are changing.						read on...






spring fling senior resource fair 


the butler county department on aging is excited to present spring fling 2025 - butler county's annual senior resource fair.						read on...






landfill rate & operating hour changes 


the butler county landfill will implement new operating hours and rates beginning january 1, 2025.						read on...






driver's license office closure - lunch 


the driver's license office located on the second floor of the historic courthouse in el dorado is closed from 12 pm to 1 pm for lunch monday through friday.						read on...






pro-rated watercraft or other tangible personal property value 


in accordance with k.s.a. 79-306e & k.s.a. 79-345; the owner of record must provide the county appraiser with documentation (bill-of-sale) of an acquisition or sale on or before december 20th of the same year.						read on...






city of el dorado central ave project 


we have been notified that the city of el dorado will begin work on a construction project on central ave in el dorado on monday, june 24th.						read on...
