latest news


	stay up to date with monticello
 






  
 
 















glow fest 


get ready to glow on saturday, february 22! presented by montiarts, this unique event features a quirky parade with all things glow: from creatures and signs to wearables and more!						read on...







city council highlights 


catch up on the latest city council highlights on the city blog. meetings are also available to stream online or watch on cable channel 12 (fibernet and tds subscribers) or channel 180 (charter subscribers).			read on...







spring tree sale 


the spring tree sale begins february 3. trees cost $50/each and quantities are limited. orders are filled first come, first served.						read on...







winter parking restrictions 


beginning november 15 through april 1 there is no parking allowed on city streets from 1 a.m. to 6 a.m.						read on...







sidewalk snow & ice 


property owners: please clear snow and ice from adjacent sidewalks within 48 hours following a snow or ice event.						read on...







press release: monticello receives grant for stick program 


the city of monticello has been awarded a grant to strengthen the city’s urban tree population, with a focus on community engagement and involvement through the stick program.			read on...







reducing manganese at home 


reduce manganese levels in your home with water treatment options like carbon filters or reverse osmosis systems. learn more about the city’s plans for a water treatment facility.			read on...







boulevard tree trimming 


boulevard trees will be trimmed during the winter months to ensure clearance for larger trucks and general street safety.			read on...







stay connected! 


subscribe to notify me to receive updates and information about community news and happenings.						read on...







read the latest edition of our newsletter! 


a digital copy of our latest newsletter is available on our website. city newsletters are published twice a year and mailed to city residents.			read on...







annual water report 


the city of monticello submits a report of water quality testing and analysis to the minnesota department of health every year. that report is shared in our utility bills and published to the city's website each june.						read on...
