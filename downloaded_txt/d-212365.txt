about kierland commonsthere's so much to discover
kierland commons is a unique and upscale outdoor lifestyle center located in the most desirable city of scottsdale, az, artfully blending shopping, dining, office and living in an intimate setting. kierland is home to 74+ specialty retailers and restaurants, including anthropologie, arhaus, chanel fragrance & beauty boutique, crate & barrel, evereve, lucky brand, sundance, sur la table, tecovas, travismathew, and vuori. dining options include chef-driven restaurants like mastro's ocean club, the greene house, north italia, zinc bistro, tommy bahama, and the mission.
