    welcome to sgcdpro


 the society of glass and ceramic decorated products (sgcdpro) provides designers, decorators and marketers of glass, ceramic and related products with resources for maximizing profitability, technical applications and regulatory compliance. included are our newsletter, deco news, which is published nine times a year, our annual directory of members which includes a comprehensive buyer’s guide of materials and services used in the industry, and access to regulatory and legislative information compiled by our legislative and regulatory liaison, sandra spence. sgcdpro also sponsors an annual conference and exhibition in the spring (see seminar and tradeshow tab).
