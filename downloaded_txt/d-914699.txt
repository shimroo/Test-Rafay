welcome to thelen auto group!
thelen auto group, a family-owned and operated automotive dealership group in bay city, mi, has been serving the people of bay city and the entire great lakes bay region of mid-michigan since 1977.  that is when our founder, tom thelen, created the company that bears his name. 
for over four decades, we have been privileged to serve customers from bay city, midland, saginaw, flint, gladwin and all of central michigan with an excellent vehicle buying experience. throughout that time, we have always maintained tom's commitment to the finest in customer service, honesty, and integrity.
that commitment got tom thelen noticed more than once: in 2007 he won the northwood university automotive dealer education award, and in 2011 he took home time magazine's michigan dealer of the year award. even today, tom's values and ideals remain an integral part of the thelen auto group vehicle dealership experience, living on through the daily input of his sons: mike, pat, and joe.
more about us
             




the thelen auto group is committed to providing our valued mid-michigan customers with an excellent selection of quality, gently used vehicles at competitive prices.  we are proud to serve the bay city, mi area, including saginaw, midland, and mount pleasant. your complete satisfaction, both before and after the vehicle sale, is always our top priority.
all pre-owned vehicles at our dealership are carefully inspected and road-tested by our experienced service technicians before being allowed to be part of our extensive inventory. we maintain a large selection of gently used vehicles of a wide variety of makes and models for you to choose from.  whether you need an efficient sedan for your daily commute, a powerful truck for hauling, a spacious suv for your family's weekend adventures or an innovative hybrid or ev, you will find what you are looking for when you think thelen in the great lakes bay region of michigan.  ask us about our super selection of certified pre-owned (cpo) vehicles from a variety of vehicle brands.
vehicles seven (7) model years old or newer with 80,000 miles or less on the odometer come with a 3-month/3,000-mile used vehicle warranty for your peace of mind. it may be put on any vehicle that passes inspection.

multiple brands for sale and lease
team thelen has the perfect vehicle for every driver in central michigan! with the super selection of makes and models we offer the vehicle shoppers in the great lakes bay region, you're sure to find something that appeals to you. we've got everything from detroit greats like chrysler, dodge, ram, and jeep, to american-built japanese and korean cars like honda, subaru, mazda, and kia.  plus, we have a great selection of dependable german vehicles made by volkswagen and audi.
in the market for a gently used vehicle? in addition to our wide selection of new models, we also maintain a large variety of pre-owned cars, trucks, minivans, crossovers, suvs and evs, all inspected and maintained (if necessary) by our expert technicians. if you demand nothing less than a bargain, our list of affordable used cars that can be yours for $15,000 or less is just a single click away.   if you need more information on any of our vehicles, you can contact us online, visit one of our bay city locations, or call us at (989) 414-2711.

you know you're in good hands when you visit a thelen dealership. whether your perfect vehicle is new or used, imported or domestic, thelen auto group will do our best to help you find it.


family owned
the thelen family has been in the auto retail business for three generations and counting, and we care about your shopping experience. thanks to spending so much time in the industry, we understand the value of repeat customers who love our cars just as much as we do, and who appreciate our considerate and knowledgeable staff.
we also take pride in actively participating in our community. that's why the thelen auto group and the thelen family foundation support over 70 charities and organizations, including the bay area women's center, the bay city state theater, michigan blood, toys for tots, and the good samaritan rescue mission of bay city.



out-of-towners welcome
thelen auto group doesn't just serve the residents of bay city; we serve everyone who lives in the saginaw bay area. whether you're from saginaw, midland, cass city, gladwin, grand blanc, west branch, mt. pleasant, birch run, or anywhere else in mid-michigan, we are eager to serve your auto buying or vehicle service needs.



service and express lane

by bringing your vehicle to thelen auto group in bay city, mi for all your service repair and express service needs, from regularly scheduled appointments in our express service lanes to major repairs in our main service center, you can count on a continued driving experience unlike any other - for every single mile of your lease or vehicle ownership. thelen auto group technicians are highly trained and certified to perform whatever work you may need on your vehicle.
