welcome to hotelguide.networks' directory of hotels and hotel reviews for orlando, florida!

like all visitors to the area, disney was struck by the miles of orange groves and undeveloped land that surrounded a small florida town built around a small lake and municipal park, the perfect site for unrestricted development of his dream. greater orlando, has since become a theme park capital in just a few decades, offering one of the largest constructed entertainment and shopping areas in the world, with a growing inventory of hotels and resorts ranging from classic old florida to high-tech contemporary. legendary theme parks are packed with blockbuster rides, dining, and nightlife options, all centrally located within an hour's drive of the john f. kennedy space center, busch gardens, and the atlantic and gulf beaches. 

many greater orlando resorts and hotels, including those in  kissimmee - st. cloud, offer the full range of amenities, from children's programs to spas, golf, and other recreation. many also provide free transportation to theme parks and other attractions. with thousands of rooms plus rental vacation home and villa properties, orlando lodging options range from budget to all-out luxury accommodatons, from bed and breakfasts, inns, and motels to high-rise hotels and world class resorts.
