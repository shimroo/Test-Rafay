welcome to the school of
computing science



the school of computing science at simon fraser university is ranked among the top computing science schools in canada for research excellence. our world-class faculty consistently produce high-quality research published in leading computer science journals. our programs are designed to meet the growing demand for graduates with the scientific knowledge and technical skills needed to excel in today’s technological world. we challenge our students to reach their full potential and develop the tools to become effective problem-solvers and make impact in the industry.






discover cs at sfu



our computing science programs cover software design, artificial intelligence, algorithms, programming, networks, databases, and operating systems. taught by world-class faculty, they are designed to prepare you to excel in today’s technology-driven world and pursue a career in academia or industry. 
computing science at sfu stands out for its dynamic and innovative approach to solving real-world problems with technology.
