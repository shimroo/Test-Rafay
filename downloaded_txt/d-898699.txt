rebuilding together peninsula is a non-profit organization providing essential safe & healthy housing-related repair service at no cost to vulnerable homeowners and community facilities throughout san mateo county and northern santa clara county.


we are a member of the national rebuilding together network, working toward a vision of safe homes and communities for everyone.


since 1989, we’ve serviced more than 3,500 homes and 400 facilities.



contribute today to support our home repair work.


as prices continue to rise, many homeowners are increasingly forced to choose between paying for necessities such as food and medications or addressing critical home repairs. by supporting our [re]building efforts, you fuel our vision of safe homes and communities for everyone.
