compomotive motorsport wheels
since 1973, compomotive wheels have successfully satisfied the demands of leading motorsport teams worldwide, producing a range of road & competition wheels conforming to the most stringent world testing standards. constant research, development & investment in the latest technology for all aspects of wheel production ensures the highest level of quality.















performance
compomotive motorsport wheels are available in an extensive range of offsets & sizes from 13 inch through to 18 inch.











heritage
the compomotive heritage range of wheels are designed to compliment your classic performance vehicle perfectly.











off-road
the ultimate aluminium rally raid wheel. heat treated material and reinforced rim edges give you stage winning capability. suitable for standard road fitments.
