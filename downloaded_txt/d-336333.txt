department of neurology



 department of neurology
 












the emory university department of neurology is a vibrant and active academic medical school department with prominent clinical, educational and research programs. our specialty clinics span the neurologic diseases and conditions, and deliver state-of-the-art neurologic care to patients and families.  quality patient care simultaneously provides critical training opportunities for tomorrow’s neurologists, as well research opportunities to better understand neurologic disease.  our translational neuroscience research programs not only seek to develop more effective treatment strategies with fewer side effects, but also to identify disease risk factors with the goal of preventing disease development.









education programs









			
																	residency
															
			


emory neurology offers residents an outstanding educational experience with rich and diverse clinical exposure to neurologic diseases, world-class research and teaching by renowned faculty.



residency program details



















			
																	fellowships
															
			


emory's competitive neurology fellowships focus on helping trainees gain knowledge, skills and experience to become clinical experts in their specialty and productive investigators.



fellowship details


















research









			
																	department research
															
			


the department of neurology at emory university is a leader in neurological research, generating new insight into brain-related diseases and disorders and developing new procedures and medications for treatment.
