hp welcome



shaped by a pioneering 27-year legacy and nearly $3.4 billion in sales since inception, engel & völkers snell real estate continues to innovatively introduce the allure of los cabos to the world. as baja california sur’s #1 top-producing brokerage, we unlock the door to exclusive beachfront retreats, majestic mountaintop haciendas, exquisite golf villas, luxury resort residences, phenomenal developer and land opportunities, as well as a diverse array of commercial properties. our portfolio caters to a spectrum of budgets, welcoming first-time buyers and seasoned investors alike. at engel & völkers snell real estate, we are dedicated to mastering the art of real estate, offering the ultimate home buying and selling services.
with a curated collection of luxury properties gracing the most coveted locations across cabo – from san jose del cabo to cabo san lucas, extending to the east cape, todos santos and la paz, – our esteemed brokerage boasts an elite team of over 60 multi-lingual top-producing advisors and dedicated support staff. guiding our vision and strategy is owner, president | ceo, vanessa fukunaga.
engel & völkers snell real estate proudly showcases an illustrious award-winning history, notably earning the prestigious title of best real estate agency in mexico by the esteemed international property awards for six consecutive years, as well as recognition from luxury lifestyle awards. not just a brand in name only, the brokerage is dedicated to staying at the forefront of the industry and elevating the profile of los cabos luxury real estate on the international stage.
today, we invite you to experience a legacy of excellence with engel & völkers snell real estate.
