jewelry packaging at its finest!
for over sixty years, novel box company, ltd. has been producing and distributing a variety of unique and high quality jewelry boxes, jewelry displays, gemstone boxes and packaging that's both endearing and elegant. novel box company, ltd. keeps on evolving and updating product designs and concepts to keep up with the latest trends with our customer's preferences and requests.  

 


 luxurious jewelry boxes 
 novel box company, ltd.
manufactures and distributes jewelry boxes ranging in style from wooden
jewelry boxes, leatherette jewelry boxes, cardboard jewelry boxes, cotton
filled boxes, clear pvc lidded boxes, velour & velveteen jewelry boxes,
gemstone boxes, drawstring pouches, pearl folders, pen boxes, leatherette
and velvet jewelry displays, jewelry supplies, packaging & more. 
we also specialize in custom design and style for jewelry boxes & displays.
