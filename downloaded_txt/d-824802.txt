							an intimate caribbean oasis that captures the essence of the island.

little cayman is world renowned for the famed bloody bay wall, pristine coral reefs, and year-round perfect diving conditions. yet just as sensational as the diving is the intimate, warm experience and outstanding resort amenities of little cayman beach resort. a spectacular oceanfront setting, exclusive valet diving service, the finest of amenities, and personal hospitality make little cayman beach resort the best dive resort experience on all of little cayman.

reef divers is renowned for offering personalized valet diving, but what does that mean? essentially, reef divers takes all the hard work out of scuba diving, from the heavy lifting and care of your boat gear, to switching out tanks and safely storing your gear at the end of the day. at our on-site dive shop, we also have separate rinse tanks for photo equipment and wetsuits and general dive gear, plus a drying room with hanging space for wetsuits and mesh bags so you don’t have to lug them back to your room.
