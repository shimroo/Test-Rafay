massillon plaque company | north canton, ohio
our 65+ years of experience, diverse range of products, creativity, and quality craftsmanship will ensure you find what best fits your needs. "quality awards & custom signage"
architectural signage | awards & achievements | custom | document reprints employee & donor recognition | gifts | office & tactile signage | wall displays
known for our elegant, world-renowned art in stainless steel technology, we can transform images into long-lasting stainless-steel plaques. by using photography with our process, we can reproduce high-resolution graphics of the finest detail. massillon plaque company has access to many mediums including acrylic, aluminum, cast, crystal, and metal.
