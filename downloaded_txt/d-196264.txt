welcome! everyone belongs here!

 

with remarkable warmth and bold, progressive worship, we are cultivating a joyful, supportive congregation where you can explore your relationship with god on your own terms. we strive to be a hub for the entire lgbtqia+ community, creating a liberating movement within and beyond our church walls. together, we commit to racial and social justice while embodying an optimistic hope for the future. we invite you to bring your full self to unite your spirituality, sexuality, gender identity, and heart for justice. come, join us!
