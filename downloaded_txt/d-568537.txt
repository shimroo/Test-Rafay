our mission




empowering tomorrow's champions: ridgefield amateur hockey association is committed to fostering a vibrant community where every player feels valued and supported, both on and off the ice. through a steadfast dedication to competitiveness, we cultivate a spirit of sportsmanship and teamwork that transcends the game itself. central to our mission is the unwavering commitment to player development, ensuring each individual reaches their fullest potential athletically and personally. by instilling these principles of community, competitiveness, and player development, we aim to inspire a lifelong love for the game of hockey while nurturing the leaders of tomorrow.

about us
ridgefield amateur hockey association is dedicated to creating a thriving community where every player is valued and supported, fostering sportsmanship and teamwork on and off the ice. with a focus on player development and a commitment to competitiveness, we aim to help each individual reach their full athletic and personal potential. our mission is to instill values of community, competitiveness, and player development through the game of hockey.
