new water customer service hours 


the water department has updated hours for the customer service office.						read on...







dike fees 


beginning march 1, the texas city dike will go cashless; only card payments can be used. the dike remains free for texas city residents, and the fees for non-residents have not been changed.						read on...







movement is medicine 


step into the new year -- and into our new "movement is medicine: the mayor's 2025 health initiative" -- with us at a line dancing class on thursday, jan. 30. lace up your dancing shoes and get ready to join us for an evening of dancing, fun and movement!						read on...







local hazard mitigation plan 


community input needed! texas city emergency management has identified natural disaster risks and vulnerabilities in our area and created a local hazard mitigation plan to minimize the impact of disasters, protecting people and property in our community.						read on...







winter storm closures 


stay up to date about weather-related closures of city facilities, schools and services. currently, all city facilities - including city hall, the municipal court, all recreation & fitness centers - are closed on tuesday, jan. 21 and wednesday, jan. 22.						read on...







2025 community calendar 


the 2025 community calendars are here! stop by city hall, moore memorial public library, the nessler center, lowry fitness center or our recreation centers now to get your free copy.						read on...







cdbg citizen advisory committee annual organizational meeting 


join us for the citizens advisory committee annual organizational meeting! on thursday, january 30, 2025, at 5:30 p.m., we will meet at the city hall annex to elect 2025 officers. this meeting is free and open to all.						read on...







bird flu information 


a bird in texas city tested positive for avian influenza. animal control officers who collected the bird tested negative in initial rapid tests and in confirmatory tests. there are no reported or confirmed cases of bird flu in humans in galveston county.						read on...







development update 


find out about commercial and residential developments being planned in texas city firsthand from the mayor in the first edition of our new, quarterly "moments with mayor johnson" feature.						read on...







new hours for municipal court 


effective september 30, 2024, the texas city municipal court has new hours of operation. the court will now be open: monday-thursday: 7:30 a.m. to 5 p.m. and friday: 7:30 a.m. to 4:30 p.m.						read on...
