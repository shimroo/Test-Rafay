welcome to mrb stone
indonesian natural stone manufacturer, supplier & exporter based in bali

mrb stone offering an extensive range of the indonesia’s most beautiful & exotic pebble stones. pebble stone bali is available in wide range of patterns, colours and sizes, so our mesh-backed pebble tiles will beautify and enhance your indoor or outdoor living spaces.
our indonesia natural stone products are commonly used for flooring, borders, entry or drive pathways, swimming pool’s accents, feature walls, shower areas, etc. where in this modern life, people are seeking for a clean, natural environment…
gathered from various locations of indonesian archipelago, they are carefully crafted, color-matched and hand-sorted into the finished articles by our highly skilled and experienced craftsmen with very stringent quality assurance procedures.
