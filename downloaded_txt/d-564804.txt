department of physics and astronomy







where stellar academics meet practical career paths


looking to uncover the mysteries of the universe and understand how everything works at its most fundamental level? whether you're fascinated by the behavior of materials at ultra-low temperatures or dream of revolutionizing energy transmission, there's so much to explore in the department of physics and astronomy. 




 







explore our program

gain the scientific and mathematical tools needed to explore our world while preparing for exciting careers in research, education, or countless other fields that value sharp analytical minds.

graduate studies

pursue studies for a phd with majors in astrophysics, condensed matter physics, high energy physics, and nuclear physics. minor credit courses are also available for students of other departments.
