our work improves the lives of 3 million older new yorkersat liveon ny, we believe that all people have a future. our work is centered on making sure that new york is a great place to age. we do this through targeted advocacy, data-driven policy, direct assistance & innovative programs. as a membership organization, we represent more than 100 non-profits from small, single-site centers to large multi-service organizations serving older adults throughout new york state. through our work and membership, we represent the 3.2 million older new yorkers and their caregivers. featured work
