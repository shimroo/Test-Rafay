our mission

        
            every one of our dogs is loved and they are a part of our family, living in our home.  we strive to give each one a chance to reach their full potential, living a happy and contented life, whether it be in the conformation ring, or dedicated to bringing joy to their adopted family.   
            
            we strive for the betterment of the cavalier and breed only dogs that have passed their health testing.  we do all we can to ensure that each puppy born into our home will leave with the best possible foundation of health, love and socialization.
