powder springs events







	check out our events

 






  
 
 















brown bag concert series 


from noon to 1 p.m. every thursday in april, enjoy your lunch in thurman springs park and enjoy live music by local artists!			additional info...







pick it up powder springs 


help keep powder springs clean and green by taking part in pick it up powder springs! meet at thurman springs park to receive pick it up supplies and a map of an area in powder springs that needs beautification.			additional info...







shred day 


protect against identity theft by having documents shredded securely on-site by a licensed, bonded and locally-owned shredding company. shred those medical bills, credit card statements and other documents that contain personal or sensitive information.			additional info...







national day of prayer 


join us may 1 at 6:30 p.m. in thurman springs park for a time of prayer, praise through music, and an inspirational message from local spiritual leaders and worship teams.			additional info...







2025 events calendar 


powder springs has a full schedule of events that occur both in the downtown area and throughout the city. there is always plenty of parking and most events are free to the public. there is something for every age and interest!			additional info...
