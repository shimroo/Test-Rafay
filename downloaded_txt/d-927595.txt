    more news
  



 





























you asked for it
you asked for it –  the bandcamp shut in singles series compiled on cd with all the original artwork and and credits for each song in the 28 page booklet.  includes the extra jimbo mathus produced version of honky tonk women. with warner hodges, andy york and chip robinson.




                        buy
                      












lakeside
lakeside — eric ambel’s 2016 studio release produced by jimbo mathus is jammed with rock and roll, some blues, some roadhouse country and a lot of guitar.  the vinyl lp is limited to 500 copies signed and numbered.




                        buy
                      












roscoe live: vol 1
roscoe live: vol 1 – excited to present this full set recorded live multi-track at the livestock music festival in bovina, ny august 27,2016 with keith christopher on bass, mo goldner on guitar and phil cimino on drums. mixed by roscoe in brooklyn. available here and at gigs. web purchase includes immediate mp3 download.




                        buy
                      












roscoe sampler
roscoe sampler – special edition mix of tracks from eric ambel’s first three records. roscoe’s gang: produced by the late lou whitney and features guest appearances by syd straw, peter holsapple and the pride of springfield, mo the morells. loud & lonesome: roscoe stomping through some real heavy tunes in brooklyn. knucklehead: tracks collected from many sessions with the bands he produced including the bottle rockets, martin’s folly and the yayhoos with appearances by steve earle and warner hodges. also includes the unreleased track “a charmer’s tale”. web purchase includes immediate mp3 download.




                        buy
                      












knucklehead
knucklehead — the 2004 release from eric ambel on the lakeside lounge label. includes appearances by the yayhoos, the del-lords, the bottle rockets and steve earle.




                        buy

honky tonk womeneric ambel released you asked for it: the shut in singles series on lakeside lounge records may 20, 2022. the release is accompanied by a video for ambel’s interpretation of “honky tonk women”. mojo nixon, who is playing the track on his sirusxm show, praised, “finally – a honky tonk version of ‘honky tonk women’ – way to go roscoe!” view the video here and listen to the album here.
