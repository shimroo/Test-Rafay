oriental merchandise calls it quits
since 1960, oriental merchandise specialized in a full line of discount party supplies and discount party decoration supplies for a wide variety of festive occasions and celebrations including your prom, luau, baby shower, birthday, holiday party or other special events.
sadly, oriental merchandise of metairie, la is no longer in business.  the business faced increasing competition in difficult economic times, and also suffered many lost customers during the recovery period after hurricane katrina.  the owners decided to close the business, and did so in may of 2008.
