more than a building

















center in the square owned and operated attractions and organizations:










free aquariums

beautiful, peaceful, educational.  enjoy our atrium's vibrant fish tanks on the first floor!




support our free aquariums











roanoke pinball museum

the roanoke pinball museum is an interactive museum dedicated to the science, art, and history of pinball. our games are fully playable and span a near-century of production from the 1930's through today.




visit roanoke pinball museum











kids square children's museum

kids square is a premier destination where families gather to explore, play, and learn through a s.t.e.a.m-enriched, educational, hands-on learning environment.




visit kids square                                        











roanoke starcade arcade museum

roanoke, virginia's interactive museum with 3000+ games!




visit roanoke starcade

 our mission



to be an active participant in economic development, both locally and regionally, by helping assure the financial health of vital elements of western and central virginia’s cultural quality of life
