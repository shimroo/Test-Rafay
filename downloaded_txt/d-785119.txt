fulfilling all your sewing and quilting needs in mid-michigan area
we’re excited to welcome you to our family-owned fabric, sewing machine, and quilting center serving lansing, mi and surrounding areas including jackson. as one of the largest sewing machine companies in the country, country stitches offers 12,500 sq. ft. of shopping with a wide variety of some of the best brands of sewing, quilting & embroidery machines as well as quilting supplies and fabric for all your sewing, quilting, and embroidery needs. since 1982, we’ve shared our knowledge and unique shopping experience with everyone who walks in the door. we offer 1,500 classes throughout the year, a yearly charity quilt-a-thon, and in-store service and repairs. let us broaden your sewing and quilting inventory today!

our products & services
we provide a wide variety of high-quality products and services for our customers. check out all we have to offer.
 

sewing machineswe sell the best sewing, embroidery and quilting machines, sergers and sewing furniture with certified staff to assist you. 
 

shopbrowse thousands of choices in our online shop for embroidery, quilting, and sewing machines and products.
 

service & repairour factory-certified service and repair department repairs and maintains all types of sewing machine or sergers.
 

events & classeswhether you’re a beginner or an expert, we offer hundreds of quilting, sewing and embroidery classes with expert instructors. we also offer special events and retreats from national educators.
 

quilt shopover 6,000 bolts of 100% quilting cotton fabric from various companies & designers.

benefits of shopping at your local sewing and quilting shop
a trip to your local sewing and quilting shop is a unique experience. we welcome you in as we help you choose the best machine, find unique fabric patterns, and guide you to get the best supplies for your needs. we’re surrounded by creativity and color all day, so we love to share our knowledge to help you create your own masterpiece. join friends to learn new trends or brush up on old skills in one of our hundreds of classes. we have all of the latest and greatest tools in the sewing and quilting industry. our doors are always open for you to learn as you browse our extensive selection.
