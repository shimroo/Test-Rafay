 about us established in 1996 in salkum, washington, rocky top arena is a family owned and operated barrel racing facility. our goal is to provide barrel racers with the best facility in the pacific northwest. our expert riders, viki and vanda bring you a combined 40 years of experience. we look forward to seeing you soon at rocky top arena!
