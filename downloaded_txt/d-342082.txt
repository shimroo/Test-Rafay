our
mission



dci donor services, inc. (dcids) is dedicated to saving and improving lives through organ, eye and tissue donation. we have the honor of serving donor heroes and their families by connecting their lifesaving gifts to those who need them most. together, we are transforming lives, and our work will not be finished until we end the waiting list for good.

dedicated to diversity
dei is integral to everything we do – we strive to be a place of fairness, belonging, and forward thinking. we are dedicated to saving all lives, and reflect that commitment through our employees and work environment. we believe in creating an equitable foundation for the future and an inclusive space for all.
