here for you.



child & family guidance center





































your health matters.at child & family guidance center we are committed to providing comprehensive, accessible healthcare services for children, adults and families across north texas. our full spectrum of affordable care includes mental and behavioral health, primary medical care and a complete suite of pharmaceutical services. with 9 clinics in 7 counties, we have a team of qualified professionals near you. we know finding a provider you can trust is important and we are committed to serving you and your family with quality care and compassion. we’re glad you’re here.

comprehensive services












cfgc offers a complete array of mental healthcare options- including screenings, counseling, and medication management.














child & family rx is our low-cost pharmacy that  serves clients in three clinics.














cfgc’s priority care is a groundbreaking walk-in service available for new and existing clients.














clients who may be at a higher risk can look towards our specialty services to find out what cfgc can do for them.
