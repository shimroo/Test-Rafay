 welcome to the united methodist foundation of louisianasince 1975, we have offered an array of financial services for churches and individuals while promoting a culture of generosity to methodist donors and churches throughout louisiana. these include investments, planned giving education and counsel, estate planning resources, and more. if you have any questions or just want to connect, please contact us.
