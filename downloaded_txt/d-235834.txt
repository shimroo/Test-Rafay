about first enterprise bank

first enterprise bank is an independent and locally-owned community bank in oklahoma city.
since the bank's founding in 1907 our mission has been to serve our communities by providing exceptional service. that means quick, local decision-making, and a bank that puts you in touch with people who understand your needs. we call it relationship banking. you'll call it the best banking relationship you've ever had because at first enterprise bank our loyalty to you comes first.
