our story



vicki's secret first opened for business in 1992, inspired by passion for couture fashion. for over a quarter of a century, we have brought fabulous, iconic apparel to our wonderful customers, providing them the finest designer clothes and accessories. we offer a large collection of modern,  high-end clothing, handbags, shoes, and jewelry from top designers around the world. because we have so many designer pieces, we are only able to offer a curated selection of them online. if you do not find what you are looking for on our website, please email us with your specific requests. also, please come back often, as we add new items to our online store regularly and follow us on instagram @vickissecretsconsignments for updates. 



our fashionista



with over three decades in the high-end clothing consignment business, proprietor jan mettner has developed a keen eye and knowledge for couture clothing, and chooses only the finest pieces to sell in both her retail store and online shop. jan’s exceptional experience has built a reputation of quality that vicki’s secret adheres to. it is her personal joy to establish long-term relationships with consigners from the san francisco bay area and across america, europe, and asia.  jan and her knowledgeable staff look forward to assisting you with accessory choices, sizes, styles, and looks that work best for you and your lifestyle, as well as suggesting appropriate choices for specific occasions.
