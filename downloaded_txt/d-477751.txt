winter storm 

with the winter storm hitting this weekend, residents are encouraged to remove their vehicles from the public streets to assist the dpw in clearing the streets.  your cooperation allows the streets to be cleared in a timely manner.				read on...







tax payment deadline approaching 

deadline to pay your 2024 summer and/or winter taxes at city hall is friday, february 28, 2025. as of march 1, 2025, all outstanding taxes will be sent to macomb county for collection, where an initial 5% penalty plus 1% interest per month will apply.				read on...







tax forms are here! 

the lois wagner memorial library now has the following tax materials available: federal forms 1040, 1040sr and 1040sp, as well as the corresponding instruction manuals.
