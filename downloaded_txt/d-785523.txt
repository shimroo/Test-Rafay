to create and promote the performing arts.












 

among the pecan groves and cotton fields of southwest georgia, live theatre flourishes under the banner of theatre albany.  this award-winning organization has offered outstanding performances since its inception in 1932.  theatre albany is the oldest and one of the most highly regarded cultural organizations in the city of albany.read about our history
