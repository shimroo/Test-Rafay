about storycenterand what we dostorycenter is an innovative participatory media organization rooted in the power of personal narratives for connection, education and social action.




    about us
  














storycenter works with individuals and organizations to use story and digital storytelling to promote understanding, connection, and social change.storycenter uses story work processes to promote healing, growth, and social change by creating spaces for listening to and sharing stories. we believe that storytelling is a powerful tool for creating connection, building empathy, and inspiring action. we are committed to helping people from all walks of life use the power of their own voices and experiences to help build a just and healthy world.
