welcome to maximum motorsports
riverhead, new york
we are located in riverhead, long island, conveniently located directly off the long island expressway (495) and fifteen minutes from the hamptons. see our map/hours page for directions. our new state-of-the-art facility boasts two floors of motorcycles, atv's, side x sides, snowmobiles, motocross bikes, scooters, utv's and personal watercraft. our dealership is a must see for everyone! you’ve never seen a dealership like this!! 

we are a consistent winner of top sales performers in the entire country! our goal is to showcase the widest inventory of yamaha, kawasaki, suzuki, triumph, and arctic cat®. our dedicated sales staff loves riding and will help you make an informed decision so that you are comfortable with your purchase. we have several good resources for financing. it's quick and easy! all credit applications are accepted. if we don't get you financed... no one can. we do same day delivery, plates, and arrange insurance for you.
