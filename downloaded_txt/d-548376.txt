with roots dating back to 1780  switzerland and 10 generations of performers the nock! family circus going so far back as being the first circus of switzerland. from performing for the queen of england to alongside the beatles on the ed sullivan show, or breaking records at madison square garden. the nock family has always looked to make people smile and inspire awe with each and every act and show they produce and perform in.  with help from the very best  performers from all over the world who have been in every and any show from ringling brothers to cirque du soleil, nock productions and the nock family circus looks forward to helping you elevate and bring your next events to new heights!
