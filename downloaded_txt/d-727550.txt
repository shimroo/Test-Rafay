welcome to cogaf!  a family of believers, churches & ministries in pursuit of jesus and his way of doing things.we are:  people of his presence we are a family of believers and churches whose lives have been forever marked by the love and presence of god. in all that we do together, we pursue jesus in hopeful expectation that heaven will be released on earth. the lord replied, "my presence will go with you, and i will give you rest."  then moses said to him, "if your presence does not go with us, do not send us up from here." ~exodus 33:14-15people of fellowship we are a community of believers devoted to biblical teaching, prayer and fellowship.  we are committed to creating opportunities for people to develop a lifestyle of fellowship with god and each other. "they were continually devoting themselves to the apostles' teaching and to fellowship, to the breaking of bread and to prayer."  ~acts 2:42                                                                                                   "but if we walk in the light as he himself is in the light, we have fellowship with one another, and the blood of jesus his son cleanses us from all sin." ~1 john 1:7people of movementour culture is characterized by worship, the presence of god, family, revival and missions.  we are a movement of people compelled by the love of jesus to go into all the world. "go, therefore and make disciples of all the nations, baptizing them in the name of the father and of the son and of the holy spirit, teaching them to observe all things that i have commanded you; and lo, i am with you always, even to the end of the age." amen ~matthew 28:19-20
