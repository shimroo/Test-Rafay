welcome to clementines restaurant

  
for banquet room reservation please email us at  eliasdeek@att.net  
 clementine's is probably the oldest single-family restaurant in the northwest 1960 area. the appetite-inspiring aromas drifting from the kitchen stand as proof of our success since our beginnings in 1977.
clementine's offers an exciting array of options for your dining pleasure. we serve american cuisine with a flair toward continental style.  
not only does the deek family serve up taste-provoking cuisine from the kitchen, we also deliver your meals with an appetizing display that enhances the taste even more. thus, we are the perfect choice for your next special occasion or catering event.
our private banquet room has a capacity for 77 people and is an ideal place for your private and special events. we have served weddings, rehearsal dinners, birthday and baby showers, anniversaries, christmas and new year's eve parties, as well as company parties and corporate functions. we will customize your event menu just to your liking and accommodate you in any possible way.
