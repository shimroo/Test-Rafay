earn your degree from golden gate university


at golden gate university, we’re focused on the future of education with online and hybrid degree and certificate programs that are designed for working adults. for over 120 years, we’ve empowered busy professionals to pursue world-class degrees from the heart of san francisco and beyond. whether you’re looking to finish your degree, start a new career, or qualify for more opportunities, earning a degree from ggu can help you make your goals a reality.

why golden gate university?

at golden gate university, we bring the energy and opportunity of san francisco to you by connecting you with a thriving network of industry leaders, groundbreaking companies, and endless career opportunities. our campus is located in the heart of the city’s financial district, giving you unparalleled access to fortune 500 companies, tech giants, and the silicon valley startup scene.
as a ggu student, you’ll learn from industry experts who are currently working at top organizations in their fields and have designed their courses to help you stand out in today’s competitive job market.
whether you’re learning online or connecting on campus, you’ll be immersed in san francisco’s dynamic culture of innovation, entrepreneurship, and forward-thinking that sets ggu apart.
















learn more about our schools








ageno school of business

the ageno school of business prepares aspiring business leaders to thrive in today’s global business environment. through innovative programs that combine theory and practical experience, our students are well-equipped to succeed in any field.

















braden school of taxation

the braden school of taxation offers the largest and one of the most respected graduate tax programs in the u.s., with many of our tax students moving into roles at the big four and global six firms after graduation.

















school of accounting

the school of accounting offers career-focused degrees and certificates created to elevate our graduates in the accounting and business world. we focus on providing students with practical, hands-on accounting education that meets the dynamic needs of the changing business landscape.

















school of law

the ggu school of law has been educating lawyers for more than 100 years. our law students share a passion for an unmatched legal education focused on practical skills and hands-on training in a supportive and collaborative environment.

















school of undergraduate studies

our school of undergraduate studies offers a wide range of degree and certificate programs designed to help working learners develop the essential personal and professional skills they will need for success in the ever-changing workplace.

ggu worldwide

ggu worldwide meets the needs of students for the next century by making higher education affordable, accessible, and in multiple languages to students worldwide.
