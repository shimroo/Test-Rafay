promotingthe welfare of retired teacherssuperannuated teachersof saskatchewan 














we are an organization of retired teachers who are dedicated to promoting the welfare of all superannuated teachers, and to maintaining the ties which bind us to each other as fellow professionals.














 
















community chapters

our chapters in saskatchewan and beyond are where our members enjoy recreation, advocacy , and community together.




about chapters







group benefits

to give you peace of mind no matter what happens, we administer the sts group benefits extended heath and dental plan to our members .




group benefits information







advocacy

we partner with the saskatchewan teachers federation to create committees that represent and support retired teachers while advocating for their welfare.
