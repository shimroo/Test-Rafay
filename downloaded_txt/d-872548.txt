projects and partnerships
 

            in addition to quarterly economic forecasts for the united states, california and los angeles and topical reports, the forecast applies its expert econometric modeling and analytic techniques to a wide-variety of projects in partnership with both public and private entities. these projects feature the same independent analysis that users have come to rely upon from the forecast.
