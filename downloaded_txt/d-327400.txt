welcome to the social enrichment center!




    enroll today!
  



the social enrichment center was founded to support individuals, families, schools and community organizations. our objective is to build children and adolescents’ self-confidence and social skills. sec provides direct care to young people, utilizing evidence-based therapeutic and instructional strategies. further, our team equips caregivers with developmentally appropriate skills and strategies to build children and adolescents’ social competencies.










































































































































































































































































































































































































































































































our services 
the services offered at sec are tailored to each clients individual goals and needs! we offer flexible programming to accommodate the client and family. services include: individual psychotherapy, individual social/behavioral therapy, group social/behavioral therapy, occupational therapy, speech therapy, and more!



learn more!


















our team 
our team is comprised of passionate licensed professional counselors, licensed clinical social workers, individual/group therapeutic facilitators, and graduate interns who are specialized in providing: psychotherapy, social skills concepts, behavioral management, cognitive behavioral therapy, applied behavioral analysis-related concepts, and more! 



learn more


















our internship
social enrichment center offers a unique opportunity for undergraduate students to gain hands-on clinical experience! our internship program is designed for psychology or related majors who are seeking a direct learning experience in a therapeutic setting. this is a multidisciplinary experience providing the ability to observe and assist in all areas of sec.
