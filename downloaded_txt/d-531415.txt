february 18, 2025 spring primary 


a spring primary will be held in the city of greenfield on tuesday, february 18, 2025.						read on...







stormwater pollution & car washes 


during a car wash, harmful debris, fluids & soap are washed off vehicles and into drains, with the potential of causing harm to aquatic environments. opting for commercial car washes over at-home car washes can reduce pollutants entering our waterways!			additional info...







free ice skating at konkel park open for the season! 


the dan jansen family fest ice rink at konkel park is officially open for the 2024-2025 seasaon! skating and skate rentals is free. the warming area and concessions is open during regular hours.			additional info...







winter/spring 2025 recreator now online! 


see what's on deck for winter/spring! registration for residents begins december 4th and december 20th for non-residents.			additional info...
