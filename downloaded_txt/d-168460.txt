looking for a costume hat for a party, theatrical 
      production, or school project?  complete your costume or 
      disguise with a costume hat from hats-usa.
we have many, many styles, including: 
      fedora hat, gangster hat, derby hat,
top hat, cowboy hat, union or confederate civil war hat, 
      and colonial hat.
make a statement with one of 
      our character hats.  be a detective with a sherlock holmes hat, 
      royalty with a 
      king's crown, or princess' tiara. try a gambler hat,
      a pirate hat, a musketeer hat, 
      a sailor hat, or any of dozens of other character hats from hats-usa.
be the life of the party with a novelty 
      hat.  select from a clown hat, a jester hat, a wizard 
      hat, or really knock 'em dead with 
      a propeller beanie cap!  let your imagination run wild with a
      novelty 
      hat from hats-usa.
holiday costuming is a snap with a holiday hat from 
      hats-usa!  we have uncle sam hats and skimmers that are great for the 
      fourth of july and other patriotic occasions. for christmas, an elf hat 
      or and santa hat. for st. pat's, a green derby hat or
top hat.
and of course, any of our hats would make a 
      great halloween hat!  get in the holiday spirit with a 
      holiday hat from hats-usa!
our selection of children's size hats includes:
      cowboy hat, king's crown, train engineer hat, coonskin cap, 
      knight's helmet, 
      yacht cap, aviator cap, propeller beanie cap, and 
      many, many more!
