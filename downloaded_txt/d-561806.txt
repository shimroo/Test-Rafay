crossroads counseling center














mental health and substance use therapy













our mission
we provide quality, cost-effective treatment that encompasses social and emotional issues, integrating home, school, and community resources. 



 


















three locations
with one location in janesville, wi and two in sun prairie, wi, we offer therapy at our clinic in individual, group, and day treatment settings. 



services



















one of my overriding life goals was to always leave whatever i touch, whether a person, place or thing, a little bit better off.  in the long run, this would help make the world a better place. 



if you are struggling and the way you deal with your life is not working, but you can't figure out what to do, we are here for you. we will be with you in your journey.



guy shilts, owner
