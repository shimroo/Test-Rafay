about us.

gonsenhausers fine rugs is a third generation rug dealer established in 1936. we’ve always believed in offering beautiful rugs that are destined to become future heirlooms. historically our expertise was rooted in quality oriental rugs, over the years we’ve come to offer every type of rug imaginable.
