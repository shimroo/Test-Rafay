property description

fairhaven apartments is a well-maintained apartment community located at 455 st. clair street in lawrenceburg, indiana. the complex offers a range of one, two, and three-bedroom apartment homes with various floor plans to suit different needs and preferences.the apartments at fairhaven feature modern finishes and amenities, including spacious living areas, fully equipped kitchens, and ample closet space. the community also offers residents access to on-site amenities such as a fitness center, swimming pool, clubhouse, and outdoor grilling areas.located in a peaceful residential neighborhood, fairhaven apartments offer residents a quiet and comfortable living environment while still being close to shopping, dining, and entertainment options in downtown lawrenceburg. the complex is also conveniently located near major highways for easy access to neighboring cities such as cincinnati, ohio, and florence, kentucky.overall, fairhaven apartments is a great option for those seeking a well-maintained and amenity-rich apartment community in the lawrenceburg area.
