welcome to the seattle metals guild



we are a washington state nonprofit organization with a mission to promote education, provide a supportive community, and advance the field of metalsmithing while broadening the horizons of its members.

become a member today



want to network and socialize with other like-minded individuals? get a discounted ticket for our symposium? apply for our grants? participate in group shows? please join us! read on for our full list of member benefits.



student membership$20 per yearsign up for a student membership if you’re currently a student (or low-income). you’ll get all the benefits of the general membership! we ask that you email us a photo of your current student id when you sign up for this membership.



general membership$55 per yearsign up for a general membership if you want to get all the benefits of being a member, as listed above! you will automatically get the newsletter, and updates about events and member opportunities.



corporate membership$70 per yearsign up for a corporate membership if you want all the benefits of general membership and you want your business or corporation to partner with smg. you will be listed on our resources page and get a shoutout in our newsletter.
