municipal servicesquickly find what you are looking for
 






  
 













building departmenthere to make sure your construction is performed to the new jersey uniform construction code and the borough's high standards for your safety.








recreation departmentoffering a variety of quality services to residents of all ages so that their leisure time may be spent in a productive, meaningful and enjoyable manner.








department of public worksproviding essential services to the residents of fort lee in a prompt, courteous, safe, efficient and cost-effective manner.








fort lee police departmentthe fort lee police department (flpd), in existence since 1904, currently has 99 active duty members under chief matthew j. hintze. 








electionsthe borough clerk oversees the entire election process within fort lee  to ensure fair and transparent elections.








parking authoritydedicated to promoting the free flow of traffic by providing, managing, and maintaining public street parking.
