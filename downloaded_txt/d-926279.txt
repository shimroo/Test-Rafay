audition for the 2025 white sabers



be a part of something great! white sabers is a dci all-age drum corps, rehearsing and performing on a weekend-only model.




    learn more
  














white sabers is a non-profit, community-based organization that gives back through performance and community outreach. white sabers strives to communicate effectively, respond to the needs of the membership, and maintain close ties with its alumni. we are an inclusive organization, welcoming members of all ages and backgrounds who seek a place in our organization. our goal is to entertain, but above all, to have a professional and approachable image in all that we do. 



western new york’s only all-age competitive drum & bugle corps




    learn more
  














upcoming events



there’s still time to join!register for a winter rehearsal to experience white sabers and learn more about drum corps. all ages and skill levels are welcome, but at least one year of marching experience is recommended for colorguard, hornline, and battery percussion candidates.what are you waiting for? come meet us at an upcoming event.

new competitive format for 2025 dci all-age championship



on the heels of the inaugural dci all-age championship at lucas oil stadium this past august, all-age corps directors voted to adjust the competitive format of their division’s world championship events in 2025. the modification will expand what was a single competitive event saturday morning of dci world championships week in 2024, to two subsequent 2025 events on friday, august 8 and saturday, august 9 at lucas oil stadium in indianapolis.

white sabers earn high brass & total effect in dci all-age open class



with a final score of 86.775, white sabers took home second place in dci all-age open class along with special awards of high brass and high total effect.
