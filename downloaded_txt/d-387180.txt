   learn about beaver bend par 3


 beaver bend par 3 is an 18-hole golf course in hummelstown, pennsylvania, that is great for the whole family and perfect for all ages. beaver bend is a hidden gem in central pennsylvania. come experience the tranquil environment with vibrant wildlife while strolling along the creek. with more than 50 years of experience, we are a premiere golfing facility that you, your family, and friends will certainly enjoy.

   golfing


 play a round of golf at beaver bend par 3 in hummelstown, pennsylvania. we feature gorgeous scenery with beautiful greens with 18 different holes to play on. in addition, we host tournaments and events throughout the year, including corporate meetings, birthday parties and golf outings.
