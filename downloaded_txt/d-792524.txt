landscaping and masonry – residential and commercial 



we specialize in creating amazing outdoor spaces! 



imagine relaxing outside on your elegant paver patio overlooking your luscious green lawn, or sitting inside cozy and warm, while someone else is managing the mounds of snow outside. sounds wonderful, right? if either of these situations entices you or if there are any other landscaping needs that you may have, then rt landscaping and masonry, incorporated is here to make your dreams come true.rt landscaping and masonry, incorporated, owned and operated by ray torres, is a full service landscaping business that provides residential and commercial lawn care. we also offer hardscaping services from installing elegant paver patios, walkways, and retaining walls. we offer landscape design that is cost effective that will enhance the look of your property.  by using rt landscaping & masonry, inc., we can create an outdoor paradise with our landscaping and hardscaping services and expertise. we value our customers and convey that through quality, reliability, affordability, and a commitment towards ensuring that you are fully satisfied.during the winter months, rt landscaping & masonry, inc. offers a full service residential and commercial snow removal service. we plow parking lots, clean and salt driveways, walkways, and treat all areas during icy conditions.
