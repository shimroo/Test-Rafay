ballater community trust seeks to enhance the experience of ballater for residents and visitors alike














what we do



ballater community trust invests resources in the sustainability, enhancement and wellbeing of the community of ballater, crathie and dinnet.we deliver projects for the community in our focus areas of improving natural surroundings, social improvement needs, community transport provision and developing recreational amenities and activities.we maintain facilities for the benefit of the community such as the greens, the network of local footpaths and defibrillators.we organise and support regular events such as walking week and the duck festival.we provide funds to local clubs and associations to support their activities.

community action plan 2023



the community action plan is the plan which local community groups use to deliver what our local community wants and needs to deliver our vision.  the ballater and crathie community council is the single point of leadership and accountability for the implementation of this plan and supported by ballater community trust.
