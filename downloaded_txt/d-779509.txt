about us
the new england predators were established in 2011.  we have certified & professional staff & we offer a unique opportunity for our athletes to maximize skill development, cultivate a true love for the sport & build a strong competitive spirit in competing. our mission is to provide a positive & fun experience while teaching children all aspects of the game of ice hockey, emphasizing teamwork & good sportsmanship. our program provides a solid balance of practice & a highly competitive schedule.
