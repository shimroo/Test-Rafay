orlando villas - florida vacation rentals
if you plan to rent a florida villa or stay in a rental home near disney for your vacation in orlando (or the gulf coast) then we have a huge selection of privately owned villas for you to choose from, in all regions from kissimmee in the east as well as to the west of disney. we own florida villas and have chosen a selection of the best value, top quality rental homes / villas in the disney area of orlando florida and the florida gulf coast to promote on orlando villas.
spend time browsing this site - we offer a variety of related services to help you plan your vacation. browse the rental villas, order florida theme park tickets (delivery to the uk, us or to your hotel / villa) for disney, universal studios, sea world, busch gardens and the other major theme parks, get quotes for cheap flights to florida, florida car hire, and many other useful services. join our free, easy to use florida forums to put your questions or hints and tips to a team of florida experts who are more than willing to offer free advice.
florida has a lot more to offer than theme parks. people worldwide travel to florida just for the golf - there are many golf courses within a 20 minute drive by car from the vacation homes we offer. many of the rental homes are on golfing communities just off the fairway and it is possible to hire golf clubs.
when you make a villa enquiry the villa rental request goes to the owner who will reply directly.
