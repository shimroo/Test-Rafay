                patriots unfiltered
              



















            patriots unfiltered: building a roster, offseason topics, latest mock drafts
          

tune-in as the pu crew dives into offseason topics, including the latest buzz around the nfl, and building the patriots roster through the draft and free agency. what should they look for, and who should remain from 2024?


















            patriots unfiltered: super bowl recap, nfl draft talk, offseason outlook
          

tune-in as we recap the super bowl, and discuss all the storylines surrounding the weekend. we dive into the draft and preparation for the nfl combine. plus, an offseason outlook including free agency, the coaching staff and more.


















            patriots unfiltered: final coaching staff updates, super bowl predictions, draft prospects
          

tune-in as we discuss the patriots finalized roster that was announced on wednesday. who stands out and what can we expect from this group? we go around the horn and give our predictions on who will will the super bowl, super bowl mvp and more. plus, a look into the patriots top draft prospects.


















            patriots unfiltered: super bowl week, nfl draft talk, patriots vs. chiefs dynasties
          

tune-in as we discuss all things surrounding the nfl this week, including this sunday's super bowl, league awards and more. plus, evan returns with his observations from the senior bowl and the crew discusses top prospects for the patriots. plus, we discuss the patriots vs. chiefs dynasty debates, including brady vs. mahomes, gronkowski vs. kelce and belichick vs. reid.
