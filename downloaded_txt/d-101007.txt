the best of all worlds
located near pittsburgh and spread across 230 scenic acres, robert morris oﬀers big city opportunities in a safe suburban setting. here, you’ll have the beneﬁts of a big research university with the feel of a small college; experience independence as well as personalized support. it’s a unique and powerful combination that’s earned rmu numerous accolades and rankings. in fact, u.s. news & world report ranks robert morris in the top half of all national universities in its 2022-23 best colleges rankings.

academics at rmu
rmu offers academic excellence with a professional focus. our four schools offer a variety of degrees, majors, and certificates, with courses taught by scholar-experts who have real-world experience. state-of-the-art facilities further enhance your learning, while regional connections give you an edge upon graduation.
