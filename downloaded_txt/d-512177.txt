we deliver sustainable prosperity across ireland



enterprise ireland supported companies employ over 200,000 people around the country. irish-owned businesses play a vital role in our economy, create sustainable jobs and drive prosperity in communities. from start-ups to smes to multinationals, we support irish-owned companies on their journey as they scale, develop capabilities and expand their global footprint.
