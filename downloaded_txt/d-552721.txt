composite
composite fabrication equipment requires superior accuracy, performance, and reliability. we offer a variety of composite manufacturing equipment, such as supply systems, applicators, resin transfer molding, and more. from fabrication to assembly, graco has you covered.









why graco composite equipment?

improving products through composite materials
at graco, your success is our business. that’s why we are committed to providing high-quality equipment for composite fabrication. our goal is to provide our customers products and technical support that together will reduce their overall costs and improve the quality of their products.










all composite products
our equipment covers a wide range of open, closed and manual fabrication processes.
