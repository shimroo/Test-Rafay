your neighborhood gun store — and so much more...




    online store
  














since 1979, shedhorn sports has been elevating your outdoor adventures to the next level. shop top local and national brands like sitka, mystery ranch, christensen arms, kuhl, swarovski, and dozens more — and check out our huge selection of new and used rifles, shotguns, and handguns. come visit us in downtown ennis, montana…














quality you can trustwe sell only the best name brand products in the outdoor industry. if we won't use them, we don't sell them, so you know you’re getting top quality.



here to helpour entire staff lives for outdoor adventure, and we love helping you find just the right products for whatever sport you plan to pursue.



a montana traditionenjoying the outdoors has been a tradition in our family for generations now, and we look forward to sharing that tradition with you and your family.












 

best selection of firearms in montana.new and used hunting rifles, shooting sports rifles, shotguns, handguns — we have a huge selection to browse. because we don’t sell firearms online, if you see something you like, call (406) 682-4210 for more information.

clothing& gear…lots of gear…



shedhorn sports is consistently called “my favorite store” because it’s a one-stop shop. we carry everything you need to do the things you want to do outside… and a lot of the things you want. even though shedhorn is the place you go to get your camping gear, your spinners, your boots, your hunting pants, shedhorn sports is also montana’s best little gun store. come visit us and see why, or give us a call! we’d love to get you fully set-up and outfitted to face the outdoors.
