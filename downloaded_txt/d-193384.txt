recreation complex		





   
 
 




eagle pointe has a number of facilities you probably will not see anywhere else under one roof. start with a state-of-the-art complex, add in some of the best equipment in the health industry and just for good measure, throw in all the sports amenities that any active person would enjoy. we have all the ingredients to fulfill your fitness and recreational goals making the eagle pointe recreation complex an unbeatable option for membership.

an outstanding strength and cardio-vascular area:we have more than 5,500 square feet of the industry's top strength and conditioning equipment. fitness equipment includes recently upgrades, top industry exercise machines with more than 8,000 pounds of free weights and dumbbells ranging from 5 to 125 pounds. and you can take the boredom out of your next cardiovascular workout with our preva theater system! plug in your headset and tune into any one of several different television stations or your choice of music.visit our memberships page for details & to request membership prices!



 
 
 





facility closures & special hours:the facility is closed new year's day, easter, thanksgiving day and christmas day. special hours in effect for new year's eve, july 4th, labor day, memorial day and christmas eve. hours of operation and restaurant hours are subject to change.
