welcome to kampersville














as one of new england's premier camping destinations, lake dunmore kampersville embodies the essence of vermont camping at its finest. immerse yourself in the crystal-clear waters of lake dunmore, where endless opportunities for fishing, boating, and water sports await. explore the surrounding wilderness through scenic hiking trails, or simply unwind amidst the serene beauty of nature. at kampersville, we pride ourselves on offering a seamless blend of relaxation and adventure, making it the ideal getaway for families, couples, and solo travelers alike. come experience the magic of lake dunmore and create memories to last a lifetime.















rejuvenateunwind and reset in nature with family and friends. as you grill, gaze at the ever-bright stars and roast marshmallows by the fireside.















commemorate create lasting memories in the great outdoors, away from technology, with your family. these moments will become cherished experiences that your kids will fondly look back on for the rest of their lives.















celebrate visit kampersville for a vacation filled with celebrations, whether it's an anniversary, birthday, or any special occasion. there's nothing better than escaping the distractions of daily life to focus on what truly matters.
