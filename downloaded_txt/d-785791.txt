public notice: splost open house in st. marys 


camden county residents have another opportunity to attend an upcoming special purpose local option sales tax (splost) open house ahead of the march 18, 2025, referendum.						read on...







camden county receives grant for interns 


the camden county board of commissioners is proud to announce that it has been awarded grant funding through the georgia county internship program, administered by the association of county commissioners of georgia and the accg civic affairs foundation.						read on...







camden county intent to opt out of hb 581 


the camden county board of commissioners intends to opt out of the statewide adjusted base ad valorem homestead exemption established under house bill 581 (hb 581).						read on...
