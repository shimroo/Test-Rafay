scgm 2022the second congress of greek mathematicians unites mathematicians from greece and beyond to share knowledge, collaborate, and strengthen the community.


  
24th jbmo 2020the jbmo 2020 brought together top math students from the balkan region to solve challenging problems. the event aimed to promote math education, and celebrate the achievements of young mathematicians.


  
22th jbmo 2018the junior balkan mathematical olympiad 2018 hosted by hms aimed to promote math education while also fostering cultural exchange through mathematics.


  
fcgm 2018the first congress of greek mathematicians aims to make athens a city of reunion of all greek mathematicians living in greece and abroad.


  
32th bmo 2015the balkan mathematical olympiad 2015 brought together talented math students from across the balkan region to compete, challenging their problem-solving skills and mathematical knowledge.


  
micom 2015the micom-2015, was a global event that brought together mathematicians and experts from various countries to share knowledge and discuss new advancements in the field of mathematics.
