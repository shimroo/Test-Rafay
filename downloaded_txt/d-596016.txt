 2024 master plan









for information about the master plan process, progress, and related activities visit the bohannan huston town of cedaredge master plan site by clicking here. we look forward to working with citizens and stakeholders during this process. the town of cedaredge has completed a draft of the 2024 master plan. the master plan will be used to provide vision and policy direction over the next 20-plus years. interested parties may submit written comments regarding revisions to the cedaredge master plan prior to beginning the public hearing process.  the draft 2024 master plan is available for review at town hall, 235 w. main st, cedaredge, colorado; and on the town of cedaredge web-site. the final draft of the master plan can be found by clicking here.
