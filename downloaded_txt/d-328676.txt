"more than words" art exhibit at springboro performing arts center 


jonathan heart's latest interest includes creating with scrabble tiles! "more than words" with special guest artist clarice moore, also showcases sculpture and painting. meet the artists 12 -2 p.m. saturday, feb. 22.						read on...







free saturday movies! "the lego movie" on feb. 22  "kung fu panda 4" on march 15 


the city of springboro presents three free movies for kids this winter at the springboro performing arts center, 115 wright station way. showtimes are noon and 2 p.m.
“the lego movie”: feb. 22
“kung fu panda 4” march 15						read on...







heart health workshop: 11 a.m. tuesday, feb. 18 


this free workshop will discuss heart failure, its causes, diagnosis, treatment and management. presented by premier community health. 11 a.m. tuesday, feb. 18 at the springboro performing arts center, 115 wright station way.						read on...







your kidneys and you: free workshop, tues., march 18 


this free workshop will discuss what the kidneys do, impact of kidney disease and how to protect and manage your kidney health. presented by national kidney foundation. 11 a.m. tuesday, march 18 at the springboro performing arts center.						read on...







water super hero! 


watch our latest clean water video and keep our drinking water clean!  public education and awareness is an important part of our city's storm water protection program.			watch video...







help us keep our neighborhood roads clear 


winter weather conditions are here! our crews are working to keep the roads safe and clear. you can help expedite snow and ice removal by:
• removing portable basketball hoops from cul-de-sacs 
• parking your car in the driveway						read on...







community wellness program offers great prizes 


“give us 10 or 30” is our citywide wellness program. just walk ten miles or bicycle 30 miles in one month then upload a snapshot of your achievement to our website and you’ll be eligible for terrific prizes!						read on...







treasure hunt bags hidden monthly in city parks 


kiddos are having a ball finding one of the five treasure bags--filled with fun treats and hidden each month in various city parks. clues are posted on the city’s facebook, twitter and instagram pages each month.						read on...







register for codered emergency notifications 


the city of springboro utilizes the onsolve codered emergency alert system as a method to notify our residents and businesses of emergency information. codered reaches most landline phones, but for cell phone alerts, you must register.						read on...







city accepting uplifting messages for gateway sign 


submit a message of hope for our gateway sign at st. rts. 741 and 73.						read on...
