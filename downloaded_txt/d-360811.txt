about us
the mid-atlantic fishery management council is one of eight fishery management councils responsible for the management of marine fisheries in the united states exclusive economic zone. the council develops management plans for fifteen species of fish and shellfish off the coast of the mid-atlantic region. learn more.

mid-atlantic fishery management council


the mid-atlantic fishery management council is one of eight fishery management councils responsible for the management of marine fisheries in the united states exclusive economic zone. the council develops management plans for twelve species of fish and species off the coast of the mid-atlantic region.
