inspired living spacescincinnati modern luxury furniture serving the greater cincinnati area for over 35 years.




    view our work
  














exceptional design.  extraordinary service.voltage, located in the heart of oakley, has over 15,000 square feet of showroom space. for over 30 years, voltage has had a reputation for consistent innovation at the highest level of design. voltage specializes in  european contemporary furniture, lighting, and design. our designers handle residential and commercial projects ranging from full-scale design in new construction homes to assisting clients with lighting or accessory selections.




    view our selection
  






































our team



jeff & julie hinkel opened voltage furniture in 1989.  an architect by trade, jeff saw the need for modern, contemporary, high-end furniture to be represented in the cincinnati metropolitan area.  we have three full-time designers dedicated to both residential and commercial projects.  our goal is to help clients create spaces for them to enjoy now and for years to come. 




    about voltage
  


























our work



we strive to help clients create environments where furniture and architecture complement each other, crafting a setting that is cultured, elegant and unique to their surroundings.  voltage specializes in european, modern furniture, lighting, and outdoor furniture.




    view our projects
  


























our showroom



voltage, located in the heart of oakley, has 15,000 square feet of showroom space over two floors.   we feature indoor furniture settings for living, dining, and bedroom as well as outdoor, and office
