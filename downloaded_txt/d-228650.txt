animal sciences & industrythe k-state asi department serves students, livestock producers and the animal and food industries through teaching, research and education. ksu asi prepares students for careers in the animal and food industries. the curriculum includes the study of nutrition, reproduction, genetics, behavior, meat science and food science with production, management, and agribusiness skills.

one of the leading programs in the country that prepares students for a career in animal sciences.
