creating a welcoming place.local colors is a nonprofit organization that celebrates international diversity and fosters the values of inclusion, equity and multicultural understanding. our events feature performing arts, artisan crafts, cuisine, education, and other elements that display cultural heritages from around the globe. our services break down silos and build a better community.

upcoming events
local colors celebrates different cultural heritages through our annual may festival and other key events. check out our events page to see what’s coming up next!

2025 local colors festival



local colors brings the world to roanoke with our signature event that brings many different cultures together for a day of performances, food, artisan crafts, cultural displays plus the procession of nations and fun interactive kids activities. this family friendly event is free admission. performer and vendor applications are now available.      click the button below to learn more. 




    click here
  



































 

local colors has been championing ethnic diversity for over 30 years.founder pearl fu established the organization in 1991, envisioning a roanoke valley which valued every ethnicity. since then, local colors has grown to be the premier multicultural program in the region. our local colors festival highlights cultures from around the world every third saturday of may, with over 50 nationalities represented. other events throughout the year provide a unique platform for specific cultures to be experienced. local colors champions ethnic diversity that builds a better community for all. we partner with schools, local businesses, community partners, and the international community to break down silos and create harmony.
