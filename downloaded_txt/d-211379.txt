  alright y’all, the cat’s out of the bag! vinnie stigma’s autobiography the most interesting man in the world will be published september 10th and is available for pre-order now on amazon and barnes & noble.

amazon: amzn.to/49sryoz

b&n: bit.ly/stigma_bn

the book covers vinnie’s incredible life and career, from coming up in nyc’s little italy, to the beginning of punk and nyhc, agnostic front, madball, his illustrious wrestling career, world travels and experiences and everything in-between. there’s a comic created by token entry/black train jack’s ernie parada, a cookbook section and a heartfelt foreword by roger miret.
