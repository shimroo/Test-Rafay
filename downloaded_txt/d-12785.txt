native american indian jewelry  vintage dooney & bourke 
horse books by cherry hillwelcome!we 
are passionate about authentic native american indian jewelry, both vintage and 
contemporary. here you will find navajo pearls, vintage navajo, zuni and hopi 
bracelets, squash blossom necklaces, turquoise pendants, inlay rings, zuni and 
navajo fetishes, medicine bags, lakota pipes and other ceremonial objects. we 
also have an extensive vintage dooney & bourke handbag store and horse books by 
cherry hill.have 
fun browsing!
