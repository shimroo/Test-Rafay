   aloha!




    welcome to the waikiki beachside hostel!
 join us, with a hard-working team and guests from around the world, in experiencing the unique local tours and activities all around oahu! we are located one block from waikiki beach on the south shore of oahu, hawaii. centrally located, the beachside hostel is close to diamond head crater, famous waikiki surf breaks, and loads of bars and restaurants. ask our friendly staff, while relaxing amongst the laidback hawaiian vibe, for advice on tours and tips on the cheapest ways to enjoy oahu. we know hawaii can be expensive, so we’re here to help you have an amazing experience while sticking to your budget.there's always something happening at waikiki beachside hostel! from movie nights and karaoke to game nights, wine & cheese socials, and excursions to watch the friday fireworks, our daily events are designed to bring guests together and make your stay unforgettable. and the excitement doesn’t stop there—stay tuned for new events coming soon! join the fun!
