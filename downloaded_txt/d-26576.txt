discover ongoing research



we're one of the main research and development arms of the university of maryland, leading fundamental and applied research to help solve some of the most pressing global challenges. whether its reducing the prevalence of lyme disease, or mitigating saltwater intrusion affecting coastal maryland farms, explore agnr's research portfolio.

make an impact


as students, your research will help provide solutions for the benefit of maryland citizens and beyond. whether it's at home, in your community or in the fields, university of maryland extension faculty and staff offer problem-solving assistance based on the research and output generated within the college. join us and make an impact!
