livesafe app

the livesafe app enables direct and discreet two-way communication with uapd using text, picture, video, and audio.
learn more and download the app.







arizona mobile

navigate campus, see your class schedule, or catch the next cattran. 
learn more and download the app.







public map

the interactive public map shows locations of health & safety resources, parking, and more. health & safety resources include: aed devices and emergency phones. 
explore the map.
