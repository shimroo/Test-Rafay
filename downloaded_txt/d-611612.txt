welcome to daignault realty




2021 – celebrating 50 years of serving the space coast!welcome to your brevard county real estate resource. our goal is to make one of your most important decisions, the purchase or sale of your home a rewarding one. daignault realty, inc. is a family run business that has been in operation in merritt island since 1971. we are the longest in business, top independent local real estate company on the space coast & our collective talent and experience within our team is unparalleled and offers unique benefits to you! from the beginning we have taken pride in watching the space coast grow and now we service all of brevard county. most of our business comes from referrals or repeat customers. we work as a team and measure our success by the satisfaction of our clients.most of our agents and staff have grown up or lived most of their lives here in brevard county and are invested in improving the quality of life here as well as preserving the natural beauty this area is so well known for. with 72 miles of coastline and thousands of waterways we pride ourselves on being waterfront specialists and can offer the best service for those who want to make their home along the waters of merritt island, cocoa, cocoa beach, cape canaveral, titusville, rockledge, viera, sebastian, satellite beach, indian harbor beach, indiatlantic, melbourne beach and melbourne. whether you are new to the area or a seasoned local we want to provide the best service, information and advice regarding brevard county. we partner with local businesses and professionals to assist our clients in all their real estate needs. we hope we can connect with you soon!
