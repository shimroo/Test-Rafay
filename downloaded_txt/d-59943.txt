the american red cross of central florida and the u.s. virgin islands
the american red cross of central florida and the u.s. virgin islands serves 19 florida counties through five chapters: florida’s space coast, greater orlando, mid-florida (winter haven), southwest florida (sarasota) and tampa bay, and the virgin islands of st. thomas, st. croix, st. john and water island.
