winter wonder: yoga & meditation seriestuesdays 6:30pm-7:30pm | east gallery | $15join us for winter wonder, a series for yoga and meditation as we connect yoga, meditation, and art together for a wondrous mindfulness and movement experience. each week, engage in a 30-min yoga flow practice and 15-min meditation. the first week of a five week session will include an introduction to meditation. bring your yoga mat, any props (a blanket, block, or bolster) and a journal and pen if you wish. starting january 21, and continuing january 28, february 4, 11, and 18.

open figure drawing seriesfriday, february 28, saturday, march 8, friday, april 4, tuesday, april 8 | rotunda gallery | $15 cac members or $20 non-membersdevelop your artistic skills with a live model to work from february through april 2025 at the carnegie art center!a live model can offer a view of anatomy and proportion, and help you work on observational skills in a self-directed environment. class will entail a quick warm up, slightly longer short poses, and then a longer pose or poses. draw at your own direction. all levels welcome, including beginners.

carnegie art center 



the purpose of the carnegie art center is to sponsor, promote, and encourage the development of visual and performing arts. the carnegie art center provides educational and cultural enrichment to the western new york region.
