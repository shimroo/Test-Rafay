        admissions
    
dalhousie is one of canada's top 15 research-intensive universities. when applying to dalhousie there are some key things as a student you must know. 




















        ready to apply?
    
you can submit your application as early as october 15 of your grade 12 year. an early offer of admission means you can get a head start on your next steps, like applying for scholarships and residence.


start your application
learn more about how to apply





















        admission requirements
    
applicants must meet dalhousie's general admission requirements, as well as program-specific requirements. 
admission requirements depend on your previous education. review the minimum requirements for your program of choice and learn how you can fulfill them.

        why dal?
    
as a student here, you’ll benefit from studying at one of canada’s research powerhouse universities, while experiencing the exceptional and individualized attention that comes with the dalhousie community. 


explore what sets dal apart


















inclusive excellence




at dalhousie, a commitment to equity, diversity, inclusion, and accessibility is part of everything we do. we aim to attract and support a diverse mix of exceptional learners and encourage you to self-identify when you apply to receive information about academic programming, unique pathways, access options, as well as designated scholarships and bursaries. 




read our equity ethos statement
