since 1972, we have supplied preschool and kindergarten graduation and regalia items to help children and their families celebrate their scholastic accomplishments with a sense of pride while creating memories that last a lifetime at an affordable price. we supply properly fitting caps, sized tassels, and graduation gowns made from high quality materials, plus many other items designed specifically for children.
 rhyme university advantages: 
 - properly sized graduation gowns: our preschool graduation gowns and kindergarten graduation gowns come in 5 sizes and are specially sized for children to ensure a perfect fit for their kindergarten graduation ceremony. 
 - properly sized graduation tassels: preschool graduation tassels and kindergarten graduation tassels are perfectly sized for children. the kindergarten graduation tassels and preschool graduation tassels will be cherished for years to come by students and parents alike. each graduation tassel includes a current year date charm. 
 - quality material: preschool graduation gowns and kindergarten graduation gowns are made from high quality poly-cotton (matte) blend material that makes for beautiful pictures. these graduation gowns can be machine washed and reused for years of enjoyment or stored as timeless keepsakes. 
 - many colors to choose from: with 11 preschool graduation cap & gown colors, 11 kindergarten graduation cap & gown colors and over 20 different colors for preschool graduation tassels and kindergarten graduation tassels to choose from you can bet we have what you’re looking for. 
 - unbeatable delivery: most orders ship the same or next day and arrive within 10 business days. do you need your graduation tassels or graduation cap & gown package even sooner? no problem, online customers can also choose 5 business day express shipping. we also offer 2 business day and next day shipping for stock items. call 1-800-877-472-3738 for details. 
 - low prices: kids cap and gown packages available at low prices. click here to find out more about these preschool graduation cap and gown and kindergarten graduation cap and gown packages 
 
 whether you’re planning a head start graduation, kindergarten graduation, preschool graduation or any other commencement ceremony for children, we have the graduation / regalia supplies you need. in other words we are your one stop graduation source. we’ve maintained ourselves as an industry leader over the last three and a half decades by listening and tailoring our preschool graduation caps & gowns and kindergarten graduation caps and gowns to the feedback of tens of thousands of directors, educators, school administrators, parents, and photographers. our preschool graduation tassels and kindergarten graduation tassels complete any graduation cap & gown package and provide a timeless keepsake. remember if you need anything for a preschool graduation or kindergarten graduation we are the best source of graduation products.
