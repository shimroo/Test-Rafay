the resort on eagle mountain lake 









 





live a resort lifestyle 



the resort on eagle mountain lake is a 540-acre master-planned neighborhood, unlike anything you have ever imagined in the dallas-fort worth area. from its unique architecture, master-planned design and lush landscaping to the many gorgeous homes and amenities, we are confident you will find yourself enchanted by this community. come to play and stay to live!
