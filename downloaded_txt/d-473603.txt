overview








know your data, empower your people, and drive your future. opentext™ portfolio digital workplace solutions empower organizations to deliver the right content to the right people at the right time, on any device. establish flexible, smarter, and more collaborative virtual work environments while maintaining data security, governance, and regulatory compliance, whether virtual, physical, or off-cloud.












what can opentext portfolio solutions do for you?




back up and recover enterprise data 
reduce risk with ransomware protection, predictive analytics, automation, orchestration, and reporting across systems, and workloads for compliance and data retention.



secure endpoints via a unified solution
protect against device theft and software vulnerabilities with end-to-end security, visibility, and management of endpoints, organizational data, and mobile devices.



streamline hybrid work and team collaboration
implement a single solution that provides secure, seamless email and team collaboration, archiving, ediscovery, file sync, and share data compliance.



manage documents and records in compliance
comply with government regulatory data privacy requirements while serving the right content to the right people at the right time via any device.











why opentext portfolio digital workplace solutions?
empower optimized hybrid work at enterprise scale with unmatched messaging, collaboration, archiving, ediscovery, data backup, and document management content integration with comprehensive governance.





deep document management and archiving
equip government agencies and regulated industries manage their business content from creation to disposal.




secure data backup and fast recovery
protect all organizational data across legacy platforms and modern virtual environments with compliance and security controls.




hybrid deployment flexibility
deploy in the cloud, on-premises, or hybrid with enterprise-grade scalability and seamless integration within the opentext ecosystem.




















featured products




gain a competitive advantage and prepare for the future of work. opentext portfolio brings together a set of digital workplace solutions that can be used in any combination to improve end user productivity, empower mobile work and protect the organization.
