who are we?






our mission

at the american international school vienna, we empower each student with the skills and character strengths necessary to be positively impactful global citizens who strive for personal growth and excellence in an ever-changing world.







our vision

a community of innovative learners realizing their chosen futures with courage, curiosity, and joy.







our values

respect: we will be kind, considerate, inclusive, and thoughtful in our interactions with others and the environment. aspire: we will pursue our passions, imagine possibilities, and make them real. achieve: we will apply ourselves to the best of our ability with effort and ingenuity, and strive for personal growth.
