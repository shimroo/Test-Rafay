front runners new york (frny) is a running club for people who identify as lesbian, gay, bisexual, transgender, queer (lgbtq+) and those who are allies. identifying as lgbtq+ is not a requirement to run with frny. we welcome runners of all speeds, ages, and abilities.




    learn more about frny
  














runs, workouts, and activitiesif you are interested in learning more about frny, we recommend joining any of our weekly fun runs, which offer a wide variety of paces and distances, plus an opportunity to socialize and meet our fantastic club members.
