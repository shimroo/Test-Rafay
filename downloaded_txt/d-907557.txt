mayor's newsletter (january 24 - february 9, 2025) 


the city held a groundbreaking event for the new public safety annex. the largest pavilion in the city has been constructed at sunshine oaks park. these are just a few things accomplished in the city of madison! learn more in mayor finley's newsletter.						read on...







pdga amateur disc golf world championships to be hosted locally 


pdga amateur disc golf world championships takes over madison county in 2027. huntsville, madison, madison county courses set to host the world’s best.						read on...






city of madison fire chief david bailey announces relocation to virginia 


the city of madison announces fire chief david bailey has accepted a new fire chief position in virginia.						read on...






madison’s civic awareness academy is accepting applications for spring 2025 


the city of madison has launched the civic awareness academy to inspire positive change in madison through community engagement and involvement. apply today!						read on...






2025 street sweeping program 


beginning january 15th, street sweeping will begin on all curb and gutter streets in the city of madison.						read on...







city of madison embraces drone technology for public safety and city operations 


the city of madison utilizes drone technology for police and fire operations, as well as for other city initiatives. find resources on how the city manages our drone program along with helpful links for flying safely as a hobbyist within city limits.						read on...






city council approves mobile application for parks and recreation 


the madison parks and recreation app is a fully responsive community portal, that will launch soon.						read on...
