rebuilding neighborhoodsone block at a time




    learn more
  














lani partners with residents, businesses, community groups and local leaders to create walkable, green, safe neighborhoods with equitable access to resources, open spaces, public transit, and economic opportunity.
























the lani model
lani promotes community members as the experts on their own neighborhoods, and implements improvement projects from concept to completion.



read more





























lani projects
lani specializes in project management and community outreach and engagement. we bring together stakeholders and partners to successfully deliver completed projects.
