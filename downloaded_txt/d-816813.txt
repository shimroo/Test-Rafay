taleo enterprise edition
configurable hiring
tailor the solution to your unique organizational needs to help you quickly get the talent you require at scale.
complete sourcing
leverage branded career sites, marketing campaigns, automated postings, and referral features to attract candidates and grow talent pools.
recruiting acceleration
simplify hiring for recruiting teams with tools for candidate screening, interview management, offer creation, and onboarding.
analytics and reporting
make smart decisions and share hiring progress with data on sourcing processes, talent assessments, candidate communication, and onboarding.
easy-to-use experiences
provide recruiters and candidates with a modern, intuitive experience—built for desktop, tablet, and mobile devices.
certified partner ecosystem
gain access to oracle’s extensive partner network, featuring more than 200 taleo-certified offerings and innovative integrations with linkedin.

















taleo business edition
multichannel sourcing
leverage job boards, social media sites,  and employee referrals to discover the right talent.
tailorable recruiting tools
easily customize candidate selection processes, interviews, and recruiter workflows to meet the unique needs of your business.
smart automation features
automate common recruiting processes, such as communications and task assignment, streamlining recruiter productivity.
analytics dashboards
keep teams informed on key recruiting kpis, such as candidate quality, time to hire, and talent costs.
branded onboarding
gain faster time to productivity and reduce paperwork with an onboarding experience that excites new employees and reflects your company’s culture.

















taleo business edition talent management
goals and assessments 
support employee performance management with streamlined goal creation, automated reviews, and career development plans.
workforce insight
evaluate talent, identify successors, and track the progress of your workforce with one location for viewing employee data.
continuous employee feedback
modernize performance management and nurture talent with ongoing conversations, coaching, and mentoring.
