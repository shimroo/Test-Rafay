about our clubeast coast gassers is dedicated to the preservation and racing of cars, both restored and newly constructed, that were campaigned during the glory days of the “gasser wars.” established in october 2001, our membership has continued to grow over the years into an organization that includes all types of period-correct race cars. check out our member cars page to see some of the old-school-cool gassers that race with our club!
