        welcome to the artificial plants shop  	  

welcome to the artificial plants shop the home of realistic, authentic and life like artificial flowers & plants, artificial trees, living walls and more! the artificial plants shop began its online success back in 2006. since then our range has expanded but our values have stayed the same, we supply only the best quality artificial plants. we know there are cheaper alternatives online but the quality is non-comparable. we back up our high end products with exceptional customer service. it is a simple and efficient service to order online but our customer care team are available to help and answer questions when needed!
whether your requirement is for one small succulent for a bathroom or you have a hotel that requires a total makeover with its planting, our team is here to help. installation and delivery by our in house team is also available alongside our courier drop services. there is also our bespoke team here to bring your ideas to life, from 9m gold artificial apple trees to 30 meters of boxwood hedging with branded troughs - anything is possible!
