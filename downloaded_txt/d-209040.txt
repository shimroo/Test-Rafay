christ church exists to show everyone god loves them unconditionally.




























experienceexperience the outstretched arms of god’s unconditional love through our worship, music, and other opportunities.



embracefeel the saving embrace of god and let god’s love abide in you as part of a community in our formation offerings for all.



extendlove in truth and action by extending our hands in love to care for those both in and outside our walls.














learn about christ churcha church is not a building, it is a community. for 190 years, a community of people have gathered together under the name christ church parish. or to put it another way, christ church is a group of people doing something old in new and exciting ways. no matter who you are, what you believe, or what you have been through, you are invited to be a part of this community.
