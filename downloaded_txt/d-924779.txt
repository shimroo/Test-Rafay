tracing our ancestry project



we’re working with the museum of new zealand te papa tongarewa to identify the portraits of our forefathers and foremothers who had their portraits taken in the 1930s.   




    learn more
  


























this is a 10 week course. classes start on the 28th april at 11am every sunday for 1 hour during school term with a mixture of bharatanatyam dance training, breathing exercises and fitness.classes open to all ages, gender and ethnicity.everyone is welcome to give it a go. you will not be judged and best of all, no previous dance experience required, come and have some fun!$4 per session or $20 for the term.
