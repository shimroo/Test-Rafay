welcome to aerialcinematography.com, the ultimate destination for exceptional aerial cinematography and unparalleled creative vision. at aerialcinematography.com, we transform the ordinary into the extraordinary, using state-of-the-art technology and decades of expertise to create breathtaking imagery that captivates, inspires, and tells compelling stories. whether you need sweeping landscapes, intricate urban scenes, or dynamic action shots, we deliver visuals that elevate your project to new heights.our missionour mission is simple: to provide world-class aerial cinematography that exceeds our clients' expectations. we aim to bring your vision to life, offering innovative solutions and artistic excellence to create unforgettable cinematic experiences. we believe that every frame has the power to evoke emotion, and we strive to harness that power in everything we do.our historyfounded in 1994 by thomas c. miller, blue sky aerials predates the modern drone technology we know today. with over 30 years of experience mounting motion picture cameras in helicopters, thomas brought a pioneering spirit to the industry. an emmy award-winning aerial cinematographer with more than 5,000 flight hours, thomas has captured stunning visuals for iconic projects such as aerial america, secrets of whales, and america's national parks. blue sky aerials was built on a foundation of passion, precision, and a commitment to excellence.to showcase the beauty and artistry of aerial cinematography, thomas launched aerialcinematography.com as a dedicated platform. this site serves as a visual portfolio, demonstrating the quality and creativity that clients can expect when working with blue sky aerials.our expertisewith a deep understanding of the technical and artistic aspects of aerial cinematography, blue sky aerials specializes in:helicopter cinematography: leveraging traditional platforms like the bell 206 jet ranger and airbus h125 to capture immersive, dynamic shots.drone cinematography: utilizing cutting-edge drones, including the dji mavic 3 pro cine, to achieve precise and versatile angles.custom camera mounts: designing and installing custom mounts for unique shooting requirements, ensuring the highest quality footage.multi-environment filming: from rugged wilderness to bustling cities, our team excels in adapting to any environment to capture the perfect shot.our servicesat blue sky aerials, we offer a comprehensive range of services to meet the diverse needs of our clients:1. aerial cinematographyour core service, aerial cinematography, is tailored
