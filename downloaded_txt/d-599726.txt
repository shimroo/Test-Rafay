woodinville’s boutique gymnastics center



at northshore gymnastics it is our passion to educate, inspire, and serve all who seek to empower themselves through the sport of gymnastics.







































our missionat ngc we aim to inspire and empower individuals of all ages to achieve their full potential through the sport of gymnastics. we are dedicated to providing a safe, supportive, and inclusive environment where athletes can develop discipline, perseverance, dedication, and confidence. we strive to cultivate a lifelong love for gymnastics, foster a sense of teamwork and sportsmanship, and nurture the dreams and aspirations of every gymnast who walks through our doors. we are passionate about helping our athletes reach new heights, both on and off the mat, as they learn valuable life skills. together, we create a vibrant gymnastics community that celebrates individual achievements, encourages personal growth, and builds a foundation for success that extends far beyond the gym.
