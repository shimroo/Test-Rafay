check out st. helena island!!


 welcome to the beautiful south carolina lowcountry!




fripp island. harbor island. st. helena island. beaufort. bluffton. hilton head.
now may just be the perfect time for you to come, visit and explore the wonderful lowcountry of south carolina. relax and enjoy the ocean, marshes, creeks and waterways of our area and savor the enchantment for yourself. hunting island state park, the most popular such park in south carolina, offers solitude and an idea of what these beaches and forests were like many years ago. then visit gated fripp and harbor islands for peaceful vacations and smooth sandy beaches, golf and tennis. explore st. helena island to see historic penn center and fort fremont along with large farms and spacious tracts of land if you prefer to be a gentleman farmer. beaufort is awash in history and was the site for the filming of a number of popular movies including prince of tides, the big chill and forrest gump. take a horsedrawn carriage tour of the historic district and marvel at the size and magnificence of homes built many years ago. bluffton is a short drive away and includes the 5500 acre sun city, perfect for retirees with its miles of bike paths and shopping, medical facilities and recreation, all accessible by golf cart. and then there is hilton head island, with shopping, entertainment and many golf courses including harbor town golf links, home of the rbc heritage tournament. savannah georgia is a short drive away and offers a glimpse of the old south with spanish moss-draped live oaks, iconic squares and fine restaurants and entertainment along the savannah river. charleston is a 2-hour drive and makes a great day trip destination to take in the history and beauty of the south as it was many years ago. these are some of the attractions but they don't completely convey the lifestyle that we here have come to love and enjoy....you need to see it to comprehend it. there is more, much more and we're here to help you discover it so call, email or text us and we'll be off and running together!
