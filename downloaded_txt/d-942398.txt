why jemmco?
jemmco is a leading supplier to the extrusion and web converting industries. our products, jemmtron™ corona treater roll coverings, jemmtac™ web cleaning roller coverings, jemmtrac™ traction roller coverings and jemmsil™ release roller coverings have put us in the forefront of roller covering technology. our line of dyne test products contain the largest variety, including accu-flo™ felt tip dyne pens, accu-flo™ jumbo dyne pens and ez-read™ poly test dyne pens, which enables us to meet the wide range of applications of our customers. our jemmstat™ line of static elimination devices, tapes and roller wraps, cleaning products, bag machine parts and supplies, laboratory equipment, etc, makes jemmco the leading supplier for film extruders and material converters.
