welcome to foust

welcome to foust textiles! whether you've purchased our fabric before, or are considering buying your first bolt, we're happy to have you visiting our website. we value each of our new and returning customers and want to provide the best product and customer service we can. for over 55 years, our goal has been to provide quality products with quality service!
our warehouse is located in kings mountain, nc  and is openmonday through friday from 8 am to 5 pm. all buyers (online & walk-ins) must have a business license or tax id # to shop!
reach out to us by calling 1-800-258-9816
or by emailing customerservice@fousttextiles.com. 
our customer service team will be happy to serve you! as always, thank you for shopping with foust textiles!
