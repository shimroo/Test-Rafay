about us
we have been doing business in pakistan since 1967. today, our locally incorporated subsidiary in karachi continues to support all business related to commercial & investment bank (cib) activities with a strategic focus towards servicing the wholesale payments needs of the local financial institutions and public sector clients. our dedicated sales coverage and client service team based in karachi continues to be a key component of our service offering.
j.p. morgan is a global leader in financial services, offering solutions to the world's most important corporations, governments and institutions in more than 100 countries.
