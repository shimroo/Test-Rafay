city council ends association with former texas rangers foundation 


the fredericksburg city council unanimously agreed to end its 14-year relationship with the former texas rangers foundation (ftrf) during its regular meeting on tuesday, feb. 4.						read on...







city to close in observance of presidents' day 


city of fredericksburg buildings, facilities and offices will be closed for presidents’ day, monday, feb. 17.						read on...







williamson named interim director of development services 


the city of fredericksburg has appointed assistant city engineer evan williamson as the interim director of development services.						read on...







fire/ems launches whole blood program 


the fredericksburg fire/ems department recently launched a whole blood program, an initiative designed to enhance emergency medical services and improve patient outcomes in gillespie county.						read on...







coffee with the city manager event scheduled for february 12 


the february edition of coffee with the city manager will be held on wednesday, feb. 12, from 8-9 a.m. at lady bird johnson golf course.						read on...







city council distributes 2025 hot grants 


the fredericksburg city council granted $519,975 of hotel occupancy tax (hot) funds to 27 applicants at its meeting on wednesday, jan. 22. the regular meeting was rescheduled from tuesday to wednesday due to winter weather in the texas hill country.						read on...







txdot slated to make traffic modifications 


the city of fredericksburg has been notified by the texas department of transportation (txdot) that it is expecting its contractors to start making signal and traffic pattern modifications along main street on sunday, nov. 10.						read on...







city enhances employee benefits with betterhelp partnership 


the city of fredericksburg recently partnered with betterhelp, the leading online therapy platform, to provide its employees with support for their mental wellbeing.						read on...







tax rate information

city welcomes two in finance department 


the city of fredericksburg recently hired two finance department employees - betzayda hernandez as a utility billing analyst/customer service representative and vanessa wainwright as an administrative accounting assistant.						read on...







development services hosts builders for lunch & learn 


the city of fredericksburg development services department welcomed nearly 20 staff members from 12 companies for a lunch & learn event on wednesday, aug. 28.						read on...







city completes purchase of hcuc facility 


the city of fredericksburg, represented by mayor jeryl hoover and city manager clinton bailey, signed an amendment to the ground lease agreement and a contract of sale with the texas hill country higher education development foundation friday.						read on...







lady bird johnson campground rolls back rates 


the city of fredericksburg recently lowered the daily rate for rv space rentals at lady bird johnson municipal campground.						read on...







cross mountain park set for entrance, sidewalk upgrades 


cross mountain park will soon have a new entrance and better sidewalk connectivity, after the city of fredericksburg received a donation from the nimitz rotary club.						read on...







promotions in finance, hiring in development services 


the city of fredericksburg recently promoted bridget flint to finance manager in the finance department and karen kirchner moved to utility billing analyst in the finance department, while hiring jeanette sanchez as the new building services coordinator.						read on...







txdot slated to begin main street repaving project 


the texas department of transportation (txdot) is scheduled to begin a repaving project of u.s. highway 290, known as main street in fredericksburg, this week.						read on...







repainting of maibaum underway 


the city of fredericksburg and the pedernales creative arts alliance (pcaa) started work this week on the repainting of the maibaum at marktplatz.						read on...







finance department recognized for budget 


for an eighth straight year, the city of fredericksburg has been honored by the government finance officers association with the gfoa's distinguished budget presentation award.						read on...







street department starts 2024 paving project 


the city of fredericksburg’s 2024 annual paving project kicked off on tuesday, july 9, and is slated to be completed over the course of the next three months.						read on...







city debuts new open records request process 


the city of fredericksburg has partnered with justfoia to streamline its public information and open records request process.						read on...







city hosts short-term rental owner meeting 


the city of fredericksburg’s code enforcement and development services department hosted a meeting for short-term rental (str) owners and operators on tuesday evening at the gillespie county law enforcement center.						read on...







city council appoints board and commission members 


the fredericksburg city council made 10 selections for a trio of boards and commissions on tuesday during its regularly scheduled meeting at the gillespie county law enforcement center.						read on...







vacek welcomed as city secretary 


the city of fredericksburg has hired leticia vacek as city secretary. she started her duties on friday, june 14, and brings nearly 35 years of municipal government experience to the role.						read on...







emergency registry now available for gillespie county 


the city of fredericksburg and gillespie county are now registered to use the state of texas emergency assistance registry (stear) program.						read on...







i.t. staff updates announced 


the city of fredericksburg recently promoted brandon gold to assistant director and hired keith jenkins as network administrator in its information technology department.						read on...







city council awards 2024 hot grants 


the fredericksburg city council granted $458,982 of hotel occupancy tax (hot) funds to 25 applicants at its regularly scheduled meeting on tuesday.						read on...







promotions in development services, electric departments 


the city of fredericksburg recently promoted tyler baethge (str specialist/development services), jan musgrove (planner 1/development services) and kyle treibs (assistant superintendent/electric).						read on...







city council okays fort martin scott operating agreement 


the fredericksburg city council voted unanimously to approve an operating agreement for fort martin scott between the texas historical commission (thc) and the city of fredericksburg at its regularly scheduled meeting on tuesday.						read on...







city adds three electric car charging stations 


the city of fredericksburg in partnership with fredericksburg shines and gillespie county has recently completed the construction of three 100-amp electric vehicle charging stations at 108 w. san antonio st.						read on...







city council adopts comprehensive plan & parks master plan 


the fredericksburg city council adopted the 2024 comprehensive plan: gute stadt (“good city”) and approved the 2024 parks master plan: gutes leben (“good life”).						read on...







building & str permits online 


the city of fredericksburg has partnered with mygovernmentonline (mgo) to offer an online customer portal for all building and str permits.						learn more...
