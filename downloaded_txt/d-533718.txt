        welcome to thompson, ct      

welcome to the town of thompson’s website. we have a strong commitment to providing information and are looking forward to your inquiries and visits. thompson is a unique town located in the northeast corner of connecticut bordering massachusetts and rhode island. in thompson, you will find many different villages with a variety of housing. also, you will find a unique rural character with a beautiful country setting. learn more
