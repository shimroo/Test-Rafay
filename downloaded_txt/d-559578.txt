deridder mardi gras events set march 1 


events will take place at the beauregard parish fairgrounds starting at 9 a.m. the parade starts at 4 p.m.						read on...







bill iles exhibit on display at realart deridder gallery 


iles, who grew up in beauregard parish and attended area schools, has over five decades of experience exhibiting his artwork locally, regionally and nationally.						read on...







disney artist keith donaldson and silhouette portraits coming to the beauregard museum 


appointments are required to secure a spot, and limited appointments are available.						read on...







unveiling set for 'deridder commercial historic district' marker 


deridder main street and the city of deridder applied for and received a $1,950 grant from the william g. pomeroy foundation to place a national register of historic places marker for the district.						read on...







tnc louisiana treesilience plants 25 trees in deridder's west park 


west park, deridder’s largest community park, lost 300 trees. but that was just the start.						read on...







beauregard parish community guide 


take a closer look at the beauregard parish community!			additional info...







safewise ranks deridder as sixth safest city in louisiana for 2024 


deridder was the sixth safest city in louisiana in 2024, according “safewise,” a home security company that contextualizes national crime data and safety trends.						read on...







"city space" now available for non-profits, community partners 


"city space" is a classroom with desks, tables or chairs - whatever the need. the room is free to use, but must be reserved.						read on...
