annual passing of the sash, sun. feb 23rd, 1-4pm 


the greater patchogue foundation celebrates the passing of the sash honoring st. patrick's day grand marshall for 2025, patchogue village trustee joe keyes on sunday, february 23rd at james joyce pub & restaurant from 1:00 to 4:00 p.m. all are welcome!			






corned beef & cabbage luncheon, friday march 14th 12-2 pm 


come join us for a fun-filled afternoon at the patchogue recreation center, 380 bay ave. $15 includes lunch, dessert and a craft. bring $3 cash or $3 of scratch-offs for bingo.  reserve by calling (631) 475-4302.  payment is due march 7th.
