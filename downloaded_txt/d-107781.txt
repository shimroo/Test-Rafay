our story







established in 1978 by rex and marilyn jackson, jackson realty has a true, deep-rooted passion for serving the people of beautiful northwest georgia and tennessee. when helping our customers buy or sell real estate, we strive to make the process as easy, enjoyable, and rewarding as possible. our goal is to build core relationships with our customers that will last for many generations to come. with 47+ years and three generations of experience in our market, we continue to provide our customers with the top-notch service and results they expect! we are proud to call northwest georgia home!












































our team






with over 47 years of experience in the northwest georgia/chattanooga market, our team of realtors are trained, knowledgeable, and ready to help you with any of your real estate needs! voted #1 best of the best residential real estate company in 2022 and 2023, we specialize in residential, commercial, and land sales. whether you are buying or selling, our trusted team is committed to thoroughly understanding your needs and achieving the most favorable terms for you.
