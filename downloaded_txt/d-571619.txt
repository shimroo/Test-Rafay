thank you for visiting our site. itsenclosures has a storied history of providing durable enclosure solutions for various sectors, including food & beverage, pharmaceutical, transit, and industrial. we’ve built a reputation for overcoming customer challenges (water, dirt, dust, chemicals, extreme temperatures), which can be a critical issue for electronic equipment stored in harsh environments.
our product lines include our best-selling industrial enclosures (like the icestation titan, dual monitor hammerhead, and the pb20-12 printer enclosure) and the strongest grade enclosures (12 & 14 gauge steel, stainless steel) on the market. they cover a wide range of needs for protecting technology investments, like computers and printers. itsenclosures uses heavy duty latches, windows, and hinges, and offers a variety of thermal management options, like fans, heat exchangers, and air conditioners.
we are proud to be ‘made in the usa!’ for more information, email info@itsenclosures.com, click on our live chat, or call 1-800-423-9911.
we are always happy to provide a no obligation quote!
