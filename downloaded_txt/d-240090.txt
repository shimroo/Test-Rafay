placentia spotlights










spring 2025 cert training 


the city is excited to announce our spring 2025 community emergency response team (cert) training. spring 2025 cert training dates are planned for tuesday nights in february and march.						read on...







sign up for the city's weekly e-newsletter 


the city sends out a weekly e-newsletter to inform the community about upcoming city events, news, information, and more.			click here to sign up







solicitors and peddlers 


did you know? solicitors, peddlers, and canvassers must first obtain a permit from the city of placentia before going door-to-door.						read on...







resident vehicle rebate program 


placentia residents purchasing or leasing a new vehicle from fairway ford or audi north oc are eligible to receive a $500 rebate with proof of residency. vehicle price must exceed $25,000 or more to qualify. visit fairway ford or audi for more information						read on...







shredding & e-waste recycling event 


the city's next free shredding & e-waste recycling event will be on saturday, march 15th, from 8:00 a.m. to 11:00 a.m. at the placentia civic center, 401 east chapman avenue.						read on...
