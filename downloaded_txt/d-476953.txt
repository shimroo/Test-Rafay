about us 







we take great satisfaction in providing elegance with a personal touch. in order to maintain our commitment to quality and service, we are continually adding new items to enable us to better serve your party needs.in 1980, bob costa started b. c. tent & awning company inc. with one tent in his father’s garage under the name boston party rentals. the company has expanded significantly since then and now manages over one half million square feet of tents. we have party rental equipment, rental tents, wedding tent rentals, and awnings.
