a-320/ 787 adjustable aft accessory access stand #65195

to access the aft accessory door the sliding ladder shifts to the left of the deck.
to access the aft accessory area the sliding ladder shifts to the right.
aft-accessory access stands manufactured by waag feature our signature design of heavy wall 1.5″ round steel tubing mounted to stair rail in two places at the lower end. as a result, this design avoids potential damage and creates a very sturdy unit both in up and down and side to side action. powder-coated with waag high visibility orange and a second powder coat of uv resistant clear. alternatively, other colors are available upon request.

waag aviation ground support equipment is designed and manufactured right here in the u.s.a.
we build world-class aircraft ground support equipment that is built to handle the rigors of everyday abuse on the tarmac.
many of our heavy-duty aviation gse platforms and stairs are designed to be operated by a single member of your ground support team.
each piece of aircraft aviation ground support equipment is built with large wheels for quick towing, kpl heavy-duty swivel wheels for easy maneuvering, non-slip grip strut (saw tooth design) platforms and stairs, single action plunge brakes and comes in the durable powder coat finish of your choice.
