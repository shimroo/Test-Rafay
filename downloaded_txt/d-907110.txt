north florida livestock agents group










 the north florida livestock agents group (nflag) consists of 14 uf/ifas extension agents from counties in north east florida. the primary goals are to provide extension educational programming for livestock and forage producers which will increase profitability and sustainability.
sign up to receive program announcements here: http://bit.ly/nflagsignup
