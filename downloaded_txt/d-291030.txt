about ramwelcome to the realtors® association of maui 
established in 1968, the realtors® association of maui is a professional trade organization serving over 2,000 realtor® members and affiliate partners. we provide programs and services to enable our members to successfully conduct their real estate business with integrity and competence, and work to preserve homeownership rights.
