about lpc



                why choose lseg lpc?




with first-to-the-market loan pricing news and comprehensive real-time and historic data, lseg lpc will keep you on top of markets trends for effective business decisions.
from offices in new york, london, hong kong, singapore, sydney and tokyo, our publications, online news, analysis, valuation services and interactive databases will help drive valuation, syndication, trading and research and portfolio management activities.
we are the one source for comprehensive coverage of the syndicated loan markets worldwide.














features & benefits



                what you get with lpc


















first to the market news, data and analysis



powerful and intuitive, lpc products provide the loan market with breaking news, comprehensive data and expert analysis.














loan market focused expertise



lpc reporters and analysts are completely focused on the global syndicated loan market.














unmatched market presence



lpc has been serving the global syndicated loan market for more than 25 years.














innovative products, multiple formats



lpc platforms and print publications cover investment grade, leveraged lending, the middle market, clos and bdcs and more.
