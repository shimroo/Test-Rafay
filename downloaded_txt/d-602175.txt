job posting - director of public works 

the city of ionia is accepting applications for a full-time director of public works, as the current director will retire this year. submit your application today to join our team!				read on...




2025 board of review notice 

the city of ionia board of review will meet on tuesday, march 4 for an organizational meeting and for appeals on monday, march 10 and tuesday, march 11. contact the city assessor to schedule an appointment.				read on...




public hearing & special meeting notice - city council 

city council will hold a special meeting at 5:30 pm, wednesday, march 5, 2025, at city hall prior to the regular city council meeting. a public hearing will be held during this meeting on the proposed six-year capital improvement plan.				read on...








public hearing notice - city council
