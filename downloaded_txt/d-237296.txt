mayor hector c. loramayor’s office
 






  
 
 














hector c. lorahector c. lora has been elected in his hometown of passaic, nj as a councilman in 2011, and has been elected twice as a passaic county freeholder now referred to as county commissioners. in 2015 hector became the first american latino of dominican heritage to become the director of the passaic county board of chosen freeholders.
