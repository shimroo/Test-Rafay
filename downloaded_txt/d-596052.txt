about this site all of my jewellery is lovingly made by me, here on the beautiful kapiti coast, new zealand.
my goal is to make unique and wearable jewellery, that you will love for many years to come.
you may see many different styles and colours, from simple to ornate, casual to elegant.
i hope you are able to find joy in my creations and find the perfect gift, or treat yourself to something special.
i hope you enjoy wearing my pieces as much as i have enjoyed creating them.
a lot of my work is one-off and unique. i can do matching sets for special events. i’m happy to make custom orders. please email me to discuss your ideas.
thanks for looking, and if you have any questions please e-mail me.
