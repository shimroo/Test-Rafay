                tap into the world’s most comprehensive economic & financial time series database




as a financial professional – whether you are working in investment management, trading and banking, central banking, government agencies or corporate treasury – you need the right data and tools to build economic analyses, create market scenarios, test investment strategies and trading ideas and research countries, regions and industries. 
datastream provides the data and analytical tools you need to keep abreast of unfolding macroeconomic events, identify global trends, generate and test hypotheses, develop viewpoints and create research.  
leverage the world’s largest repository of economic and financial data, cutting-edge charting and analytical tools to execute your team’s workflows faster and with greater accuracy: 

track market moving, headline economic data releases in real-time – our economic monitor delivers comprehensive, real-time economic data to help you visualise the impact of events on markets.
analyse and compare domestic economies using standardised economic data for 51 key economic indicators across 130 countries.
create proprietary indicators using our analytical functions and share with your team across the globe.
create and easily update insightful, on-brand charts for powerful data visualisations using features such as correlation, slicing, recession bands and interactive annotations.
create a library of dynamic charts and share with colleagues and customers via the microsoft office suite.

unique content includes lseg ibes global aggregates, worldscope fundamentals, lseg/ipsos primary consumer sentiment indices, lseg comparable economics, world-check’s country risk rankings, lipper fund data and beyond ratings. further unique data and insight available from fathom consulting.
we offer our data and tools via flexible access points to cater for your unique needs: access via lseg workspace, bulk data feed or api.
a secure bulk feed is available for populating data lakes with static and time series data. a flexible api supporting file types adhering to market standard is available for use in customer applications or for third-party applications such as python, r, eviews and matlab.

features & benefits



                what datastream offers you


















automation



leverage advanced automation as well as powerful charting software and analytical tools for greater impact and efficiency.
















comprehensive coverage



tap into unmatched depth and breadth of data with global coverage, including exclusive, differentiated and specialist data. we offer historical and point-in-time data, so you can explore the evolution of data points.
















convenience



benefit from comparable, standardised data that allows like-for-like analysis – and take advantage of the new features and data sets that we continually introduce. 
















flexible access



enjoy flexible delivery via excel, desktop, bulk feed and api: our data is accessible whenever and wherever you need it on a 24/7 basis.















product in action



                datastream in action












for trading






















receive real-time market signals that can impact asset values, and derive quantitative signals about potential market moves. research programmatic trading model ideas, and back-test models using historic tick data.






















for compliance and risk






















use intraday/end-of-day economic data to identify abnormal market movements and price volatility. mark-to-market your portfolios and settle trades efficiently.






















for index management and structured products






















update indices with intraday/end-of-day data, and update macro forecast data in structured products, while leveraging reuters poll consensus to influence long/short positions.






















for research, economic modelling, and algo creation and back testing






















create macroeconomic models explaining domestic, regional and global economies. establish relationships between asset price movements.
