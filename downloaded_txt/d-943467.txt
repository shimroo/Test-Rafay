welcome to st. peter’s episcopal church!we would love for you to join us in worship at one of our services, or reach out to us herest. peter’s welcomes all, including infants and children, and is ada accessible.sundays at 10 am holy eucharist followed by coffee hourwednesdays at 6:30 pmevening servicest. peter’s is located at 617 west belmont avenue in the east lakeview neighborhood of chicago. there is both free and paid parking in the area and we are a short walk from the belmont el stop (brown, purple, red lines), and several cta bus routes (8, 22, 36, 77, 146, 151, and 156).














where to find us


   



we are located near of the corner of broadway and belmont in the lakeview east neighborhood of chicago.we serve the lakeview east and lgbtqia+ communities but our parishioners come from all over the chicagoland area. st. peter’s episcopal church617 w belmont avechicago, il 60657
