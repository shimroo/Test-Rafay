2025 memberships

















carved out of the new england countryside, cyprian keyes offers an 18-hole championship course and a 9-hole par-3 course. our beautiful colonial-style clubhouse offers first class amenities for golfers and guests in a relaxed, comfortable atmosphere. offering some of the best golf and views in the worcester area, the course and surrounding hills can be seen from the dining room and outdoor patios. guests can enjoy a casual, relaxing meal at cyprian’s or book a private party in any of our four function rooms.
