carolina canners, inc. (cci) is an independent pepsi bottler and production cooperative, based in cheraw, sc.we have a rich history in the beverage industry in the carolinas dating back to 1963. in addition to our facility in cheraw, we are proud to include various subsidiaries, along with three selling and distributions centers. today, cci currently employees approximately 600 people and distributes 28 million cases of drinks per year throughout the carolinas and the southeast.




    learn more
  




























shareholderscci is a company rooted in family business. generations of family-owned independent pepsi bottlers are at the foundation of the business.















bottling partnerscci supplies many neighboring bottlers with soft drinks from its modern production center located in cheraw, sc.















industry partnerswith our high-speed production lines, we offer packaging needs for a wide range of can sizes and packaging configurations to many industry partners.
