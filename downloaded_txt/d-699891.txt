news flash










employment opportunities - wlh police department 


the west lake hills police department is looking for you to join our team! we have multiple openings for police officers and will be accepting applications until the positions are filled. we believe in policing that responds to the needs of the community.			click here to apply







travis county ss4a social plan draft 


travis county and its partner cities are developing a safety action plan to reduce fatal and serious injury crashes for all road users, and they want your help evaluating the draft ss4a project locations! read on to learn how to share your comments.						read on...







installation of license plate recognition cameras 


the west lake hills police department will be installing 9 license plate recognition (lpr) cameras to solve and reduce crime. the cameras are built by flock safety. for more information, read on.						read on...







road and drainage improvements 


multiple road and drainage improvement projects are underway in west lake hills. to see a list of streets and their scheduled project dates, use the "read on" link below.			additional info...







travis county cdbg community needs survey for 2025 


the travis county health & human services cdbg team is asking for your input about community needs for low- and moderate-income families, persons with disabilities, seniors, and youth who live in west lake hills. read on for links to the cdbg survey.						read on...







oak wilt management 


oak wilt is a lethal disease of oaks at epidemic levels in central texas. the trimming or cutting of red oak and live oak species for purposes other than protecting public safety is prohibited between february 1st and june 30th. read on for more info.						read on...







loop 360 project updates 


thanks to the more than 100 neighbors in the corridor that joined last week for a virtual community update on the westlake drive/cedar street progress. the presentation is available online if you missed it. read on for more info...						read on...
