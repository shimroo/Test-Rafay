small town charm with so much to discoversmall towns like ours have a certain magic that can’t be found just anywhere. the town may be small, but there’s an abundance to explore from farm to table cuisines and finger lakes wine to museums and family-friendly festivals. so head out for the day, the weekend, or longer–we can’t wait to say hello.  


























eat & drink



geneseo boasts a variety of local food & drink including farm-to-table restaurants, local breweries, and wineries. > explore bars & restaurants 
















play



satisfy your sense of adventure around geneseo with incredible parks, hiking, skiing and family-fun destinations.> explore outdoor & recreation
















stay



find the best hotels and places to stay from the heart of downtown historic geneseo to the shores of conesus lake. > explore places to stay
















learn



the learning doesn’t stop outside the doors of suny geneseo. discover museums, cultural centers, and more.> explore museums & sites 


































see what’s happening



whether you’re a foodie, an artist, a parent, or a student–there’s something for everyone to enjoy in geneseo, new york. check out our calendar of upcoming events with exciting things to do all year round.
