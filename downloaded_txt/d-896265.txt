cross-handles

whether it’s with a long blade or a short lateral drive, the ergonomic handle allows you to carry out fast screwdriving tasks as well as powerful applications. the handle allows you to keep your wrist straight while holding the screwdriver and protects it. the lateral drive makes it easier to unscrew firmly screwed-in screws.
