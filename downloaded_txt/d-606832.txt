learn spanish in 
guanajuato


travelling to guanajuato means delving into a land of contrasts. its extensive volcanic mountain ranges turn into vast plains, where the magical towns of the region are located. the city's colonial treasures will surprise you with bright colors and unique architectonics. guanajuato, an old mining town, is famous for its history, its mummies, and its cuisine. among the many typical local dishes, you'll find sweet treats like charamuscas. studying spanish in guanajuato can quickly become a journey to the heart of mexico. make the most of the international cervantes festival and other important events in the city to put the spanish you've learned to the test. be amazed by the history of the city walls, which have been declared a world heritage site, and experience everything guanajuato has to offer.

our school in guanajuato

calle pastita 76, paxtitlan
36090 guanajuato, gto.
mexico









thanks to its spacious central patio, natural light shines into our spanish school in guanajuato, lighting up our classrooms and inspiring our students. enjoy learning spanish in an authentic mexican environment, complete with bold colors, traditional tiles, leafy plants, and teachers dedicated to your language success. after class, you and your new international friends can walk along the pastita river and relax in the nearby parks or take a short stroll into the heart of one of mexico's most important historical and cultural colonial cities.

travel through guanajuato

embark on a journey through history and art after your spanish classes in guanjuato. discover the don quixote iconographic museum, where you'll find the masterpiece's biggest secrets, descend into the legends that run through the underground streets, or climb up to your favorite lookout point to enjoy the wonder and beauty of guanajuato. learn spanish in guanajuato and immerse yourself in history, art, life, and legends, and the people who will guide you through your time in this mexican city. let the most beautiful historic center in all of mexico come into your life.









































museo casa diego rivera
the birthplace of the famous mexican painter diego rivera today houses a museum that not only shows us his work, but also tells us the story of his life, his inspiration and his influence.


museo iconográfico del quijote
guanajuato is known as "ciudad cervantina" and the iconographic museum of don quijote is replete with works of art referring to the adventures of the gentleman and his squire sancho panza.


calle subterránea miguel hidalgo
miguel hidalgo street in guanajuato is a true architectural jewel. an underground street where the guanajuato river once flowed, today is home to a multitude of legends!


mirador del pípila
the pipila lookout has the best views of guanajuato. marvel at all the colors of the city, with its characteristic architecture and impressive natural backdrop.


ex hacienda gabriel de barrera
the ex hacienda gabriel de barrera is a beautiful testimony to life in guanajuato in the eighteenth and nineteenth centuries. travel back in time and let yourself be carried away by its magic. it will not disappoint you!

discover guanajuato

every month of the year brings great opportunities to experience the city. regardless of when you start your spanish course in guanajuato, you'll have a traditional fiesta to enjoy, from religious festivals to military parades, local fairs, the very famous day of the dead, and many more. it's always a good time to learn a little more about the culture that surounds spanish in guanajuato.





fiestas of saint john - june 3 to 9

artistic and cultural events that finish with the olla dam opening to clean the river.





candy festival - october or december

wide variety of sugar figurines. skulls take center stage thanks to day of the dead.





león state fair - january 10 to february 4

shows, pyrotechnics, cockfights, bulls, and much more attractive people from all over mexico.
