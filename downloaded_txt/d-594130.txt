last year was a busy one in montgomery county! we took historic and impactful steps to build our communities, boost our economy, protect voting rights and to prioritize the health and safety of residents. learn more about what we accomplished in 2024!						read on...







keep up with montgomery county! 


interested in keeping up with the latest news from our county in, say, a once-monthly email newsletter format?

check out montco monthly, our new e-newsletter, to stay in the loop with the latest news and events from montgomery county, pa.			






check out engage montco! 


engage montco is our county's online civic engagement platform.  

there you'll find many opportunities to provide feedback on projects and help montgomery county gain a better understanding of how you want the county to plan for its future.
