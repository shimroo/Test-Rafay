dive into the enchanting world of anastasia, crafted by the tony award-winning trio terrence mcnally, stephen flaherty, and lynn ahrens—famed for broadway classics like ragtime and once on this island. this dazzling show whisks you away from the twilight of the russian empire to the exhilarating streets of paris in the 1920s. follow the brave young anya as she embarks on an epic adventure to unlock the mystery of her past, with the help of a charming con man and a delightful ex-aristocrat. this captivating tale of love and family will resonate with audiences of all ages!















get ready to be swept off your feet by hello dolly! this jubilant musical, based on thornton wilder’s beloved play the matchmaker, is brimming with humor, romance, and some of the greatest songs in musical theatre history. join the indomitable dolly gallagher levi, a turn-of-the-century matchmaker, as she orchestrates delightful romantic escapades that will leave you in stitches. this vibrant, energetic production promises to entertain and thrill audiences—old and new alike!















experience the heart and soul of new york city with in the heights! this poignant musical tells the story of the vibrant community in washington heights, a neighborhood rich in culture and tradition. with the aroma of fresh coffee wafting through the air and the sounds of three generations of music echoing on the streets, this tale beautifully captures the hopes, dreams, and pressures of a community on the brink of change. join us as we explore which traditions are cherished and which are left behind.

we have an exciting 2025 season planned with “anastasia,” “hello dolly,” and “in the heights”! this year, we implore your support to replace our aging lighting system. help us light the stage with magic and memories. your contributions play a vital role, allowing us to continue to share the woodminster experience with our community.

producers associates, inc. is the nonprofit organization that has presented woodminster summer musicals in oakland’s joaquin miller park for 50+ years. three musicals are presented each summer, one each in june, july, and august. each production has 7 performances. over the course of the summer, 25,000 ticket holders pass through woodminster’s gates to see the performances.
