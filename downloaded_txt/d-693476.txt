welcome to williamsport.



























the williamsport business association is a 501(c)(6) non-profit incorporated organization whose mission is to connect, educate and promote small businesses in the greater williamsport, pa area. we are comprised of small businesses and business professionals working together toward a common goal of shared prosperity.historically, the williamsport business association has also played an active role in helping to coordinate many events such as downtown trick or treat, second saturday, shop small passport program and catch the spirit holiday market. recently we entered a new collaboration for the inagural german christmas market with lycoming college, city of williamsport and lycoming arts, taking place annually the first friday in december.
