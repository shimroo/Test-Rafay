welcome to
doc's h-d®
doc's harley-davidson® has been in business since 1955. we are a legacy in st. louis, located right on the famous route 66. doc's delivers a premium experience for all of our new and pre-owned bike customers. our sales team are riders and know the harley-davidson line up like no other. our service department is one of the best in the nation, consisting of h-d certified technicians and master technicians. customization, performance and design is where our customers love to discuss their dreams for their motorcycle with one of our experienced parts specialist or one of our service advisors. 


if you are just starting your journey with riding a motorcycle, doc's has classes to take with our riding academy. we have terrific coaches and a variety of classes to help you learn to ride or better your skills. the motorclothes girls are the heart of the store. doc's has a great selection of riding gear, helmets, t-shirts and all things harley-davidson. our customer service in every department makes our store the place to shop. plus, our kirkwood h.o.g.® chapter has a variety of rides and events to participate in too. the doc's family is sincere in making your experience the best in the business. our loyal customers are the reason for our success. stop by and see why we say,


"it's always a party at doc's harley-davidson®!"
