lane joins the 101st
on the 19th of june 2024, lane was declared an honorary member of the 101st airborne division (air assault) by general cody for her continued work for our military and works with mwr at ft. campbell.

lane brody: colonel aide de camp
on february 1st 2021, lane was awarded the highest honor a civilian in the state of tennessee can be awarded, “colonel aide de camp”. the certificate was signed by governor bill lee and presented by senator marsha blackburn.

new cmhof plaque
lane was inducted to the country music hall of fame’s walkway of stars in 1996, along with suzy bogguss, james burton., tracy byrd, yodeling slim clark, marcel dadi, sawyer brown, toby keith, lucille starr and bryan white. now finally there is a plaque that is on demonbreun st. in the walkway of fame park at the hall of fame, listing her and all the inductees prior to 1997.

please support walden’s puddle
