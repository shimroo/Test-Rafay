welcome to knapp medical center
located in weslaco, texas, knapp medical center is proud to rank among the nation’s top hospitals for clinical quality and patient safety. serving more than 30,000 patients from across the rio grande valley our commitment to excellence has earned us numerous accolades, including recognition from prestigious sources such as clinical excellence awards from healthgrades.
in addition, knapp medical center received the “target: stroke honor roll”, “stroke gold plus” and “target: type 2 diabetes honor roll” awards from the american heart association (2024) and is certified as an advanced primary stroke center by the joint commission.
our dedicated team of healthcare professionals is committed to providing compassionate, high-quality care. thank you for choosing us to be your healthcare partner.
