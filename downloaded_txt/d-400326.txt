teacher resources and youth publications









                            this educator resources and youth publication site provides educational curricula in sustainable forestry, natural resources, water, earth sciences, and wildlife.
                        






the lesson plans and publications on this site have been designed by and for teachers for actual use in classrooms or outdoors. they meet many of pennsylvania's environmental and ecology education standards. each lesson plan indicates subject matter, grade level, and regional applicability. each publication has been field-tested with youth. feel free to adapt them to meet your specific needs and locations.
inquiries and comments concerning this website can be addressed to sanford smith, ph.d, natural resources and youth education extension specialist, penn state extension.
