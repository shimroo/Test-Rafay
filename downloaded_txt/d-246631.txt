for the love of paper…established in 1985, union street papery is the ultimate boutique destination for fashionable, elegant stationery design in the san francisco bay area.we have developed an international following among event planners and paper lovers everywhere, seeking the latest in custom designed wedding invitations, save the dates, savvy personalized stationery, distinctive party invitations, baby announcements and holiday cards.we have a passion for paper and a deep-rooted dedication to detail and design - combined with incomparable customer service amidst a fabulous collection of fresh, fine paper goods.

weddingwe are here to help you set the perfect tone for your wedding! inspiration, suggestions & advice are just a small part of everything we can do.with hundreds of samples on display, we can create a beautiful, custom suite for your special day.we’ve got a great selection of letterpress, foil stamped, engraved, embossed and digital options ready just for you!




    invitations gallery
  

   
















calligraphydazzle your guests with timeless, personalized calligraphy for your wedding invitation suite and envelopes.our supremely talented in-house calligrapher, mo seder, offers a variety of styles that can be tailored to your individual event.mo’s work has appeared in martha stewart weddings and style me pretty, and is exclusive to union street papery clientele.




    calligraphy gallery
  

 




  

occasionsimportant celebrations deserve incredible invitations! soirees that sizzle, dinner parties that dazzle, showers that shine, and birthday bashes that blow you away!invite and excite your guests with custom printed invitations from one of our exclusive designers.need something super-specific?our in-house designers can’t wait to help you create…union street papery will help set the scene for the fête of your dreams!




    occasions gallery
  

 









 




 










  

stationerymake your statement with personalized stationery, letterhead, note cards and business cards designed especially for you. whether sophisticated, formal or fashionable, union street papery’s got hundreds of styles to show you. need a signature design? enlist our design experts to create a motif or logo unique to you or your business. we’ve got a great selection of letterpress, engraved, thermography and offset stationery with your name on it!
