about us

black hills shooters supply is one of the largest wholesale reloading component distributors in the us, carrying many major brands of brass, bullets, primers, and powder to serve your needs.bhss has been operating for 40+ years and we continue to offer competitive pricing with fast, friendly, accurate service.we sell directly to oem’s and dealers only. no retail sales.











products

brass, bullets, primers, powders, and other items to serve your rifle and pistol reloading needs.we distribute products for speer, cci, federal, hornady, fmp, winchester, remington, hodgdon, imr, accurate, alliant, starline, barnes, berger, nosler, sig sauer, jagemann, norma, ervin, berrys, lapua, and more. please see list of items pdf (above) for complete list.we do not sell fully loaded ammunition.











email list / current status

dealers/oem’s - please sign up for our email list to receive current information on in stock products, updated pricing, and new information.the last couple of years have created many challenges, such as product shortages and constant price changes.bhss accepts backorders on many items and will notify you when product arrives. at times, products become massively backordered and we are forced to shut off backorders until some progress is made.
