your horse supply and western clothing shop & in canada
the horse barn is your one-stop horse supply and western clothing shop! we have one of the largest selections of western horse tack, english horse tack, western apparel, farm and cottage equipment, animal feeds, country furnishings, gifts and art in b.c.’s interior!
whether you’re an experienced or beginner rider, a country cottage dweller or simply a country western enthusiast, we have what you need to bring a piece of the west into your home, farm or town!
our experienced staff are here to answer your questions!
