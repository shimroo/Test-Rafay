expect more from life at lake nacimiento! 



with the sunlight dancing on the surface of the lake on a warm summer afternoon, a ski boat speeds by with a wake boarder kicking up white water as kids and their friends play at the shoreline, energetically splashing each other and laughing heartily. these are the sounds of life at lake nacimiento. how would you like to live in such a relaxing, enjoyable environment? perhaps you’d enjoy spending your family vacations or retirement years living here. oak shores is a great place to live, work and play!the casual, laid-back atmosphere of oak shores at lake nacimiento is apparent everywhere you look. with its gorgeous mountain backdrop and majestic oak trees, you’ll never tire of this ideal location. you’ll get more from life at lake nacimiento.oak shores is a gated community located on the north shore of the lake. situated on 328 acres of oak covered foothills in central california, lake nacimiento is the prime destination for water activities such as swimming, wake boarding, water skiing and fishing.you’ll find everything you need right here in oak shores at lake nacimiento. expect more because we’ll deliver!
