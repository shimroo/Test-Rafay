            about us
          



              women’s clothing brand established 1968 in los angeles, california.

manufacturing missy, petite and plus size apparel.
featuring stretch denim leggings and stretch denim jeans.

private label “just in time” replenishment programs using
vendor managed inventory systems.

kayo’s mission
to exceed our customers expectations in quality and value through continuous improvement.
to maintain a superior level of integrity in interactions with business partners and associates.
to protect the environment that is vital to our customers, employees and a healthy planet.
