life together



forest city church is not just a church, it's a community that cherishes the shared journey in christ. our mission is to guide you in discovering god, fostering deep connections with him and fellow believers, and nurturing a spirit of generosity towards others. we are not afraid to take risks together to advance our mission and empower you to fulfill your divine potential. as a diverse, multiethnic church, we extend a warm invitation for you to come and experience the unique spirit of forest city church!there are young families with kids, seasoned believers, and those who used to go to church, had never been to church, or are now rediscovering church. we hope to see you this sunday at 9 a.m. or 11 a.m. service.




















join us every sunday • 1280 s. alpine rd. - 9am & 11am
