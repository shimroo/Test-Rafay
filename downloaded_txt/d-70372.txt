important information regarding the upcoming city commission meeting 


there are a number of ways for the public to participate in the february 19th city commission meeting. learn more here.						read on...







hydrant flow-testing underway 


fire hydrant flow-testing is underway in the area located between sheridan street to hollywood boulevard, from n. 46th avenue to n. park road. this is required routine maintenance that will take place approximately through the end of march.			






construction to begin at keating park 


the keating park renovation and new dog park is on the way.						read on...







expanded qless hours & new in-person services starting feb. 10 


the city of hollywood will extend our qless consultation hours to monday through thursday, from 7 am to 9 am. in addition, we are now offering in-person services with select plan examiners.						read on...







get your motorist services at florida licensing on wheels 


on monday, february 24, 2024, from 10:30 a.m. to 2 p.m., the florida licensing on wheels (flow) mobile office will be at the hollywood branch of the broward county library to bring motorist services directly to you						read on...







taft street transit corridor improvement project to begin 


construction of a new shared-use path along taft street, from sr 7/us 441 to n 40th street will begin in february 2025.						read on...







latest edition of new horizons 


check out the latest edition of new horizons, available in english and spanish.						read on...







city of hollywood earns perfect score in 2024 lgbtq+ municipal equality index 


the city of hollywood has earned a perfect score of 100 on the human rights campaign foundation’s 2024 municipal equality index for having programs and policies that are inclusive of the lgbtq+ community.						read on...







comprehensive plan update underway
