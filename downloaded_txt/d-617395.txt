edt was founded in kaohsiung taiwan in september, 1994. since then, we have established a comprehensive global network to serve and support our customers worldwide.
 
our mission is to create exceptional user experience (ux) through smart user interfaces (ui). our offerings and technologies have evolved across  various marketplaces. with our specialty in touch display design and manufacturing, today, we are an intelligent user machine interface (umi) solution provider, helping you achieve easier product integration and a simpler supply chain management.
