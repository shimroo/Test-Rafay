city of west fargo presidents’ day closures and schedule changes 


the city of west fargo will close monday, feb. 17, in observance of presidents’ day. 

there is no garbage or recycling collection monday, feb. 17. collection will be delayed one day all week.						read on...







west fargo city commission chambers moving to west fargo city hall at 2515 6th street e 


west fargo city commission chambers will move to west fargo city hall at 2515 6th street e beginning monday, feb. 3, 2025.						read on...







city of west fargo pruning project scheduled december through april 


the city of west fargo department of forestry will undergo a city-wide tree pruning project beginning the week of december 30, 2024. forestry staff will conduct inspections throughout west fargo and will focus pruning efforts on the areas of greatest need						read on...







city-wide ash tree removal and replacement program in response to emerald ash borer 


the city of west fargo announces the launch of a comprehensive ash tree removal and replacement program to combat the impact of emerald ash borer (eab).						read on...
