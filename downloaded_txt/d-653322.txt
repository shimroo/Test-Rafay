our mission















the mental health association in delaware strives to strengthen knowledge, resources and support around mental health and recovery.




we aim to




















create an environment in which everyone feels supported throughout their mental health and recovery journey.respect the dignity and worth of every person.treat every person compassionately, free from stigma and prejudice.promote community understanding of mental health.advocate for those with lived experiences to have their voices heard.collaborate with others and actively look for partners to strengthen mental health.work together with integrity and a sense of purpose.
