parks marina on east lake okoboji was established in 1983 when leo “butch” parks purchased the then gibson sporting goods. what was once a small fishing boat sales and repair facility, has expanded into a diversified three location business, with marinas, sales, service, storage, boat rentals, pro-shops, and specialty retail stores. featuring boat brands by barletta pontoons, cobalt boats, crownline, crest, formula, grady white, moomba, nautique, premier,  robalo and supra
