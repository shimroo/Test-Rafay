the world's largest toyota dealership
located in el monte, ca

longo toyota was founded in 1967 by dominic longo in a small storefront in the city of el monte. with a total of five employees, the team worked diligently to achieve success. in its first year of operation, longo toyota became the #1 volume toyota dealership in the nation by selling 469 new and used vehicles. since then, longo has sustained its #1 ranking each year and continues to grow, set, and break sales records nationally.

longo toyota: a legacy of excellence
longo toyota has earned its reputation as the highest-volume toyota dealer in the united states due to decades of exceptional service and customer satisfaction. known for its expansive inventory, longo toyota offers an unmatched selection of new and preowned vehicles to suit every need and budget. our legacy is built on trust, transparency, and a commitment to excellence in every interaction. whether purchasing, leasing, or servicing your toyota, our longo toyota team continues to set the standard as leaders in the automotive industry.
comprehensive vehicle selection at longo toyota
if it's time for a new toyota,visit the experts at longo toyota. we can help you create a wish list of features and recommend vehicles based on your needs and budget. for instance, if you're looking for a midsize pickup truck with excellent towing capacity, we offer a wide selection of new vehicles, including the new tacoma. you can also find the commuter-friendly, fuel-efficient camry to make the drive to work more enjoyable. if family adventures await, check out the highlander, which is perfect for your next summer road trip. below are a few highlights from our top new toyota models.
rav4 plug-in hybrid
the 2025 toyota rav4 plug-in hybrid combines efficiency, power, and versatility. it's perfect if you're an eco-conscious driver, as it offers up to 42 miles of all-electric range and a combined 94 mpge. its 302-horsepower powertrain ensures responsive performance, while advanced tech features and spacious seating make it ideal whether you're transporting your family or just heading out on an adventure.
corolla and corolla hatchback
stylish, efficient, and fun to drive, the 2025 corolla and corolla hatchback deliver versatility for every lifestyle. with advanced safety features, modern tech, and impressive fuel economy, both models are perfect for daily commutes or weekend getaways. the sporty hatchback offers dynamic handling and extra cargo space, while the sedan combines classic elegance with everyday practicality.
tundra
the 2025 toyota tundra blends strength, innovation, and style to deliver a powerful full-size truck experience. its robust engine options offer impressive towing and hauling capabilities, while advanced off-road features ensure it's ready for any terrain. enjoy a refined cabin with modern technology, premium materials, and ample space, making it perfect for work and everyday adventures.
certified preowned and used toyotas: quality and reliability
at longo toyota, we also have an extensive selection of certified preowned (cpo) and used toyotas to help you stay within your budget while enjoying the latest features. toyota provides two cpo tiers:

gold: gold-certified vehicles include eligibility for new car financing rates, a comprehensive 12-month/12,000-mile limited warranty, and a rigorous 160-point inspection. vehicles must be 6 years old or newer and have fewer than 85,000 miles to qualify for the gold cpo designation.
silver: the silver-certified program applies to vehicles up to 10 years old with mileage ranging from 60,000 to 125,000. benefits include 12 months or 12,000 miles of 24-hour roadside assistance and a thorough 136-point quality assurance inspection, ensuring reliability and peace of mind.

unmatched customer service and experience
when you choose longo toyota for your vehicle purchase or service needs, you'll experience exceptional care from the moment you arrive. we're proud to offer outstanding support, from test-drives and financing to expert service and genuine parts. with over 50 years of dedication to customer satisfaction, our team is committed to meeting your automotive needs well beyond the sale. at longo toyota, we treat you like family, not just a customer.
in november 1999, longo toyota made history as the first automotive dealership in the country to host an on-site auto club. today, our aaa branch offers various services, including department of motor vehicles assistance, airline, car, and hotel reservations, and discounted movie and local attraction tickets. conveniently located in our new car showroom, our aaa branch is open monday through friday from 9 a.m. to 5 p.m. and saturday from 10 a.m. to 2 p.m.
state-of-the-art service and parts department
longo toyota is dedicated to delivering expert care and service you can trust. our toyota-trained technicians have spent countless hours mastering every toyota model and exclusively use genuine toyota original equipment manufacturer parts for all repairs and maintenance. longo toyota's state-of-the-art service and parts department is staffed by expert technicians who ensure your toyota runs smoothly with top-tier care and precision.
enjoy our on-site amenities, including starbucks and subway, a complimentary car wash, free wi-fi, beverages, and a relaxing guest lounge. for your convenience, we're open 7 a.m. to 7 p.m. monday through friday and 7 a.m. to 5:30 p.m. on saturday. experience service excellence with a team that helps keep your toyota running like new.
