our mission | manatee county medical society



helping physicians and patients since 1906



our mission is to guard and foster the mutual interests of our member physicians and to promote excellence and high-quality health care for the people of manatee county.

public trustto enlighten and alert the public, and to merit its respect and confidence towards the medical community
