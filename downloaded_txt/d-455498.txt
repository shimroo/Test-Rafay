tell us about your project




after 45 years of building hand-crafted log homes, maple island is proud to refer you to american log restoration for all your repair and maintenance needs. their team includes many former maple island employees who are experts in restoring and maintaining maple island homes. american log offers comprehensive repair and restoration services as well as the same high-quality hand-peeled pine logs that were originally used in your maple island home.  their use of the same techniques employed in building your home ensures the highest quality results. visit american log restoration to learn more about their services and materials or fill out this quick form.
