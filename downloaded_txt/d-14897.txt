spotlight
  
 

 


career online high school
  
career online high school offers adult learners (19 years and older) an opportunity to obtain their high school diploma. limited scholarships are available.
  
submit an interest form today







 


helpnow
  
offers free online tutoring for grades k-12 and college students, along with additional services related to the free application for federal student aid (fafsa), collaborative study sessions, chess tutoring and more.
  
live homework help







 


link+
  
link+ provides free access to more than 9 million titles from public and academic libraries. borrow books and media by searching the library's catalog and placing a request. 
  
get started







 


cloudlibrary
  
free ebooks and audiobooks for use on your favorite digital device. 
  
browse titles







 


hoopla
  
borrow free movies, tv shows, music, ebooks and audiobooks with your library card. 
  
browse content







 


library of things
  
the library of things (lot) provides items for check out such as sewing machines, gardening tools, board games, bakeware and more. this collection promotes sustainability in borrowing an item instead of purchasing or allowing patrons to "try before you buy."
  
browse the collection







 


curbside pickup
  
curbside pickup allows patrons to place items on hold through our online catalog and, once materials become available, pick them up at our curbside pickup areas.
  
learn more







 


abcmouse.com
  
free online learning program for children ages 2-8 that strengthens reading and math skills through educational games and activities.
  
register here







 


readyrosie
  
sign up for readyrosie to receive free customized video playlists to support your child's educational growth. just visit the link below and enter the code rcpl.
  
register here







 


mango languages
  
online language-learning system to develop conversation skills in over 70 languages.
  
start learning today







 


digital magazines
  
full-color digital magazines for anytime, anywhere reading. 
  
browse magazines







 


novelist
  
read-alike recommendations to connect you with your next favorite book.
  
find a great book







 


new york times online
  
full-text articles from the new york times. register using a valid email account. 
  
get started







 


learning express
  
contains nearly 1,000 online tutorials and practice tests.
  
take a practice test







 


volunteer at the library
  
support your community in variety of ways. from tutoring adults and children to delivering books to those who are homebound.  
  
apply today
