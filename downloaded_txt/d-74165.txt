brick wheels and traverse city light & power are teaming up!brick wheels bike shop is excited to offer a $300 instant rebate on the purchase of any new electric bicycle for tclp customers. electric bikes offer an eco-friendly, efficient, and enjoyable way to navigate traverse city. this rebate makes switching to an e-bike more affordable than ever. don't miss out—ride green and save green today!











how it works:tclp is partnering with brick wheels to provide instant discounts on high-quality e-bikes. .present your latest tclp bill and driver's license to enjoy an immediate $300 discount on any of our premium e-bikes.give yourself a pat on the back and enjoy a stress-free commute across town, bypassing traffic.spread the word to all your friends about your incredible new lifestyle.* limit two rebates per service address per year *











why choose an electric bicycle?environmental benefit: e-bikes markedly decrease carbon emissions versus cars, promoting cleaner air and a greener planet. affordable: cut costs on fuel, parking fees, and upkeep expenses. convenience: avoid traffic jams and experience a smoother, often quicker commute. e-bikes offer route flexibility, including bike lanes and trails. health advantages: incorporate physical activity into your daily life, enhancing cardiovascular wellness and overall fitness levels.
