welcome to the legacy of louisiana thoroughbreds



formed in 1963, the louisiana thoroughbred breeders association represents and upholds the interests of the breeding and racing of thoroughbred horses in louisiana. the organization strives to advance the industry through its sponsorship of races and sales as well as its breeding program. this website showcases the breeding, racing and sales programs that the organization manages on behalf of its members.visitors can find leading sires, champions, and forms, along with business of the organization. the louisiana horse magazine portal will take visitors to the latest news, feature articles, educational seminars and more.
