thanks for 
stopping by and welcome to oak tree vintage. we are dealers of used, vintage, new and retro stuff, with an emphasis on
	stereo / hi-fi gear from the 1960's-80's. we also specialize in vintage 
microphones & cables as well as old tube & solid state 
vintage electronics and test gear. there's used / vintage guitars,
amplifiers / effects pedals, keyboards and synthesizers, pa gear, tube and 
	transistor radios, antique electric fans, refurbished 8mm & 16mm film 
projectors, old electric clocks, 
space-age / deco furniture and much more.  
		we also have new, reproduction radio / tape player / phonograph / cd players telephones and reproduction 
antique music boxes and jukeboxes. 
		we launched the web site in 1996 and have been involved in the electronics 
business professionally since 1983! concerning the vintage audio gear, we 
		primarily sell gear 
that has been restored / refurbished back to as close to new as is monetarily practical, not 
only cosmetically, but internally / functionally as well. most then receives our posted warranty. 
we 'ready' gear as if we were going to use it for the 
		next many years, and mostly buy gear that we would use ourselves.
		our gear will not be "cheaply" priced,  but again, has been painstakenly 
restored / refurbished / fully serviced and will be actually be ready to use 
for the "long haul" and should last many years. a bit snobbish i suppose, but after 
		35+ years in this business, we have 
learned that works best for us and our customers for the long run.
