pig & whistle historic pub corner of haupapa and tutanekai street, rotorua














rotorua’s historic pub



with a wide range of new zealand craft beer + cider on tap, new zealand wines, great pub food, + live entertainment every weekend, our friendly staff will make your visit one you’ll want to repeat.centrally located in a classic piece of bold 1940’s architecture, the pig & whistle is a local landmark with a great history.
