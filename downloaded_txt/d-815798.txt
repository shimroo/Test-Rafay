our mission
caring to improve the lives of our employees and the people we serve
case & associates was the brainchild of mike case, who founded the company in 1983 with the idea that property management could be more personable and customer-service oriented. his philosophy extended to the members of his team. he surrounded himself with like-minded, experienced real estate professionals, including his son, scott case, who now leads the company. the core team at case & associates has over 200 years of combined experience in the industry and, most importantly, shares a commitment to constantly improving the customer experience.
