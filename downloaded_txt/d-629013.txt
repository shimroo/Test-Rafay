committed to your safety—call 911 for fast response


by  chief nate hartinger

january 14, 2025






calling 911 is completely free, and our team is ready to respond quickly to keep situations under control. whether it’s a medical concern, a fire alarm, or something that doesn’t feel right, don’t hesitate to call. the sooner our dispatchers are notified, the sooner our first responders can act.

on the evening of january 7, uc davis fire’s brush 34 engine and its crew joined the yolo county strike team responding to fires in southern california.





 




uc davis celebrates arrival of new ladder truck with traditional push-in ceremony


by  hayley s morris


november 25, 2024






on nov. 19, the uc davis fire department celebrated the arrival of its new fire truck with a traditional push-in ceremony. this tradition dates back to the era of horse-drawn fire carriages when firefighters would physically push a new firefighting vehicle into the station. held at station 34, the event marked a milestone for the department, which only replaces its ladder truck every 15–20 years.
