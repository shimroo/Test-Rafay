latest single















california dreamin’




“i wanted to share a song of hope, inspired by my fellow californians during the challenges of the la wildfires. ‘california dreamin’, originally by the mamas & the papas, felt like the perfect way to capture not just the feeling of this winter, but also the resilience and beauty of this place. i hope it brings warmth to those who need it and inspires those who can make a difference.”

— sharyn maceren
















































about sharyn maceren



sharyn maceren is the bold new electric spark in the future of dance music. her latest album, queen of dreams, is taking dance & edm lovers on an exhilarating thrill ride through high-voltage anthems and ethereal, dreamlike soundscapes with tracks like “feel the vibe,” “call my name,” and “electric sky.” drawing inspiration from superstar djs, edm festivals, as well as her own life story— queen of dreams celebrates what life feels like when you’re living your blessed life— and the miraculous journey of faith it took to get there. dreamlife energy is more than just sharyn’s sound—it’s a movement. from her dreamlife energy podcast to her dreamlife vip site for #1 fans, sharyn brings her core values to life, inspiring others to live out their dreams and discover their soul— all to the beat of her music.
