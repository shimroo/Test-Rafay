randolph fire department responds to three-alarm apartment fire 


the fire occurred at randolph village apartment complex that was reported at approximately 12:00 am, caused significant damage to a substantial portion of the building’s left side. twelve apartments were affected.						read on...







note regarding snow removal and mailbox damage 


one of the unintended consequences of any municipal snow removal program is mailbox damage. residents are advised that it is the general policy of the township to not replace mailboxes damaged during the snow removal process.						read on...







affordable housing update 


affordable housing update from our mayor joe hathaway. read for more information...						read on...







free covid-19 test kits available 


the randolph health department is offering free covid-19 test kits to residents to help ensure the health and safety of our community.						read on...







sussex turnpike road construction (saturday construction updates) 


morris county’s contractor will be removing trees, rock scaling and installing metal netting along existing rock slopes at two portions of sussex turnpike (cr 617)  between arlyn and ash lanes and between arthur lane and washington valley. read more...						read on...







new option available to pay your randolph taxes and water/sewer bills 


current autopay participants must create an account by january 15, 2025, if you wish to continue your autopay for your taxes and  water/sewer bills. read for more information...						read on...







new reporting app now available to randolph residents! 


see click fix, is a community-driven communication tool and request management system that bridges the gap between randolph residents and township officials to improve citizen services!						read on...

randolph township storm water infrastructure mapping 


the township has contracted with stormwater compliance solutions to perform gis mapping of township-owned stormwater infrastructure, including storm drains and pipes.						read on...







winter termination program 


the nj department of community affairs has implemented a winter termination program to prevent service discontinuation for eligible residential customers receiving residential water and/or sewer from november 15 through march 15.						read on...
