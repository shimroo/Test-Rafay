bringing the campus to the community


based at the virginia tech roanoke center, the center for organizational and technological advancement (cota) provides university-community engagement programs and leadership development opportunities in conjunction with virginia tech faculty.


today's professional must be capable of leading change and people; generating results; managing human, financial, and information resources strategically; facilitating cooperation and teamwork; and deploying technical knowledge and skills effectively. we can help foster best practices and innovation across professions and sectors including business, industry, k-12 and post-secondary education, government, and nonprofit.


programs are offered at the historic hotel roanoke & conference center in roanoke, virginia. the hotel and conference center is a shining example of a campus-community partnership between virginia tech and the city of roanoke.

planning a conference? we can help.


cota provides grants to support conferences and programs at the hotel roanoke & conference center. funding guidelines have been established by virginia tech and are awarded by the cota leadership team. the following programs are eligible for consideration and will be prioritized thusly:

virginia tech-affiliated activities.
other university and educational organizations.
state and federal agencies.
professional associations and other nonprofit organizations not affiliated with virginia tech.
private-sector companies and organizations.
