higgins powersports
at higgins powersports, we are your local, family-owned dealership, providing exceptional service to the vibrant communities of central massachusetts and beyond. with 48 years of expertise in the powersports industry, we are dedicated to providing top-notch service from seasoned professionals.

step into our spacious showroom to witness our extensive collection of premium atvs, side-by-sides, motorcycles, and snowmobiles from renowned brands like can-am, kawasaki, cf moto, and polaris, including the thrilling 3-wheeled models like the polaris slingshot and can-am spyder and ryker.

we understand the unique needs of our customers, which is why we also offer a diverse range of products from other trusted brands such as husqvarna power equipment, dr power equipment, giant electric bicycles, hammerhead off-road go-karts, argo, ls tractors, and more. our award-winning service department, staffed by four factory-certified technicians who are also riders, provides top-quality maintenance and repair for all the products we sell.

at higgins powersports, we are more than just a dealership - we are your one-stop shop for a thrilling and unforgettable powersports experience!
