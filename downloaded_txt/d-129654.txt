about palace cafe
located at the foot of the french quarter, this classic new orleans restaurant honors the rich history of new orleans cuisine in an upbeat and lively grand café. the restaurant, housed in the historic werlein’s music building, has won a number of local and national awards since opening, including best new restaurant from esquire magazine and usa today, the prestigious ivy award from restaurants and institutions magazine, and wine spectator’s award of excellence.palace café is owned and operated by dickie brennan, steve pettus and lauren brennan brower.
