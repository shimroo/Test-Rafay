access to all terrains.

 

debug all terrain beach and surf chairs’ low pressure wheeleeze™ tires glide with ease over sand, gravel, grass, and other uneven terrains. all debug beach wheelchairs are made of 316l stainless steel. they will resist corrosion caused by beach conditions and last far longer than aluminum and marine-grade steel used by other manufacturers. the optional elevating leg rest and many other custom features provide additional comfort, support, and mobility.
