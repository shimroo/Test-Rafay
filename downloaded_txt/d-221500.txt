a premier therapeutic equestrian center



pegasus farm is a path intl. premier accredited center














the mission of pegasus farm is to create a community that holistically supports and empowers people with diverse needs through therapeutic equestrian programs, vocational services, and recreational and social activities.


































equestrian programs

our take flight program offers unique, tailored equestrian activities for individuals facing diverse challenges. click to see how we can support growth and joy through equestrian activities.




equestrian program - take flight











adult day services

the wings program offers vocational services with the opportunity for equine-assisted activities for adults with developmental disabilities. click for paths to employment and empowerment through ohio's waiver program.




adult day services - wings











military & first responders

connect with our military and first responders center with one-on-one horse interactions to family events, exercise, and counseling. click to discover how we honor and serve those who protect us.




military & first responders center











country store

your destination for wholesome goods, from organic treats to farm-fresh produce, all while supporting a meaningful cause. managed by our wings participants, our store offers unique products and job training opportunities. click to view our products and learn about our wellness garden.




pegasus farm country store











annual gala 2024 jerseys & jeans - be a game changer

join us for an evening of inspiration at our gala, 'jerseys & jeans’ hosted by the pro football hall of fame on november 9, 2024. support our mission, enjoy a night of dinner, auctions, and celebrations,  tickets coming soon!




annual gala celebration











wellness garden

explore the wellness garden at pegasus farm with a self-guided tour. witness the lush produce, from eggplant to aromatic herbs, nurtured by our wings program participants. support our mission and enjoy the freshest picks at our country store. 




wellness garden
