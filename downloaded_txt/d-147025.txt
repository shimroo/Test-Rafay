social responsibility 
 we strive to benefit individuals and the society 







from africa to asia and across the globe, sarine has always strived to uplift and empower our surrounding environments. whether it be donations to orphanages and schools in rural india, food parcels for underprivileged communities in namibia or mask giveaways during the covid-19 pandemic, we have engaged in numerous charities and volunteer programs. for us, sustainability doesn’t start and end with diamonds, it extends to the world as a whole.
