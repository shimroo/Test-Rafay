all saints episcopal school is an independent school for students age 3 through the twelfth grade. all saints is the premier school in east texas.
as a school of inquiry, innovation, and impact all saints is designing programs and spaces that allow students to explore their questions, passions, and interests in hands-on, experiential learning environments. our missionis to empower students to reach their full potential academically, artistically, athletically, and spiritually through creativity, collaboration, and innovative learning in an inclusive, nurturing christian environment.
