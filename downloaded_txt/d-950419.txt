the grays harbor advantagelocated on washington state’s pacific coast, the port of grays harbor operates seven lines of business throughout grays harbor county including 4 deep-water marine terminals with dual class 1 rail service and available marine terminal uplands, the westport marina, washington state’s #1 commercial seafood landing port, the satsop business park offering abundant manufacturing and office space just 30-minutes from i-5, and a suite of available industrial properties to suit a variety of needs.
