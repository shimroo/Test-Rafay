 feel at home in the northwoods — however you staycome watch the northwoods come to life during the spring thaw. fish for your dinner during the long days of summer. hike and hunt as you enjoy the fall colors. ski, snowshoe, or snowmobile on our extensive system of trails in the winter.















a-framesour classic northwoods a-frame cabins are comfortable for couples, families, or groups. they offer an open floor plan with kitchen, living, and dining room. the wood stove fireplace in the cabin is supplied with wood for your northwoods experience.















cottagesyour family cabin in the woods, the 2-bedroom cottages offer a contemporary interior with northwoods décor. they have an open-concept kitchen, living, and dining area and are great for couples or small families.
