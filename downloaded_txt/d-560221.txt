come as you are.



you don’t have to clean yourself up before you come to jesus…in fact, the only way you can truly come to him is just as you are.  so, no matter your questions, your past, or your struggles, you will be welcomed here by an imperfect spiritual family doing our best to follow jesus.  it’s our desire that you come as you are and that, as you encounter love and truth, you are forever changed.
