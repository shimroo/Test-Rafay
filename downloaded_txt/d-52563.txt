welcome toappleton harley-davidson®
located in clarksville, tennessee
our harley-davidson® motorcycle dealership has been serving middle tennessee and western kentucky for decades. in fact, we are one of the oldest harley-davidson® motorcycle dealers in the nation! we sell the full lineup of harley-davidson® motorcycles and can order you the latest models. in stock, our shop carries both new harley-davidson® motorcycles for sale and used bikes. check out our in-store selections of  street®, sportster®, softail®, touring, trike, and cvo™ models. we also service motorcycles, sell genuine harley-dvaidson® parts, create custom bikes, and offer harley-davidson® financing. appleton harley-davidson® is located in clarksville, near nashville, springfield, cumberland city, and fort campbell, ky. we have proudly served fort campbell soldiers for more than 70 years. visit or contact our dealership today to enjoy the freedom that comes with riding a harley-davidson® motorcycle!
