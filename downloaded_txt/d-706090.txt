artistauthorlecturer          laurence caruana is a maltese artist, writer & lecturer noted for his contribution to the contemporary visionary art movement. his highly-detailed works explore the interplay of myths, symbols & styles from different sacred traditions ~ always seeking the greater unity at their source. 
 
          this archive is designed for visitors to discover & explore his multi-facetted works. each page lead you deeper into the site through sub-menus and links. new material is always being added to this online chronicle of his life & work. please enjoy your journey of discovery...                        artistauthorlecturerchapelacademymediablogbiographylcaruana.com
