northwest veg is dedicated to bringing awareness to the power of a plant-based, vegan lifestyle and helping support people in their transition toward making healthier, more sustainable & compassionate food choices. we welcome everyone and encourage you to explore the issues at your own pace. 




















“so nice to be around like minded, compassionate people. great events, and even better company.”— jazmin havens, facebook review








why veg?



see how plant-based choices can support a healthy planet, compassion for animals, your own health, and human rights.
