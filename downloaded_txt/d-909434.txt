welcome to swcaa
since 1967, the southwest clean air agency has been working to keep the air clean for the citizens of southwest washington. we monitor air quality and enforce federal, state, and local air pollution laws. we believe everyone plays a part in keeping our air healthy.
							
— uri papish, executive director
