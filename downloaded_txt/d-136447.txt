screen printing



superior quality screen printing with over two decades of experience - our team of ink pros and press operators use industry standard equipment to perfectly place your design or logo on the garment of your choice. gimasport evaluates your logo or design to see how it best fits the goods you want to print on to ensure you will love the end result.gimasport’s screen printing department can handle production runs of tens of thousands all the way down to a very small number.






 









embroidery



embroidery made easy - from digitizing, sewout approval and all the way to the final embroidered product! gimasport offers quality embroidery decoration on everything from fleece, polos and outerwear to hats, beanies and much more.if you already have an embroidery file, we can analyze it to make sure it is digitized properly for the type of material you would like to sew it on.







 









direct to film



direct to film (dtf) printing is ideal for designs/graphics that are too complex for the traditional screen printing methods. dtf is also an incredible option for anyone that would need small quantities of decorated apparel. whether you only need one shirt or you want to print a photo realistic picture, direct to film printing is the smart option for you!
