welcome tomainstreet deland!


















































award winning main streetdowntown deland is an award-winning florida main street community. many dedicated businesses and individuals work daily to maintain a vibrant and economically successful downtown. this is where our history and our future connect and we invite you to be a part of it.deland was among the first five communities to receive the main street designation in florida in 1985 and was recognized as a “great american main street award” winner in 1997 by the national trust for historic preservation main street center. in 2017, mainstreet deland won the “america’s main streets” contest presented by independentwestand.org. we are the only community in the nation to have earned both awards!spend a little time with us and you’ll encounter an authentic, thriving, great american downtown. whether you live here, are coming for business, or for pleasure, we believe you’ll find what you’re looking for.

deland bike rallymarch 1, 9:00 am to 5:00 pm















get ready for a day of roaring engines, gleaming chrome, and good times as motorcycle enthusiasts from near and far gather in the heart of downtown deland for the deland bike rally on march 1! enjoy live music, check out the incredible lineup of bikes, and browse vendors offering gear, accessories, and more. grab a bite at one of the local restaurants or kick back with a drink while soaking in the atmosphere.volunteer at the event

florida wildflower & garden festivalmarch 29, 9:00 am to 3:00 pm















discover the beauty of florida’s native plants at the florida wildflower & garden festival on march 29 in downtown deland! held on west indiana avenue, the event features expert speakers, plant vendors, and garden-related exhibitors, all celebrating florida’s rich botanical heritage. whether you’re a seasoned gardener or just love flowers, come explore, shop, and learn in a fun, family-friendly atmosphere.

mainstreet deland association celebrating 40 years!















in 2025, the mainstreet deland association is proud to celebrate its 40th anniversary! for four decades, our dedicated organization has worked to preserve the historic charm of deland while fostering a vibrant downtown community. join us throughout the year as we commemorate this incredible milestone with special events, tributes, and celebrations that honor our past and look forward to an exciting future. thank you for being part of the mainstreet deland journey!

downtown cra & gateway grant programs



at least once per year and contingent upon funding, the city of deland provides competitive grant programs designed to encourage building improvements within the downtown redevelopment area (cra). also contingent upon funding, the city of deland provides competitive grant programs designed to encourage building improvements within the city’s designated gateways.

visiting deland by sunrail?



explore deland with ease by taking sunrail to the new deland station, just minutes from historic downtown deland. whether you catch the free voride circulator shuttle, free voride on-demand rideshare, or bike, getting to the heart of deland is simple and convenient.
