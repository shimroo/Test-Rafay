falkirk cultural center, owned and operated by the city of
san rafael, is a beautiful 19th century eleven-acre country estate
listed on the national historic register.
open hours
tuesday - friday | 1:00 pm - 4:30 pm
saturday | 10:00 am - 2:00 pm
please call ahead to make sure we are not closed for a private event. 
this elegant three-story home, a blend of queen anne and eastlake styles, was built in 1888. in 1906, captain robert dollar purchased the estate and later it was named falkirk in honor of his scottish birthplace. the cultural center has served the marin community since 1974.
to view falkirk cultural center for an event and to book a private tour, email us at falkirk@cityofsanrafael.org
