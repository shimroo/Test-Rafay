downtown ann arbor’s authentic irish pubconor o’neill’s, affectionately known by locals as “the pub” or “conor’s” has been ann arbor’s favorite gathering spot for years.  we are located on one of the usa’s most iconic main streets in the heart of the downtown, just walking distance from the world-famous university of michigan stadium, “the big house”. the pub was designed in ireland to bring a truly authentic pub experience to ann arbor.proud to be named best irish pub in michigan by yahoo & delish (2020) and awarded best food & drink pairing at ann arbor art & brew (2019).
