latest news
stay up to date with us
 






  
 










bar harbor 2035 comprehensive plan is available for public review 

the bar harbor 2035 comprehensive plan is available online and in-person.				read on...




moratorium ordinance on certain transient accommodations adopted 

on february 4, the town council adopted a moratorium ordinance regarding certain transient accommodations. this regular ordinance takes effect march 6, 2025. the previously adopted emergency moratorium remains in effect through march 7.				read on...




rfp for renovation of the chambers room and the auxiliary room 

the town of bar harbor (“town”) is seeking proposals from qualified contractors for the interior renovation of three rooms in the municipal building: chambers room, large gathering room and the auxiliary room.				read on...




public hearing 2/4 on port and harbor ordinance amendment 

the town council will hold a public hearing tuesday, february 18 at 6:30 p.m. to hear comment on a proposed amendment to chapter 153, port and harbor, to add a provision for abandonment of mooring assignments.				read on...








water, sewer budget ordinance amendments adopted 

on january 21, the town council adopted ordinance amendments to adopt the fy2025 water and sewer budgets.				read on...




applications open for sustainable tourism management task force 

the town council is establishing a sustainable tourism management task force. anyone interested in applying to serve on the task force is asked to submit an application to the town clerk's office.				read on...




statement on sidman vs. town of bar harbor decision 

justice mckeon of the business & consumer court has dismissed the remaining claims in the sidman vs. town of bar harbor complaint.				read on...




the bar harbor 2035 executive summary & navigation guide are available 

if you want an easy way to start learning about the bar harbor 2035 comprehensive plan, check out the executive summary and navigation guide.				read on...
