stewards of maui’s historyas caretakers of the land, artifacts, photographs, and documents entrusted to our care, it is our responsibility to ensure that the cultural roots and history that define our community will continue to be here for future generations.
























hale hōʻikeʻike 
hale hōʻikeʻ'ike exhibits a major collection of pre-western contact hawaiian artifacts. the maui historical society also maintains an archival resource center which includes over 10,000 historic photos as well as maps, manuscripts, genealogy records, historical documents and more than 2,000 objects.
