hot jambalaya













opens march 7 @ 8 pm
preview performances: february 28, march 1 & march 6 @ 8 pmfamily ties, lies, secrets, and the treachery of old new orleans aristocracy boil over in hot jambalaya, an exciting new murder musical comedy from dad’s garage!when papa joe “sewer king” van horn takes a fatal spill from a french quarter balcony, his eccentric family, friends, and professional associates gather for his funeral aboard the aging steamboat fleur de lis only to make a series of startling discoveries: papa joe was murdered, every single person present wanted him dead, and the killer intends to strike again!click here to learn more!





this friday!













february 21 @ 8 pm
murder is on the ballot here at dad’s garage!this president’s day, the improvised whodunit murder, she improvised returns with bipartisan hijinks featuring the highest officers in the nation: the presidents of the united states! step into the west wing and help the presidents you know best (or totally forgot about) solve the mysterious murder of one of their own!





blackground













february 22 @ 8 pm
blackground is an all-african-american comedy troupe. they take classic movies like star wars and indiana jones and improvise what the black people were doing during the movie. it is a hilarious show featuring killer comedy, incredible guests, and a fun-filled celebration of blackness. come laugh, celebrate, and embrace the essence of blackness in a way you've never seen before. don't miss out on this unforgettable experience where laughter meets legacy. 






    get your tickets now!
  



the dad’s garage lobby opens one hour before the show for all patrons. if you purchased tickets ahead of time, skip the box office line and present your mobile tickets linked in your confirmation email to our ticket scanner. if you prefer, you may still pick up your ticket(s) at the box office.be sure to grab a drink at our bar!*all tickets have an additional $2 processing fee for online orders.

our mission



at dad’s garage, it’s our mission to transform people, communities, and perspectives through laughter.through our dedication to nurturing artists in the beginnings of their careers, dad’s has become a launch pad for creative success. our performers, designers, and other creative professionals go on to become leaders in their fields, and work with other theaters across the world. by saying “yes, and…” to emerging creative voices, we give them the support necessary to succeed in the arts.
