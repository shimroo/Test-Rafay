deputies, resident receive commendations from sheriff’s office for courageous actions 


the clark county sheriff’s office awarded commendations to several deputies and one local resident for their courageous actions in the line of duty on feb. 12 at the board of clark county commissioners meeting.						read on...







utilities dept. to raise water and sewer rates in 2025 


the rate structure was approved by the board of clark county commissioners at its meeting on wednesday, jan. 15.						read on...







rittenhouse elected president of the board of clark county commissioners 


she takes over for melanie flax wilt, who served as board president from 2019 through 2024.						read on...







patterson sworn in as clark county commissioner 


patterson previously spent more than 30 years in public service, including 23 as clark county health commissioner.						read on...







ebt card skimming fraud targeting snap recipients 


the ohio investigative unit is issuing an alert about an escalating threat of electronic benefits transfer (ebt) card skimming fraud targeting snap (supplemental nutrition assistance program) recipients.						read on...







commissioners select four residents as 2024 clark county luminaries 


the clark county luminaries – new carlisle residents john and jim bobo, springfield resident john brown and springfield resident rex fent – were honored at the dec. 27 commission meeting.						read on...







commissioners approve new permit fee schedules, effective jan. 1, 2025 


the new fee schedule includes updates to zoning, residential building and commercial building permit fees.						read on...







clark county, donnelsville agree to extend water access to service residents 


the ohio epa will transfer the balance of $750,000 in h2ohio grant funding previously awarded to the village to clark county to begin the process of designing the infrastructure to provide water access to village residents.						read on...
