curatorial exhibitions is a nonprofit organization that expands public opportunities to view and experience significant and overlooked works of art. 



we collaborate closely with artists, private collections and museums to develop and circulate diverse and innovative exhibitions, and offer learning and audience engagement programming, for museums and art organizations worldwide.

land acknowledgment 



curatorial exhibitions headquarters exists on the ancestral lands of gabrielino-tongv and kizh nation people who continue to call this region home. we express our gratitude to these indigenous peoples as the traditional caretakers of this landscape, for the opportunity to learn from their wisdom. we respectfully acknowledge their unique and enduring relationship with this region.
