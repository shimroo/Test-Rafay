st. thomas more: a parish for all seasons
seasons ii capital campaignin these days of the pandemic, i share with you what i am doing;   as your pastor, i encourage you to “hang on to jesus!”these days are difficult, but if we walk with jesus, we will make it through.we will be scarred, but we will be better people once this is behind us.my prayers are with you and your family!in these days, our lives go on and the life of st. thomas more parish continues as well.and because of your commitment to st. thomas more,   we are not only surviving but we are thriving!in january 2018, we began our three-year capital campaign:   “st. thomas more: a parish for all seasons.”for all who participated, thank you for helping   st. thomas more to not only survive, but to thrive.the seasons campaign was successful but not to the degree that was needed.click on the link below for a “recap” of seasons campaign i   and a preview of seasons campaign ii.please take time to read and reflect on this information…   also watch, pray, and reflect upon the two special videos created for our first campaign,“st. thomas more: a parish for all seasons” at the left.if you have any questions or concerns, please feel free to contact me orron briseno, our business manager/director of development.let us continue to make st. thomas more parish not only survive but to thrive!“hang on to jesus!
