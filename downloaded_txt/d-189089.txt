we are 2nd markets corporation of tennessee. our owner, jon warren, wrote the book on movie posters. his movie poster price guides have been helping collectors since 1985. we are a full-time business licensed in hamilton county, tennessee, an a+ rated member of the better business bureau, and a professional buyer and appraiser with over 30 years experience buying direct from individuals and estates. we were doing this before there was an internet!
