we have moved some pages. you may need to update your bookmarks or home page to reach the site you are looking for.
watch fios tv can be found at: tv.verizon.com/watch/
my verizon account home can be found at: verizon.com/myverizon
outage information can be found at: verizon.com/outage
 











to change your home page:

internet explorer: open internet explorer. go to the webpage you want to set as your home page. tap or click the tools button, and then tap or click internet options. under the general tab, do one of the nofollowing: to set the page you are on as your home page, tap or click use current. to set a blank home page, tap or click use blank.

firefox: open firefox. click on the menu button (three lines) in the top right corner. select options. in the general tab enter the address of the site to set as the homepage.  you can also just open a tab with the web page you want to use as your home page. then drag and drop that tab onto the home button (it is the icon of a house).
safari: select preferences, then click general. enter a webpage address in the homepage field, or click set to current page to use the webpage you are currently viewing.
