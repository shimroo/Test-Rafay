the central & southern ohio region of the american red cross
the central & southern ohio region of the american red cross serves 6.1 million people in 47 counties from richland county, oh to northern kentucky and from southeast indiana to athens county, oh.




























central ohio tornadoes
have you been  impacted by the tornadoes and need information or a place to stay?
