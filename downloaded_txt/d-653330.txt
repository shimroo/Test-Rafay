abandoned, orphaned and vulnerable kids face immense challenges. without caring parents, their chances of success may seem low, but at caminul felix we make sure every child gets the love & care they need. for over 30 years, we have been a source of hope for these children, giving them a safe and loving place to grow up. our team of parents and support staff work with all their hearts to create families where children can heal, learn & step into a beautiful future. we believe every child deserves this & we are committed to changing their lives for the long haul!
