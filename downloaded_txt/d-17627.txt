about us


blue cross and blue shield of texas is proud to be the state’s only statewide, customer-owned health insurer. we are also the largest provider of health benefits in texas, serving more than 10.4 million members in all 254 counties across the state. for more than 95 years, we’ve provided our members with comprehensive, affordable health plans, making texas a healthier place to live, work and play. learn about the many ways we are contributing to the well-being of our local texas communities in the latest corporate social responsibility report.
