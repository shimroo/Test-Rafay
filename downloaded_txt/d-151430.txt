student enrollment, engagement & services











 
old dominion university's division of student enrollment, engagement & services is focused on recruitment, engagement, active learning, and student success. from admissions, leadership, and student involvement to graduation and lifelong career services, our staff is here to support you and help you make the most of your collegiate experience. make your monarch experience the best it can be!
