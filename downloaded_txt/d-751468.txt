roots restaurant & bar, located in the riverstone marketplace in camas, offers an innovative choice in dining. overflowing with pacific northwest warmth, the restaurant provides an open view to the bustling chefs counter, a warm and welcoming dining room, a fully stocked bar, professional and friendly service, and most importantly, cuisine that exemplifies the northwest’s best.



brad root’s lifelong passion for culinary arts will be evident as patrons savor every last bite of his edible masterpieces. with a seasonally-changing menu, guests will get to sample a variety of root’s exquisite, handcrafted dishes. we cordially invite you to experience the new taste of the pacific northwest at roots restaurant & bar.
