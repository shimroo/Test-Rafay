about

cemus is a student-initiated, transdisciplinary centre at uppsala university. cemus started as a student initiative with its first course humanity and nature in 1992. we offer undergraduate and masters level sustainability courses, have different meeting places in uppsala and work with different collaborative outreach projects and activities.
