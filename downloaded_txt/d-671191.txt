if you, or a loved one, needs help from substance abuse or trauma, you don't have to go another day without hope for recovery. teen challenge norwestcal nevada can help.























































teen challenge norwestcal nevada has ten residential recovery centers welcoming men, women, teens, children, and families. 



our centers



































women | women & children

there is a women’s center as well as two centers for women and children.




learn more











families

a unique center in the united states. we offer a place for families to heal together.




learn more











men

there are three centers, each in a different location, for men 18 and older.




learn more











teen boys

for young men 12 - 17.




learn more











teen girls

for girls 12 - 17.




learn more











re-entry centers
