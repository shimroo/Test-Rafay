welcome to stonover farm




    book your stay
  














stonover farm is a small luxury inn located in the beautiful berkshires, home to outstanding natural scenery, picturesque new england towns, and a hub of cultural activity including tanglewood, the summer home of the boston symphony orchestra. the inn has three en-suite guest rooms in the main house, a one-bedroom suite in the school house, and a two-bedroom rock cottage with its own kitchen.














enjoy cozy winter activities in the berkshires.  come join us for all of your favorite wintertime activities in the berkshires like skiing, sitting by the fire and enjoying the holiday seasons in style at stonover farm.



we have been enjoying lots of snow in the berkshires.  come enjoy the skiing at one of our local ski resorts and settle back into a cozy evening at stonover.  or, come for the beautiful wintry landscape and relaxation.  we’re here to welcome you!
