welcome to christ churchchrist episcopal church is a community committed to walking the way of love and building god’s kingdom. if you are seeking a community where you can be accepted and encouraged as you respond to god’s call in your own life, we invite you to join us. there are many ways for you to participate in ministry at christ church, and we encourage you to explore our website, check out our church calendar, and come worship and serve with us!




    learn more
  


























our worship is rooted in holy scripture and the deep traditions of the episcopal church. as episcopalians, we are followers of jesus christ, and part of that identity includes a call to respect the dignity of every human being. we seek to live out this call in our worship and every time that we gather. everyone is invited to participate fully in our worship. 




    learn more →
  















we believe that making music is a form of prayer. it awakens our soul and draws us closer to god and each other by inviting us into beauty. at christ church our music leadership works to empower everyone into the work of making a joyful noise. regardless of background, if you are interested in being a part of our music ministry, you will be welcomed!




    learn more →
  















no matter where you are in your life’s journey, we want to grow with you. we are committed to this call, which is why christ church offers dedicated formation for children of all ages. we also welcome all children into full participation in our worship, and we welcome families with small children to enjoy our soft play area in the sanctuary. 




    learn more →

we are committed to life-long formation at christ church because we believe that we our faith informs the way we live in the world. so, we welcome our children of all ages to full participation in our work. questions are always welcome, and diverse opinions are respected. 




    learn more →
  















rummage is the oldest and largest faith-in-action ministry of christ church. despite its many volunteers and significant revenues, rummage does not raise funds for christ church. in fact, rummage gives away the funds it raises to support local non-profits that are seeking to make the world a better place!




    learn more →
  















at christ church, we believe that every member of our community is called by god to the work of love! everyone has unique gifts, and there are many ways that you can put your faith into action at christ church. we need you as we continue to seek and serve god in all people!
