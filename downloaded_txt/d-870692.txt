tethr it now
npk is always looking to make our products support department as accessible as possible. introducing tethr it now, a universal remote video platform that is used by various customer service specialist and product support technicians. it’s browser to browser connection allows no app download and no installations. it is the fastest and easiest way to provide our customers with excellent support with the following situations:

remote troubleshooting / diagnosis
equipment rental triage
equipment inspections in the field
jobsite progress review
parts/tools identification aid
virtual demos
visibility to component repairs in rebuild centers
used equipment inspections for quoting
product support rep technical support in the field
formal delivery process
three-way calls with customers to show additional work needed
