great golf in the poconos! our 18-hole golf course, touched by legendary architects william flynn and donald ross, offers an experience, unlike any round you've ever played. a cool and expansive layout with an old-school feel, you'll encounter quirky par-3 holes that will keep things fun and challenging along the way! warm-up and cool-down on our three-hole loop next to our practice range, available anytime. stop in and grab a cold beverage and quick snack at the clubhouse and come check out our golf shop equipped with all the necessary merchandise and where we prominently display our course’s long history. come tee off where giants like arnold palmer, sam snead, and our very own art wall jr. have played!for additional information on group golf, packages, or for any questions you may have, please reach out to tom stem, general manager, at tstem@oliphantcompanies.com or (570) 839-1389.
