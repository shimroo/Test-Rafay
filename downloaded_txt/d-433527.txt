welcomeon behalf of the town of halfmoon and the halfmoon town board, i would like to welcome you to our website.  halfmoon was founded in 1788 and is a scenic community with a current population of over 23,000 residents. halfmoon offers the “best of everything”. we are a growing community with excellent educational institutions, friendly neighborhoods, numerous historical sites, senior programs and recreational trails and parks that have many activities for individuals of all ages. i hope you find our website informative, user friendly and convenient. it is constantly being updated and improved and is intended to be your source to obtain useful information about town programs, meeting agendas and minutes, forms and more.  please take the time to subscribe and always be up-to-date on town and community happenings.  make sure to check out the community section on our updated webpage, which we hope you will find as a great resource for all things halfmoon! your halfmoon town team is constantly striving to work with our residents to provide additional services and programming.read more
