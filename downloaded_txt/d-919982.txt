the weddings at lake windsor


are you looking for beautiful wedding venues in madison, wi or surrounding areas? come to us for a venue that'll make your special day just as lovely as you've dreamed. of course, our space is ideal for more than just weddings. at lake windsor, we cover your event needs: we include personalized room set-up, clean-up of the room, servers, bartenders, white linen tablecloths, white or black linen napkins, white skirted and illuminated banquet tables (cake, gift, dj, buffets, name cards, head table, etc.), round dining tables, cocktail tables with crystal candles, banquet chairs, easels, all white china, cake cutting and serving, indoor microphone system, piped in cocktail and dinner music, complementary couple taste testing, an on-site event coordinator, and a head chef. we also offer wedding ceremonies and decorating rentals for your convenience!
at lake windsor country club, we've got everything you need to ensure your special day is memorable, fun, and goes off without a hitch. that's why we're one of the most sought-after wedding venues in madison, wi! even if all you have is questions or concerns regarding our services and what we can offer you, don't hesitate to reach out to us today. one of our friendly and knowledgeable team members would be happy to address them with you. if you're ready to get started with making reservations, give us a call or fill out our online form and we'll get back to you as soon as possible. learn more about what lake windsor has to offer. we look forward to hearing from you and making your event a success!
