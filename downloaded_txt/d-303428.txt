cultivating wise and virtuous citizensthrough a distinctive classical, christian educationat redeemer, dedicated faculty partner with parents to provide pk - 12th grade students with a quality education that prepares them for a lifetime of learning and leading well. our classical christian curriculum utilizes time-tested methods and content to train students to think critically, communicate winsomely, discern the truth, and promote meaningful change. redeemer touts both robust, college-preparatory academics as well as a nurturing christian community — a combination that develops wise and virtuous citizens who value and promote the good, the true and the beautiful. our mission >
