about

		northside christian school is located on 28 acres in st. petersburg, florida. we have been educating students from preschool through 12th grade since 1971. our goal is to provide a safe, nurturing environment where we partner with parents to help children reach their potential in academics, athletics and fine arts in a christ-centered environment.

welcome to northside christian school

northside christian school is a vibrant community where students learn, grow, and build lifelong friendships. recognized across florida for its exceptional academics and extracurricular programs, northside christian is a christ-centered school of excellence, fully accredited by advanced and acsi.
with an open enrollment philosophy, we welcome families from diverse backgrounds to experience an education grounded in biblical principles. at ncs, god’s word serves as the foundation for everything we do (ii timothy 3:16-17)








academics
we offer a variety of academic levels geared toward multiple learning styles -  from advanced placement and dual enrollment to the praxis program which helps students with learning difficulties to be successful in the classroom. 




athletics
our junior mustang sports program introduces athletics to students as early as preschool, providing preschool and elementary students with exposure to a wide variety of sports. at the middle school, junior varsity, and varsity levels, we offer 26 competitive sports teams, supporting students’ athletic growth and development. 




fine arts
in kindergarten and first grade, children begin learning to play the piano. band participation is required for all elementary students, fostering a strong foundation in music. our legendary, state champion marching band reflects our commitment to excellence. additionally, we offer a wide range of opportunities in voice, digital music, and performance arts at the elementary, middle, and high school levels.
