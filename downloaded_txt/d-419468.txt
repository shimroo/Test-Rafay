welcome to
m & s harley-davidson®
m & s harley-davidson®. chambersburg, pa dealership started out as pipers motor cycle sales, which sold english motorcycles (triumph and bsa) from 1953 to 1964. m & s cycles, established in 1964 by ennis moats and bob smith, sold triumph, bsa, ducati, and suzuki. m & s started with a 625 square foot building selling just 15 to 20 cycles per year. in 1976, harley-davidson® was added to it's product offering. m & s harley-davidson® (originally a division of m & s cycles), a family-owned dealership operated by darren and bryan moats, was established in 2001.
