at sands whitewater & scenic river trips our main concern is providing you with the rafting experience of a lifetime. we offer whitewater rafting trips as well as scenic rafting trips through the snake river, where our professional guides will showcase the spectacular scenery, wildlife, and history of the area. as one of the largest rafting companies in jackson hole, wy, you will find your perfect rafting experience.
