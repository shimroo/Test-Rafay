where original hospitality, creative culinary, & modern energy meetexperience the evolution of dining with portland city grill - where sophistication meets trendsetting cuisine.  sitting on the 30th floor of the us bank tower(the big pink), portland city grill boasts the best view in the city, with a spectacular panorama of the cascade mountains. sip a standout wine or craft cocktail with a fantastic bar scene with live music nightly. dazzle your palate with an imaginative blend of classic cuisine, with northwestern influences bestowed upon usda prime steaks, seafood, and an extensive sushi menu.
