venus by the sea condos

a family beach vacation favorite, venus condominiums is quietly situated just a short walk from white sandy beaches and the emerald green waters of the gulf of mexico. venus captures all of the warmth and charm of old florida, a true “home away from home,” with units overlooking the large grassy courtyard surrounding an over sized pool with sunbathing deck, picnic tables and a family-sized bbq grill. venus has several amenities to enjoy such as shuffle board, basketball and tennis courts. conveniently located in the heart of fort walton beach, venus is your destination for a vacation filled with memories... venus condos located in fort walton beach florida is a great family oriented vacation destination located steps from the gulf. proud to be part of the holiday isle properties family of vacation rentals here in fort walton beach and destin on holiday isle.
