welcome to the south carolina dental association!
the south carolina dental association represents member dentists in order to promote and provide optimal oral health care to all citizens of the state of south carolina by serving as an advocate to advance the profession of dentistry.

  the scda has over 2,000 member dentists in south carolina. we are the leader in the promotion of oral health care excellence and the advancement of the profession of dentistry in south carolina.
