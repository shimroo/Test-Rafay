選ばれ続ける魅力

福岡高校の強み










 






















世界を身近に、意見を発信する bringing the world closer to you and sending out your opinions県内唯一の英語コース  the only english course in toyama prefecture 

					世界を見つめる広い視野と、英語の４技能５領域【聞く、読む、話す（やりとり・発表）、書く】を存分に駆使して自分の考えや意見を発信できる能力を身に付けます。本校では、２名のalt（外国語指導助手）の助力を得て、少人数学習等によるきめ細かい指導を行っています。また、英語コース独自の多彩な行事を通して、実践的な英語力を鍛えます。

students acquire the ability to express their thoughts and opinions by making full use of the broad perspective of looking at the world and through the four skills of english (listening, reading, speaking (interaction/presentation), and writing). at our school, with the help of two alts (assistant language teachers), we provide detailed guidance through small-group learning. in addition, through the variety of events unique to the english course, you will train your practical english skills.				












 
詳しく見る


























考える力・表現する力を強化strengthen the ability to think and express生きる教養を育む文系コースliberal arts course nurtures living culture 

					人文科目をしっかりと学べるカリキュラム
一つ一つの事柄に対する「なぜ」「どうして」という疑問を大切にし、分析しながら根拠をもとに意見を論理的に構成していく探究活動が魅力です。国語力の醸成を軸に、様々な物事に対する思考力や表現力を高めることができます。the curriculum thoroughly converts humanities subjects. what is attractive about this research activity is that it places importance on asking “why” about each matter, and analyzes it while logically constructing an opinion based on evidence. focusing on the development of japanese language skills, you can improve your ability to think and express yourself on various topics.				












 
詳しく見る













探求心を高め、学びを深めるenhance your inquisitiveness and deepen your learningキャリアを描く理系コースscience course for a career 

					理数科目をしっかりと学べるカリキュラム。
専門科目をたくさん学び、豊富な経験をすることで、将来進みたい道や究めたい分野が
はっきり見えやすいことも理系コースの魅力です。
自然科学系の興味や教養を広げることができます。we have a curriculum that gives you a firm grasp of science and mathematics. by learning many specialized subjects and gaining a wealth of experience, you will be able to find the path you want to take in the future and the field you want to pursue. one of the appeals of science courses is that they are easy to translate into a career. you can broaden your interest and education in the natural sciences.
