we can finally call ourselves new yorkersover the past fifty years the new york arts program has placed over 4,500 students in theaters, museums, dance companies, publishing houses, literary agencies, performance venues, and with thousands of individual artists, writers and musicians.




    our story
  



what kind of artist are you?what kind of artist do you want to be?



at the new york arts program, we consider all of our students to be artists, whether they’re writers, designers, dancers, musicians, actors, art historians, filmmakers, dancers or visual artists. we meet you wherever you are along your journey as an artist, helping you construct the next steps to get you wherever you want to be. no one’s path is identical and a wide range of internship opportunities exist. each student’s internship placement is customized to suit their distinct curiosities, goals and experiences.
