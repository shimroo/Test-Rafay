creating a community we can all be proud of since 1932we are a community center based in stratford, connecticut. for 90 years, we have welcomed and served generations of children and adults. our mission is to create impactful & inclusive programs and services that engage and cultivate a thriving community.

what we do






youth athletics & clubs

we offer a wide range of extracurriculars for your child to engage with! all programs qualify for our financial assistance program. 











adult programs

pickleball, yoga, art; our adult programs are a low stress environment designed to connect our community members in a fun, new way! 











youth development

these are enrichment focused programs with an education foundation; summer camp, after school, & preschool! all programs qualify for our financial assistance program.











resource connection

from food pantry to holiday help; we aim to connect the dots, fill the gaps, and support you on your journey. 











volunteering

build a connection with your community by volunteering with us; rake for seniors, stock our food pantry, help throw our fundraising parties! 











special events

have fun and support our mission at the same time! join us at our fundraising parties! 






















financial aidwe have financial assistance available for all of our programs!
