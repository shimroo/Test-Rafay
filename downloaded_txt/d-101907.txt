money back guarantee
most mattress companies will offer a “money back guarantee” or replacement policy for your foam mattress. it is your responsibility to discover if the “guarantee” is valued for life, or expires after a few months. instead of offering the condition to refund your money, manufacturers also give consumers the ability to replace them mattress if they are not satisfied. yet again, the responsibility is on you to ask the duration of the replacement policy.

											can you tell me more about adjustable memory foam beds?
									 	

adjustable memory foam beds 
adjustable memory foam beds are generally used by someone who is enjoys sleeping in different positions, or cannot sleep on a regular mattress because of health reasons. the memory foam mattress ensures that anyone who has to lie down for an extended period, will not suffer from bed sores. 
adjustable beds are also excellent for situations where there is pain and swelling, especially in the feet. the memory foam bed can be bought to relieve pain and pressure as well as having the ability to adjust the mattress upwards to relieve swelling in the feet.

											how much does memory foam mattresses cost?
									 	

the price of memory foam mattresses
not all memory foam mattresses are created equally, therefore expect the price of each bed to vary compared to the manufacturer and the brand name. 
the first company to manufacture memory foam mattresses was tempur-pedic. they are considered the standard in the memory foam industry. expect to pay a little more for tempur-pedic brands of memory foam mattresses, which can costs from $600 and above. 
another type of memory foam mattress is manufactured by serta. these include a style of memory foam mattress such as the serta sleeper. serta memory mattresses can range from $200 to $1500.

											how can a memory foam bed improve my allergies?
									 	

say goodbye to allergies 
mattresses and linen, along with curtains and carpet, are the main areas where allergens collect and wreak havoc on your immune system. a memory foam bed will not trap the dust and pet dander that cause some people to have an allergic reaction. 
a memory foam is hypoallergenic because of the material it is constructed from, and the way the material is rearranged. regular mattresses are made of polyester, whereas memory will not contain any of these holes.

											how can memory foam help my neck problems?
									 	

memory foam and neck problems 
with the ability of memory foam to straighten the spine and reduce tension in the neck, purchasing one of these beds are ideal for anyone with chronic neck pain, headaches, recent neck surgery, or whiplash. chronic neck pain can cause a reduction in the amount and quality of sleep. you owe it to yourself to buy a quality memory foam bed to reduce your chronic neck pain. talk to your doctors about possible sleep aids, including memory foam pillows and memory foam body pillows.

											how can a memory foam bed help my back pain?
									 	

goodbye to back pain 
sufferers of chronic back pain can tell you how debilitating it is to live with this condition. usually people experiencing back pain will try almost anything to reduce their level of pain felt. 
memory foam beds are often used to help back pain sufferers obtain a decent nights sleep. these mattresses have the unique “body-contouring” ability. therefore, the mattress will distribute the weight of the person relieving the strain placed on the spine. a memory foam bed will absorb the heat from your body, and as the material stretches, these particular areas of the mattress will be much softer. the ability of memory foam to soften, and also support the spine, are bonuses for all back pain sufferers.
