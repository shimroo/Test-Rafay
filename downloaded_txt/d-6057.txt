welcome
      to www.cflradio.net home of the history of central florida radio. our tour
      will include stations whose signals reached  all of  central florida. i
      hope you enjoy your stay. and i hope we can evoke some memories for you and that you'll keep making many more 





any additional
      information, pictures and/or corrections are welcomed
      drop me a line at 
      cflradio1@gmail.com





this site would not have been achievable without the
      help, support and inspiration of my wife, wilma. 
      thank you to all of the folks who appear on these pages who have supplied
      stories, photos, sounds and other information. it's with their help
      this site can become a true history of central florida radio

      what's new? 







you'll find a new feature on the website. i
      have added links to radio station official websites and you can reach
      those by clicking on the station's logo. also i have a link to their
      internet stream if one is available





                
























        special note: dick
        camnitz (dick shane)
        was kind enough to start a site where we can all meet, discuss and
        share memories. dick and other folks have posted many photos and even
        sound clips. it's a place where you might find old friends and
        colleagues, and i'm sure will jog some memories. dick is a true
        historian and very serious researcher and i personally want to thank him
        for taking the time to add this great resource. i am sure this will
        enhance the cflradio website as well. check it out.
