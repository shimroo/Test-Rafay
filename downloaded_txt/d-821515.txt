orlando/disney area vacation rentals  
elite vacation homes offer the best selection of vacation rental pool homes and resort condos located in kissimmee, florida.  our luxury vacation rentals are conveniently located about 10 minutes from the walt disney world® resort, 10-20 minutes from universal studios orlando, seaworld orlando, florida's world famous beaches and close to all of central florida's many restaurants, world class golf, shopping and much more.

families & groups welcome 
our vacation rentals are perfect for families, sports groups, and reunions, who are looking for the benefits of a spacious rental home for the price of a hotel room! we have something for all budgets and tastes. 
 

best location 
all of our elite vacation homes are located about 10-20 minutes from central florida's major attractions, including the walt disney world® resort, universal studios florida®, seaworld orlando® and legoland®. 
 

superior value 
why pay for a small hotel room, when you can stay in a vacation rental for the same price? each of our vacation properties are privately owned and a great alternative to hotels. you’ll never want to stay in a hotel room again! 
 

expert property management 
our trusted expert property management team understands the market and will help you to manage your business. our proven techniques are designed for transparency to benefit the property owner. 
 

luxury amenities 
our 3-7 bedroom vacation rental pool homes offer a range of amenities like private screened pools and spas, game rooms, 2 master bedroom suites, televisions in every room, big-screen televisions, themed bedrooms, water views, high speed internet, fully equipped kitchens, washer & dryers, and spacious living room areas. selections of our pool homes are located in private gated communities and feature a community pool and clubhouse. 2-4 bedroom resort condo’s offer a choice of resort amenities like access to resort pools, children’s play areas, tennis, sand volleyball court, barbecue, gym facilities, community billiards/pool table, arcade games and much, much more.
