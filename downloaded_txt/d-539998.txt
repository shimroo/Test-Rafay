“enriching the lives of vulnerable children and youth in ukraine with hope, love, and inspiration.”










































































































































































































































































































































































































































































at ucare, we believe that every child and youth in ukraine deserves a future filled with hope, love, and inspiration. our mission is to empower vulnerable individuals through innovative programming that enriches their lives, nurtures their potential, and fosters resilience. we are dedicated to creating opportunities that uplift spirits, instill confidence, and build brighter futures. together, we strive to transform challenges into possibilities, ensuring that every young heart can dream, grow, and thrive.



what do we do?




    learn more
  




























strategic objective emphasis for 2025-2028 years approved by the board:given the ongoing traumatic impact of the war in ukraine, ucare strives to create a hopeful future for orphaned children and those who have lost parents due to the war. our mission is to nurture the emotional and mental well-being of these vulnerable groups with a specific emphasis on girls and young women during these challenging times, recognizing that the wounds of war run deep. mental health is crucial, especially in times of crisis, which is why we focus on equipping these young individuals with coping tools and self-care practices to help them heal and build mental resilience.  by empowering young women to discover their inner strength and transform pain into hope, we inspire a generation of future leaders ready to drive change and rebuild their communities. together, we create a ripple effect of healing, strength, and empowerment—one resilient heart at a time.
