jciahs pioneer day 2015 event photos by david voy


















here are some of david voy's favorite photos from the  jackson county iowa historical society pioneer day 2015 event.

all his photos can be seen along with many other area event photos on his official event photography website  http://davidvoy.photography/events/pioneer_day_2015/

david offers all his photos freely available for anyone to easily download and save.   

keep watching his voy.photos site or facebook page or twitter for updates on newly published photos from events.












lowell carlson giving a presentation about the history of bellevue, iowa at the jackson county iowa
 historical society pioneer day 2015 event.






lowell carlson (l) talking to trevis mayfield (r) at the jackson county iowa historical society pioneer day 2015 event.  
lowell carlson spoke about the history of bellevue, iowa.trevis mayfield is the new owner of the maquoketa sentinel-press, the bellevue herald-leader and the dewitt observer.

  festivities will begin at 10:00am with a beautiful array of classic
