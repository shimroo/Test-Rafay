about quality boats 



welcome to quality boats. 



welcome to quality boats! quality boats has been serving the boating community of tampa bay, florida since 1974. we are proud to offer the best products in the industry through our partnerships with grady-white, pursuit, regal, tiara yachts, barletta, crevalle and sea pro. quality boats is honored to be a hall of fame member of boating industry top 100 dealers in america.
we have four convenient locations on the west coast of florida; our main showroom in clearwater, waterfront marina and service center on clearwater beach, sarasota and charlotte harbor.
