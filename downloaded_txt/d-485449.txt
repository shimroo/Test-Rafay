we’ve been here a while & the party hasn’t stopped.



opened in 1931, the railroad pass casino is nevada’s longest running casino. we're not just a historic landmark—we're the spot where the good times never took a break!with beautifully renovated rooms at the attached ramada by wyndham hotel, our property is one of the most convenient locations to kick off your travel adventures to las vegas, boulder city, hoover dam, lake mead, and beyond!
