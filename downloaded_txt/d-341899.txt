annual town report cover photo contest 


the town of longmeadow is seeking your assistance in creating the cover to the 2024 annual report. please submit your original photo or drawing. here are the rules:						read on...







snow removal on sidewalks and around fire hydrants 


residents are reminded to please clear snow and ice from sidewalks within 24 hours after the precipitation ends. if you have a fire hydrant adjacent to your property, please ensure that it remains fully accessible after snow storms.						read on...







nomination papers are now available 


the annual town election is set for tuesday, june 3, 2025. residents interested in running for elected office may obtain nomination papers by contacting the town clerk’s office at townclerk@longmeadowma.gov or 413-565-4103 to schedule an appointment.						read on...







town managers newsletter - january 2025 


here is the january 2025 edition of the town managers newsletter. last months newsletter covered updates on a variety of topics and events. read the full newsletter here.						read on...







2025 annual town meeting 


the annual town meeting has been set for tuesday, may 13, 2025 at 7:00 p.m. in the lhs gymnasium. citizen petition articles, with certification by the town clerk, are due in the select board’s office no later than 4:30 p.m. on monday, january 27, 2025.						read on...







the town of longmeadow begins first phase of work to create a town-owned fiber network! 


the town of longmeadow is pleased to announce the commencement of work approved by voters at the 2024 fall special town meeting.						read on...







town treasurer's abandoned funds notice 


as per mgl chapter 200a sec 9a(c) the following is being posted on behalf of the town of longmeadow. "notice of names of persons appearing to be owners of funds held by the town of longmeadow, and deemed abandoned"						read on...
