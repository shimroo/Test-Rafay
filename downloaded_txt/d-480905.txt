                        about the ma movers association                    

the massachusetts movers association (mma) is a nonprofit trade association representing the moving and storage industry in massachusetts and other new england states. the mma encourages professionalism and integrity among its members.the members of the mma truly believe in blending old time values with the latest cutting edge technology to provide consumers with the moving or storage experience they so richly deserve.we strongly encourage consumers to hire mma members, and we equally reach out to new england movers and storage businesses to join our association and benefit by the educational, legislative, networking, ethical, business and training opportunities we provide.
