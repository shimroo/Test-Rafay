improving lives by connecting and mobilizing community resources














we envision a strong, equitable, and accessible network of support for working households in harrisonburg and rockingham county.




    learn more
  































our workwe connect you to the issues you care most about and empower you to make an impact, right here in our community.




    learn more
  




















get involvedwe strive to improve the lives of people across harrisonburg and rockingham county but we can't do it alone. all it takes is to give, advocate or volunteer. how will you live united?




    learn more
  





















give



when you make a donation to united way of harrisonburg and rockingham county, you help local people make ends meet by giving them access to quality childcare, affordable housing, and reliable transportation.




    donate
  








volunteer



give the gift of you. volunteer!united way of harrisonburg and rockingham county engages more than 800 community volunteers each year.  




    learn more 
  








advocate



your voice is a valuable tool that you can use to get involved. advocating for a cause you are passionate about can go a long way.
