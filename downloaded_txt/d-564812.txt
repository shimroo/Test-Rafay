				optical design software and services
			
		



advanced solutions for imaging, illumination, automotive lighting, photonics, and more


which solution is right for you?
























































			
				code v
			
		



design, optimize, and fabricate reliable imaging optics with the most powerful ray tracing and optical design software available.

















































			
				lighttools
			
		



model, simulate, optimize, and visualize illumination optics with a complete illumination design software environment.
















































			
				imsym
			
		



enable end-to-end simulation of imaging systems on this industry-first platform, powered by code v and lighttools.
















































			
				lucidshape
			
		



design exterior automotive lighting systems and simulate night driving in real time.

				rsoft photonic device tools
			
		



enhance your designs with rsoft photonic device tools, offering the industry's most extensive suite of simulators and optimizers for both passive and active nanoscale devices, as well as metaoptics.
















































			
				optical scattering measurements
			
		



augment optical simulations with precision light scattering data obtained with our advanced hardware and services.
















































			
				optical engineering services and consulting
			
		



let us help you solve your optical design challenges with expert, cost-effective imaging, illumination, and systems engineering services.
