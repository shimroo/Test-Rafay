									milton keynes heritage association - mkha - formed in 1994 by a number of local heritage groups and museums, with the aim of encouraging and developing co-operation between organisations having an interest in heritage in milton keynes, north buckinghamshire and south northamptonshire. it also has a central role in promoting heritage, by working closely with councils and other agencies.
