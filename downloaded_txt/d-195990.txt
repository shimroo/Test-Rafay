the #1 personal chef service in north america
hireachef.com is the premier personal chef directory brought to you by the united states personal chef association. since 1991, our uspca member chefs have provided exceptional culinary experiences to clients throughout the united states and canada. with a wide range of culinary skills and specialties, our chefs are equipped to meet customers’ dietary and nutritional needs.

your personal chef can provide you with the following:

weekly meals that fit your dietary and nutritional needs.
fine in-home dining for special events for couples and groups.
dining options for seniors and those with special diets.
food and beverage pairing events.
cooking lessons.
vacation home cooking
vacation meal preparation.

why use our service
we provide the largest and most comprehensive platform for connecting people with affordable and healthy eating alternatives through professional personal chefs in their area. our user-friendly directory website makes finding and hiring a personal chef easy and informative.
