sun and sandals tours has been established since 1995. it is a combination of extensive retail and wholesale experience. sun and sandals tours started as “the island specialists”. over the years we have traveled the globe researching and visiting a vast number of different islands.
in and around 2002 it commenced a specialized division dealing with destination weddings. we have arranged numerous weddings across the globe.
in 2012 we also commenced christian pilgrimages a site dedicated to church groups and those seeking a soulful journey and christian pilgrimage to the holy land, israel, lourdes, jordan, turkey, and greece.
