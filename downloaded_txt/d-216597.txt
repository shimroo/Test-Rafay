learn about mtbr




    about
  



since 1990, middle tennessee boxer rescue has worked to rescue, re-home, and educate about the boxer breed in middle tennessee.if you are interested in fostering, adopting, supporting, donating, or educating yourself about mtbr; you have come to the right place!














ways to help










adopt & foster

we are always looking for adopters or foster folks!  please apply here and don’t be put off by the forms,  we work hard to make sure all of our dogs go to the perfect homes!




application











volunteer

middle tennessee boxer rescue is a 100% volunteer based non-profit organization.  we rely on the good hearts within the rescue community, regional community and local community.




learn more











donate

middle tennessee boxer rescue offers many avenues to support our mission.  each dollar is greatly appreciated and put to good use to support the rescue, rehabilitation and re-homing of the boxer breed.

mtbr’s top dog sponsor



happy retales pet supply and more, a pet retail and grooming shop in brentwood, tn, was founded eight years ago with the mission of providing funds for animal rescue and adoption. in fact, 100% of the store's profits are donated to animal rescue. mtbr is fortunate to be one of the rescues receiving a portion of the profits. visit happy retales at 101 creekside crossing, suite #700, brentwood, tn.
