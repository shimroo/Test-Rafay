about usthe reagan years is the east coast's original 80's tribute band.  talented musicians performing 80's pop, 80's rock, 80's new wave and 80's heavy metal greatest hits of the m-tv era.  every song was a massive hit and you will sing along to every song!  
formed in 1996, the reagan years has delighted hundreds of sold out crowds throughout the maryland, pennsylvania, virginia, delaware, west virginia and dc area. the groups has also performed corporate events as far away as hawaii along with foreign countries such as germany, spain, mexico, jamaica and republic of panama.  whether you see the group in a nightclub, festival, or need a band for your private event, corporate gala or wedding reception, the reagan years is the life of the party! the reagan years is an amazing 1980s tribute band that plays your favorite 1980s hits!
