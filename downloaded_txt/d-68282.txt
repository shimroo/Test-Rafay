charming extended-stay apartments
in manhattan’s iconic upper east side
welcome to 1871 house, an extended-stay experience nestled in a classic 19th-century townhouse at the southern end of the upper east side’s luxurious lenox hill neighborhood, placing you within easy reach of midtown. please note: we require a minimum stay of 30 days, ensuring our guests enjoy an extended retreat in the heart of new york city.  our studio and one-bedroom apartments provide a blend of privacy and convenience in one of manhattan’s most desirable locations.  surrounded by picturesque blocks of historic prewar buildings, brownstones, and grand townhouses, 1871 house offers a retreat that embodies the elegance and old-world glamour of new york’s upper east side. each apartment or suite can comfortably accommodate one or two guests only. 
designed as a cross between apartments, hotels, and traditional bed-and-breakfasts, our furnished rentals offer the look and feel of a sophisticated english inn, balanced with the vibrancy of manhattan just steps away. here, antique details and inviting décor combine to create an intimate atmosphere that feels like home—a tranquil retreat amidst the energy of the city.
each apartment offers a unique blend of historic charm and modern comfort
our apartments feature thoughtfully curated details, including:

high ceilings and sun-filled rooms in most suites, with select rooms overlooking the lovely east 62nd street.
original architectural elements, such as carved marble fireplaces, decorative ironwork, and a selection of antique beds, including charming four-posters.
well-equipped kitchens in several suites, while others offer kitchenettes or convenient amenities like fridges and microwaves for light meal prep.

each suite is designed to provide a cozy, refined atmosphere—perfect for unwinding after a day in the city.
prime location near nyc’s landmarks and cultural centers
situated within the luxurious lower lenox hill neighborhood, 1871 house offers easy access to central park, the metropolitan museum of art, moma, the frick museum, premier shopping, fine dining, and top medical centers. in addition to these nearby attractions, guests can explore manhattan’s exclusive decoration & design building (d&d building) and manhattan art and antiques center, both of which draw architects, designers, and collectors from around the world with their remarkable collections of design showrooms and galleries.
whether you’re here for business, medical needs, or a leisurely stay, our location provides convenient access to midtown and puts you at the heart of new york’s vibrant culture and amenities.
why choose 1871 house?
1871 house offers more than a standard stay. blending the comfort of a private apartment with the elegance of a boutique hotel, each residence showcases:

elegant shabby chic and ralph lauren-inspired interiors
ornate moldings, parquet wood floors, and oriental rugs
charming common areas with fireplaces and cozy seating

ideal for professionals, medical visitors, corporate relocations, and extended vacations, 1871 house appeals to guests who value historic charm, architectural beauty, and serene privacy amidst new york’s energetic pulse.
experience iconic nyc moments
imagine living in an upper east side apartment that brings to life scenes from gossip girl, sex and the city, and breakfast at tiffany’s. designed in the italianate style by renowned architect john sexton, our townhouse echoes the timeless elegance of classic new york brownstones, giving your stay a truly cinematic feel.
central to midtown & medical facilities
located on a quiet, tree-lined block between park and lexington avenues, 1871 house offers:

convenient access to midtown, lenox hill hospital, newyork-presbyterian / weill cornell medical center, the mount sinai hospital and other world-renowned medical centers
proximity to madison and fifth avenue shops, museum mile, and leading arts institutions like carnegie hall and lincoln center.

from dining and shopping to museums and theaters, everything you need is right around the corner, with easy access to subway lines, colleges, and cultural landmarks. enjoy the perfect balance of city excitement and residential tranquility in a place that feels like home.
