welcome to killer creek harley-davidson®

killer creek harley-davidson® -- an all-american harley-davidson® dealership nestled in the heart of georgia -- is your ultimate destination for harley-davidson® bikes, service, parts, financing and all things related to this incredible motorcycle brand. we strive to earn your satisfaction in every facet of our business, and we firmly believe in our hometown roots while delivering an experience that always matches the stellar harley-davidson® reputation. we look forward to seeing you soon!

new & used harley® sales in roswell, ga
enjoy undiscovered riding adventures when you bring home a new harley-davidson® or used bike from killer creek harley-davidson®. explore our new motorcycle inventory for a collection of robust and captivating harley-davidson® motorcycles that are sure to turn heads. we also house an extensive pre-owned motorcycle collection, offering powerful and reliable used harley® models that feel as good as new. if you're set to make a purchase, our harley-davidson® financing team is ready to go through your options and help you save by showing you our harley-davidson® specials.

killer creek harley-davidson® service, parts & accessories
already own a harley-davidson® you love? preserve its lifespan by frequently visiting killer creek harley-davidson® for maintenance, upgrades or parts. our motorcycle service department professionals possess an unmatched combined harley-davidson® expertise and offer stellar customer service while understanding the precious value of your bike. if you prefer doing the tune-ups on your own, visit our harley-davidson® parts department for every component you need.

visit our roswell harley® dealer today!
positioned conveniently in roswell, just a short ride from atlanta and other nearby towns, our local harley-davidson® dealership caters proudly to the georgia community and even those visiting across state lines from chattanooga and beyond. if you have a passion for american-engineered bikes and live for the open road, don't wait -- visit killer creek harley-davidson® today!
