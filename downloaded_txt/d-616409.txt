    what we do
  














new zealand natural ice cream limited is an international premium ice cream company.established in 1985, new zealand natural ice cream limited owns market leading brands includingnew zealand natural, killinchy gold, zilch and chateau. our products are distributed across a number of channels such as grocery, food service and ice cream parlours. this includes strong partnerships with some of the largest retailers in the world. we have been consistently awarded gold and best in category medals from the new zealand ice cream manufacturers association. currently exporting to more than 20 countries, our head office is located in auckland, new zealand.
