look who’s on the news!















paint a miracle is an art studio uniquely designed to offer people living with disabilities the opportunity to explore the visual arts in an inspiring and innovative environment.




























classes



paint a miracle's staff takes care to develop curriculum and adaptations to ensure that all artists have an enriching and enjoyable fine arts experience. artists work in a variety of mediums, including painting, drawing, ceramics, and fiber arts. paint a miracle is a place of independence, artistic freedom and inspiration - a place where one can enjoy the creative process amongst friends.read more →















exhibitions



paint a miracle provides its artists with a number of opportunities to exhibit their work, including venues such as the grand rapids art museum, paint creek center for the arts, and the rochester library. works are also exhibited and sold at the bizzy buzz artisan market in downtown rochester.















store



one of paint a miracle’s goals is to present our artists with opportunities to sell their work. this provides our artists with income as well as a sense of accomplishment. visit store →
















encourage creativity promote dignity



paint a miracle strives to build a supportive and encouraging community of artists that recognizes the important contribution artists with disabilities bring to the world. please help us continue to build this community and enable more people to become part of it.
