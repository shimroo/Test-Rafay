astral voyage: what is it about?


whether you call it astral projection, the out-of-body experience, remote viewing, or even lucid dreaming, here you'll find articles to help you achieve, understand and improve this alternate state of being. there are also articles on spirituality, as you will find that the deeper you go into astral projection, the more you'll naturally start to awaken to a new perspective on life and self.


anne has been interviewed by steve g. jones, owner of theartofastralprojection.com, on the following topic: winning the lottery with astral projection interview with anne moss, something she also wrote about in the article winning the lottery at psychic-experiences.com. the interview is about 40 minutes.


astral projection
what is the nature of astral projection and out of body experiences (obes)? how to have your own experience, and what to expect? here you'll find numerous articles on the subject.

general articles and opinion pieces on the subject of astral projection, out of body experiences (obes), near death eperiences (ndes), lucid dreaming and the astral planes.
