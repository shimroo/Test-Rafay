welcome to green cactus grill


we are a family-owned mexican restaurant that operates in 4 locations throughout suffolk and nassau county.











authentic mexican and latino cuisine


if you are craving authentic mexican food, you've come to the right place! with over 24 years of experience, green cactus grill serves a wide variety of mexican and latino dishes that are freshly prepared everyday.
rest assured that all of our meals are available at affordable prices.



meals are freshly prepared everyday










excellent customer service


green cactus grill is a warm and welcoming family restaurant where you can enjoy a memorable dining experience. whether you like your food spicy, mild or have any dietary requirements, we source the finest and most aromatic ingredients to prepare each mexican dish. our expert chefs take your tastes and preferences into consideration while preparing your meals.
