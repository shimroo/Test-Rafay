our mission
cummings mobility conversions & supply inc. has been providing conversions and adaptive devices for the physically
                            challenged for over 30 years. the manufacturers we represent are the most respected in the industry, they
                            include braunability, ricon, and bruno among others. we provide quality accessibility to both commercial and
                            private customers. you will be able to speak with one of our experienced mobility consultants to assist you
                            in choosing the products that fit your lifestyle. check out our local wheelchair van pages.
our service/warranty department is staffed with factory-certified technicians to service your vehicle or
                            product and get it corrected often times under warranty.
