stuck on maui is a small business specializing in cut vinyl stickers. all of our decals are made from automotive grade vinyl for outdoor and indoor use. our best sellers include turtle stickers and hawaiian flower stickers. we offer free shiping monday through friday. place your order with confidence, we have 100% positive feedback from thousands of happy customers. we started making stickers back in 2002!
choose from our product list to the right or click the “shop” button at the top to see all of our sticker designs. please note that all of our designs are federally copyrighted with the library of congress. please don’t borrow our sticker designs for your projects, it’s illegal. contact us if you have a need for one or more of our designs. we are happy to help.
