leaders in primary care & endocrinology
stanford medicine partners in emeryville has been caring for patients in the east bay since 1991. we specialize in preventive medicine and the diagnosis and treatment of adults and older teens. we have specialists in endocrinology and internal medicine, and we can provide access to clinical trials at stanford health care as needed. we partner with stanford health care to give you the best health care possible: individualized and convenient with access to stanford specialists and technology.
