welcome to bartram trail golf club!



a beautiful, rolling golf course located in columbia county, georgia




    book a tee time
  


























a beautiful, rolling golf course located in columbia county, georgia, just minutes away from augusta – one of the great meccas of the golfing world. bartram trail is the only public golf course in columbia county to offer mini verde fine-bladed ultra dwarf bermuda grass and feature five par 5’s and five par 3’s.














we are now accepting group outings, along with requests for weekly golf leagues with a limited number of memberships.private golf lessons can be scheduled by appointment with each of our professional instructors:keith walker  |  doug cameron | chip deasonall three are certified teaching professionals that enjoy working with novice to advanced players; while focusing specifically on the player’s improvement and sustained success.experience the course, wildlife, and southern hospitality from our friendly staff.
