 a birthstone is the "gemstone associated with
                            the date of one's birth, the wearing of which is commonly thought to bring good luck or
                            health. supernatural powers have long been attributed by astrologers to certain
                            gemstones."

birthstones come in a colorful array of sparkling jewels and glowing gemstones, each one with
