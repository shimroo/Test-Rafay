the church of the covenant is a diverse congregation in the vibrant cultural center of university circle in cleveland, ohio.keep an eye on our calendar for special events! learn more about joining us for worship here.we are proudly led by our pastor rev. jessie macmillan.





























































“home is a place where you are loved and you love unconditionally. covenant has accepted me with all my funny quirks, and my closest friendships are within the covenant community.”– jenny conner














“most importantly, i know covenant is a congregation of caring. no matter my own self-doubts and failings, i know people care about me and appreciate me. covenant is a rock on which to stand during the current storm of life.”– joan drushel
