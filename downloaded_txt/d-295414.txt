welcome to treasure island yacht club



est. 1966 | gateway to the san francisco bay






















membership

tiyc is now accepting new members! we welcome power and sailing boaters interested in racing, social activities, and cruising. members enjoy access to our facilities, a variety of regattas and cruises, and social events at our treasure island clubhouse.




learn more











events & cruise ins

tiyc welcomes visitors and guests from reciprocating yacht clubs and picya affiliates. visitors may cruise in by yacht or by land. our lighthouse bar is open during the season on saturdays and sundays from 12-5pm (no reservation required). 




learn more











visit us

treasure island is going through a historic transformation! there are a number of ways to visit treasure island, whether you arrive by boat, car, or public transportation. learn more about visiting treasure island!
