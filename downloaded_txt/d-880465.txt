       professional and experienced engineering company







   
anderson engineering consultants, inc. is an arkansas corporation specializing in geotechnical/environmental engineering and quality assurance/control testing for the construction industry. aeci is a multi-discipline, full-service firm with offices in
little rock and jonesboro, arkansas, and a staff of over 50 personnel, including; engineers, geologists, technicians, and drillers. aeci is recognized by arkansas business weekly as one of the top engineering firms in the state of arkansas, satisfying clients on over 600 projects across the united states each year.



      we support our industry through our company's memberships in organizations such as the american society of civil engineering, national water well association, and association of firms practicing in the geosciences, just to name a few. the principals of the firm are members of the american society for testing and materials (astm), one of whom serves on the d.18 committee.
