welcome to the denton county tax officethis site is made available as a convenience to our customers. we offer online payment options for property taxes and motor vehicle registration renewals. we hope that you will find this website a useful tool. please feel free to call us or visit any one of our 6 locations for personal service.missionto ensure that your tax dollars are accurately and efficiently collected with the utmost integrity and honesty.goalto exceed your expectations by ensuring the highest level of service possible using technology, tools and services to assist our taxpayers in transacting business with our office.mottoservice with excellence.paymentsplease make checks payable to dawn waye, denton county tax assessor/collector.we also accept:checksdiscovermastercardvisaconvenience feesfree e-check. credit card transactions are subject to a convenience fee up to 2.25% on property tax and motor vehicle transactions.receipts of fundsinternet property tax accounts are paid pending receipt of funds from the financial institution.



 
 




 


 
 




public noticepursuant to tax code section 1.085 the office of the denton county tax assessor-collector offers taxpayers the option of receiving their property tax statement via email. to enter into an agreement to receive electronic property tax statements, please complete the following form 50-801 and 50-843.please be aware that wait times may be longer than usual when conducting business in our offices.  we value your time and know how important it is to complete your transactions.  in most cases, you can renew your registration and pay your property taxes online. e-checks are free *credit cards have a 2.25% convenience fee with a $2 minimum.
