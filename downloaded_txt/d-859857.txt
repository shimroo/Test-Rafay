expertise in forestry workers’ compensation



for over 50 years, the wood products industry has counted on forestry mutual to protect their businesses and employees so they can get back to work.forestry mutual covers medical bills, lost wages, and return-to work assistance. they have proven injury management programs and a loss control team of experts that give hands-on training and safety advice to control your costs and provide the best possible outcome.for more information, please call 800-849-7788. be sure to tell them you’re a tennessee forestry association member.
