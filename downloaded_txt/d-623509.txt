a catholic community made for you.



at mary immaculate, we can’t wait to walk this journey with you. from life to death, we offer sacramental graces, blessings, ceremonies, ministries, and more. with the richness and beauty of the catholic church combined with lived experience, we are here to serve you and act as a reminder that you are not meant to do this alone.




    i'm new
  



















































prayat mary immaculate, you are invited into a community of prayer and faith. our personal relationship with the lord is the most important aspect of our lives. we want to make prayer easy and accessible for you. pray with us.















growat mary immaculate, we understand that we are made for community. in a beautiful community, we can thrive in our relationship with god and all those around us. grow with us.















serveat mary immaculate, you are invited to serve with us. in our response to the poor to saturday morning catechesis, we are each given a call to serve his church. serve with us.
