welcome to burgers motorcycles online. burgers motorcycles has been serving the powersports community since 1958. opened originally by dick and liz burger in baptistown, new jersey. it was moved to its present location at 749 rt. 202 three bridges in 1965. the burgers retired and moved to florida in july of 1969 turning the dealership over to the present owners at that time. currently franchised for honda, suzuki, kymco, snapper, snapper pro, motor trike and simplicity, we are always on the alert for quality products to move on into the future. some of the marques that were represented through the years included b.s.a., norton, matchless, ariel, ducati, moto guzzi, ossa, premier, and sachs. many of these brands have disappeared, but burgers is still prospering. learn more about us
