the city of valparaiso will hold a public meeting at 6:00 pm on tuesday, february 25, 2025, in valparaiso city hall presenting the american with disabilities act (ada) transition plan draft.						read on...







valpo discontinues frost law beginning february 10, 2025 		



with temperatures and precipitation stabilizing, the city of valparaiso discontinues the frost laws beginning monday, february 10, 2025.						read on...
