    welcoming families from bucks county, montgomery county and the surrounding areas

at rainbow academy for little scholars, inc., located at 1240 grenoble road, ivyland, pa, we provide enriching and engaging programs tailored to toddlers, preschoolers, kindergartners, and elementary-aged children. with our before and after school programs and vacation day trips, your child's education never stops! call us at
(215) 355-6498 to schedule a tour and experience a nurturing environment where children play, create, and explore.

  a nature-inspired learning environment in bucks county

rainbow academy offers a one-of-a-kind educational setting on a scenic five-acre farm where children engage with gardens, greenhouses, farm animals, playgrounds, and pools. our peaceful, nature-filled surroundings foster creativity, curiosity, and exploration‚Äîvital elements to childhood development.convenient access from newtown, churchville, richboro, southampton, doylestown, warminster, warrington, horsham, hatboro and the surrounding areas.



  





    uniquely designed spaces for your child's growth

our thoughtfully designed "schoolhouse" is built with young children in mind, providing the perfect blend of fun and educational opportunities. each classroom caters to different age groups, promoting a hands-on learning experience. the low staff-to-child ratio ensures every child receives individual attention and support, making them feel valued in our community.

    a young boy is watering flowers with a watering can.
  






    







  


    exceptional early childhood education standards¬†

at rainbow academy, our curriculum aligns with the pennsylvania learning standards and the core curriculum. we keep detailed portfolios for each child, tracking their developmental milestones and sharing progress with parents during conferences. as a keystone stars 4 preschool and daycare, we are licensed by the department of education, private academic schools, and accredited by the national association for the education of young children (naeyc).



  


    inclusivity and educational support

we believe in creating an inclusive learning environment. our programs are specially designed to accommodate children with special needs, ensuring that every child is respected and given the chance to thrive within our community.

  convenient location and flexible hours

conveniently located just minutes from doylestown, newtown, jamison, richboro, southampton, and warwick, rainbow academy is easily accessible for families across bucks county. our hours of operation‚Äîfrom 7:00 am to 5:30 pm‚Äîare designed to fit your busy schedule.
