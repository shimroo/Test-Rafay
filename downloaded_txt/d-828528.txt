we’re growing for you.



originally a landscape design and installation company, the perennial farm's landscape roots and over 45 years of gardening expertise allow us to provide our customers with the highest-quality plant material, the best customer service, and the fastest delivery.  at the perennial farm, we're growing for you!




    learn more
  


























































































experience rooted in over 40 years of business.



perennials, ornamental grasses, hardy ferns, ground cover, flowering shrubs & vines and much more are grown here at the farm and shipped directly to your door. our plant material arrives quickly and beautifully - landscape-ready for the shelves at your independent garden center or for immediate installation at your job site.


























over 1,200 varieties to suit various needs.



through many years of combined education and expertise, we have expanded our selection to over 1,000 varieties of plants. each variety is tested both on-site and with our growing partners to ensure our customers receive the highest-quality plant material we can offer.




    learn more
  


























the delivery specialists.



from north carolina to maine… on one-way racks or in boxes… using our trucks or common-carrier…we get you the plant material you want when you need it!
