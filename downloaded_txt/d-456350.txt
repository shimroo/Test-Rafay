we have a directory for locating free 3d models, our 3d model resource page is very handy if you 
are a 3d artist looking for software, or that special model. some of the 3d models that can be 
found there include poser, studio max, dxf, obj., lightwave, bryce and maya. 
we design and build websites, some of the types include, airbrushing, automotive, business 
directories, chamber of commerce, churches, photographers, product resale, real estate, and 
resorts to name a few. we create product labels, magazine ads, logos, album cover art, video 
commercial presentations, and 3d animation. sample 3d art and product packaging can be found 
in our gallery at digital dream designs.
digital dream designs offers a lot of free stuff, everything from classified ads to wholesalers. we 
encourage you to explore and hope you enjoy your visit. feel free to drop us an email if you need 
help with anything.
