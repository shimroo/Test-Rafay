columbus fire department equipped with ballistic vests! 


the columbus fire department recently took delivery of 20 ballistic vests that were previously approved for purchase at the oct. 7, 2024 city council meeting.						read on...







city and keep columbus beautiful partner for new community recycling effort 


the city of columbus is collaborating with local nonprofit keep columbus beautiful to strengthen residential recycling efforts in the community.						read on...







longtime businessman/pharmacist dick tooley reflects on years of service on bid board 


dick tooley has seen the trackside district-downtown columbus blossom and evolve tremendously since he began working at his family’s tooley’s drug & homecare in 1958. but his love for it and the columbus community as a whole has never changed.						read on...







city parks and rec take spotlight of 1st city coffee chat of 2025 


mother nature is expected to deliver blasts of winter weather this and next week, but the city of columbus' parks and rec hopes to soon heat things up with excitement about plans for the future.						read on...







columbus public library's newest annual report highlights growth and success 


the columbus public library has seen tremendous growth and success in the last year.						read on...







press release: longtime sergeant to serve as interim police chief 


columbus, nebraska - columbus police sgt. bret strecker has accepted the position of interim chief of the columbus police department, effective today, feb. 6, 2025.						read on...







notice of 3 upcoming public hearings

city public works director urges caution on roads amid winter weather

cpd welcomes marine corps veteran as new officer recruit 


the columbus police department continues to press on after chief charles sherer recently announced his plans to retire, as another new police officer recruit was introduced to the city council on monday night.						read on...







columbus public library gearing up for return of its winter carnival this weekend!
