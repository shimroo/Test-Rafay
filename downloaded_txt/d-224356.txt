dbt product information (windows and mac)
duxbury systems leads the world in software for braille. the duxbury braille translator (dbt)
is used by virtually all of the world's leading braille publishers. 
no one supports more languages than duxbury systems. dbt supports
over 180 languages in either uncontracted or contracted (when such rules exist) braille.
our software can produce contracted and uncontracted literary, mathematics, and technical braille.
