welcome to cabo san lucas, baja california sur, mexico for november 2010. since 1996 carlos fiesta's goal with cabo expo has been to provide cabo san lucas visitors with information on this spectacular seaside vacation destination before heading south of the border. carlos has been researching cabo since the 1970's and he continues to visit the cape regularly to update this web site. 
with all of the spectacular beauty and natural resources this special corner of paradise has to offer, not to mention the great weather and warm ocean temperatures, it's no wonder that cabo san lucas is one of the most popular vacation destinations on the planet. ready for an adventure? pour yourself a margarita, grab your mouse and begin your adventure to cabo san lucas to cabo expo right here with carlos fiesta!
