is mediation right for me? divorce mediation: a smarter alternative to court
mediation offers a cost-effective and collaborative way to resolve divorce disputes with the help of a neutral attorney mediator. whether simple or complex, mediation keeps you in control of decisions and avoids the stress, delays, and expense of court proceedings.
key benefits of mediation:

save time and money: avoid costly legal battles.
maintain privacy: handle sensitive issues discreetly.
work for all cases: effective for both straightforward and complex situations.
custom solutions: address property, assets, pensions, and custody collaboratively.

meet the team about divorce help
founded in 1989 by forward-thinking california attorneys, divorce help was created as an alternative to the adversarial divorce process. instead of the traditional approach—where spouses face off in court with expensive legal teams—our model empowers individuals to navigate divorce with confidence, self-determination, and cost efficiency.
our team of attorney mediators, private judges, and paralegals is dedicated to providing high-quality legal support tailored to your unique needs. we specialize in divorce mediation, consultation, and customized legal service packages, allowing you to manage your divorce or legal separation in a collaborative and less stressful way.
over the years, we’ve helped thousands of individuals take control of their divorce process, ensuring they achieve fair outcomes while saving time, money, and emotional energy. with divorce help, you’ll have experienced legal professionals guiding you every step of the way as you transition into the next chapter of your life.
