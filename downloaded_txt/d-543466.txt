welcome to schrader's   






schrader honda yamaha suzuki in yorkton is saskatchewan's largest powersports dealer serving customers from as far away as regina, winnipeg, saskatoon, swift current and weyburn. we carry a huge inventory of new and used atvs, utvs, motorcycles, and snowmobiles and the accessories to go with them!
use the search bar above to check out our powersports inventory. schrader's also offers our customer complete parts, service and accessories departments. whether you just need a part to do the repair yourself or want us to fix it for you, our talented team can help you out.
we are sasktchewan's largest parts and accessories dealer for motocross, motorcycle, snowmobile and atv parts, accessories and clothing.
we carry a wide variety or clothing, helmet, tires, exhaust and luggage rack brands from fox racing, oakley, ogio, shift, monster, hjc, reeves, and more! remember, we ship anywhere in canada!
are you a do-it-yourselfer? visit our parts department and order your part. or if want us to do the repair for you, email us to inquire about booking your service appointment.
