about us 



all-spec enclosures, inc. – unveiling excellence since 1998innovating enclosures, forging relationships 









our journeyat the heart of all-spec enclosures beats the spirit of innovation and a relentless pursuit
of excellence. born in 1998, our journey commenced with a dream - to revolutionize
the world of enclosures. over the years, that dream has evolved into a resounding reality. 



pioneers in mesh and steel enclosureswe stand tall as trailblazers in the realm of mesh and steel enclosure manufacturing.
our craftsmanship, honed to perfection over decades, is a testament to our unwavering commitment to innovation, quality, and exceeding customer expectations. 



where quality meets innovationstep inside our 15,000 square foot manufacturing haven, and you’ll discover more than machines; you’ll find the embodiment of precision and craftsmanship. our cutting-edge equipment breathes life into enclosures that are not just functional but pieces of art. 



from vision to realityimagine your vision taking shape, from a prototype to a full-scale production run. at all-spec enclosures, we bridge the gap between dreams and reality. with our in-house design and dedicated customer service team, your project is in the hands of true artisans. 



a network of excellenceour influence stretches far and wide. with distribution centers strategically planted in
cerritos, california, sacramento, california, and phoenix, arizona, we’ve woven a tapestry of excellence that blankets the nation. 





 





your vision, our passioneach enclosure we create is more than just a product; it’s a testament to our passion for excellence. we’re not just crafting steel and mesh; we’re sculpting trust, innovation, and partnerships that stand the test of time. 



get ready to experience excellencewelcome to all-spec enclosures, where dreams take shape, quality finds its home, and innovation has no boundaries. we’re not just building enclosures; we’re crafting legacies. join us on this extraordinary journey towards excellence.
