welcome
hello and thank you for stopping by our website.all items ordered are sent using australia post trackable envelopes or satchels.
patchwork by sea was originally established in 1998 in a small shop on jetty rd brighton.patchwork by sea provides a very wide and comprehensive resource for quilter’s, including a huge range of fabrics, threads, patterns and books.
we offer a comprehensive range of block of the months which is constantly being added to.we look forward to helping you with your purchases here on our website, or meeting you when you come to visit our shop.
