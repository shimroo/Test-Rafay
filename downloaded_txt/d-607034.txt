about us



























dermagraphics by miller is a premier permanent make up practice located in erie, pa, specializing in the art of permanent makeup. our services include permanent eyebrows, eyeliner, eyelash tinting, lips, beauty marks, freckles, scar camouflage, nipple and areola tattooing, and scalp shading. we also offer natural eyebrow grooming for both men and women.stephanie miller, owner and certified derma technician, is passionate about helping clients enhance their natural beauty with safe and long-lasting results. we use only the highest quality pigments and state-of-the-art techniques to ensure your satisfaction.whether you aspire to wake up with perfect eyebrows, have a more defined lip line, or conceal scars, we are dedicated to helping you attain your desired look.
