  resume examples from quest careerquest career has gathered sample resumes written by professional resume writers that you can use as a guide or resume template to help you with your job search.as you develop your resume, be sure it includes keywords relevant to the type of position you are targeting.to develop your keyword list, review two or three job postings for the type of target position that interest you. identify keywords that appear in these job postings to create a keyword list.most employers use software applications that allow them to find, screen, and score resumes using a keyword matching feature.to help you do well with these automated resume screening tools used by most employers, incorporate your keyword list into your resume bullets.thanks to outplacement provider quest outplacement for supporting those pursuing career transitions by supplying sample resumes for our site.resume examples:
