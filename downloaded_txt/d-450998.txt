proud to be serving conejo valley for over 60 years! the thousand oaks meat locker is thousand oak’s original bbq restaurant. a family owned business since 1957, the thousand oaks meat locker started off as a custom butcher shop. as the times changed, the meat locker was transformed into a unique bbq restaurant. serving fresh grilled, tri-tip, chicken, and ribs since the mid 1970’s, the thousand oaks meat locker has gained celebrity status. specializing in a lunch time serve, our speedy service is one that will always please! come try our famous entrees which are cooked fresh right in front of our restaurant every day!
