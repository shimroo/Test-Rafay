about mohawk
the original touch up company™
mohawk finishing products is the leading manufacturer and distributor of professional wood touch up, repair and finishing products.
high quality
we offer the highest quality wood coatings and finishing products, furniture polishes and cleaners, and touch up and repair products for wood and leather in the industry.
specialized products
we specialize in products for the furniture, cabinet and flooring markets.
environmentally friendly
we consider the best products as well as the best practices.
