our mission
is to provide a positive experience to every relationship in which we are involved: one guest, one client, one team member at a time.






who we are
a family company steeped in tradition – but adapting to a constantly changing environment – thompson hospitality is a place where exceptional people can excel. 
view our history











about thompson hospitality
thompson hospitality is the largest minority-owned food service, and one of the largest retail food and facilities management companies, in the country. we are a family-run organization, built on values gained from more than 30 years of client, customer and community relationships.
