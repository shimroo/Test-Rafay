about the society 



the santa clara county dental society is a professional organization of dentists in private practice. as a local component of the ada and cda, we provide our members with continuing education opportunities, social engagements and compliance assistance. 






















about us 



the mission of the santa clara county dental society is to serve its members while assisting them in their service to the public. 







contact us
