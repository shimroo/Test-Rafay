about usabsinthe brasserie & bar is located in san francisco's vibrant and charming hayes valley neighborhood, and is among the city’s beloved longest-running dining institutions. it features a creative menu of french brasserie fare, along with an inspired selection of classic cocktails. with lively, informal décor that evokes turn-of-the-century france, absinthe’s warm, convivial atmosphere has romanced locals and visitors alike since its founding in 1998.
