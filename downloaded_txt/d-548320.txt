find year-round fun at our full-service skating facility
skatequest is reston’s premier ice skating rink, locally owned and operated, and has been a cornerstone of the community for over 30 years. our full-service facility welcomes skaters of all ages and skill range, from world-class athletes and adult hockey leagues to beginners learning to skate and people who simply enjoy skating. open year-round, skatequest features nhl and olympic-sized rinks, public staking, full-service pro shop, café, birthday party rooms, and more to make us the ultimate destination for all your skating needs.

engaging events for skaters of all levels
we offer a variety of events designed to enhance your skating experience. our instructors teach the learn to skate® curriculum in a supportive environment, ideal for beginners and those looking to refine their skills. enjoy weekly public skating sessions, where skaters of all ages and experience levels can have family fun on the ice. be sure to check our calendar for special events throughout the year, featuring unique themes and activities that bring our skating community together.
