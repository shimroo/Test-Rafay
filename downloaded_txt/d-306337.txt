welcome to glotech
founded in 1976, glotech began as a spares and vacuum repair shop (finchley vac centre) based in north finchley in london. from our humble beginnings we have expanded to become one of london's largest independent domestic appliance and kitchen retailers and one of the largest appliance repair companies in the u.k.
why not give the team a call today on - 0330 024 9093 to find out more?
our stores have been around for a very long time and have allowed us to build a fantastic rapport with our local customers. due to word of mouth and fantastic customer service we have been able to expand our kitchen design department which has been growing year on year.
