news from the washington county board of commissioners meeting, feb. 18 


the washington county board proclaimed march 2025 as women's history month and approved contracts for banking services, liquor licenses, and infrastructure projects. woodbury student idrees ahmad was recognized for winning the congressional app challenge.						continue reading...







share your historical photos for washington county’s collaborative photo mural project 


do you have historical family photos or photos of interesting artifacts to share? this february, the public is invited to submit photos for "picturing the past," a collaborative photo mural to be displayed at the washington county historic courthouse.						how to submit photos...







washington county community survey coming to select households in february 


washington county residents should check their mailboxes in february to see if they are one of the 4,500 households randomly selected to take part in the 2025 washington county community survey.						read on...







featured library events for adults in february 


don't miss this month's "write in out midst" series. visit a washington county library to learn about digital skills and get helpful resources.						keep reading...







featured events for children and teens at washington county libraries
