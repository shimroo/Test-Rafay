welcome to the school of medicine
located in the heart of downtown new orleans, our school was founded in 1931 with
                                             a dual mission of training outstanding doctors who are committed to care for the underserved,
                                             and providing excellent healthcare for the people of louisiana.
learn more

mission


the mission of the louisiana state university school of medicine new orleans is to
                                    serve state and regional communities through excellence in teaching the next generation
                                    of physicians and scientists, advancing biomedical knowledge, and providing skilled
                                    and compassionate clinical care to all.

departments & centers
with twenty-three departments and four centers of excellence, the school of medicine
                     (som) is the largest school within lsu health new orleans. our faculty and learners
                     contribute to exciting advances in medical science in both the laboratory and the
                     clinic.

som in action




 
                        








simulation
students utilizing one of our many simulation rooms in the learning center.











cadaver lab
plastic surgery residents work to refine their surgical skills.











ent workshop
multidisciplinary group of students learn about microanatomy in a hands-on setting.











vascular surgery course
residents are lucky to receive one-on-one training with international experts.











neurosurgery nerve course
this involves medical students, residents, and any other practitioners with interests
                                                in peripheral nerve anatomy.











senior surgical anatomy elective
students are able to mimic operative approaches and simulate surgeries using both
                                                open and minimally invasive techniques.
