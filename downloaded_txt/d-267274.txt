office profile
announcing century 21 in scarsdale. we aim to provide our clients what they are looking for in a real estate experience.we have been providing outstanding service in listing and selling real estate for the past 25 years. we are located in scarsdale, a central part of westchester county, only 30 minutes from new york city.moving from royal fine homes to century 21 is not going to change our exceptional service but it will help our customers getting larger exposure to a global leader in real estate. we still believe in personal service to all our customers, past and present, who have always relied on our dedication, trust and integrity. we are determined to return this compliment and we are all committed to the business of real estate by staying abreast on industry news and education to better serve you. additionally, we are the short sale/foreclosure experts in the westchester area.our agentsour office consists of 40+ multilingual agents with access to several communities; who work full time seven days a week to ensure the best service. our agents are high caliber professionals with diverse backgrounds. some are former educators, executives, travel agents, and even professional athletes who are well versed in our concierge service and our market specializations.our agents have developed a very strong sales team, made even stronger with specialized daily training for all agents - along with a separate administration department that handles the paperwork, leaving our agents free to spend more time with buyers and sellers.our specialtiesfine homes market specializationcommercial investment network market specializationabr - accredited buyer representativecrs certified residential specialistcips certified international property specialistgri graduate of realtor  institutesres senior real estate specialisttrc transnational referral certificatione-pro internet professional
