president's message 2025dedicated to: “strength in unity and the value and enjoyment of property”the information provided on this website by the “california association of homeowners associations, inc. (cahoa).” is intended to assist “board of directors” and support homeowners in the management and administration of their homeowners association (hoa).homeowners often express their concern with the administration of their hoa, the financial strength of the hoa and the future value of their property investment. homeowners often say their association is not professionally, formally or satisfactorily managed.   the cahoa is prepared to assist homeowners and boards of directors in solving these and many other concerns, to help improve the conditions of their association. our philosophy has always been to serve, with distinction, and in so doing, contribute to the general welfare of the industry. we are prepared to assist board of directors with quick and efficient professional services such as:financial accountingbudget preparation minutes of meetingsreinstate suspended associationelection of boardadministrative consultationwhen the “hoa” benefits, “homeowners” will also benefit; it is a good philosophy.as a courtesy to the industry, we offer a brief consultation to homeowners and board members, this free consultation is limited to 10 minutes a call.remember - you will never go wrong if you build your business on "quality, integrity, and service."richard s. monson mbaplease call  951-634-9266626-786-1568calmonson3 @gmail.com
