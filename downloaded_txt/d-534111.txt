
















life at st. michael and all angels









































































we are an episcopal church community in tucson, arizona.  we enjoy an anglican expression in our worship, celebrate mass in two languages, and are committed to be socially involved in our tucson community, our country, and the world. we are located at the corner of fifth street and wilmot road. 

the rev. david benedict hedges, n/bsg, rector. telephone 886-7292.contact 
st. michael's school at 722-8478.

holy week and easter schedule 2017









palm sunday, april 9
the liturgy of the palms will precede all masses at the usual time.
the passion gospel will be sung at the high mass.

holy monday, april 10
6:30 pm: low mass

holy tuesday, april 11
6:30 pm: low mass

holy wednesday, april 12
12:10 pm:  low mass (no anointing)

maundy thursday, april 13
6:30 pm:  solemn mass with foot washing and procession to the altar of repose.
agápe supper follows the mass.
stripping of the altar follows the agape supper.
the watch with the blessed sacrament follows until morning prayer on good friday.

good friday, april 14
12:10 pm: stations of the cross
6:30 pm: solemn liturgy of good friday
the passion according to john will be sung.
veneration of the cross and holy communion from the reserved sacrament.

holy saturday, april 15
stations of the cross at the immaculate heart novitiate (details tba).
7:30 pm: the great vigil of easter
holy baptism will be administered to erin mcmullen.
the first eucharist of easter.

easter day, april 16
7:45, 9:00, 10:15, 12:30 masses as usual.
no 5:00 pm mass will be offered.

confession available every saturday in lent
the sacrament of reconciliation will now be available every saturday until april 8, from 9:00 am until 10:00 am.  the confessional is located to the right, just inside the church doors.  on april 1, the confessor will be fr. jorge sotelo, who will hear confessions in spanish or english.  on april 8, the confessor will be br. dave hedges, who will hear confessions in english.

friday, january 27, 2017








tea, tapas, textiles, and terri (our woman in antigua guatemala) 








saturday, january 28, 4 p.m., st. michael and all angels episcopal church, 602 n. wilmot (at 5th street), parish center.

terri smith, spouse of st. michael’s rector emeritus the rev. john r. smith, visits tucson briefly and will share personal insights and st. michael’s guatemala project updates.now resident in antigua guatemala, the smiths help with legado elisabet, a project fund for children; and fr. smith is vicar of st. alban episcopal mission. expect fresh perspectives from terri, project information from the coordinator, good cheer, eclectic refreshments, limited sale of exceptional maya textiles, and photos, including two 1980 photographs on sale. after the general program, people interested in joining summer teams will have a focus meeting while others interact informally with terri. free. donations welcomed.

information:  ila abernathy, coordinator, (520) 623-3063, ilaa@mindspring.com.

casa maria - help us feed the hungry!

casa maria ~ parish center. st. michael's volunteers get together every fourth friday evening in the parish center to prepare sack lunches for the homeless and disadvantaged of tucson. your help is needed! volunteers can always use a few “eggstra” hands and as always “shelled” hard boiled eggs. join them for fellowship and to help with this good work. the usual meeting time is 5:30 pm. please bring a box for transporting the lunches. thank you!
