missionfamily  works provides compassionate, effective, and affordable services that  build essential parenting and life skills for people with cognitive  disabilities.  














our impact

 

family works’ impact extends far and wide as we continue to teach independent living skills to adults and parent support services to adults with developmental disabilities. the success of our agency is achieved through the collaborative services we provide to 4 regional centers and partnerships with agencies such as the arc, abilities united, local counties health and human services departments and social vocational services. 

 











650+clients served12 counties throughout the bay area including; sonoma, napa, solano, marin, san francisco, san mateo, contra costa, alameda, santa clara, santa cruz, san benito, and monterey counties.













46 yearshelping families meet life challenges by providing independent living skills and parenting support services for adults with intellectual and developmental disabilities.













goals for 20241) expand services in current counties.  2) expand board membership to 8 by end of year 2024.3) develop the optimal staffing model through training and professional development to enhance services and create promotional opportunities within the agency.
