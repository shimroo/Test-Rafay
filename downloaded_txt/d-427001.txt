dr. geddes publishes new book



event date:
june 19, 2003



lawyers & judges publishing company has just announced the publication of 
medical and bioengineering aspects of electrical injuries by raymond m. 
fish and leslie a. geddes, with contributing author charles f. babbs.

this comprehensive guide provides an in-depth look into the medical and 
bioengineering aspects of electrical injuries. topics include the medical 
aspects of these injuries with a focus on diagnosis and treatment, the 
bioengineering aspects with an emphasis on electrical theory and human 
physiology, sources of electricity that can lead to injury, and accident 
reconstruction.

located in tucson, arizona, lawyers & judges publishing company (founded 
1963) is devoted to the making of top quality resources in various legal 
fields, especially accident reconstruction and litigation. in 2002, they 
published dr. geddes' book, medical device accidents and illustrative cases.
