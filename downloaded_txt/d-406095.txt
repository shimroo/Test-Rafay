janet biggs is a research based, interdisciplinary artist known for her immersive work in video, film and performance



















biggs’ work focuses on how individuals define and maintain a senseof themselves in extreme landscapes or situations

 

















navigating territory between art, science and technology

 

















where sound, moving images, and language interact to create new perspectives and possibilities
