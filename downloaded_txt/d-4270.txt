about

through fun, adventure, and learning, the santa ana zoo empowers its community to save wildlife and wild spaces.
the santa ana zoo is part of the city of santa ana's parks, recreation, and community services team. supported by nonprofit friends of santa ana zoo, the monkey zoo has been a community touchstone since opening in 1952.
