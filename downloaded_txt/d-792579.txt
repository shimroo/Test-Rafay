union creek resort 
cabin lodging near crater lake 





come stay at union creek resort, a historic getaway near crater lake national park offering cozy cabins and comfortable lodge rooms year round. with easy access to hiking, waterfalls, and more, you will see why union creek resort has welcomed guests and travelers since 1922. 
learn more











 
points of interest

                        convenient access to crater lake national park and more.
                    







 
hiking

                        endless trails to explore and discover.
                    







 
waterfalls

                        from thunderous to peaceful, take in the refreshing tranquility of the many waterfalls near union creek resort.
                    

















cozy accommodations
our cabin and room options are perfect for any need – including cozy, romantic bungalow cabins for couples, multiple rooms for families, and large 4-bedroom cabins for large groups and families of up to 10 people.
