helping bouviers live longer



















bouvier health is up to you the bouvier health foundation (bhf) is committed to increasing the longevity and quality of life of the bouvier des flandres (bouvier). whether you are thinking about getting a bouvier for the first time or have had many; our goal is to offer tools and knowledge to allow  your dogs to live long, happy healthy lives.every year we learn more about ways to enhance the health of this beautiful breed. bhf’s continued support of research is key in ensuring we continue on a path of prevention, early detection and effective treatment of health issues specific to the bouvier and other large breeds.bhf is currently working on a number of research projects and has developed educational materials and resources that we hope you will utilize  to help keep your bouvier healthy.thank you for your support in making this happen. the bouvier community is relatively small compared to other breeds and we all need to help in this effort.everything we gain, you gain.




    here is how you can help
  














judith kasper legacy lives on through her bouvier grooming guidelong time bouvier fanciers will remember the artistry of judy kasper from her pen and ink drawings to her grooming techniques.  even though judy has passed, the bouvier health foundation has been given permission from her husband larry to pass on judy’s artistry in the form of her grooming book.  all proceeds will benefit the glaucoma research fund. electronic copies are available here.bhf donations sustain our work and on behalf of our breed, we thank you.
