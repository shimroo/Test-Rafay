cart delivery for city’s new waste disposal and recycling program starts february 18 


delivery of new trash and recycling carts will begin tuesday, feb 18 and should be completed by thursday, feb 27. residents will receive two 96-gallon carts from constable sanitation — one for trash and one for recycling, delivered directly to their home.						read on...







sign up for the rethink maguire newsletter to always be in-the-know before you go

new year, new codes! city adopts the 2024 international building codes 


the warrensburg city council recently adopted building and fire codes updates. the new codes go into effect on march 1.						read on...







new waste disposal and recycling program to begin march 1 


the city of warrensburg is pleased to announce a comprehensive solid waste disposal program for our residents. the city has contracted with constable sanitation to provide affordable, quality solid waste, recycling, and yard and bulk waste services.						read on...







check out the january issue of the warrensburg legend! 


extra, extra, read all about it! the january 2025 issue of the warrensburg legend is hot off the press. with articles about winter road conditions, rethink maguire, local resources, solid waste services, and more, you don't want to miss out!						read on...







city council appoints mike schrage as city manager for warrensburg, missouri 


the warrensburg city council voted on monday, december 23rd, to approve a contract for mike schrage to serve as the next city manager for the city of warrensburg. mike is a seasoned city manager with over three decades of experience in local government.						read on...
