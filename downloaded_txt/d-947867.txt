careers at florida state





thank you for your interest in employment with florida state university! fsu is home to over 15,000 employees. from eminent scholars to a variety of professionals, each employee plays a role in transforming the lives of our students and shaping the future of our state and society. we invite you to explore our career opportunities to find out how you could help build the future of fsu.
