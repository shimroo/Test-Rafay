ownby design
ownby design is an industry leading luxury interior design firm located in scottsdale, arizona. our main areas of operation are the greater phoenix area and the hawaiian islands.
our mission is to always deliver a flawless product that is a true reflection of the client’s vision. we strive to transform everyday living spaces into one-of-a-kind indulgences, with a unique fusion of minimalism and lavishness. our commitment to detail makes us one of the most sought after firms in the valley.
our team has been recognized with numerous industry and community awards for residential designs in both arizona and the hawaiian islands. we continually deliver beautiful and functional spaces that go beyond our client’s needs and expectations.
