a pioneer in rehabilitation —providing the best opportunities for healing and recovery since 1974














your goals. our mission.










advanced subacute care + treatment

our experienced team of rehabilitation specialists use the latest treatments and best practices to provide comprehensive care and rehabilitation. our nursing and respiratory therapy services provide 24/7 care, ensuring patients complex medical needs are met to provide the best opportunities for healing and recovery. 




learn more about inpatient care











full service outpatient therapy services

our whole person approach to rehabilitation encompasses one’s body, mind, heart, and spirit. we call it the science of caring®, and it is at the center of everything we do. helping you find increased independence and a smooth path to recovery is our top priority.




view outpatient services











transitional + long term residential care

maximize independence in 24/7 supervised living residential settings with easy access to full service therapy, support services, and campus amenities. barrier-free settings accommodate the needs of persons with disabilities while maintaining a warm, family-like home setting .




see our residential programs
