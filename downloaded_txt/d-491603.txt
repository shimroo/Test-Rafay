discover doylestowndoylestown, pennsylvania is located in the heart of beautiful bucks county. as the county seat, doylestown offers a rich history, passionate community, and an eclectic mix of businesses, restaurants, and services. discover doylestown is a non-profit organization, made up of local business and residents, that works to promote the town through events, outreach, and volunteer efforts.
