revaluation appeals process:municipal valuations are no longer scheduling appointments for informal hearings. the next opportunity to appeal is with the board of assessment appeals.  forms must be completed and returned to the assessor's office by february 20, 2025.upon return of the appeal form to the assessor's office, your appeal will be forwarded to the board of assessment appeals.  the board of assessment appeals will notify you of your hearing date, time and location via mail.applications are now available in the assessor's office, and on the assessor's web page, or by clicking here.
