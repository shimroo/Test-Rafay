swope motorsports
swope motorsports is kentucky's premiere motorcycle shop! we are the only aprilia, ducati, ktm, and triumph dealer in louisville. our state-of-the-art 19,000 sq ft facility at 625 e jefferson street includes a dyno room, machine shop, two stories of showroom, warehouse for bike storage, large storage rooms for inventory, and a workshop with 8 lifts. we are more than capable of serving all your motorcycle needs. come in and see for yourself, you won't be disappointed! also, check out our ducati motorcycle blog for the latest info on ducati products.
