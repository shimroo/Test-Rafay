four campaigns creating long-term social change.










housing justice

across the twin cities metro, we are organizing to protect tenants, prevent displacement, preserve naturally occurring affordable housing, and produce safe, stable housing that is affordable for the most marginalized in our community.  




learn more











decriminalizing communities

the criminal-legal and immigration systems are unjustly targeting communities of color across minnesota. we are organizing with immigrant and incarcerated-led organizations to keep families together and end deportations and mass incarceration. 




learn more











combating hate

we are educating and organizing to push back against antisemitism and white nationalism to create an inclusive and equitable state where everyone is safe.




learn more











multiracial democracy

we are joining together with partners across lines of difference to build a better future for all of us, a future where every vote is counted, every voice is heard and every person has the resources they need to thrive.




learn more
