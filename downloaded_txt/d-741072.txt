​about us



















 
​about ushello, my name is denna shelton with doodleheavenpuppies.we are a premiere breeder of top quality bernedoodles and  australian bernedoodles located outside little rock , arkansas.​located in a little town called mabelvale. this laid-back, country setting provides our dogs the perfect environment with plenty of room to exercise and play. our love for the doodle designer breeds span over 20  years and we are very pleased to be one of the first "doodle" breeders in the state of arkansas. when we first started our breeding program, we raised the beautiful goldendoodles, which i have since retired.then i started breeding the australian bernedoodles and fell in love all over again!our breeding program is kept small in scale so that we can give every puppy born here at doodle heaven the very best possible start to life! we provide them an abundance of love, care and human nurturing that is benificial for optimal mental health and physical development. daily stimulation, interaction and socialization are key and sets the foundation for them to thrive and become happy, healthy adults.over the years, we have been very fortunate to have work with some of the most amazing families. our long list of clients come from all walks of life, backgroundsand professions. we are truly blessed and honored to provide families with loyal companionship and unconditional love, one puppy at a time.

how we raise our precious puppies 





here at doodle heaven we strive to raise our puppies in a comfortable and health environment where they are handled, loved socialized and trained in our home from day one, we work very closely with each family to match them with the perfect family member.we perform the ens -early neurological stimulation on our puppies, this is done at age 3 to 16 days. we have used this method since the beginning . ​​our puppies are born and raised in our home, we firmly believe that raising puppies within the home environment benefits and the puppies are less susceptible to illness do to the sanitation of the area, they are more socialized and we can observe the mother and puppies closely during the critical ages and they are use to the inside noises, such as vacuums, tv's, house hold noises and other dogs (big and small). and they all receive the extra tlc needed to become a perfect family companions!
 






 



we do not have outdoor kennel's for our dogs , they are either in our home or in our building next to our home that is fully equipped with ac/heat, a grooming station and a t.v for them to watch =)
