large item pickup
the disposal of large items will be the 1st and 3rd friday of every month.  to schedule a pick up, provide the clerk's office with your name, phone number, address, and a description of what is to be picked up, along with $35 (cash or check only) per item, no later than 3pm the thursday before pick up.
