the east atlanta community association is dedicated to improving the quality of life for all east atlanta residents. 



we provide services, engage in public policy, and support development initiatives. join us in our mission to make a positive impact on our community.



























our monthly community meetings















eaca holds monthly community meetings the second tuesday of each month. these meetings serve as a forum for conversation and debate on the things that matter to the community. we also hear updates from our public officials, discuss key community initiatives, and vote on matters such as alcohol licenses, variance requests, and more. join remotely via zoom (click below to register) or in person at the brownwood recreation center in brownwood park: 607 brownwood ave, se, atlanta, ga 30316.
