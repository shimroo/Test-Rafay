“how did we say___, in spanish?” no problem, this section gives you the answers: pronunciation, numbers, verb usage, indicative, subjunctive… whatever your level, we have the language resources for you.




donquijote





spanish language resources












there are many strategies for learning a new language. while taking courses and studying is important to assimilate basic concepts, there are lots of other things you can do to improve your spanish level. and we want to make it easier.
in this section you have all kinds of materials to build your spanish knowledge and review what you already know. feel free to browse through our articles covering everyday topics for spanish learners, from numbers and the alphabet to common expressions.
there’s much more to spanish learning than studying, so we also have a selection of fun and interesting content for your enjoyment. you’ll find song lyrics, talented writers and their most popular books, and even an online summary of don quixote of la mancha. take your spanish out for a spin and practice in real life!
