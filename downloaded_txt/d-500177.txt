our classes
the art league of long island offers classes and workshops in a variety of mediums throughout the year. we offer programs in drawing & painting, watercolor, printmaking, pastel, sculpture, jewelry, ceramics, photography, as well as ongoing open studios and more for adults, teens, and children. classes are available for anyone 3 to 103 with no experience necessary.

mission statement
the art league of long island is a not-for-profit organization dedicated to broad-based visual arts education, providing a forum and showcase for artists of all ages and ability levels.

since its inception in 1955, the mission has focused on enhancing long island’s cultural life by promoting the appreciation, practice and enjoyment of the visual arts. collaborative events and outreach programs spread the joy of creativity among every segment of the population.
thanks to a private donor sponsoring our lgbtqia+ programming! we have re-scheduled our create nights and look forward to relaunching the youth pride in the arts mentorship & scholarship soon.
