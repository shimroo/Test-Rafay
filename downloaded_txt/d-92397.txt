atlanta supply is now jedco supply



atlanta supply was founded in 1959 by george templeman at 602 marietta street. in 1973, the company became incorporated in the state of georgia. ownership passed to jim and dot templeman in 1985. the following year, jack alexander began working in the warehouse while attending georgia tech. after graduating in 1991, he was promoted to general manager. in 1994, atlanta supply moved to 643 10th street, and the next year, jack alexander was promoted to president. the company relocated again in 2003 to 1333 logan circle. in 2015, atlanta supply was acquired by jedco supply.
jedco supply was founded by jack, erik, and drew, and has now transformed what was once a small supply house in atlanta into one of the largest distributors of registers and grilles in the country. we continue to provide top tier customer service and support, and seek to build long term relationships with our customers. that philosophy is the basis of how we have chosen to run our company. we look forward to serving you!
