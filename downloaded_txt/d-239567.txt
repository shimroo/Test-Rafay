meet neil castellane

neil has been selling real estate in the lowcountry for over 22 years. he has prior experience as a broker in charge for a top producing real estate company and continues to lead in his role as an agent at charter one realty servicing the hilton head island, bluffton and okatie markets. as broker in charge, his company secured the president’s cup in 2011 and in 2012 he was voted realtor of the year by the members of the hilton head area association of realtors. neil is originally from washingtonville, new york. in 1998 after graduating from suny plattsburgh with a degree in hotel, restaurant and tourism management, neil moved to hilton head island to run the food and beverage segment at marriott vacation club. in his free time, neil is an avid boater and has his uscg captains license. he and his wife kimmie (and dog yoda) love to visit the sandbar, daufuskie, bluffton, savannah, beaufort and several local area restaurants by boat.
with sales of well over $100 million and several hundreds of transactions under his belt, he has the knowledge, experience and proven track record to help you find a new home or sell your property. neil is a member and former board member of the hilton head area association realtors and is also a past board member of community service based organization vanlandingham rotary and am a founding member and board member of the local civitan organization.
my promise to you
contact me today and get the representation you need to find property on hilton head island and the surrounding low country. get the facts: rental quotes, costs and hidden fees, past sales, floor plans, current mls and fsbo listings, even properties that are not currently listed for sale yet. i have the knowledge and experience to give you only the best service and all at no cost to you!!! my record speaks for itself and my clients will tell you, all of them!!! desire...that sums up my persona in one word. the desire to accomplish goals in the workplace, in the community, and in my personal life. since moving to hilton head island and working with charter one realty, that concept is stronger than ever. a resident for over 25 years, i have already established a foothold in the community.






beth doudoukjian
beth doudoukjian is originally from columbia, sc. beth is from a close knit family and graduated from university of south carolina in 2005. shortly after finishing college, beth started working in wine and spirit sales. between that and her love of the beach, she took the opportunity to move to hilton head island to continue with her wine and spirit sales career. after being in the sales business for all these years, and loving working with people, she realized being a realtor and helping others find their dream homes in hilton head was what she wanted to do.
