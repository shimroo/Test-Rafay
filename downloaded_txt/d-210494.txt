the ford historic home district’s holiday home tour 2024 is a celebration of dearborn history you won’t want to miss. this tour of historic homes in the district offers a chance to learn about the history of the ford homes and enjoy them while they are decorated for the holidays. this fundraiser supports beautification of shared spaces in the district as well as neighborhood events throughout the year. 
this year’s tour begins at:dvuall elementary school 22561 beech street dearborn, mi 48124sunday, december 8, 2024 02:00 pm – 06:00 pm
