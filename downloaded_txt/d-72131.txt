                you'll find it here
                #



as a social work student at iu bloomington, you’ll enjoy all the advantages of a big 10 research university while forming tight-knit bonds with peers who share similar interests. varied opportunities for real-world experience make bloomington an enriching place to study, learn and grow. and the area’s lively arts and cultural scene and unique recreation and entertainment offerings mean there’s always something to do as you prepare for your future.










				degrees and programs
				#






"url(#clip0_20_46)">
















 our nationally recognized academic programs prepare you for the next step in your journey.








							bachelor of social work
						


our bachelor’s degree prepares you for general social work practice—and the chance to make a difference.





							master of social work
						


take the next step in your professional journey with our leading msw program.  





							labor studies
						


iu’s leading labor studies program offers associate’s and bachelor’s degrees, a labor studies certificate, a minor, and courses customized for your organization.
