scripps whale acoustic lab
								




our mission is to advance the study of marine animal population  abundance, seasonality and behavior using acoustic techniques.
								

























what do we  do?

we develop new acoustic instrumentation and analytical approaches to improve our understanding of the ecology of marine animals and the impact of anthropogenic sounds on them.
