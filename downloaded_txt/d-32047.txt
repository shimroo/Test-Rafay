diaz compean student union
the diaz compean student union is a place where students can relax, study, eat, and
                                    utilize on-campus resources. it houses more services than any other building on campus,
                                    and its central location makes it easy for students to stop by between classes.
take a virtual tour of the student union.















spartan recreation and aquatic center
the spartan recreation and aquatic center is the #1 fitness, wellness, and health
                                    destination on campus. whether it's our state-of-the-art facility, club sports, intramural
                                    sports, fitness, or outdoor adventures, we are here to keep you up, active, and having
                                    fun.
