bring home real wisconsin cheese


schurman's wisconsin cheese has been part of wisconsin's rich tradition of cheese making for over 70 years. we take great pride in our excellent cheddar cheeses, especially our aged xtra sharp cheddars. try any of our varieties of other cheeses for great flavor and a real taste of wisconsin!

	our warehouse in dodgeville has a cheese outlet store with a wonderful assortment of wisconsin cheeses, and is open from 8:00 a.m. to 6:00 p.m. central time, monday through friday and 8:00 a.m. to 4:00 p.m. central time on saturday.

you may order any of our famous wisconsin cheddar cheeses or our other wisconsin cheese varieties. we would be happy to take your order over the phone. 608-935-5741. we ship cheese year-round, packing carefully to preserve quality. during the holiday season, schurman's wisconsin cheese also offers cheese gift boxes for your gift giving pleasure. we also have a selection of cheese spreads and other products to enhance your holiday party.

	 

please call 608-935-5741 to order your schurman's wisconsin cheese!
