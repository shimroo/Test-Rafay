celebrating 50 years of new trier hockey




new trier hockey club is the largest and most successful high school hockey program in the state of illinois. our mission is to develop our players to meet their individual hockey goals, and for our teams to win championships. the cornerstone of nthc's success is our relentless commitment to teamwork, sportsmanship, attitude and effort!
