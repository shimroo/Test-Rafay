be not afraid of anything. come into the heart. come home and accept yourself just as you are. — hilda charlton








































hilda charlton…was an extraordinary being who taught classes for more than twenty years in new york city in which she gave practical lessons to thousands for living a life of truth, courage and love.
her teachings incorporated the fundamental principles of the world's major religions. foremost in her classes, she stressed the importance of self-mastery, giving and forgiving, unconditional love, and remembrance of god.
















hilda’s teachingshilda’s lectures included poignant stories of her life, practical lessons for everyday living, and guidance in prayer and meditation. her style combined humor, practicality, and deep insight into the human mind and spirit. her teachings had a profound effect on the lives of thousands of people from all walks of life.hilda’s weekly lessons of life were recorded—many of which you can find on this website. her teachings also include seven books: master hilarion, divine mother speaks, skanda, hell bent for heaven (her autobiography), the new sun, pioneers of the soul, and saints alive. you can read an interview from the new sun, and there are excerpts from all of her books on their shop pages.we hope you find something encouraging–even life changing– as you explore the life and works of our beloved hilda charlton.
