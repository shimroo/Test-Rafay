totally absurd 
            inventions 
 explores 
            the funnier side of our inventive spirit by featuring actual usa 
            patented products. 

obtaining a 
            patent is a costly and time consuming process.  inventors must 
            have unstoppable faith in their vision in order to realize their 
            dream of acquiring a patent.
but sometimes these 
          inventions come from a creative place so deep, they can be perceived 
          by some as offbeat, unusual and possibly a bit eccentric.  and 
          that's where we step in unveiling america's goofiest patents!
join us now and glimpse 
          into the mind of genius.  indulge yourself and behold the most 
          incredible patents in the world!
