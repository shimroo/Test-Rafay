the horse park at woodside is a nonprofit 501(c)(3) that provides opportunities for educational, recreational and competitive activities in a variety of equestrian disciplines that encourage the growth and development of youth and adults.














support the parkwe rely on our cherished community. as a 501(c)(3) nonprofit, the park depends on the dedication and generosity of our donors, volunteers and sponsors. your contributions both support the park today and sustain it into the future.
