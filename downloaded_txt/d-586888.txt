welcome to the nelson triathlon and multisport clubwe are the home of triathlon, duathlon & ocean swimming in the nelson and tasman regions. we also support multisport and adventure racing in the region.
as a club we put on numerous events throughout the year, from ocean swims and triathlon in the summer time, to off road duathlon in the cooler months.
our goal is to bring as many people as possible into these sports we love so much.
we welcome people of all ages and abilities to our events and we encourage you to get in touch if you would like to know more about our sport and getting involved.
