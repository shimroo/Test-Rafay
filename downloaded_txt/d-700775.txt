develop

development & acquisitions
let’s put our heads together.
our development is the cornerstone of hills properties success. we have been involved in the development of land for the various end uses for more than 60 years. our experience includes the development and redevelopment of both urban and suburban land. at hills, we continue to fine-tune our approach to the key development components – site selection, entitlement, design, solid financing and value generation, to turn land into communities and apartments into homes.
