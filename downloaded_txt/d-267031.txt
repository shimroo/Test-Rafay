the central classroom building is home to the department of nutrition, food science,
                                    and packaging. 





first nutrition department in the california state university system
since 1911, we have been empowering students through knowledge, skills, and practical
                                    experience to become qualified professionals in the fields of dietetics, applied nutrition
                                    and food sciences, food management, and packaging.
