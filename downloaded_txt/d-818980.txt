            about us
          



              founded in 1986, bcc is one of cleveland’s most experienced independent investment banking firms, providing advisory and investment banking services to entrepreneurs, privately held and publicly held middle-market companies, private equity firms, and family offices. our experience includes advising on the sale of businesses, strategic acquisitions, private capital, valuations and related matters. since bcc’s inception, we have remained consistent in our mission to private owners and entrepreneurs and have completed hundreds of transactions and valuations for our clients with one goal in mind: adding value through successful outcomes.

bcc is unique in that we offer our clients direct access to senior-level advisors, a high level of transaction-execution experience, independence, a vast range of industry experience, and midwest-based resources and values. bcc undertakes a limited number of engagements to provide our clients the highest level of attention, service and success.

our team knows that business is about people and serving entrepreneurial companies means understanding the unique combination of needs of the business and its constituents, including shareholders, management and employees. we are driven to fulfill our clients' objectives, creating value, and using our experience and expertise to become a trusted advisor to build long-term relationships.
