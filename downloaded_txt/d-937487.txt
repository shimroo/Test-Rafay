winsted welcomes you!
welcome to the city of winsted.  if you set your sights on winsted, you'll love what you see.  from the charm of a small town to the excitement of a growing community, winsted is a great place to live, work and play!
winsted is a growing community, located just 45 miles west of the twin cities metro area.  our warm, friendly residents take great pride in winsted's uniqueness and demonstrate tremendous community spirit through civic and cultural events.  winsted can offer residents the convenience of many local businesses and professional services.  residents can also enjoy recreational amenities, such as the luce line trail, ball fields, and parks.  
we're proud of our past and are even more excited about our future.  whether you are planning a visit, moving to the area, looking for a business site, or considering a place to raise a family or retire, we are delighted to welcome you and we hope you find what you are looking for!
