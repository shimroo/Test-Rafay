our mission is to combine community and individual resources to respond to the identified unmet basic human needs of individuals in the ojai valley.  take me to the program directory…



























“i love our community, specifically the gracious, smiling older friends we serve...” — home delivered meals driver
















each year help of ojai enjoys the dedication and service of over 250 volunteers. while some volunteers come in weekly, others help on specific annual projects. we couldn’t do all that we do in the ojai valley without the dedication and service from our community. come find a service opportunity that meets your goals and interests.



“as the only social services provider in ojai, many of our neighbors rely on help. that’s why i give.” —  recurring donor
