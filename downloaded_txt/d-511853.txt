narragansett bay wheelmen
 
the narragansett bay wheelmen, founded in 1879, is one of the oldest sustained cycling clubs in america.  our organization is dedicated to promoting and advocating safe and enjoyable cycling.  we organize group rides each sunday for all skill levels and abilities.

check out our event calendar and join us!

contact us at info@nbwclub.org
