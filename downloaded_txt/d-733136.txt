historical impact & contributionsfrom our initial grant to the american red cross a century ago, the rockefeller foundation’s history is marked by bold initiatives, breakthroughs, and unique partnerships, driving transformative change over the past 111 years.learn more    achieving health for allachieving health for allfrom eradicating hookworm in the american south and funding the nobel prize-winning yellow fever vaccine, to scaling covid-19 testing during the pandemic, the rockefeller foundation has shaped public health‘s principles, practices, and institutions.       growing good foodgrowing good foodto fight food insecurity, the rockefeller foundation helped boost crop yields in asia, africa, and beyond through modernizing agriculture practices. today, we are championing regenerative agriculture and helping americans access nutritious, affordable, and sustainably-grown foods.       connecting people to energyconnecting people to energynearly half the world's population lacks sufficient access to the energy that offers opportunity, health, and stability in the 21st century. the rockefeller foundation has advanced solar mini-grid prototypes in india, helped create the u.s. energy foundation, and co-leads the global energy alliance for people and planet, whose mission is to reach one billion people with reliable renewable power.       leveraging innovationleveraging innovationfrom the earliest days of nuclear physics to the cutting-edge of ai, the rockefeller foundation has worked across sectors and political lines to develop technologies and
