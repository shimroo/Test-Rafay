our mission laughter  and music have extraordinary powers to heal.   laughter heals aspires to become a leading source of information,  education,  therapeutic programming and resources for those dealing with chronic and/or terminal illness.   additionally,  our efforts will also provide assistance to those facing mental,  emotional or occupational stresses.   laughter heals aims to reduce the tension and anxiety we all face on a daily basis,  utilizing comedic and music-based entertainment and its restorative powers of renewal and healing.the mission of laughter heals foundation (“lhf”) is to promote the awareness of the powerful healing properties of laughter  and music have for our physical/mental/spiritual wellbeing and for the health of our relationships.at a time when the health status of the u.s. population is in decline,  lhf’s access to dedicated comedic and music talents, internet specialists, industry professionals, and media leaders will help it make a healthy difference in the lives of thousands of people.
