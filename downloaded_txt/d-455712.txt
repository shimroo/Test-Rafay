what merlin stands for


since 1975, we’ve grown with you and your local community, dedicated to providing you with the full range of ‘a to z’ automotive repair and maintenance services, from fixing your ac to replacing your wipers.
your vehicle needs to last you a long time and we want to help with that. our locally owned shops see your vehicle for what it can do for you in the long run, so we work to keep your vehicle in top condition for years to come.
we believe life is better without car payments and for your vehicles working hard for longer, with our drive for 200,000 miles vehicle longevity program. we keep you safe on your journey with everyday repairs, preventative maintenance, brake services, and more.
