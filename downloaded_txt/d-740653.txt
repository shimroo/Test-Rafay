convertible eyewear
our convertible eyewear have the ability to easily convert from sunglasses to goggles by simply
                    adding the included foam gasket to the frame, then removing the temples, and inserting the goggle
                    strap in its place. this ability to convert from sunglasses to goggles allows the convertible to be
                    a versatile eyewear that can be utilized in a wide variety of activities and environments.
frame
all convertibles come with a specially designed frame front that offers the ability to hold both
                    temples and goggle strap.
np





frame

                                front that offers the ability to hold both temples and goggle strap.




foam
removable foam gasket allows for the convertible to be
                                worn with or without extra foam padding.




temples
change the convertible from goggles to sunglasses by
                                easily replacing the goggle strap with the included temples.




strap
remove the temples from a convertible eyewear and replace
                                with the included strap and now you have a functional goggle.
