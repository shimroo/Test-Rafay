a beautiful violin, tibor horvath -violin and yelena savchenko-piano have been performing together since 1992. they have entertained thousands of times at weddings, private parties and regular jobs at restaurants and retirement facilities. the repertoire includes popular and show tunes, classical and music from all over the world (french, italian, spanish, latin, irish, chinese, filipino, vietnamese, viennese, persian, jewish, russian, romanian and hungarian gypsy).  together, they have been featured at many of the most prestigious gatherings in the san francisco bay area. now residing in reno, they are available to entertain for you.



tibor is a virtuoso violinist who studied for several years under a european teacher. he has performed for president ford and actress, lana turner. yelena has two degrees in music (music theory and piano performance) from russia.
