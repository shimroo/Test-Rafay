nags head hotel near jennette's pier.



nestled on serene white sand shores, holiday inn express nags head oceanfront hotel offers over four acres of pristine beachfront, providing direct private access to the atlantic ocean. located in a peaceful residential area, guests can unwind on the beach or dive into local activities like surfing, fishing, and dolphin tours. minutes from top attractions like jennette’s pier, jockey’s ridge state park, and the wright brothers national memorial, we ensure you’re close to everything the outer banks has to offer, while providing a relaxing retreat.
enjoy complimentary amenities, including an indoor heated pool, a full hot and cold breakfast buffet, free wi-fi, a 24-hour fitness center, and a business center.
at the end of the day, sink into plush bedding in our spacious rooms for a good night’s rest and awaken to beautiful ocean views. relax, recharge, and let your nags head coastal adventure begin!

fuel up with free breakfast

start your day with our free express start® breakfast buffet featuring hot and cold favorites. enjoy eggs and bacon, hot pancakes, our signature cinnamon rolls, and fresh coffee. or opt for cereal, yogurt, and fresh fruit. breakfast is served daily from 6:30am-9:30am. it’s the right way to start your day!


                                        learn more
                                    





















refresh with free guest laundry

enjoy the convenience of our complimentary guest laundry facilities during your stay. whether you're here for a short trip or an extended visit, our free laundry service ensures you can keep your clothes fresh and ready for your next outer banks adventure. it's just one more way we make your stay comfortable and hassle-free!


                                        learn more
                                    





















indoor pool – year-round fun

relax year-round in our heated indoor pool. open daily from 8am-10pm, our pool offers a perfect escape no matter the season. with quick, easy access to the beach right outside the door, you can effortlessly transition from swimming in the pool to enjoying the sands of the atlantic.


                                        learn more
                                    





















step from your room to the sand

experience the ultimate beachfront getaway at our oceanfront hotel, where you can step right onto the sands of the atlantic coast from your room. enjoy private beach access, breathtaking ocean views, and the serenity of nags head, nc.




















your gateway to nags head best

stay just minutes away from some of the outer banks’ most iconic attractions! our prime location puts you close to jennette’s pier, jockey’s ridge state park, and the wright brothers national memorial, while offering a peaceful retreat. it’s the best of both worlds! enjoy nags head, with both adventure and relaxation right at your doorstep.

faqs for holiday inn express nags head oceanfront



does holiday inn express nags head oceanfront have a pool?


no, holiday inn express nags head oceanfront does not have a pool.




how much is parking at holiday inn express nags head oceanfront?


complimentary parking is available at holiday inn express nags head oceanfront for hotel guests.




can i cancel my booking at holiday inn express nags head oceanfront for a full refund?


always check the hotel's cancellation policy before booking a rate. room cancellation is subject to rate booked at time of reservation and cancellation policy varies by rate.




does holiday inn express nags head oceanfront have a restaurant on site?


no, there is no on site restaurant at holiday inn express nags head oceanfront.




is there free breakfast available at holiday inn express nags head oceanfront?


breakfast is free with your stay at the holiday inn express nags head oceanfront.




when is breakfast at holiday inn express nags head oceanfront?


breakfast at holiday inn express nags head oceanfront is available from 6:30 am to 9:30 am.




is there a complimentary shuttle available at holiday inn express nags head oceanfront?


no, there is not a complimentary local area shuttle available at holiday inn express nags head oceanfront.




what are the check-in and check-out times for holiday inn express nags head oceanfront?


check-in at holiday inn express nags head oceanfront is from 4:00 pm, and check-out time is 11:00 am. contact the hotel directly for options available for early check-in or late check-out.




does holiday inn express nags head oceanfront offer free wi-fi?


yes, the holiday inn express nags head oceanfront offers free wifi!
