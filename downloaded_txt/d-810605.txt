welcome to garden grove hospital medical center

garden grove hospital medical center, a member of prime healthcare, is centrally located in orange county, california at 12601 garden grove boulevard in garden grove. the medical center opened in 1982 to help meet the culturally diverse medical needs of the community. today, the 167-bed acute care medical facility is the largest employer in the city of garden grove with over 500 employees and more than 550 affiliated physicians.
core services include a 24-hour emergency room, medical/surgical unit, intensive care unit, diagnostic services including an mri center, and inpatient and outpatient services.
garden grove hospital medical center is fully accredited by the joint commission on accreditation of healthcare organizations, the nation’s oldest and largest hospital accreditation agency.
