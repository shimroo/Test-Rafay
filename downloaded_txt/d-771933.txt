              welcome to the alarm clock doc website!  



our 
			business is dedicated to the preservation of vintage mechanical 
			alarm clocks like your parents, grandparents and great-grandparents 
			owned and depended on.   whether you are looking for the 
			perfect gift, you are a collector of these classic antique 
			timepieces, or a homesteader living off the grid, you have come to the right place.  
			we can restore your heirloom clock or sell you a fully restored 
			clock in just about any price range.  we fully guarantee every 
			restoration for at least as long as the original manufacturer's 
			warranty, and most times much longer.  the majority of our 
			clocks were made in the usa, but we also sell quality clocks from 
			all over the world. 

       if you found our site because you 
			simply have a question about a clock, please visit our 
            
frequently
            asked questions page. if you 
			don't find your answer there, just send us an email and we would be 
			pleased to assist you.
