about becket








welcome to the town of becket, mathe town of becket is an upland town that was established in 1765, having begun as plantation number 4. the town was originally laid out in 1735 along with three other towns along the wilderness trail that connected the lower housatonic valley with the connecticut valley and boston.arts & culture  |  becket bulletin  |  links of interest  |  learn more
