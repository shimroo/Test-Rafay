welcome!

one man caribbean steel drum band & dj service
escape to the islands with the authentic steel drum sounds that originate from the islands of trinidad & tobago.
this unique island sound is sure to put a smile on your face and bring a warm, tropical feel to your special event.
steely pan combines the acoustic sounds of steel drums with expertly sequenced bass and drum tracks to give a full steel band sound at an affordable price. no job is too large or small.
steely pan one man steel drum band features the talents of tim delaney on steel drums. through his talent, he proves that you don’t have to be from the islands to be a great steel drum performer.
when you hire “steely pan”, tim delaney will be the steel drummer performing at your event. no surprise substitutes.
“steely pan” one man steel drum band is licensed and insured.
