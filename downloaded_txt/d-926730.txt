national terrazzo tile and marble, incprofessional installers of custom commercial, industrial and residential solid surface floor, counter and wall systems


























about usnational terrazzo, tile and marble, inc. is a family-owned and operated, 100+ year old company of managers, artisans and craftsmen that install the finest exterior/interior floor, wall and stair finishes in commercial, hospitality, municipal and industrial spaces.  the quality of our installations are second to none and we are the oldest and most premier marble, granite, tile and terrazzo sub-contractor in houston, texas. we have successfully installed thousands of projects in various buildouts within houston, across texas, as well as around the u.s.  through collaborations with notable architects and designers from firms all across the region, we can execute a particular design aesthetic as specified.  our relationship with stone suppliers from around the world provides a distinct ability to import, fabricate and install projects in a turn-key fashion.our only limitation is the creativity of design.  we will execute installation of a flawless design for elevator lobbies, office entrances, commercial lease spaces, exterior stone cladding, large scale/high-traffic flooring, counter tops, stairs, columns, and virtually any other surface as specified.
