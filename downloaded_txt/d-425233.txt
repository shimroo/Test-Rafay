esac is a community-based nonprofit and multi-service agency that strives to improve the quality of life for residents of boston and eastern massachusetts. focusing on children and the elderly through innovative programs in home ownership, education, and community service, esac helps identify problems that threaten individuals and communities, and works in partnership with other agencies to find creative, long-lasting solutions. by sending forth ripples of hope, esac serves as a catalyst for strengthening neighborhoods and building communities.
