welcome to dentemax


dentemax is one of the largest leasable dental ppo networks in the united states. our clients of insurance companies, third-party administrators and various groups lease the dentemax ppo network for use in their dental benefit plans in order to service their more than 28 million members nationwide. dentemax dentists agree to charge these members prices below their usual fees in order to gain access to them. members then experience these price savings each time they visit a dentemax dentist.
