 founded in 1992, st. john technology is engaged to build a worldwide company specializing in developing and marketing of integrated computer & communication products. with a group of experienced engineers and sales experts, the company was focusing on manufacturing advanced computer peripherals such as cooler for cpu and hard disk drives.
starting from 1997, the company's r&d team successfully designed and launched a series of removable 2.5" hard disk case & usb products. these unique and advanced products made possible to fulfill the needs of notebook and leap-top computer users who expect huge-size data storage devices with portable and easy-to-carry capabilities, and hence greatly enhance the flexibility and productivity of their business. since the first release at q1 1999, these products are fully accepted to all kinds of business and technical professionals.
