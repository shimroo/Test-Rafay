a shining legacy 



history at southfork began in 1978 when lorimar productions chose the north texas showplace as the site for the cbs tv series dallas. the hit series, which counted millions of viewers across the united states and in 95 countries as fans, was originally broadcast in the united states from 1978 to 1991. the series continues to be broadcast in syndication and still is in first-run status in many countries around the world. because of the ranch's high profile on television, the beautiful white mansion, the pool, the barns and the surroundings, it quickly became a tourist mecca.​
