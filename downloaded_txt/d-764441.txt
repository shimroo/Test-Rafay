pete nevin, land consultant and realtor for unique properties
unique properties for sale
in the specialized niche of ranch and land sales there is no substitute for the experience gained and the relationships built over time. pete nevin has been involved in northern nevada, northern california and west coast property sales for 25 years. as the highest producer for farwest r&c sales/mgt company and broker alex gassiot, pete is one of the few realtors in the area who specializes in ranches, land, homes, estates and waterfront properties for those who are seeking a truly unique lifestyle or investment.
working closely with his clients to achieve their goals regarding property acquisition and asset preservation, pete helps his clients preserve their land and gain financial benefits from conservation programs.
