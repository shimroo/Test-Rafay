welcome to
east coast cycle center
at east coast cycle center, we are powersports enthusiasts and we are excited to help you experience the fun and adventure that a quality can-am, honda, spyder, sea-doo, or polaris can bring into your life! we are a powersports dealership serving the bensalem, pennsylvania area, offering the best in new and pre-owned atvs, motorcycles, scooters, utvs, personal watercraft, and cargo trailers. our friendly and knowledgeable sales, financing, service, and parts departments are ready to offer outstanding service at every point, from assisting while you're making your choice to ongoing maintenance and customization. at east coast cycle center, we value the opportunity to create a long term relationship with our customers.
