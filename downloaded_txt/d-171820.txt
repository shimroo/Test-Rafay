town spotlightsee what’s new in cave creek
 






  
 
 















town hall closed for presidents day 


town hall will be closed for the presidents day holiday on monday, february 17. town hall offices reopen on tuesday, february 18. due to the holiday closure, the town council meeting is tuesday, february18 starting at 6:00 p.m. in the council chambers.			






groundbreaking for daisy mountain fire station 144 


the new station will be staffed jointly by the cave creek fire department and daisy mountain.						read on...







fire department hosting fire preparedness meetings 


the cave creek fire department is hosting two community meetings to discuss the town’s preparedness for any wildfires in our area.						read on...







spring brush, branches and weeds roundup 


to support the community in creating defensible space in their yards for fire prevention, the town of cave creek is hosting two drop-off events for residents (only) to discard green yard waste. march 15 and april 19						read on...







mayor gives annual state of the town address 


mayor robert morris provided an overview of 2024 and shared the upcoming projects and vision for the town of cave creek in 2025. watch the video of the presentation held on wednesday, january 29. (visual courtesy bob hughes)						read on...







council approved user fees go into effect february 1 


town council approved the adjusted user fee schedule as proposed, the fee schedule goes into effect on feb. 1, 2025.						read on...
