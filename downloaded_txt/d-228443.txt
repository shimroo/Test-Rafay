welcome to piedmont piano company. we are a family owned and operated business providing top quality pianos to the san francisco bay area, northern california and the world for over 40 years.we are factory authorized representatives for new yamaha and fazioli pianos, and we specialize in fine used pianos and always stock a large selection of pre-owned steinways. in addition to buying and selling pianos we offer music lessons, practice studio rentals, piano rentals by the day or by the month, and moving and storage of pianos.we work in association with the other branch of the family business, callahan piano service, who perform tuning, repairs, and restoration for our customers.we have an active concert series presenting the best local, national and international classical and jazz artists on stage in our beautiful showroom. we look forward to meeting you!
