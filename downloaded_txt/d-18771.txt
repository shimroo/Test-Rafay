the ultimate experience in arrive and drive kart racing.

procup karting and endurance karting were founded in 2001 in separate states, but both shared the vision of fun karting for all levels of drivers without the extreme costs and exhausting work associated with participating in motorsports. gone are the weeks of preparation and trailer hauling only to have your race end before the checkered flag dropped. in 2013, procup and endurance karting combined to provide the ultimate experience in arrive and drive kart racing.
