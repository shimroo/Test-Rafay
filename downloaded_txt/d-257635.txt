about us



                blue moon florist has been a full service florist in downingtown, pa for over 28 years, with outstanding quality flowers and services. we guarantee every flower delivery, whether it's in chester county, philadelphia, or worldwide.

blue moon florist makes every effort to give you the paramount floral experience time and again. blue moon florist was started with the vision of becoming the premier florist in chester county. our staff has over 100 years combined experience in the flower industry, and it has been our staff’s dream to please every customer we touch. we continue to stay above the curve of the flower industry to provide you the best floral experience possible.

we currently are members of the national society of american florist.
we deliver flowers to all towns in chester county pennsylvania including, downingtown, chester springs, west chester, malvern, coatesville, glenmoore, elverson, morgantown, thorndale, parkesburg and frazer.

ami and kevin



                ami started in the flower business at the age of 15 as an after school job. she soon fell in love with the industry and set off to college for business and combined her learned skill set captured from on the job training, working for various sized shops; with a sensible business acumen to start a flower shop in 1995. her husband kevin joined the business in 2003 and manages the sales department, oversees delivery and all it. kevin came from a career in sales working in the home sales and telecom industries. the couple have both served on many committees and boards in chester county over the years including the march of dimes women of achievement, the men's garden club, the chester county chamber of commerce, the downingtown chamber of commerce and the exton chamber of commerce and are active members of the western chester county chamber of commerce and the downingtown chamber of commerce. they believe in supporting the community they serve through their business and make several charitable donations to various non- profit agencies and organizations throughout the county.     

they have one son and live in elverson, pa with 3 cats and a dog.
