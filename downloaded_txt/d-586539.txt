serving your construction workwear needs with quality & durabilityfrom manufacturing to construction and every industry between, zane western apparel & work gear makes it easy for workers to find the high-quality, rugged clothing they need on the job. you’ll find top brands, like carhartt, ariat, and under armour, in our assortment. no matter where work takes you, we make it easy to stock up on essentials from jeans, shorts, flannels, outerwear, and insulated overalls to accessories like wallets, tool bags, socks, hats, and gloves.work boots and shoes are essential for construction sites, the factory floor, farmers’ fields, and more, keeping you safe, comfortable, and supported through every movement. we carry a variety of steel- and composite-toe work boots, plus a host of other options that deliver the performance you need to get the job done. to help you achieve the perfect fit and ultimate comfort, we stock numerous boot heights, toe shapes, and options for insulation.for the most demanding work environments, we offer a complete line of flame-resistant work gear from ariat and carhartt. these pieces meet standards set by the national fire protection association (nfpa) and feature optimal arc thermal performance value (atpv) ratings. items in our flame-resistant work gear collection also boast a number of key features, including:
flame-resistant jersey cotton construction
rib-knit collars and cuffs for easy wear
taped inside back seam to eliminate neck irritation
raglan armholes for greater ease of motion and reduced chafing
moisture-wicking technology that keeps you dry and comfortable
dark navy color-blocking in high-wear areas
side-seam construction to minimize twisting
