about desert sky mallthere's so much to discover
as a fully bilingual center, desert sky mall is the shopping destination for all families throughout the phoenix area. shoppers can visit curacao, burlington, dillard's clearance center, america cinemas, victoria’s secret, jd sports, foot locker and more than 130 other stores. dining options include flaming wok, la carreta de lily, mercado de los cielos, cinnabon, dairy queen and other restaurants.
