welcome to prairie point junction
we are a retail quilt shop in cozad, nebraska, that stocks 5,000 bolts of 100% cotton fabrics, 130 colors of wool blend felt, a large variety of 100 percent wool, valdani pearl cotton, plus hundreds of books, patterns, notions, and plenty of inspiration for all your quilt and sewing needs. 
we're just minutes off interstate 80 in central nebraska, so a perfect place to stop if your travels take you across our state.
hours: (central time)
monday-friday 10:00-5:00
saturday 10:00-4:00
we will be closed on the following holidays:
thanksgiving
november 29th (black friday)
christmas eve
christmas day
new year's eve
new year's day

cozad, nebraska is the place to be for a fun day out.
traveling?  visit cozad to enjoy all our boutiques, grab a bite to eat, check out our two musuems, and view our outdoor art.  we are known for our barn quilts, rhakenna's wings, cozad creates palette icons, and the sculpture garden at wilson public library.  visit barnquiltsdc.com for maps and more info.
