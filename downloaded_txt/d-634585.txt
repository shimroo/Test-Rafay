bps depot - the home of ladders and scaffold towers
here at bps depot we have over 10 years’ experience supplying british tradespeople and members of the public with the highest quality height access equipment. over the years we’ve grown to understand exactly what our customers want and need, and our customer service has been praised as second-to-none from independent feedback.
our customer service hotline is open seven days a week – 8:30 to 5:30 monday to friday and 10:00 to 4:00 on saturdays, sundays and bank holidays. if you ever need advice on which ladder is best suited to your particular needs or require help with an order, our team is always on hand.
not only do we value our customer service operation, we also believe that you should be able to buy the equipment you need to do your job without having to break the bank. we price all our products very competitively, offering regular promotions, deals and discounts across our product range. on top of this, we also offer free next day delivery across the uk, and free lifetime guarantees on our products.
it doesn’t matter what job you’re preparing for, we have a ladder or other height access solution which will fit your requirements. we stock all main ladder types, including a number of different extension ladders, combination ladders, step ladders, loft ladders, and more, as well as more specialised equipment like podium steps, and scaffold towers,.
our products are all rigorously tested, meeting all necessary safety regulations and featuring full, clear use instructions and guidelines. it doesn’t matter if you’re an experienced user or you’re just buying your first ladder, we can help you get the job done safely.
have a browse of our site to see which products we have in stock, and don’t hesitate to give us a ring if you want to know more.
