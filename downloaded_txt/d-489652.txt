who we are



we are a community-oriented church, and as episcopalians, we’re called to honor the dignity of every human being and care for all of god’s created order. we are growing, and we would love for you to be part of our future! you are welcome here. you have friends here. 




    worship with us
  


























our mission



we invite everyone to share communion around god’s altar.we strive to be a welcoming and relevant christian community doing what matters through prayer, worship, teaching, outreach, fellowship and service.we will demonstrate the real-life application of scripture, tradition and reason by providing a safe place to respond to each other’s needs, hurts, joys and interests.




    serve with us
  


























small groups



fellowship allows us to share time outside of worship and continue to spread god’s love. each week we have a social hour after church, when we enjoy snacks and beverages along with each other’s company. we also have foyer groups, small groups that share meals. resurrection has a chapter of the faith-based group the order of the daughters of the king® that meets monthly.




    gather with us
  


























our faith



we seek to grow our spirituality, and we offer formation classes for children and youth as well as adults.
