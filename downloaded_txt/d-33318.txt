welcome to diecast direct




diecast direct, inc. began in the early 1980's out of a love for diecast models and model trains.established in frankfort, ky, we are centrally located and are able to offer quick and efficient service to any location in the u.s.in the early years, we began with a handful of loyal customers and we offered less than 100 items from only two manufacturers.today, over 135,000 customers have entrusted us to provide them diecast models from a current selection of over 8,000 items from over 175 manufacturers!thank you for visiting our website and we hope that we can be of service to you!
