* when...through this evening.

* impacts...extensive street flooding and flooding of creeks and
rivers are possible. area creeks and streams are running high and
could flood with more heavy rain.

* additional details...
- two to four inches of rain has already fallen, with numerous
reports of life-threatening flooding and several flash flood
warnings in effect. an additional half inch to one and a half
inches, with localized amounts up to two inches, are expected
to fall through midday today. rainfall on top of excessively
saturated soils and elevated flows in moderate to major flood
stage could cause high impact flash flooding and river
flooding. rock and mud slides will also be possible.
- http://www.weather.gov/safety/flood

precautionary/preparedness actions...

you should monitor later forecasts and be alert for possible flood
warnings. those living in areas prone to flooding should be prepared
to take action should flooding develop.
