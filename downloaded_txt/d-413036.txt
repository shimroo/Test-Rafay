our mission




sri narasingha chaitanya ashram is situated on the banks of the sacred kaveri river in south india providing a sanctuary for those seeking spiritual advancement in life. the ashram is far away from the stressful hustle and bustle of city life and is the perfect atmosphere for meditation, devotional practices and holistic living.
