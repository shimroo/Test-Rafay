the acr provides cross-sectoral perspectives and leadership on resource opportunities and challenges in alberta’s resource sector.we connect members and stakeholders.we collaborate to find solutions.we advance discussions and ideas.




    learn more
  














a thriving, responsible and diversified resource sector can support prosperity and quality of life for all albertans.

our memberswe are a member-driven organization. that means we listen and respond to what’s on your minds. we track the issues, bring everyone together, and seek solutions and ideas that support your company and the entire industry.



 











our committees
our committees are inclusive places of discussion. outputs can vary: papers, positions, coalitions, education sessions, or sounding boards to test new regulatory, policy or social ideas.
