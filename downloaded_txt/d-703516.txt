welcome to the port of longview







operating since 1921, the port of longview has 8 marine terminals and waterfront industrial property spanning 835 acres on the deep-draft columbia river, 66 miles from the pacific ocean in southwest washington state.

economic benefits







the port plays an important role in economic development by creating jobs in manufacturing and international trade. here at the port, we develop infrastructure to support these jobs, creating a positive economic ripple that strengthens the local community.

tour the port of longview!







as an economic cornerstone of our community, we think it is crucial that each citizen has the opportunity to see the port in action and get a crash course of the port’s 100-year history. we’re proud of the work we do here, and our summer tour series provides the perfect opportunity for the public to see its tax dollars at work. from cargo handling, to what it means to be washington’s working port, to how we compete globally to achieve local benefits, you’ll learn it all on our summer tour series!
