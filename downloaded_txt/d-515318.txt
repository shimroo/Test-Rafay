public noticebitter creek shooting sports complexjanuary 2025this public notice offers an opportunity for all interested parties to comment on the proposed development of a shooting range facility, providing information necessary to inform the city of altus for evaluation and review. this public notice is issued jointly with the oklahoma department of wildlife conservation (odwc).click to view full noticethe city of altus, has prepared a draft environmental assessment (draft ea) in accordance with the national environmental policy act (nepa) of 1969, as amended, for the proposed development of a shooting range facility in jackson county, oklahoma. the draft ea was prepared to assess and document potential effects to the human and natural environment of the project’s proposed action. the draft ea and supporting information are provided with issuance of this public notice on 30 january 2025, to initiate the 30-day public review and comment period. therefore, the 30-day public comment period for this draft ea runs from january 30, 2025, to march 1, 2025.click to view draft environmental assessment
