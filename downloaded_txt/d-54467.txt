ea is an executive non-departmental public body, sponsored by the department for environment, food & rural affairs, supported by 1 public body.

all statutory and legal documents (as well as all important and time-critical correspondence), should be served by email to legalsupport@environment-agency.gov.uk, with a hard copy posted to the address above. you should only use alternative contact details if you have been formally notified in writing by an environment agency legal representative.all other correspondence including complaints should be sent to enquiries@environment-agency.gov.uk.
