welcome to mid tenn powersports
welcome to mid tenn powersports, your premier destination for all things powersports in lebanon, tennessee! as a trusted powersports dealer, we proudly offer an extensive lineup of industry-leading brands, including polaris, honda, suzuki, yamaha waverunner, atlas golf carts, and bennington pontoon boats. whether you’re looking for an adrenaline-packed atv, a smooth and powerful personal watercraft, or a stylish pontoon, we have something to fuel every adventure. shop our in-stock inventory of side by sides, quads, motorcycles, pwcs, jet boats and more. mid tenn powersports is located on leeville pike in lebanon, tn, near mt. juliet, gallatin, hendersonville, smyrna, murfreesboro, and nashville. visit our dealership or contact us today!
