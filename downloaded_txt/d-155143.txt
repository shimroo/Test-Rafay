equipment division















proudly representing…











































































at danko emergency equipment, we know the fine margins between life and death – and the difference a matter of seconds makes. have complete confidence in your equipment when you partner with us – since 1974, we’ve manufactured and sold high-quality emergency vehicles and equipment that helps you save lives. 































after shopping around, our customers come to realize our quality and efficiency are simply unmatched. our manufacturing capabilities stretch throughout engineering and designing our own pumpers, tankers, tenders, wildland, quick attacks, brush trucks, mini-pumpers, and more for a number of fire stations. we sell a variety of equipment products and represent other oems selling type i, type ii & type iii ambulances, rescue trucks, mini-pumpers, custom pumper, aerials, platforms and more, we service everything we sell! after all, your equipment plays a vital role in allowing you to carry out your job to the best of your abilities – and without it, you wouldn’t be able to tackle those emergencies head-on. our in-house technicians can repair anything from small items to vehicles, while our evt-certified field technicians can come to you to carry out pump testing, ground ladder testing, repairs and service for all brands. discuss your needs with our team today.
