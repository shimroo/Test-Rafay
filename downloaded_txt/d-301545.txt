welcome to amari hotels and resorts
brighten your world with amari

                            amari’s dedication to enriching your every delightful moment goes beyond typical hospitality. every facet, from architecture to design, art, cuisine, and service, reflects the distinctive essence of its locale. from picturesque coastlines to vibrant city centers, amari cherishes guests' moments of joy, crafting extraordinary through cultural narratives. here, surpassing conventional norms, local heritage merges seamlessly with contemporary expressions, brightening your world with vibrant experiences. always forward-thinking and attuned to your needs, amari provides personalised warmth at every guest interaction. experience the inviting world of amari, where every stay is a celebration of contemporary local flairs rooted in tradition.

the amari experience
a warm welcome
feel the warmth of amari from the moment you arrive. our amari host is the friendly helping hand to greet and guide you throughout your stay, from naming the best restaurants to advising what to do in town. with the sort of intuition you’d expect from a best friend, amari hosts are there to help make each destination your own.




















the amari experience
a journey of culinary discovery
all-day restaurant, amaya food gallery showcases fresh asian produce in a contemporary and hip dining environment. inviting live cooking stations and street food favourites entice you to taste, share and enjoy the flavours of modern asia. throughout the day, amaya transforms to create a space perfect for social interactions with food and drink ideal for casually sharing with friends and colleagues, asian style. so get together and discover amaya.




















the amari experience
a family experience
we want to make sure all our guests have a memorable experience, even the young ones. to make the trip extra special, our amari hosts are on hand to make sure our little guests are happy and entertained with an assortment of games, souvenirs and treats along the way. from poolside games to creative crafting, there are countless activities for families to enjoy together at amari.
