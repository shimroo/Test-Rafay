the mission of the mcconnell foundation is helping build better communities through philanthropy.
















														grantseekers												 

													as a broad-based funder, we award grants to 501(c)(3) nonprofit organizations, public education institutions, and government entities located in our giving area which is modoc, siskiyou, shasta, trinity and tehama counties, and the countries of nepal and laos.												




learn more about our grants













														meeting facilities												 

													the mcconnell foundation at lema ranch has meeting space available on a complimentary basis for use by 501 (c)(3) nonprofit organizations, school groups, and government agencies.												




learn more about our facilities













														visit the trails												 

													one way the mcconnell foundation shares its space is by providing eight miles of trails available to walkers and runners from dawn until dusk every day.												




learn more about our trails
