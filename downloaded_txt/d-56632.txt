gary j. alter, m.d.
world renowned plastic surgeon








board certified plastic surgeon
& board certified urologist
over 25 years of experience in plastic & reconstructive surgery






gary j. alter, m.d. is a world renown plastic surgeon with double board certification by both the american board of plastic surgery and american board of urology. for over two decades he has been recognized by his peers for his pioneering work in female genital surgery, labiaplasty or labia minora surgery, penis/scrotal surgery, penis enhancement, and transgender surgery. he is an expert in male/female genital anatomy, scar minimization, tissue transferring, and cosmetics. dr. alter believes all clinical judgements should work to meet the needs and desires of each individual patient. he currently has two practices in beverly hills and new york city.

board certified plastic surgeon
board certified urologist
assistant clinical professor of plastic surgery, ucla
member, american society of plastic surgeons
member, american association of plastic surgeons
member, american urological association
member, society of genitourinary reconstructive surgeons
