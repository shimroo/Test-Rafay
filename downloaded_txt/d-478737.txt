important scam alerts
ignore any social media accounts clamining to be affiliated with penpalsnow.  penpalsnow is not present on any social media.

ignore and delete any e-mails:

mentioning penpalsnow lottery as there is no such thing;
requesting credit card, bank information or money transfer from you;
asking you for personal information, id cards etc. in order to transfer money or wealth to you

such e-mails are virtually always an e-mail phishing or scamming attempts.
