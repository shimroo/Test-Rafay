restorations and service

our service department can perform any service and/or restoration on your fiat. we work by appointment only. please call us at 831-423-0218 to discuss your car's needs.

how to use the site

we use original equipment fiat part numbers (new parts ending with an "n" and used parts ending with a "u"), but in many cases part numbers from other companies might work, so give those part numbers a try when using our search feature. if you don’t know the part number that’s ok, you can search by department as used by fiat, and those departments break down into categories on the left side of the screen. you can also narrow down your search results by choosing a car model, as well as using the other options under the advanced search capability.

if you have any questions, contact us and we will try our best to help. we look forward to many enjoyable years of fiat motoring with you.we can be reached by phone @ 831-423-0218 from 1:00 pm to 6:00 pm pacific standard time, on monday, tuesday, thursday, & friday. it's our pleasure to help keep these wonderful autos on the road!
