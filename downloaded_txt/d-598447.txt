  about robert i. merrill co.

 our team

we have a team of architectural hardware consultants (ahc) and certified fire and egress door inspectors (cfdai) on staff, ensuring that we stay up-to-date and well-versed in industry standards and practices. our expertise in both mechanical and electronic systems sets us apart in the industry, making us a highly respected provider in the market. unlike other companies that specialize in only certain aspects of the door-opening process, we offer a comprehensive range of products and services, allowing us to take full ownership and accountability for every project.






      



     




 our goal

at robert i. merrill company, our ultimate goal is to earn your trust and establish a long-lasting relationship with you. customer satisfaction is our top priority, no matter the size of the order. we are dedicated to delivering excellent customer service while staying ahead of industry trends and business practices. our loyal customer base is a testament to our unwavering commitment to treating every customer with the utmost respect and care.






       more than just the standard - custom shops for custom doors & frames

in addition to our dedication to customer service, we pride ourselves on our diverse range of offerings. alongside our extensive inventory, we operate custom shops specializing in both wood doors and hollow metal doors. this enables us to cater to a wide array of project needs, whether you require the warmth and natural beauty of wood or the durability and security of metal. our custom shops boast fast turnaround times, ensuring that your project schedule is met efficiently and effectively.
