we’re behind you the whole way!
herold trailer sales, locally owned rv dealer serving central iowa since 1964. we have been located for 55 years at hwy 92 west or 1806 west 2nd ave in indianola. we sell and service new and used travel trailers, fifth wheels, rv's and fold down campers in the greater des moines and surrounding iowa and missouri communities. our large inventory includes some of the most popular manufacturers in the industry. our service department has qualified technicians, and we are ready to help you with all of your service needs — including winterization, complete restoration, and we do insurance work estimates. we have a large inventory of parts in stock at our indianola location, near des moines, or you may take advantage of ordering parts and accessories online at heroldtrailersales.com or by phone at 515-961-7405. let us help you with all your camping needs. we are behind you all the way.
