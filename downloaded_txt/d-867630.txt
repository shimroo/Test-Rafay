 miami’s trusted destination for 



boating enthusiasts



located in the heart of coconut grove, grove harbour marina has been a premier destination for boating enthusiasts for over 50 years. our prime location on biscayne bay, combined with exceptional marina services, offers unparalleled access to the bay’s pristine waters. experience the ultimate in luxury boating at grove harbour.visit our boatyard

our legacy
