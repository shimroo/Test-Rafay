state college, pennsylvania, the home of penn state university, is the next host for the state games of america (sga) in 2026! the keystone state games, happy valley sports & entertainment alliance, happy valley adventure bureau, and penn state university are thrilled to welcome all of the state games athletes from across the country to compete in happy valley, pa. 










the state games of america is part of the national congress of state games (ncsg), a membership organization comprised of 30 summer state games and 10 winter state games organizations. the ncsg is a member of the united states olympic committee’s multi sport organizational council. the mission of the ncsg is to support state games member organizations in the promotion of health, fitness and character building through multi-sport competitions and physical activities all across the usa.
