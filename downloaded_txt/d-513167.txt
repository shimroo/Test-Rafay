authentic tastethe taste of vail, in its 34th year, is a non-profit organization founded in 1990 by a group of food and wine enthusiasts. we support the amazing chefs and wineries of our valley and from all over the world. moving forward we want to work closely with innovative chefs, vintners, brewers, and distillers who are excited to share their unique perspectives.
