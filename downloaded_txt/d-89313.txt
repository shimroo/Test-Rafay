monitoring board
the tv parental guidelines monitoring board is comprised of experts from the television industry and public interest advocates who are responsible for ensuring there is as much uniformity and consistency in applying the parental guidelines as possible. the board also reviews complaints about specific program ratings to help ensure accuracy.
