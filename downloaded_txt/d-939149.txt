faith first.




        a faith-lived community with gospel values through catholic education
      



























































faith in our future enrollment initiativeone hallmark of catholicism is that it calls us together as a family of faith to support one another, our parishes, and our schools. with a look toward our future, we are excited to announce the two-phase faith in our future enrollment initiative that will support an increase in school enrollment. 




    learn more about our new transfer grants!
  



academic excellence and an enriching high school experiencealleman catholic high school is a catholic educational community that by our actions, values, and modeling, exemplifies a caring atmosphere in a faith-lived community.we strive to provide a foundation for the religious, moral, intellectual, emotional and social development of each individual. we’re determined to produce a catalyst for change and for growth in the catholic community as well as in society.














100%graduation ratealleman catholic high school is mindful of the uniqueness of individuals and appreciative of the differences among us.  we provide an academic atmosphere that promotes educational experiences needed for each student to meet his/her potential.  we not only educate our students, we help form them.



$16 millionscholarship offered to alleman studentslast year graduating seniors from alleman high school were awarded a combined $16 million on scholarship money. 78% of our students went on to a 4-year degree program, 16% of students went on to a 2-year degree program, and 2% of our students joined a military or vocational program.














student life













athleticsalleman catholic high school considers the athletic experience to be an important  part of our students' overall development and are proud that upwards of  65% of our student body participate in one or more of the programs.  we will work hard to maintain the proper perspective that a student-athlete is a student first and an athlete second.













our faith… in actionview our 2024-2025 spiritual schedule here!alleman catholic high school develops a faith-lived community by exemplifying gospel values through catholic education. by our actions, values, and modeling, we  exemplify a caring atmosphere in a faith-lived community.













academicsalleman students benefit from an individualized academic program that is grounded in faith and enrichment.  the average act composite of alleman students is 5 points higher than that of other local schools.  through a predominantly college-preparatory environment, alleman works  hard to prepare your student for the next step in their education.
