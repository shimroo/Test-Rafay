ics is an anglican mission agency serving english-speaking people, of which there are around 1.5 billion, all over the world. our mission and ministry extends to europe, north africa, south america, the south atlantic, and southeast asia where we support and collaborate with approximately 48 chaplains and assistant chaplains in new and existing congregations. in addition, our short-term mission provides services in popular vacation spots in europe. come join us as we share the message of christ’s love with people all over the globe!
