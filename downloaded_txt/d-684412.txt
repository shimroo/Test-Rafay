electrical preventive maintenance
billitier electric, inc. is proud to be a part of the movement towards higher efficiency designs and sustainability in construction, whether it’s a new building, or an existing renovation.






 

leed services
billitier electric, inc. is proud to be a part of the movement towards higher efficiency designs and sustainability in construction.







 

engineering & design
our highly innovative design team always employs the latest cad electrical design software. we offer fast-track value oriented technologically advanced solutions.






 

electronic control systems
billitier has experience in the latest automated temperature control systems, whether it is commercial or industrial full building systems or upgrades. 






 

industrial & commercial
our “construction” teams bring it all together for the full array of both commercial and industrial electrical contractor services projects.






 

fiber optics
the billitier communications department is on-call 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. for all emergencies including fusion splicing certified repairs with an otdr (optical time domain reflectometer).






 

network/alarm/sound
the billitier teledata/sound team brings it all together for a broad array of auxiliary systems. our team can install network wiring and paging systems from a small office up to 50k sq. ft.






 

retrofits/upgrades
before investing in all new replacement systems, talk to us about our creative, cost saving retrofit services – from machinery upgrades to technology retrofits for existing equipment.






 

service/maintenance
billitier keeps it all together after your facility opens with a full electrical maintenance crew. they will routinely check your systems to ensure your facility is running smoothly.






 

service locations
billitier provides full electrical contractor services to major locations in new york, pennsylvania, and florida. visit our contact us page for office locations.
