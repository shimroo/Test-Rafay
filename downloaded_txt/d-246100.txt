what we do
our solutions are at the heart of functions that sense, regulate and drive electro-mechanical systems.

                            our products are in automotive powertrain and safety systems, in hyperscale data centers and the automation enabling efficient factories. whether you drive a vehicle, use cloud data services or receive a package delivery, you are benefiting from our technology.
