new miyuki tila beads
the tilas are back with a new range of full, half and quarter tila beads.

we have lots of free downloadable projects for the full, half and quarter tila beads.

new preciosa terra intensive lined seed beads
new range of inner lined terra intensive seed beads just in, joining the very popular terra intensive glossy and terra intensive matt ranges.

new hearts necklace pattern
