visit lake quinault, washington
welcome to the southern gateway of olympic national park! 
we are a community centered around lake quinault and surrounded by temperate rain forest. take a drive around the lake to view beautiful waterfalls, record breaking trees, and breathtaking wildlife. relax in a welcoming hotel or campground, hike miles of trails through the rain forest, come fish in our rivers and swim in our lake!
