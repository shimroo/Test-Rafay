the herb cottage


the herb cottage is an herbal wellness business


and herb plant nursery focused on using herbs in all their wondrous ways. i create herbal tea blends, handmade skin care products, extracts and bitters with an eye toward good health and staying well. instead of reaching for an over-the-counter medication, look to your kitchen cabinet. many of the spices you already have will offer you a hand in overcoming some of the daily problems and irritations we all encounter.
i have herb plants in season for your culinary adventures, to grow for tea and to bring pollinators and other beneficial insects to your garden areas. 
there is a wealth of information throughout the website. if your interest in herbs is in growing them, check out the gardening & growing page. you’ll find information on growing herbs, vegetables, house plants and more. 
the herbs and wellness page has information on using herbs responsibly for your good health and wellness of you and your family. 
you can find me most days here at the herb cottage. 
call, email or text me to make sure i’ll be here if you’d like to come on out. phone: 361-258-1192. email: cindy@theherbcottage.com



my table at the gulf coast herb fair in galveston at the moody gardens hotel, may 2023.

if you are looking for a speaker for a garden club, master gardener class or other group or event, i am available to speak at your venue. my talks range from growing herbs, using herbs in the kitchen, using herbs for wellness, all about herb tea, growing herbs for pollinators, best practices for succulent growing and more. 
call, email or text me for more information. phone: 361-258-1192. email: cindy@theherbcottage.com

i am a proud affiliate of botanical interest seed. i earn a small percentage of each sale at no additional cost to you!
