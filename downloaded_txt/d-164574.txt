purpose:
  teaneckshuls@groups.io is an announcement 


  list for the jewish community
  and shuls
  in the teaneck / 


  bergenfield / new milford vicinity. (created sep
  8, 2000.)

to subscribe go
  to 
  https://groups.io/g/teaneckshuls/join



  or send a blank email to 
  teaneckshuls+subscribe@groups.io



  keep your eyes open for a questionnaire from
  teaneckshuls+


  owner@groups.io which you will need to reply to in order
  to 


  be granted membership (if you are joining from a groups.io 


  email, make sure to
  check that email for the questionnaire.).

to send an email to the list, address it to 
  


teaneckshuls@groups.io  reply only to sender.
