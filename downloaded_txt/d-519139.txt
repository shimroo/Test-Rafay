the zmbh is a center
	for research and higher education in molecular biology and
	biomedicine. our research aims at fundamental questions of
	molecular and cellular biology at the level of molecules,
	cells and organisms. joint research interests resulted in the
	formation of
	the dkfz-zmbh-alliance,
	a close collaboration of the zmbh with the research program
	cell and tumor biology of the german cancer research center
	(dkfz).
