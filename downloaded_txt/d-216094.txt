    about wow
  














    get in touch
  



for over 30 years, a white orchid wedding known as wow, has specialized in unique island weddings and receptions from simple to lavish. island settings such as exquisite resorts, pristine white sand beaches, private estates and charming chapels await your arrival. recognized for our attention to detail along with exquisite custom wedding and event designs, a white orchid wedding ensures the most memorable and spectacular events tailored to your desires. over the years, wow has curated the ‘best of the best’ and our professional team of maui wedding planners will assist in your every need.  wow offers the finest venues on all of the hawaiian islands, including oahu, maui, big island, lanai, and kauai. we are dedicated to find the perfect venue to complement your hawaii wedding, vow-renewal, birthday, special celebration or corporate event. a white orchid wedding offers both custom event curation and hawaii wedding packages with all-inclusive services available.  clients of wow have full access to our in-house team of professionals comprised of the most talented, dedicated, creative and passionate event and design specialists in the industry. with the collaboration between your personal event coordinator and our design team, you will receive expert professional support and advice coupled with unmatched vision and creativity. above all, we welcome you with open arms and the true essence of the aloha spirit in all that we do.
