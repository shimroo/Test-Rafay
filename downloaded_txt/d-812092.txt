3:00 pm – 4:30 pm15:00 – 16:303:00 pm – 4:30 pm


first congregational church of evanston’s free concert series music first returns on february 16th at 3 pm. the afternoon will showcase local musicians sonya major and zach kidder (major duo) performing jazzy favorites in first congregational’s acoustically superb sanctuary at 1445 hinman ave. the artists will join attendees for a cookie reception following their performance.blending intricate guitar artistry with soulful, emotive vocals, major and kidder have performed with some of chicago’s most acclaimed musicians, captivating audiences across chicago with their fresh interpretations of classic jazz and pop songs. their combined artistry brings sophistication and depth to every performance.music first is dedicated to supporting local musicians, promoting public arts, and building community through free concerts in evanston. it is a project of first congregational church of evanston (fcce), a progressive congregation of the united church of christ committed to musical excellence and the arts.this concert is free and open to all: join us for a jazzy afternoon!

worship with us

our church gathers on sundays at 10 am to sing praise to god and build the beloved community. our services typically last an hour and feature soul-stirring music, powerful preaching, and profound prayer. we hope you will join us — in-person or online!




learn more







learn about us

we are a progressive christian church filled with hope and abounding in joy. we believe all people are created in god’s image and are equal in god’s sight. we believe god loves us just as we are and that god calls all of us to reconciliation and transformation.




learn more







serve with us

first congregational serves the hungry and unhoused. we stand with the vulnerable and oppressed. we seek the liberation of all peoples. join us as we seek to build a world established with justice, overflowing with mercy, and abounding in peace.




learn more











































actionwe put love in action by donating our time and talents, serving the hungry and unhoused in our city, and amplifying the voice of marginalized groups, including immigrants and the lgbtq+ community.















compassionwe are a caring, joyful community that embraces differences of age, ability, race, sexual orientation, gender identity and expression, and national origin. we mean it when we say “no matter who you are or where you are on life’s journey, you are welcome here!”















justicewe are grounded in the radical inclusivity of god’s love. we are committed to becoming an anti-racist congregation through financial commitment, education, and action. we are a community that is open to and affirming of our lgbtq+ siblings. we are an immigrant welcoming congregation.
