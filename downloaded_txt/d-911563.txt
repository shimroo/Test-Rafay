the public archaeology laboratory, inc. is a leading new england cultural resource management firm.



























pal’s widely respected reputation within the crm industry is a result of four decades of high-quality performance in support of more than 4,000 development and planning projects.




    learn more
  














our services















archaeology
archaeological surveys, cemetery investigations, construction monitoring, tribal consultation, and laboratory & curation services.




                        learn more
                      












architectural history
historic architectural survey, archival documentation, national register nominations, historic tax credits, visual impact assessments, and historic structure reports.




                        learn more
                      












historic preservation planning
cultural resource management plans, regulatory compliance, municipal surveys & preservation plans, expert testimony, and avoidance, protection & post-review discovery plans.




                        learn more
                      












education
interpretive exhibits, publications & presentations, educational programs, and pal resources.
