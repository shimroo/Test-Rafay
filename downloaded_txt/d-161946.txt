brea 8k street closures 


the annual brea 8k classic race will take place on sunday, february 23. roads in the area will start to close starting at 4:00 a.m. and the course should be fully cleared by 9:30 a.m. starting this year, the race start time will be 7:00 a.m.						read on...






construction on birch st & kraemer blvd 


construction at the intersection of birch st and kraemer blvd will begin on february 10 to may 23 from the hours of 7:00 a.m. to 4:00 p.m. this project consists of the removal and replacement of existing water mains, pvr vault, and paving upgrades.						read on...






go. serve. brea 


the city of brea is proud to announce go. serve. brea. taking place on saturday, april 26. this will replace the previous love brea event. go. serve. brea. is a year-round city-wide volunteer initiative that will include this day of service.			find out more here






register for engage brea 


engage brea is being offered again this spring. this eight week community engagement program is hosted by the city and provides an overview of brea’s local government operations, departments, and external agencies that partner with our community.						read on...






purchase overnight parking permits for 2025 


apply now for your new overnight parking permit for 2025. without a visible 2025 parking permit, vehicles can be ticketed if left on public streets between the hours of 2:00 - 6:00 a.m. create your online account and purchase your new permit below.			apply now






spring 2025 digital brea line now available

city opposes scaqmd amendments 


the city is opposing two south coast air quality district (scaqmd) amendments affecting residential furnaces and small water heaters.						read on...
