our missionsaving orangutans by protecting their tropical forest habitat, working with local communities, and promoting research and education.

  

120+orangutans translocated from potential conflict back to the wild



24orphaned orangutans cared for and released back into the wild



500,000+acres of tropical rainforest actively protected
