about us 



at el monte rv sales we sell what we call “like new used rvs”. the majority of our sales units have come from our rental fleet where they were rented for approximately 18-months before hitting the sales floor. because we purchase a large volume of rvs right from the manufacturer we can sell our units at lower prices than you’ll find anywhere else.our buyers prefer our units to the competition because every one must pass a thorough multi-point inspection before we sell them. plus it’s only with el monte rv sales that you’ll find the exclusive road bear certification. being road bear certified means that every unit we sell is truly ready to hit the road when you pick it up.we’re proud of our over 50-year history of providing the finest used motorhomes in the business. stop by one of our dealerships today and discover what makes us different.
