    welcome to carolina doggie playland!

open since 2004, carolina doggie playland is one of charlotte's oldest and most established dog daycare and boarding facilities. carolina doggie playland is not a franchise, but a small business owned and run by charlotte residents tina and mike manns. we love to do out-of-the-box activities, like frozen peanut butter kongs and licky mats or bubble dance parties for mental and physical stimulation - we also play a lot of catch!  we strive to show how much we love our pack! here at cdp, we pride ourselves on our focus of giving the very best care for your dog while also having fun!offering complimentary first day of daycare!
