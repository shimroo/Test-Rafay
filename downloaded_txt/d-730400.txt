✨program highlight february 2025✨ 4-hers enrolled in the beef, sheep, swine, dairy, goat, rabbit, and poultry projects met up for fast-paced learning during the 4-h livestock league clover university event. as part of the event, participants learned to identify feed grain seeds, discovered the nutritional value of feed components, built bunny nesting boxes, sampled a variety of dairy products, learned to read swine ear notches, got a close-up view of the 4-parts of a cow's stomach, and met with walworth county fair junior animal superintendents.
