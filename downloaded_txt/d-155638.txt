our ingredient pledge
at breyers®, our pledge to start with only high quality ingredients has made us a family favorite since 1866.








we always start with high quality ingredients






100% grade a
milk and cream






 










we partner with american farmers for 100% grade a milk and cream that comes from cows not treated with artificial growth hormones.*
*the fda states that no significant difference has been shown between dairy derived from rbst-treated and non-rbst-treated cows.







naturally sourced
colors and flavors






 










we use naturally sourced colors and flavors, from sun-ripened strawberries to real vanilla bean specks you can see!







sustainably sourced
fruit and vanilla**






 










we work with the rainforest alliance and others to ensure all fruit and vanilla in our sweet treats is 100% sustainably sourced.**
**in breyers® cookies & candies, our product, before the inclusion of other cookies, candies, sauces or fruit from other suppliers, will abide by this claim.
