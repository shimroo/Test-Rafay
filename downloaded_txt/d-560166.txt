about us



welcome to bone daddy’s – where fun and bbq meet.we’re a texas lowcountry bbq restaurant focused on providing the best possible experience as soon as customers walk through our doors. take in the sights and sounds, with vibrant décor and lights covering the walls, tvs queued up with texas sports at every corner and tables filled to the brim with those enjoying good food and good friends. we’re open to all and provide a place where food and fun go hand in hand.
