   classics, muscle cars, resto-mods, customs, & hot rods suburban rod & custom classics is a full service restoration and rod shop.  in our original location since 1963, we are your one-stop shop for top quality restorations, custom fabrication, custom paint, graphics, mechanical, interior, and glass. we handle everything from full and partial builds to minor repairs, as well as full service mechanical work.  no job is too big or too small for us….. we do it all and we do it right!we’ve built a reputation for our meticulous attention to detail as well as our dedication to using only the best quality parts and the latest technology available.  we’ve spent years assembling a team of the finest technicians in the area for mechanical installation and repair, body repair and fabrication, and finishing.we offer design assistance, we can provide you with a detailed insurance appraisal, and we can help you with title problems. we have a long list of very satisfied customers and will be happy to provide you with that list.we invite you to take a tour through our website and revisit frequently. we always have vehicles for sale and a photo gallery of vehicles we've built and restored for our customers. we also invite you to give us a call to set up a tour of our shop! your one-stop shop for body, paint, mechnical, glass, & interior.
