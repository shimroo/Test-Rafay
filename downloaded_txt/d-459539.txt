the division of educational outreach 






 

educational outreach proactively cultivates personal and professional growth through
                              high quality courses, programs and services.

distance and online programs
wcu is home to top-ranked programs in fields ranging from nursing to education to
                                 engineering. we build online learning communities that bring accomplished students
                                 together with professors dedicated to their success. discover the possibilities at distance.wcu.edu.

military student services
wcu is committed to providing exemplary support to members of the military and veterans,
                                    their spouses, and their college-aged children. we provide one-on-one admissions and
                                    financial aid counseling to ensure our military students are able to maximize their
                                    benefits and receive a high-quality, affordable education. visit military.wcu.edu for more information.
office of continuing education and workforce training
find fulfillment, explore your interests, learn new skills or further your career
                                    in our community classrooms. throughout the year, we offer special events, ongoing
                                    programs and educational assistance to people of all walks of life in our local, regional
                                    and global community. explore what we offer at learn.wcu.edu.

testing center
the testing center provides on-site testing at the cullowhee campus. we offer clep, critical
                                    reading sat for nursing program applicants, mat, teas®, dsst, praxis, gre, toefl and online
                                    test prep courses. learn more at testing.wcu.edu.
