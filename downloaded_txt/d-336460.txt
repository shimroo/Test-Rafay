      welcome to tenants & neighbors                    

 

we are a members-led statewide center for tenant power in new york! our tenant organizing and advocacy work are fueled by your energy, commitment, moral and financial support. your ongoing support helps us build tenant power to keep new york affordable for all. thank you!




    show your support
  














 do you have housing questions?
























responding to covid-19as new york city tenants slowly recover from the deadly impacts of covid-19—tenants & neighbors is playing a crucial role is ensuring that the needs of tenants, especially, tenants who were already at-risk of being displaced due to rising rents in rapidly gentrifying new york city neighborhoods are not displaced or face eviction. 




    learn more
  













join the movementannual membership dues are currently $35 for individuals and $10 for tenants residing in subsidized housing, tenants with reduced income and students. when you pay your dues, it is extremely helpful if you are able to make an additional donation . for a copy of our membership brochure click                                                   here.                                                                               




    learn more
  













housing justice for all tenants & neighbors is a coordinating member of the housing justice for all (hj4a) upstate/downstate alliance that won historic rent law reform in new york state in 2019.  on june 14, 2019, the upstate downstate housing alliance won the most sweeping & radical changes to new york state’s rent regulation system in the past 20 years.
