the robey theatre company’s mission is to develop and produce plays about global black experiences. 



the organization was found in 1994 and began as a readers’ circle centering on “black classics.”  the robey theatre company was formally incorporated in 1996 by founders ben guillory and danny glover, veteran actors and friends who are dedicated to ensuring opportunities for theatre actors in hollywood.  we are a community of artists and creatives who believe in the transformational power of plays. we are dedicated to  providing a nurturing and supportive environment for bipoc artists and creatives. our approach is collaborative and encourages exploration. 





























the robey theatre company has and will always serve our community. in the words of dr. dubois, "by us, for us, about us, and near us."— danny glover, co-founder

advanced scene study workshop



actors are encouraged to develop their craft through the study and practice of theatrical disciplines - voice, speech, movement, analysis, and human behavior. 
















    learn more
  



playwrights lab



the playwrights lab is dedicated to the art of storytelling and high-quality playmaking. the process is collaborative and designed to encourage artistic growth and expression.  built into the design are opportunities for ongoing feedback through guided discussion.
















    learn more
  



award-winning productions



for more thirty-years the robey theatre company has been known for its award-winning plays. told from the lens of a global black experience, the organization is dedicated to high-quality, socially -conscious, and authentic work.
