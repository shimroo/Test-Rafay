welcome
welcome to beautiful nassau county, located in the northeast corner of florida along the atlantic ocean and interstate 95. we are proud to serve the eastern gateway to the sunshine state. from our historic island, sandy beaches, and championship golf course to our scenic rivers, green pastures, and majestic timberlands, we truly offer something for everyone.
