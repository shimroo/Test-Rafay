 about wilbar truck equipment inc.
  to get a heavy duty job done right, you need the right equipment. since 1957 wilbar truck equipment has been providing superior products and friendly service to keep your fleet on the job operating efficiently. we are proud to be “  your truck equipment specialists” call for a free estimate.

 job ready work trucks
  wilbar provides a full line of truck equipment. from tandem dumps, lube rigs, mechanics bodies, vans, and stakes to tool boxes, ladder racks, lift gates, cranes, tarps, and much more. wilbar does it all.
