trestle to trail bridge 
the trestle to trail bridge allows connectivity into johnston to enjoy our 45+ miles of trails. the bridge was complete in september 2022.








camp dodge

johnston city hall

terra park
this 200-acre park with an eight-acre fishing lake surrounded by a great lawn for concerts and games, an open air civic shelter for large gatherings, a picnic pavilion, smaller shelters, a fishing pier, trails, children’s play structures that are handicap accessible

city office buildings and library closed for presidents' day 


the johnston city offices, including the library, will be closed on monday, february 17, in observance of presidents' day.						read on...







give back to your community 


the city has several positions available on boards and commissions, with many terms set to expire on june 30. this is your chance to make a difference and have a say in important decisions.						read on...







johnston celebrates kosovo's 17th independence anniversary with flag display, february 16-23 


the city of johnston congratulates kosovo on its 17th anniversary, wishing it prosperity, health, and peace ahead. to celebrate, the kosovo flag will be displayed at the memorial by the johnston public library from february 16 to february 23, 2025.						read on...







johnston city hall receives aia excellence in design and impact awards 


johnston city hall has received two prestigious awards from the american institute of architects: the excellence in design award and the impact award.						read on...







public input for little beaver creek sewer extension segments  2a, b and c 


a reaffirmation (re) has been issued for the johnston little beaver creek sanitary sewer extension segments 2a, b, & c cwsrf project. public comments regarding the project may be sent to srf-pc@dnr.iowa.gov.						read on...







mayor dierenfeld will run 2,805 miles 


mayor paula dierenfeld challenged the johnston community to get her to 2,500 miles! the community gave back in a big way and surpassed the goal. this year, she will run 2,805 miles for the mayor’s run for food.						read on...







jgmfd receives new tower ladder truck 


the jgmfd received a new tower ladder truck on january 22, 2025, that will enable firefighters to reach 100-feet during an emergency call for service. the truck also carries a hose and has a 300-gallon water tank and a pump.						read on...







apply now for special census jobs in johnston 


the u.s. census bureau is hiring for various positions in preparation for the upcoming special census in the city of johnston. help ensure our community is accurately counted!						read on...







enjoy the johnston town center ice rink this season 


the johnston town center rink is open for everyone to enjoy! concessions and skate rentals are available on fridays, saturdays, and sundays, although holiday hours may vary. skate rentals are just $5. we encourage you to come out and have fun at the rink!			






johnston prepares park restrooms for winter 


the city of johnston parks department will start preparing outdoor recreation facilities for winter on tuesday, october 29. these closures include dewey, terra, johnston commons and lew clarkson parks.						read on...
