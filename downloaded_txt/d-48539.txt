about los cerritos centerthere's so much to discover
located between bustling los angeles county and vibrant orange county, los cerritos center offers a little something for everyone. with top-tier stores including apple, nordstrom, zara, mac, and lululemon, as well as dining and entertainment destinations cheesecake factory, p.f. chang's, north italia (now open), the lazy dog restaurant and bar, sandbox vr and harkins cerritos 16, the center is the go-to destination for southern california shoppers.
