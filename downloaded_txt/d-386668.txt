about us 

crown paper mill is one of the leading producers of tissue paper jumbo rolls in the region 





since 1996 















the mill covers a total area of 50,000sqm, with an annual production capacity of 100,000 tons. 

headquartered in the industrial city of abu dhabi (icad), the mill’s “prime quality” jumbo rolls come in various grades such as facial, toilet, kitchen towel, napkin, c-fold and carrier tissue. all high-quality tissue grades are produced from the highest virgin pulp and recycled grade sourced from all over the world.
