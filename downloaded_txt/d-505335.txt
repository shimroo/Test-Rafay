your trusted toyota dealership in alcoa, tn
rick mcgill's airport toyota is a locally owned dealership conveniently located near the mcghee tyson airport and the downtown knoxville area. our customer satisfaction is our #1 priority. whether you're in the market for a new or used toyota, rick mcgill's airport toyota has you covered. stop by our dealership to browse our inventory and drive away in your perfect toyota today!


specials galore
at rick mcgill's airport toyota, we don't have any underhanded sales tactics or gimmicks just to get you in the door. instead, we offer great specials on used cars, new cars, and service. some of our deals change throughout the year, but we also have regular specials for seniors, military personnel, and college students. check out our website regularly to see all the deals we're running.
fast and easy financing
are you looking for a secure and easy car finance? if so, you are in the right place. we know not everyone can plop down tens of thousands of dollars in cash for a new car. that's why we offer quick, easy, and affordable financing options. if you're unsure where you stand on credit, take two minutes to fill out our online financing application. from there, our finance team will take over and figure out the best terms, rates, and pay dates to fit your schedule. rather than make a buck off your credit situation, we want nothing more than to see you satisfied with the car of your dreams.


amazing inventory
if you are seeking a brand new toyota, chances are we have it on our lot, complete with the color and features you want. for those with a slightly smaller budget, we also have an impressive selection of certified pre-owned toyota vehicles, as well as many other makes and models. to make the purchase easier on your wallet, we give you fair trade-in value for your vehicle so you aren't stuck trying to sell it on your own. at the end of the day, you'll drive away with the car you want at a price that's easy to live with.
come take a chance on us
at rick mcgill's airport toyota, we don't compromise on quality. we're always around to hear your concerns and forge a long-lasting, mutually beneficial relationship. don't take a chance on the other dealers. instead, take a chance on us.
