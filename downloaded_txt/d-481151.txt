eõ inn

downtown orlando boutique hotel 
tucked within a shady enclave of downtown orlando, where thornton park meets lake eola, you’ll find a orlando boutique hotel destination quite unlike any you’ve experienced before. housed in a beautifully restored, 1920’s-era building, eõ inn offers both a very modern sensibility and a feeling of intimacy.
