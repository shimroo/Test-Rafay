about us



welcome to texas longhorn equities corporation  

welcome to texas longhorn equities, your resource for professional property management and real estate investment services in the greater fort worth area. we are real estate professionals focused on providing targeted management solutions for property owners throughout the greater fort worth area. offering a full range of property management services, we can help you realize the full potential of your rental investments.our company offers full service property management – including tenant screening, marketing, rent collections, owner statements – as well as a la carte services, including tenant qualification and placement. we also provide special services, like one time evictions and lease preparation.texas longhorn equities is a small company, which allows us to remain responsive to the needs of our clients and tenants. we provide one on one service and management tailored to your specific requirements. if you own rental properties, please contact us now to learn more about our management services.if you are interested in finding a new home, please review our available listings or contact us directly to discuss your rental requirements.*smoking is strictly prohibited in all of our rental units including garage areas.
