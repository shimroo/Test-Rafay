active lives for all australian’s with disability



supporting people with disability to be active more often is at the heart of disability sports australia's mission.




    learn more
  


























over 70% of people living with disability want to be activeyour tax-deductible donation fuels our vision of active lives for all australians with disability. you can make a difference by donating today.




    donate now
  














disability sports australia is a national sporting organisation and charity and our role is to:










connect

we connect people with disability to participate in local opportunities through the delivery of programs.











promote

we actively promote opportunities in sports and recreation for people with disabilities, utilizing the compelling force of storytelling.











collaborate

we collaborate with a range of stakeholders to ensure that people with disability have equal opportunity to be involved in sport in a way that is meaningful to them.











build capacity

we build the capacity and confidence  of the sports and active recreation sector to be inclusive of people with disability.


















our programs















abilities unleashed program



an experiential come and try experience connecting kids and adults with disability to local sport and active recreation activities.















sports incubator



a program which supports national sporting organisations and recreation program providers to initiate and grow social and active pathways for people with disabilities.















get active



finding the right sports and recreational opportunities can be challenging. to assist you, we’ve compiled a comprehensive library of adaptive sports you could try.















disability sports northern territory



enhancing the lives of people with disability in the northern territory our nt business unit promotes active lifestyles and fosters participation.















accessibility champion



a free online course and resource which supports grassroots sporting clubs and leisure providers to be more inclusive.















our advisory committee



we are seeking people with disability or who are a parent of a disabled child, to establish a disability advisory committee to guide our work.
