the polynesian oceanfront hotel
a no-frills vacation in the heart of myrtle beach

enjoy a classic, old-school myrtle beach hotel right on the ocean with all the comforts of home. we offer a variety of accommodation and oceanfront efficiency types, many with private balconies, perfect for the budget conscious traveler. while the hotel may show signs of age, it possesses an undeniable charm that transports you to a tranquil, oceanfront retreat.find the essential beachside amenities you are looking for at a great price, including two large pools (one indoors), kiddie pool, hot tub, coin laundromat and parking garage. although we may lack the lavish amenities of larger resorts, we compensate with genuine care and a commitment to ensuring your comfort with excellent customer service and classic motel charm.conveniently located close to major myrtle beach attractions, you'll have the privilege of immersing yourself in the captivating beauty of the atlantic ocean. stroll along pristine sandy shores, indulge in beachside activities, and soak up the vibrant atmosphere of this beloved coastal destination. as the day winds down, take solace in the knowledge that a warm welcome awaits you at the polynesian oceanfront hotel. book your stay today and let us be your home away from home on the beautiful shores of myrtle beach.
