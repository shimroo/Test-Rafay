over 4 million children lack access to art education in the us



our vision is that every child has equal access to quality and meaningful arts education that helps prepare them for future success.



























here’s what we do










we offer complete art programs for k-8th grade

whether you’re looking to teach art to a single class or a whole school, we’ve got you covered.




explore our curriculum











we train your teachers and  classroom volunteers

having a culture of art at your school means everyone can get involved!




login to our teach site











we have the art lessons and materials

you can purchase program-specific art supplies needed for a year of art!
