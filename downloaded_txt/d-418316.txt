 welcome







we offer personalized business solutions, including file cabinets, file folders, medical filing, custom file cabinets, forms, hipaa products–and now ancom also offers storage systems and products for large -scale files.
ancom is a team of 40 employees who manufacture and distribute filing and storage system solutions and supplies for healthcare, government, legal and business professionals nationwide. we are dedicated to earning the confidence and trust of our clients by achieving a superior level of customer service with a personal touch.
customers are assigned personal service representatives who welcome the opportunity to create and develop standard and custom solutions that meet individual budget, space, décor and capacity requirements.  we offer an unlimited number of options via our custom manufacturing capabilities and large selection of reliable, high quality brand name suppliers.
