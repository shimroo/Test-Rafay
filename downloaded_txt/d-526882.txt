preserving and sharing our town’s past



centerbrook, essex and ivoryton














engaging and inspiring our community



the essex historical society is your local center of excellence for the collection and sharing of historic resources for the three villages of essex - centerbrook, essex and ivoryton.







































we invite you to explore our many programs and activities that inspire a deeper understanding of our town’s past, including:  our active collections and archiving program; our students as historians work with local schools;our historic walking tours;the  pratt house museum . . . and so much more!
