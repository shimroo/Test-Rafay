scid-iv



the structured clinical interview for dsm-iv (scid-iv) published in 1994 is a semi-structured interview guide for making dsm-iv diagnoses.
a newer version of the scid for dsm-5, which was published in 2013 is now available. learn more about the latest scid-5 version.
access a summary of differences between the scid-iv and scid-5.
