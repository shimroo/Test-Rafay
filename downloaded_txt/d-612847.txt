our menu



the definitive fine dining experience in the capital region



main menu

































at 677 prime, high-quality ingredients and exceptional service give you our exclusive fine-dining steakhouse experience. located in downtown albany, you can expect memorable meals thanks to our chef-driven cuisine, world-class wine program, and unparalleled service from our knowledgeable staff. owner jaime ortiz and executive chef ken kehn lead our culinary team and menu design to provide a meal you want to savor. while prime steaks anchor the menu, innovative seafood, modern takes on traditional steakhouse classics, and over-the-top desserts accompany them exquisitely.we invite you to chart your own course, start at the bar for a cocktail and a bite, and then explore items from every section of our menu. we welcome you to enjoy the exhilarating energy that is 677 prime.
