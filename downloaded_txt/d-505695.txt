why join piug?
learn.
share.
connect.

learn. the patent information user group, inc. provides a supportive environment in which you can learn about cutting edge developments in searching, analyzing, organizing and understanding patent information. you will learn from your international colleagues new skills that will help further develop your knowledge about patent information.

share. share your experiences, expertise or unique solutions to patent information challenges and reap the benefit of building your professional network and advancing your career.

connect. become a part of an international community of intelligent, resourceful and generous individuals, all of whom have a professional, technical or scientific interest in patent information. the patent information user group, inc. community is a vibrant and highly respected network of professionals. see for yourself, come join us.
