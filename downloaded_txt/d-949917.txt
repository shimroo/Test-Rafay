welcome to jillian square apartments, located on davis road. we are situated on the west side of fairbanks, conveniently located near shopping and dining. we offer one and two-bedroom apartments in a wide array of floor plans and styles. your new home includes large floor plans, walk-in closets (in most units), on site laundry facilities, courtyard with a playground, assigned parking, and 24 hour on-call emergency maintenance and office staff for your convenience. please feel free to stop by and visit with our friendly onsite management team, no appointments necessary. we look forward to meeting you!
