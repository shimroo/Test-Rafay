newstart invites you to experience christianity with us as a way of life, not a set of creeds and doctrines demanding total agreement or checking your brain at the door.
on our website, you can learn more about our beliefs, our times of worship & location, the nazarene church & our leadership.
we would love for you to join us as we seek to unveil the meaning of the bible for our time, looking to scripture, faith, and reason — interpreted by love to lead us to live lives that truly make a difference in our world.
because we are each at a different place along our journeys, there is encouragement and support we can offer each other as we discover more about god’s great love and acceptance.
as we grow in our relationship with christ together, the relationships in our lives are strengthened. this partnership with god and other faithful men and women is how his kingdom is built, a kingdom that welcomes all people.
we believe that in our time the church must recover, above all, its radical hospitality — welcoming all persons into its midst.
we invite you to experience god with us!
