welcome to the treacher collins family
            support group!



the
              treacher collins family support group was set up in 1987 by a
              group of parents to meet the needs of families and individuals.
              the aims of the group is to offer support and friendship to
              treacher collins families and individuals. we also seek to gain
              greater knowledge and understanding of the syndrome.
