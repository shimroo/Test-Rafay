commonword is the national bookstore and resource centre of mennonite church canada and canadian mennonite university. we hold the values of anabaptism – community-building, peacemaking, and christian discipleship – and offer personal and congregational resources that inspire such faith.
we are celebrating 50+ years of providing resources to our constituency. read our newly published history click here.

mennonite church canada
mennonite church canada is a covenanted partnership among:
mc eastern canada mc manitoba mc saskatchewan mc alberta mc british columbia
the overall mission of the partnership is the vision, healing, and hope statement:
god calls us to be followers of jesus christ and,by the power of the holy spirit to growas communities of grace, joy and peaceso that god's healing and hopeflow through us to the world.
