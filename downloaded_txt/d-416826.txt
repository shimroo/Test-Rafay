setting a new standard in luxury home construction.trusted for over three decades in central florida


























since 1984, goehring & morgan construction has been providing central florida with custom homes and luxury estates built with enduring quality of work, superior craftsmanship and unparalleled customer service.




    our approach
  


























founded by kim goehring and chris morgan, the luxury custom home builder and full-service residential remodeling construction firm has received numerous awards and recognition for its work including top custom home builder in central florida by orlando business journal and builder of the year by master custom builders council.
