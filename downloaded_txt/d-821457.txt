1. what types of tv screen issues can be repaired?
tv screen repair services can address various issues, including dead pixels, lines, discoloration, and damage caused by impacts or overheating.

repair times can vary based on the issue's complexity, but many common problems are resolved within a few hours to a day.
3. is it more cost-effective to repair a tv screen than to buy a new tv?
in most cases, repairing the tv screen is more cost-effective than purchasing a new tv, especially for larger or high-end television models.
4. do tv screen repair services work with various tv types and brands?
professional repair services are equipped to handle a wide range of tv types and brands, ensuring compatibility with your device.
5. are there any warranties for the repaired tv screens?
