department of philosophy







philosophy major
philosophy minor
philosophy (m.a.)











philosophy seeks to answer fundamental questions such as:

what is the nature and extent of our knowledge?

do we have free will?

what does morality require of us?

how do words get their meanings?

what is the nature of time?

how do our minds relate to our bodies?

our faculty specialize in a range of areas, including the history and philosophy of science, early analytic philosophy, value theory, metaphysics, philosophy of language, and environmental philosophy. we have a flexible curriculum with significant one-on-one time between students and faculty. we offer the bachelor of arts and master of arts degrees, and we participate in two interdisciplinary doctoral programs: aspect and sts.
