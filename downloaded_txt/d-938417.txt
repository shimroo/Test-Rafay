      about    

physical resources builds, operates, and maintains the physical assets of the guelph campus. several areas of specialization provide customer oriented, and essential support services to the entire campus. these include: business operations (work order administration, grounds, custodial and environmental sustainability, transportation services, stockroom/stores, materials handling and event rentals), design, engineering and construction, and  maintenance and energy services.
alternate formats of information, documents and communication supports can be requested by emailing the work order desk at wo@pr.uoguelph.ca
