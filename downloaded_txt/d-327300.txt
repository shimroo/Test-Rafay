online boxing - a free multiplayer boxing game


online boxing is a multiplayer online fighting game where you fight others online in real time.
rather than being arcade-like, the aim is at a realistic gameplay, as close to real boxing as possible.

it is for us boxing fans, who have long missed a boxing game that pays tribute to our beloved sport !.
click here  to play it. you need java 1.5 or later to run it.
online boxing is also available in a 3d version.
