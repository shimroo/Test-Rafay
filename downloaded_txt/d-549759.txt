        welcome to
  brady/mcculloch county chamber of commerce




    

   

 






   the brady/mcculloch county chamber of commerce promotes the business community. our primary goal is to support and 
advocate for the local business community, helping to create a favorable business climate that encourages growth, prosperity, and innovation. 
we offer a range of services and resources to our members, including networking opportunities, business education and training, access to 
funding and capital, and advocacy on behalf of business interests at the local, state, and national level. 
we help promote local businesses and create opportunities for collaboration and growth.



   
the brady/mcculloch county chamber of commerce will host their annual award banquet & awards celebration
"betting on brady" on thursday, april 24th, at the ed davenport civic center.the award celebration is an opportunity for the chamber to thank their members, celebrate successes of the
past year, and recognize outstanding citizens that have made a difference in mcculloch county. doors will open at 5:00pm and awards ceremony will begin promptly at 6:00pm. event will be byob.the chamber will recognize the following categories during the banquet: distinguished public service, outstanding educator,
citizen of the year, lifetime achievement, agribusiness of the year, small business of the year, large business of the year,
and outstanding community organization.nomination forms can be picked up at the brady/mcculloch county chamber of commerce office (106 w. main)
or you can fill out the form online.﻿nomination forms are due friday, april 4th at 4:00pm.we are excited to once again present our annual silent auction at this years event. all proceeds go back to the
chamber program of work to better serve & enhance our memberships.banquet tables (seats 10) or individual tickets can be purchased online.if you have any questions please feel free to call the brady chamber office at (325) 597-3491 or email info@bradytx.com
