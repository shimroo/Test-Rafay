welcome!hello from beautiful cape cod!



cape cod winery is located on oxbow road in the neighborhood of east falmouth, just a couple of short miles from the ocean. much like the beach, our winery is a place of relaxation and we invite you to join us in one of our many seating areas placed over crushed seashells to sip and savor the flavors of cape cod. with over ten different varieties of wine and sparkling wines to choose from there is surely something for everyone, whether it is our beloved rosé mermaid water or a refreshing sauvignon blanc. our wines will bring you that tranquil cape cod feeling.
