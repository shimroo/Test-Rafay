peace, love & guacamolethank you to everyone that joined us for the 2024 festival! the 39th california avocado festival will take place in downtown carpinteria october 3-5, 2025 complete with 3 full days of peace, love and guacamole. we will have 60 bands, 50 arts & crafts and commercial vendors, 15 delicious food vendors and over a dozen non profits will be on site for you to help support. reminder all proceeds of the festival go to the carpinteria education foundation and the future farmers of america! 



schedule:friday - 1 pm to 10 pm - locals block party saturday - 10 am to 10 pmsunday - 10 am - 6 pmlocation:downtown carpinteria on linden avenue between carpinteria avenue and 6th streetyou can use address 800 linden avenue, carpinteria, ca 93013
