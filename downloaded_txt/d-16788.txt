historic estate + grand gardenswe are a wedding and events venue located in the heart of austin, just northeast of downtown and fifteen minutes from the capital – a large estate offering sprawling, landscaped gardens and grounds and several unique buildings designed to hold various capacities for guests. barr mansion captures the essence of contemporary, organic beauty combined with full service features – offering unparalleled settings and exceptional progressive american cuisine prepared on site by our culinary team.for over 40 years we have been perfecting our space creating a memorable place to host special occasions of all kinds.all love welcome!
