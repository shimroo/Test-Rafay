happy new year!



with the final act of the 2024 festival season behind us, and the 2025 season upon us, the board of directors of the bear valley music festival would like to thank all of the patrons, donors, staff, volunteers, musicians, and supporters for their continued support to help us bring outstanding and diverse acts to bear valley, and we wish you all a happy new year.save it in your calendar:   july 18-august 3, 2025
