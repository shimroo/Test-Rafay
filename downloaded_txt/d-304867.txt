about the windsor palms resort
enjoy windsor palms, featuring gated resort style living at its finest! this beautiful, orlando vacation resort is just 2.5 miles (4km) from the gates of walt disney world. the perfect location for families to enjoy disney magical gatherings.
windsor palms florida vacation homes range from comfortable 2 and 3 bed condos, 3 bed townhomes, to spacious 4, 5 or 6-bedroom pool homes (villas). so you're sure to find lodging accommodations to suit your orlando vacation rental home needs.
