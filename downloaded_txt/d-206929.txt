taking you from first year to career



we know what it’s like to be new on campus. setting yourself apart can be challenging among other high-achieving students. alpha lambda delta provides a community of scholars, a platform to develop your leadership skills, and financial support through scholarships and educational partners. we have been guiding students to academic and career success for 100 years – accept membership and jumpstart your journey.
