kirkwood sports offers best-in-class youth soccer programs from ages 4-18 as well as foundational field hockey via our anchor partner delaware sharks field hockey club.  our advantages include dedicated personnel to address any needs and questions for kids, parents, and coaches.  assistance with first time coaching for our volunteers.  specialized training for both soccer and field hockey to establish foundational skills for future advanced play.
