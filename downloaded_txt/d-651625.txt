        community      

the city of maquoketa is nestled in the rolling hills of northeast iowa with multiple geological features making this area home to a wonderful and welcoming community. the city is ideally located to the famous maquoketa caves state park and is the county seat of jackson county.

maquoketa caves state parkwith more caves than any state park, maquoketa caves is one of iowa’s most unique outdoor attractions. enormous bluffs tower throughout the park, and a six-mile trail system winds through geologic formations and forests brimming with natural beauty. as one of the state’s earliest state parks, maquoketa caves has been a popular destination for picnickers and hikers since the 1860s. grab your hiking shoes and a flashlight before traveling to maquoketa caves, because this state park is nothing short of adventure.
