about our pictures...












all of our desktop pictures are offered in 640x480, 800x600, 832x624 and 1024x768, and many are available in high resolution, widescreen and hard-to-find sizes, including 1152x768, 1152x864, 1152x870, 1280x800, 1280x854, 1280x960, 1280x1024, 1400x1050, 1440x900, 1600x1024, 1600x1200 and 1680x1050. many of our pictures are also available in 1920x1080 (hdtv format), 1920x1200, 2560x1600, vertical formats for portrait mode screens, and very hi-res print sizes up to 3600x2400. we're also adding 1600x900 (to fit almost all new widescreen desktops) and 2560x1440 to our free wallpaper sizes as we update our galleries. all of our desktop wallpaper and high-resolution images are offered without any logos or notices, for a more attractive desktop background and print.
