our vision











the muskego area chamber of commerce provides service to the businesses of muskego, big bend, town of norway, and wind lake. our vision is to create an atmosphere where businesses flourish and communities are enhanced.
the chamber businesses and organizations support programs and community events that matter to the family, economy, and community at large. we advocate for local business, provide resources to help local economy flourish, and create partnerships that make our community a destination for work, home, and play.
we are poised to increase business awareness while building strong connections among business and community members. we appreciate the solid, healthy relationships formed between the chamber, local officials, schools, and citizens.
