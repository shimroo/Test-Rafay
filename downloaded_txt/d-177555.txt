hi, i’m kevin.



for over six years i’ve been on a mission to reintegrate psychedelics into western society through policy reform at the local and state level.humans have used psilocybin mushrooms as sacraments and technologies for healing and consciousness expansion throughout millennia in cultures all over the world. when used responsibly, we know psychedelics can save lives. since we’re facing a mental health disaster in the united states, access to psychedelics is a national readiness issue.using plant medicine is a human right, and we need the freedom and choice to work with them in our own respectful way now, more than ever.people come to me to solve the problem of transforming hearts, minds, and laws with psychedelics through education, media, and local and state policy reform.i’m on a mission to teach and work with others to accomplish what we did in denver and colorado that inspired a nationwide movement and beyond.
