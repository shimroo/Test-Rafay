how it works










the keith® walking floor® system is a hydraulically driven (un)loading/conveying system that features a series of floor slats that moves material out as it moves.








customer-driven innovation
keith has the material handling system suited to your needs.









trailer unloaders
we don’t build trailers, we unload them



details
keith® walking floor® self-unloading systems bring mobile unloading options to where tippers and dump trailers can’t: inside buildings and tunnels, under power lines, and on uneven or unstable ground.
