production credits







as a member of iatse 667, i have worked for several years as a 24 frame motion picture playback coordinator.
i set up display devices such as tv's and computer screens which are seen on camera.  displays are colour corrected for various types of lighting, and are free of shutter bars or pulsing.
i specialize in making "hero" displays which interact with the talent to add realism to the scenes.
