about us​ 

lone star stone is more than the nation’s premier supplier of manufactured stone veneers. we’re the answer to your architectural and engineering challenges, and the supplier that can make your next project – commercial or residential – simply spectacular.
our products, which come in the largest variety of styles and colors anywhere, are lightweight, affordable, easy to install and maintenance-free. that’s why architects, developers and designers across the country demand lone star stone for their projects … and you should too. the beauty and durability of lone star stone accentuates any project, large or small, in any environment, inside or out.when it comes to construction, lone star stone is … the natural choice.
