welcome to mountain lakes country day school!
 
 
 
 


are you looking for childcare for your infant through 6-year old child?
 
 
 
 


mountain lakes country day school provides quality care for infants, ages 6 weeks to 18 months; toddlers, ages 18 to 36 months; preschoolers, ages 3 to 4 years, and pre-kindergarteners, ages 4 to 6 years. classrooms are broken down by age group and activities are planned for small groups on the principles of developmentally appropriate practices.
our center is open all year with limited closings for holidays and staff training. whether you are looking for a full day with built-in childcare, or a partial school day option, we provide choices that best suit your family.
we invite you to take a look into our classrooms and read about the exciting things happening in our school. check out our current news to see our monthly newsletter, learning experiences in the classrooms, and exciting events taking place in our school. but the best way to get to know our school is to schedule a tour!
