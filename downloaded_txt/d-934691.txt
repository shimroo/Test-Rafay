client-centeredreal estate consulting



jm zell is the singular point of contact for industry leading teams in need of comprehensive real estate expertise regardless of industry, complexity, or geography.














about



since 1989, jm zell has worked with clients to achieve overarching business goals through the implementation of creative real estate solutions. clients who work with jm zell are able to successfully shift their real estate approach from cost center management to creating value for their organizations by utilizing real estate as a lever. we are well regarded for our innovative, comprehensive, solutions-oriented approach to real estate. we become part of the c-suite building consensus for clients with complex leadership structures, creating and implementing complex strategies, analyzing and negotiating critical transactions, and leading construction project management and development efforts.




meet the jm zell team



who is jm zell partners?we are a team of commercial real estate experts focused on providing our clients with customized real estate solutions that align with business and operations goals. our solutions are built utilizing our entire team’s combined real estate knowledge and experience which spans all areas of commercial real estate with demonstrated success across industries.our core service offerings include real estate strategy, tenant representation, construction project management, and development services.



what is our role?jm zell is most effective when we operate as our client’s single point of contact for commercial real estate. in this capacity, we work directly with client leadership, key stakeholders, and other specialized advisors to develop and implement creative real estate strategies that result in positive impact across our client’s organizations.



how is our expertise applied?from navigating business cycles to managing industry disruption, our clients continuously leverage our broad real estate expertise to achieve their overarching business and operations goals.
