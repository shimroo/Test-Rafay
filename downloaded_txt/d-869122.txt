ayco is the hub for all things youth circus
our mission is to promote the participation of youth in circus arts and support circus educators.
here are some of the things we do:

offer memberships for circus youth and supporters
produce regional and national youth circus festivals
produce young artist showcases
connect circus people
provide insider info about circus opportunities
set standards and guidelines for safety
support circus educators through the american circus educators association
