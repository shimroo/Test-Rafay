leading the future of low-speed mobility 



waev inc. is an electric vehicle pioneer founded in 2021 to manufacture, distribute, market, and support the gem, taylor-dunn, and tiger ev brands. gem is an established leader in the low-speed vehicle (lsv) space and has been for 25 years. taylor-dunn has a rich 75-year legacy of providing trusted industrial vehicles to the market. tiger heavy-duty tow tractors have been towing cargo, baggage, parcel, and other equipment in ground support applications since 1981. waev inc. is a strategic partner dedicated to solving the mobility market’s challenges with nimble innovation and comprehensive mobility solutions.
