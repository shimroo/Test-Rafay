discovering the magic of santa fe decor


the inspiration behind our unique style
santa fe, new mexico is a city like no other. its rich cultural heritage and stunning natural beauty have inspired generations of artists, architects, and designers to create something truly unique. at santafe decor, we draw inspiration from the city's adobe architecture, its vibrant art scene, and the breathtaking landscapes that surround us.
our design philosophy is deeply rooted in the region's history and traditions. we believe that every piece of furniture, every textile, and every decorative element should tell a story and evoke an emotional response. that's why we're committed to using only the finest materials and collaborating with local artisans to create one-of-a-kind pieces that reflect our city's essence.




the art of mixing old and new
at santafe decor, we're passionate about blending traditional techniques with modern sensibilities to create a truly unique aesthetic. we believe that the old and the new can coexist in harmony, resulting in spaces that are both timeless and contemporary.
our design approach is all about balance and restraint. we carefully curate each piece to ensure that it not only complements the space but also tells a story. whether it's an antique rug or a handcrafted wooden chair, every element serves a purpose and contributes to the overall narrative.




our commitment to sustainability
at santafe decor, we're deeply committed to sustainability. we believe that design should not only be beautiful but also responsible. that's why we prioritize eco-friendly materials, locally sourced whenever possible, and partner with artisans who share our values.
we're dedicated to reducing waste and promoting a culture of reuse and recycling. our goal is to create spaces that not only reflect the beauty of santa fe but also contribute to its well-being.
