welcome to shorewood home & auto
3 locations 75,000+ square foot showrooms over 1,000 pieces of equipment in inventory
shorewood home and auto is your one-stop shop for lawn mowers, power equipment, utility vehicles, snowblowers, atvs, snowmobiles, trailers, waverunners and more. we stand behind what we sell and offer customer service and expertise you won't find at "big box" stores. our parts department has what you need to keep your equipment running at peak performance, and our service department services everything we sell - and even some equipment we don't.

we've been in business in shorewood since 1974 and expanded to a second location in crete in 2008. now, we can help even more people, whether they are at work or play!

at shorewood home and auto, we offer products, parts and service from the most trusted and popular brands. they include:

polaris®, john deere, john deere commercial worksite products, yamaha waverunner, echo, stihl, honda power equipment, toro, exmark, billy goat, yamaha, bear cat, troy-bilt, bluebird, traeger, brown, wright, snowex, triton trailers, mac-lander, weber grills.
