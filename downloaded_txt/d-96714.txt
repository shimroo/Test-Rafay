welcome to motorcycle mall
belleville, nj
motorcycle mall is the tri-state area's premier motorcycle and powersports dealership. located in belleville, new jersey, motorcycle mall is just minutes from new york city and only a short distance from jersey shore, pennsylvania, and new york. motorcycle mall has been awarded top industry honors each and every year including a top 50 dealership by powersports business and also awarded the coveted #1 dealer in the usa by dealernews magazine.


motorcycle mall has thousands of new and used motorcycles (scooters and dirt bikes), powersports vehicles (all-terrain vehicles, personal watercraft, and snowmobiles), and power equipment (generators, snow blowers, and lawn equipment) in-stock from the following manufacturers: aprilia, benelli, can-am on-road, can-am off-road, cfmoto, ducati, gasgas, honda, husqvarna, indian motorcycles, karavan trailers, kawasaki, ktm, mv agusta, piaggio, polaris, sea-doo, segway, slingshot, ssr motorsports, suzuki, vespa and yamaha. as part of our sales department, we offer financing for customers, in-house insurance, and even motorcycle rentals as an eaglerider rental facility.


our apparel department offers everything a motorcycle and powersports fan needs to be safe while riding as well as looking good. we offer a variety of items for your safety such as: helmets, jackets, gloves, riding boots, dirt bike protection, googles, etc. we also have a variety of clothing available such as t-shirts, sweatshirts, hats, pants, and more made by the leading industry manufactures.


our parts department is one of the largest in the country carrying everything you are looking for your powersport vehicle and motorcycle including tires, exhausts, frame sliders, fender eliminator, grips, levers, oil, fluids, oem replacement parts (as well as aftermarket accessories) from the leading manufacturers. in addition, we have a full-service service department with factory trained and certified technicians for all of the manufactures we carry and master technicians for bmw, triumph, and harley-davidson®!
