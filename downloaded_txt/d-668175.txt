the web's home of guaranteed authentic original autographs.

if you are looking for an authentic, obtained in person, original autographed item, you have come to the right authentic celebrity autograph dealer.

we offer authentic memorabilia.

we are the web's leading supplier of genuine signed photos and original autographs. we specialize in autographed photos, but carry all types of signed memorabilia.

our genuine celebrity signatures make great, unique gifts and are highly collectible.

we offer a lifetime guarantee of authenticity on all our autographs for sale.

 we are autograph fair trade association (aftal) approved dealer #ad123 and are required to hold ourselves to their strict code of ethics.

we are also members of the universal autograph collectors club (uacc) and hold ourselves to their strict code of ethics.

most of our high-end, authentic signed photos, signed collectibles and celebrity autographs are 3rd party certified authentic by acoa (autographcoa), jsa (james spence authentication), psa/dna (professional sports authenticators) or bas (beckett authentication services). this provides an even higher level of trust and can ease potential future resale.
payment types we accept: most major credit and debit cards, paypal, amazon pay, google pay, samsung pay, microsoft pay, apple pay, venmo, affirm and sezzle.
