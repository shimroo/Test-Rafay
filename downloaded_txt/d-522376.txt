i'm graduating from high school...
graduating from high school and looking for a college that allows the flexibility to learn anytime and anywhere? look no further! get degrees and certificates in traditional classroom settings, 100% online or a combination of both. can't wait for graduation? take college courses at coastline while in high school! whether you are graduating soon or looking to get a jump start on college, at coastline we've got you covered!

i would like to transfer to coastline...
imagine choosing a school only to discover it's not the right fit or attending a school that's an excellent fit but suddenly having to relocate due to family or work obligations. today, one-third of all college students transfer at least once before graduation. at coastline we help to make your move easy.

i'm looking for a certificate...
if you're curious about which type of certifications will provide the most job advancement opportunities or to help launch your career, look no further! dedicated to workforce development and lifelong learning, coastline provides technical certificates to learners who have completed a specialized course of study that is related to specific vocational skills.

i want post-service education...
whether you are active duty, a spouse of a service member or a veteran, coastline allows you to take the next step in your career path. we offer flexible options for busy adults, and our veterans resource centers provide academic support and vet2vet networking.
