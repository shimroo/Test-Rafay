latest news and events














previous button













fst launches researcher career development group 
lancaster university’s faculty of science and technology (fst) researcher career development group (rcad) launched on december 13, 2024, co-chaired by abi frith, xiaoyun chen, luis pinho, and janine morley. the event focused on representing research staff, fostering career development, networking, and community building. 










hearing impairment may be a sign of increased risk of parkinson’s disease
there may be a link between hearing impairment and an increased risk of developing parkinson’s according to research led by lancaster university.  










researchers develop trial design to quickly deliver new treatments for covid-19 
researchers in the school of mathematical sciences have been working on trial design to bring safe new treatments for covid-19 more efficiently. 










calls for urgent policy action as research reveals real extent of human trafficking
new research by academics has revealed a more accurate estimate of those trafficked across the world, from 49.6million to 65.3million, with urgent action needed to prevent this criminal exploitation and coercion. 










statisticians improving health in older adults by monitoring their activity changes
statisticians have created a new approach to monitoring activity changes in older adults to improve their health. 










lancaster phd student to become a statistics ‘ambassador’
a lancaster university phd student is set to become a statistical ambassador for the royal statistical society (rss).
