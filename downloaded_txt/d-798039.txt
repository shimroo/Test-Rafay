our services




















annual trophy engraving



each year, we help clubs, schools, and businesses honor their champions with custom trophy engraving. whether it’s for a golf club, sports league, or corporate awards, we ensure each winner’s name is etched with precision. from small local events to large national competitions, we’ve got the expertise to make your trophies a lasting symbol of achievement and tradition.




















custom engraving



we specialize in engraving everything from jewellery and watches to sports medals and trophies. whether it's a personalised gift, corporate award, or sentimental keepsake, our in-house engraving services bring your items to life. using advanced rotary diamond cnc, sand blasting, and laser techniques, we handle projects large and small with care and precision. please pop in with your requirements or alternativly email us your enquiry along with a photo to info@trophiesmedals.co.uk




















corporate event awards 



we provide custom trophies and awards tailored to corporate events, conferences, and recognition programs. whether for a team recognition, an annual award ceremony, or a special corporate event, we craft awards that reflect your company's values and make a lasting impact. we offer bespoke designs, personalized engraving, and even on-site services for a seamless experience.







































our promise at trophies, medals & more



as a family-run business with over 60 years of combined industry experience, we are committed to delivering exceptional quality and service in everything we do. we pride ourselves on building lasting relationships with our customers, offering personalized solutions with a focus on reliability and attention to detail. whether you’re seeking custom engraving or unique trophies, we treat every project with the care and expertise it deserves—because to us, it's not just business; it's a tradition of excellence we’re proud to uphold for generations to come.
