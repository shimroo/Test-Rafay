we believe that what we do is valuable and necessary to american culture. we believe it is our duty to ensure that making studio furniture remains a viable profession. we do this through educating our customers and the public on the value of good design and craftsmanship in furniture and the effort required to bring it to life. we understand that we offer more than heirloom-quality furniture—we offer a luxury service. it is our job as professionals to collaborate with our clients and meet their needs without sacrificing the integrity of our designs or the quality of our work.

we created northwest woodworkers gallery so that each of us could pursue our passion and contribute lasting works of art to the world.

we create art with purpose.
