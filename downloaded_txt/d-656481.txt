about sunrise marine 



about us 



for over 20 years, sunrise marine has been the place to go for new and used boat sales, service and parts in the florida panhandle. we are continuing that tradition at our daphne, al and orange beach, al stores with the same great selection, pricing, and most of all-unsurpassed customer service, that made destin sunrise marine one of the top rated marine dealerships in the country.
we have a large selection of bay boats, fishing boats, center consoles, cruisers, deck boats, bowriders, runabouts, catamarans, and more!
sunrise marine carries models from top brands in the industry including: robalo, chaparral, bennington, jupiter, world cat and cape horn.
