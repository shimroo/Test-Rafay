request for proposal (rfp) for hwy 7 transportation management organization study 


the city of shorewood seeks a qualified consultant to provide professional services to develop a comprehensive study and financial plan for a transportation management organization along the trunk hwy 7 corridor.			learn more...







presidents' day is monday, february 17 


city offices will be closed in observance of the holiday.			






shorewood welcomes newly appointed commissioners 


at the february 10, city council meeting, shorewood councilmembers appointed park and planning commissioners.						read on...







aarp tax-aide appointments full 


all aarp tax-aide appointments have been filled. for additional free tax preparation sites, visit the mn department of revenue's website.			visit revenue.state.mn.us







join our team! 


bring your leadership skills to direct the city's comprehensive land use planning initiatives and serve as the city’s planning director.						read on...







making sense of local government: implementing the comprehensive plan 


the city is reviewing and updating its land use ordinances and zoning map to align with the comprehensive plan. the planning commission will review all changes and take public comment on the proposals before preparing a recommendation to city council.						read on...







january/february shorereport 


the city's latest newsletter is available to view online. this issue includes: 2025 mill & overlay projects, implementing the comprehensive plan, aarp tax-aide information, 2025 city budget details, and more!			continue reading...







new website tool to report non-emergencies 


beginning in 2025, citizen request tracker™ (crt™) will serve as the new online tool to report non-emergencies in the city of shorewood. to submit a request online visit shorewoodmn.gov/report						read on...







ice rinks & warming house hours 


outdoor skating season is generally late december to mid february, or as weather permits. schedules are subject to change based on weather and safe ice conditions. visit shorewoodmn.gov/rinks for latest updates.			visit shorewoodmn.gov/rinks







2025 city budget approved

galpin lake road closure: april 22, 2024 until june 2025 


the city of chanhassen is improving galpin boulevard from highway 5 to the chanhassen-shorewood city limits in 2024. galpin lake road will be closed from mayflower road to the south to the chanhassen border.						read on...
