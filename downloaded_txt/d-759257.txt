family owned since 1922.

we are proud to be located in one of the most historical regions of the country. the great falls of paterson, new jersey (as shown here) supplied the power necessary for the tremendous growth of various industries in the latter part of the 19th and early part of the 20th centuries.
feldman brothers electric supply is a 4th generation family-owned business that started in 1922 in a small store front on bridge street. we now can offer an extensive inventory that features complete lines of high quality electrical products and fixtures from the world’s leading manufacturers housed in our 100,000 square foot warehouse.
