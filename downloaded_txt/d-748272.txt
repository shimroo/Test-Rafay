forward house is a non-profit society that provides community-based resources, structured psychosocial rehabilitation programs, and services to adults needing support with their mental health and/or addiction recovery. we promote healthy, meaningful, and independent lives.




























our programs and services



advocacy and resourcesour advocates are available to help community members who are experiencing mental health challenges and are needing information or support. recreation programsour recreation program improves social bonds, promotes inclusion, reduces loneliness or isolation, strengthens social skills, builds self-esteem - and is a lot of fun! education and group programswe offer a variety of educational and group programs throughout the year to support people living with a mental health challenge, in addiction recovery - or their loved ones. a.c.e (ability. connections. empowerment)a mental health psychosocial rehabilitation program. this strengths-based program focuses on goal setting, skills of daily living, vocational skills, psychosocial skills, and community connections. forward recovery programprofessional peer facilitated workshops, support groups and 1-to-1 recovery coaching focusing on recovery from substance use and addiction challenges and nurturing mental wellness.
