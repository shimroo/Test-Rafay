                        about us 
                    
 

what started in 1985 as a corporate christmas decorating companyâ -  by 1987 had morphed into a full blown visual display manufacturing and distribution company as customers asked for more and more year-round foliage and decor. today, we have customers around the country and across the globe who have trimmed their facilities with artificial foliage or organized their stores with our ever expanding line of acrylic display fixtures.
from quality artificial christmas foliage to made in the usa acrylic displays - we wholesale thousands of items 'to the trade' year round. since 1987, our growing product lines include over 4,000 acrylic  displays for; gems & minerals, jewelry, food & beverage,  eye-wear, literature, store fixtures, clothing displays and  more.    
for the holidays our trees, wreaths, garland, topiaries, sprays, stems,  ornaments and props have adorned the white house, disney, tiffany's,  macy's, scheels - you name it!   our customers range from single stores to mass-retailers, hospitality,  healthcare and religious facilities, commercial properties, libraries, botanical gardens, tv shows,  movies and special events. 
and if you can't find what you need, we offer custom acrylic displays manufacturing and branded artificial christmas foliage with store direct distribution. 

we look forward to  working with you on your next project!
