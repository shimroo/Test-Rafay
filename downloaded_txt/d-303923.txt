grow your knowledge and support your community: master gardener and naturalist training programs this summer!do you have a passion for nature, gardening, conservation and giving back to your community?  this summer, osu extension of medina county is offering two exciting opportunities to expand your skills and connect with fellow outdoor enthusiasts.  

pesticide license recertification for 2025recertify your pesticide and fertilizer license for 2025.

if your pesticide or fertilizer license expires on march 2025, you need to earn 3 hours of recertification credits to renew. you will need at least one hour of core and at least 1/2 hour in your licensed categories to recertify.
