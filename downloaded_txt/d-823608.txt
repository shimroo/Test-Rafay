for over 20 years, we have been providing solutions for the commercial apparel decoration industry.  astech solutions started literally from the back of adrian’s car in 2000 – providing repair and training services for individuals and businesses in the game of apparel customisation.over the years, along with our customers, we have enjoyed measured growth and now boast a small but dedicated team. together with our network of like-minded associates across australia, we are positioned to work with new and existing customers in finding the right solutions to move their business forward.
