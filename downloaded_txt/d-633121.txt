 i'm an artist, educator and curator based in los angeles and the founder and executive director of machine project. together machine and i have produced over 1500 events, workshops and installations. i am an associate professor of art at pomona college and serve on the board of directors of the andy warhol foundation for the visual arts. i received my mfa in visual art from the california institute of the arts.i look and sound like this guy (see below).
