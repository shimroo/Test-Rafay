vail valley dining guideeast to west - a culinary collectionfor over 20 years, the vail valley dining guide has been your most comprehensive source for the vail valley’s finest restaurants, event venues and dining establishments.  as we have for over 25 years, this comprehensive guide seeks to aid you in discovering or perhaps rediscovering the rich, exciting and truly international culinary scene that the areas in and around the mountain resorts of vail and beaver creek have to offer. as restaurants innovate and adjust to a changing world, your patronage and support remain vital — and is greatly appreciated.
