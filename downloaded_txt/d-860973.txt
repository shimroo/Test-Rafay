office of admissions



are you ready to apply?
if you are, then apply now and let’s begin your future.
uno recognizes that taking either the sat or act at this time may be challenging. uno may admit freshmen with a minimum 2.5+ gpa or greater on the louisiana core 4. for the fall, summer, and spring terms, applicants with a 2.5+ core gpa who do not meet test score requirements, or do not submit test scores will be admitted based on core gpa alone. for any questions please contact the privateer enrollment center at admissions@uno.edu.
all domestic undergraduate application fees are currently waived!

privateer enrollment center
the pec is the university of new orleans' "one-stop shop" for enrollment services. staff at the pec can assist students with academic advising, financial aid, admissions, registering for classes, paying fee bills, obtaining parking decals, and any other enrollment needs.
