los angeles county employment law firmfighting for workers’ rights in orange county, san bernardino, & riverside county
the employment attorneys at kesluk, silverstein, jacob & morrison, p.c. focus their practice on employment law and workplace rights. we represent everyone from hourly workers to executives. we take cases to trial on a regular basis and handle class actions. in addition to safeguarding employee rights, we also have experience with labor union issues. furthermore, for undocumented workers, our employment practice centers around making sure all workers get equal protection under california and united states employment laws.
californians have significant protections as employees. we understand how devastating it is to be discriminated against, harassed, have your job threatened, or to lose your job. we are here to help!
