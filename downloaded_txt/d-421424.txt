who we are awarded both the new york state and nassau county ems agency of the year in 2018 bellmore-merrick emergency medical services is a longstanding non-profit ambulance service that has faithfully served the towns of bellmore, merrick, north bellmore, and north merrick for over 50 years. committed to swift and professional medical assistance during emergencies, this team of dedicated first responders plays a vital role in prioritizing the health and safety of the community.﻿
