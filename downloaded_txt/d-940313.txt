latest news: december 23, 2023 - gsi's xmas gift box 2023 - free app and sample expansions





this is gsi's gift to thank all of you for the great support and love you showed us throughout the year 2023. this gift box includes a free virtual instrument app for ios and free piano sample expansions for genuinesounds vol. i, just piano and gemini.

find the contents here:

cantoria - free physical modeling pipe organ for ipad and iphone
sample expansions for gemini
to find the new content in genuine sounds vol. i, open the app, go to settings and click/tap open sound bank manager
to find the new content in just piano, open the app then tap the download icon on the top left corner


we wish you all a very merry christmas and a great new year 2024! thank you!
