who we are 











our services include commercial real estate investment sales and acquisitions, 1031 exchanges, buyers representation, leasing and financing.  whatever your needs are, let us know. you can expect to be contacted by telephone or e-mail within one day if you fill out our information request forms completely.











 
























we take our fiduciary duty to our clients seriously and follow the following principles





put our clients interest above our own.develop long term relationships with our clients.treat our clients investment properties or purchases like it’s our own.











 

















 










provide up to date market pricing information for purchases or sales.work with all outside brokerage firms on an equal 50-50 basis to get our clients the best deal whether buying or selling a property through us.present all offers to listing brokers or sellers and let the principals make the decisions and not their brokers.if we receive multiple offers on a property we represent we counter all offers at the same price and terms.specialize in off market properties and buyers in 1031/1033 exchanges.
