welcome!welcome to the dorchester county public library: cambridge or hurlock branches!                                          the friends of the library are currently accepting donated materials at the central branch on the second saturday of each month from 9am-1pm during the book sale. materials should be in good condition and limited to two boxes at a time. we cannot accept encyclopedias, textbooks, or any mildewed or damaged items. if you have any questions, please contact the library.    thank you for your continued support of our library system. we hope to see you soon!                    happening at the librarythere are always programs and events happening at our libraries! for a complete list of programs, events, and closures visit our calendar, facebook, and instagram for the latest updates. if you prefer a quieter experience at our library branches, we recommend visiting during school hours up to 3:00pm or saturday mornings, from 9-11am (at the cambridge branch). wednesdays are busy program days at both branches, with noisier mornings (around story time) and busier after-school times at the cambridge branch (around teen anime club). please plan accordingly or call either branch for further recommendations.   



the library is at your fingertipsyour library card gives you access to two free digital collections for ebooks, audiobooks and so much more. available for your computer, phone, tablet, and some smart tvs.
