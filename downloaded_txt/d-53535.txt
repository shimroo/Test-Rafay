about us
south moon under offers a curated assortment of elevated wardrobe staples & on-trend fashion essentials from beloved brands and emerging designers, hand-selected for the effortlessly cool woman & man at every stage of life. for special days and every day...



explore our year-round swim shop, featuring established and indie brands from around the world, dresses for every occasion, from wedding guest to graduation to everyday casual, & an assortment of premium denim in classic styles and on-trend silhouettes. south moon under stores & southmoonunder.com receive new arrivals and new brands weekly for women and men, including brands like z supply, free people,
astr, french connection, surf side supply, johnnie-o and the good man brand. it is our mission to cultivate the individuality and confidence of our customers by offering an ever-evolving, curated assortment of elevated, effortless styles.
