send postcard art to friends



in a world overwhelmed by instant messages, an art-inspired handwritten note to a friend or loved one can be a wonderful surprise and a more meaningful connection.




    write today
  
































            1. buy a postcard pack
          









buy a pack of postcards based on how ambitious your writing plan is. each one includes a postage stamp!








            2. check your mailbox
          









these postcards will be physically mailed to you, so start thinking about all the cool messages you'll write to your family and friends.








            3. write your message
          









each postcard comes with a matching us postage stamp, so all it needs is a name, address, and a lovely message from you!








            4. mail it!
          









drop off handwritten postcards at your local post office or city mailbox. if your mail carrier is friendly, they’ll pick it up from your home mailbox (raise the red flag if you have one).
