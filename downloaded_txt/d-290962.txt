experienced

since 1999, advanced hazmat life support (ahls) has been the international leader in hazmat courses and medical toxicology education. ahls specializes in training healthcare professionals across disciplines to care for patients exposed to hazardous materials and dangerous goods.
learn more










elite

join the group of over 25,000 paramedics, physicians, nurses, pharmacists, and other healthcare professionals from 96 countries and territories who have acquired training and expertise from ahls to prepare for the clinical challenges of caring for patients exposed to chemical spills, toxic terrorism, and other threats.
take a course










expert

ahls is co-presented by the american academy of clinical toxicology (aact), a trusted source for toxicology education. ahls curriculum, course materials, and manuals are continually refined by the interdisciplinary ahls scientific advisory committee and other national and international experts.
