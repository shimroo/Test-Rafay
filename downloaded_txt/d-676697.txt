gregg gerson is a distinguished platinum and gold recording artist, as recognized by the riaa, and a grammy-nominated drummer and flutist. he is celebrated for his contributions across various musical genres, including rock, traditional jazz, classical and fusion. at just 4 years old, he captivated audiences by playing a marching snare drum on the boardwalk of atlantic city, new jersey.gerson's unique style features precise note placement and duration, resulting in playing that is both impeccable and deeply groovy. throughout his remarkable career, he has collaborated with legendary artists such as jason mraz, iggy pop, billy idol, mick jagger, carlos alomar (associated with david bowie), roger daltrey of the who, roy orbison, stanley jordan, michael brecker, wayne kramer of the mc5, steve jones of the sex pistols, dominic miller of sting, and gloria estefan, among many others.
