train like a champion, compete like a legend.










defend the house!

premier national sports is the home to independence fc and dragons lc, the premier youth soccer and lacrosse clubs in new jersey! come play in front of all your family and friends on the banks of the hudson river. our club programs deliver top level coaching and player development at the most reasonable cost to families anywhere!




play here >>











become a champion!

be challenged across the country by having your club play in the premier national sports champions league events with a chance to get invited to the prestigious championship cup tournament in bradenton, fl at the end of the 2026 season. premier national sports hosts tournaments in soccer and lacrosse for boys and girls clubs.




compete here >>











get to the next level!

premier national sports and ncsa are here to help the college recruiting process and student-athletes get recruited. using hands-on guidance, college matching tools and network of 35,000+ college coaches, more than 200,000 student-athletes have made commitments to their college choice.
