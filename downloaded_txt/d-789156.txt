where creativity thrives & imaginations soarwelcome to our performing and visual arts community. 














fairmount center for the arts has been offering classes in the visual and performing arts for over 50 years. fairmount is committed to "enriching lives through the arts" through classes, workshops, performances, festivals, and exhibitions.
