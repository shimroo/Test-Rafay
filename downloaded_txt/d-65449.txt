welcome to hotelguide.networks' directory of hotels and hotel reviews for atlanta, georgia!

filled with southern heritage and charm, vibrant, diverse atlanta combines rich history with a bustling, modern economy. atlanta, long ranking as a premier business destination and a top convention draw, also boasts powerhouse attractions from underground atlanta to six flags over georgia. among newest leisure magnets are the georgia aquarium (opened in november, 2005 and billed as the world's largest, with five beluga whales), and atlantic station, a 138-acre midtown development with retail and entertainment, plus 11 acres of public parks. boasting more than 400 hotels with more than 64,000 rooms, greater atlanta lodging options range from quaint historic inns to internationally known luxury hotels.
