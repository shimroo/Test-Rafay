 outdoor power equipment sales and service in oxford, nc

steve’s lawn & garden features the best selection of outdoor landscaping and lawn equipment in oxford, nc, along with detailed repairs and exceptional customer service. we serve all of granville county and the surrounding area, including wake forest, henderson and south boston, va. whether your lawn mower needs to be repaired, just a tune-up or you’re searching for a brand-new weed trimmer, we have the tools to make your yard look its best.



   
 over three decades in business

with 37 years of experience, we have the proven ability to complete any repair job. whether you are a landscaper or homeowner, we understand the importance of your equipment and strive to provide you with the best service possible. we’ll finish the job efficiently so you can return to caring for your yard. our experienced technicians will ensure your machine functions at peak performance long into the future or until you run over that stump again.

 family owned & operated

at steve’s lawn & garden, we are proud of our reputation as an honest and reliable local business. we strive to give each of our customers the same attention and respect that we would expect. many of the people who walk through our doors have been doing so for many years, and have become a part of our lawn and garden family. we are a good alternative to the big box stores by providing knowledgeable sales and continuing service. we surprise people with the selection of parts we stock; however, if we do not have what you need, we can order it for you.



   
 leading lawn care brands

we are pleased to provide customers with top-of-the-line brands of lawn mowers and other equipment, including hustler®, cub cadet®, stihl®, and shindaiwa®. we carry high-end lawn mowers at fair prices, so you’ll get a superior-quality device at a budget-friendly rate.

 get in touch

our full-service shop offers comprehensive solutions to your problems, from small engine repair and electrical issues to fixing flat tires. contact steve’s lawn & garden today for the latest deals, and feel free to stop by our store to learn more about what we can do for you and your machine.
