who we are


the osce stands for the organization for security and co-operation in europe. with 57 states from europe, central asia and north america, the osce is the world's largest regional security organization.
learn more




what we do


our activities cover a wide range of security issues such as conflict prevention to fostering economic development, ensuring the sustainable use of natural resources & promoting the full respect of human rights and fundamental freedoms.
learn more 



where we are


the osce has field presences in south-eastern europe, eastern europe, the south caucasus and central asia.
