serving the wholesale packaging industry since 1950
superior paper opened its warehouse doors in 1950 selling paper products and cleaning supplies into the louisville, ky market. shortly thereafter, it expanded its operations to include selling gift packaging supplies and custom printing to local businesses. in 2002, we made our first online sale under the name superior giftwrap. in 2006, superior giftwrap branched off into the separate business entity it is today. we are now headquartered in southern indiana and are pleased to offer our years of expertise to help you with your wholesale packaging needs.
