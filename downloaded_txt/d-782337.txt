tool store go-kart shop
hello! we are the tool store go-kart shop located on the corner of 47th and harlem ave, the tool store go-kart shop has been serving forest view, il for over 35 years. our store is half tool side and half bike side. in the tool shop, we mainly sell automotive tools such as compressors, jacks, air guns, sockets and so much more! on our bike side, we sell atv's, dirt bikes, scooters, and more! also, we sell parts and have a service department to help get your motorized bike up and running! stop by or call us today!
