crane house & historic ywca museum open every sunday

the crane house & historic ywca museum is open every sunday for self-gudied tours from 12 to 3 pm. self-guided tour admission is $8/person; children under 2 are free.free admission programs include: families first program, military personnel, narm, mhc members, montclair students, and montclair public library museum pass.
