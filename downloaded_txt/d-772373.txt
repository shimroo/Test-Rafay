200 years of building better men 
							1824-2024 
							
 





















bicentennial celebration 










									“our brotherhood has become the first men’s social fraternity to celebrate 200 years of existence. chi phi now has the unprecedented opportunity to show the world what a vibrant, values-based fraternity looks like going into its third century of existence.								



									and as the oldest social fraternity, it is only fitting that we celebrate this historic milestone in a manner befitting the occasion. to that end, chi phi will hold a year-long series of celebratory events across the country to help ensure that, regardless of where you live, there will be an event in or around your region.								



									we are all blessed to be chi phi’s at such a momentous time. i hope you will join me in celebrating our first 200 years as we look forward to the next 200 years and beyond.”								



									– steven w. hopkins, nu 1999, grand alpha

our mission is to build better men through lifelong friendships, leadership opportunities, and character development. 



chi phi creates leaders by providing young men with skills that prepare them for a lifetime of success. united through the strong bonds of brotherhood, chi phi men build lifelong friendships while learning what it means to be productive global citizens and give back to their communities.
