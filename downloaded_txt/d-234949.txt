our goal is to place the right poodle into the right home!poodle rescue of new england is dedicated to rescuing, rehabilitating, and re-homing poodles throughout new england (maine, new hampshire, vermont, massachusetts, connecticut, rhode island) and upstate new york within a 1/2 hour of albany.

































make a donation.poodle rescue of new england is a 501 c 3 non-profit organization. we receive no federal or state funds and are supported solely by private donations. poodle rescue of new england does not have a shelter. all of our dogs are cared for by volunteers in temporary foster homes.
