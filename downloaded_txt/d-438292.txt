union marine

 at union marine we strive to provide you with a top tier pacific northwest boat ownership experience. we offer new and pre-owned boats from brands like bennington, bayliner, aviara, boston whaler, crest, heyday, mastercraft, nautique, sea ray, and trophy.
 as the leading dealership in the pacific northwest we provide unmatched customer service, expert knowledge, and seamless boating experiences from purchase to maintenance. with six locations in seattle, fife, issaquah, and bellingham, plus mobile services, union marine is your trusted partner for all your pacific northwest boating needs.
