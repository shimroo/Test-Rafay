one meadow glen groundbreaking celebration 


the city of millbrae is thrilled to announce the groundbreaking of one meadow glen, a mixed-use development that will bring 278 residential units, affordable housing, vibrant retail spaces, and a beautiful public plaza to the heart of our downtown.						read on...







millbrae recreation center receives national dbia awards 


the city of millbrae was honored alongside partners blach construction (blach), els architecture & urban design (els), and swinerton management & consulting (smc) with three design-build institute of america awards for millbrae recreation center.						read on...







registration now open for winter recreation programs! 


we are excited to announce that registration for the city of millbrae’s winter recreation classes is now open! this season, we are offering a diverse range of activities for all ages, ensuring there is something for everyone to enjoy.						read on...







faq: next steps after the certification of  councilmembers recalled 


please review the frequently asked questions regarding next steps after the certification of councilmembers recalled.						read on...







appointment for district 2 and district 4 council seats 


the city council is looking for individuals who want to make a difference in bringing the community together and have a thorough understanding of the issues facing the city of millbrae.						read on...







taylor middle school drop-off & pick-up areas 


this year, the millbrae school district is implementing two new student drop-off/pick-up areas to help better facilitate traffic flow around the school and increase safety.						read on...







registration now open for fall recreation programs! 


we are excited to announce that registration for the city of millbrae’s fall recreation classes is now open! this season, we are offering a diverse range of activities for all ages, ensuring there is something for everyone to enjoy.						read on...







public memo on redistricting millbrae's current 5-district election system 


please read the the attached memo from millbrae city council that provides information to answer the question "how to change city's current 5-district model for council elections?"						read on...







millbrae goes to the movies 


the city of millbrae is excited to continue our beloved "millbrae goes to the movies" summer series! this year, we are bringing an incredible lineup of family-friendly movies to our beautiful central park!						read on...







fire assessment passes with 60% majority 


the city of millbrae is pleased to announce the passage of the property-based fire assessment that helps fund direct and vital fire and emergency medical services for the millbrae community.						read on...







residential design standards and zoning code amendments questionnaire 


take the online community questionnaire for the city of millbrae citywide objective residential design standards and zoning code amendments.						read on...

city of millbrae city hall and recreation center certified as a green business 


environmental programs collaborated with the green business program to recertify millbrae city hall and the recreation center as a certified green business.						read on...

city of millbrae housing element certified by california department of housing and community develop 


the california department of housing and community development (hcd) announced that the city of millbrae’s 2023-2031 housing element received state certification today, signifying the city's compliance with the state housing element law.						read on...







city of millbrae housing element meets state requirements 


the city of millbrae received confirmation from the state of california that its draft 2023-2031 housing element substantially complies with all state requirements and will be certified upon approval from the millbrae city council.						read on...







community emergency response team (cert) training offered in chinese 


the community emergency response team (cert) training session will be offered and conducted in chinese. this training aims to equip chinese-speaking individuals with the necessary skills and knowledge to respond to emergencies effectively.						read on...
