advocating for mountain bikers since 1987





























austin ridge riders is a nonprofit mountain bike club that builds, maintains, and advocates for trails, and creates a welcoming community to ride with.



the austin ridge riders mission is to develop trails by promoting trail stewardship in cooperation with local land managers, improve mountain bike awareness and access, and bring people together to build a strong off road cycling community.














we can do more, together!



help support our advocacy work by becoming a member of our club. with over 600 members, there is strength in numbers when working with land managers, governments, and other organizations. join for as little as $5 per month!




    here's how you can help
  



































build



mountain bike trails in and around austin exist because of hard work and local advocacy. learn more about our trail crew, and the trails we helped create!






















ride



find places to ride and people to ride with. see what events are happening, and check trail conditions before you ride.






















grow



meet our directors, trail stewards, and ride leaders, and learn about our mission as an organization dedicated to growing our riding community.
















join our mailing list
please add your email to our list so you can stay in the loop. we send you a monthly newsletter, plus occasional updates regarding group rides and trail crew workdays.
