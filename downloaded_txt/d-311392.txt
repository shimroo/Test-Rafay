the wedding partyfrom 2000 - 2005, the wedding party pioneered initiatives that opened hearts and minds to the inequity of gay people. focusing on gay marriage rights, we united hundreds of gay and non gay couples. we raised awareness and consciousness, as we and provided hope and inspiration to all.our message of diversity, equity and inclusion, gained national and international coverage by bringing the faces of equal marriage rights into america's living rooms. and the rest, is his & herstory.
