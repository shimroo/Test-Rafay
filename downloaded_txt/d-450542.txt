fostering opportunities for individuals, families, & communities
community concepts is a community action agency and a 501c(3) non-profit organization that provides resources to move local residents and businesses forward.



administering housing support, mental health, family and children's services -- support cci's mission of helping mainers! 

support cci with a donation
















supporting maine communities since 1965
since 1965, community concepts has provided the communities of androscoggin, franklin, and oxford counties resources and services that support both the basic needs of families and promote self-sufficiency. learn more about our organization, history, leadership, and programs!
learn more














our service focus areas
community concepts provides resources and services grouped within different focus areas. explore each to learn how we help move maine residents forward.
explore services

children & family
head start, parenting workshops, maine families home visiting program, and more. see how community concepts helps support and educate children and their families.














housing & energy
explore services and support for homeowners, future homebuyers, and renters. learn about purchasing a home, homebuilding, energy efficiency, and more.














community concepts finance corporation
ccfc offers business loans and advisory services, homebuyer's education, financial coaching, community wifi installation, and more.














oxford county mental health services
crisis response, residential care, counseling, therapy and more
