at gold star harley-davidson®, dedication to service for our community, our country and our customers is our only mission. to honor that mission to service, along with our dealership name change (formerly known as surdyke harley-davidson®) in 2018. additionally, 10 percent of all profits at gold star harley davidson will be donated to the cadet thomas surdyke memorial foundation.

at gold star harley-davidson® we're not just a proud harley-davidson® dealership, we're also riders – so we know the freedom and adventure you'll find with the right motorcycle. located in festus, missouri, we serve the area by offering the best in new and pre-owned bikes. our friendly and knowledgeable sales, financing, service and parts departments are prepared to make sure your experience is outstanding, from assisting while you're making your choice to ongoing maintenance and customization. at gold star harley-davidson®, we value the opportunity to create a long term relationship with our customers, and we are committed to our dedication to service.
