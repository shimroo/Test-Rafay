     welcome to priority hyundai
we are committed to helping drivers in chesapeake, virginia beach, and hampton, virginia, find their next favorite way to travel. our massive selection of cutting-edge models is sure to have something that entices you. at priority hyundai, we're more than just a dealership; we're your trusted partner in your automotive journey.

our commitment to maintenance: ensuring your hyundai runs like new
at priority hyundai, we understand the importance of keeping your vehicle in peak condition. that's why we offer hyundai complimentary maintenance, a program designed to keep your new hyundai running at its best. this includes regular engine oil and filter changes, tire rotations, and factory-scheduled maintenance for three years or 36,000 miles (whichever comes first).
we use only hyundai filters and quaker state oil, ensuring that your vehicle receives the highest quality care. it’s our way of keeping your drives safe and smooth well into the future.

priorities for life: our award-winning lifetime value package
our exclusive "priorities for life" program exemplifies our dedication to your satisfaction. this lifetime value package, which our customers have been enjoying for years, includes state inspections, and a lifetime engine guarantee for as long as you own your car.
we also offer a lifelong engine guarantee if you buy a new hyundai from us. drivers who opt for a certified pre-owned hyundai from within the previous seven years and under 80,000 miles will also enjoy a 10-year, 100,000-mile powertrain warranty*. our commitment is to provide unparalleled service and peace of mind, making your experience with priority hyundai truly exceptional.

special programs: tailored offers for our heroes and future leaders
at priority hyundai, we believe in giving back to those who serve our community and country. our military program offers a $500 additional bonus towards purchasing or leasing a new hyundai for active duty, reservist, veteran, and retired us military personnel. the first responders program extends the same gratitude with a $500 bonus for active first responders and their spouses towards purchase or lease. additionally, we support the ambitions of recent graduates through our college grad program, providing a $400 bonus towards the purchase or lease of a new hyundai.

your satisfaction, our priority
at priority hyundai, your satisfaction is our number one priority. we strive to provide an automotive experience that is as enjoyable and worry-free as possible. from comprehensive maintenance programs to special offers for military, first responders, and recent graduates, we are dedicated to serving your needs and exceeding your expectations.
