mulehide territory managers
our territory managers are your go-to resource for commercial roofing. they help you craft the right solution for your job and walk the roof before the job starts to ensure that the stage is set for success.
they provide product and installation training to keep you and your crew at the top of your game. they are always ready to answer your questions and help ensure that your job runs efficiently, smoothly, and according to specification.

mulehide training programs
training is one of the most powerful tools in a roofing professional’s arsenal, and we take great pride in offering one of the best programs in the industry. our rise (roofing installation and systems education) program provides the range, depth, and flexibility needed to keep you and your team operating at the highest level.
