perspective: speaking up for patients and protecting access to care

the funding of the government, the budget reconciliation process, and the need for congress to raise the debt ceiling in the coming months are all heating up, and it’s more important than ever for hospitals and health systems to engage with their federal lawmakers and urge them to protect access to 24/7 hospital care for patients across america.


            read the latest perspective
          









chair file: building trust in today’s environment

the aha knows how important public trust and confidence are to advancing the health of all individuals and communities. in fact, we included it as one of the principles in our 2025-2027 strategic plan, which was built on listening to feedback from our members.


            read the latest chair file
          









2025 aha rural health care leadership conference 

showcasing strategies for rural hospital executives, trustees and clinical leaders to improve access to care, leverage unique workforce approaches, implement digital technologies and prioritize health equity.


            register for the rural conference now!
          









the value of aha membership

take a look at all we’ve accomplished together this year.


            download the annual report
          









insights to guide your workforce strategies: new 2025 aha health care workforce scan

the 2025 aha health care workforce scan offers trends, expert insights and practical recommendations to help hospitals and health systems retain and attract staff while supporting their well-being and resiliency.


            learn more about the workforce scan
          









next gen fellowship: now accepting applications

the aha next generation leaders fellowship is a unique opportunity to be paired with an executive-level mentor who will guide you through the planning and execution of a year-long capstone project at your hospital or health system, tackling key issues and challenges affecting health care affordability, cost, quality and safety.


            apply for the aha next generation leaders fellowship
          









supply chain updates and resources

the aha is sharing information and resources on the supply chain situation following the closure of a baxter manufacturing plant in north carolina.


            view now
          









new report shows hospital patient safety performance improving

the aha today released a new report showing that hospital and health system performance on key patient safety and quality measures was better in the first quarter of 2024 than it was right before the covid-19 pandemic and that hospitals made these improvements while caring for more complex patients with more significant health care needs.


            read the report
