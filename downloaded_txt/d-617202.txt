summer camps 
academic enrichment summer camps for rising grades 1-12. programs include engineering, science, art, math, roboting, reading and more.


summer term 
summer courses are a great way to catch up, get ahead and graduate on time. every year, our summer term program offers classroom and online academic credit courses for incoming freshmen, current students, and visiting  students. there is something for everyone, with hundreds of courses available in a wide variety of subject areas.
