mobile is one of twelve centers for independent living within the state of ohio.for over forty years, mobile has partnered with many key national, state, city, and county stakeholders.














about us
















in memory of beverly j.rackettbeverley j. rackett dedicated herself to improving the quality of life for individuals with disabilities by founding m.o.b.i.l.e. (mid-ohio board for an independent living environment) and serving as its executive director for over 30 years. her passion for the independent living philosophy was reflected in the main principles of m.o.b.i.l.e., which affirm the rights of persons with disabilities to live their lives with a full measure of liberty and human dignity.

our services and programs



mobile offers a wide range of services and programs dedicated to empowering people with disabilities and enhancing their quality of life. our initiatives include assistive equipment loans, personalized support services, advocacy, and educational workshops. we aim to promote independence, accessibility, and inclusion by providing essential resources and tools tailored to meet individual needs. our dedicated team works tirelessly to ensure that every person we serve can overcome barriers and achieve their fullest potential. join us at mobile and discover the transformative impact of our programs and services.
