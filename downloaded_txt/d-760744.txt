explore. play. discover. grow. — together
























why csf?located in the heart of hatboro and willow grove, pa, carson simpson farm provides 60+ acres for kids to explore, connect with others, and grow in a christ-centered environment. learn more about carson simpson farm and why campers come back summer after summer. 




    learn more
  













registrationregister your campers and middle school teens for a summer of building positive memories and friendships. our rates are all-inclusive, including benefits like a daily hot lunch, a snack, swim lessons, and 25+ on-site activities. register by the week, with 9 weeks in the summer. 




    learn more
  













camp newswant to know what’s new and happening at carson simpson farm? take a look at our camp news to learn about our fundraisers, events, and current activities! read brief articles written by camp staff members and learn how to make the most of your camper’s experience.
