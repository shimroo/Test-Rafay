university life at penn nurtures an inclusive campus life environment where students grow intellectually, engage in meaningful experiential opportunities, and deepen intercultural and interpersonal understanding in preparation for a life of meaning and purpose.

giving to university life 



				the moh foundation gift of $2.6 million has positioned the weingarten center to create a state-of-the-art test center that addresses the diverse needs of students with disabilities. transformative describes the foundation's impact on how the weingarten center represents a catalyst for change with the inauguration of two specialized roles: case manager for integrated care and instructional design specialist. the test center and new staff positions coincide with penn's mission of innovation, reflecting how the integrated care initiative has become a reality because of the moh foundation's generous spirit.			

- director of the weingarten center
