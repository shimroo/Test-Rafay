ride the bluff!
bluff mountain adventures offers guided atv trail rides on 6,000 acres of smoky mountain trails that explore the foothills of east tennessee. your tour will climb the mountain, cover some rugged terrain and experience spectacular views!









​join us on the bluff

come and drive one of our atvs or utvs to the bluff! we offer guided tours that have beautiful views, and offer an adventure that will make a long lasting memory. anyone under 16 years old must be 57″ tall to participate. rides go out monday through saturday at 10:00 am, 12:00 pm, 2:00 pm, and 4:00 pm.  we stop selling tickets 1 hour and 15 minutes prior to the listed ride times.  tickets must be purchased in person at the ticket office; we do not accept reservations over the phone or online.  however, tickets can be purchased a day or two in advance; again must be in person.  when coming in to purchase your tickets, everyone going on the ride must come in with you and make sure 16 and older have their ids with birthdate with them. unfortunately, we do not have public restrooms at the ticket office.
thank you for making us the most visited atv experience in the smokies!!!  
since we do not accept reservations,  the lines to purchase your tickets can be long at times.  availability changes with every ticket sale.  please feel free to call us for information and updates!
if you can’t be our guest this time, keep us in mind next time!!  we appreciate you!!!
come join us; it’s time to see what the hype is all about!
