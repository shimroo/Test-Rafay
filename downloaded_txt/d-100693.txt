specializing in waterfront & residential propertiesfox lake is located about 74 miles from milwaukee, 52 miles from madison, and 161 miles from chicago. the greater fox lake area offers a diverse assortment of outdoor activities both in summer & winter. it is an outdoor enthusiasts’ dream come true. activities include: 18 hole golf course, walking trails, aquatic center, sport fishing, cross country skiing, boating/swimming, dining, hunting, shopping, snowmobiling, bird watching, resort & boat rentals, public library, salons/boutiques, and much more.
