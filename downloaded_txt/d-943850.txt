transformative visions: studio portraits of black women in leadership















our new publication, transformative visions: studio portraits of black women in leadership is now available in our shop! this collection seeks to rectify and oversight by offering an exploration of black women leaders in the canadian context, through a lens of perseverance, creativity, and pride.get your copy!
