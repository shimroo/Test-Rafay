the canadian 
				lebanese human rights federation is a coalition of canadian and 
				canadian-lebanese volunteers devoted to advocate the human 
				rights cause in lebanon & canada. the prime mandate of the 
				federation is to help the lebanese people in lebanon and in the 
				diaspora to be able to defend and practice their basic human 
				rights without fear of persecution or discrimination.
