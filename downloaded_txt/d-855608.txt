the roosevelt county chamber of commerce, located in downtown portales, new mexico is actively involved in partnership with its members, promoting the growth and well-being of the community. among those partnerships are eastern new mexico university and cannon air force base’s 27th special operations wing. among the attractions in the area are the blackwater draw museum, the blackwater site national historic landmark, the dalley windmill museum, and oasis state park.

mark your calendar



every day is beautiful in green valley
with a wide variety of events and opportunities, green valley has something to offer everyone. celebrate the fun of the minnesota state fair with us each summer. enjoy a scenic stroll through our many parks or play a round of golf at one of our three courses. have your picture taken with the world’s largest owl and marvel at all the beautiful outdoor murals throughout the town.

our community offers a thriving economy, superb schools, traditional values, faith and fellowship, helpful services, and many entertainment options. green valley is truly the ideal place for new ventures and new growth because every day is abeautiful day.

business of the month: the barn at tinsley crossing & volunteer of the month: kynzi shelley – leslie creighton – nieves davis



congratulations to the barn at tinsley crossing for being awarded business of the month by the roosevelt county chamber of commerce! their dedication to providing top-notch service and creating unforgettable events has truly made them a standout in our community.


a special shout out to our incredible volunteers of the month: kynzi shel-ley, leslie creighton, and nieves davis! your unwavering support and tireless efforts, especially with the stunning decor for the annual banquet, have made a huge difference. thank you for always going above and beyond to help out and make our events extraordinary!
