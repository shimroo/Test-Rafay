service and good work.our foundation, our future. 



pickering is a full-service engineering and architectural firm founded in 1946.  headquartered in memphis, tennessee, with additional offices throughout mississippi and arkansas, pickering is 100% employee-owned. we are proud to have dedicated employees that take pride in our mission.




    see our work.
  
















“alston construction company, inc. has worked on over two dozen projects over the last seven years with pickering firm. pickering’s commitment to ensuring their client’s satisfaction and a successful project has been evident on every project. turn-around times on submittals and rfi’s are always prompt to help our fast-track projects stay on schedule.”— c. lindsay | alston construction company, inc.














pickering was founded on the principle of "service and good work",and we rely on our staff to help reach that goal.



we strive to attract the most talented people by providing an environment where they find professionalism and mutual respect. join us!
