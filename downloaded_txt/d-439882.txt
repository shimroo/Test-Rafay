"welcome to goel ganga group" 



real estate developers in pune, mumbai, nagpur & bangalore 



goel ganga group is a real estate development conglomerate spread across pune, mumbai and nagpur in the indian state of maharashtra. with over 4 decades of deep dedication to customer service we have created landmark projects that have changed the skylines of these cities for the better. we have always worked with the vision to create real estate solutions to the ever-evolving challenges posed by rapid urbanisation. at the core of our design and development philosophy is something we call real rich thinking. this means that every action that the company takes is guided by design principles, urban planning innovations and service ideologies that are committed to making all our customers, the extended community and india real rich, that is, wealthy and abundant in the real, sustainable sense…

wtc nagpur 



wtc nagpur is a prominent retail and commercial landmark located in the cbd of nagpur city. the project is presently under construction.
spread across 1.5 million sqft of gla, wtc nagpur is a mixed use, transit-oriented real estate development, constituting retail and commercial business office spaces, with direct access and connectivity from the sitabuldi metro interchange. anchored by a 1 million sqft “glocal square mall” and 0.5 million sqft of wtc commercial office spaces, the project has a retail trade mix consisting of department stores, specialty retail, fec’s, 10-screen multiplex, 300+ seater food court, lounge bars and fine dine restaurants and a commercial trade mix constituting a wtc convention center for exhibitions and trade shows, wtc business club with conference rooms, business center, recreational club and wtc skill enhancement center for trade education and research.
