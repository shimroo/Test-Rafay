who we are



newport convertible engineering is a company that specializes in designing and manufacturing unique, high-end vehicle customizations. they are known for their attention to detail and their use of advanced technologies to create luxury convertible vehicles and modifications that are truly one-of-a-kind.

            consultation
          










where we gather information about the client's goals for the project, as well as their desired aesthetic. we then create a series of design concepts, which are presented to the client for feedback and revisions. once the final design is approved, our engineering team begins the process of creating a detailed plan for the modification.








            modification
          










our team begins the process of modifying the body, using cutting-edge technology and the latest design techniques to create a custom look that is both visually stunning and aerodynamically efficient. we use high-quality materials and construction techniques to ensure that the custom body is not only beautiful, but also durable and long-lasting.








            completion
          










once the modifications are complete, the vehicle is reassembled and thoroughly tested to ensure that it meets our high standards for quality and performance. finally, the vehicle is delivered to the client.




















our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction has made us a leader in the industry. we design vehicles that are both beautiful and built to last.
