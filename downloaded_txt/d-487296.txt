municipal messenger monthly/ february 


meeting dates; dpw dates; '25 holidays; '25 household hazardous waste drop-off event days; '25 primary election date change; coffee w/ the mayor; feb 1 property taxes; annual dog & cat license renewals; roses fundraiser			additional info...







no trash & recycling collection monday, february 17, 2025 (president's day) 


in observance of president's day on monday, february 17, 2025, the department of public works (dpw) will be closed, and no collection services will take place. please see the adjusted trash and recycling schedule for the week of february 17-21.			additional info...







"fall protection: protect yourself now" / tuesday, march 11, 1 pm 


learn valuable tips on preventing falls & protecting yourself from injuries. this session will be led by bridget crimmins, a physical therapist specializing in orthopedic care, who will provide expert guidance on maintaining balance, strength, & safety.						read on...







exciting events from your local fire department in 2025! 


check out what your local fire department has planned for 2025! stay tuned for community events, safety programs, and more.						read on...







moving 2025 primary election date 


per a-5152/s-3965 the primary date for elections has been moved from june 3rd to june 10th, 2025, to accommodate the jewish holiday of shavuot.						read on...







annual dog & cat license renewals 


dog and cat licenses are renewable annually. renewal applications are mailed out in january and are due in february.						read on...







'25 household hazardous waste drop-off event days 


the morris county household hazardous waste disposal program is designed to provide morris county residents with an environmentally-sound way of recycling or disposing of hazardous materials found in and around the home.			additional info...







winter farmer's market! 


get ready for the return of the morris winter market! ?starting december 1st, the market will be open every sunday until april 27th! come see your many beloved vendors as well as new ones! bundle up and bring the whole family - and your dog too!						read on...







volunteer opportunities 


the senior citizens advisory committee continues to update the senior resources website, and one area we believe you’ll find especially interesting is volunteer opportunities.						read on...







april 2025 clean community month! 


april is clean community month in morris township! the department of parks & recreation invites residents and community groups to participate by cleaning up one of the township’s parks, community facilities, or your own neighborhood.						read on...







southeast morris county municipal utility authority (smcmua) service line inventory project 


we have been receiving numerous calls from residents regarding the letter sent by the southeast morris county municipal utility authority (smcmua) about the replacement of lead and galvanized water services. please contact smcmua directly at 973-326-6880.						read on...







state announces affordable housing numbers for municipalities, morris township 


last friday, the state department of community affairs released its fourth round affordable housing obligations for all nj municipalities as part of the new affordable housing law passed earlier this year.			additional info...







township will exclusively send news thru our municipal messenger 


effective sept 1, 2024, the township will be moving away from our website's mass email marketing platform, civic plus's "news flash" and will instead be using their original email service, municipal messenger, exclusively.						read on...
