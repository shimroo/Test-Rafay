about
multiple disciplines. regional locations.  unparalleled expertise and efficiency.
hancock associates is made up of talented land surveyors, civil engineers and wetland scientists dedicated to excellence in land/coastal development, construction and preservation. we maintain our position as an industry leader through our investment in exceptional staff, state-of-the-art equipment/technology and the acquisition of multiple land surveying and civil engineering firms and historical land surveying and civil engineering records dating back to 1852.
