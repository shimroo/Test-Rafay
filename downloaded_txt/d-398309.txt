retrain the brain, a multi-sensory handwriting program.a program to help individuals from kindergarten to adulthood gain impulse control and improve focus through handwriting exercises.
















the retrain the brain program is the culmination of over 15 years of research by certified handwriting remediation specialist jeanette farmer. the program uses a combination of fine motor exercises paired with therapeutic music to engage both hemispheres of the brain, improving focus and emotional regulation.
