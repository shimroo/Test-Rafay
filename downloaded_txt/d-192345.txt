welcome to the department of plant & soil science at texas tech university
we are a student-focused and research-intensive department, offering a range of multidisciplinary
                              coursework and academic programs. plant & soil science faculty conduct research from
                              the cellular level to large-scale ecosystems and post-production processes, and they
                              are committed to helping you reach your educational goals. if you have any questions
                              about our department, are a prospective student, or want to visit with our faculty,
                              we would be delighted to hear from you!

pss researchers developing novel method to help high plains growers protect their
                                 vineyards

                              the application site, and for perennial species such as grapes, this potential volatility
                              and drift exposure may affect plant productivity and yield for many years. researchers
