maitland spotlights


stay up to date
 






  
 
 















road resurfacing 


road resurfacing continues today in the dommerich hills neighborhood. chinook trail, huron trail and chickapee trail will be milled and repaved. nicoma trail, lightning trail and portions of ponca trail and temple trail are scheduled feb. 19-20.						read on...







community cleanup 


the city of maitland will host a community cleanup event from 9 to 11 a.m. saturday, march 8, at lake sybelia. the staging area will be at lake sybelia beach, 290 jackson st. volunteers are needed to gather litter from areas surrounding the lake.						read on...







pickleball lessons 


join coach terri for pickleball lessons tuesdays and thursdays at minnehaha park, 1011 horatio ave. classes are open to beginner and intermediate players ages 18 and older. cost is $20 for city residents or $25 for non-residents.						read on...







recycling 


the city of maitland encourages residential solid waste customers to properly recycle materials. recycling bins should be used only for plastics, metals, glass, paper and cardboard. clean certain recyclables such as food containers.						read on...

boardwalk reconstruction 

the recreational boardwalk at maitland community park will close for reconstruction for six months beginning in early january. the project will widen 600 feet of boardwalk from 8 feet to 10 feet wide ...				read on...




farmer's market
