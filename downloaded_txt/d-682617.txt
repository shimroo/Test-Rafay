about wilton mallthere's so much to discover
serving the saratoga springs community, wilton mall is renowned for its great selection, value and diverse store, dining and entertainment offerings. visit our variety of shops from well- known retailers including dick's sporting goods, jcpenney, homegoods, bj’s wholesale club, ulta beauty, american eagle, talbots, kay jewelers, bath & body works, and old navy, plus fun local boutiques. you can also enjoy dinner and a movie at healthy living marketplace & cafe, wendy’s, yum yum kitchen, whole harvest, 99 restaurant & pub and scene one cinemas.
