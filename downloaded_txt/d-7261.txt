armed robbery at cvs on preston

utility billing payment changes effective december 19 


we want to inform you of some important changes to our utility billing payment system. starting december 19, we will transition to a new payment platform, watersmart.						read on...







fatality accident at preston and lovers

university park earns national accreditation in parks and recreation

city receives high marks in resident satisfaction survey 


in spring 2024, the city of university park worked with etc institute—a nationally recognized government research and survey company—to administer a community survey. this is the first community survey etc institute has administered for university park.						read on...
