    come see what we’re all about

sharon presbyterian church is a faith community, a campus full of activity, a haven of support where you need it, and a second family for many in southpark. since 1831, sharon has been the center of this community, growing with the local area, and meeting spiritual needs. we invite you to take a look around and find your place at sharon presbyterian church.
