welcome to
timms harley-davidson® of augusta
the only harley-davidson® dealer in the csra! sitting on the border of georgia and south carolina, harley-davidson® landed in augusta in the late 1960s. timms harley-davidson® of augusta strives to share the excitement, fun, and adventurous spirit of motorcycling with its customers. we are located off of i-20 in augusta, ga. in our 60+ years of operation, we are proud to be the only harley-davidson® dealer in the csra. we believe that customer service and quality merchandise are the most important things we can offer to our customers.
