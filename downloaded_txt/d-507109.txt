historic tyler, inc.














historic tyler, inc. is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization. our mission is to promote the preservation and protection of historic structures and sites through advocacy, education, involvement, and public and private investment.

an invitation to preservation: a photographic collection of the historic homes of tylerthe book features 293 pages of exquisite photographs and the fascinating history of over 80 historic buildings in tyler dating from 1861-1973. this publication—four years in the making—will serve as an additional fundraiser for our nonprofit organization to help strengthen our financial capacity and catalyze more preservation projects.authors and contributors include: ashley washmon (concept and design), allison jarvis (writer), tiffany tuley grassmuck (writer), and casey nelson squyres (photographer).
