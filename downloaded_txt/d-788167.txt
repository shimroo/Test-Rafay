we celebrated 100 years of public golf in 2024!



the stories of dubsdread's past & present are filled with local lore & legend, world famous sports legends and local heroes. from carl dann's original vision in the early 1920's, to tom bendelow's original design in 1924, and finally, mike dasher's masterful redesign in 2008, dubsdread has been the leader in public golf for 100 years!today, new history is being made by our talented staff and passionate golfers alike.take a moment or two to explore our history page and learn about our upcoming driving range renovation project!
