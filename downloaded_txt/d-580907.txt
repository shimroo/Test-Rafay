welcome!














whoever you are and wherever you find yourself on your journey of faith you are welcome here at holy comforter where we are committed to transforming ourselves and the world through love. church of the holy comforter is an episcopal church in the diocese of chicago, first established in 1902. for over one hundred and twenty years, we have been committed to love and hold it as our highest value. we share and experience the love of god every day here at holy comforter. love shapes all our other core values, including worship, life-long learning, outreach and service, responsible stewardship, and living as an inclusive community. love informs all we do. we hope you will come to know the love of god and share it with us. welcome!
