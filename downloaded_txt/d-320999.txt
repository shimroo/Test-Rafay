case studies



yamazen
yamazen: traceability optimizes the supply chain
machine tool distributor yamazen has digitized its facility in elk grove village, illinois, to optimize its operations. interlake mecalux has installed the easy wms management system in its cloud-based mode along with the supply chain analytics and slotting modules.

read more	










dufry
dufry: direct access and massive storage of 30,000 skus
interlake mecalux and partner andersen material handling have outfitted a 150,000 ft² warehouse in miami, florida, for airport retail chain dufry. equipped with adjustable pallet racks and push-back racking, the two systems offer a total storage capacity of 9,200 pallets.

read more	










adidas
the need for e-speed: new adidas distribution, fulfillment center designed to move product
adidas, the popular footwear and sportswear company, has equipped its warehouse in pennsylvania (usa) with pallet racks and shelving for picking from interlake mecalux. the facility is designed for online order preparation and deliveries to retailers and sportswear shops.
