city news
stay up to date with local news
 






  
 














city facilities closed presidents day on february 17 


city facilities will be closed for presidents day on monday, february 17. trash service is not affected.						read on...







wildfire & emergency response community meeting feb. 24 


the meeting will discuss how chino valley fire, chino hills police, and the city of chino hills work together to protect the community during emergencies, as well as lessons learned from the nearby wildfires in los angeles and recent power outages.						read on...







spring brochure & recreation guide 


the city of chino hills 2025 spring "in the hills" brochure is now available and includes information on city news, special events and programs, and recreation classes. registration for spring classes begins on tuesday, february 11.						read on...







take the ada public survey 


the city is seeking input from the community and organizations that assist persons with disabilities through an ada public survey available now through march 31, 2025.						read on...







general plan update 


as part of the city’s general plan update, the community is invited to review and provide feedback on the draft subsequent program environmental impact report (speir) and the proposed associated 2025 general plan elements.						read on...







eucalyptus avenue road work 


learn more about the construction for the eucalyptus avenue recycled water line and pavement rehabilitation.						read on...







english road and village center drive road work 


road work is taking place on english road now through the fall of 2025.						read on...







sce to perform aerial inspections over the next few weeks 


southern california edison will be conducting equipment and pole inspections in chino hills over the next few weeks using drones and/or helicopters.						read on...







new city regulations prohibit e-bikes on city sidewalks 


the city of chino hills has implemented new regulations prohibiting electric bicycles (e-bikes) and motorized scooters on public sidewalks within city limits which are now in effect.						read on...







community park parking lot expansion 


the final portion of the project will expand the existing parking lot at the southwest corner of the park near the mccoy loop trailhead.						read on...







temporary change to mobile recreation location on wednesdays 


the mobile recreation program location for wednesdays is temporarily moved to the community center due to construction at community park.						read on...







registration available for mccoy family campout overnight event on may 17 - 18 


attention campers! the mccoy family campout returns to the mccoy equestrian & recreation center for an overnight event on saturday, may 17, through sunday, may 18.						read on...







tiny tots registration available for spring session beginning february 24 


tiny tots registration is now available for the spring session beginning february 24 through may 23.						read on...







heavy rain expected - nws issues flood watch for chino hills 


heavy rain is expected through friday evening with a flood watch in effect from 10:00 am on thursday, february 13 through 4:00 am on friday, february 14.						read on...
