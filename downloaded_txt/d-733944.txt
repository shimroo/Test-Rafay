loving people to life















you’re invited!welcome to river road united methodist church! we are a vibrant community of faith committed to spiritual growth, meaningful outreach, heartfelt worship, and promoting diversity and inclusivity. whether you’re seeking a church home or just passing through, we welcome you on our journey of faith and fellowship. come as you are, and be uplifted by the warmth and love of our congregation.








            visit
          









at river road umc, our mission is to radiate warmth and acceptance, embodying our ethos of "loving people to life!" our diverse sunday worship, from contemporary to traditional styles, welcomes everyone with open arms. whether you're in a suit or shorts, young or old, you'll find a place here. join us and be part of our inclusive community!








            fill out a connection card
          









regardless of where you stand in your faith journey, we're here to guide you toward your next meaningful step.fill out a connection card.








            coming with kids?
          









kids are a joyous part of our community! at river road umc, children are always welcome, celebrated, and cherished. we believe in nurturing their spirits and letting their light shine bright. bring your little ones and let them experience the warmth and love of our church family!learn more about our youth group.learn more about our kids ministry.








            service times
          









join us for worship at 9 am in the fellowship hall or our traditional service at 11 am in the sanctuary every sunday. see you there!watch onlineprayer & praise service: thursdays at 10 am in the chapel
