welcome to fun ho! toys - classic hand-made toys from taranaki, new zealand




	from the foundry furnace to finished product these unique tough aluminum sand cast toys are all hand made in the fun ho! national toy museum foundry.

	 

	a ever changing selection of about 100 models are available at any given time out of the range of 275 plus that have been produced since the first sand cast toys were made in 1942. the range of toys change as different models are reproduced in small runs using the original fun ho! moulding plates. the range includes tractors, trucks, cars, planes, trains and other miscellaneous toys. please contact us if you have a model you want that is out of stock and we will place it back on the casting list.

	 

	richard jordan and daughter phillippa peters are the toy makers and fun-ho! toys are made on site. if the toy makers are in-house and the furnace is fired up, you are welcome to watch them make the toy of the day. the toys are available to be ordered through this web site.

	 
please note that the colours of models are an indication only if you would like a particular colour please put in order note.  
most models will need to be cast which does mean a wait. you will be contacted by email if this is the case.

if you wish to speak to us in person please call 06 7567030 or you can email funhotoys@funho.com
