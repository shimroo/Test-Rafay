our location
 






  
 
 



 














louisa county is located in the central piedmont region of virginia near the geographic center of the state. louisa is just 15 miles west of greater richmond, the state capital, and 15 miles east of greater charlottesville, home of the university of virginia. louisa county is located just 90 miles south of the nation's capital and 140 miles northwest of the port of norfolk.
