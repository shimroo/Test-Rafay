returns & cancellations
given the perishable nature of our items, all items on carlosbakery.com are final sale. we are working diligently to provide expedited and fast processing therefore there are no returns, refunds or cancellations allowed on any orders that are in processing. for cancellation requests on unshipped orders we ask that you please provide the request at least 4 business days prior to the earliest estimated delivery date. we do our very best to accommodate requests, however, not all requests are guaranteed to be fulfilled. the sooner you get in touch, the better!
cake builder items are sold as is, with no refunds, cancellations, and/or modifications.
all packages are shipped via ups or fedex and therefore we cannot ship to p.o. box addresses.
if an item arrives damaged or spoiled, our customer support team will work with you to determine an appropriate solution. please reach out to our team using the help button on the bottom right corner of your screen, to resolve the issue within 7 days of delivery.
order change requests
we are not able to make product edits on orders, and our fulfillment center is not equipped to handle special requests. everything ships as it was ordered.
if you would like to modify your gift message or shipping information, we ask that you please provide modifications at least 4 business days prior to your earliest estimated delivery date. we do our very best to accommodate requests, however, not all requests are guaranteed to be fulfilled. the sooner you get in touch, the better!
if you reach out with less than 4 business days until your earliest estimated delivery date, we unfortunately are much less likely to be able to make any changes. we appreciate your understanding!
to avoid a situation like this, please be sure to double check all your order information prior to finalizing your order.
