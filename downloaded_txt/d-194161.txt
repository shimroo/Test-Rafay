important dates & information for upcoming election 


on april 1, 2025, each of wheaton's four district representative positions on the city council is up for election. voters can only vote for one candidate for city council in the district in which they reside.						read on...







wheaton's new building codes effective march 1 


the wheaton city council recently approved the transition of the city’s building codes to the 2024 international code council model code and 2023 national electrical code. the new codes will be in effect starting march 1, 2025.						read on...







parkway tree pruning program continues 


starting next week, crews will begin the next phase of the city’s parkway tree pruning program in the area bordered by: parkway drive to the north, north president street to the east, east harrison avenue to the south, and north main street to the west.						read on...







save the date for native plant sale april 26 


wheaton has an opportunity to “grow native” with the return of the native plant sale from 8:30 a.m. to 11 a.m. saturday, april 26 at the city’s public works storage yard, 820 w. liberty drive.						read on...







city installing 2.5 miles of new sidewalks to improve safety and accessibility 


this year, the city is preparing to add approximately 2.5 miles of sidewalks in areas where there are currently no sidewalks to make our neighborhoods safer and more accessible for all.						read on...







apply for 2025 shared cost parkway tree program 


with the start of a new year comes a new opportunity for you to add a parkway tree in front of your property. the city’s forestry division is now accepting applications for the 2025 shared cost parkway tree planting program.						read on...







comed to perform tree trimming in select areas 


within the next three months, comed will trim trees, branches, and vines that interfere or have the potential to interfere with power lines. this work helps prevent safety hazards and power outages.						read on...







periodic traffic pattern change on main street near front street starting jan. 28 


there will be periodic lane shifts for both lanes of traffic on main street near the front street intersection starting tuesday, jan. 28 through march 21.						read on...
