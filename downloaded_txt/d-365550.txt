presidents' day office and refuse collection schedules 


the town of kill devil hills offices will be closed on monday, february 17, 2025, in observance of presidents’ day. all town offices will reopen at 8:00 a.m. on tuesday, february 18, 2025.						read on...







temporary road closure 


the town will have a temporary road closure of east baum street from 8:00 a.m. on  february 19th through 5:00 p.m. february 20th. the closure will take place at the intersection of wrightsville boulevard.						read on...







trash attack! event and poster contest 


you can help make kdh a cleaner and brighter place by joining your neighbors at this year's trash attack! event and/or participating in its poster contest.						read on...







winter weather preparedness 


it's always a great idea to review winter weather safety tips, even if wintery precipitation is not in the immediate forecast. by staying prepared, you can reduce risks and ensure safety for yourself and others.						read on...







surveying at the disc golf course 


you may notice survey flags around the casey r. logan disc golf course. these flags are not an indication of any changes to the course layout. they’re simply part of a surveying process. avoid touching or moving the flags while you're there.						read on...







popular annual financial report 


the town is proud to present the second edition of our popular annual financial report (pafr). this report is designed to provide a summary of the town's financial operations in a concise and user-friendly format.			additional info...







2025 advanced solid waste collection schedule 


the 2025 advanced solid waste collection schedule highlights the entire year including holidays, bulk collections and other special collections.			additional info...
