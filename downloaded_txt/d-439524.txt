welcome to la palma intercommunity hospital
care begins with a safe place.

welcome to la palma intercommunity hospital, a vital 141-bed acute-care community hospital and proud member of the prime healthcare foundation, a 501(c)3 public charity. for the past 46 years, we have been the cornerstone of healthcare in la palma, serving residents of la palma, orange county, and los angeles county with exceptional medical services.
recognized by healthgrades as one of america’s 100 best hospitals 17 times in 5 different clinical categories since 2014, we are proud to provide superior healthcare services tailored to the needs of our community. recently earning an “a” hospital safety grade from the leapfrog group, we uphold rigorous standards to ensure patient safety and satisfaction.
at la palma intercommunity hospital, care begins with a commitment to excellence and safety, making us the trusted choice for healthcare in our community for over four decades.
