grand avenue is a charming shopping destination in the heart of st. paul with something for everyone. residents and visitors alike enjoy strolling along the avenue to find cozy coffee shops, unique retail, and award-winning restaurants with plentiful patio dining options.many also choose to make grand avenue their home with a wide range of living options; from historic homes and apartments to newly developed luxury living.
