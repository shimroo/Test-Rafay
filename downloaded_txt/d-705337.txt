welcome to ourcommunity of faith!come explore st. peter’s. join us for worship, small groups,  lively music, and serving the community. we strive to build a better community. our worship is relaxed and reverent, traditional and modern. curious about life at saint peters? view the photogallery. worship sundays at 8:00 am and 10:15 am livestream available at 10:15 am




    about us
  































–– connect



we invite you to find friends at st. peter’sthe people of st. peter’s are happiest when we are together whether it is for simple conversation, seasonal celebrations, small groups, or coffee and donuts after worship. we seek to host spaces for people to gather where meaningful connections and relationships are cultivated. 




    view upcoming events
  














come explore st. peter’swherever you are on your journey of faith, there is a place for you at st. peter’s. join us for worship, faithful classes for children, youth, and adults, and lively music programs. serve the larger community with us both near and far.

–– across the generations



families are a cornerstone of st. peter’s st. peter’s understands that parents are the primary way children grow in faith. we offer spaces for children and youth to explore the big questions of faith and for parents to support one another in the christian life.  families are invited to participate in cornerstone family events.

–– serve



build a better community close at home and far away.as baptized members of the body of christ, we inherit jesus’ role of servant. we’re chosen by god and helped by the holy spirit to bring justice to our community and our world. st. peter’s offers a variety of ways to do god’s work in the world, whether it is ministry in the church, in the local community, or internationally.
