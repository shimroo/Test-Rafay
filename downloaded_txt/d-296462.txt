about the festival 



the walnut valley association was formed in 1972, with its sole purpose to produce the walnut valley national guitar flat-picking championships festival, also known as the flat-picking championships, now known as the “walnut valley festival” or simply “winfield” to the long time attendees.still the third weekend in september, in winfield, kansas, at the winfield fairgrounds, the walnut valley festival has long prided itself on being educational as well as enjoyable, but most of all, the festival is a homecoming. and a homecoming not just because of the music and how folks act, but a coming home to a place in the soul and the heart with a spirit of celebration.
