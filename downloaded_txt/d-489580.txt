welcome to cyclemax
     we are a locally owned and operated family business that has been proud to call wilson home since 1974! we are a factory authorized dealer of honda, yamaha, kawasaki, suzuki, ssr, and kymco, carrying a full
                line of motorcycles, atv’s, dirt bikes, side by sides, scooters, electric bicycles, and generators. recently we’ve added hustler and bigdog zero turn mowers to our expanding showroom, along with the largest
                pre-owned inventory in eastern north carolina including hand picked low mileage harley davidson’s, can-am spyders, and polaris atv’s. come visit us today at 6723 ward blvd in wilson and say hello!
