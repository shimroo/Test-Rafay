about us
welcome to wechillit, one of the uk's top suppliers of catering equipment and commercial refrigeration. wechillit started an online presence in 1999 selling mainly commercial refrigeration. prior to selling commercial refrigeration products on the website the directors of the company attended county councils, hospital, convenience stores and local businesses through mid wales, shropshire, and borders. utilizing the engineering experience and product knowledge the website was formed offering quality commercial refrigeration products. wechillit now occupies offices within stockport, manchester. if you would like to visit we suggest a quick phone call to ensure the correct member of staff can meet with you to discuss your requirements. offices are open 9am to 5pm, monday to friday.
