youfoundit! ~ bunny
participating shops in the february 2025 fabshop hopâ¢ 
february 1-28, 2025

read the how to hop guidelines and the rules of the game then scroll down for the list of shops.
below, you will find a list of all the sites participating in the february fabshop hop. at each site you visit, look around to find the hot pink bunny with light pink background that says 'youfoundit!' with february dates — click on the bunny, and a prize registration page will come up — enter your email address and click on the "register for prizes" button.
this sends a message to our database that you registered for prizes at the site.  you can then continue shopping at that site or go back to the home page for that site and continue on the fabshop hop. you can also view a list of shops where you have already successfully located the 'youfoundit' bunny in the member area
