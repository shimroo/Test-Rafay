discover the hidden gems of northwest georgia


explore the natural beauty of our region
northwest georgia is home to some of the most breathtaking natural landscapes in the country. from the rolling hills and scenic vistas of the appalachian mountains to the tranquil waters and lush forests of our many lakes and rivers, there's no shortage of opportunities to connect with nature.
whether you're an outdoor enthusiast or just looking for a peaceful escape from the hustle and bustle of daily life, we have something for everyone. take a hike through the rugged terrain of red top mountain state park, kayak down the tranquil chattahoochee river, or simply sit back and enjoy the stunning views from one of our many scenic overlooks.




experience our rich history and culture
northwest georgia is steeped in history and culture. from the civil war battlefields to the vibrant arts scene of our charming towns, there's no shortage of ways to learn about and engage with our region's fascinating past.
visit historic sites like the battle of kennesaw mountain battlefield or the big oak tree park, which played a significant role in the american civil war. explore the many museums and galleries showcasing local art, history, and culture.




indulge in our local cuisine and entertainment options
northwest georgia is also home to a thriving food scene that's sure to tantalize your taste buds. from farm-to-table restaurants serving up fresh, locally sourced ingredients to traditional southern comfort food, there's something for every palate.
and when the sun goes down, our vibrant nightlife comes alive with live music venues, comedy clubs, and more. enjoy a night out in one of our many charming towns or cities.
