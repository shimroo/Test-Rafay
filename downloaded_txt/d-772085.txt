ref 2021 results


the school of earth sciences has been ranked second in the country in the ref2021 results. overall, 100% of our research was rated as ‘world leading’ or ‘internationally excellent’. 
of this, 67% was ranked as the highest 4* category. in the school of earth sciences, 100% of our impact case studies were also considered ‘world leading’ or ‘internationally excellent’
