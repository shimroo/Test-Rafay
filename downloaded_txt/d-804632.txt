school of mathematical sciences
learn about our flexible undergraduate and postgraduate degree programmes, our supportive community and key areas of research.










undergraduate

renowned for our experienced staff and supportive teaching, we offer our students the opportunity to follow a degree that fits their interests.















postgraduate

our programmes focus on the real-world applications of mathematics and the critical role it plays in all areas of modern society.















research

our research covers a broad range of key subjects in pure and applied mathematics, and is consolidated into research centres.















upcoming seminars

we offer a wide selection of seminar series and are involved in seminar series at other colleges and universities in london. 















events

we're proud to host a lively schedule of events, from our weekly research seminars to workshops, conferences and public lectures.
