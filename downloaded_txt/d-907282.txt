race 



founded on passion and drive. established in 2002, we are made up of an experienced and dedicated team, driven to succeed at the highest levels of professional and amateur motorsport. the only thing we’re missing, may be you. 






start your race











 



tune 



some say modify and pray, we say refine and rely. whether you need a little more power and poise to handle traffic, or your project car is ready for another stage. we stand ready.unlock the potential in your dreams. 






refine your machine











 



street 



our passion & dedication does not stop when we return from the track. even the most reliable work horses need to be cared for. experience the difference, get the same service as the pros.nothing deserves less than perfection. 






preserve | protect | perform















 











north american technical partner & mid-west dealer 



every race horse needs a stable. performance and purism, united in a unique, extreme design, and armed for adventures on the most legendary race tracks in the world, the ktm x-bow gtx & gt2 are setting new benchmarks in racing. a racecar, as pure as a racecar can be, whether it’s on the nürburgring nordschleife, in professional racing or during track days. alongside importing and distributing these ultimate track weapons into north america, we also provide technical, parts, and performance support for all ktm x-bow customers and dealers in north america. 






are you ready to race?













 



ford performance racing programs 



starting in 2025, chicago performance with the help of race cars for you, now offer racing programs and support for the latest and greatest racecars ford has ever produced. all based on the dark horse s650 streetcar, the new lineup of racecars from ford allows customers the unique opportunity to venture into their racing careers on the same base platform across a broad spectrum of racing. mirroring the proven models of some of our favorite brands, ford performance & mutlimatic motorsports have developed an ecosystem of turn-key, track ready monsters. feel the difference and see for yourself how ford has changed the game. 






discover the stable of ultimate pony cars













 











our founder & leader 



from an extensive factory-trained dealer background, our founder, president, & team principal, al uscinski, has steered chicago performance for over 20 years. with grow, development, and constant improvement being the backbone of every new program, al has built a team of extremely experienced motorsport and street service personnel, but where did they come from? what drove al to form chicago performance? and what are we pushing towards today?
