welcome to cv. raisa house indonesia (cv. rhi), your premier destination for exquisite indonesia furniture manufacturer and craftsmanship. situated in jepara, central java, our company stands at the heart of the global furniture industry, boasting 25 years of excellence in manufacturing and exporting fine furniture worldwide.
indonesian furniture manufacturing excellence
cv. rhi is dedicated to producing high-quality furniture with a specialization in french furniture styles and antique mahogany style. our extensive range includes french beds, bedside, chests/nightstands, sofas, dining chairs, arm chairs, dining tables, bookcases, buffets, mirrors, consoles, armoires, wardrobes, commodes, desk chairs, office desks, office chairs, coffee table, occasional table, tv cabinets, hallstands, vanities, bedroom sets, living room sets, and children’s furniture. each piece reflects the timeless elegance of french design, encompassing styles such as louis xv, louis xvi, and louis philippe.
craftsmanship and customization
at raisa house, we take pride in our meticulous craftsmanship and attention to detail. we use premium materials such as mahogany wood, mindi wood, durian wood, & teak wood, ensuring durability and beauty in every piece. our furniture is available in a variety of finishes, including varnish (salak, salak brown, pecan, brown mahogany, red mahogany) and painted finishes (white, cream, black), providing customers with endless possibilities to match their unique preferences and interior designs.
global reach and project solutions for hotel, apartment, villa
our commitment to quality has earned us a strong presence in global markets. we export our products worldwide, serving clients in the usa, uk, europe, australia, south africa, the middle east, and asia, including japan, korea, thailand, hong kong, india, brunei darussalam, new zealand, and saudi arabia. beyond residential furniture, we specialize in crafting solutions for commercial projects, hotels, apartments, restaurants, and luxury homes. whether you require a single custom piece or a complete furnishing solution for a large-scale project, raisa house indonesia is dedicated to meeting your needs with professionalism and expertise.
visit us and experience excellence, best suppliers & exporter
we welcome you to visit our facilities and witness firsthand the skill and dedication of our craftsmen. located in jepara, our factory showcases the latest in woodworking technology alongside traditional craftsmanship techniques. we invite designers, architects, and discerning homeowners to collaborate with us on bespoke designs and large-scale projects. at raisa house indonesia, we are committed to exceeding expectations, delivering not just furniture, but a testament to indonesia craftsmanship and global excellence.
customer satisfaction and long-lasting partnerships
our philosophy revolves around delivering high-quality furniture that not only meets but exceeds customer expectations. we prioritize customer satisfaction and aim to build long-lasting partnerships based on trust, reliability, and exceptional service. every piece from raisa house indonesia is a testament to our dedication to craftsmanship and passion for furniture making.
