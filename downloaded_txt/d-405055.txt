            your journey begins here
          










inner state beauty school is an accredited and licensed cosmetology school that prepares you to not just enter the beauty field, but to excel in it. we help you reach new heights of personal and creative success to earn the career you’ve been dreaming about.








            ignite your passion
          










our course offerings include cosmetology, advanced esthetics, advanced cosmetology, manicuring and advanced manicuring. inner state beauty school offers salon quality student services to our patrons at affordable prices.








            explore your creativity
          










our reputation within the beauty industry has been earned through the outstanding success of our students. since 1982 we have helped over 5,000 beauty professionals ignite their passion and begin their careers. don’t just duplicate the latest beauty trends—be the innovator of the trends at inner state beauty school.
