quiet mind. open heart.somewhere deep inside, you know that you must slow down, reconnect with yourself, and listen to your own inner voice. yoga among friends, through a combination of traditional yoga techniques and elements of contemporary body-mind psychology, encourages a deeper connection with self.through this deeper connection, you are able to know yourself more fully. you can easily embrace the opportunity for change, growth and enhanced wellbeing in body, feelings, thought, and spirit. yoga among friends empowers you to change your life from the inside out, with one-on-one and group classes.

prenatal yoga classesprenatal yoga helps you adapt to your growing belly and changing body while relieving the discomforts of pregnancy. classes will help you improve your stamina, strength, balance, relieve lower back pain, and prepare your body for birth.book a prenatal class >
