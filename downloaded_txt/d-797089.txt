news flashstay up to date
 






  
 
 















state of the county address 


district 1 commissioner and this year's board chair, anne brown, delivered the annual state of the county address at today's bocc meeting.						state of the county address







county offers free vegetation management evaluations 


did you know that in colorado managing noxious weeds is a law? noxious weeds are weeds that are not native to the state and weeds that cause large-scale environmental and economic harm.						more on vegetation management...







county land use code now online 


san miguel’s land use code (luc), an extensive document that details the regulations governing the development and use of land throughout the county, is now available on the county website.						online luc...







bocc reviews placerville survey results 


county residents living in the 81430 postal zip code were offered a survey in mid-november with questions designed to help county officials determine areas of concern specific to the placerville area. those results are now available.						survey results now available







sign up for the chronicle 


san miguel county's communications staff has initiated a quarterly newsletter dubbed the san miguel county chronicle. the initial newsletter hit email inboxes this month. sign up now to receive yours!						become a subscriber







an agenda platform primer 


san miguel county is excited to announce an updated agenda platform and a new live streaming service for meetings. there are a few changes to highlight for those attending or participating in any of the county’s meetings of boards and commissions.						learn more about the new agenda platform







seasonal big game gate closures start dec. 1 


the grand mesa, uncompahgre and gunnison (gmug) national forests will begin their annual seasonal closure of big game wildlife gates on the norwood and ouray ranger districts dec. 1.						closures protect big game habitat.
