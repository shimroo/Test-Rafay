american sanders began in 1903 as the american floor surfacing machine company and built the first floor sanding machine on record with the u.s. patent office. one hundred years later, american sanders remains the worldwide leader in the design
                and production of wood floor sanders and edgers for the professional contractor as well as the do-it-yourselfer. today we continue this heritage with innovative equipment designs focusing on performance, reliability and user ergonomics
                while furthering our investments in state-of-the-art product testing facilities, quality enhancement processes and upgraded production methods.
