your transportation service
 
a-1 limousine has been growing for 60 years across the tri-state area and globally. with over 150 vehicles and 24/7 service, we are the leading chauffeured transportation provider in the northeast. with our incredible affiliate program, for people going places, we also offer ground transportation services anywhere in the world.
we value your safety above all else which is why our professionally trained chauffeurs undergo a vigorous background check that includes fingerprinting, regular drug testing and a complete review of their driving history.
additionally, we provide flat rate fares and no surge-pricing, not something that rideshare and taxi services can offer! 
so sit back, relax and enjoy your ride. a-1 limousine is at your service.
