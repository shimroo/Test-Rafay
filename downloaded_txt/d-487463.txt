welcome to the republican party of los angeles






























are you a republican in la?



with over 1 million republicans registered, los angeles county is the largest republican county party in the usa. we are organized into the 24 california assembly districts (ads) that call los angeles home.don’t move! we need you here to stop incompetent one-party rule. we want to connect you with the fellow angelenos who want good governance and who believe in individual liberty.you are not alone. join us today in the fight to take back our golden state! become a member today by entering your information here and we’ll send you an application.friends of freedom give up to $99.partners give between $100 and $999.chairman’s circle give over $1,000.
