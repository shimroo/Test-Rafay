search suppliers

                                    don't have time to browse our wedding directory? we can save you hours of research and help you stick to your budget with our low-stress
                                    wedding packages.
                                









explore venues

                                    from outdoor garden wedding venues to beautiful wineries, our extensive wedding venue directory includes over 1,000 wedding venues across urban and regional australia.
                                    for a more bespoke approach, our venue experts in sydney, melbourne, brisbane, and perth are available to help guide you to the perfect venue for your plans —
                                    we'll gather pricing, coordinate site visits, and get your venue sorted at no cost to you.
                                









planning tools

                                    our simple wedding planning tools will help you stay on track.
                                    your virtual wedding to do list, budget calculator, guest list, rsvp tracker, seating planner, and countdown to your wedding day
                                    can all be accessed in one place once you sign up for an account with easy weddings.
                                    not only are our wedding planning tools free, but we also regularly run competitions and cash giveaways just for planning your wedding with us.
                                









mobile apps

                                    australia’s favourite wedding planning app is available on the app store
                                    and google play.
                                    you can use the easy weddings app to manage your wedding to-do list, budget, guest list, supplier browsing, and vision board.
                                    one of the app's most popular features is the wedding style quiz. download the free app today, and start planning your wedding from the palm of your hand!
                                









advice and inspiration

                                    our comprehensive collection of over 4,500 wedding articles and blog posts covers everything you need to know about planning your wedding.
                                    topics include advice from our wedding planners, style trends, real wedding stories with inspiring images, celebrity wedding stories, vow wording suggestions, song ideas for your wedding, and so much more.
                                    have a specific question you’d like to ask a wedding expert? head to our expert advice corner.
