            welcome to the ridge golf course & event center
        

            a truly exciting layout for golf with a picturesque course nestled between the golden foothills of placer county; complete with a full-service bar & grill, and event venue.
        








x
















you just found excellence


                the ridge golf course & events center has a truly an exciting layout for golf, a course in the golden foothills of california just a short drive from sacramento designed by robert trent jones jr., known the world over for creating golf holes that take full advantage of their sites while preserving the natural beauty of the land.once you’re done golfing, come enjoy our full-service bar & grill. you can kick back and relax while enjoying an ice-cold beverage and delicious meal. our chef prides himself on using only the freshest ingredients, going out of his way to visit local farmer’s markets to incorporate premium local produce. and don't forget to include us in your next exciting event - we are event experts ready to make you the host with the most at the ridge golf course & event center.
