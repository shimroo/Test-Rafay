                                        about us
     ftaa is a  nonprofit public benefit organization      the florida turkish american association (ftaa) is dedicated to fostering a vibrant turkish-american community in southern florida. through cultural, educational, and social programs, we celebrate and preserve turkish heritage while promoting cross-cultural understanding across generations. our initiatives include cultural festivals, art and dance clubs, language classes, and support for newcomers. we strive to create a welcoming environment where community members can connect, share their heritage, and contribute to the broader community.

 clubs                   ftaa folk dancing club     join our ftaa folk dancing club for fun, exercise, and the expression of turkish culture through stories shared through movement. at the ftaa folk dancing club, we celebrate and honor traditional turkish folk dancing choreographies from various regions, including karadeniz, atabari, and trakya.whether you are an experienced dancer or just starting, you will find a warm, welcoming space to learn and enjoy this performance art that involves great team work.this club usually meets weekly and includes the preparation of several public performances during the fall/winter and winter/spring seasons.

                ftaa atatürk school     students between 4-14 years of age across all turkish language proficiency levels are welcome to join our ftaa atatürk school which includes activity-based learning opportunities related to turkish language, history, geography, culture, food, music, and art. each class offers fun educational activities for your children that enrich their free time while reinforcing their turkish language skills in a bilingual setting. our interactive classes take place every sunday from 12:00pm to 1:30pm during the fall/winter and winter/spring seasons.

 what is the mission of the florida turkish american association (ftaa)?  the ftaa’s mission is to coordinate cultural and social programs and services to nurture a dynamic and vibrant turkish-american community across southern florida.
    


 what types of events and programs does ftaa organize?  ftaa organizes a wide range of events and programs, including cultural festivals, educational workshops, community gatherings, art exhibitions, and humanitarian assistance initiatives. we also offer support programs for newcomers to florida.
    


 how can i volunteer with ftaa?  to volunteer with ftaa, visit our “become a volunteer” section on the website. fill out the volunteer application form, and we will get in touch with you to discuss how your skills and interests can best contribute to our community efforts.
    


 what is the ftaa folk dancing club, and how can i join?  the ftaa folk dancing club is dedicated to preserving and promoting traditional turkish dances, including karadeniz, atabari, and trakya. to join, check our schedule on the website and attend a practice session. no prior experience is necessary—just a passion for dance and culture!    


 how can i donate to ftaa?  you can support ftaa by making a donation through our website. visit the “donate” section, where you can choose to make a one-time or recurring contribution. your generosity helps us continue our cultural, educational, and humanitarian programs.    


 what are the cost and benefit of membership?  ftaa membership benefits include free or discounted tickets on all ftaa activities. members comprise the ftaa general assembly which meets annually to discuss the needs of the community and set priorities for the association’s strategic plans. members in good standing also reserve the right to become a board member candidate and/or vote in board member elections every two years.the membership process is easy. simply complete an online application form and follow the instructions based on your package preferences with this link: https://ftaa.com/become-a-member/                          learn more about ftaa            home     about us     events     clubs            ftaa is dedicated to celebrating and preserving turkish heritage through cultural, educational, and community-focused programs in southern florida.     our mission is to build a vibrant turkish-american community, fostering connections and providing support to enrich lives locally and globally.
