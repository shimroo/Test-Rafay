we are the mills family



we are the mills family. our family has been farming here in cass county, indiana for more than a century. we raise 100% grass-fed beef cattle, pasture pork, and pasture poultry on our farm in northern indiana.  along with our pasture-raised meats, we also grow and mill our own heritage grains for specialty flours and cornmeals.  we are passionate about regenerative agriculture, and are excited to share our family farm with you.
