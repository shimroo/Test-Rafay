the courses


                falmouth country club is a challenging 27-hole, year round public golf course not far from the atlantic beaches, cranberry bogs, and all that is cape cod. walk or ride our championship 18 hole, par 72 course "the osprey course" or enjoy the 9 hole, par 37 "talon" course.
