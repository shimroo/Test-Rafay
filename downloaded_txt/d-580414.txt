melges 24 - never gets old
the reputation of the melges 24® precedes itself.
best known for its very competitive disposition and easy-to-sail personality.
this modern sportboat maintains its status as a leader in high-performance, one design sailing.
the melges 24 is a lifestyle with competitive racing and a focus on fun.
this america’s cup inspired boat generates excitement.
there's nothing like downwind on a melges 24!
