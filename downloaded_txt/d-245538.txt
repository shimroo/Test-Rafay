our vision







all k-12 students deepen their understanding of scientific ideas over time, engaging in practices that scientists and engineers actually use, so that students are informed and thriving global citizens and succeed in science/stem careers if they choose those pathways.




















about laser







washington state laser is a state science education leadership program in partnership with the office of superintendent of public instruction, educational service districts, and the institute for systems biology.
