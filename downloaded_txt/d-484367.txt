welcome to salon elanyour happy place.














our missionsalon elan is an aveda trained team committed to meeting your needs and fulfilling your expectations all in a relaxed and nurturing environment. our team empowers and supports each other and our guests with each service experience.  


























we believe in collaborative service.



at salon elan, we are a team that educates and collaborates together in order to put the needs of our guests first. our goal is to make sure you can easily get an appointment that fits your needs and busy schedule. we achieve this by utilizing a team-servicing system. you can see any one of our talented stylists guilt-free!because we work together, we can offer you a calm, relaxing, and flexible atmosphere.



our core valuesteamwork - everyone working together towards the same goal.respect - consideration for the client, co-coworker and companytrust - believing in each others abilities and systemsintegrity - honesty; upholding the beliefs of the companysupport - being aware of each others needs and taking action


























we use aveda color and products because we share their belief in clean, high performance ingredients. aveda is 100% vegan and developed with a mission of care for the world we live in. using aveda means we minimize waste and our carbon footprint while also providing natural and stunning hair and skin.
