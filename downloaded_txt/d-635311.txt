leading collaboration and innovation to improve health

for over 50 years, stratis health has been a trusted partner and expert in facilitating health improvement for people and communities. we work in inclusive, systems-oriented ways that are centered on equity. our work is wide-ranging while emphasizing the care and health of older adults, people living in rural places, people experiencing substance use disorders, and people experiencing health disparities. 
 
our expert and passionate staff across the country lead improvement efforts through which we are conveners and facilitators, trainers and technical assistance providers, and advisors and subject matter experts. we use leading-edge methodologies to deliver data-driven insights and evidence-informed and culturally relevant interventions that inspire and enable organizations and communities to achieve solutions to their most complex and diverse health improvement challenges.
