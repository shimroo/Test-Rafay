city of morro bay top officials are advocating for the city's interests at the state capitol and attending a leadership academy this week.		additional info...






fire at vistra's moss landing power plant, monterey county 

the city council and administration want to assure our community that city leadership is closely monitoring the developing fire at vistra’s moss landing power plant in monterey county.		additional info...






pavement rehabilitation project update 

the city's pavement rehabilitation project construction started october 28. click on additional information for links to the street list, maps, and dates for work being performed. souza construction will post street closure notices 72 hours in advance.				read on...

the city of morro bay announces the appointment of chris munson as its new harbor vitality director, bringing over 11 years of experience in harbor management and a proven track record of enhancing waterfront operations and community engagement.				read on...
