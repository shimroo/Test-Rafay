 history our hotel 


the best western plus® boomtown casino hotel is the perfect resort getaway in reno, nv. boomtown is nestled in the scenic foothills of the sierras located just 8 minutes from downtown reno and only 30 minutes from beautiful lake tahoe. the boomtown casino hotel welcomes you and your entire family to our beautiful and attractive destination! call us today to make your reservations!





boomtown casino hotel’s newly renovated 316 hotel rooms have plenty of space for both rest and relaxation! choose between beautiful mountain and valley views, poolside, or near the elevator.  your stay will be far from unforgettable. our best-in-class guest hotel rooms offer new comfortable pillow top beds, flat screen televisions, free wi-fi access, refrigerator, microwave, complimentary coffee and water, spacious bathrooms, and more!
