what we do 










wireless advocates is a leading provider of wireless products and services both online and in over 600 retail locations nationwide. by partnering with major manufacturers, carriers, and channel partners, wireless advocates is able to provide the best wireless offers, products, services, and industry expertise to help all of our customers stay connected to their world.





















































our goal 



















we are dedicated to providing the most innovative solutions to connect you to your world.
