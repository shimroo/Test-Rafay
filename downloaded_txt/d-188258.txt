about deptford mallthere's so much to discover
just minutes from philadelphia and atlantic city, southern new jersey’s deptford mall offers over 125 specialty stores and restaurants including cotton on, dick’s sporting goods, h&m, eastern mountain sports, loft, sephora, and xfinity. shoppers can also choose from food favorites red robin, starbucks, chick-fil-a, and more for a post-shopping meal or snack. for those looking to have some fun, they can visit round1 bowling and amusement or bonesaw pilot house brewery and taproom.
