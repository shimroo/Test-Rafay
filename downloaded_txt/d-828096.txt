mayor kevin andersonkevin b. anderson was sworn in as the 62nd mayor of fort myers on november 16, 2020, after serving as ward 4 councilperson. he retired from chico's fas, inc. in june 2019 after 15 years. prior to joining chico's, anderson had a more than 24-year career with the fort myers police department.view
 




city missionthe city of fort myers is committed to providing exceptional municipal services to residents, businesses and visitors in a fiscally responsible manner while preserving the city's rich history, diverse culture and charming lifestyle.view

staff & boardsthe board of commissioners of the fort myers community redevelopment agency (cra) is a distinguished group of community leaders appointed to guide the strategic direction of our initiatives. their wealth of experience, knowledge, and commitment to the community is instrumental in driving our mission to revitalize the city of fort myers, enhancing the quality of life for all residents.view
 




community redevelopment agencysince 1984, the cra has managed redevelopment trusts to revitalize blighted areas in the city of fort myers.view
