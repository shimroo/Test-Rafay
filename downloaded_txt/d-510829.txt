snow streets & snow emergencies 


snow streets are designated throughout the city. designated snow streets are marked with blue and white signs. any vehicle parked on a snow street must be removed within two hours after a snow emergency is declared.			additional info...







register for codered emergency alerts 


codered provides the city with the ability to notify residents in the event of emergency situations or critical information such as water boil advisories, snow emergencies, or large scale electrical outages.						read on...







brown bag music series returns 


the city of bowling green parks and recreation department along with the college of musical arts at bgsu are pleased to announce the return of free musical performances for the community to enjoy at the simpson building this winter and spring.						read on...







begin or end utility services 


welcome to the city of bowling green's utility services portal! whether you're moving into our vibrant community or planning a departure, we've made it easy for you to manage your utility services online.						read on...
