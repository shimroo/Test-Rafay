        a health care home for the whole family


for over 40 years, nbch has been providing patient-centered and community-focused care in southeastern massachusetts.


at new bedford community health, we believe that everyone in the community has a right to quality healthcare that is
        accessible and affordable, which is why we offer such a wide array of healthcare services to every patient, regardless of their ability to pay.


whether you are looking to schedule your annual physical, enroll in health insurance, fill a prescription, or see a nutritionist to manage your diabetes, we
        have you covered. we offer a vast range of primary and specialty care services right here at the health center.
