pacific rim equipment, penticton bc

welcome to the home page of pacific rim equipment, located in penticton and kelowna b.c. we have been supplying construction equipment to the okanagan valley and world since 1989. the staff at pacific rim equipment has the experience to supply you the high quality equipment your construction project needs, whether it be excavators, compactors, wheel loaders or more!
pacific rim equipment offers both sales and rentals of many types of construction equipment. use the links listed above to view our inventory. as you review our construction equipment, you will find summarized details as well as the make, model, and year of the machinery. if you are looking for more information, or you do not see what you’re looking for, please don’t hesitate to contact us.
