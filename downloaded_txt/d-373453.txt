rochester’s original craft brewerywe’ve got your beer.



we like to think of rohrbachs not just as a brewery, but a community. we’ve been brewing craft beer for our friends & neighbors for 30 years. we’ve seen life moments happen over a pint at the brewpub, we’ve shared a beer with people across new york state, we’ve seen our brew crew grow to the amazing team we have today. craft beer truly brings people together and we can’t wait to see what comes next.
