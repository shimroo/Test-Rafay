 x   budapest, hungary 
   budapest, hungary’s vibrant capital, is a city of stunning contrasts, blending historic charm with modern energy. divided by the majestic danube river, the hilly buda side boasts the iconic buda castle and panoramic views, while the flat pest side buzzes with bustling markets, world-class museums, and the grandeur of the parliament building. known for its thermal baths, ruin bars, and eclectic cuisine, budapest offers something for everyone. whether strolling along the chain bridge or exploring the lively jewish quarter, the city captivates at every turn.
