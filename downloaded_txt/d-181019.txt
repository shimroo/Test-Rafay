welcome to morrison motor cars 
concord pre-owned vehicles
back in 1970 when mr. morrison came back from vietnam war, he and his brother began selling special-interest automobiles near its home in concord, north carolina. today, morrison motor company is one of the most active buyers and sellers of collector cars, trucks and harley-davidson motorcycles in the carolinas. located just 10 minutes north of lowe's motor speedway in the heart of nascar racing country, this family-owned business has been a vital part of the local automotive scene since its founding in 1970.

the modest lot - packed to capacity with corvettes, mustangs, camaros, trucks and suvs - is a ''must-see'' stop for any performance enthusiasts visiting the area.

it is the goal of this web site to display the entire morrison inventory in an up-to-date fashion; however, should you have a question about a car, truck or bike not represented on these pages, please call (704) 782-7716 or e-mail one of the morrison staff at mmc1170@gmail.com
