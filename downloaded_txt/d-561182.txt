ohio real estate auctions 


founded in 1999, by third generation auctioneer, barry baker, ohio real estate auctions, llc has grown to become ohio's largest auction firm specializing in the sale of real estate at public auction. the company currently carries the licenses of over 40 auctioneers - with representation in every ohio county.









the advantages of selling a property at auction by barry baker, auctioneer.




orea's auctioneers are well versed in the sale of real estate at auction ranging from residential to commercial & industrial to investment portfolios, to farms. we have experience in selling real estate at auction in many circumstances including: seller-opted, estates, foreclosure, pre-foreclosure, receiverships, reo (bank owned real estate) and many more.
 our company prides itself on staying ahead of the curve by participating in the myriad of quality training courses offered by the national auctioneer's association such as, certified auctioneer's institute (cai), accredited auctioneer real estate (aare), certified estate specialist (ces) and many others.
