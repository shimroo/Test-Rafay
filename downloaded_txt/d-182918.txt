        helping people in southwest florida


goodwill industries of southwest florida’s mission is to provide programs and services for people with disabilities and other barriers to
        employment.


we provide job training/placement, affordable housing, youth programs, and other needed services in charlotte, collier, hendry/glades, and lee
        counties. our programs are funded partially through our 38 southwest florida retail & donation centers.
