woolcock 
          antique auto parts inc 
          supplies reproduction parts, good used and nos parts (new 
          old stock) for ford & mercury cars & trucks, mustangs, cougars 
          and more antique auto for sale.
          in stock items include weatherstripping, window anti-rattlers, carpets, 
          emblems, interiors, and mechanical parts. we also stock a variety of 
          good southern fenders, doors, and running boards for your antique auto 
          restoration. 
           
          woolcock antique auto formerly at 100 bessemer rd, london ontario relocated 
          in 1997 to an auto wrecking yard a half hour north of london. this move 
          allowed us to consolidate our stock of southern classic vehicles, with 
          a new warehouse to store parts vehicles and some un-restored cars and 
          trucks.

          on our regular trips south (every four to six weeks) we have built up 
          a network of contacts enabling us to supply parts for ford galaxie, 
          fairlane, falcon, thunderbird, mustang, gmc, 
          chevrolet, pontiac, buick & chrysler.

          from motors to manuals, hoods to headliners, wiring to wheels just fax, 
          phone, email, or mail a list and we'll try to help you with your restoration 
          or find that complete vehicle or antique car you've been 
          looking for. we can help you with old ford part or parts and 
          many antique car models and pickup you are looking for.
