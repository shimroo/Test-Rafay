about groovy judy




groovy judy is one of the san francisco bay area’s most recognizable music acts, having played many of the city’s longest running events and most popular venues.her music captures the true spirit of the 60s, presented with contemporary flair.  she's logged hundreds of shows and events in her career of over 20 years, as well as being featured in guitar player magazine!groovy judy's third lp release "love" is available now on limited edition cd and digital download.
