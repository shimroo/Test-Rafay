building a sustainable future

at cyber acoustics, we're constantly researching new packaging, shipping methods, and recycling methods so that we can reduce plastic waste and lower our carbon footprint. moving forward, all product packaging will be space-conscious and utilize 100% recyclable material where possible. that means no more single-use plastic in our packaging.



        learn more
      








e-waste recycling program

furthering our commitment to sustainability, our headset recycling program is expanding to now take any pc peripheral, as long as it does not have batteries or a power supply, we gladly accept headsets, headphones, earbuds, usb speakers, and webcams from any brand.
