solent renegades car club
        started in april 1999 with just a few of us around the table hoping for
more members. we now put on 3 shows a year. two of them at the farmhouse pub
and we also work with beaulieu to put on their show on fathers day.

start of season show







		  falls on the 3rd sunday of the month. for 2025, this will be sunday 20st april.
		  we raise money for charity, the charity is selected by the farmhouse pub.around 120 classic and american vehicles. after the show we do the southsea cruise which cruises at 5pm for the first and last show of the year.

solent renegades start of season show 2024 - sunday 21st april



beaulieu custom and american show







falls on fathers day each year. for 2025 this is the 15th june. we help beaulieu put on this show where many american vehicles attend. 
beaulieu custom and american show 2025



end of season show







falls on the 3rd sunday of the month. for 2025, this will be sunday 19th october.
we raise money for charity, the charity is selected by the farmhouse pub. around 120 classic and american vehicles. after the show we do the southsea cruise which cruises at 5pm for the first and last show of the year.

solent renegades end of season show 2024 - sunday 20th oct



southsea cruise







falls on the 3rd sunday of the summer months. april and october is at 5pm, may until september is 7pm. the portsmouth / southsea cruise is a get together of american and classic cars in the car park opposite clarence pier (near the ilse of wight hovercraft). we stand around having a chat and looking at what cars have turned up this month. that at the time we cruise around southsea and old portsmouth
