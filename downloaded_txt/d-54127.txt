						welcome!					








i was a porn and sex addict for over 20 years, yet i was “set free” from my addictive behavior in an unusual way in 1999 (see my story).  since that time, i have learned how to live porn-free and created this website to share my story and what i’ve learned .
sex is an awesome gift from the creator that can be abused like a drug for a variety of reasons.  pornography and masturbation are two ways for people to fall captive to a form of bondage or addiction, where they seem powerless to stop the activity.   my goal is to help people discover the truth about porn and sexual addictions, and share how it is a possible to live a “new life,” free of porn and sex addiction.
jesus said,  “i have come that they may have life, and that they may have it more abundantly.”  this abundant life, one of joy and peace, is available to all people through a relationship with god.
