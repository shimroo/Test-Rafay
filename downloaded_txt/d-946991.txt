digital resources
 





regional ebook and audiobook download systemthe regional ebook and audiobook download system (r.e.a.d.s) is a free online service available to members of the putnam county library system. you will need your putnam county library card and a device with internet access.

the tennessee electronic librarythe tennessee electronic library (tel) is an online library that gives tennessee residents access to magazines, journals, newspapers, essays, e-books, primary source materials, test preparation, homework help, genealogy records, career search and more! tel resources are available free to the public from any computer with internet access in tennessee.
learn more
 

 
 




ancestry.com library editiona special collection of records in the ancestry.com library edition. in addition to a vast resource of collections, we offer a comprehensive research experience combining our advanced search technology and image viewing of original documents.
learn more
 

 
 




newsbank digital librarynewsbank online resources provide researchers of every kind with valuable information on any topic imaginable. over 12,000 searchable titles span a wide array of source types, from newspapers to blogs to newswires to videos and more.
learn more
 

 
 




salem press digital reference shelfthe salem press digital reference shelf is a collection of electronic reference books. these are the online versions of salem press reference items about topics such as health and history that were originally purchased in print and are now electronically accessible from anywhere.
learn more
