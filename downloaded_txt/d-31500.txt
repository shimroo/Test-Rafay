our mission














 

onehealth.ca is a national health portal dedicated to providing information and services to health care professionals working in first nations communities.

            a web portal is a "supersite" on the internet that provides a comprehensive entry point for a huge array of resources and services. our portal provides information and resources, news, research and statistics, online tools, discussions and newsletters pertaining to first nations health and the delivery of health care on reserve.
