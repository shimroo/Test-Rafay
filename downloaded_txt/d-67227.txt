welcome to the 71 
429 cobra jet mustang 



and cougar megasite!




 

.

the purpose of this enthusiast site (not 
affiliated in any way with ford motor company), is to provide 
hard to find information on these extremely rare cars as well as the 429 cobra 
jet and super cobra jet engines they came equipped with. also included is a 
photo gallery of 429 mustangs and cougars and comprehensive information and 
photos of the rare components used on these cars. click the links at the top of this
page to visit the photo gallery of members cars or any other topic of interest
    on the 1971 429 mustang/cougar. we hope you enjoy the site!
