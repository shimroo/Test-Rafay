dan singer

public servant

as a student of public administration and public policy dan has spent his career serving communities at the local government level.  for more than sixteen years dan has had the privilege of serving as city manager of three fine organizations in california.  he is recognized as a compassionate leader with high ethical standards and a proven ability to lead and inspire employees. he prides himself on being a consensus builder and a problem solver.  dan is motivated to work with elected officials, citizens, and local organizations to make communities better, financially stronger and safer.  it is his goal to continue in this fine profession as a public servant.
