the department of surgery at weill cornell medicine is internationally recognized for outstanding and innovative surgical expertise.  our reputation for excellence is rooted in a three-part mission that encourages our team to provide the best and personalized patient care possible; to develop new and better surgical techniques and procedures; and to preserve and pass on their knowledge to future generations of scholars and healthcare providers.
