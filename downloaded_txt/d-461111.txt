the band











  

the band is...the real deal behind bobby’s legendary stage antics. world class musicians dean mickey, michael locke, jerry riggs, and leroy myers are all part of the bfb arsenal.shows run the gambit from festivals for 10,000 to private events, all bringing the same fun and incredible performances. the band has played throughout the us with 6 cd’s worldwide, miller beer commercials, and  multiple florida jammy awards.bobby’s goal is to do whatever it takes to entertain his crowds and make lasting memories for years to come.bobby friss.. the real deal of rock and roll!
