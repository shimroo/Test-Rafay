about stonewood centerthere's so much to discover
stonewood center in downey, california, is the region’s premier shopping and dining destination, featuring some of the most popular retail brands like macy’s, macy’s home, kohl's, jcpenney, h&m, pandora, hollister, and candeeland kids, and great dining options like bj’s restaurant & brewhouse, acapulco's, raising cane's, buffalo wild wings, toast kitchen and bar and olive garden. stonewood center is a great stop for the whole family.
