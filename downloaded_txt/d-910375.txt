welcome to novus air conditioning




if you’re looking for a commercial air conditioning solution for your premises, then you have come to the right place.
 
with years of experience, novus air conditioning is able to offer your company many different solutions for your business requirements. from design to installation, we are on hand to provide an outstanding service that will provide the optimum conditions for your business to thrive.
