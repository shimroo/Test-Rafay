      jump navigation     mpc     sn     cs     sn-mt     ip     mpo     lc     sc           your source for optical interconnects precision fiber optic connectivity throughout the network.  senko advanced components provides precise, user-friendly, and application-focused fiber optic connectors, enabling network operators to achieve the performance and reliability needed to meet the world’s unquenchable demand for data. optical interconnect is our passion and we deliver our reliable products worldwide.      markets       fiber to the chip  mpc connectivity couples light directly to the chip within high data rate copackaged switches and transceivers, providing compact, reliable solutions for ai and quantum computing applications.          hyperscale data center  vsff connectivity offers the highest density and fastest deployment, supporting ultra-high data rates like 800g and 1.6tb, providing future-proof fiber solutions for rapid scaling.         enterprise data center  double the density of brownfield infrastructure with cs or sn connectivity. implement greenfield topologies that are more sustainable by eliminating chassis’ and cassettes.         telco core networks  with the cs connector doubling lc density and the sn uniboot offering a durable alternative to mpo, we equip your network for future demands and high-density deployments.         fttx  senko offers legacy ftth (fiber to the home) connectivity for seamless integration in an interconnected world, supporting high-performance networks from the core to the home.          wireless  senko’s ip series connectors offer flexible solutions with either fiber-only or fiber-and-power in a single robust interface, ensuring reliable performance for wireless applications.
