undergraduate admissions





        a diverse student body learning alongside award-winning faculty in nearly 100 undergraduate programs. we invite you to live, learn and engage with a vibrant local community that is as "unique" as your interest - a place you can call home. 
            



























graduate admissions





        at odu, graduate students engage in small classes with dedicated faculty who are professionals in their fields. each of our graduate programs offer challenging courses as well as excellent research opportunities.
            



























international admissions





        adjacent to norfolk's urban center, odu is a state assisted, regionally accredited doctoral research institution, enrolling an international student and scholar population of over 1,100.
            



























oduglobal





        let odu help you fit education into your busy life. as a student learning online, you'll have access to the same resources and support as students on campus.

annual security and fire safety report| old dominion university is very committed to assisting all members of the community in providing for their own safety and security. as required by federal law the jeanne clery disclosure of campus security policy and campus crime statistics act, 20 u.s.c 1092(f), known as the "clery act"), the university publishes an annual security and fire safety report, which contains information regarding campus security, crime reporting polices, personal safety and crime prevention, and fire and crime statistics for our various campus locations. to request a paper copy of the current report, please contact the odu police department by calling (757) 683-4000 or in person at 4516 monarch way, norfolk, virginia 23508.
