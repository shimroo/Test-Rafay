one of the things we enjoy the most is taking memory or idea and turning it into metal art.this driveway gate was built for a family that had ties to the farming community in eastern washington. his dad had a 1941 farmall tractor that has been handed down to the grandson. the property owner joked and said he would like a tractor gate!!!we built a half scale tractor that is very close to an exact replica of his family tractor. we used real rocks and custom made reeds to match the landscape commonly found in the eastern washington area.we installed a hysecurity gate operator with safety and exit loops to make this a very user friendly automatic gate system which also provides security.this art work earned us a first place in the national ornamental & miscellaneous metals association. click on the thumbnails above for larger pictures

a local landscape architect wanted us to make a entry gate that would reflect an idea they had came up with. working closely with the architect, we came up with a operating system that allowed us to create this security gate and have it lift up allowing access to the property.  





 the challenge in this project was to make a reliable operator with all the built in safety requirements for safe continuous operations in all weather conditions. this driveway gate system has a telephone entry and vehicle detection sensors integrated into it. 











at ornamental gate and fence, we make every effort possible to insure your automatic ornamental gate or manual system is working at it's best, whether it's a gate system we installed one that another company installed.






















ornamental gate & fence won the nomma top job award on this custom driveway gate.this 2012 national award was voted top driveway gate in the nation.we enjoyed building this 3-d artistic metal work into a automatic entry gate.environment west drew up a concept and provided photo copy of a motorcycle.our team of dedicated fabricators and gate specialist worked together to producethis powder coated driveway gate that has a working headlight and tail light.we used custom finishing techniques on the fabricated motorcycle








   this hand crafted sunflower garden gate was built to complement a historic home on the south hill where award winning sunflower plants are grown. we forged a variety of metals like copper brass and bronze to create life like stalks. our goal was to come up with a unique one of a kind stalk that would tie in with sunflower head with hand cut leafs to create a nice 3d look that would look as good from the inside as well as the outside.
