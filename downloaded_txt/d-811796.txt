automotive batteries 
as a producer of automotive batteries under the waste batteries and accumulators regulations 2009, finning uk & ireland are obliged to receive, free of charge and within a reasonable time frame, waste automotive batteries for treatment and recycling from final holders e.g. sites, quarries, end-of-life vehicle authorised treatment facilities, civic amenity sites, etc. we are required to do this in any calendar year we place new automotive batteries on the market.
