 independence harbor is a waterfront community located in edgewater new jersey – just a few minutes from new york city. the housing development is centrally located between the lincoln tunnel (in weehawken) and the george washington bridge (in fort lee).
independence harbor features beautiful one, two and three bedroom condominiums and a few townhouses set in a stunning location on a historic pier in the hudson river overlooking the manhattan skyline. independence harbor is located in edgewater, bergen county, new jersey.
