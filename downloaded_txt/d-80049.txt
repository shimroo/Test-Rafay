







djhome, welcome and introduction 











     djhome.net  
back in 2002 a website called djhome was 
created to promote our vacation condo property in hawaii.  we delighted in 
summarizing information about the island of kauai for out guests.  people 
regularly compliment us on all our information about princeville on our nearly 
advertisement free website.  our reward is a happy vacation condo rental 
guest.  helping people with their vacations to kauai worked so well that we 
added information about winter park ski resort in colorado and made our condo at 
zephyr mountain lodge available for rent.  however, now that we no longer 
rent vacation condos, these pages have been removed [technically 410'd]as time went on, we added to djhome.  
we put descriptions of our businesses up on the website.  and we started 
putting some accounts of exotic travels on web pages and just posted those web 
pages at djhome.  today, the website is a bit of a potpourri.  this 
causes search engines a bit to indigestion and our ranking is not as good as it 
might be if we were to play by their rules.  perhaps to our disadvantage we 
continue to prioritize providing content about winter park, princeville, 
cyclopraxis, and other topics above advertising, extraneous links, and search 
engines.
