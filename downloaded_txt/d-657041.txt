why pdt?philadelphia dance theatre is the region's premier classical ballet school and performing company. under the direction of joy delaney capponi, the school provides comprehensive dance training for the recreational dancer or the aspiring professional. pdt provides a culture where each student is valued and nurtured within a community that promotes excellence, encourages personal responsibility, and emphasizes integrity. alumni have gone on to study with internationally recognized schools. pdt's performing company presents an acclaimed nutcracker each december and a new works each spring as well as performing as members of regional dance america northeast.

missionsince 1998, philadelphia dance theatre has been offering high-quality, richly creative, pre-professional dance instruction for both children and young adults in the philadelphia area. it is our mission, to enrich, inspire, and uplift lives through classical ballet, dance education, and performance.
