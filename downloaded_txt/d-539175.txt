








            barr.org
        
















	                        bibliographic association of the red river
	                    












	                    	barr is a consortium of libraries located in grayson county.
	                    

	                        the libraries offer:
	                    

an online catalog which includes holdings of the grayson college library, sherman public library, and denison public library.
	                        
a barrcard which provides residents of sherman, denison and the students of grayson college access to the materials and services of grayson college's library, sherman public library, denison public library, and austin college's abell library.
	                        
a courier system which delivers books between the four libraries
	                        
public internet access
