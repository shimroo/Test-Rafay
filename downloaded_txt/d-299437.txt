port austinup north, close by.



visit port austin, the tip of michigan's thumb, and be immersed in the best that lake huron has to offer: beautiful sunrise and sunset over the water, sandy beaches and dunes, unique rock formations, fishing and water sport rentals to mention just a few.  gift shops, a top rated farmer's market, a variety of parks, camping, accommodations and visitor 'fun stuff' are all plentiful. travel west on m-25 and find the shoreline dotted with parks, beaches and recreational facilities. turn east and visit historic areas such as grindstone city, the restored lumber town museum of huron city, and lighthouse county park featuring historic pointe aux barques lighthouse.














life in port austina beautiful place to visit, an even better place to live.



are you looking to slow down and live the “lake life”? consider a move to the greater port austin area! we have resources to help you find a job, a place to live, organizations to volunteer with, and more.














explore grindstone city



grindstone city is a historic village minutes from downtown port austin. with a rich history rooted in the grindstone industry, and its picturesque location on the shores of lake huron, grindstone is a notable destination for history enthusiasts and outdoor adventurers alike. learn more here.














get involved



port austin has a wonderful group of volunteers but we can always use more! if you’re someone who likes to be involved with the community, events, preservation, or just likes the social aspects of volunteering, we have plenty of local organizations that can use your help. find an organization.
