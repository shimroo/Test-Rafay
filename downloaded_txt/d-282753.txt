              simmered low and slow            

all stagg® chili varieties start with a signature recipe made from a robust blend of savory spices and choice ingredients. the chili is slow-simmered to bring out the rich, full flavors from each of the ingredients.

          how to prep        



















      slowcooker

          direct link    




 















      microwave
    










empty chili into microwave-safe bowl.
cover loosely.
heat on high (100% power) 2 to 3 minutes or until hot, stirring once. stir before serving.



          direct link    




 















      stovetop
    










empty chili into saucepan.
heat over medium heat 5 minutes or until hot, stirring frequently.
