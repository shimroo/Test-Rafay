about us

our mission is to help businesses prosper through advocacy, education and exposure. the st. lucie county chamber of commerce maintains various roles in our community: first, to be the voice for business; to unite the business and professional people in our community in an effort to expand the economy of the area; and to build upon our membership diversity to provide businesses opportunities.

our events
we as a chamber have found the importance of networking for success of your business. we have various opportunities to network through attending and sponsoring our annual, quarterly, and monthly events.
