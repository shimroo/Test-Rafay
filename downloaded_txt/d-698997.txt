                   since 1915
              


                   a true of art does no simply appear out of nowhere. it begins as an idea in the mind of its creator, who then turns into a tangible form through his skill and artistry. for the final creation to be genuine, this process must remain uninterrupted fron the conception to completion.this philosophy has been central to the making of aya shotguns since 1915.





                   every aya gun embodies the very best knowledge and the skill of generations of leading gunsmiths, and for this reason it is considered throughout the world to be one of the finest examples of the combination of science and art, of reliability and elegance which only the finest gunmakers can produce.
              










                aya fine shotguns and rifles
            


                aya make double barrelled shotguns in both side-by-side and over-and-under versions, in boxlock and sidelock versions, as well a double rifles, and all models share a common denominator: their exceptional quality. the ownership of an aya shotgun makes one part of a special and exclusive community.
