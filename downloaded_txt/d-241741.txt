established in 1995, hamacher produces small lot, hand-crafted, aged willamette valley wines. 




    visit us
  














with gentle handling of my wines in the cellar and by seeking balanced, low yields in selected vineyards, i strive to produce handmade wines of individuality and elegance. i intentionally age my pinot noirs so you can enjoy aged wines in our tasting room or in your home. - eric hamacher


























visit us



we are the closest willamette valley winery to portland, oregon, located in beaverton, oregon. now residing at winery lane collective, formerly known as the hamacher wines tasting room. open thursday-monday: 12 - 6 pm 




    visit us
  


























our pinot noir, chardonnay, rosé and several other special releases are in extremely small supply. get them at winery lane collective or order online.
