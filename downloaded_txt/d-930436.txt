berakah quad biking weekend.

this past weekend we dicided to test out the new quad trailer that
            i bought and go to the berakah
                eco trail  for an outride.

            it turned out to be one of the best quad experiences we've have had in a long
            time!!

            the quad track is layed out in such a way that everyone who gives it a try,
            will have a good time.

            the obstacles are scary but fun, and for those with a weak heart there is always
            an alternative route.

            tacha spent most of the time behind the camera, so she isn't in any of the shots,
            but she went wherever i did :)
