about freehold raceway mallthere's so much to discover
freehold raceway mall is the premier shopping destination of central new jersey. with a wide variety of popular stores including apple, macy's, primark, zara, lululemon, ll bean and aerie, plus delicious dining options including the cheesecake factory, pf chang's, brio tuscan grille, marmara mediterranean cuisine, chick-fil-a, and starbucks, you're bound to find your favorite brands that make it worth the trip.
