about us
shafer's classic reproductions (scr) is a manufacturer of classic auto parts for:

1947-72 chevrolet
1967-74 camaro
1964-72 chevelle
1953-82 corvette
1972 buick
1957-74 oldsmobile
1957-65 pontiac
1966-77 bronco
1960-70 falcon
1955-73 full size ford
1964-73 mustang

welcome to shafer's classic reproductions (scr). we have been providing quality classic car parts for over 30 years! with an inventory of over 5000 classic car parts in stock we offer hard to find parts, original and modified that you need to restore your classic auto. we offer everything from brake lines, gas lines, transmission oil cooler lines to brake hardware kits, disc brake conversion kits, gas tank sending units, switches and exhaust systems. we accept mastercard, visa, discover and american express. thank you for shopping with shafer's classic reproductions. please contact us if you have any questions.
