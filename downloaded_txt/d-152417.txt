welcome
the uf/ifas department of animal sciences is an academic department of the college of agricultural and life sciences (cals), a unit of the institute of food and agricultural sciences (ifas). in the areas of teaching, research, and extension, our faculty integrate the most modern technologies available with personal expertise and attention to the needs of students and our industry.
