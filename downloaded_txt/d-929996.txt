welcome to york jersey underwriters inc.
there are many types of insurance that you may need in the united states. we offer many types of insurance to help you be well-insured for many risks. when you need insurance, call york jersey underwriters inc. to get started. 
auto insurance
it's required by law in new jersey to have auto insurance for your vehicles. most states in the u.s. require insurance. we offer this insurance so that you're covered as required by law. insurance is important from the first day you purchase your vehicle. 
home insurance
every homeowner should have this type of insurance to protect themselves. it covers a wide range of risks, like certain disasters. it's never a good idea to go without this vital insurance. 
commercial insurance
if you run a business, you may need commercial policies. every business needs to be protected against risks. the type of business you have will largely determine which kinds of commercial insurance you need. some need commercial auto, while others need general liability or cyber insurance. 
flood insurance
flood insurance is important because home insurance doesn't cover floods. every homeowner should have flood insurance in place. this can protect against a number of types of flooding that can cause a lot of damage to your home. 
renters insurance
if you rent your home, having renters' insurance is a must. it's important to protect your belongings and your liability when renting. most landlords also require you to have this insurance for everyone's protection. 
boat/watercraft insurance
boat and watercraft insurance works similarly to auto insurance. it can cover liability or damage to your own vessel, depending on which types of coverage you get. it's important to have liability coverage for your boat to avoid financial hardship later. 
rv insurance
rv insurance is very similar to auto insurance as well. however, it also covers personal belongings and equipment that many rvs have inside. having both liability and collision coverage for your rv is recommended. 
motorcycle insurance
another policy that works like auto insurance, this policy is important to have. motorcycle owners are required to have motorcycle insurance. 
condo insurance
if you own a condo, there is a specialized insurance type just for you. it covers the parts of your home that you own as well as your belongings and more. 
recreational insurance
if you have a business that includes outdoor activities, you need to have recreational insurance. it's important for protecting your finances.
