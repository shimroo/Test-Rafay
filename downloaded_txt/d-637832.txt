important announcements




































washington dulles airport hotel with shuttle



our free, 24-hour guest airport shuttle, friendly service and a passion for excellence await you at the holiday inn® washington - dulles international airport hotel. our hotel's location near dulles airport (iad) provides a convenient place for business and leisure travelers to stay while visiting the washington, d.c. area.
our hotel's corporate guests can rest easy knowing that we offer a well-equipped business center along with free high-speed, wired and wireless internet access for all their technical needs. thanks to a central location, an array of businesses, such as raytheon, amazon, orbital atk, rockwell collins, northrop grumman, and faa are just minutes away.
from defying gravity at the national air and space museum's steven f. udvar-hazy center to shopping at the leesburg corner premium outlets, there's plenty to do in sterling, va. our hotel's leisure guests are especially encouraged to visit one of the many civil war battle sites or tour a beautiful local winery or microbrewery while visiting.
we invite you to take advantage of our hotel's location near dulles airport and make plans to stay with us during your next trip. amenities like our on-site fitness center will help you keep up with your routine during your travel. don’t forget to fill up on breakfast at chantilly’s or stop in for a drink at o’malley’s. make your reservations today!

monday–thursday: 5pm–9pm
friday: 3pm–10pm
saturday: 10am–10pm
sunday: 8am–2pm

hours subject to change at any time, holiday hours vary.

faqs for holiday inn washington-dulles intl airport



does holiday inn washington-dulles intl airport have a pool?


yes, holiday inn washington-dulles intl airport has an indoor pool.




how much is parking at holiday inn washington-dulles intl airport?


complimentary parking is available at holiday inn washington-dulles intl airport for hotel guests.




can i bring my pet to holiday inn washington-dulles intl airport?


pets are welcome at holiday inn washington-dulles intl airport. our pet policy: pets up to 75 pounds are welcome in select guest rooms and must be crated while unattended. a non refundable 100 usd fee will be applied per pet, per stay. one week max; 100 usd per additional week.




can i cancel my booking at holiday inn washington-dulles intl airport for a full refund?


always check the hotel's cancellation policy before booking a rate. room cancellation is subject to rate booked at time of reservation and cancellation policy varies by rate.




when is breakfast at holiday inn washington-dulles intl airport?


on weekends, breakfast is available from 6:30 am to 10:00 am.




is there a complimentary shuttle available at holiday inn washington-dulles intl airport?


no, there is not a complimentary local area shuttle available at holiday inn washington-dulles intl airport.




what are the check-in and check-out times for holiday inn washington-dulles intl airport?


check-in at holiday inn washington-dulles intl airport is from 3:00 pm, and check-out time is 12:00 pm. contact the hotel directly for options available for early check-in or late check-out.




does holiday inn washington-dulles intl airport offer free wi-fi?


yes, the holiday inn washington-dulles intl airport offers free wifi!
