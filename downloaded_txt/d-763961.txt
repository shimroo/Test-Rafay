the city of fulton police department, in partnership with the community, serves to protect the people, enforce the law, and enhance the quality of life of our citizens.














“i try to be very transparent to the community, let them know what we do as a police department and who we are as a department, to try and build a really strong connection. this community is already supportive, so we’re very fortunate in that area, but we always want to try and build that bridge even stronger.”



- chief michael curtis
