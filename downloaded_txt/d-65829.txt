turtle island inspires action to protect and restore our oceans and coasts.
we combine actions by people like you with education, science, policy, and law to create a healthier planet for sea turtles, whales, sharks and all living things.

our impact













  





 50,000 sea turtles saved annually after shutting down a slaughterhouse and ending all legal slaughter of turtles in mexico. 








 250,000 square miles of the pacific ocean closed to longline and driftnet fisheries to protect marine animals. 








 150 sharks tagged with acoustic telemetry to collect data to create an international migratory swimway in the pacific.
