upcoming events






young adults praise and worship night

february 19 at 7:00pmcalling all 18-28 year olds to join us for a praise and worship night! we will be focusing on humility and thanksgiving, and will gather in room 101-102 from 7-8:15pm. come worship christ with us!












legacy lunch 

thursday, february 20th @ noonlunch with pastor marty & sheila in the flc.pastor marty & sheila will join us for lunch and reflect on their early lives and their years of ministry at grace. (lunch provided)












the remnant retreat

feb 28 - mar 1 at wildwood hills ranchthis overnight retreat, that is only a 30-minute drive away from des moines, is designed to encourage and equip us to raise a generation of women with resilient faith.at the retreat, you’ll enjoy a peaceful, scenic setting that’s perfect for deepening your relationship with god and connecting with other women. throughout our time together, we’ll worship together, eat meals together (that you don’t have to cook!), and hear from other women of grace during sessions that will ignite your faith.
