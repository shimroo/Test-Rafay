ccsl @ agra rodeo sunday feb 16




ccsl sunday social @ agra rodeo – free admission for ccsl members!saddle up and join us for a rootin’ tootin’ good time at the 39th annual arizona gay rodeo at corona ranch & rodeo grounds in laveen, az!free admission for all ccsl spring 2025 registered members on sunday, february 16 – so grab your teammates and head over after your games!location: 7811 s 29th ave, laveen (phoenix), azrodeo starts: 11:00 am | awards: 7:00 pmexperience thrilling rodeo action, including bull riding, barrel racing, steer decorating, wild drag race, and more! plus, enjoy the full-service bar and games on the green from 12 pm – 4 pm.don't miss out on this unforgettable day of fun, community, and cowboy spirit!yeehaw! see you at the rodeo!

about us
founded in 1994, cactus cities softball league (ccsl) is an adult slow-pitch softball league dedicated to personal growth and athletic development. our primary purpose is to serve the lgbtia+ community in and around phoenix arizona. cactus cities softball league is a 501(c)(3) charitable organization.
