welcome to cali camp














the best summer camp for kids in los angeles — full of laughter, growth, and adventure



nestled in a wondrous natural world in the heart of rock-lined topanga canyon, cali camp is a welcome kids camp adventure in los angeles. a place to roam streams, meadows and oak trees. forget the city – enter the new world. established in 1955, our iconic summer camp welcomes campers, ages 4 to 15, to the santa monica mountains. our staff holds hundreds of years of combined experience.cali camp is a place of ultimate fun. yet, activities – from rock climbing for kids to art, from adventure camp to equestrian camp – only launch every camper’s experience. in a transcendently-beautiful sky-ceilinged land, campers reclaim their connection to the environment and its springboard for their imaginations. welcome to the best summer camp in los angeles!
