what is the s club 7  




the unrelentingly bubbly, upbeat pop group s club 7 is the creation of simon fuller, driving force behind and manager of the spice girls. considering fuller's reputation as a star maker, sc7's success should come as no surprise. the group's first single, "bring it all back," scored big in britain in 1999, and a bbc tv series built around the group, "miami 7", became a hit about the same time. having conquered england, the group set their sites on america in 2000 with the u.s. release of their debut album, s club 7 in 2000.
