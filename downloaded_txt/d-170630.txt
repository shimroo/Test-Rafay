bekaert strengthens its offering in synthetic offshore lifting and mooring solutions  
as part of its growth strategy, bekaert has acquired bexco, one of the leading companies in synthetic ropes for lifting and mooring in offshore energy and marine applications. bekaert already has significant experience in this field via its bridon-bekaert ropes group business unit, and now further strengthens its offering.   
by combining our expertise and capabilities in the offshore wind industry, we will create a global offshore rope solutions provider to support the offshore wind industry's future growth. this will ensure our commitment to fulfilling bekaert's strategic promise of driving growth through sustainable solutions and practices that have a positive impact.

bekaert’s integrated annual report 2023 provides material information on bekaert for the year ending 31 december 2023. it offers a comprehensive view on how we create value for our stakeholders through the progress we make on financial and esg-performance indicators. our approach puts the value and impact we create, as a company, in a broader perspective:  beyond reporting, beyond financials, beyond tomorrow.
