courtesy vehicles
ford courtesy vehicles are low mileage vehicles that are eligible for new vehicle retail incentive offers as well as a courtesy discount. the balance of the new vehicle limited warranty is included at time of purchase. these vehicles were formerly used as loaner and demonstration vehicles through the ford courtesy program. these vehicles were cared for by our very own service department and qualify for the greatest discounts available.
