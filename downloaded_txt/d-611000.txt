 the art of web publishing:

my fundamental axioms of web publishing are:

web documents shall comply to the standards set by the w3c !
the strength of web publishing is actuality, not layout !
actuality and accuracy are much more important than appearance !
the web shall not only provide the freedom for everyone to publish, but also the reader shall have the freedom to enjoy the information the way she or he wants !
if it is not there, don't mention it !
