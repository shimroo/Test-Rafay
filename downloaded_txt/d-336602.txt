lgrc in the news

 los gatos rowing club is a non-profit public benefit corporation that exists to promote amateur rowing in the santa clara valley. to do this, we provide the settings, facilities, equipment, offering offer junior programs for high school rowers, master programs for adult rowers, outreach programs as wells as community service.
