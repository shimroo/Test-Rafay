about the accpdthe department has an authorized strength of 244 sworn positions, 64 civilian positions, and 33 crossing guards. we were the 48th police department to be nationally accredited by calea and the first to receive state certification through the gacp. 



 
 









 
 
 









 







 







 







 
 
 





the department is divided into three main bureaus, each led by a deputy chief.



 
 
 





field operations bureau the field operations bureau consists of the east and west patrol divisions as well as the communications and technology division.



 
 





operational support bureau the operational support bureau consists of the criminal investigations division and the administrative services division.



 
 





community services bureauthe community services bureau consists of special operations division and auxiliary services.
