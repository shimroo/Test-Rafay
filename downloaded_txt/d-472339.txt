who we are 



creative closets, the affordable answer, has been serving new york city, west chester county, and surrounding areas since 1984. our staff of experienced designers and highly trained installers will transform your closet, laundry room, garage, attic, basement, office, mud room, or any other area into a highly organized, efficient, and useful space. our knowledge and expertise enable us to offer many different creative designs, allowing a style for every taste and a design for every budget.

why work with us 



we are not franchised, we’re localized. we know how difficult making the most of your new york space can be. we live here, too. and we know that what works in california doesn’t always work here. our goal is to provide every client with a custom design that best suits their space using the highest quality materials at the best possible price. we want each of our customers to join a long list of clients that keep coming back for all their storage needs, from home to home and, after 39 years, generation to generation.
