experience what makes pie-tanza a local favorite since 2005!

 

welcome to pie-tanza, a family-friendly restaurant located in north arlington's north harrison neighborhood. we pride ourselves on offering affordable, high-quality meals with all the freshest ingredients. kid-friendly options too! our open-air kitchen and brick oven take center stage, creating wood-fired pizzas with all the toppings. our menu is crafted with intention, ensuring there is something for everyone. come see why our customers turn into friends. buon appetito!

 

dining room open daily 11:30am - 9pm! curbside carry-out also available. call 703.237.0200 to place your order.
