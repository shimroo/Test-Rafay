welcome!

 

ncbc is a community of believers with a heart and passion to love god, love others, and bless the world. our prayer is that through our faith community, you would encounter god and grow in your love and worship of our lord. whether you are new to the silicon valley or a bay area native, a student or working professional, single or married with kids, a life-long christian or just seeking to learn more about god—all are warmly welcome in our community.our sunday worship services are held weekly at 9:30am (hope chapel) & 11:30am (grace chapel) in mountain view, california. all are welcome to either service, however the 9:30am service is geared towards post-college and up; and the 11:30am service is geared towards college students. we look forward to sharing a journey of faith with you!
