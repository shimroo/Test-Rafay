lavalette landscaping










  


welcome to lavalette landscaping, formerly lavalette nursery. since 1947 we have provided the tri-state area with complete, professional landscaping that reflects our client's wishes. we design and install projects that are exciting and beautiful while never losing sight of the practical. our jobs range from planting that one special tree, to complete home landscapes from the preliminary design ideas to the final details.we can keep your landscape looking good with regular maintenance and provide answers to any questions you might have later on. for people who want to do their own landscaping, we have the plants, materials and design ideas to ensure your success.call or email today and let's get started.









 


no job is too large or too small. from planting that one special tree to a complete landscape overhaul, we can get the job done.
