emanuel morez
emanuel morez, located in northridge, california was founded in 1991 and is one of the finest quality lighting & furniture design and manufacturing companies in the united states. we stand apart in our unique design, superior quality, customization, and customer service. all of our lighting fixtures are hand made in house and built from start to finish, using a lean manufacturing system. streamlining our production process allows for a remarkably short lead time of 3-4 weeks , for both standard and custom pieces.
