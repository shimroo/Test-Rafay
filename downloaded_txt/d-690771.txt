frequently asked questions





where can i purchase wired?





                please visit our where to buy and
                click on the appropriate state. we try our hardest to keep this
                information up to date at all times, however, we may have a
                couple discrepancies. if you have any specific questions about
                availability in your area please feel free to reach out to us at
                contact @ unique-beverage.com.
              






can i buy directly from you or in bulk?





                we do not offer online sales, however any store that carries
                wired may be able to special order you in some by the case.
              






do you have wired coupons?





                we do not have manufacturer coupons; but our products are often
                on sale and we will from time to time run a specific coupon with
                a store. please watch your local ads or follow our social media
                accounts for notifications.
              






how do you read the codes?





                we use the julian calendar based on production date. on the
                bottom of the can there is a code, an example of that code:
                (letters)24001 - 24 (year), 001 (day of year), this product was
                produced on january 1, 2024. cans have an approximant shelf life
                of 2 years.
