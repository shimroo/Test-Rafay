about inland centerthere's so much to discover
located in the heart of the inland empire in san bernardino, ca., inland center is the area’s premier destination for shopping and dining. the center includes many of your favorite stores and dining. visit macy's, jcpenney, h&m, forever 21, vans, valliani jewelers, hollister co., windsor, victoria’s secret, tilly’s, boxlunch, t-mobile and other popular stores and dine at delicious eateries including cinnabon, charley's philly steak, sbarro, great khan, ruby thai kitchen and more.
