city news








city offices and facilities will be closed february 17th 

all city offices and facilities will be closed on monday, february 17th in observance of president's day.  city offices and facilities will reopen on tuesday, february 17th at 8:30 am.	



city council meeting (february 18th) 

city council will be meeting on tuesday, february 18th at 6:30 pm where several proclamations will be presented, a presentation of the annual audit will be given to council, and two public hearings will be being held with the planning commission.				council meeting information




apply now to join city committees, commissions, and boards 

we are now accepting applications for the new parks, recreation and events committee along with all the other city committees, commissions and boards.				more information to apply




comprehensive plan update survey 

your voice. your city. your future!  

tega cay residents—this is your chance to make a lasting impact on our community!  the city of tega cay is updating its comprehensive plan, and we need you to help shape the future!		comprehensive plan update survey
