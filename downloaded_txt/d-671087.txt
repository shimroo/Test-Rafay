lifestyle with style™
                                    welcome to gals on and off the green - a shop designed by women, for women! since 2004, gals on and off the green has been helping women look and feel better about themselves, by offering stylish golf, resort, sports and casual wear in sizes ranging from zero to 22.

                                    at gals on and off the green we are absolutely passionate about fit, fashion, function (and fun!) for women of all ages, shapes and sizes! at gals, we believe you should not buy something simply because “it fits”, but rather because it fits well and is stylish and you feel great when you put it on!

                                    we carry an amazing selection of stylish fashions in a variety of sizes and fits and lengths so that you have great choices. from country club classic, to edgy and fashion forward - from sporty chic to feminine – from more modest, longer length skorts and elbow sleeve tops, to modified razor-backs and lean looks -  gals on and off the green offers the styles and sizes that work well for you. our team at gals will work with you one on one to help you make great choices in the perfect looks and fashions for every you!

                                    we believe gals really do rule! let’s face it - women wear so many hats during the day! we need stylish fashions that can transition from the office to the golf course, from the golf course to the clubhouse, or from running errands or the soccer field, and then out to dinner – a woman’s typical marathon day!

                                    at gals on and off the green you can find all of these lifestyle looks. gals on and off the green truly is all about lifestyle with style™! learn more about gals.
