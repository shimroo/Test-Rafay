pregnant?for support, call or text 559.905.1000facing an unplanned pregnancy is hard. no one should have to go through it alone. if you have questions about parenting, adoption, or other options, we are here to give you the support you need. our services for expectant parents are always confidential and completely free.

about adoptionif you are just starting out in your adoption journey, congratulations! please take a look around our site, as many questions will be answered here. for many people, it is helpful to attend information meetings. if you have questions about adoption and would like to speak with a chrysalis house staff member, please contact our office to schedule an appointment.

adoptive parentschrysalis house is a full-service non-profit adoption agency. we offer fost/adopt, domestic, dual-track, and embryo adoption programs. are you looking for information regarding the adoption process? you have come to the right place! we look forward to walking with you on your adoption journey!




    more info
  















“life is a family affair!”since 1985, chrysalis house, inc. has been dedicated to offering compassionate and professional adoption assistance in fresno and surrounding counties, providing a full range of services including foster/adopt, domestic, dual-track, and embryo adoption programs. our mission is to ensure that every child has the opportunity to grow up in a loving, supportive family environment. our founder believed that “life is a family affair!” we would like to invite you to be part of our family. we provide long-term services and support to the families we have the pleasure to be a part of, including post-adoption support and counseling.
