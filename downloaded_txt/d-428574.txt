the mission of pennwriters inc. is to help writers of all levels, from the novice to the award-winning and multi-published, improve and succeed in their craft. pennwriters believes words are powerful tools and can overcome prejudice, discrimination, and harassment. we support all writers in telling their own stories in their own voices. pennwriters is committed to being a safe and welcoming community.
