bio









experience
we have over 20 years of experience playing festivals, clubs, and concert halls around the world, and have shared the stage with artists such as the amazing rhythm aces, buddy miles, pinetop perkins, terell stafford, dick oatts, ran blake, and many others. we play music drawing from a wide variety of musical traditions, from rock to classical, jazz to bluegrass. 






music
jacob has a newly-released album called "seconds", available for purchase on bandcamp, apple music, cdbaby, and for streaming on spotify. there are several hiser brothers singles available for sale on itunes and cdbaby, as well as three cd's: "drivin' the blues" (2006) presents a variety of blues originals. "you gave everything" (2004) offers originals in blues, country, rock, and modern classical styles. "a little bit of fiddlin'" (2002) holds a mixture of fiddle music including bluegrass, cajun, scotch/irish, blues, and one jacob hiser original.






current
jacob and kane currently live in the boston area. jacob teaches music at umass lowell, and both freelance around the northeast.
