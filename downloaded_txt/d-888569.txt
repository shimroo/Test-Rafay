 welcome!


first calvary missionary baptist church of sanford, north carolina is a beloved community of committed believers of the lord jesus christ. we are a caring church that loves god and loves people. it is our vision to bring the awesome life of christ to a lost and broken world. if you are ever in the raleigh-durham-fayetteville area please stop in and experience the love and care of jesus extended by our warm & loving congregation. if you are seeking a church home, i join you in prayer that the lord will show you his will, and hope that he leads you to us. if you have any questions, or if we can be of assistance to you, please feel free to call or write. to check out our live service each sunday or to hear a sermon from dr. smith please go to the thomas e. smith sr. facebook page or also on sunday morning at 11:00 call 1-978-990-5212 with an access code of 5325613# if you don't do facebook. also if you feel led to donate to this ministry or if you are a member or friend out of town and wish to become a partner and donate you may do so in the following ways:
