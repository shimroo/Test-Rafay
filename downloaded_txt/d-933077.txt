bringing the gift of yoga and halotherapy to the heart of east memphis since 2004.












































 

beyond the threshold of our magnificent doors, you will find a serene and welcoming space dedicated to discovering and exploring the benefits of hatha yoga and halotherapy (dry salt therapy). rejuvenate, replenish, restore, and invigorate your mind, body, and spirit while discovering the infinite possibilities within.we strive to provide a nurturing and nonjudgmental community honoring your individuality by teaching with integrity and compassion, and encouraging you to grow and discover your best self.














we currently offer covid safe, limited capacity in-studio yoga classes as well as online classes. 




    online streaming classes
  




    studio yoga classes
  
















we offer the mid-south’s largest salt cave. halotherapy, or dry salt therapy, is a natural and holistic treatment for adults and children that has been known to aid in detoxifying the respiratory system, healing skin conditions, promoting better breathing, sounder sleep and overall wellness. we offer salt relaxation sessions, salt yoga classes, and a range of other salt services. 




    learn more
  
























 











when the weather is right, we move classes outdoors. this spring, come practice in nature with us in our covid-safe yoga garden.
