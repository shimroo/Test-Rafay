        welcome to 3d displays  	  

boasting over 40 years of experience, we are one of the uk’s leading manufacturers of acrylic displays and point of sale equipment. we supply our products to wide range of shopfitters, retail specialists and online sellers and their feedback is always how easy it is to shop with 3d displays. we manufacture at a fast pace, meaning your bulk order can be completed and shipped to you in a matter of days so stock never runs and we offer wholesale prices throughout our ranges.
our stock is also available to order by shops, museums and even for your home, subject to our £35 minimum order. we've seen products used in card shops and corner shops all the way to children's reading corners in local libraries and as storage solutions on boats.
we've have always been committed to quality and have a reputation throughout the uk for not only manufacturing exceptional quality displays, but also being committed to excellent customer service. over 90% of our display equipment is manufactured at our uk facility and faces stringent quality control before being passed through to our customers.
 as well as our ready-made products, our team of expects can help design and manufacture a completely bespoke solution if you require something a little different. stocking materials including acrylic, pvc, petg, foamex and many more, we can find the right material to give longstand use and the experts at 3d displays will be able to work closely with you from the initial consultation, through to the delivery to ensure you’re completely satisfied. 
need to stock your business ready for roaring success, don't hesitate to start impressing your customers!
want to update your retail space with high quality acrylic displays which are designed and manufactured from a trusted uk company? browse through our full range of solutions online or contact us for a bespoke quote.
