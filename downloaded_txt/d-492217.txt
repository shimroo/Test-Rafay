welcome to cap world! we are your one-stop-shop for all your vehicle accessory needs. having been in business since 1985, you can trust that you are getting the best in products, sales, installation, repairs and pricing! 
we specialize in trucks, vans, jeeps and suvs- and our new england and maryland locations have cargo, utility, dump and equipment trailers in stock! 
we have everything from truck caps and tonneau covers, stainless steel side bars, spray-in bed liners, bike carriers, floorliners, cargo boxes, led lighting, grill guards... (the list is too long to mention them all!) we sell top name brands like a.r.e., leer, access,  tracrac, thule and weathertech.
take a moment to browse our website. if you have questions or can't find what you were looking for, contact us at any of our cap world locations in fl, ma, md and nh. we look forward to helping you!
