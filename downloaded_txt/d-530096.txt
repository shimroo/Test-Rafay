'we are wells' emergency funds support those in need 


donations to the "we are wells" fund are used to assist wells residents that find themselves in need of assistance after being affected by a pandemic, emergency or unexpected event who exceed the guidelines for general assistance or the special fuel fund.						read on...

please be aware, there will be an upcoming lane width restriction due to bridge rehabilitation on buffam bridge, located at webhannet falls park, on route 1 in wells. there will be single alternating lane traffic throughout the duration of the project.						read on...
