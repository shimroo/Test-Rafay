welcome to the city of billings website!







billings montana is a dynamic, growing community with a metropolitan area that has grown to more than 185,000 residents and serves one of america’s largest trade areas covering much of montana, wyoming, north and south dakota. our city provides the region with outstanding medical, finance, retail, and cultural amenities. add the scenic beauty of the rims, yellowstone river, beartooth mountains, bighorn canyon, and nearby yellowstone national park and you can see why billings is a great place to call home! you can find some of our most requested links in the carousel below.
