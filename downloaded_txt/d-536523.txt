home





the department of agricultural, environmental, and development economics (aede) is a national leader in applied economics with cutting-edge programs in agricultural economics and agribusiness; environmental and natural resource economics; sustainability; international and development economics; and urban, regional and community economics. turn your passion into problem-solving with one of our following degrees:

b.s. in agriculture: agribusiness and applied economics (aae)
b.s. in environment and natural resources: environment, economy, development, and sustainability (eeds)
master of applied economics (mae)
phd in agricultural, environmental, and development economics (aede)

aede agribusiness club gains industry insights through field trip over winter break
feb 10, 2025
[columbus] with a goal to gain some real-world exposure of unique agriculture opportunities in a different region of the country, ohio state’s agribusiness club took a field trip to louisiana and mississippi over their 2024 winter break. 


phd student malik earns anthony grano scholarship award from agricultural & applied economics organization
jan 27, 2025
[columbus] khyati malik, a phd candidate in the department of agricultural, environmental, and development economics at ohio state, was awarded the anthony grano fellowship award for 2025. the award, presented by the aaea trust committee and the aaea executive board, is aimed to support her interest in agricultural policy and the policy formation process. 


providing economic expertise

dr. amy ando, professor and chair of the agricultural, environmental, and development economics, continues to represent the department on a national level, providing economic expertise on key topics.
