tentative ruling



the tentative rulings are available online or by calling (209) 533-6633 no later than 3:00 p.m. the court day before the hearing.












justice links



links to other california court websites as well as additional county, local and state resources.












local forms

                                    can't pay your traffic ticket?
                              


if you cannot pay the full amount of a fine for an infraction offense (including most traffic tickets), you may ask the court to reduce the amount you owe based on your ability to pay using our new online tool.

court clerk assistance hours
court clerks are available to assist at the clerk counter or by phone: (209) 533-5555
monday - friday 8:00 a.m. - 3:00 p.m.
if you call before 8:00 am or after 3:00 pm no one will answer.
