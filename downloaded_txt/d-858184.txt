rogers lake aerator 


			posted
							on january 3, 2025


the aerator installed at rogers lake is now in operation for the winter months. aerators create thin ice and open water in the area where they are located; please obey the posted warning signs and stay a safe distance away from the aerator location.						read on...







winter parking restrictions 


			posted
							on december 18, 2024


parking is prohibited on any public street from 2–6 a.m. nov. 1 to march 31. parking restrictions are also in place on all public streets after any snow event resulting in two inches of snow or more, until the street is plowed from curb to curb.						read on...
