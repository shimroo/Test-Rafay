stage rigging 



tiffin scenic studios specializes in rigging for auditoriums, theatres, studios and educational facilities. we make draperies, track systems, motorized stage rigging, pipe and wire grids, light bridges and a variety of other staging solutions.

athletic rigging 



athletic & performance rigging manufactures versatile masking and divider net systems for arenas, stadiums, field houses, gymnasiums, halls and multi-purpose venues. we also provide draperies, tracks, motor controls, trussing, chain motors and other athletic solutions.
