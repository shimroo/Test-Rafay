what if you can change the world for someone? you can, and when you do the world changes for you.



please be respectful when sharing shoes with those less fortunate. drop-off locations accept no more than 3-5 pairs at a time. please do not donate used slippers. we accept all types, styles, and sizes of new, and clean gently worn shoes.if you hosted a shoe drive, please make arrangements with our office to discuss delivery options.




    contact us
  
















in many parts of the world families live on less than $2 a day and cannot afford something as simple as a pair of shoes. millions of people live in poverty in villages with no running water, no electricity, in unsanitary conditions, often walking miles each day to get clean water.
