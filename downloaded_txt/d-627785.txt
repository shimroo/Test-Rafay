about us



the t-34 association was formed in july 1975 so that individuals purchasing then military surplus t-34a's  had an organization which would provide support for these aircraft.  the idea was to share operating and maintenance information to all members.  as the popularity of the t-34 mentor grew over the years so did the t-34 association.  today there are over 600 members in the association and many are not aircraft owners.
