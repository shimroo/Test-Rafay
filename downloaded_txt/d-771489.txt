guided hikesall guided hikes are free to the public and are published on this page and our social media. be sure to wear hiking boots and bring water, insect repellant, and sun protection.  see our calendar for details. a list of self-guided hikes can be found here.volunteer restoration restoration parties are held on sundays, dates are published on this page and our social media. see our calendar for details. if you plan on volunteering with a group please call 858-863-7393 to organize the event. see you on the trail!historic adobe ranch tour guided tours of san diego’s second oldest standing residence, rancho santa maria de los peñasquitos occur every sat. at 11 am or every sun. at 1 pm call county parks office at 858-484-7504 for weekday options.  museum is available 7 days a week. the ranch house is located on canyonside drive off black mountain road near i-15 between mira mesa and rancho peñasquitos.  https://www.sdparks.org/
