home : about us
 
established in 2001, cheerexpo produces exciting competitve cheerleading events that are renowned for being well organized, with on-time scheduling, high production value and bunches of fun! our flagship event, the cheerexpo national championship, hosted in partnership with cheer nova scotia, is the largest event in the atlantic provinces. the event has been hosted in the beautiful city of halifax, nova scotia, canada since 2004...over 20 years! always on the cutting edge, this highly competitive championship event is a qualifier for some of the most prestigious and sought-after events on the planet.
