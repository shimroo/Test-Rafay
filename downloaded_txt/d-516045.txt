surteco north america















surteco north america:  with an extensive portfolio now including films, laminates, and coated fabrics from omnova - a division of surteco, we offer a holistic approach and a world full of inspiration to our customers with a wide range of options from one single source.
with our many years of experience, technical know how and design expertise, surteco provides more than a product; we provide a comprehensive solution. a leading, full-service provider of state of the art designs, surfaces and edges.

	introducing


	
design















where does it all begin? the surteco design center is a "think tank" where visions become reality. here, the designers work daily on new ideas, trends, encourage and inspire one another.
in this innovative space, designs are developed from wood, stones, metals or anything else we get our hands on. an in-house carpenter processes the raw material to bring out natural characteristics and add life.  then it is ready for the next step, with a state of the art scanner, the materials are easily transformed into digital information. it is now that our graphic artists truly bring the design to life.
the design center is a perfect space for customer interaction. we encourage them to play an active roll in collaborationg and developing new designs.
possibilities are endless, visit the design center and let us show you how!
