design shoji was founded by husband-and-wife team brian consterdine and wren okasaki. we create custom solutions to meet our client’s unique needs, with applications from residential to commercial spaces. premium materials, special construction techniques, and heavy-duty hidden hardware are all used to create custom shoji that are as beautiful as they are durable.
