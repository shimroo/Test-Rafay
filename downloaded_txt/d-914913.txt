2024 dsani buddy walk – september 14th, 2024welcome to the down syndrome association of northeast indiana (dsani)dsani represents people with down syndrome and their families in the eleven counties surrounding fort wayne, indiana. the mission of the down syndrome association of northeast indiana is to enhance the lives of people living with down syndrome, advocate on their behalf, provide information and support to families and professionals, and promote acceptance and inclusion of people with down syndrome.

buddy walkthis year we raised over $134,000!
