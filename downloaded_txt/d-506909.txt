uno

                                                        registrar
                                        








the office of the university registrar assists students in registering for classes, ordering transcripts, and offers many other support services. it is also our duty to safeguard the accuracy and confidentiality of uno’s academic records. we provide the uno community with comprehensive technologies and services for enrollment and academic records management.
