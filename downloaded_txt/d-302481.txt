welcome to capoeira brasil los angeles!  please tour our site and don't forget to look us up on facebook and instagram.  training the art of capoeira requires no experience, only the desire to challenge your body and mind; it is a continuous process of self-discovery within a creative and supportive community that's committed to cultural awareness, health, fitness and fun.  come take this journey with us.  the reward is an experience of a lifetime.   get fit, get strong, get culture!
