founded by brothers hal and malcolm white in 1984, hal & mal's has become a cornerstone of jackson's vibrant live music and dining scene. always known for its delicious lunches, fantastic music, and lively atmosphere, this iconic venue at 200 commerce street has thrived under the new ownership of mary sanders and damien cavicchi. hal & mal's is a bustling hub of entertainment, constantly offering something exciting to do in jackson. whether you're in the mood for live music, stand-up comedy, or mouthwatering food, hal & mal's is the place to be in central mississippi.experience the history and charm that makes hal & mal's the ultimate destination for a great time in jackson.
