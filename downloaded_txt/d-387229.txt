the boy who loved batman



the boy who loved batman, based on the memoir of the same name, tells the must-be-seen-to-be-believed story of michael uslan, a blue-collar kid from new jersey whose perseverance and open heart helped him become a pivotal force behind batman’s groundbreaking resurgence in american cinema as the dark knight. in this inspiring and heartfelt comedy, michael learns how to fight for his dream and, along the way, finds love, purpose, and the hero in himself.
