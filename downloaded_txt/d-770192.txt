the essential french brasserie
with a rich history, marseille has stood as broadway and hell’s kitchen's quintessential french brasserie for several decades. renowned as the beating heart of the neighborhood, marseille is the ideal destination for locals, theater enthusiasts, performers and tourists alike.
learn more













our food
marseille serves provençal-inspired dishes, crafted by chef xavier monge. we celebrate marseille's history as a port city, with a menu that features french, italian, greek, and north african influences. from classics like bouillabaisse and our perfect steak frites, to savory tagines, gourmet burgers, succulent short ribs, fresh pastas, and delightful hors d'oeuvres, our menu offers something for everyone.
