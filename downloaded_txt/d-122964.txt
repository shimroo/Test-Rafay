keep up with danville
 






  
 
 















applications open for the danville citizens police academy 


the danville police department is accepting applications for the citizens police academy, an in-depth program designed to provide residents aged 18+ with an inside look at law enforcement operations. interested individuals should apply by april 1 at 5 p.m						read on...







town talks with the mayor debuts new format 


town talks with the mayor is back for 2025 with a new format. mayor reneé morgan hosts this year’s series, which will be presented in a monthly short video format. the series begins with a discussion about wildfire preparedness.						read on...







find something special for someone special in danville 


planning the perfect valentine’s day for someone special is easy in danville. the town is launching “i ? danville: something special for someone special” feb. 1, highlighting unique gifts, foodie destinations, and memory-making activities through feb. 16						read on...







spring 2025 activity guide now available 


our spring 2025 activity guide is now available to view online! this quarterly activity guide focuses on programs, classes and events for the entire community. registration for danville residents begins february 11 and open registration begins february 18						read on...







town seeks residents for open commission and committee positions 


the town invites residents to bring their skills and perspectives to public service by applying for vacancies on various commissions and committees. these opportunities are a vital way to shape danville’s future and contribute to the community's character						read on...







auggie answers: town of danville launches ai chatbot 


leveraging the power of artificial intelligence to provide quality customer service, the town has launched auggie answers, a customer service chatbot. the community is invited to help test the chatbot during the initial launch period.						read on...







winter 2025 live locally newsletter now available 


our winter 2025 live locally newsletter is now available to view online! our quarterly newsletter focuses on programs, projects and events, and features vibrant photos, informative articles and graphics.						read on...







innovate to elevate art exhibition to open with public reception 


danville announces innovate to elevate, a new art exhibition celebrating the creativity of students from the san ramon valley unified school district. this showcase will open at the village theatre art gallery january 30 and will run through march 28.						read on...







sandbag station ready to assist in flood prevention 


to help protect homes & businesses during storms, the town sets up a sandbag station to prevent flooding. the sandbag station is located at the town service center, 1000 sherburne hills rd, & provides sand, bags, and a shovel in a do-it-yourself setting						read on...







town of danville continues grant program for local businesses 


the danville town council has approved a grant program that will allow danville-based brick and mortar businesses and local organizations to be reimbursed for up to $5,000 for collaborative events and programs that promote patronage of local businesses.						read on...







danville police chief shields to retire; town appoints new chief 


danville police chief allan shields will retire december 31. under its contract with the contra costa office of the sheriff for police services, the town selected sheriff’s lieutenant thomas rossberg to succeed chief shields.						read on...
