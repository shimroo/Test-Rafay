43rd buffalo bill art show & sale totals more than $1.45 million in sales



at the close of the 43rd annual buffalo bill art show & sale (bbas), bidders raised their paddles for more than $1.45 million in artwork between the weekend’s live auction, quick draw, and silent auction events.according to auctioneer troy black, who has officiated the sale for decades, this was the highest total in bbas history and the first time that the live auction alone has surpassed $1 million in sales.click here to read the full results press release.

rendezvous royale is an annual celebration of the arts held each september in beautiful cody, wyoming. rendezvous royale includes events organized by three prestigious organizations: the buffalo bill art show & sale, by western hands, and the buffalo bill center of the west.patrons of the arts are sure to enjoy this week-long melding of art, design, and dance. the rendezvous royale schedule offers western art sales, the patrons ball, lectures, design classes, painting workshops, and more!
