apply and receive aid


virginia tech uses the fafsa and our general application in scholarship central to determine eligibility for many scholarships including general scholarships from our office, grants, work study, and federal loans. the 2024-2025 fafsa and the 2024-2025 general scholarship application are now open.
















information about your financial aid offer


view your financial aid offer here once your package is available. incoming students offered admission will receive their financial aid offer with their offer of admission. returing hokies - we will notify you of your financial aid for next year in early june.





















vt scholarship central


the 2025-2026 general application for scholarship central is open. 
scholarship central user guide

















money matters informational sessions


money matters registration is open. this session is for students and their families who have accepted their offer of admission to virginia tech for fall 2024.
















hokiebird chatbot


try the hokiebird chatbot! - hokiebird can answer your questions and help you find information you need on the website. go ahead, give hokebird a try!
















hokie wellness


hokies can check out hokie wellness financial services for everything you always wanted to know about personal finance. schedule a one-on-one appoinment with a financial coach or attend a financial wellness workshop.
