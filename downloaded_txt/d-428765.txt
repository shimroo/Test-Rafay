live and let live.


in today's age people are fragile. like crystal. what has society done to create such a weak and insecure populace. so many in the masses feel their existence threatened by a mere difference of opinion. concern abounds that about a people that live and act differently. it is not just an american phenomena but america does seem to have taken a leadership role in fragility. not sure how this plays out, but we like to think someone is working on it at the home office. however can we be expected to produce great leadership when our general populace seems to be unable to agree that shoe string color does not matter?
