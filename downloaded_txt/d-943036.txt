saturday, october 30, 2010








fryderyk chopin -- demasting, 2010





the tall ship fryderyk chopin has been demasted in force 9 gale winds on her voyage to the caribbean.
all hands are safe, and she is being towed back to port in england.

for more information, go to:
   www.oldsaltblog.com/.../fryderyk-chopin-polish-training-vessel-loses-mast-in-force-9-gale/
if you would like further updates, write to me at:

                   tallships2010@yahoo.com


godspeed, chopin and capt. baranowski

friday, june 11, 2010








boston visit of juan sebastian de elcano, 370' tall ships from spain





o


the 4-masted, 370' top-sail schooner juan sebastian will visit boston, june 13th-21st, 2010.to the right, her entrance to boston in 2008.below, her appearance in valparaiso, chile in april 2010 as part of the vela sud america regatta.



built in 1927, with a design from famed yacht designer nicholson, jsde is one of the most elegant tall ships in the world.









the juan sebastian de elcano, a  4-masted top-sail  schooner of the spanish navy will be calling  on the port of boston and berthed at the world trade center, one seaport  lane,  boston, ma 02210 from june 13 to june 21, 2010. there will be  public boarding and viewing of the ship on wednesday june 16 from  3:30-4:30 pm , friday from 11:00 am – 1:00 pm, 3:30 pm – 6:00 pm and  7:00 pm – 9:00 pm, saturday june 20th from 11:00 am to 1:00 pm and 3:30  pm – 6:00 pm and on sunday june 20th from 11:00 am – 1:00 pm with final  visiting hours on sunday from 3:30 pm – 6:00 p.m.  naturally times are  subject to change.
 juan sebastian de elcano in "parade of sail" opsail, new york 2000.

oswego festival of sail, june 25, 26, and 27th 2010. oswego, ny





oswego, new york will host the oswego festival of sail on june 25th, 26th and 27th.
3 tall ships will be present:  pride of baltimore ii, the privateer lynx and the tallship unicorn.

            privateer lynx                                                               tallship lynx

saturday, june 05, 2010








privateer lynx--extensive schedule for great lakes 2010








  the privateer lynx will be making a number of stops on its tour of the great lakes during the summer of 2010, for which there are places and berths on board.  go to the link provided in this posting, and you will be able to see what is available.





privateer lynx will be making sails from:
   from       halifax to oswego, new york   from       oswego to sandusky, ohio   from       sandusky, oh to cleveland, ohio   from       cleveland to bay city, mi   from       bay city to toledo, ohio   from       toledo to port colbourne, ont   from       port colbourne to harbor springs, mi   from       harbor springs to green bay, wi   from       green bay to port washington, wi   from        port washington   to chicago   from       chicago  to traverse city, mi   from       traverse city to erie, pa   from        erie, pa to montreal 

for more detailed information go to:            http://www.privateerlynx.com/calendar.html
