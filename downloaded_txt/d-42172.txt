snow plowing begins 6 a.m. saturday; please remove vehicles from streets 


with more snow in the forecast for saturday and sunday, our public works team is preparing to plow all city streets (not county roads). please help your snow crew by getting your vehicles off the street by 6 a.m. saturday when our team begins plowing.						read on...







livonia water service line update 


the city of livonia is committed to helping residents maintain quality drinking water. livonia’s water source does not contain lead. if you are a livonia residential water customer, please visit checkyourpipeslivonia.org.						read on...
