welcome to the lough lannagh holiday village

self catering castlebar 
time to experience lough lannagh for yourself! your search for self-catering castlebar and b&b accommodation castlebar or a holiday destination in mayo is over. whether you’re looking to go on a family holiday, somewhere to stay for the night or you need to book your group in for a meeting or break, talk to us at lough lannagh holiday village castlebar – we’re sure we can help you.
so what do you need? we have facilities for bed and breakfast, self-catering holidays, conferences, meetings, and groups of all sizes.
 
self catering mayo
for excellent self-catering accommodation in mayo, why not consider our holiday cottages. you’ll enjoy homely, great value holiday accommodation and there’s so much to do here and in castlebar town – just a walk away.
find out more
for bookings/availability please email info@loughlannagh.ie – online booking coming soon

 
touring caravan & motorhome bay
lough lannagh village is perfect for touring caravan & motorhome holidays. with 20 hardstanding bays, touring caravan and motorhome holidaymakers are more than welcome at lough lannagh. in a beautiful, scenic and family oriented environment, we’ll give you a warm welcome.
book online
(open 28th & 29th march 2025 – for munster vs connacht)

venue for events & meetings
for group meetings and events in castlebar, county mayo, you won’t go wrong with lough lannagh as a conference venue. we can hold up to 120 residential visitors in our b&b and holiday cottages mayo, and we are also suitable for many smaller groups.
