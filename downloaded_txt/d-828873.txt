welcome to hyde's rv & boatshyde's rv & boats is a third generation marine dealership, in business since 1949, almost 74 years ! hyde's specializes in high quality compact pontoon boats by apex marine makers of qwest and gillgetter pontoons, along with hotwood's pontoons. we also feature top quality yamaha outboard motors. hyde's has a combination of products and top quality service that can't be beat ! we are also your destination if you want electric and or solar powered boat. hyde's are experts at electric powered boats because we have sold more than anyone else in the area. we offer minn-kota e-drives, torqeedo and elco electric options.
all of our boats can be powered by world famous yamaha outboard motors. hyde's is a full line yamaha outboard, generator & pump sales and service facility. hyde's boats is also proud to have the only yamaha master technician in the inland northeast.
parts, parts, and more parts, makes hyde's a key source for rv or marine parts and accessories in the capital district. hyde's has the largest stock of draw-tite & reese hitches in upstate new york.
hyde's is an active member of the eastern new york marine trades association.
