our services















our tree spade services provide customers with options to relocate plant material on site, as well as install large trees. our crew is trained in machine and hand-digging and able to transplant material of almost any size.

 we sell over 250 varieties of deciduous, shade and evergreen trees as well as a handful of shrubs. with an inventory of approximately 80,000 trees, we are able to fill a growing number of orders at a significant value for quality.
