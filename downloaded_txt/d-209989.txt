latest updateswhat’s happening in cedar falls
 






  
 
 















charting tomorrow: cedar falls launches strategic plan 


the city of cedar falls is excited to look to the future with charting tomorrow, a community-driven strategic plan initiative which invites citizens to share their aspirations and goals for the years ahead.						read on...







cedar falls ragbrai committee announces plans for overnight stop 


ragbrai is coming to cedar falls on thursday, july 24. the des moines register's 52nd annual ride is making its fifth stop in cedar falls.						read on...







join the cedar falls housing commission 


make a difference! the cedar falls housing commission is accepting applications for members.						read on...







public information meeting: 2025 cdbg sidewalk infill project 


the city will host a public information meeting regarding the newly planned sidewalk along colorado road and dallas drive on january 23.						read on...







american discovery trail society names cedar falls a trail town 


the city of cedar falls has recently been recognized by the american discovery trail as a trail town. cedar falls is the first trail town in iowa and the second in the united states.						read on...







citizen public safety academy 


cedar falls public safety is inviting the community to learn more about the vital role of police and fire/ems operations during the citizen public safety academy.						read on...







your city, your parks 


the city of cedar falls is inviting the community to share their vision for the future with your city, your parks! find out how you can get involved here.						read on...







overfull refuse carts 


to help provide efficient service to our community, the city of cedar falls will start charging for overloaded garbage cans and return collections for refuse carts not out at their scheduled time of service beginning january 1, 2025.						read on...







christmas tree disposal information 


christmas trees will be picked up on mondays by appointment only from january 6 through february 10, 2025. call public works at (319) 273-8629 to make an appointment.						read on...







city of cedar falls popular annual financial report 


to help the community better understand the city's finances, the city of cedar falls is proud to present the popular annual financial report (pafr) for the 2024 fiscal year.						read on...







holiday refuse collection reminder 


view the holiday refuse collection schedule here.						read on...






218 north open 


the iowa dot has completed construction on the 218-north ramp, and it has been reopened to traffic.						read on...







cedar falls 'name that plow' winners 


view the full list of winning names for the 2024 'name that plow' contest!						read on...







cedar falls holiday hours and refuse collection 


view holiday facility hours and refuse collection schedule.						read on...







cedar falls tourism activities and economic development services application 


the city of cedar falls is requesting proposals from non-profit or governmental agencies that contribute to increasing tourism activities or economic development services.						read on...







island park construction 


the cedar falls public works department will be conducting construction updates at island park (218 center street) starting the week of october 21, 2024.						read on...






traffic pattern change - ia 27/58 and ridgeway 


starting friday, october 18, 2024, the iowa dot's contractor, pci, will start the next phase of reconstruction work at the ia 27/58 and ridgeway avenue intersection.						read on...







cedar falls public safety welcomes new k9 


cedar falls public safety is welcoming a new member to the team. zara, an 18-month-old female dutch shepard, has been sworn in as the department’s k9.						read on...







city of cedar falls maintains aaa tax rating 


moody’s ratings has announced that the city of cedar falls has maintained its general obligation unlimited tax (goult) aaa rating. this is the top rating attainable by moody’s. cedar falls has held this achievement since 2020.						read on...







your city, your parks 


share your vision for future park improvements, new parks, and open spaces by helping to create the new cedar falls parks master plan. learn more and sign up for alerts here!						read on...







paid parking to resume in college hill area 


a reminder that paid parking in the college hill municipal lots will resume starting august 1 to may 31.						read on...







mothers against drunk driving honors cedar falls officer 


mothers against drunk driving (madd) has honored cedar falls officer kyle janikowski for his outstanding enforcement efforts, naming him a madd hero award winner.						read on...







follow the hello cedar falls podcast 


the city podcast airs on spotify with new episodes premiering the first and third tuesday of the month.						read on...







cedar falls earns bicycle friendly community bronze status 


the league of american bicyclists honored the efforts of cedar falls to build better places for people to bike with the renewal of the city’s bronze-level bicycle friendly community (bfc) status.						read on...







city of cedar falls receives raise grant for main street reconstruction project 


the department of transportation has announced the city of cedar falls will receive a rebuilding american infrastructure with sustainability and equity (raise) grant for the main street reconstruction project in the amount of $10,000,000.						read on...







cedar falls public safety to offer monthly cpr/aed training 


cedar falls public safety will be offering an american heart association-supported cpr/aed training and curriculum class on the first sunday of every month at the public safety building located at 4600 south main street.						read on...






start of construction: west 27th street reconstruction 


beginning tuesday, may 31, 2022, the city's contractor, pci, will be starting phase i of the west 27th street reconstruction project.						read on...
