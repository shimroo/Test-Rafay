ukri metascience ai early career fellowships



the uk metascience unit will fund a cohort of early career fellows to build our understanding of how the growing adoption of ai is changing the research landscape, including what epistemic, metascientific, ethical and socioeconomic questions this raises, and how governments, industry, and funding organisations should respond.




opportunity status: 
open


funders: 

economic and social research council (esrc), arts and humanities research council (ahrc), biotechnology and biological sciences research council (bbsrc), engineering and physical sciences research council (epsrc), medical research council (mrc), natural environment research council (nerc), science and technology facilities council (stfc) 


co-funders: 
department for science, innovation and technology (dsit), alfred p. sloan foundation


funding type: 
fellowship


total fund: 
£4,000,000


maximum award: 
£260,000


publication date: 
13 february 2025


opening date: 

13 february 2025 9:00am uk time 


closing date: 

10 april 2025 4:00pm uk time 








era-net neuron interdisciplinary approaches to the neuroscience of pain



apply for funding as part of a transnational consortium of three to five partners to improve knowledge of the neuroscience of pain using interdisciplinary approaches. this is an era-net neuron joint transnational call. the medical research council (mrc) are one of 19 organisations offering funding through the initiative.




opportunity status: 
open


funders: 

medical research council (mrc) 


co-funders: 
18 other international funders through the european research area networks network of european funding for neuroscience research (era-net neuron)


funding type: 
grant


total fund: 
£2,000,000


maximum award: 
£312,500


publication date: 
6 february 2025


opening date: 

7 january 2025 


closing date: 

6 march 2025 1:00pm uk time
