located on the picturesque st. margaret’s bay, our club is a welcoming hub for sailors and power-boaters of all levels. we offer top-notch sailing programs, competitive racing, and a safe harbour with modern facilities for visiting boaters. whether you’re looking to refine your sailing skills, explore the coastline, or simply enjoy the camaraderie of a passionate community, st. margaret sailing club has something for everyone.visiting sailors can enjoy convenient guest berths, access to our clubhouse, and a warm, friendly atmosphere. members benefit from exciting regattas, engaging social events, and a shared love for life on the water. join us and experience the best of sailing in st. margaret’s bay!
