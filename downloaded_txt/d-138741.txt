european antiques & decorative wholesale dealers & shops

	antiq.com is a trade portal site designed to find antiques and decorative objects from professional antique dealers, traders, wholesalers, and exporters from france, belgium, the netherlands, germany and even the usa. easily find information about european antique auctions, shippers, antique fairs and brocante markets. antiq.com is updated daily with new items (over 250,000 items from 500 dealers). antiq.com is an ideal way to prepare for your antiquing tour or to find the items you're looking for.
