welcome to brandywine trucks & equipment
brandywine trucks & equipment has the trucks, new and used equipment and trailers that you need! we are conveniently located in brandywine, maryland which is close to washington dc and richmond, virginia. the port of baltimore is also a neighbor and can be a huge help in your exporting needs.

	we have a large selection of equipment including: aerators, air compressors, excavators, dozers, pavers, forklifts, generators, cranes, skid steers, sweepers, rubber tire loaders, crawler loaders, compactors, a variety of attachments & much more!








trailers are also available at our dealership including: dump, flat, reefer, tag, drop, rolloff, van & more! ask us about a globe or witzco trailer as we are a dealer for both.

if you're looking for a truck, we've got you covered there too! dumps in quad, tri, tandem and single axle set ups, sweepers, utility/service, flat bed, waste, recovery, day cabs and sleepers, fuel/lube, flushers, boom, crane and the list continues!

stop in and see us when you get a chance. new customers are welcome and it is always great to see return buyers.
