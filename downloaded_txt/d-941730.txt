home to two championship 18-hole golf courses, created by world-renowned designer, robert trent jones, jr. over 300 acres of golf, tennis, aquatics and fitness.

		dining in the member grille 2600, formal royal palm room, picturesque patio dining or in an intimate private dining space with live chef action stations and regionally inspired menu items.

		associations, clubs, specialized private/group instruction and amenities for all ages and experience including weston hills golf academy and vintner’s club.
