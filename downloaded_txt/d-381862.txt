uw professional & continuing education
as part of uw continuum college, uw professional & continuing education expands the educational impact of the university of washington, breaking down barriers so learners of all types can access quality opportunities that fit their lives. 
we know how important continuing your education is in addressing the evolving needs of the workplace. we also believe education makes life more meaningful. that's why we're in a constant phase of research and development, experimenting with new topics and new approaches that keep the new traditional, nontraditional learner top of mind.
whether you're pursuing a higher level of education or simply forever curious, you'll discover innovative and relevant programs that fit your schedule and your budget – so you can keep learning at the university of washington.
choose from more than 80 certificate programs and specializations, nearly 70 degrees and about two dozen courses designed for you, with flexible options in the evening, on weekends and online.
