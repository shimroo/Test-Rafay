welcome to mad duck cycleryat mad duck we are proud to have served the north texas area and beyond for over twenty years.  we try to bring you the best bikes, accessories and service on the planet.  stop by our little stores and say hi, join us for a ride and enjoy a cold beverage.  we are bike nerds, and proud of it!  so if you are a bike nerd or just bike curious, we would love to meet you!!
