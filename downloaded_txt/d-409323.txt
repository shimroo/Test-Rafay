museum annual passes
our annual pass allows you free entry to the museum and its associated events. the annual pass includes all activities – perfect for keeping the children entertained in school holidays! if you buy an annual pass from our shop at the end of your visit, you can deduct what you’ve paid for your tickets that day from the price. 
annual passes also make a great gift for all occasions:

adult (16 years+) £24
child (5 years+) £12
concession (65 years+, disabled adult & military id) £20
family (2 adults & up to 4 children) £72
carer £12 (with proof of status)



events with additional charges
nighttime events, outdoor events, workshops, experience days, holiday clubs, school workshops, uniformed group evenings, private events, and special fundraising events will all have an additional charge.
