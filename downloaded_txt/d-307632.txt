 yankee custom truck & suv accessories 
 



new england's aftermarket accessory resource for your truck, van, or suv 


yankee custom has been supplying truck and sport utility accessories to its customers for over 50 years. once a small company building truck caps from a garage, yankee custom has evolved into a full-line supplier automotive accessories with the largest retail inventory in new england.






yankee custom truck & suv accessories is a century - ranch - weathertech dealership












truck accessories


from truck caps, bed covers, cargo lids and bed protection options to steps, racks, tool boxes, and snow plows, yankee custom has the experience and inventory to help you customize your truck.

van equipment


keep your business organized and running efficiently by making your van work for your specific needs. we supply van shelving, partitions, roof racks, and van mats for a variety of applications.

sport utility


whether it's everyday driving or a rugged weekend excursion, we offer a full line of suv accessories including rain and mud guards, roof racks, floor mats, seat covers, fog lights and more.

 five convenient locations to serve you 

with over 45 years in the accessory business, we have the products and expertise to get you exactly what you need, when you need it. we have the largest retail inventory in new england at five locations throughout massachusetts, new hampshire, and maine. each location has a knowledgeable sales staff, qualified technicians, and modern service facilities for installation of your product.
