about bellisima lingerie, sherborne, dorset 



bellissima supplies women with beautiful, quality underwear and swimwear, designed to make you look and feel great. launched in 1999, the shop was opened by nurse and midwife from dorset, jane hopkins. having worked with women who had undergone reconstructive breast surgery, jane wanted to inject comfort and confidence into their lives.with this simple aim in mind, jane runs bellissima from her store in sherborne, dorset, stocking over 800 stunning styles from a range of well-known quality brands. sizes start from 30” up to 44” and from an a cup to a k cup. jane and her instore team love assisting women in finding the right product and can recommend, advise and inspire you to find the right style, shape and colour for your body.if you would like to arrange a fitting, please get in touch using our enquiry form and one of the team will get back to you as soon as possible. once you’re booked in, you can come along, meet the team and try as many styles as you like, until you find one that’s perfect for you.
