our ministries















adults
we seek to provide opportunities for disciples  we serve to grow in faith, to find their place of mission, and to build community.




                        learn more
                      












children & youth
we hope to be a catalyst for young people and children of all ages become faithful disciples of jesus christ.




                        learn more
                      












baptism
baptism is a symbol of christ’s burial and resurrection. our entrance into the water during baptism identifies us with christ’s death on the cross, his burial in the tomb and his resurrection from the dead.




                        learn more
                      












care & support
our congregational care ministries exist to provide love and support to people who are struggling with life’s challenges.




                        learn more
                      












salvation
salvation is god's grace. it is the gift of freedom from our sins that jesus made possible by taking the punishment for our sins on the cross.




                        learn more
                      












music & arts
the purpose of the music and worship arts ministry is to blend music, dance and voices in a manner that honors god, connects with scripture, and adds to each worship experience.
