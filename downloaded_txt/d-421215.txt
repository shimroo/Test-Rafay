2024 popular annual financial report (pafr) now available 


the city of columbia is pleased to announce the 2024 popular annual financial report (pafr) is now available. this report provides residents and stakeholders with an easy-to-read overview of the city’s fiscal stewardship.						read on...







city of columbia acquires the columbia aquatics and recreation center 


the city of columbia is excited to announce the successful acquisition of the columbia aquatics and recreation center, formerly muletown rec, located at 1446 oaks springs drive, for $4.3 million.						read on...







city of columbia announces clean audit report for fy 2024 


the city of columbia is pleased to announce it has received a clean audit report for the fiscal year 2024. this clean audit reflects the city's ongoing commitment to fiscal responsibility and prudent stewardship of taxpayer funds.						read on...
