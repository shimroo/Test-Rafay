 theodore w. austin-sparks (1888-1971) was born in london, england. called to speak in england and in countries around the world, he left a treasury of messages filled with the wisdom, life and revelation of jesus christ. our goal is to make these messages available online, for the further establishing and strengthening of the body, that in all things christ might have supremacy!
