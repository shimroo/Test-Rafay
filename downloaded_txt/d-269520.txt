city highlightssee what's new in laramie.
 






  
 













the city of laramie has created the city manager’s budget advisory committee to foster collaboration between city staff and community members who sit on the committee and offer input on budgeting transparency, financial documents, and outreach. budget advisory committee
 




access all of the laramie city council agendas and minutes and find out what they're working on!city council agendas and minutes
 




the laramie police department, in partnership with volunteers of america, the albany county sheriff’s office, and the university of wyoming police, is launching the albany care team program to address albany county’s mental health crisis through collaboration between law enforcement and mental health experts.
