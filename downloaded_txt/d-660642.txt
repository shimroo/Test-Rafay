ammbusher mowers & brush cutters


for over 25 years, ammbusher has set the standard for power, productivity, and reliability in brush cutting. acquired by premier attachments in 2017, the ammbusher name continues to represent top-tier performance, safety, and durability.premier’s ammbusher brush cutters and mowers are built to handle the toughest jobs, featuring proven designs, premium components, and american-made quality. our lineup includes 2 mowers, 8 heavy-duty brush cutters, and 2 high-flow industrial-grade models, all engineered to fit any skid steer loader brand. when you need a brush cutter that delivers unmatched power and longevity, trust ammbusher by premier attachments.
