miller's marine
ocala, florida
the owners/management of the new miller's marine, is continuing the longtime legacy of millers boating center, and they have been working closely together to ensure a smooth transition. miller's marine would love to continue serving the wonderful boating community that has been long-time customers, as well as adding new long-time customers. we have a large array of boats, outboard motors, trolling motors, and boat trailers. we also have inventory of used boats and consignment boats. miller's marine store is equipped with lots of accessories and replacement parts. our parts department is knowledgeable and can help you with the needs of yamaha or mercury engine parts. millers marine service and rigging departments are available for all you needs(from installing new items or repairing existing items). our staff is ready to assist in every way possible or to lead you in the direction of another staff's advice.
