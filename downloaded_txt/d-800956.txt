our student population
as of fall 2024, csusb has a student population of 17,900 (ftes 15,169), 88% from san bernardino and riverside counties, 3% from other countries, 9% from other california counties, and <1% from out-of-state; 2,138 (12%) students take at least 1 unit at the palm desert campus. of the 17,900 students, 10,948 (61%) are female and 6,952 (39%) are male.  eighty-four percent of students are seeking an undergraduate degree, 82% attend on a full-time basis, 78% are first-generation college students (parents without a bachelor's degree); 70% are hispanic, 10% are white, 3% are non-resident foreign students, 5% are african american, 6% are asian, 4% unknown, 3% are two or more races, and <1% are native american/alaskan native or native hawaiian/pacific islander. eighteen percent of our students are freshmen, 11% are sophomores, 25% are juniors, 30% are seniors, 3% are post-baccalaureate students, 13% are masters, and <1% are doctoral students. fifty-four percent of our undergraduates are low-income students (pell grant recipients). average age for our undergraduate students is 23 and 31 for our graduate/post-baccalaureate students. our current student success measures are as follows: first-to-second year retention rate of first-time full-time freshmen is 80%, four-year graduation rate is 24% and six-year graduation rate is 55%.
