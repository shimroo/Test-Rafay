edgmont country club was  established
          in 1963 as  a private  non-equity 18 hole golf club located on the northern boarder of 
          delaware county and chester county, pa.
the management of the club decided not to open for the 2016 season. the last day of golf operations was march 27, 2016 and the properity was sold march 31, 2016
we would like to thank our members, employess and other supporters for 52 great years.
