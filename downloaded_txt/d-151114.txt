holiday inn san diego - bayside

dining



start your day with breakfast as early as 6:00am in our bright and cozy point loma cafe, also serving lunch daily.  be sure to also join us on our patio under the market lights and palm trees for happy hour or dinner basking in san diego's idyllic temps.























in-room dining hours: 6:00 am - 9:30 pm

                on-site restaurant with outdoor patio dining
            














on-site restaurant with outdoor patio dining

nourish your body with a hot cooked-to-order breakfast at our point loma cafe as early as 6:00am daily.  we offer all of the breakfast classics plus some regional specialties, like our chilaquiles. lunch is available daily until 1pm.  you will want to end your day at the bayside bar with weekday happy hour specials and patio dining available.






































point loma cafe


 cuisine: american

 location: next to pool area
                        

restaurant phone: 1-619-2260255
                        
open daily for breakfast 6am to 11am mon thru fri and until noon sat and sun. lunch is served from noon to 1pm. dinner is served from 4pm to 10pm.  

energize yourself before heading out with one of our hearty breakfast selections, like our american skillet or local breakfast burrito. enjoy the convenience of a dinner onsite served in our bayside bar or outside patio offering your classic american fare like burgers, pastas, flatbreads, tacos, and fresh fish.
