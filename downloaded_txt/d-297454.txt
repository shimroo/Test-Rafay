find an icpa chiropractor





find an icpa chiropractor



acquire the skills to better care for the families in your community. 





webster certification

13 hours, 1 course
written and hands-on evaluation
in-person and yearly livestream






perinatal certification

80 hours, 10 courses, 10 online exams
includes webster certification
in-person and online options






pediatric certification

200 hours, 20 courses, 20 online exams, capstone exam
includes webster certification and 7 perinatal courses
60 hours of in-person instruction required






diplomate program

400 hours total
200 hours obtaining webster+perinatal+pediatric certifications
200 hours of in-office clinical applications and research
