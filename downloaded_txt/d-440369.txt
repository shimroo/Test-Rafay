olympian shannon miller expecting second child







seven time olympic medalist shannon miller is expecting her second child.  the news of the new addition was a shock for the 35-year-old former gymnastics star who had undergone a battle with ovarian cancer in 2011. 

"my world stopped," she said of the cancer diagnosis.  "instead of calling my parents to tell them they had another grandchild on the way, i was calling them to tell them may have cancer.  our baby plans were put on hold indefinitely."

she ultimate had her left ovary removed and went through a nine week round of chemotherapy. 

this will be the second child with husband john falconetti.  the baby will join big brother rocco, 3.

william and kate: a summer baby for the royal couple!







 it will be a summer baby for the duke and duchess of cambridge!

the royal couple have finally confirmed some of the details of their baby's upcoming arrival and put some rumors to rest.  there are no twins on the way and the baby is expected to arrive in july.

"their royal highnesses the duke and duchess of cambridge are delighted to confirm they are expecting a baby in july," reads a statement released by the palace.  "the duchess' condition continues to improve since her stay in the hospital last month."  

the couple are keeping some of the details to themselves and will not be revealing the gender of the baby before their big arrival.
