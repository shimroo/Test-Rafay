public programs



we bring the jazz experience directly to communities through programs presented in venues throughout chicago. 99% of our programs are free and open to the public. our programs keep chicago jazz musicians working by creating a wide array of performance opportunities, build bigger audiences for chicago jazz by presenting concerts and festivals that are free and available for all and keep our community thriving.




    learn more
  
















education programs



engagement in the arts encourages a lifelong application of creative thinking. the jazz institute of chicago jazz links education programs empower an ever-growing community of teachers and students through collaboration, experiential learning, professional development and direct connections to chicago’s rich jazz legacy.




    learn more
  



















join our jazz family today!



as a member of the jazz institute of chicago, you will receive special discounts and exclusive offers for concerts and events only available to members. you’ll have distinct opportunities to meet and engage with jazz musicians, enjoy tailored jazz news and programming, and mingle with jazz audiences who are a part of our thriving community that loves and supports jazz.
