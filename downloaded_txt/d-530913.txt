a community of faith in actionthe christian church (disciples of christ) in the southwest  is made up of believers and leaders dedicated to serving our region with purpose, love, and faith. each year, our community supports initiatives that uplift, empower, and connect individuals and churches across texas, new mexico, and beyond. together, we strive to build a community that reflects justice, compassion, and unity.you have a place here. whether through active participation in ministry, learning or volunteering your time, there is a meaningful role for you in our collective journey to proclaim the gospel.
