 king urban life center strives to be a resource hub for the east side of buffalo by uniting the community and connecting children and families to free and impactful programming. 


























parentchild+ programparentchild+ supports caregivers in preparing their children for academic success, strengthening families and communities through intensive, consistent long-term home visiting.















 steam after-school programthe king urban life center s.t.e.a.m. team after-school program supports a variety of innovative early-childhood and school-age programs through various partnerships throughout the city of buffalo.
