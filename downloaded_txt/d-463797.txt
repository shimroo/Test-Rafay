database spotlight 




 




ibisworld provides industry reports for over 700 u.s. industries, and coverage of selected industries on a global scale. each report includes key statistics, market characteristics, leading competitors, risk ratings, industry performance, and more. 











 



research assistance 



our customized research guides are a great place to start your research. need more help? contact our librarians if you would like to ask a question or set up a research consultation. 







 



explore our collections 



from top-shelf business research databases to an amazing selection of academic and trade journals, researchers from the sha community have an extensive collection of material to choose from.
