about reedley irrigation and supply

for over 100 years, reedley irrigation has been the valley’s first choice for quality flood, micro and drip irrigation installation and supplies. at ris, a large experienced staff of experts serve the changing needs of our surrounding agriculture and landscape environments. inside our retail showroom, stocked with only the highest quality name brand irrigation products, we provide advice and design consultations for your ag irrigation projects.

ris proudly serving fresno, tulare, kings, madera and kern counties.
