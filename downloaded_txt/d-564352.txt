about prestige concrete
whether you’re looking for a commercial site concrete contractor for your downtown high rise or just looking to restore or resurface your existing driveway, patio or pool deck, prestige concrete has the right solution for you!
as a san diego based concrete contractor with an unparalleled reputation for quality concrete and masonry construction and restoration, prestige concrete has been serving all of southern california since 1992. although recognized for our expertise in decorative concrete flatwork and hardscape, prestige concrete is a full service concrete and masonry contracting, restoration and repair company offering a wide variety of services for commercial, residential, public and private customers. with a passion for exceeding the expectations of our customers, we have the ability to meet the needs of our clients on just about any level. having developed a reputation for quality, integrity and innovation, we have become known as the “contractor of choice” within the concrete construction industry.
some of our services include:

concrete stamping
concrete staining
concrete coating and overlays
exposed aggregates
concrete etching
concrete cleaning and sealing
concrete pavers
