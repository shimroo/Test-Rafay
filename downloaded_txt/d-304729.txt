based near wilmington, delaware, creations gallery features unique american-made, hand-crafted gifts, furnishings and accessories in wood, glass, metal and ceramics for home and office. after 26 years in brick and mortar locations, creations closed its hockessin, delaware store in june, 2018 while expanding its online presence initiated in 2016.welcome to our online catalogenjoy an easy checkout with fast and free shipping on all orders!just use the menu on the left to get started(if you are using a mobile device, look for the ≡ menu icon in the extreme top corner)



your gift purchase from our e-catalog can be sent directly to you or to a recipient.most items even come complete with artists’ cards and gift wrapping at no extra charge. we offer free shipping on all items within 2-5 business days to any address within the continental united states via usps, unless otherwise noted.  certain large or fragile items are available for local delivery only.
