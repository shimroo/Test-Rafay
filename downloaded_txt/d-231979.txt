why choose missouri furniture?
missouri furniture was founded in 1977, with its first showroom located in camdenton. since opening our doors, every team member continues to be dedicated to excellence in creating a memorable and exciting experience while
we help you transform a house, into your home. you can shop furniture, mattresses, appliances, and hot tubs at
any one of our eight missouri locations!
jefferson city / camdenton / kirksville / st james / sedalia / ozark / columbia / washington
we offer the largest selection of leathers, fabrics & finishes to customize your new pieces and we select only the top-rated consumer brands, including brands from right here in missouri. missouri furniture will deliver a stress-free experience to every new and existing customer. visit our showrooms today and let us help you love where you live.
