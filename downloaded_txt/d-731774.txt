gjba 13u/14u team(s) [rmjbl league play only] - spring 2025
registration for spring 2025 13u and 14u baseball.  gjba teams compete within the rmjbl.  teams are coached by volunteer parents.if required due to registration numbers, gjba head coaches will hold baseball tryouts on sunday 01/26/2025 at golden high school (time tbd) and head coaches will conduct roster cuts soon thereafter in order to form teams.  full payment is collected at time of registration.  players not added to a team roster will be provided with a full refund and references for neighboring youth baseball associations.  practices are generally twice a week during the preseason, beginning with indoor batting practices in february.  games for 14u are played from march to may, and games for 13u are played from april to june.  games are played on monday-friday evenings and nights on fields located across jefferson county and the denver metro area.  games are typically not played on saturdays within rmjbl.fee includes: league fees, division game schedule (weather/cancellation dependent), game hat, game jersey, umpire fees, field use fees (games/practices), preseason batting cage fees, use & maintenance of team-issued baseball equipment.  fee excludes: baseball glove (required), baseball pants (required), baseball socks (recommended), protective cup (required for boys), padded chest protector undershirt (optional & recommended), plastic cleats (optional & recommended), personal bat (optional), personal helmet (optional).

gjba 13u/14u team(s) [tournament play only] - spring 2025
registration for spring/summer 2025 13u and 14u baseball.  the program is for players ages 13 & 14 years old.  teams will be formed by invite and/or tryout only.  full payment is collected at time of registration.  the total cost of this program is tbd at this time, but it will depend on input from the head coach and will likely be approximately $2,000.  the $550 is a down-payment only. gjba offers this program as a travel-ball alternative for players/families that don't necessarily desire a club-baseball level of commitment (time & cost).  teams are organized and coached by volunteer parents.  coaches will propose a tournament schedule of approximately 5 to 8 weekends between the march and early july timeframe.  most gjba players are highly involved in other sports/activities in addition to baseball at this age. for more information, please contact gjba (admin@gjba.net).
