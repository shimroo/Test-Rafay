  about usrace tech suspension has over 36 years of creating hi-performance suspension solutions for motorcycles, atvs, utvs, and snowmobiles. rt specializes in revalving stock suspension components using proven gold valve kits that can be user or dealer installed. rt can rebuild most stock and aftermarket suspension components. for many models rt manufactures complete replacement custom g3-s shocks and g6 forks that utilize gold valve technology.race tech engines offer state-of-the-art, precision engine services and parts to all engine builders. rt specializes in optimizing flow and velocity by utilizing flow bench, dyno, and track testing. our staff has over 65 years of championship-winning engine development experience formally educated by the world’s most renowned engineers.support includes a global network of race tech centers to do quality installations. rt also provides tools, online instructions, tech support, seminars and race tech’s motorcycle suspension bible. gold valvesgold valves® and gold valve cartridge emulators® are race tech's custom valving kits. they are designed to surpass the limitations of stock damping designs.  hi-performance springsrt hi-performance fork and shock springs have the highest stiffness-to-mass ratio in the industry. this means they are lightweight for their particular rate.  toolswe offer a wide range of tools that make getting the job done easier.  g3-s custom shockswhether you ride a modern bike or vintage, we can build g3-s custom shocks that will out-perform your highest expectations. guaranteed!  enginesbuilding a reliable, "world class" engine requires a combination of state-of-the-art equipment and experienced, knowledgable technicians.  tnk fork tubestnk fork tubes are oem quality replacements. not only are they the highest quality, they are generally less expensive than the original equipment.  g6 custom forkswe are now offering race tech custom fork legs for street and road race motorcycles. all custom forks are built to order.  g2-r cartridge kitsg2-r 25mm cartridge kits replace the stock damper with a high-performance cartridge.  each set is custom built to order for the application and valved to the rider's specs, preference and ability.  vintagevintage (pre 2000) dirt and street wish we knew then what we know now. but then again, it's now!
