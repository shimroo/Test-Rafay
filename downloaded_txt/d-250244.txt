welcome to plaster fun time! we are the drop-in art center for kids of all ages. our family-friendly stores provide the perfect environment for kids to express themselves and have fun!  our friendly staff is there to help kids paint their pieces any way they want. with convenient locations across massachusetts, our 100% “made in the usa” products are a boston-area original. hosts of award-winning birthday parties for over 25 years! plaster fun time is the destination for family fun… at family friendly prices.
