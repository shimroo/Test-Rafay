since 1958
a washington dc landmark
since ben and virginia ali opened the doors in 1958, ben’s chili bowl has been a pillar of the washington, dc community. over the decades, our steadfast commitment to our guests remains the same. we thank our regulars and those visiting from across the country and worldwide for their support over the years.
