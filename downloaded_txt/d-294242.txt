our mission is to operate mls now on a backbone of advanced technology and to always put our customers first. we foster cooperation, collaboration, and communication to deliver timely, accurate, efficient, and meaningful data as well as products and services. we empower our customers to achieve high levels of productivity and professionalism.






hands on in addition to hands-on education in our state-of-the-art lab, we also offer office visits done by one of our account executives.   


online documentation we offer online tip sheets, tutorial videos, manuals, and an online forms library – all in the mls now system.   


support & help desk we have the largest number of mls staff in ohio ready to serve you. our help desk is available via phone or online and manned by long term local staff.
