tips for ordering



the line at pat’s king of steaks moves quickly, so it’s best to be prepared - know what you want, and how to order it by the time you get to the front of the line!



step 1specify if you want your steak: with (wit) or without (wit-out) onions



step 2specify plain, cheez whiz, provolone, american, or cooper sharp



step 3have your money ready. cash is king. (credit card surcharge will apply)



step 4order just your steak at the first window. order fries and drinks at the second window.



step 5eat up while it’s hot!
