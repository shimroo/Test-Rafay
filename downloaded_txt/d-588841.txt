bringing joy to the childhood cancer journey.
















welcome to rapahope!every child deserves a chance to play, have fun, go on adventures, and be themselves. rapahope is a nonprofit that proudly exists to provide these opportunities to families walking through childhood cancer. discover how we give kids the gift of joy!




    learn more
  














have a child (any age) who has ever been diagnosed with cancer? with a quick online process, create an account with us so you don't miss out on any opportunity with our rapahope family!
