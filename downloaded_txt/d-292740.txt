inside southwest detroit



















inside southwest detroit’s programming and places are made possible through the sharing of time, talents, treasures by our partner artists, neighbors, and sponsors and the generous support of these foundations.








design+build: our home at 8869 avis8869 avis is a portal for neighbors to the wider world of art and culture. it transformed an empty building and lots into a plaza and market space where residents connect with each other socially and artistically. participatory design instructed decision-making for development and design. our celebrated arts-led, place-based development was four years from idea to completion. see the design+build process here
