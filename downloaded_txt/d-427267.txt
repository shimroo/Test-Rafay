the way of the future...






lighten your load with instant, current and searchable access to board policies!







the internet revolutionized how we access and use information - and policy manuals are no exception.

osbas online policy service gives school districts, esds, public charter schools and community colleges the instant, current and searchable access they need to board policies.
save your staff countless hours in copying and distributing new or revised policies.
give your public the information they need, at their fingertips.









the benefits 



instantly available to staff, public



easy to use



searchable



saves time, resources



customized



links to oregon revised statutes



links to oregon administrative rules









with one centrally located board policy manual thats
                                            updated electronically, all administrators, staff and public with internet access have instant access to current
    										board policies.

hyperlinks to orss and oars
osbas quarterly policy update subscription
osbas policy maintenance service (osba will maintain the boards policy manual to include updating any policies changed by board action.)
instantly available to your staff and community.
