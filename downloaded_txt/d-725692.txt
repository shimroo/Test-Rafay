little lefties provides quality clothing and accessories for women and children with positive, empowering messages regarding themselves, the environment, and the future. our thought-provoking images include environmental, equality, political, and rainbow themes to help invoke social change!
we are proud to use only american made, sweatshop free garments. all of our garments are screenprinted in an eco-friendly process, using completely non-toxic, water based inks.
