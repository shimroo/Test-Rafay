welcome to  azalea middle






here at azalea middle school we strive to create a quality learning environment. a critical component to your child's success is a strong partnership between the school and the student’s home. please take advantage and use this resource so that you and your student will be successful this academic year. on our website you will find important information about our processes and procedures, our calendar, digital resources your child can access from home, and direct contact information for staff members who can answer questions or address concerns. let's make this school year the best ever!
