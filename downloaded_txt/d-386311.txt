active transportation advisory commission vacancy 


are you interested in volunteering to advise the city of batavia government in developing policies to support bicycling, walking, and public transportation? apply to become an active transportation advisory commissioner.						read on...







warming center offered at city hall 


during extremely cold weather, batavia opens a warming center for people needing a break from the cold. visitors should check in with the receptionist at the batavia police department, 100 n. island ave.						read on...







city council meets monday 


the batavia city council agenda for monday, feb. 17 includes a black history month proclamation, a donation presentation to the batavia post prom committee, and an update on the wastewater treatment plant rehabilitation project.						read on...







did you miss the newsletter? 


the city of batavia's bi-weekly newsletter details news and information about all things batavia -- delivered to your inbox. learn how to subscribe, and read the latest issue.						read on...







spring parkway tree program discount offered 


batavia residents can enhance their parkways with a tree through the city’s annual spring parkway tree program. thanks to funding from the non-reusable bag fee, residents receive a discount, paying only $45–$65 per tree, depending on the tree species.						read on...







it's coyote sighting season: what to do if you see one 


coyotes are a natural part of local wildlife, and they are more active and visible in  winter and early spring. most are harmless. learn what to do if you see one, and tips for keeping them away.						read on...







avoid frozen pipes 


as temps drop, your risk of a frozen pipe increases. the city of batavia water department has several tips to help you avoid frozen pipes in your home or business.						read on...







batavia is salt smart certified 


the city of batavia streets department staff's winter street maintenance and de-icing efforts have earned salt smart certification from the conservation foundation. learn more about the city's strategy to keep the roads safe while using salt wisely.			additional info...







electric department starts spring vegetation management 


the city of batavia electric department has started the spring and summer vegetation management program in order help keep power lines clear from inadvertent contact that could cause outages.						read on...
