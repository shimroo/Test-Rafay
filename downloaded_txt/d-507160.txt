overviewwelcome to the department of molecular & cellular biosciences (mcb) at the university of cincinnati academic health center. please take a few moments to browse through the site and learn about our department, our research, and our graduate programs.the department is part of the university of cincinnati’s college of medicine and is currently comprised of 15 primary faculty members with interests in the areas of structural biology and biochemistry, microbiology and immunology, and cellular signaling, regenerative biology and neuroscience.
 the department of molecular & cellular biosciences is also the home of the university of cincinnati's graduate program in molecular genetics, biochemistry, and microbiology.we are hiring!the department of molecular and cellular biosciences is searching for assistant /associate professors to join our current faculty. applicants should have research interests that align with our three research pillars including structural biology and biochemistry, microbiology and immunology, and cellular signaling, regenerative biology and neuroscience.  special consideration will be given to applicants with an interest in structural biology and cryo electron microscopy. see the center for advanced structural biology and the cryo-em core for more details. applications will be reviewed beginning in fall 2024 for an anticipated start date in summer 2025. click here for more information and to find the job posting.
