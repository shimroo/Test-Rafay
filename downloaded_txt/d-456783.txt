news flash








housing workshop feedback 

through an online public comment period, the city council would like to hear feedback from the community about the information that was discussed during the february 11 housing workshop.				read on...




letter to tenants of buildings 15, 17, 18, and 35 of the pepperell mill 

the city of biddeford has outlined the rights guaranteed to tenants of buildings 15, 17, 18, and 35 of the pepperell mill as apartment units in these buildings are converted into condominiums.				read on...




january 21, 2025 municipal referendum election return of votes cast 

the unofficial results of the january 21, 2025 municipal referendum election are now available.				read on...




statement regarding biddeford police sergeant jacob wolterbeek 

it is with profound sadness that we share the news of the passing of biddeford police sergeant jacob wolterbeek in a tragic accident on his way home at the end of his shift. sergeant wolterbeek was an 18 year veteran of the biddeford police department.				read on...




geraldine matherne named city of biddeford’s next finance director 

geraldine matherne will serve as the city of biddeford’s next finance director. she will assume the responsibilities of the position on january 5, 2025.				read on...




sewer billing information 

the city of biddeford is moving forward on the next phase of its conversion to a new sewer billing system to be provided by maine water. the conversion process is expected to be completed by april 1, 2025.		additional info...




rotary park skate park closed until further notice 

due to safety concerns, the skate park located at rotary park is closed until further notice. the recreation department will be conducting an assessment of the skate park and making recommendations to the city council on the future of this facility.				read on...




city council approves new approach for serving unhoused residents 

the city council approved an interim plan to provide indoor services for unhoused residents. the plan prioritizes finding long-term solutions that tackle the root causes of homelessness while mitigating the immediate risks associated with street living.				read on...
