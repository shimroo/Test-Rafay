                                life is better with plants
                            


vintage hill farm offers over 1700 varieties of annuals, tropicals, houseplants, perennials, roses, trees, & shrubs. many varieties are tried and tested here in our gardens before being proclaimed worthy for the gardens of our challenging midwestern climate. 
our plants take off because they have not been chemically treated and are free from the stresses of mass-merchant and shipping. we thrive on introducing underutilized, heat tolerant, tough plants to give your gardens and containers the "wow" factor you deserve.
