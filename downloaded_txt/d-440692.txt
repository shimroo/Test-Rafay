about us
future fortunes, inc. was created to provide an incentive program that offers more money at a barrel race without additional entry fees.  starting in 2004 with $38,000 in bonus money at 2 events, future fortunes has expanded its program to over $1.4 million cash payout throughout the united states.  no additional entry fee for bonus money. one-time enrollment fee.  $500,000 super championship tour: $50,000 each at 10 events. $375,000 regional championships: $25,000 each at 15 events.  $66,000 in fast 5 team races at 12 team races $5,500 per event. three ff slot races: juvenile sires’ slot and fortunes’ round – $25,000 to the champions of juvenile sires slot and fortunes round.  derby dash 2d slot race $150,000 purse-$25,000 1d champion & $20,000 2d champion.
since 2004, future fortunes has paid out over $14 million in cash!
