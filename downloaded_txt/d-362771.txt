radiology









forging the future of radiology




 the uab department of radiology is an integral part of one of the nation's largest health systems. nationally known for our compassionate care, cutting-edge research, and comprehensive training programs, we're dedicated to delivering the most advanced care.

















our programs


preparing the next generation of physicians and scientists
 

we're dedicated to nurturing and guiding the future generation of physicians and physician-scientists, recognizing that our trainees are the cornerstone of our nation's healthcare system.

our people


home to leading experts and distinguished scholars
