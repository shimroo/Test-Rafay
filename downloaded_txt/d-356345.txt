a legacy of business leadership



for more than 60 years, the wilmington- clinton county chamber of commerce has worked to promote, enhance, and serve our business community.each of our programs support businesses that improve our area economy and enhance the quality of life for all our residents.at the heart of our work is the active participation and involvement of our members. by joining together, our businesses and organizations have a positive effect on our community and the business environment in our region.please explore our site and learn about how the chamber can help your business build a legacy of success and commitment to our community.














our mission



promotebusiness through collaboration and communication.



enhanceeconomic and community development.



serveas a catalyst for improving the overall quality of life in the community and region.
