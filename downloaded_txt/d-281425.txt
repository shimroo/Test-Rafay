31st annual shadow day program

join us on thursday, april 17, 2025, as the ywca hosts underserved high school students from the east bay to shadow a uc berkeley student and explore their future goals in a supportive environment. mentees have the opportunity to attend college classes, tour campus, and get a glimpse of undergraduate life through panels, activities, and events.

the ywca berkeley/oakland is dedicated to eliminating racism, empowering women*, developing leaders, and promoting peace, justice, freedom, and dignity for all.














what we do ↘we provide empowerment programming, advocacy, and opportunities for marginalized individuals and families, especially women, girls and female-identifying people of color. all while working to dismantle structural barriers to equity and social justice.






















racial justice

our one imperative: eliminating racism wherever it exists and by any means necessary.




learn more











reproductive rights

we advocate to ensure all women have autonomy over their bodies and access to critical health care. 




learn more











food justice and accessibility

our priority is to make healthy foods accessible to the community through our food pantry, ensuring everyone has the opportunity to nourish themselves.




learn more











workforce development

we provide career coaching, financial literacy, tech skills and more to women and girls.




learn more


















our programs ↘ywca berkeley/oakland provides direct services, empowerment programming, and advocacy to support marginalized individuals and families, especially women and girls of color, while working to challenge structural barriers to equity.































career coaching

our weekly small group workshops and 1:1 sessions are led by a certified career counselor.learn more







tech lab workforce development

programming instruction + workforce training for communities underrepresented in the tech industry.learn more







teen financial literacy

we teach personal finance, digital skills, and job skills to young women to increase independence.learn more







digital literacy

helping elders with critical digital skills and access to an increasingly digital world.learn more







english in action

volunteers meet with partners one hour a week to practice english conversation on a one-to-one basis.learn more







mentoring program

middle school girls learn about healthy relationships, boundaries, health, and their own rights.learn more
