about portland fire fighters' association, iaff local 43
the portland fire fighters' association, iaff local 43, has approximately seven hundred firefighters and emts working for portland fire and rescue and for the port of portland fire department at the portland international airport. our executive board consists of 14 members and is led by our union president, isaac mclennan. the portland fire fighters' association was one of the first locals to join the international association of fire fighters (iaff) when it was founded on february 28th, 1918.
mission statement: establish and protect a livable standard of wages, benefits, and safety for fire fighters and the community. to provide a powerful and collective voice for workers that is inclusive, democratic, and enhances unity among workers.

                fill the boot
              
×



                may 20, 2019
              

                since 1954, iaff members have shown an unwavering commitment to defeating muscular dystrophy and related life-threatening diseases. funds are raised primarily through mdasignature fill the boot campaign, in which member fire fighters gather on streets across the country collecting money in their boots from neighbors and passersby. for 60 years strong, our nations heroes have raised more than $558 million to save and improve lives of families fighting muscle disease. dollars collected in boots throughout the decades are translating into game-changing research progress today.
