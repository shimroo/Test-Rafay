seattle pride 2022





hey you! come ride with us. join our vibrant community of gay motorcycle enthusiasts! established in 1969, we've been exploring the pacific northwest together for decades. our weekend runs take you to the most picturesque locations in the region. whether motorcycle you ride that can maintain highway speeds, you're welcome to join us for a weekend of adventure and fun. rain or shine, we always have a great time on our runs. to join us, simply reach out to one of our members for an invitation. find a brmc event that you want to be a part of by visiting our events pagewhen the weather cools down, we keep the fun going with our monthly meetings. open to both members and invited guests, these meetings are a great way to connect with like-minded individuals within our gay motorcycle community.throughout the year, we also host meet and greets and various day runs or social events, giving you plenty of opportunities to ride, explore, and socialize with our community. join us for an unforgettable journey, and be a part of our rich history as one of the longest running gay motorcycle clubs!
