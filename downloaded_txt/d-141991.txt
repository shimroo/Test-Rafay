welcome to poasthe key to costa rica

with nearly forty years in the costa rican car rental industry, poas rent a car has grown from three humble cars at the hotel ambassador to its current, up-to-date and well-maintained 200 vehicle fleet, which now services two international airports. 
we thrive because of our daily commitment to our customers by providing local expertise, superior customer attention, and transparent fees to make for the best possible rental experience.
our san jose office is just 7 minutes from juan santamaria international airport (sjo), close to major highways for easy access to popular vacation destinations, and a short 30 minutes from either poas volcano or downtown san jose.
daniel oduber international airport (lir) is serviced by our fine staff in liberia, located a mere 10 minutes away on highway 21, and on the way to papagayo, coco beach, hermosa beach, the four season hotel and other favored guanacaste locations.
we invite you to discover costa rica with poas!
