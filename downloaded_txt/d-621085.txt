rec it at the ucr student rec center today!
all ucr students are automatically members of the student recreation center (src), a 155,000 square foot state-of-the-art facility for exercise, sports activities, events and general recreational use.
take a virtual tour  watch the src video tour


 







































attention: everyone (students, src members, non-src members) interested in registering for an activity class must sign a liability waiver.
all classes require appropriate workout clothing. jeans are not allowed.
