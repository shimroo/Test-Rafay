accent bedroom furniture
bedroom styles for the way you live
accent furniture is an importer of affordable solid wood and wood veneer bedroom sets, offering superior quality, value, price and service to all of our customers.
whether you are a furniture dealer or a consumer, a current customer or a potential new customer, we hope that this site will provide you with the information you are looking for. by exploring this website you will get to know more about what constitutes a piece of quality furniture, and about the quality of accent bedroom furniture in particular. whatever your needs or taste, we offer a variety of collections styled to suit most any situation.
as many of you may already know, the accent furniture you have been familiar with for years has recently undergone a remarkable change... eliminating our domestic production and transitioning to overseas manufacturing. this conversion means we can better accommodate your ongoing needs and requests. this highly significant change provides numerous benefits to you as an accent furniture customer:

greater variety in the master bedroom category, including oak, pine and cherry
more diversified styles: contemporary, traditional, mission, country, nostalgic, casual and more to come
a wider range of finishes and improved construction
higher quality
stronger customer value
reduced lead times

be sure to spend some time with our furniture expert: on our questions and answers page you will find the answers to many of the most important questions you may have when looking to purchase a piece of quality furniture.
please check back with us from time to time, as we add new collections on a regular basis.
