welcome to the los angeles convention center 

 


 


plan located in the heart of vibrant downtown la, the los angeles convention center is a leading venue for conventions, trade shows, and exhibitions. the center is committed to delivering excellent service and exceeding expectations for clients and guests at every event.
 plan





 


visit the los angeles convention center, the preeminent venue of conventions, exhibitions, meetings, and special events, offers guests a welcoming environment in which to conduct business. the spacious venue offers multiple amenities to welcome you, such as a business center, charging stations, on-site dining, quiet working areas, and more.
