town of normal news










town of normal shares latest community survey results 


town of normal residents again gave top marks to the community’s overall quality of life in the recent town of normal community satisfaction survey.						read on...







normal improves community with $4 million in grants in 2024 


in 2024, the town of normal secured $4 million in grants to improve the community with infrastructure improvements, public safety initiatives and quality of life enhancements.						read on...







town of normal inspector to retire, reece announces organizational realignment 


greg troemel, town inspector and director of inspections & zoning, announced his dec. 31 retirement following four decades of storied service.						read on...







crowd-sourced time-lapse photo stations installed throughout community 


the town of normal urges the community to help document changing landscapes of various sites throughout the town via 10 photo stations recently installed by the parks & recreation team.						read on...







reece announces staffing changes 


town of normal city manager pamela reece announced several staffing changes in key leadership positions at the town of normal, effective immediately.						read on...







normal responds to unacceptable mass gathering 


town of normal mayor chris koos says finding a solution is the no. 1 priority to the results of a mass gathering on sept. 28 that resulted in the loss of life in normal.						read on...







normal to receive $50,000 nea grant for underpass mural 


the town announced approval by the national endowment for the arts (nea) for a grants for arts projects award of $50,000 to support the design and installation of a mural by professional artist(s) inside the tunnel of the uptown connector underpass.						read on...







koos names sonja reece 2024 citizen of the year 


mayor chris koos announced sonja reece as the town of normal’s 2024 citizen of the year at the town’s annual appreciation reception.						read on...







public art working group convenes in normal 


the town of normal convened a new working group to inform and expand the town’s public art activities.						read on...







town of normal awarded grant to boost tree canopy 


the morton arboretum’s chicago region trees initiative (crti) awarded the town of normal a $25,000 subgrant to improve the community tree canopy.						read on







vendor application open for 2025 event season 


he 2025 event season vendor application is now open and can be accessed at the town of normal’s cultural arts website.						read on...







normal to rename fairview aquatic center for kent karraker 


the town of normal announced plans to change the name of fairview aquatic center to kent m. karraker aquatic center at fairview park in honor of former mayor kent karraker.						read on...







normal launches community satisfaction survey 


the town of normal introduced a community survey designed to better understand resident reactions to services provided by the town and a variety of quality-of-life issues.						read on...
