due to an increase in fraudulent orders, we are enforcing new security measures for online orders. bow cycle reserves the right the verify the purchasers.  if ordering on-line for shipping, you may be asked to provide proof of identity. when picking up an online order, you will be asked to present a government-issued id and the credit card you used to purchase the items. if you need someone else to pick up your item, please leave their name and phone number in the comment section of your order before checkout - they will also need to present a government-issued id at pick up. please allow 24 hours processing time of all online orders for in-store pickup. you will receive an email letting you know your order is ready for pickup. please wait for this email before coming to pick up your order.  questions about your online order? contact etailing@bowcycle.com or call (403) 288-5422 ext 236.
