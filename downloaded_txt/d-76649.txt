i want to sell





we are a full service auction company. from luxury goods to small collectibles, entire estates to clean outs, automobiles and more, we sell your items through our online auction service for top prices to buyers in ohio, the u.s. and internationally.

 




we thoroughly sort, organize, photograph and catalog your items and estate. no job is too big or too small. we transform crammed estates into empty, clean homes ready for an open house and resale. we often find value that would otherwise have been overlooked.  we typically have the flexibility to work with your schedules, coordinating with realtors and closing agents.

 




once an auction is posted, customers can start pre-bidding immediately.  the auction will be available to customers for 1-2 weeks.  on the auction night, we actually go live and sequence through the entire auction in order online.  although you don’t smell the burnt popcorn or hear the multiple auctioneer’s chants on a friday night, we feel this process gets as close to the feel of a real auction.  with the actual live online bidding we control and monitor the process working in real-time to get the highest value for your items.
