welcome to
shipwreck harley davidson®
shipwreck harley-davidson® is located in wilmington, just minutes from the beautiful beaches of north carolina. wilmington is a historically rich city full of southern hospitality and charm. the all new shipwreck harley-davidson opened its doors in november 2024 and has become a vibrant member of the community. we proudly serve wilmington, jacksonville, topsail, surf city, leland, fayetteville, oak island, carolina beach, myrtle grove, wrightsboro, kings grant, bayshore, myrtle beach, burgaw, and surrounding areas.

our store features harley-davidson® motorclothes® merchandise, parts and accessories, service and, of course, harley-davidson® new and pre-owned motorcycles. shop all of our motorcycles and trikes for sale and our used harley-davidson® motorcycles for sale!
