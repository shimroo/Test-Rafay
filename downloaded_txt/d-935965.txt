creating a safe future for vulnerable homeless childrenthe crisis of street children in tanzania is massive. but together, we can solve it. amani is dedicated to taking children off the streets, providing them with loving care, safe shelter, and the chance to go to school. your support makes a difference. join our mission and help us change lives!






















our approach

what sets amani apart is our holistic approach: we rescue children, provide them with education, ensure their mental and physical health is stable, and help them return to a safe situation with their family. we actively support both the children and their families.

the impact of your support 














thanks to your generous support, we can make a meaningful difference in the lives of vulnerable children. here are the results that showcase the impact of our work together:
























250+ child rescues

every year, amani rescues over 250 children from dangerous streets and severe abuse, providing them with immediate access to safety, medical care, and education.
















20+ years of proven experience

amani has been working to support street children build a better future for more than 20 years. our holistic approach is based on experience and demonstrated impact on the lives of the children we support.
















family reintegration & continued support

more than 250 former street children are reintegrated with families and communities every year. we continue to support and guide these children to build a brighter future.
















350+ continued education 

amani provides on-going educational support to over 350 children a year, including regular school follow-ups, to ensure that children continue their education even after returning to their families.
