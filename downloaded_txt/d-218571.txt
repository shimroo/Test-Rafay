welcome to cdmanufacturing.ca

we provide comprehensive cd, dvd and blu-ray duplication and replication services, as well as mailers, jackets, inserts printing, product packaging and commercial printing services.
call now for a quote on your project: 416-493-8900,  email sales@cdmanufacturing.ca, or chat online by clicking on the chat icon below.
besides cd, dvd and blu-ray replication and duplication, we also supply usb, blu-ray, commercial brochure and book printing, packaging solutions, cd-r, dvd-r and bd-r printing, digital printing, assembly and fulfillment services.
                    
we offer custom-printed cd-r, dvd-r and bd-r discs at wholesale prices. all recordable media supplied are certified to be of the most current duplication speed and quality.  
cd and dvd replication
our cd & dvd replication service uses the most technologically advanced equipment available to reliably produce high quality dvds from tested master discs. advanced equipment means fewer failures, more ecologically friendly manufacturing processes, faster turnaround and better pricing for cd, dvd  and blu-ray duplication, dvd replication and packaging. we offer cd and cd-rom replication of any quantity starting from 250 discs. smaller runs are handled by our digital printing process while larger runs are accomplished with offset printing techniques.
we also offer an extensive variety of printed inserts, digipaks, boxes, mailers, posters and cases. our award winning graphic design team has the prepress skills to make sure your dvds and cds look grand. 
great customer serviceour priority is to make your vision a reality. our dedicated customer service co-ordinators are assigned to each client account to monitor projects, co-ordinate with the internal departments and make sure that client's expectations and deadlines are met. we will work with you to create a superior product. 
thank you for exploring our website. we look forward to serving your cd, dvd and blu-ray manufacturing needs.
