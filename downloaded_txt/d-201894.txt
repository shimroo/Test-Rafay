new book— in english and in french:















interactive techniques for learning (english) or/ ou techniques interactives pour l’apprentissage (français)a new book with 60 activities, explanations and instructions for how and why we do what we do, and even a bit of psychology and learning science. the book is over 500 pages long in english, and 600+ pages in french… meaning it is chock full of goodies. some activities will be familar with an updated feel, others are brand-spanking new! ebook is immediately available. print will be available in july.
