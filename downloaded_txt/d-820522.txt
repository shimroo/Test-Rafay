research


the school of biological sciences has international leading research expertise in a wide range of biological disciplines from genomics to macroecology, and across all levels of biological organisation from molecules and cells to entire ecosystems. this places us in a unique position compared with many biology departments across the world. we have the capability to provide diverse perspectives and novel solutions to the largescale challenges we face.


 

teaching and assessment


our degree programmes are flexible, and all students complete field courses or laboratory workshops and independent research projects chosen from a wide range of options.
