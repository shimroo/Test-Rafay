culture




located in the greater new haven area we thrive on our connections to the arts, humanities, and cross-cultural education, both on campus and off. our cultural arts center is a wonderful venue for concerts, performances and lectures, and also houses the gallery, featuring artwork from residents and professionals.  to find out more please call (203) 974-0001.

community




for over 40 years one of our core values is developing collaborative relationships in greater new haven and connecticut. residents and team members are involved with many state and local organizations, companies, and events, which makes everyone’s lives richer.

community




one of our core values is developing collaborative relationships in hamden and the greater new haven area. residents and staff are involved with many local organizations, companies, and events, which makes all our lives richer.

confidence




because whitney center is a life care community, you’ll have access to any level of health care you might need, with virtually no increase in costs. from everyday health services to assisted living, rehabilitation, memory care, and skilled nursing, you can have confidence in a future that promises quality care that’s centered on you.  you and your family won’t need to worry about the what ifs in life and the increasing costs of higher levels of care.  for more information call (203) 974-0001.
