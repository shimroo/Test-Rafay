     about priority chevrolet greenbrier
priority chevrolet greenbrier is your hometown chevy dealer in chesapeake, servicing hampton, yorktown, and williamsburg. for over two decades we've offered unrivaled sales and service on all chevy vehicles, with lifetime guarantees on the vehicles we sell.
give us a call at 757-573-2043 or stop into our showroom to see the priority difference for yourself today!


priorities for life
priority chevrolet greenbrier customers tend to be customers for life, because of our priorities for life guarantees. when you buy a car, truck, or suv here, you're part of a program that will save you money on ownership cars for the life of the vehicle. that means lifetime engine guarantee, state inspections, and parts and service guarantees.
it's not just about selling you a car at priority chevrolet greenbrier. it's about making sure you love that car and can keep driving it for many years to come. when you buy from us, you're not just getting a great new car or truck, you're getting one that will last while saving you on ownership costs.
treating you like one of our own
it's not just about saving you ownership cost money or getting you into the cabin of a new chevy equinox, tahoe, traverse, colorado or 1500-series pickup truck at our dealership. it's about offering you a one-of-a-kind and welcoming experience that you won't find anywhere else. it's about treating you with warmth and respect like you're one of our own.
when you walk into our dealership, we have only one goal, and that's to provide the very best customer service. everything we do is geared towards providing you a stress- and hassle-free experience every step of the way.


supporting our community
our priorities aren't just geared toward car sales, either. we live and work in the same communities as you, so we also have the priorities for change program, supporting many local charities and organizations that have a positive effect on our region, such as the boys & girls club, ability center of virginia, children's hospital of the king's daughters, roc solid foundation, seton youth shelters, stop, inc., our military kids, and many others.
we also host the priority toyota charity bowl to raise money for the joy fund and other children's charities every year. keep your eyes open for our presence at local charity events, and be sure to say hello when you see us!
outstanding sales and service at priority chevrolet greenbrier
of course, all of this comes back to our outstanding sales and service. we are, after all, a car dealership. we are proud to offer an unmatched selection of new and pre-owned cars, trucks, and suvs, and to encourage you to take all the time you need to find the one you want. we'll get you the best deals and incentives for any credit situation, and we'll keep your car running with genuine, oem factory chevy parts for years to come with expert chevy technicians working from a state-of-the-art repair facility. when you're ready to do it all again, we'll still be here with the same priorities, geared towards a perfect experience.
