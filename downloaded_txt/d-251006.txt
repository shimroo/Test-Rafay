fire agencies and red cross hold apartment landlord training on april 24 

tualatin valley fire & rescue, hillsboro fire & rescue, clackamas fire district #1 and red cross cascades region team up for in-person training for property managers and staff about how to prevent fires at multifamily housing complexes. registration open.				read on...
