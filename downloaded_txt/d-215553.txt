mkm is a collaborative structural engineering firm delivering innovative, safe, and reliable design solutions.














practice



over 42 years of experience, founded in the inspiring landscapes of northern california.



with sustainable construction practices and licensing in seventeen states, we can work wherever you are.




    learn about our practice
  


























work



we design for safety and aesthetics, balancing art with specifications.



from functional and luxurious michelin-rated dining and hospitality spaces to local residential remodels, our expertise spans all project types.




    view our projects
  


























integrity



people make up the foundation of our practice - and ignite innovation.



our blend of engineers, drafters, field technicians, and administrative specialists work together to foster a workplace embedded in integrity.
