days inn jekyll island 




the “award winning” days inn & suites is centrally located on beautiful jekyll island offering the perfect setting for the leisure or business traveler.  our hotel is within walking distance to  the beach village retail / dining area & the jekyll island convention center.   the hotel features 84 newly appointed guests rooms and 40 oceanfront one-bedroom suites. the suites are complete with a galley kitchen, separate living room, queen sofa sleeper and patio or private balcony.the hotel offers a complimentary continental breakfast bar in our 2nd floor oceanfront schooner’s landing overlooking stunning sunrises over the beach.  guest amenities include two pools (one heated in winter months); hot tub; kitty pool; burn pit; volley ball & pickle ball court, and bike rentals on property; guest computers / printers; handicap beach wheelchair and direct access to the beach.   our onsite snack shack offers a great convenience for our guests with many snacks, pizza, sunscreen, ice cream, beer & wine!
