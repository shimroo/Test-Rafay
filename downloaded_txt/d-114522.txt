the auto care industrykeeps vehicles moving, and the auto care association is dedicated to helping every one of those vehicles last longer, perform more efficiently, and keep drivers safe. independence drives us: our global member companies manufacture, distribute, and sell vehicle parts and components as well as perform services, maintenance, and repairs on every class of vehicle on the road.




our impact $516 billionindustry (projected $617 billion in 2027) 4.9people employed in the industry 295 millionvehicles on the road 268,000service outlets
