lebanon pd responds to homeless encampment 


lebanon police and city officials recently responded to an unoccupied encampment with scattered trash and containers of unidentified fluids. violations of open burning, camping, and littering ordinances were documented.						read on about impending actions

fire destroys downtown lebanon’s allen building 


a fire late sunday night destroyed lebanon’s historic allen building, forcing immediate demolition due to safety concerns. crews battled the blaze into the early hours, but the beloved downtown landmark was declared a total loss. investigation is ongoing....						read on about the allen building fire
