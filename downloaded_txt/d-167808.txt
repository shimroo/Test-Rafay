county commissioners court
▲





cochran county commissioners will meet in regular session the 2nd business monday and next-to-last business day of each month. 

meetings are held at 8:30am in the commissioners courtroom on the 1st floor of the courthouse. agendas are posted on the agendas tab at least 3 days prior. our citizens are always encouraged to attend.
