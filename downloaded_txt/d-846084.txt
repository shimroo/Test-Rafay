usage of vastu shankh yantra 







north direction 



vastu shankh yantra is used in the north direction to increase money flow or financial gain. 



west direction 



a pair of vastu shankh yantra is used to block west direction of any house for retention of wealth & prosperity. 





 





east direction 



vastu shankh yantra is used in east direction to bring harmony in family and marital relationship. 



south direction 



a pair of vastu shankh yantra is used to block south direction of any house for retention of wealth & prosperity. 









 





vastu tips 









 

 



																window in the west in the bedroom							


window in the west in the bedroom may cause health issues, specially cancer, keep the windows closed and curtained. 





 

 



																relationship problems amongst husband ...							


relationship problems amongst husband and wife may arise if east is blocked of the bedroom or the house. use a sun painting in the east.  





 

 



																toilet in the east in the bedroom....							


toilet in the east in the bedroom leads to health issues, keep it closed.  





 

 



																puja room should be kept clean...							


puja room should be kept clean and tidy and with no gods photos or idols one behind the other. 





 

 



																photos and idols of gods or our ancestors...							


photos and idols of gods or our ancestors should never be placed on the back wall of the bed or the office.
