                                                            partner with komen                                                         







support susan g. komen’s mission by joining local businesses, organizations, and community partners raising critical funds in support of komen’s vision for a world without breast cancer.
give from your donor advised funds (daf)
a donor advised fund (daf) is a tax-advantaged account where you can donate money or assets, receive an immediate tax deduction and invest tax-free. the funds grow over time, allowing you to make charitable gifts. giving tuesday is dec. 3 – consider supporting people facing breast cancer through your daf or encourage others with a daf to support komen. make a gift from your daf!
go pink for the cure
when you organize a go pink for the cure event, your school community’s passion and determination can help make a real difference in our mission to end breast cancer forever. whether it’s a sports or school group, pickleball for the cure, golf or tennis, we have resources for all. let’s get started! learn more.
vipink
vipink is komen’s new ambassador fundraising program. it’s a community of bold leaders who are committed to helping create a world without breast cancer. through vipink, professional and community leaders across the u.s. unite to raise critical funds to help build a world without breast cancer through research, community, care and action.
do you know someone who would be a great vipink ambassador? or are you a great candidate? please visit the vipink nomination page to make a submission.
worship in pink
worship in pink is open to all faiths, empowering faith-based communities with knowledge and resources to take an active role in their breast health. become a volunteer ambassador and serve as a breast health advocate.
special thanks to all our community partners who hosted events during national breast cancer awareness month (nbcam)
for more information on how to get involved or to get your workplace, business, or organization involved, contact your local komen staff.
