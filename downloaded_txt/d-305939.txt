what's happening in bg
 






  
 














temporary fire station readies for operations 


in preparation for the upcoming demolition and reconstruction of buffalo grove fire station 25 on dundee road this spring, operations will be temporarily moving from this location to the former public works facility while the project is ongoing.						read on...







stay informed on 2025 construction projects 


construction season is almost here and we know how frustrating the activity can be, especially when it's right outside your front door. but it's a lot less frustrating when you know what to expect and when.						read on...







candidates filed for the 2025 consolidated election 


the 2025 consolidated election will take place april 1, 2025. three four-year trustee seats and one two-year trustee seats on the village board will be up for election.						read on...







bg launches “smart. with heart.” online store 


just in time for the holiday season, the village has launched its new online store, vbg.org/store, offering a wide array of community-branded apparel and merchandise items designed to showcase the village’s spirit of being “smart. with heart.”						read on...
