financial crime management technology
  

our financial crime management platform, verafin, is the cornerstone of our efforts to fight financial crime. over 2,500 banks and credit unions use verafin solutions to comply with regulatory mandates while also protecting the financial system against a wide spectrum of financial crime, including money laundering, human trafficking, terrorist financing, and fraud.




























  explore how verafin helps serve the financial ecosystem.
















  fraud detection




using our unique consortium data approach, verafin provides insights into fraud risk that goes beyond a single institution’s view to prevent losses and strengthen fraud prevention across all channels including deposits, checks, wires, ach and instant payments.










  anti-money laundering




verafin strengthens anti-money laundering efforts and ensures regulatory compliance for financial institutions with end-to-end aml/cft solutions, including  targeted ai-based analytics for financial crime typologies.










  high-risk customer management




verafin's intelligent risk approach automatically identifies, segments, stratifies and actively monitors customers in higher-risk categories to reduce the compliance burden of managing high-risk customers.










  sanctions screening




targeted analytics, automated watchlist updates and integrated case management on top of high-quality alerts strengthen sanctions compliance and decrease reputational risk of noncompliance in a fast-moving sanctions environment.










  information sharing




verafin provides a centralized platform for private-to-private information sharing that allows investigators at financial institutions to easily send, receive, and respond to requests for information directly in our secure environment, expediting decision making in the investigation process
