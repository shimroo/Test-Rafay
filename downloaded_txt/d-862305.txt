our services



fully insured & compliant
as a long standing member of the ara, we proudly hold a $5,000,000 bond along with a $3,000,000 coverage policy. we have been performing kentucky repossessions for over 55 years and our experience and professionalism speaks for itself.






remarketing & liquidation services
imperial has the ability to liquidate equipment. we repossess quickly, bringing you maximum dollar value for condition of goods. we also have close relationships with many car dealers who come in to bid on repossessed items. public bidding also available.






wrecker & towing services
imperial recovery is also a 24 hour towing company and wrecker service providing towing to lexington, kentucky and surrounding area. should you need a tow, we are available 24 hours a day, 7 days a week and can get you to where you need to go.
