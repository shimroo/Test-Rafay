   about us who we are
 midwest vinyl products, inc. was founded by blake hagen from his home in 1993. today, we have grown to service our customers as a wholesale supplier/fabricator of high quality vinyl and aluminum outdoor living products. midwest vinyl products, inc. is a family owned business located in west burlington, iowa and no job is too big or too small for us to handle.
