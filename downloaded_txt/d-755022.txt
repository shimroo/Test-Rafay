the fresh flavors of florida
looking out on international drive in orlando, with its modern hotels, bustling orange county convention center and popular tourist attractions, it may be difficult to remember that you are in the center of the sunshine state. that is, until you arrive at everglades restaurant at rosen centre® hotel and experience the authentic tastes of florida.
using fresh, local ingredients and incorporating florida flare into many traditional dishes, everglades restaurant outshines other restaurants on international drive. our chefs are always striving to offer the finest seafood, steaks and produce in our orlando steakhouse. dishes on the constantly changing menu include fresh florida grouper, florida rock shrimp, buffalo, and duck. visitors and locals alike enjoy daily specials created by our award-winning chefs, including caribbean crab cakes and alligator bay chowder.
inspired by the florida everglades, the subtropical wetlands in south florida, everglades restaurant supports the green efforts and conservation that has earned rosen centre® hotel a two palm green lodge distinction — among the first hotels in central florida to receive this recognition. but unlike the everglades wetlands, which are preserved to protect its natural beauty, everglades restaurant is constantly changing and evolving so that every time you dine with us, you have a truly unique experience.
be sure to visit everglades restaurant for a gourmet dining experience unmatched by other restaurants and steakhouses in central florida. our international drive restaurant is a great place for your next special event, birthday or anniversary. we offer a complimentary champagne toast, personalized menu items, dessert, and complimentary self-parking for your convenience.
