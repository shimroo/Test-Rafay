why t.r. lawing? 



    1

  experience
 for more than 60 years, we’ve provided long-term care and management of residential rental properties scattered across our 12-county region. we’ve built a reputation as specialists, surpassing our competitors in both experience and scope of services. 
  2

 expertise with the most certified property managers (cpms) in the region, t.r. lawing offers a deep local knowledge of the rental market and a track record of exceeding the expectations of both residents and investors.


    3

 reliability we’ve earned the trust of our residents and owners, and we don’t take it lightly. residents rely on us to ensure their homes are safe and well cared for, while owners count on us to manage the day-to-day operations of their investments, from marketing and applicant screenings to maintenance and rent collection.
  4

 variety from mooresville to rock hill and salisbury to shelby, we offer the largest variety of rental houses, duplexes, condos and apartments in the charlotte region. whether it’s the suburban ranch of your dreams, with a fenced yard and plenty of room to roam, or the perfect uptown condo with private balcony, we can help you find it – and everything in between.
