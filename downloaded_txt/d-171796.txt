rubber soul the tribute
rubber soul the tribute features the entire beatles history in one night! starting with the early beatles, they also feature the psychedelic era of sgt pepper/magical mystery tour and then include the “white album”, through “abbey road" and "let it be.” they also mix in solo hits of john, paul, george, and ringo, and feature all the costumes, instruments and gear from their live performances. this is a show for all ages featuring the timeless hits of the world’s most famous band, the beatles!
