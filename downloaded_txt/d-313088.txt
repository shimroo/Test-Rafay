our story













rolux randburg, previously known as loxtons was started by dennis and deanne loxton as an independent outlet in 1975, servicing garden lovers & providing a natural outlet for the locally made lawnmowers.
over the past 5 decades our store has continued with the principles instilled by dennis & deanne:

supplying only products that are built to last a lifetime;
on-site service, repairs & maintenance;
highly trained staff;
a great customer experience.
over time we have added complimentary products, like the stihl range of power equipment.  we sell lawn and garden equipment, with the emphasis on products that will give years of service:
