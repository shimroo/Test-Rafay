the university of illinois band program is among the most prominent, historic, and largest collegiate band programs in the world. we build upon that legacy through superb and innovative musical performances and experiences, while honoring our rich traditions. we hope you will explore our website to discover our rich history and learn about our ensembles, events, faculty, and staff members, or make your way to the marching illini site to enjoy the sights and sounds of “the nation’s premier marching band."if you are a prospective student, there is a place for you in the incredible illinois bands community.
