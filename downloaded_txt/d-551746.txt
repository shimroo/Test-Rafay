fatcat bakery is a specialty food manufacturer and wholesale bakery based in sacramento, ca. creating gourmet, artisan quality scones, scone dough, cookies, cookie dough, biscuits, savory scones, muffin batters and bars, fatcat uses only the best all natural ingredients with no trans fats and no preservatives. moist, tender and delicious, fatcat scones artisan pastries are available as raw dough, ready-to-bake or baked frozen, thaw and serve. fatcat is also a nut-free facility for those with allergen concerns. for the foodservice professional, including school foodservice, or home chef alike, with fatcat bakery you’ll enjoy the highest quality pastries that are moist, luscious, labor efficient and profitable.
