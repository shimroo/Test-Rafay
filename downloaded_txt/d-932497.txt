who are we?
bay coast realty is one of the largest independent delaware beach real estate companies in southern delaware.  bay coast realty is ranked in the top 25% of sale & listing sides in sussex county delaware this year.   our company began in 2002 and has grown and expanded today to be recognized as a leader in southern delaware real estate specializing in coastal and inland bay residential community sales.  our year round rental property listings are extensive.  our top notch customer service with a personal touch makes the difference.
bay coast realty continues to be locally owned and operated. driven by the needs of our customers and agents, we provide a professional environment with innovative real estate products and services to meet the demands of our clients and agents.
whether it's through the online listing sections on our websites, or through our experienced team of seasoned agents and our extensive networking technology, we are here to help you with your real estate needs.
our reputation of integrity speaks for itself.
