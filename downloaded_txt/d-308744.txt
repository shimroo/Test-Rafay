    board of commissioners


the executive body of the county is made of a board of commissioners. they are charged with executing the acts legislated by the council, collecting revenue, and managing the day-to-day functions of the county government.
  

read more


  county council


the county council is the legislative branch of the county government and controls all the spending and revenue collection in the county. they are responsible for setting salaries, the annual budget, and special spending.
