welcome!


the human interface technology lab (hitlab) is a multi-disciplinary 
research and development lab whose
work centers around human interface 
technology.  lab researchers represent a wide range of departments from 
across the university of washington campus, including
engineering, 
medicine,
education,
social sciences,
architecture
and the design arts.
