the tampa bay blues festival returns to vinoy waterfront park, st petersburg, florida on april 11, 12, & 13, 2025
join us on the tropical shores of tampa bay for a fabulous weekend of blues music. artists include christone "kingfish" ingram, tab benoit, jj grey & mofro, walter trout, taj farrant, ana popovic, curtis salgado, ruthie foster, north mississippi allstars, joanne shaw taylor, selwyn birchwood, mathias lattin, tad robinson, dk harrell, sierra green & the giants .
america's finest waterfront blues festival, with great food vendors, full liquor bars, craft beers and wines . winner of the international blues foundation's "keeping the blues alive" award as the nation's top blues festival . please order your tickets and vip passes now to join us in st petersburg, florida for three great days of blues by the bay !
