undergraduate programswe offer a bachelor of social work or social welfare for aspiring social workers. whether you’re passionate about helping individuals, families, or communities, our program equips you with the knowledge and skills needed to make a positive impact.



            undergraduate  programs

full-time msw programour full-time master of social work (msw) program offers a comprehensive curriculum designed to prepare you for impactful social work practice. we place a strong emphasis on field education and experiential learning in this program.



            full-time masters in social  work
more

 











part-time msw programour part-time master of social work (msw) program is designed for students who are not able to pursue full-time classes while pursuing their graduate degree.



            part-time masters of social  work
more

 











phd in social welfareour phd program in social welfare offers a rigorous and interdisciplinary plan of study, focusing on both basic and applied social research. doctoral candidates are generally guaranteed funding for at least five years.
