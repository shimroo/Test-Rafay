get your tan on

get a tan through the most comfortable, best looking shirts and swimwear in the world. when you're at the beach, boating, on vacation or just relaxing in the yard, you can get a tan naturally right through the fabric. the cooltan line of swimsuits and shirts not only let you get sun right through the fabric, they are also lightweight, comfortable and dry extremely quickly!


 




the secret is in the fabric

 cooltan tan-through shirts and swimsuits are made from a unique, high quality fabric that allows you to get a natural tan right through the fabric about as fast as you would with a medium level spf sunscreen. the microsol fabric that makes it all possible is amazingly comfortable and lets sunlight through like a medium level sunscreen, eliminating the need for messy oils and lotions.

 




cool & comfortable
