since 1931
an iconic tradition in boston’s historic “little italy”, cantina italiana serves the tastes that make the north end famous. opened in 1931, cantina italiana is the oldest restaurant in the north end of boston. we boast over 80 years of tradition in providing the best in homestyle italian american cuisine, service, and experience.cantina italiana’s warm and charming atmosphere gives the restaurant a comfortable and relaxed setting to enjoy your meal and drink amongst the most important part of your dining experience: family, friends, and food! enjoy and cheer as we would at home: mangia, beve, e buona salute!
