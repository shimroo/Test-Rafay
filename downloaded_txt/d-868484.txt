experts in business valuation
intangible business focusses on business valuation, with specialist knowledge and expertise in intellectual property, natural resources and commodities. dedicated to asset valuation for balance sheet, management and litigation purposes.
spirits and wines industry experience
over the last 16 years intangible business has advised on the valuation of over 20 businesses and portfolios of global spirit and wine brands, involving all categories of assets and liabilities. intangible business provides the most experienced independent valuation and advisory service in an industry where the values of inventories and brands are so significant.
other industry expertise
intangible business has extensive experience and expertise in the following industry sectors. natural resources, telecommunications, pharmaceuticals, banking services, travel, retail, fashion, music, bands, information technology, insurance, soft drinks, beer, media (film, documentories, series), literary works, utilities, health practices, charities, consumer products, valuation training, pensions.
