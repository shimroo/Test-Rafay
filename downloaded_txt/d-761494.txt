vintage puzzles
challenge yourself with a beautiful cavallini jigsaw! with dozens of designs to choose from, you'll keep busy while spending quality time with loved ones. each puzzle comes packaged in a decorative puzzle tube with a mini poster guide and drawstring muslin bag to keep everything together. to top it off, this puzzle packaging is 100% plastic free!


























2026 calendars
since 1989, cavallini has been creating classic wall and desk calendars. each month, enjoy an image selected from the cavallini archives. printed on cavallini’s signature archival paper.


























vintage posters
turn your favorite cavallini poster into a vintage inspired chart with the vintage poster kit. made in the u.s.a. of sustainably sourced oak with easy assembly instructions included. the hardest part is picking just one design!


























vintage tea towels
add some color to your kitchen with these beautiful tea towels made of 100% natural cotton. these favorites for the home are soft, absorbent and perfect for any kitchen. featuring a convenient hanging loop made of cotton twill and packaged in a muslin drawstring bag. designed with vintage images from the cavallini archives.
