welcome to our chapter

eaa chapter 96 is a local chapter of the experimental aircraft association. we are based at compton/woodley airport (kcpm). our members enjoy building, restoring and flying aircraft of all kinds. 
we are aviation enthusiasts, pilots and aircraft builders who get together to share information and ideas and talk about all things that fly.  so if you've ever wondered how to become a pilot, our members can help you find out. our meetings and events are open to everyone. please come visit as our guest.
chapter 96 meetings are held at 11:am on the first saturday day of each month and are open to anyone interested in aviation. we feature speakers and interesting topics and plenty of time to check out the planes in the hanger. we also do a cook out bbq around noon after the meeting. come by and meet and hang out with others who share your interests in all things flying. 
meeting location: compton airport, chapter 96 hangar, 1017 w. alondra blvd. compton, ca 90220.  
be sure to follow us on facebook  www.facebook.com/eaachapter96
if you would like to receive notifications of chapter activities, please subscribe here
the chapter holds young eagles rallies. young eagles rallies are events where pilots volunteer themselves and their airplanes to give kids aged 8-17 a free 20 minute introductory flight.
