pirates!tucked away on the south end of myrtle beach, sc,  pirateland family camping resort will provide a camping experience to treasure for years to come!  




    view recreation amenities
  















celebrating over 55 years of treasured memories! 



we are conveniently located along the oceanfront just outside of the myrtle beach city limit. pirateland was one of the first myrtle beach area campgrounds, opening in 1966, and continues to be among the best oceanfront camping options on the east coast. we offer lodging options for every family vacationer - full hook up rv campsites, shaded campsites -great for tents, fully furnished vacation rentals, and yearly leased sites!  all with easy access to 1/2 mile of beachfront along the atlantic ocean.  fall through spring we're a great destination for snow-birds and we offer monthly and extended stay rates!
