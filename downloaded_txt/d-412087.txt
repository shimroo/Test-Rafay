this is a list of people named elissa with or without e-mail and wwweb site links by or about an elissa. in 1994 elissa beach decided we all deserved to have a place to meet other than a search engine. if you are an elissa or know an elissa who would like to join our group, please use the contact form or e-mail mailto:elissa@elissa.org, we always welcome more elissas! if you are looking for someone named elissa, you may find her name on these pages, however if you don't find her and would like to leave your name, use the contact form to tell us who you are trying to find. names are listed alphabetical by last name and if their maiden name (nee) is given on the form they will be listed under all names given.
