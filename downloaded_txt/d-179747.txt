rear yard drains: what your need to know 


the predominant soil type in the city is clay loam, a clay-heavy mix that does not absorb water well. with clay-heavy soil, the ground absorbs much less water, making flooding more common.

let’s explore some options to keep yard flooding to a minimum.						read on...
