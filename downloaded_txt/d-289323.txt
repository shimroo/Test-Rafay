upcoming at laurier
spring open houses
ready to explore campus? step into the shoes of a golden hawk this spring and discover what life at laurier could mean for you. register for open house and meet with current students and faculty members to discuss all your student-life and program-related questions.

register for waterloo campus: saturday, march 8
register for brantford campus: saturday, march 15
register for milton campus: saturday, april 5

academic, creative and engaged research showcase
the academic, creative and engaged research showcase (acers) at laurier brantford recognizes student and faculty research, displays creative projects, and provides learning experiences for the general public and for the laurier community. join us for acers 2025 on thursday, march 20 in the one market atrium.
