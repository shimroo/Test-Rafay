marine tex® 



marine tex® epoxy systems repair, bond and fill fiberglass, aluminum, plastics such as pvc, abs, polyethylene (starboard® marine building sheet), inflatables, glass, masonry, ceramics, wood, dissimilar materials and more! dependable high-strength epoxy products, coupled with a knowledgeable technical staff provide the confidence to tackle almost any project.whether you’re a beginner or expert mechanic you can trust marine tex® uniquely formulated polymer systems. for over 40 years, the industry standard for repairing surf boards to a damaged hull, patching a fuel tank to sealing leaky rivets, enhancing the beauty of a teak deck or many other boat and home repairs.
