        the information on this page has been compiling since the early
        1980's. this site has been active on the internet, at one url or another, since 1996. 'kills' refers to the number of
        people killed by the character in a movie using our guidelines. movies may appear twice on this site if a proper name
        category (ie. mel gibson) is one of the occurences, otherwise there is only be one entry per
        movie.
        movies must meet minimum run-time (all categories) and kill
        (miscellaneous categories only, stated at the top of the page) requirements.
        star ratings are out of 5 and are rated in increments of .5.
	further details on our requirements are found in our guidelines.

        the statistics on this site are updated on an on-going basis by the scms group. 
for more information about scms, including our history, use one of the links on this page.
descriptions of each category are at the top of each
        page (miscellaneous categories only). 
please see the legend below the statistics table or click on the column title for an
	explanation of what each column means.
        
looking for a specific movie title? use our search facility!
follow this link for recent movie updates to our site. movie reviews appear via a
	(review) link beside the movie name.
