welcome to rising sun farm inc
since 1996, rising sun farm has been breeding quality bred border collies and australian shepherds. our dogs are selectively bred for their structure, intelligence, temperament, drive, and health, consistently producing competition winners, top quality working dogs, and great family companions.

read more














puppies typically available in spring and summer months
rising sun farm is a small, family owned and operated breeder of border collies and australian shepherds. we live and work on the premises and are committed to consistently producing top working, healthy animals with excellent temperament, structure and intelligence. we promote proactive health maintenance, including dna testing and veterinary care. all of our puppies and dogs are wormed, vaccinated and come with health guarantees.
