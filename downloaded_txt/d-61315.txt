thrive at nova southeastern university!
the office of campus recreation and wellness (recwell) is all about helping our nsu
                              community level up their learning and well-being. we offer tons of cool programs and
                              activities for everyone, in a space that welcomes all. explore our diverse fitness
                              classes, state-of-the-art facilities, and personalized wellness programs designed
                              to help you reach your full potential—mind, body, and spirit.












 



the recplex will be open 6:00 a.m. to 8:00 p.m., monday, march 3rd through friday,
                                    march 7th, in recognition of spring break.













visit the recplex
the hub of nsu's recwell is the state-of-the-art recreational complex (recplex). located
                           in the don taft university center, the recplex is more than 100,000 square feet and
                           includes our aquatic center, home to the largest pool in south florida! the recplex
                           also includes:

basketball courts
pickleball courts
racquetball courts
multipurpose studios
fitness center
saunas
and more!

join a club or intramural sports team
college is a whirlwind, and intramural/club sports offer a fun way to dive in. you
                           love staying active, but imagine the camaraderie of a team with a shared passion!
                           build friendships, create memories, and maybe even surprise yourself with hidden talents.
                           plus, hone skills and improve your game in a supportive environment. give it a shot
                           – you might discover your next favorite activity and a whole new crew of friends to
                           cheer you on!

check out intramural sports teams








get moving with group fitness classes
group fitness classes can be your workout gamechanger! from heart-pumping cardio to
                           strength building and stress-busting moves, we offer something for everyone. plus,
                           the energy of a supportive group makes every workout a fun, social blast. dive into
                           our schedule and join the movement!
