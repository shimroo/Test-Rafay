eastman acquired solutia and taminco














in 2012 and 2014, respectively, eastman added two large companies to its portfolio: solutia and taminco. if you’re looking for products from these companies, they can be found in our product finder.














solutia is a subsidiary of eastman

eastman acquired solutia inc. in 2012. 
through this acquisition, eastman added products to our portfolio such as vanceva™, saflex™, performance films, therminol ®, skydrol™ and many more. visit each website to learn more about each brand’s offerings. safety data sheets (sds) can be found here. for invoice inquiries, review the invoice inquiries process. questions about solutia’s portfolio of products? contact us.







taminco is a subsidiary of eastman
eastman acquired taminco, a global specialty chemical company, in 2014.  
through this acquisition, eastman added products to our portfolio across taminco’s three business divisions: functional amines, specialty amines and crop protection. these divisions produced products used in everyday manufacturing for markets such as agriculture, water treatment, personal and home care, animal nutrition and energy.  
to find all eastman products, visit www.eastman.com/products.
