get help with 211

find resources for housing, food and much more with 211, a free, confidential, information and referral service that provides a central access point to local human services providers. anyone can obtain immediate referrals to mental health services, addiction support and rehabilitation, crisis counseling, supplemental food programs, shelter and affordable housing options, employment and education support, financial support, and more.
