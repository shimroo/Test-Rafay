why dick's auto parts

                    dick's auto parts has been in business for the past 25 years we also have been family owned and operated. we started out as a small body shop in 1979 and were offered the chance to sell parts and accessories in 1985.
                

                    we then grew to become the largest in the midwest to carry southern used truck beds and new takeoff parts. in 1989, we moved from our old body shop location that we outgrew into our current location with a 3,000 sq. ft. showroom, and a 6,000 sq. ft. warehouse. in 1998, we added another 12,000 sq. ft. warehouse and filled it to the top with parts. currently we are stocking over 5,000 different parts at our current location.
