



actor database











the actor database is dedicated to all the performers appearing in the irwin allen productions. this includes the stars of all the irwin allen movies and television series, as well as all guest stars, stunt performers and extras. we continue researching all the irwin allen productions and are striving to identify as many performers as we can. visit the irwin allen productions pages to see who we've identified so far, and if you can help put faces to names, please do contact us.
