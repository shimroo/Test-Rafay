        welcome to milton, nh      

milton was incorporated in 1802 and is located in the northeast section of strafford county. the principal villages, milton and milton mills, combine to encompass twenty-seven thousand acres and has a year-round population of approximately four thousand citizens. milton is located forty miles from concord, the state capitol and is twenty miles northwest of the county seat in dover. learn more
