modern miami beach apartments with stunning views of biscayne bay
 
welcome to your new bay-front home. located in south beach, miami overlooking biscayne bay, these fully updated homes feature the best of resort-style living. homes feature floor-to-ceiling windows, private patios, smart home technology, and sweeping views of the bay. community features include an infinity-style pool, lap pool, private poolside cabanas, and a bayside lounge. take advantage of the expansive fitness center or yoga studio, and enjoy easy access to dining and retail shops. ev charging stations are available on-site. enjoy classic, memorable attractions like ocean drive, miami beach ocean walk and much more within a few minutes down the road.










































find your perfect 1-bedroom
expansive 1-bedroom homes with floor-to-ceiling windows and an entryway patio. enjoy modern finishes, 9 foot ceilings, smart home technology and more! 
view 1-bedrooms


















































even more in store
southgate towers is home to the best of south beach, offering an array of enriching retail experiences, from celebrated restaurants including pura vida to innovative fitness and wellness offerings, all just steps from your front door.  
retail experiences






















































south beach state of mind
designed by the prolific miami designer, melvin grossman, southgate towers' art-deco architecture melds with picturesque south beach sunsets inspiring ocean-view alcoves and wellness on demand. everything you need is at southgate towers.  
elevate your life


















































your personal enclave
thoughtfully designed homes include immaculate beach, city, and bay views, floor-to-ceiling windows, social balconies, soft-close european style cabinetry and porcelain tile flooring. these modern upgrades combine touches of art deco history for an unparalleled living experience. 
view residences


















































ultimate access and atmosphere
southgate towers is just 7 minutes from the iconic ocean drive and across the street from highly rated oliver’s bistro and craveable pura vida. enjoy views of flagler memorial island, private marina access, and flamingo, lummus and pointe parks only 10 minutes away. 
discover your neighborhood

saide is your community manager here to do whatever it takes to make you feel at home. as a resident of southgate towers for the past 13 years and counting, you can rely on her and her dedicated concierge team to provide 24/7 world-class customer service with a personal touch.
