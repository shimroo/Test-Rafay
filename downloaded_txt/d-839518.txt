public notice: hartford cso abatement project 


the vermont department of environmental conservation intends to issue a finding of no significant impact (fnsi) for the town of hartford’s combined sewer overflow (cso) abatement project.						read on...







town and school annual report available 


find more information about the 2025 annual report.						read on...







hartford announces hiring of new police chief 


the town of hartford, vermont has chosen its next police chief. lt. commander constance “connie” kelley.						read on...







local option sales tax overview 


learn more about the proposed 1% option sales tax measure on the town meeting day ballot.						read on...







town and school meeting information 


stay informed about the upcoming town and school meeting with details on key dates, election information, and the positions up for vote.						read on...







town of hartford announces police chief greg sheldon’s resignation 


chief of police greg sheldon has announced his resignation from the hartford police department, effective february 28, 2025.						read on...







meet atlas and officer michael goslin: hartford's second k9 team 


the hartford police department is enhancing its law enforcement efforts with the addition of a highly trained k-9 team. this resource will play a big role in supporting the department’s efforts to ensure public safety in the community.						read on...







hartford fire station no. 2 breaks ground 


construction has officially begun on hartford fire station no. 2 in quechee.						read on...







downtown white river junction parking meter update 


the selectboard voted to buy and install parking kiosks throughout downtown and prospect street. approximately 220 parking spaces along the street and the town square parking lot will be metered.						read on...







2nd annual environmental stewardship mix & mingle 


the hartford conservation commission is sponsoring a gathering of local environmental organizations to hear about each group's work and opportunities to get involved. refreshments provided.						read on...







winter pruning workshop 


upper valley apple corps & resilient hartford combine efforts to offer a free, hands-on workshop!						read on...







guide to navigating the new u.s. route 5 changes 


discover the updates to u.s. route 5 in white river junction, including new signals and dedicated bike lanes, and learn how to navigate the protected intersections.						read on...
