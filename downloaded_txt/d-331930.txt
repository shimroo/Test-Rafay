proudly servinggrand traverse county


if you’ve ever visited grand traverse county, you know it’s a special place. many of us have chosen to live and work here because of the unique balance of natural beauty in the region coupled with the modern conveniences that come with being the largest county in northern michigan. grand traverse county is a grand place to live, work, play and do business. protecting our beautiful natural resources, advocating for sustainable economic growth and caring for the health and well-being of our community are just a few of our priorities beyond serving public needs on daily basis.

grand traverse county:a grand place to live. play. do business.


grand traverse county is committed to being a leader in providing responsive, effective, quality service to our community. we're delighted to be recognized at a national level as a spectacular region. we're even more proud of the work we do at a local level to maintain this grand county we've been entrusted to serve.
