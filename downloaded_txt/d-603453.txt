sewing machine needles and accessories for all trades
as the north american distributor for organ needle co., ltd of japan, we supply just about every type of needle to meet the most demanding sewing tasks. our sewing needles are used for a variety of applications including apparel, automotive, bag closing, bookbinding, embroidery, filters, furniture, home fashions, industrial textiles, mattresses, non-wovens, quilting, and special applications.  you have the need, and we have the needles.

using needles as the core of our business. we draw upon our national and worldwide resources to manufacture, procure, and contract the production of quality replacement parts and supplies for all types of equipment used in needle trades.

our alliances with leading factories enable us to supply good quality products, economically priced, and stocked to make timely deliveries.  we are essentially a service company and we never forget that we’ve got to earn your business every day.
sewing machine needle inventory and selection
diamond needle maintains one of the most extensive lines of sewing machine needles, parts and supplies for all trades.  we supply just about any needles to serve any sewing application.

we have over 100,000 parts and supplies for sewing trades. these including needles, replacement parts, scissors, lubricants, chemicals (cleaning, adhesives, etc), specialized tools, drive belts, crayons & markers, safety aids (gloves, needle protection), electric busway, abrasives, and more.
all about diamond needle
as one of the world’s largest distributors of sewing machine needles, we offer one of the most comprehensive lines of sewing machine needles, sewing machine parts, cutting machine parts and related supplies.  we work closely with our factories to create and supply products to meet the demands of sewing markets.

our buying power allows us to keep our selling prices low.  our huge inventory makes it possible to make dependable, fast delivery.  it takes great commitment to live up to our credo:  give our customers good quality products, economical prices, and fast delivery.

we’re a service company.  we maintain a vast inventory that allows us to fulfill most orders and offer same-day shipping. we are committed to offering our customers the best quality sewing needle products at money-saving prices.
