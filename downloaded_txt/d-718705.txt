county news



  
2025 private pesticide and  fertilizer recertificationlicking county is offering three opportunities to acquire recertification hours in 2025. two session february 19 and march 5 will include both fertilizer (category 15) and pesticide (categories 1,2,3,4,5,6,7). one session february 27 will be fertilizer (category 15) only.  

 national diabetes awareness monthnovember is national diabetes awareness month.

join ohio state university extension’s diabetes team at noon on fridays in november for a complimentary 30-minute diabetes class, live online.  

2024 fall weeds universitydesigned to keep agronomic producers on the cutting edge in weed control for their cropping operations. topics addressed will include hot topics in weed control, local weed issues, biology, identification of weeds, control strategies, and evaluating herbicides. hands-on exercises will be included.
