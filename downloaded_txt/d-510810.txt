21st tsc best squad competition (2024)
soldiers from the 16th sb, 18th mp, 30th med, and 7th en, competed in the 21st tsc best squad competition on camp aachen, grafenwöhr, germany, from july 7 to july 11, 2024. 
















u.s. conducts field hospital exercise in baumholder
soldiers with 30th medical brigade constructed a field hospital in baumholder airfield as part of a hospex.














first full-scale missing prisoner exercise held by only u.s. military correctional facility in europe
testing the capabilities of soldiers guarding prisoners at europe’s only correctional facility as well as strengthening military and host-nation cooperation came into play during an exercise dec. 12 on sembach kaserne, germany.













remote maintenance soldiers establish personal ties with ukraine armed forces
remote maintenance distribution cell-ukraine began operation in south-eastern poland to provide weapons and equipment assistance to ukraine armed forces by soldiers from 87th division sustainment support battalion and 16th sustainment brigade













team, facility, program: 3 reasons why knight’s lair is one of the army’s best warrior restaurants
representing u.s. army europe and africa, the knight’s lair warrior restaurant in baumholder prepares to compete in the department of the army 56th annual philip a. connelly award for excellence in food service competition.
