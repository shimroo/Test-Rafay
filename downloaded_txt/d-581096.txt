team history
pete demos, president of the chicago wise guys, started this racing organization in 1995. we are headed into our 31st year in 2025. this team has grown larger and faster over the years, thanks to all the support of you fans coming out to the track to watch one of the best forms of match racing and circuit drag racing. all the drivers and pit crew members and everyone involved is an advantage to our organization, we strive each year to be the best we can and give the fans the “best of the best” match circuit drag racing possible. once again thanks for your support.
                                    …..the entire chicago wise guys team
