looking glass inn
861 sw 51st street,lincoln city, or 97367toll free: 1-800-843-4940


the historic taft district of lincoln city, oregon.
planning a trip to lincoln city, oregon? stay at looking glass inn one of the few lincoln city hotels in the historic taft district. enjoy a wonderful location across the street from siletz bay and the beautiful pacific ocean. you’ll love the views of the bay and ocean. relax and rejuvenate in a room with a beautiful view, whirlpool tub, gas fireplace, and deck, or choose a larger suite with a separate bedroom, living area with sofa bed, and kitchenette.

looking glass inn is one of three ocean-view lincoln city hotels owned by westover inns.we invite you to stay with us and see why guests come back year after year.
westover inns hotels – family owned since 1976 – stay at the water’s edge.visit www.westoverinns.com for more information.
