 description 



what it is
lipsense® long-lasting liquid lip color is the senegence hallmark product, and the one that made us famous!
 
what it does
lipsense®  provides waterproof lip color that won’t smear, rub, or kiss off for 4-18 hours! it’s available in a variety of texture finishes, including matte, shimmer, and frost, that can be worn individually or mixed for custom effects.


 how to use 




 
 
step 1: shake the lipsense® lip color like you mean it 
step 2: clean your lips and pat dry 
step 3: apply color on lips in single, fluid motion. no swiping. let dry 
step 4: layer it on two more times. let each layer dry  
step 5: gloss it over with lipsense® moisturizing glossto seal the color. reapply throughout the day*
step 6: remove any color mishaps or at the end of the day with ooops™* 
 
*lipsense® moisturizing gloss & ooops™ is sold separately
