statement of purpose




this site is designed as a means to offer
      encouragement and support to people who enjoy gaining weight and their
      admirers. whatever terminology you prefer to use to describe your
      lifestyle, if becoming intentionally fatter and/or encouraging someone in
      this practice is a part of your life then this page is a resource for you!
we believe we represent an alternative lifestyle,
      and are therefore entitled to all the respect and acceptance normally
      extended within civilized society. we will not permit or tolerate any form
      of hate directed at this site or anyone who uses it as a resource.
we hope to provide an oasis of understanding,
      support, encouragement and joy for all those who are part of this
      lifestyle.
