experience excellence: we are a historic fine dining steak house offering five-star service, irresistible cuisine, and unmatched ambiance. nestled in the vibrant river north neighborhood of downtown chicago, chicago chop house® invites you to step into history at our renovated 120-year-old victorian brownstone, built in 1897 at 60 w. ontario street. immerse yourself in timeless elegance and rich heritage as you dine in one of the city's true historic landmarks.our legendary menu features hand-cut in-house usda prime steaks, fresh mouth watering seafood, decadent house-made sharable sides and irresistible scratch-made in-house desserts to perfectly culminate your dining experience! we are open nightly for dinner monday through sunday from 4 p.m. – 10 p.m.                                               all major credit cards are accepted*. reservations can be made through opentable or by calling (312) 787-7100for parties of 6 or more a service fee of 18% will be automatically be applied to your bill.
