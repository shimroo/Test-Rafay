cleveland county breaks ground on fairgrounds event center 

cleveland county broke ground sept. 6, 2024 on an arpa funded remodel of the cleveland county fairgrounds event hall. the $10 million project has been in design for several months with construction to start following the ongoing county fair.				read on...
