our services 















fiberglass hull repair



ewhether you've hit a rock, endured a collision, or your hull has simply seen better days, we have the expertise to restore your investment. our fiberglass hull repair service caters to boat owners who demand the highest quality craftsmanship to keep their prized possessions in pristine condition, season after season.















mechanical work  



at the heart of every well-functioning vessel lies a meticulously maintained engine room. our mechanical work services ensure your propulsion system is operating at peak efficiency, thanks to the expertise of our seasoned marine mechanics. with our thorough inspections, precision diagnostics, and expert repairs, you can trust that your engine room is in the best possible hands.















bottom painting



a fresh coat of bottom paint is essential for keeping your boat in top condition. our skilled team will thoroughly clean and prep your boat's bottom, then apply a high-quality, long-lasting paint that will protect your investment. with our bottom painting service, you can enjoy hassle-free boating all season long.
