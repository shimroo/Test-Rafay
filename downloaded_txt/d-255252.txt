promoting, collaborating, & empowering our membersthe southwestern vermont chamber is committed to promoting, leading, and advocating a unified effort that supports the success of our business and nonprofit members. our vision is to cultivate a vibrant regional economy through innovation and collaboration, ensuring that commerce thrives alongside our stunning natural landscapes and diverse, welcoming communities. by driving inclusion, fostering collaboration, and championing innovation we aim to create a healthy business climate and a high quality of life for everyone in the shires of southwestern vermont. through strategic initiatives that empower, collaborate, and promote, we actively seek to strengthen our regional growth and amplify the voices of our community, making southwestern vermont an ideal place to live, work, and play.
