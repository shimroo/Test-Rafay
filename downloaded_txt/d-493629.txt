who we are



for over 25 years stray cat blues inc., a 501(c)3 non-profit, has been working to help stray, abandoned, and feral cats in montgomery and bucks counties, and the surrounding areas. in addition to fostering and adopting friendly cats and kittens, our group actively encourages trap neuter return spay and neuter of feral cats.
