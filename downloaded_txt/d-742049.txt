                        institute of medical physics and microtissue engineering                    
the institute of medical physics and microtissue engineering is an interdisciplinary research institute at the medical faculty at friedrich-alexander university erlangen-nürnberg (fau). the research at imp focuses on the study of cells in the context of their microenvironment (kristian franze) as well as on adaption processes in a macroscopic perspective (wolfgang kemmler).
