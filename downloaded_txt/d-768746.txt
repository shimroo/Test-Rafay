let’s celebrate together! 
hidden grove is that perfect mix of your parent’s backyard with an elegant garden feel! our outdoor event venue in woodland, ca is only 15 minutes from downtown sacramento and davis, ca. we can host any event including intimate weddings, rehearsal dinners, baby showers, birthdays, fundraisers, and farm to fork dinners! our idyllic property can fit up to 150 guests in a secluded, beautiful setting.
