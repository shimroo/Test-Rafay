quality healthcare - with a personal touch.como park animal hospital in st. paul, mn has provided progressive veterinary medicine with a caring, hands-on approach for over 35 years. our veterinarians and staff work hard every day to live out our mission.




    about us
  



we are dedicated to providing:high quality, progressive veterinary care for animals and to deliver exceptional service to our clients. we intend to operate as a team that is committed to making como park animal hospital a positive, professional and rewarding place to work.we will always remember that it is an honor and a privilege to care for our clients and their pets. it is our goal to treat our patients as well as we would treat our own pets and show their owners the kind of care and concern that each of us would expect to receive.fax: (651) 487-1941*please call the clinic to setup your appointment.

here for you24 hours a day,365 days a year.como park animal hospital is a unique veterinary healthcare service in the twin cities metro. we offer primary care and emergency room services all under one roof. in addition, we offer boarding, medical grooming, onsite pharmacy, all at one convenient location.
