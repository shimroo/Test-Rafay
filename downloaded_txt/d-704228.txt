welcome back to nature!
tofino, bc, canada is an oasis of old-growth rainforests, wildlife, beaches and ocean. locals include whales, eagles, bears, salmon, ospreys, otters, seals, cougars, wolves, herons and steelhead; and visitors are migrating whales, sea lions and birds. tofino is recognized as a peaceful place where one can kick back, breathe, feed and enjoy. and people can enjoy it too! welcome back to nature.
