how opentext business network cloud can benefit business




automate information flows
implement an end-to-end supply chain platform that meets dozens of global standards, including x-12, and seamlessly connect external and internal business systems.



improve visibility
obtain real-time insights across all internal and external data flows to help improve process optimization and overall business operations efficiency.



strengthen end-to-end customer experiences
provide the flexibility and scalability to support changing customer requirements or address dynamic market conditions with a cloud-based integration environment.



operate from a single b2b integration platform
access a flexible mix of easy edi tools and managed services to underpin digital transformation initiatives and enable future business growth.



collaborate securely
enhance supply chain collaboration, prevent data breaches, and improve business ecosystem security with scalable identity management.



ensure compliant e-invoicing
automate electronic invoicing and ensure compliance in more than 45 countries, across procure-to-pay and order-to-cash processes.
