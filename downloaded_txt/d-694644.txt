evil eye pictures creates and supports innovative storytelling, real-time, unreal engine dev, ar/vr, imagery and film for artists, directors, technologists & visionaries. we bring art & technology together to create next-gen immersive experiences

 









originsfounded in 2004 after contributing award-winning visuals to the matrix films, evil eye pictures produced imagery for the visual effects industry for 15 years.  from tent pole to indy, evil eye rode the industry twists and turns to then pivot and boldly aim for something new. today evil eye collaborates with content creators, innovators and engineers exploring immersive, compelling visuals and experiences for the evolving world of real-time, multi-platform entertainment.
