“we are the underdog, we’re the fighters getting ready for the next round…”martha redbone is a vocalist/songwriter/composer/educator, known for her unique gumbo of folk, blues, and gospel from her childhood in harlan county, kentucky infused with the eclectic grit of pre-gentrified brooklyn.
