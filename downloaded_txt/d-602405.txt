			the beachcombers running club is an all-volunteer, non-profit 
			organization made up of walkers, joggers, and runners who have 
			embraced walking, jogging, and running as lifetime fitness 
			activities. our mission is to support youth running in daytona 
			beach, florida










mission
the mission of the daytona beachcombers running club is to nurture a sense of community and camaraderie within the club through running and social events; to offer opportunities for guidance, 
    coaching, and development to members; and to promote the benefits of a healthy running/walking lifestyle for adults and young people.
