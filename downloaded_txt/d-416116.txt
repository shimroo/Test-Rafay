secrest arboretum sustains life by connecting people and plants.
secrest arboretum supports the vision of the ohio state university college of food, agricultural, and environmental sciences by providing an accessible, sustainable, well-documented collection of horticulturally relevant trees, shrubs, and herbaceous perennial plants. the arboretum serves the land-grant mission as a living resource for horticultural education, research, and outreach to diverse stakeholders.
