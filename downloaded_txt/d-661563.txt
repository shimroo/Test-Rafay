 news flash
















weiss farm affordable housing lottery 


the devon at weiss farm is a newly constructed residential community of 259 apartments and townhomes located in stoneham.  the lottery for the affordable units is now open. please read on for lottery and application information.						read on...







override questions & comments - we'd like your feedback 


the select board voted to place a $14.6 million override question on the april 1st town election ballot.						read on...







stoneham shuttle program update 


as of tues, jan 21, 2025, you can use the stoneham shuttle to go anywhere in stoneham and the greenwood commuter rail station in wakefield during service hours. just use the circuit stoneham app to select your pick-up & drop-off locations.						read on...







winter parking restrictions in effect december 15th-april 1st 


this year overnight parking will not be allowed on any street in stoneham from december 15th - april 1st. cars may be ticketed between 1 am-6 am.						read on...







board & committee opportunities 


the town of stoneham depends on volunteers to serve on boards & committees.  no special expertise or skills are necessary.  the only requirment is residency in stoneham. please read on for a list of openings.						read on...







stoneham public library will be closed february 24th-february 28th 


the library will be closed for spray foam insulation to be placed in the attic as part of the green communities grant the library received to upgrade the hvac system to heat pumps. the library will reopen on saturday, march 1st from 10am-2pm.						read on...
