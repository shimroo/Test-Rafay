welcome to the information technology laboratoryitl’s purpose | cultivating trust in it and metrologythe information technology laboratory (itl) is one of nist’s six research laboratories. itl focuses on it measurements, testing, and standards, and is a globally recognized and trusted source of high-quality, independent, and unbiased research and data. itl’s mission, to cultivate trust in information technology (it) and metrology, is accomplished using its world-class measurement and testing facilities and encompassing a wide range of areas of computer science, mathematics, statistics, and systems engineering.this non-regulatory role, along with itl’s deep technical expertise in all fields of information technology, helps increase trust in it worldwide.
