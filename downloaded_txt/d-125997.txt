historic barclay farm property
barclay farmstead offers award-winning educational programs, exhibits, and community events annually.  the farmstead is closed for the season but will open again in march. click here for more information about the property.








scarborough covered bridge
one of two still standing covered bridges in new jersey.  this bridge was built by bob scarborough in 1959 and designed by noted local architect malcolm wells.
