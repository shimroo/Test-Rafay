who we are

this site is the primary site of the museum of computer art. it hosts the digital 
and computer art of thousands of artists. established more than 30 years 
ago, it has now created a complementary cross-linked site hosting
the spectacularly new and vibrant art of artificial intelligence. 
clicking links on this page accesses all art on both sites.

this is what we do

there's no charge to viewers. no charge to artists. no advertising.  no facebook or meta.  no twitter or x.  no distractions. 
dedicated solely to the online exhibit of digital fine art in its various forms and manifestations.
