lighting the way 



in partnership with our community, we ignite positive change and create a legacy of impact.


























at the chautauqua region community foundation, our work goes beyond simply processing donations, making grants and awarding scholarships. we are deeply committed to creating a vibrant region where every resident feels connected and has the opportunity to thrive.



our vision




    learn more
  














as a philanthropic leader in the chautauqua region, we address our community’s most pressing needs, create opportunities for innovation and growth, and work with donors to support the causes they care about now and into the future.
