find your forever home

with emmer development










at emmer group, we are here to make your home search for new construction a memorable and pleasant experience!










emmer development corp. is a home builder that offers brand-new home communities in gainesville, such as lugano, as well as apartment properties in pensacola, orlando and gainesville. under the umbrella of emmer group, we have emmer development and emmer management corporations.



























































 why emmer development?


























new construction.by choosing new construction and emmer development as your home builder, you are also choosing energy savings. each home features a strong monolithic concrete slab & foundation, termite treated sub-soil, energy efficient tank-less water heater and many more energy efficient features.















creatively designed.emmer development’s new construction models are carefully and creatively designed by a team of professionals to combine easy living with functional efficiency. to make your forever home as comfortable as possible, we offer the opportunity to make some custom choices.















exceptional features.as a new construction home builder, we know the importance of indoor features and outdoor comforts. each emmer home comes with front yard maintenance and complete irrigation, a full lighting package, 9 or 10 foot ceilings, and many more must-have features that make your house a home.
