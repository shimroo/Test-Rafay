welcome to star antenna
experts in broad band and out of band antenna designs
					communication electronic, founded in 1990, is engaged in design, development and manufacturing of standard and customized rf antennas. our antennas are manufactured under the brand name star antenna. our company has a dedicated team of talented professionals having core experience in design, development and manufacturing of rf antennas meeting regulatory standards.
					    communication electronic has the capability to deliver antennas for hf / vhf / uhf and microwave bands for telecommunications, navigation, intelligence, surveillance and reconnaissance applications. at communication electronic, we are committed to provide you quality, reliability and personalized services. we look forward to an everlasting and enriching relationship with you.
