 about camex
 hosted by the national association of college stores (nacs), the campus market expo (camex) is the largest educational conference and buying expo for campus  retail. it combines hands-on learning, facilitated sessions, and a dynamic trade show to drive success in collegiate retail. with industry-specific education, valuable networking opportunities, and a chance to explore new vendors and merchandise, camex is aimed at supporting the growth and innovation of campus stores.
