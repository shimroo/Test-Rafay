community radiology imaging centersas one of the largest imaging providers in the region, community radiology (cra) offers a complete range of imaging services including mri, ct, digital & 3d mammography, ultrasound, pet-ct, nuclear medicine, fluoroscopy, x-ray, dexa | bone density and more.cra operates numerous convenient outpatient locations throughout montgomery, frederick, prince george's & carroll counties, and provides consultative services to medstar montgomery medical center. all referrals and most insurances accepted. foreign language interpretation services available.
