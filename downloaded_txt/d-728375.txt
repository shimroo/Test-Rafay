littleton parks, recreation, and community education office is located at 41 shattuck street, littleton, ma 01460 (2nd floor)long lake beach is located at 20 town road, littleton, ma 01460fay park is located at 20 foster road, littleton, ma 01460castle in the trees playground is located at 300 king street, littleton, ma 01460the mission of the littleton parks, recreation & community education department is to provide affordable year-round high quality indoor and outdoor recreation and enrichment activities for children and adults in littleton and the surrounding communities. our goal is to provide cultural, social, and athletic programs in well maintained parks and facilities.
