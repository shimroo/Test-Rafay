on may 1, 2023, rsm us llp acquired the equity of wise consulting associates, inc. wise consulting completed its transition to rsm on dec. 1, 2023, retiring the wise consulting brand and aligning with rsm’s brand, operations and systems.
together, we provide holistic workforce solutions for the middle market, including dayforce, ukg and payroll services from a dedicated team committed to delivering industry-leading support and service. we also offer access to expanded human capital services, including organizational design, compensation and benefits review, workforce and human resources (hr) transformation, and hr outsourcing and strategy services, along with rsm’s broad range of consulting offerings and world-class assurance and tax services.





















        good for our clients, good for our people
    



the time has never been better to work with our team.
this step forward enables our teams to provide an enhanced client experience with access to additional services, capabilities and networks. at the same time, we provide our people with new training and professional development opportunities to help them advance in their careers.
we are excited to begin serving you. 






                hcm technology and transformation
            






our industry-leading ukg pro suite and dayforce certified consultants provide comprehensive implementation, optimization, change management, advisory and technical solutions. rsm is the only ukg and dayforce implementation provider offering managed services to support the entire back office: payroll, accounting, finance, information technology (it) and human resources (hr). our depth of solutions encompasses many interrelated people-focused aspects of human resources, payroll, benefits and technology to build an optimized and forward-facing people strategy. our international footprint enables us to support clients with operations globally.











                ukg
            






rsm’s ukg consulting services offer the guidance you need to be successful with your ukg investment, driving engagement, productivity and profitability while maximizing your company's greatest asset—people. the assembly and management of the right people, processes and technology, while maintaining alignment and value as growth occurs, provide a competitive advantage.




learn more











                dayforce
            






rsm’s dayforce consulting services deliver tailored solutions to leverage the all-in-one human resources (hr) and payroll platform to maximize your company's greatest asset—people. our team takes a holistic approach to your people, processes and technology, transforming your human capital management (hcm) environment with the tools and flexibility that unlock the potential of the modern workforce, provide a competitive advantage and help ensure your long-term prosperity.




learn more











                payroll services
            






our payroll services team continues to provide best-in-class full and partial payroll administration from industry-certified (u.s. and canada) and experienced payroll professionals. offering comprehensive payroll processing, tax oversight, secure and compliant payroll practices in alignment with an organization's strategy, and optimal utilization of invested technology, our managed payroll team ensures accurate, secure, compliant payroll continuity that supports growth and change.
