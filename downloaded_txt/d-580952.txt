the uf/ifas department of agricultural education and communication is a group of faculty, staff, and students committed to connecting people with agriculture through agricultural communication, education, leadership development and extension education. our department offers bachelor's, master's and doctoral degrees in agricultural education and communication for degree-seeking students, while also offering professional certificates for industry professionals that wish to enhance their skills in a particular area related to our discipline. our work as social scientists focus on the application and adoption of cutting-edge scientific research, and we hope that you will join us in connecting all people with agriculture and natural resources.
