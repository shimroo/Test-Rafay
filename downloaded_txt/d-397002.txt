2024 university city annual report now available!
university city is proud to present the 2024 annual report, a comprehensive overview of the city’s progress, achievements, and ongoing initiatives. this report highlights the significant investments and developments that have shaped our community over the past year, including:major economic growth – the market at olive continues to expand with new retail anchors like target and dierbergs, while new developments such as local on delmar and avenir apartments are adding much-needed housing.public safety enhancements – the university city police department has led a 45% decrease in violent crime since 2019, while the fire & ems department has responded to over 6,700 service calls and expanded emergency training programs.infrastructure & community improvements – investments in parks, recreation, and public works have led to renovated facilities, upgraded roads, and expanded green spaces, ensuring a thriving and vibrant community for all residents.commitment to transparency & communication – the city continues to enhance engagement through digital platforms, community outreach, and an improved website experience.the 2024 annual report serves as a reflection of our city’s dedication to progress, transparency, and community well-being. we encourage all residents to explore the full report and stay informed about the future of university city.read the full report here: view here
