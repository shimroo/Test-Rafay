taste the difference with our valencia peanut butter






















east wind peanut butter is manufactured exclusively using the valencia peanut, renowned for its sweetness and nutritional benefits. high in protein and antioxidants, valencias deliver a healthier, more flavorful option. our standout peanut butter will help you create a standout product!




    organic valencia
  



























collectively made. community owned.



east wind nut butters are made by a community with a proud history of working and living together. since 1981 we’ve put the same communal care into every jar. it’s nut butter made by real people who believe in simplicity, quality, and a cooperative way of life. peace, love, and nut butter!
