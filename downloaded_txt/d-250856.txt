welcome to the hba of lake-sumter!
the home builders association of lake-sumter (hba-ls) is your “go to” resource and leading authority for the homebuilding and construction industry in lake and sumter counties. a membership and networking organization comprised of professional homebuilders, construction-related tradesmen and real estate industry partners, hba-ls advocates for ethical and intelligent construction practices and regulations, private property rights, affordable/workforce/attainable housing legislation and construction-readiness career programs.
when people in lake and sumter counties want to buy, build, finance, remodel or maintain a home, their first stop should be the hba-ls because we have many of the best in the homebuilding, remodeling and construction industries who have worked hard to earn the trust of so many.
the hba-ls is centrally located at 1100 n. joanna avenue, tavares, fl.
