welcome to
u.s. 27 motorsports & trailers

we offer a wide range of options, including atv, utv, sports side by side, motorcycles, scooters, personal watercraft, snowmobiles, cargo trailers, and generators from renowned brands such as honda®, can-am®, polaris®, ski-doo, sea-doo, yamaha, cf moto. additionally, we have over 800 trailers to choose from, including those by yacht club, sure-trac, discovery, quality trailers, and genesis.

new 2024 & 2025 atvs, sxs, snowmobiles, motorcycles, and trailers are now available for sale and in stock! our process for acquiring a new machine is incredibly easy, with flexible financing options available for individuals with good or bad credit. we offer programs that cater to almost everyone's needs.

as the leading dealer in the midwest, we specialize in meeting all your powersports and trailer requirements. our convenient location at 5301 n us 27 in st. johns, mi 48879 is just 20 minutes north of lansing, making it a convenient stop to michigan's beautiful up north. at us 27 motorsports & trailers, we boast over 30 acres and 25,000 square feet of the finest toys in the industry. our selection of "set-up and ready-to-roll" vehicles and trailers is unmatched in three states.
