young citizens awards 


the maryville community improvement board is again sponsoring the annual young citizens of the year award for students in kindergarten through 12th grade. please "read on" for details about the program & nomination instructions.						read on...







hometown hero banners 


the maryville community improvement board is accepting applications for hometown hero banners for the 2025 season. please see the below link for the application form. applications need to be received by 3/31/2025. banners are displayed along highway 159.			banner application form







surveillance cameras & network video recorders - request for proposals 


the village of maryville is seeking rfp's for the purchase & installation of surveillance cameras & network video recorders at maryville parks. *amended* information is available under the "village of maryville bid postings" tab under "government".			





scheduled limb/brush pickup program discontinued 


due to rising costs, the village will no longer be able to provide scheduled limb/brush pickups. the village will continue to provide limb pickups after severe storms. please click on the below link for rules concerning storm pickups.			storm damage limb pickup rules







water system improvement project 


the village of maryville will soon begin construction of a new water main in the pat drive / keebler road area.  please read on for the iepa funding notice.						read on...







ada public meeting 


the village of maryville will hold a public meeting on friday, march 7, 2025, at 10:00 a.m. at village hall. the purpose of this meeting is the annual review of the americans with disabilities act self-evaluation & transition plan for the village.			ada public meeting notice







codered notifications for boil orders 


the public works department will now use the codered system for water boil orders. please read on to find out how to sign up for these notifications. you can also access this information on the public works department page of this website.						information
