our philosophy has always remained constant; to offer our customers the highest quality in furniture, service and value without compromising style and comfort. villa vici is your one stop furniture store and interior design resource located in the heart of magazine street in new orleans, providing eclectic modern designs alongside reclaimed wood and one-of-a kind objects. we offer a carefully selected product mix from upholstery to tables, storage to lighting, floor coverings to window treatments with furniture for indoors and out chosen from the finest vendors around the world, to ensure a timeless home environment that excites the senses.
