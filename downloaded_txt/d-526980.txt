dept. of health unveils new “main street goes blue” colon cancer screening awareness campaign banner 

mount morris, n.y., feb. 12, 2025 — colorectal cancer awareness month may begin in march, but the dept. of health is wasting no time in spreading a life-saving message to all: if you are 45 years of age or older, get screened for colorectal cancer today.				read on...







youth bureau supports local youth sports programs with state grant funding 

geneseo, n.y., feb. 11, 2025 - the livingston county youth bureau has awarded more than $38,000 in funding to local youth sports organizations across the county. the county backing supports the availability of sports activities for youths under age 18.				read on...







lima expands outdoor recreation  offerings with arpa-funded dog park shelter 

lima, n.y., feb. 7, 2025 - the town of lima has installed a new pavilion in the dog park at mark tubbs memorial park, in part, through funding from the american rescue plan act (arpa) administered by livingston county.				read on...
