welcome to mirror image fashions
our missionmirror image fashions specializes in helping men and women compile their professional wardrobes. whether you are wearing business suits, professional dresses, or casual wear, the clothes you select help determine the image and impression you convey to others.how do we help?advice - let us help guide you through your options. we take time to understand your profession, work environment, and personal style. we use that knowledge to recommend apparel that is most appropriate to you.selection - we help select items from our key vendors, custom-made suits, dresses, or shirts, or tux rentals. tailoring - it's not enough to buy a garment. it should be tailored and fitted to each individual's body type. let our professional tailor help ensure that your garments are crafted to look their best on you with precise measuring.
