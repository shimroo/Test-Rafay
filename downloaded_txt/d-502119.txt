for a tête-à-tête about the state of design, and celebration of women leading the overhaul of established norms, cultured’s editor-in-chief sarah harrelson and woven president danielle barr brought together a group of changemakers for a summer lunch in los angeles yesterday.

woven place

over 400 guests enjoyed spring cocktails and live music as we celebrated our highly anticipated new london showroom, woven place.

woven place
london dinners

in celebration of woven place, our new london showroom, we hosted a series of intimate dinners in our new gallery in the pimlico district. guests got a sneak peek of the new space, while dining on seasonal ingredients grown by chef johnnie collins.
