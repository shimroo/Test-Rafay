our vision
the east allegheny school district, in partnerships with families and the community, responsibly engages, inspires and prepares each student for individual success, citizenship and life in the 21st century.  
 
our mission
the mission of the east allegheny school district is to recognize that each student is unique and that through cooperation with parents, community and schools, each student will have educational opportunities to achieve individual success within district fiscal constraints.
