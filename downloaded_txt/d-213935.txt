new online utility billing payment system–no water shut off/late fee during transition 


city has launched a new & improved online utility billing portal. no shut off/late fee will be applied to utility accounts during transition. all auto-pay credit card customers must create a new account as info. did not migrate & current bill is not paid.						how to pay current utility bill







city launches new online building permit portal - a new account must be created 


a new & improved online building permit portal is now live on the city website. during the transition to the new online portal, only in-person cash/check/and now credit card & mail-in check payments can be made for building permits.						read on...







winter garden music festival – saturday, february 22 


this free community event on sat., feb. 22, 11 am – 10 pm, features 30+ live music acts across 6 stages in historic downtown. beer/wine vendors (no outside coolers/alcohol permitted). bring a chair. pedestrian-friendly with downtown streets closed.			music schedule & details







downtown roads closed for winter garden music festival 


w. plant st. from dillard st. to park ave., & portions of adjoining streets, will be closed from saturday, feb. 22 at 2:00 am – sunday, feb. 23 at 2:00 am. road closures are due to free winter garden music festival in downtown. free event shuttle.						read on...







opening of veterans memorial park delayed until 10 am on sat., feb. 22 


the gates of veterans memorial park will not open until 10 am on sat., feb. 22. there’s no access to pickleball/tennis/basketball courts and park facilities until the park opens. the park (420 s. park ave.) serves as offsite parking for wg music festival.						read on...







curbside recycling suspended, effective january 4, 2025 - see cart removal schedule 


the city suspended recycling collection effective jan. 4, 2025, due to escalating costs, contamination, and lack of markets for recycling material. your recycling cart will be picked up according to a preset schedule. click link for schedule.						read on...







revamped utility bill assistance program for low income seniors provides savings of up to 90% 


city of winter garden has revamped its utility bill assistance for low income seniors program in 2025, lowering the residency & income requirements and  providing up to 90% reduction on base charges on city utility bills. application deadline: may 1, 2025						read on...







wastewater treatment facility undergoing an upgrade-construction activity to be expected 


construction to upgrade the wastewater treatment facility commences mid-february 2025 and continues for approx. 36 months. located at 101 e. crest ave., please expect construction-related activities in the area during weekday business hours.						read on...







tickets on sale now for evening at the pops – march 29 


tickets now available for an enchanted evening of live music by the plant street pops orchestra at the shore of lake apopka. pack a picnic and blanket/chair. presented by rotary club of winter garden with city co-sponsorship. general admission: $10			event details & ticket info







section of tildenville school road closed, feb. 24 - april 18 


from february 24 - april 18, a section of tildenville school road, south of plant street and north of brandy oaks subdivision, will be closed. please follow detour signs and allow extra travel time.						read on...







business impact estimate for proposed ordinance 25-02 


in accordance with section 166.041(4), florida statutes, see the business impact estimate for ordinance 25-02 amending chapter 74 -traffic and vehicles to create a new article v of the city of winter garden code of ordinances.						read on...







business impact estimate for proposed ordinance 25-01 


in accordance with section 166.041(4), florida statutes, see the business impact estimate for ordinance 25-01 amending chapter 74 -traffic and vehicles to create a new article vi “school zone speed enforcement” of city of winter garden code of ordinances.						read on...







boys and girls club june family branch in east winter garden holds groundbreaking ceremony 


the boys & girls clubs of central florida, in conjunction with the city of winter garden, held a groundbreaking ceremony on oct. 24 for a 20,000-square-foot facility, the june family branch. located at 459 ninth st., the club will serve kids ages 6-18.						read on...







sr 429 construction commences in winter garden 


there are temporary lane and ramp closures to accommodate construction work for the sr 429 capacity improvements from tilden road to sr 414 project. read on for project information and for link to construction alerts.						read on...
