habitat for humanity of the lowcountrythe missionhabitat for humanity of the lowcountry partners with families in need of an affordable home of their own, fostering stability, self-reliance, and a strong sense of community. since 1990, hfh lowcountry has put 190 families (soon to be 192!) in to homes in beaufort and jasper counties and has less than a 2% foreclosure rate. affordable housing changes lives and the community for the better.
