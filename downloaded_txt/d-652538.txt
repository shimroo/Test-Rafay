huntington beach events 


welcome to the vibrant coastal city of huntington beach, located along the stunning coastline of southern california. with its iconic pier, pristine beaches, and laid-back beach vibes, huntington beach is a haven for surfers and beachgoers and a hub for a wide range of events that add excitement and flavor to this coastal paradise. check out our huntington beach events, from once-in-a-lifetime events, to our yearly favorites!

events in huntington beach for everyone

huntington beach, california, is a vibrant coastal community that boasts diverse upcoming events to suit every interest. there's always something happening in this bustling beach town, from annual surf competitions and outdoor concerts to art festivals and food fairs. surf enthusiasts can catch world-class surfing competitions like the us open of surfing, while music lovers can groove to live tunes at the summer concert series or the huntington beach airshow. art enthusiasts can explore local galleries and attend the huntington beach art walk, while foodies can indulge in the taste of huntington beach, showcasing the city's culinary offerings. families can enjoy seasonal events such as the 4th of july parade and the annual cruise with santa, while fitness enthusiasts can join the surf city marathon or participate in beach yoga classes. with its vibrant and diverse event calendar, events in huntington beach offer an exciting and dynamic destination for visitors to experience a wide range of activities and celebrations throughout the year.
