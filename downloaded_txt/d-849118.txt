learn more about the park's history 





the badlands national park is the largest protected mixed grass prairie in the united states. this unique region, located just 70 miles east of rapid city, was ruthlessly ravaged by wind and water that became a scenic wonderland for national park explorers to enjoy.











 
cedar pass lodge cabins

                        cedar pass lodge welcomes guests to enjoy the incredible beauty of badlands national park as the only in-park accommodation.

                        enjoy the vast open prairies of badlands national park and set up camp at one of cedar pass campground’s tent and rv sites
