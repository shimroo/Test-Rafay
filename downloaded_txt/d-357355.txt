about this location
henry county health center has a rich history of serving our community since 1921 and has continued its legacy since partnering with great river health in 2021. located at the crossroads of southeast iowa, hchc is a 74-bed facility, featuring 25 acute care beds, along with our 49-bed long-term care facility, park place elder living.we're proud to offer local, compassionate, and high–quality primary care, emergency department, walk-in clinic, mental health, women’s health, elder living, and pharmacy services in mount pleasant. by establishing your care here, you gain access to great river health’s entire system of services, providers, and clinics throughout southeast iowa to manage your health and wellness. in need of services that great river health doesn’t offer? we can help you get the care you need. to schedule an appointment, call 319-385-3141.right place. right care. right here.
