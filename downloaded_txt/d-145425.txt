2024/25 season
northshore concert band continues its tradition of musical excellence in its 69th season, commemorating over 20 years of artistic leadership by mallory thompson with works celebrating the human spirit.



view the schedule


















lifetime of music
a core element of the band’s educational outreach, the lifetime of music initiative enriches the lives of young musicians with an opportunity to perform alongside ncb.



learn more


















hire ncb for your event!
ncb is regularly invited to perform in prestigious concert halls, high school gyms, hotel ballrooms, and a variety of outdoor venues. be a part of the 2024/25 season by hiring the band for your upcoming event!
