donations make a difference



westcave outdoor discovery center a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization is a unique geological gem hidden in the texas hill country. since 1976, we have shared this magical space with thousands of children and adults to inspire a lifelong practice of enjoying and protecting nature. 



please consider a gift to westcave that will support our much-needed elementary and middle school field trips, our girls+ outside program and critical conservation work.  




    donate
  



your year-end gift will help westcave effectively steward our serene grotto, hiking trails (several ada accessible), bird blinds, prairie restoration, rainwater ponds, butterfly gardens and more.  additionally, your gift allows westcave to continue to inspire thousands of area children through our outstanding education and community outreach programs.
