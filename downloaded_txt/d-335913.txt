stokes appliance parts
stokes appliance parts is the largest independent distributor of domestic, commercial and industrial appliance spare parts to the australian market.
with the most comprehensive range of spare parts, product information and collective years of experience, we are here to assist and provide you with all your appliance needs.
we have over 100,000 parts available for ovens, cooktops, rangehoods, microwaves, dishwashers, washing machines, clothes dryers, vacuum cleaners and our range include major brands such as aeg, amana, ariston, artusi, baumatic, blanco, bosch, chef, damani, de dietrich, delonghi, dishlex, electrolux, euro, euromaid, frigidaire, general electric, grimwood, honeywell, hoover, ilve, jenn-air, kelvinator, kleenmaid, lg, linea, maytag, neff, omega, simpson, smeg, speed queen, st george, westinghouse, whirlpool, vax, venini and many others.
