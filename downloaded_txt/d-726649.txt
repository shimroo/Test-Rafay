terrazzo artisansquality in service since 1919from concept, to design, to final polish, steward mellon surpasses owners' and design professionals’ expectations. with our cradle to grave service, we proudly perform all of our work with our own craftworkers.
















our creative terrazzo solutions are backed by our reputation, service and assured quality. 














our relationships are built with knowledge, integrity and follow through.for over 100 years, steward mellon has been proudly installing beautifully designed and executed terrazzo flooring and precast. we pride ourselves in our long standing relationships and are proud to be members of the iubac (international union of brick layers and allied craftworkers), the ntma (national terrazzo and mosaic association), and the cmfa (construction financial management association).
