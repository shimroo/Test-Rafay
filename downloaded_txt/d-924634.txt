starting with the spring 2025 semester, texas tech will be moving some courses to
                           raidercanvas from blackboard.
most students will still be taking course in blackboard, but some courses will be
                              taught in raidercanvas. 
if you are enrolled in one of the raidercanvas courses, you should have received an
                           email with instructions. please follow the steps in the email to log in to raidercanvas
                           using your eraider credentials.
