land acknowledgement



the nabet 700-m unifor office is located on the traditional territory of many nations, including the mississaugas of the credit, the anishnabeg, the chippewa, the haudenosaunee and the wendat peoples. it is now home to many diverse first nations, inuit and métis peoples. we also acknowledge that toronto is covered by treaty 13 with the mississaugas of the credit.learn more about our land acknowledgement on the city of toronto website.
