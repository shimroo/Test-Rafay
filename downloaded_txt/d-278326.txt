about us



tpw is a family owned company that provides professional community and property management, and real estate sales and rental services. tpw’s team of accredited managers understand the maintenance and management needs of all types of residential property. condominiums, co-operatives, homeowner associations, townhouses, and resort vacation property are all included in properties served by tpw. our philosophy is to use technology, accountable metrics, and proven systems to provide the highest level of service to our loyal customer base.
