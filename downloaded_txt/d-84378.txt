make your mark on history here.natchez may be the oldest city on the mississippi but we also have the brightest future! natchez is a walkable, vibrant, and beautiful historic city, where preservation and progress go hand in hand. today's natchez is affordable, livable, and especially attractive to those with an entrepreneurial spirit. we enjoy our reputation as a friendly, welcoming town that offers inclusion and community.please explore our official website to learn the many ways that the city of natchez is working to serve you.
