how opentext analytics cloud can benefit business




reduce data costs
lower analytics expenses by addressing inefficiencies in computing, hidden costs, and workload management for a more efficient and cost-effective solution.



transform decision-making
empower employees with real-time analytics and ai assistants to make data-driven decisions, improve operations, and drive innovation across your organization.



activate ai initiatives
build a strong data foundation to implement and optimize your ai strategies, enabling seamless integration and enhanced ai-driven capabilities.



make data actionable
convert complex data into actionable insights for predictive decision-making, boosting your competitive advantage while reducing operational costs.



scale with efficiency
manage growing data volumes effortlessly—maintaining high performance and reducing overhead—allowing teams to focus on strategic goals.



improve flexibility and integration
integrate data from multiple sources to eliminate silos, enhance visibility, and achieve greater agility and operational efficiency.











why analytics cloud?





transparent pricing
enjoy predictable costs with no hidden fees. all features are included, and you only pay for the resources you actually use.




optimized computing
reduce your total cost of ownership with optimized computing and energy use, preventing overspending and boosting efficiency.




flexible deployment and efficient workloads
deploy analytics in the cloud, on-premises, or in hybrid setups, optimizing workloads and managing costs based on your needs.




instant, ai-driven insights
access real-time insights with ai, predictive analytics, and text mining—turning diverse data into actionable intelligence rapidly.




security and compliance
protect your data with advanced security controls and compliance measures, ensuring safety across all deployment environments.




scalable performance for growing data
seamlessly scale analytics and reporting to manage growing data volumes while maintaining high performance.




unified data integration
integrate data from multiple sources and formats for unified insights, allowing users to easily explore all types of data.




complete analytics for every use case
address diverse business challenges with a full suite of tools, like visualizations, dashboards, and predictive analytics.
