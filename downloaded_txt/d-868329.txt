shimmer physical media still available!





back when shimmer formed in 2005, the standard method of distributing video content was via dvd, as physical media was king! we developed a worldwide fan base as a result, and realize that many of you have extensive collections of the dvds in the shimmer series from years past. be sure to keep your shimmer physical media collection going, as we continue to put out more unreleased footage during the coming months!
  
view the match lists below to purchase titles in the shimmer series which are currently in stock and available for immediate worldwide shipping, and use the buttons below each selection to place your order!

thank you for your support of shimmer!
