welcome!
are you a birth junkie? do you need to find your daily fix? if you’re anything like me, you can’t seem to get enough of anything related to birth: stories, photos, videos, etc… i’ve given birth many times myself, not to mention all the births i’ve attended as a doula, childbirth educator and midwife assistant, but i still find myself looking for more information. i just can’t get enough!
if you consider yourself a birth junkie, then you have come to the right place! be sure to check out the section of birth stories, from hospital and birth-center births to assisted and unassisted home births. we have a lot of information for you to browse. please consider sharing your own birth story for others to read.
