pet diabetes easy reference
list



educate yourself. 
you are responsible for the
daily care of your pet. 
diabetes in pets is not a death
sentence!


eventually, you must have a thorough enough understanding
of diabetes so that you can be confident in managing this 
disease instead of fearing it. if your regular
vet is unavailable when a minor question arises, you must have 
the confidence to make a decision based on your
understanding of diabetes and how it affects your pet.
