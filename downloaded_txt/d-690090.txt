black is the new white.
distributors have had little choice to offer their customers – white or white with a hint of colour. stella products have delivered with 4 modern colours suitable for any new project or refurbishment.

stella own and run high tech paper converting plants in europe and asia – so we’re able to ensure our quality is second to none – you’re buying directly from the source.
our modern dispensers are designed and manufactured in italy using the highest quality components. they’re light, strong and stylish – bring a touch of class to your washroom with stella.



defendo range
we also stock the defendo range – active silver ion antibacterial treated paper direct from our parent company in italy. if you’re serious about hygiene, this is a fantastic offer at a competitive price point.
