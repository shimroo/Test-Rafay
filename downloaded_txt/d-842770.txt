

lord nelson's gallery - gettysburg art gallery for wildlife, historical art, books and gifts




































































 
 
 


 
 
 


 
 
 


 
lord nelson's gallery offers a diverse collection of historical,  wildlife and nostalgic art, along with unique handcrafted gifts and jewelry. we  specialize in contemporary 18th century eastern woodland indian/frontier  art with an equally impressive collection of related books and fine accoutrements.

              the gallery has been in downtown gettysburg since 1990 and  shares the space with gettysburg custom framing. together we work to provide  you with quality art, gifts, framing needs and exceptional service.
