peter pan
february 20 – 23, 2025
funded in part by orlando health
follow peter pan on a daring journey as he whisks wendy to neverland. a ballet full of heart, humor and high-flying action, don’t miss this epic adventure through a fantastical world featuring the lost boys, tinker bell, and captain hook.
get tickets 

















balanchine, graham, pires
march 27 – 30, 2025
distinctly dynamic. boldly ballet. featuring george balanchine’s divertimento no. 15, martha graham’s maple leaf rag and alysa pires’ skyward, this captivating production combines three iconic ballets to one ingenious performance.
get tickets 

















the neverland ball
april 5, 2025
presented by frontline insurance
this year’s highly-anticipated event will take place at the four seasons resort orlando at walt disney world® resort where we continue the rich tradition of creating fully immersive and unforgettable experiences.
purchase your tickets now 

















giselle
may 1 – 4, 2025
nothing will stop her from protecting her true love – not even death itself. featuring gripping drama and graceful dance, giselle is a must-see performance of a romantic-era ballet.
