behind the movement















one of the oldest and largest dance programs in the country











renowned faculty with global experience in the profession











ample performance opportunities including a series of 7 professionally produced concerts annually alongside various other performance opportunities











research intensive university located in beautiful salt lake city, which boasts a vibrant arts community











innovative curricula offering breadth and depth in performance, choreography, screendance, pedagogy, theory, and music











beautiful state-of-the art facilities - fully staffed professional theatre with proscenium stage built for dance, black box theatre, fully staffed costume shop, 8 dance studios, dance clinic and pilates space, spacious locker rooms











opportunities to pursue minors and second majors
