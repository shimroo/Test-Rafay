welcome to the catholic student center at washington university!!















i’m fr. brian fallon, chaplain to the catholic student center at washington university. a special welcome to everyone, those of faith or no faith, seekers and searchers longing to find fulfillment in a faith-filled community.my hope is that you might find a spiritual home here and that we at the csc can help you to more experience god’s love. we strive to do this by providing opportunities to gather, study, share, serve and pray together. and we’ll have a lot of fun while we’re at it.so come visit us! if i personally can be helpful to you on your faith journey, don’t hesitate to reach out. i’m a follower of the lord jesus, and it’s my honor to serve you.fr. brian fallon, chaplain




    meet our team
  



























our missionthe catholic student center at washington university is a house of formation and a community of hospitality to students of any or no faith.  



our visionour dream is that every student who encounters the catholic student center community is known, loved, and cared for. we will walk with them in the way of christ to deepen their capacity for giving and receiving love and grow into compassionate, courageous leaders. we want students to go into the world healthier, happier, and holier.
