come as you are.
join us for passionate discipleship as we journey through 2 timothy, verse by verse.  learn how to live as disciples of christ, living with him as the center of our lives and entrusting this message to others who can pass it on as well.
  
st. rosalie - old church hall
thursdays at 9:30 am - 11:30 am
starting thursday, february 13 through april 10

make your gift online!
we are blessed to have parishioners who are willing stewards! sharing your financial gifts helps us to continue evangelizing and caring for the people in our community and beyond.  
however, even great stewards go on vacation or are away from time-to-time. online giving provides a safe, easy, and convenient way to share financial stewardship.
 
place your smartphone camera app over our code to access the link to faith direct. you may use your bank account or credit card to set up a recurring or one-time gift. 
 
many thanks for sharing our gifts with us!
