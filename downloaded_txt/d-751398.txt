the st. simons fishing center caters to anglers seeking a memorable fishing experience in the golden isles area. to meet the demand for fishing trips, captain mark noble established the golden isles charter fishing association, inc., comprising a team of expert captains specializing in fishing excursions around st. simons island, jekyll island, sea island, and cumberland island. our fishing center offers a diverse range of fishing opportunities, from inshore species like speckled trout and red drum to deep-sea expeditions near the navy towers for snapper, barracuda, amberjack, and more. with an abundance of species including trout, tripletail, tarpon, shark, and many others, there's something for every angler to enjoy. come and experience the thrill of fishing with us! hey there, are you ready for the best time of the year for speckled trout and red drum? they love the cooler water temperatures, so it's the perfect chance to plan a family fishing trip or a company outing. you'll make unforgettable memories with your loved ones that you'll cherish forever! give us a call at 912-638-7673 to book your trip.
