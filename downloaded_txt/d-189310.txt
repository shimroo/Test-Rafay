what we treat







what we treat





                                                    substance use and co-occurring disorders
                                                
the uncontrolled use of drugs or alcohol despite negative consequences, often accompanied by another mental condition.





                                                    anxiety disorders
                                                
the persistent, excessive and unrealistic worry about everyday things and situations that lasts at least six months.





                                                    treatment resistant disorders
                                                
the argent program - silver hill hospital’s comprehensive psychiatric assessment and evaluation service - provides new direction for patients suffering from complex psychiatric and substance abuse disorders that have previously failed to respond to treatment as desired.






mood disorders
the elevation or lowering of mood to a degree that interferes with everyday life, including depression and bipolar disorder.



                                                    depression
                                                    
persistent feelings of sadness, hopelessness and helplessness that interfere with thinking and daily activities.


                                                    bipolar disorder
                                                    
a multi-faceted mental illness that causes changes in a person's mood, energy and ability to function.





personality disorders
characterized by inflexibility of thought, intense emotions, erratic changes in mood, or harmful behaviors.



                                                    borderline personality disorder
                                                    
a multi-faceted condition that can include instability in mood, self-image and personal relationships.






                                            trauma and dissociative disorders
                                          
a treatment program designed for individuals with trauma and dissociation requiring an extended duration of care. this therapy-based program features a multimodal approach progressing from stabilization to processing to integration.







                                                    the neuropsychiatric program
                                                
carefully developed for over a decade of intensive care, this transformative experience successfully treats patients who experience persistent symptoms that are unresponsive to conventional care methods.




psychotic illnesses
characterized by an impaired relationship with reality, possibly including either hallucinations or delusions.



                                                   schizoaffective disorder
                                                    
the co-occurrence of reality distortion and mood disorder, with the features of reality distortion persisting.


                                                    schizophrenia
                                                    
characterized by a loss of appropriate emotional responses and the inability to organize thoughts and behaviors.


                                                    bipolar disorder
                                                    
characterized by changes in a person’s mood, energy and ability to function.

how we treat







how we treat




co-occurring disorders
when two or more illnesses occur at the same time in a patient, including substance use disorder.




dialectical behavior therapy
a proven therapy for a variety of mental health and substance use disorders that builds skills to regulate emotions, control behaviors and improve relationships.




comprehensive psychiatric evaluation
an extensive assessment program to help individuals gain a comprehensive and deep understanding of their whole self and a plan and recommendation for moving forward.






cognitive behavioral therapy
a psychotherapy that identifies problematic thought patterns and helps patients understand how those thoughts affect their feelings and behaviors.




additional therapeutic approaches
behavioral, medical and skill building therapies that increase options for successful treatment.




alternative and complementary therapies
a variety of medical and creative therapies designed to reinforce and support a patient’s treatment program.




ketamine treatment program
a powerful therapeutic intervention for individuals with treatment-resistant depression.






levels of care
patients move seamlessly between levels of treatment depending upon their need.



                                                    inpatient
                                                    
the most intensive form of care, indicated for a phase of illness that requires a great deal of management.


                                                    residential
                                                    
treatment on an extended basis for patients with stable symptoms who are proceeding with recovery.


                                                    intensive outpatient
                                                    
patients living at home while continuing treatment and skills development at silver hill.

treatment for adults
when addiction has an additional component, recovery can be challenging
while you may be seeking treatment for addiction, we find that there is often a psychiatric disorder that occurs along with it such as anxiety, depression or bipolar disorder. our treatment team has the expertise and experience to treat even the most complex presentations.

for clinicians
do you have a patient that could benefit from an environment of care?
from initial diagnosis through return to your care, we are here to partner with you in the successful treatment of your patients. silver hill hospital is renowned for its clinical expertise, proven treatments and nurturing support. see what some of our former patients along with their families have to say about their time at silver hill hospital.

the steward house at silver hill
a refuge of healing and recovery exclusively for executives and professionals
the steward house brings together the collective clinical strength and longstanding track record of excellence of venerable institutions, silver hill hospital and yale new haven health and yale medicine. our premier living program is designed exclusively for professional leaders and executives—the stewards of their organization—who are struggling with serious mental health or substance use problems. our state-of-the-art program allows patients to undergo the intensive therapy necessary for recovery in a private, safe environment.

financial assistance 
financial assistance is available at silver hill hospital 
silver hill hospital was founded on, and committed to the principle of helping others. our long-standing financial assistance fund ensures our services, inpatient care and our residential programs are made available to those who meet the clinical and financial criteria to receive treatment.
