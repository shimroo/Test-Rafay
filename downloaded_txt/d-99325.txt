usace rock island district awards first construction contract for brandon road interbasin project
the u.s. army corps of engineers, rock island district, recently awarded the first construction contract for the brandon road interbasin project. the $15.5 million contract covers site preparation and riverbed rock removal for the engineered channel.

news release











beaver island habitat rehabilitation and enhancement project ribbon cutting
a ribbon cutting ceremony marked completion of the beaver island habitat rehabilitation and enhancement project on oct. 1. it is the 63rd hrep completed as part of the upper mississippi river restoration program and is located in pool 14 of the upper mississippi river near clinton, iowa.

project website















brandon road interbasin project overview
a comprehensive overview of the brandon road interbasin project that uses satellite imagery and digitally rendered structures to provide a detailed visualization of the multi-layered technologies being designed to prevent the upstream movement of invasive carp on the illinois waterway.














new regulatory request system
usace is introducing its new regulatory request system, an online application portal that allows the public to submit permit applications and other information when requesting permission to dredge, fill or conduct activities in jurisdictional wetlands and waters of the u.s.
