welcome to soul ii soul’s official website, where we celebrate the rich legacy of our musical journey while keeping you in the loop with our latest projects.since our inception, soul ii soul has been a beacon of soulful rhythms and timeless melodies, capturing hearts across the globe. here, you'll find everything from our classic hits that defined an era to our newest releases that continue to push the boundaries.join us as we honour our roots, relive the iconic moments that shaped our path, and embrace the future with fresh sounds and exciting updates.

soul ii soulclub classics vol.onereleased for national album day - 19th oct 2024 club classics vol. one has firmly cemented itself in uk soul music history. originally released in 1989 and was the first album from soul ii soul. the album includes the uk #1 and us top 5 + grammy award winning hit back to life (however do you want me) as well as uk top 5 and us top 20 hit keep on movin’. released for national album day (19th oct). the release comes as a limited edition 140-gram picture disc album.available from soul ii soul bandcamp.
