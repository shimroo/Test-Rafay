the wharton initiative on financial policy and regulation (wifpr), established in 2021, sponsors research, organizes conferences and events, and supports faculty and students at the intersection of finance, law, and policy. headed by director itay goldstein and co-director christina skinner, and under the umbrella of the stevens center for innovation in finance, wifpr focuses on three main pillars: scholarly engagement, student support, and public policy.
