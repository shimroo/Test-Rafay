a nursing education for a better, healthier world.the latin phrase cura personalis, or “care of the person,” means a lot to us in the college of nursing. here, caring is a career, and through a creighton education, students enter the world with the diverse skills needed to become life-changing professionals. as one of the largest catholic health professions educators in the u.s., creighton offers first-rate nursing programs at the bachelor’s (bsn), master’s and doctoral levels—along with a variety of post-graduate aprn certificates.  we meet you wherever you are today—from starting your career to advancing through your profession.

simulation at creighton  
our state-of-the-art simulation facilities in phoenix and omaha help health science learners gain skills and confidence in a safe, encouraging and realistic environment. simulation at creighton is led by evidence-based research and provides inter- and intradisciplinary experiences, providing students with unparalleled opportunities to provide holistic, team-based care.
