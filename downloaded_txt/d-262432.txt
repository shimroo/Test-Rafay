welcome to the heart and soul of east texas...jacksonville!  the jacksonville chamber of commerce is a local organization of businesses whose goal is to further the interests of businesses. while we collaborate with government agencies and advocate for pro-business policies, we are an independent entity and not a government body. we're here to support the business community through networking, resources, and leadership on issues that impact our members.
