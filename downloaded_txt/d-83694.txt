bell to bring new manufacturing facility to denton county 


bell textron, inc., a global aerospace leader headquartered in fort worth, will expand its operations and open a new aircraft manufacturing facility in southwestern denton county to support the u.s. army’s future long range assault aircraft program.						read on for more details







denton county commissioners court  welcomes embraer expansion 


denton county commissioners court today approved a tax incentive agreement with embraer, which announced an expansion of its maintenance, repair, and overhaul services network to support e-jets fleets through a new service center at alliance airport.						read on for more details







nws releases surveys of tornado damage across region 


the national weather service (nws) began releasing survey assessments of several tornadoes, ranging from ef1 to ef3, that caused damage to homes, businesses, rv parks, manufactured homes, and marinas across montague, cooke, denton, and collin counties.						read on for more information







denton county assists families at rv park, other areas with recovery following tornado 


damage from a possible tornado was reported to the lake ray roberts marina and rvs on the sanger side of ray roberts lake. power outages, uprooted trees, and downed limbs were reported from west sanger across the lake along fm 455 into pilot point.						read on for more information







samantha taylor named as  office of emergency management director 


the denton county commissioners court officially named samantha taylor, a denton county native, as director of the office of emergency management.						read on for more details
