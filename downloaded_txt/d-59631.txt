city of grapevine – surplus park model cabins for sale 


the city of grapevine has made six used surplus park model cabins available for purchase from the vineyards campground & cabins. the cabins will be sold “as is” and must be transported to their final destination by the buyer within 14 days of purchase.			additional info...







fall proofing your home 


one of the most frequent calls for emergency medical services that the grapevine fire department receives is because someone has fallen. in january, nearly 11% of their total calls for service were due to someone falling. view these helpful tips...			additional info...







view your 2025 property tax statement 


view your property tax statement and pay your property taxes online.			additional info...







report street light outages to oncor 


if you have a street light outage to report, please do so at oncorstreetlight.com.  if you have general inquiries/service requests for oncor, please call 888.313.6862.  outages may be reported by calling 888.313.4747.			






speaking at a city council meeting 


the city of grapevine welcomes all who wish to be a part of their city government by participating in city council meetings. we do request all citizens, staff members, and city council members demonstrate respect and courtesy to all in attendance.						read on...







grapevine partners with 6 stones to provide home repairs for community revitalization 


the city of grapevine is now accepting applications for the 6 stones community powered revitalization (cpr) program. the cpr program coordinates corporate and community volunteers to provide exterior home repairs for quailed homeowners.						read on...







utility billing updates 


all utility billing forms are now digital.  access various features such as make a payment, apply for new service, disconnection of service, and more.			additional info...







interactive public parking map 


view the new interactive public parking map for historic downtown grapevine at parkgv.com.			additional info...







seeking critical information 


our grapevine fire department is seeking critical information (voluntarily) from all grapevine residents so they may better serve you and the members in your household in the event of an emergency.						read on...







kubota tractor corporation unveils their north american headquarters in grapevine, texas 


kubota tractor corporation unveiled its new north american headquarters building in grapevine with governor greg abbott, masatoshi kimata, president and representative director of the kubota group, along with state and local officials.						read on...
