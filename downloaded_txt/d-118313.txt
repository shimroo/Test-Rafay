explore with us
discover unique adventures and world-class entertainment nestled in the ozark mountains with silver dollar city parks & resorts in branson, missouri! from record-breaking coasters and festival entertainment at the 1880s theme park—silver dollar city, to water-filled thrills at white water and live performances with dinner and a cruise aboard the showboat branson belle, plus an escape to nature at silver dollar city’s campground…your next adventure awaits!

silver dollar city
founded atop a national landmark that is missouri’ s deepest cave, silver dollar city offers unique rides like record-breaking coasters, live entertainment, concerts and world-class festivals. also known as the home of american craftsmanship, silver dollar city features a crafts colony of over 100 demonstrating artisans and down-home country cooking in award-winning eateries.


discover more 




 

showboat branson belle
the 700-seat, 1890s-style paddle wheeler, showboat branson belle, is the nation’s largest ship ever built on a land-locked lake and america’s most entertaining lake adventure. on stage see live performances from talented singers and dancers backed by a 4-piece band, alongside comedy and magic. plus, every cruise on table rock lake features a delicious 3-course meal.


discover more 




 

silver dollar city campground
the silver dollar city campground features campsites, rv sites and trailer rentals, and hand-crafted rustic log cabins, all conveniently located just minutes from branson’s top attractions, table rock lake and the best outdoor activities the area has to offer. guests can enjoy numerous amenities including complimentary shuttle service to silver dollar city, a clubhouse stocked with gear, snacks, supplies and souvenirs, an air-conditioned lounge with free wi-fi, swimming pool and much more.


discover more 




 

white water
explore your own piece of paradise tucked into the ozark mountains with over 2 million gallons of water-filled thrills. voted a top 5 outdoor water park, discover 13-acres of water slides and waves for every age and thrill-seeking level. plus, private cabanas available to rent and tropical treats around every corner to refresh your family!


discover more 




 

silver dollar city resort
already under construction and set to open late 2026, the 262-room resort will feature never-before-seen vistas of the ozark mountains and table rock lake, along with unparalleled access to silver dollar city theme park. the new resort will also be located just a stone’s throw from the company’s other family attractions, including white water water park, showboat dinner cruises, pink jeep adventure tours and the silver dollar city campground.


learn more 




 

pink adventure tours
experience the magic of the ozarks like never before from the comfort of a custom pink jeep® wrangler. choose your family’s 4x4 adventure by time of day from a variety of tour options. wind past lakeside landmarks and ascend off-road trails to the summit of baird mountain while expert, local guides bring centuries of ozark mountain history and culture to life along the journey.
