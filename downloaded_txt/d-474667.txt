reston black focus

founded in 1969, reston black focus was formed to advocate for and express the black community’s interests in education, politics, and cultural activities. view their founding documents and photographs of their annual black arts festival in this virtual exhibit.




view exhibition











reston women pioneers

since the 1960s women have impacedt every aspect of reston's live, work, play lifestyle. learn about the pioneers who designed homes, established cultural institutions, and advocated for the interests of the reston community in development and government. 




view exhibition











lakeside pharmacy icons

an example of pop art, reston's goal for accessibility, and representative of reston notsalgia learn about the history behind the lakeside pharmacy icons and the story behind reston's preservation effort to keep them at lake anne plaza.




view exhibition
