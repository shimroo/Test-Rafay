 description


kemiko stone tone concrete stain creates a stunning decorative natural stone appearance for both interior and exterior surfaces. rich mottling effects, in various multi-hued colors, areachieved by chemically bonding specific metallic ions with the lime component present in the concrete. due to the reactive nature of the stain, your floor will transform into a unique, one-of-a-kind surface that will compliment many types of design.typical applications include residential, restaurants, wineries and brewery’s, hotels, educational environments, healthcare facilities, commercial retail floors, and many other applications that desire quality yet cost-effective color-enhanced floor appearance that simulates the look and feel of natural stone and marble.for the best application, kemiko stone tone concrete stain, is mixed with equal parts water and applied using an all-plastic, garden type pump-up sprayer.low voc and leed compliantlow odoruv resistant11 earth tone colorsmade in usacoverage: 400 sq. ft. per gallon when applying recommended two applications
