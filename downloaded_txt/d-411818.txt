about us
titus tools inc. takes pride in quality!
titus tools takes pride in making the best tools possible. we also appreciate quality when we see it. that is why we drive a fleet of spectatular vehicles, and our shops were built from the ground up to our specifications.
titus is creating new technology to improve the industry!
we have been creating new innovative products with our own internal development. we also assist new developers with their ideas from start to finish. our goal is to bring the oil & gas industries new technologies to improve well safety and save time.
the leadership
our ceo tracy k. (klotz) has over 40 years of experience in the oil industry, having started as a roughneck on an oilwell service rig, then working his way up to sales and marketing manager for a calgary based oil tool company. for over the past 20 years he has been president of titus tools inc. which he formed in june of 1997.
titus tools inc. is involved in the community
tracy k is actively involved in his local community and the global community. he has been involve in many charitable events and mission trips and has a passion for people and making a difference in the world. honor and integrity are his number one goals in business.
