nested in the woods, whippoorwill campground offers a restful camping experience just 3 miles outside of ocean city new jersey.whippoorwill campground offers the finest in resort accommodations in a wooded, seashore vacation setting. with campsites set in sheltering woods, whippoorwill is truly a relaxing and friendly option from among the mind-boggling selection of camping choices in the jersey shore region. our sites are level and wooded, all including a picnic table and a fire ring and most featuring a three-way hookup with cable tv. whippoorwill accommodates any type of camping unit, from a tent to a large motor home. or, perhaps you would like to settle into one of our cozy, air conditioned rental cabins.
