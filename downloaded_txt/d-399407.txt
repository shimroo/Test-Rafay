how does metal roofing compare to traditional roofing materials?
metal roofing is more durable, energy-efficient, and long-lasting compared to traditional roofing materials like asphalt shingles. it offers better protection against extreme weather conditions and requires minimal maintenance.

can metal roofing be installed over existing shingles?
yes, metal roofing can often be installed over existing shingles without removing them. this helps reduce installation time, labor costs, and environmental waste.

is metal roofing energy efficient?
absolutely. metal roofing reflects solar heat, which can reduce cooling costs by up to 25% in warm climates. it’s an ideal choice for improving your home’s energy efficiency.

how long does it take to install a metal roof?
installation time depends on the size and complexity of the project. however, a typical residential metal roof can usually be installed within 2-5 days by a professional team.
