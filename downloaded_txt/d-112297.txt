melodies of resilience: a concert honoring ukraine and its culture



3:00 pm – 4:00 pm15:00 – 16:003:00 pm – 4:00 pm



journey of hope united methodist church


eso presents: the chicago bandura ensemblethe week of february 24th marks eleven years since russia first started its war on ukraine and three years since the start of the full-scale invasion. with this concert we honor the resilience of the ukrainian people, commemorate all who have perished through the power of music, and thank the american people for their support.join us in standing with ukraine and celebrating its culture. experience the captivating sounds of the chicago bandura ensemble as they perform on the iconic ukrainian instrument, the bandura, in a tribute to ukraine, its people, and its enduring spirit.
