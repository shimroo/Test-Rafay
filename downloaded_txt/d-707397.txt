the massage clinic

our student massage therapy clinic provides clients from the region a welcoming, affordable, comfortable environment to receive a massage. students will work on clients to fulfill the requirements for their hand-on practicum




book now











continuing education

we offer continuing education programs to support the needs of licensed massage therapists; to stay up to date on research, trends and practices; to build professional community among practitioners in the area. from regular cpr and ethics classes (required for licensure) to workshops on neuromuscular therapy and connective tissue or back to the basics swedish posture and mechanics, we have something for everyone.
