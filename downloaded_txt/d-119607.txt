dsp motorsports
barrington, illinois
we welcome you to our new larger and upgraded facilities here in barrington il, and to our new company name, dsp motorsports.

since 1983, you have known us as des plaines honda, then dsp honda, and now dsp motorsports! we've upgraded the name of our company to accommodate the expansion of products we offer, (still) honda powersports products, kawasaki products and quality hisun side by sides and atvs.

for almost 40 years, our customers have trusted us with product selection, purchasing, financing, parts and accessories, and most importantly, keeping their machines in top shape with our factory trained service techs and saving big money with our exclusive vip membership programs.
 
we would welcome the opportunity to assist you with your next powersports purchase, parts or accessory needs, service requirements, or just stop in to chat with some of the most dedicated powersports enthusiasts in the chicago area. let us help you experience the fun and adventure that a quality honda, kawasaki, hisun and more can bring into your life!

at dsp motorsports, we value the opportunity to create a long-term relationship with our customers, and we do that by trying to exceed your expectations.  if you have a question or a curiosity, i'll bet that we have your answer.
