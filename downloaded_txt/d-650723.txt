welcome to brick town veterinary hospital!
since 1973, brick town veterinary hospital has provided quality veterinary care to families just like yours in the brick, new jersey area. we see dogs and cats and we are proud to serve as your family pet's trusted and primary source of veterinary health and wellness.
from routine vaccinations to our pet dental referral service, our first-class team of veterinarians and caring staff are here to provide your companion animal with the compassionate, quality care you have come to expect and deserve.
our veterinary hospital is located in ocean county, along the jersey shore, where we provide a friendly and welcoming environment that caters to the comprehensive needs of your pet.
we look forward to serving all of your pet's routine health and advanced veterinary needs! please call (732) 477-9440 today to make an appointment.
