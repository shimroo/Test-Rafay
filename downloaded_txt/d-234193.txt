learning


what makes an exceptional department? exceptional faculty. the faculty and staff in our department have expertise in a variety of disciplines and are enthusiastic to share their knowledge and experiences.






















global engagement


the via department of civil & environmental engineering (cee) is committed to developing well-rounded, globally competent and conscience engineers. cee students need professional global skills that enable them to understand their discipline from broad and inclusive points of view.  













cee structural engineers at work








about us


the charles e. via, jr. department of civil and environmental engineering is ranked among the top 10 accredited departments in the united states. the mission of our department is to provide a high-quality learning environment and create opportunities for students to develop skills to advance engineering principles and practices.
