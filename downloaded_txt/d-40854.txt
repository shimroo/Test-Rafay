stampa tessile
advanced textile printing
the inkjet textile division of konica minolta italia is a cutting-edge technology center, able to offer you the most advanced digital systems for printing on fabric. the konica minolta inkjet textile center in bregnano (co) is the only showroom in the world that presents three of the most advanced systems for the textile market: the pioneering nassenger sp-1, nassenger 10 and nassenger 8.konica minolta is a major player in the global market with its successful models, by high-performance, multi-nozzle printheads developed in-house; its printers are known for high efficiency and improved print quality.in addition to cutting-edge technologies, the textile division avails itself of a service team able to support customers from all over europe.
