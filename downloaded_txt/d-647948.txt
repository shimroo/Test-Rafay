frozen dough specialists®



since 1908 it’s been a matter of pride at guttenplan’s to make sure all of our products meet the highest standards for quality. today at our manufacturing facility in middletown, new jersey, we produce more than 175 different products, putting the finest ingredients into formulations that are tested and proven, utilizing our exclusive superior frozen dough process.
