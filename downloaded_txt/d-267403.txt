ravine flyer ii
top 10 wood coaster

since 2009, ravine flyer ii has been recognized by amusement today magazine’s golden ticket awards as a top ten wood coaster. with two exhilarating drops of 120ft and 105ft, ravine flyer ii has the largest drops of any wood coaster in pennsylvania! this unique, white-knuckle experience is also the only coaster in north america to travel across a 4-lane highway. did we also mention the amazing views of presque isle and lake erie?

view attraction














rocket blast
get ready for takeoff!

experience the rush of our newest attraction, rocket blast – a thrilling combination of water slide and roller coaster. this first of its kind attraction in the region features exhilarating uphill launches, wall-hugging flying saucer turns, and a twisted helix finale all while twisting over and under water world attractions. you won’t want to miss the power of rocket blast which was the winner of amusement today’s golden ticket award for best new water park ride in 2023!
