parke county, indiana



welcometo parke county, home to indiana’s largest festival, the parke county covered bridge festival™ and “the covered bridge capital of the world” with our 31 covered bridges all found in a rustic, charming setting. each covered bridge comes with its own unique past. start planning your visit here.
