welcome to charles river wheelers bike club
experience the beauty of boston and beyond with the charles river wheelers bike club. our dedicated riders enjoy weekly adventures, friendly camaraderie, and exciting social events all year round.
don't miss out on the fun! explore our ride calendar and join our esteemed club today for exclusive membership benefits:

hundreds of exhilarating rides annually
special discounts on crw century rides
full access to crw’s ride with gps club account
discover new routes with our extensive ride library
and so much more…

embark on a journey with us and become a part of the long-standing charles river wheelers legacy!
