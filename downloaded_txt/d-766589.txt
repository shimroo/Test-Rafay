central tile: a flooring contractor serving kalamazoo since 1936



central tile & terrazzo co., inc is a full service flooring contractor specialized in the design and installation of tile, carpet, terrazzo, wood flooring, vinyl flooring, and granite countertop products. central tile & terrazzo is located in kalamazoo, michigan and has been providing high quality service in southwest michigan since 1936.
