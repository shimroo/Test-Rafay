welcome to
toledo harley-davidson®
toledo harley-davidson (formerly signature harley-davidson) is your local destination for everything you crave as a harley enthusiast. whether you ride, or love someone who does, toledo harley-davidson is the area’s premier destination for riders. our huge selection of new and used harley-davidson motorcycles is only the beginning. you will find everything a rider needs including parts and accessories, service, riding gear, and casual apparel. 

as our dealership begins a new chapter as toledo harley-davidson, our mission is to fulfill dreams of personal expression and freedom through a commitment to superior customer experience and unmatched enthusiasm for our riders. we know riders because we are riders. our commitment to you is to ensure integrity, knowledge, and teamwork are at the core of what we do each day.
