something about
who we are
since 1977

city tire & auto is a 100 percent locally owned and operated business.
we are committed to providing top quality automotive services across newfoundland & labrador. with roots in many communities province-wide, it is important that all customers receive the best automotive care available.
we have 10 locations throughout the province. from st. john’s to corner brook, our team of nearly 100 highly-trained employees are eager to serve their community.
