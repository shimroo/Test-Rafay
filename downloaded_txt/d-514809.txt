weather alert


...cold weather advisory now in effect until noon cst today...
...extreme cold warning remains in effect from midnight tonight to
10 am cst tuesday...

* what...for the cold weather advisory, very cold wind chills as low
as 32 below zero. for the extreme cold warning, dangerously cold
wind chills as low as 42 below zero expected.

* where...price, ashland, bayfield, burnett, douglas, iron, sawyer,
and washburn counties. this includes the tribal lands of the
northwestern area of the lac du flambeau band, the st. croix band
in burnett county, the lac courte oreilles reservation, the red
cliff band, and the bad river reservation. other locations
including the apostle islands national lakeshore and madeline
island.

* when...for the cold weather advisory, until noon cst today. for
the extreme cold warning, from midnight tonight to 10 am cst
tuesday.

* impacts...the dangerously cold wind chills as low as 40 below zero
could cause frostbite on exposed skin in as little as 10 minutes.

* additional details...wind chills may briefly decrease as low as 35
to 40 degrees below zero for a couple hours early monday morning.

precautionary/preparedness actions...

dress in layers including a hat, face mask, and gloves if you must
go outside.

use caution while traveling outside. wear appropriate clothing, a
hat, and gloves.
