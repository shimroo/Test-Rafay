tustin outdoor fitness programs 





what we offer




    group fitness
  













experience functional fitness classes for all levels in the park, rain or shine. meet your goals in a fun and safe environment with group fitness.




    yoga in the park
  













gain strength, flexibility and reduce stress with yoga in the park!




    run club
  













run club works on form and building speed for runners of all levels. from 5k to marathon training, this group will get you to the finish line!




    video analysis
  




    personal training
  



customized and comprehensive fitness plans that get you to your fitness goals. focusing on your overall fitness foundation to support your athletic endeavors. 



video analysis helps you train safely in the short term for long-term results. we analyze habitual biomechanics and potential imbalances to correct form and prevent injury.
