quality ag parts

founded in 1887 by two brothers, herschel manufacturing quickly became a leader in the harvesting, hay and mowing sickle section industry by producing quality sickle heads, sickles, and guards.
for over 130 years herschel has been an innovator in the cutting parts industry and introduced the chrome plated section (1950), tiger jaw®, super 7® sections and the tiger sharktm section.
we are proud to still make our popular cutting products at our factory in indianola, iowa (ia). shop herschel parts' wide selection of agriculture replacement parts for tillage, hay and mowing, harvesting, farm supplies, planter parts !
don’t forget to check our sales for popular parts like bale spears, rakes, rotary cutter parts, disc mower parts, flails, guards, tillage chisels, nh3 parts, sweeps, drill discs, and disc blades.
