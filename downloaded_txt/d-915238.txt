our engineering & manufacturing team

technical expertise
 
– over thirty three years of in-house development of proprietary electronics hardware, firmware, embedded software and enclosure technology along with in-house knowledge gives us the ability to respond quickly to all requests.

– with our modular design approach our team can add features and specifications as needed to a library of existing product configurations that have already been sold, tested and proven reliable in the field.
