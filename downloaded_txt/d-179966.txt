department of music



in a relatively short time, the uab department of music has distinguished itself as one of the finest centers for educating young musicians in the southeast. faculty and students perform in the acclaimed alys stephens performing arts center (with its four performing venues) and around the globe. we recently became the first program in the state of alabama to be designated an all-steinway school, making the department one of only 73 schools worldwide so named by piano maker steinway & sons. the university of alabama at birmingham is accredited by the national association of schools of musicopens in a new window.
