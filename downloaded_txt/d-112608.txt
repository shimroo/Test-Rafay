hi, i'm megan.
i'm the human behind hipstitch academy. i’m passionate about making stuff, teaching others to create & helping people embrace alternatives to fast fashion. i do this by teaching people how to sew, encouraging thrifting,  mending & altering clothes. i’ve also created a line of alternative fashion pieces & goodies made from items that already exist in the world.
