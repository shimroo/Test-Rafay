who is this app for?pregnancy prayers is not just for pregnant women. this app is for family, friends and anyone who feels led to pray for babies. if you are a pregnant mother you can grow your prayer team by getting your friends and family to pray over your baby with you using the pregnancy prayers app. we will alert you via email when people download the app to pray for your bundle of joy!













how the app started...pregnancy prayers was written by a mother pregnant with her second child who was searching for the right words to pray over her baby. when she couldn't find anything to show her how to pray, she decided to write pregnancy prayers to lead herself and others in daily prayer for their babies. as she studied the weekly developments, she sought out scriptures that reflected the bible's message about what was developing in her baby that week.

     




      

 "i will not worry about anything;instead, i choose to pray and seek god's wisdom."-pregnancy prayers app
