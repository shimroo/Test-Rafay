the paris connection experience
at paris connection our mission is to provide you with the highest level of personalized service and expertise in a warm and friendly environment. we are the exclusive san francisco retailer for justin alexander signature and justin alexander collections, as well as sincerity, and lillian west bridal gown collections, all complemented by our unique selection of european accessories. we are happy to assist 'last minute brides' with last minute appointments, and can also provide consultation on accessories. in addition, we offer full-service seamstress fittings to ensure you look your best.
whether your style is contemporary or vintage, we understand your needs and work with you to fulfill your wedding gown dreams!
