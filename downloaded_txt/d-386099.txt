mobile and card solutions 





mobile access control, which lets people use their smartphone to access car parks, buildings and more, can increase convenience and security.
mobile and cards solutions


























license plate recognition solutions





license plate recognition is a convenient, cost-effective solution for secure vehicle access control. anpr cameras are great for granting temporary or incidental vehicle access for employees or visitors.
license plate recognition solutions






















semi-active rfid solutions





readers using rfid technology can be used to identify vehicles and drivers quickly and automatically from a distance. they provide high levels of security, even in harsh environments.
semi-active rfid solutions
















passive uhf solutions





by using uhf rfid technology, vehicles and drivers can be quickly identified via a tag or a card. it’s a cost-effective way to increase security.
passive uhf solutions
