custom labels

custom static clings
static cling vinyl has no adhesive, but will cling to most flat, smooth surfaces such as glass, glossy plastic and glossy painted materials.our static clings are either screen printed or full color digitally printed depending on the final use or application.



  

custom banners
our premium banners are very durable and can be displayed indoors or outside. we have a huge selection of trade show banner displays from table runners to elaborate backdrops and a wide selection of custom printed pop-up canopies.





  

point-of-purchase
designery specializes in extra large window decals from 250 to 2,880 square inches in full color for stunning images on white vinyl, clear polyester, clear removable vinyl or clear static cling materials. we offer a full line of pop products including floor graphics.



  

trade show signs
we can supply just about everything for your next trade show, convention or outdoor event. with over a hundred varieties of retractable displays, banner displays, trade show pop-ups, sail signs, custom printed beverage coolers and pennant strings.
