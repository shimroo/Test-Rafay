sks bottle and packaging is a container and packaging supplier, consultant, and designer. we specialize in providing top-quality plastic bottles, glass bottles, plastic jars, glass jars, metal containers, and closures to meet your packaging needs. with our extensive online catalog, you'll find a wide range of containers and closures, curated to offer the best selection of products at competitive prices.
	
choose from over 6,000 top quality packaging options, including frosted, amber and clear glass bottles and jars, as well as clear, amber, blue, purple, green, black, white and natural plastic bottles and jars. each container has an assortment of colors and closure styles to choose from, allowing you to customize your packaging solution. we take pride in our global customer base, serving businesses of all sizes and individuals alike. whether you're a large corporation or a passionate crafter, we have you covered. our services cater to everyone, from small repacks for individuals and crafters to bulk wholesale containers and closures with quantity discounts for larger customers. sks bottle and packaging also offers various added services including decorating, custom mold design and production, and more. if you have a unique vision for your product and your required quantity qualifies, we can bring it to life through our custom container mold options. our team is ready to assist you throughout the entire process, from conception to creation, ensuring that your packaging stands out from the competition.
	
we invite you to explore our expansive collection of glass, metal and plastic containers. if you have any comments or questions, please don't hesitate to contact us. we look forward to serving you further and hope you enjoy our website!
