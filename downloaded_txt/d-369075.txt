helen pensanti, m.d.



helen pensanti m.d. began her practice with traditional gynecology but in her fourth year of private practice she became alarmed at the serious side effects her patients experienced from synthetic hormones. dr. pensanti refused to write another prescription and diligently searched for an alternative. she has since helped thousand of patients through traditional medicine and alternative health products. she continues to practice medicine and share her passion for health and products that work.dr. pensanti’s patients have always been at the forefront of her work and will continue to be the reason she has dedicated over 40 years of her life to the practice of medicine.
