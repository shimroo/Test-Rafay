long way up wheels

























custom harley-davidson livewire™ bikes





harley-davidson’s brand new direction towards sustainability made them the perfect partner for ewan and charley’s 13,000 mile trip from argentina to los angeles. ewan and charley rode prototype livewire™ electric motorcycles for this season’s adventure, the first electric motorcycle to be produced by a major motorcycle manufacturer. 




    more
  

 










electric adventure vehicles from rivian





the long way up team kept up on the three month trip from argentina to la in two of rivian’s all-electric, 2.6 tonne pickup trucks. the electric vehicles’ unique features allowed us to travel anywhere, regardless of terrain, temperature or distance.
