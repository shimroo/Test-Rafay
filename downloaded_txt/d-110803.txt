lake buchanan’s premiere ecological and scenic tour
vanishing texas cruises is a nature based tourist attraction located in central texas on lake buchanan and the colorado river. we’ve been operating on the lake since 1981. explore the scenic wilderness of the texas hill country’s highland lakes region on one of our tours. cruises offer excellent opportunities for viewing regional and migrating birds including the wintering bald eagles, native texas wildlife, cascading waterfalls and towering cliffs. 



book now





























year-round kayak and canoe rentals
in addition to our cruises, we offer kayak and canoe rentals year-round. take a paddle just inside beaver creek cove to view birds such as osprey, blue herons, white egrets, hawks and ducks or paddle out to garrett island and enjoy the natural wildlife and a picnic lunch on the beach.
