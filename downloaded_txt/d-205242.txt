campus rec newsjoin us for spark week, february 24-28it’s our third annual spark week, february 24-28. inspired from the book spark: the revolutionary new science of exercise and the brain by dr. john ratey, campus rec will host activities and events the entire week to promote the importance of physical and mental health! from our interactive art piece all week at the student union, pilates and mindfulness workshop on landis green, and our end-of-week- celebration, spark fest, we’ve got a lot in store for you! visit our spark week page to learn more.
register this month for intramural sports, outdoor pursuitssoftball, sand volleyball, and canoe battleship are among our intramural sports set to begin soon.  check out registration dates and details on the intramural sports page for all of this spring’s offerings.  and, over a dozen outdoor adventure trips in march and april will begin accepting registrations on tuesday, february 25th at 10:00 am.  visit our outdoor pursuits page for all the details.
limited facility hours for spring breakcampus rec will operate limited hours at its facilities during spring break, march 7-16.  the fmc will be closed on friday, march 7th, then open 11:00 am until 5:00 pm each weekday during the break.  the leach center closes early on friday, march 7th (2:00 pm), then is closed through march 16.  outdoor suports facilities are open daylight hours only, weather permitting.  the fsu lakefront park will be closed march 7-16 as well.  visit our facility hours page for complete details.
try a group fitness or f45 class this weekspinning, barre, pilates, cardio dance and more… check out the spring group fitness schedule and take a free class this week. get your spot early, register on rec connect 25 hours prior to the class time.  elevate your workout with f45 team training, take a single class for $15 or get an unlimited pass through spring break or all spring semester. see the f45 schedule for details.
use the myfsu mobile app to find fsu rec app features on your phonego mobile with campus rec.  get access to your id barcode for entry, class registration, and more on the fsu rec page in the myfsu mobile app. yes, simply click on the campus rec circle spear logo in the university’s myfsu mobile app and login with your fsu credentials. download the myfsu app and get help with account management and registration tips on our fsu rec in myfsu mobile help page.
