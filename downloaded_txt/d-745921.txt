brittina (elizabeth house alumna) shares the legacy she hopes to leave for her family and generations to come. 




    watch more videos
  


























founded in 1994, elizabeth house takes a trauma-informed approach to break the cycles of homelessness, abuse, and suffering. through shelter and lifelong connection, elizabeth house empowers pregnant and parenting women in need.the holistic residential program has served over 900 women, children, and grandchildren, providing essential supports such as shelter, case management, health and wellness, scholarship funding, mental health services and so much more. learn more



our mission
















our programs




    learn more
  



elizabeth house is the only organization in l.a. and orange counties that welcomes pregnant mothers and their children under the age of 5 into the residential program. our holistic shelter program serves the physical, emotional, mental, and economic of pregnant women experiencing homelessness.elizabeth house continues to provide lifetime support services for alumni women and their children after completing the program.
