 we value
 1. care: we have a responsibility to create an environment where our diverse community feels comfortable to ask questions, team members listen to understand, and we communicate with respect and honesty valuing each person’s cultural and identity differences.
2. guidance: we provide accurate and timely information to our community while presenting options to allow them to make their best decisions. the work we do is designed to provide collaborative support and present our community with a seamless service experience.
3. access: we provide support, resources and data to facilitate higher education opportunities for our community. when appropriate, we encourage use of on-demand service options.
4. efficiency: our team members are inspired by opportunities to improve operations. we employ best practices and encourage innovation to maximize our systems and technology.
