weather alert


...winter weather advisory in effect from 5 pm this afternoon to
midnight cst tonight...

* what...light freezing rain and drizzle expected. total ice
accumulations of a few hundredths of an inch possible.

* where...portions of north texas from the dfw metroplex northward.

* when...from 5 pm this afternoon to midnight cst tonight.

* impacts...very slippery sidewalks as well as slick roads, bridges
and overpasses are possible. the hazardous conditions could impact
the tuesday evening commute.

precautionary/preparedness actions...

slow down and use caution while traveling. the latest road
conditions can be found at drivetexas.org.

weather alert


...extreme cold warning remains in effect from 9 pm tuesday to 9 am
cst thursday...

* what...dangerously cold wind chills as low as 10 below zero in
north texas and 5 above zero in central texas expected.

* where...portions of north and central texas.

* when...from 9 pm tuesday to 9 am cst thursday.

* impacts...the dangerously cold wind chills between 10 below zero
and 5 above zero could result in hypothermia or frostbite on
exposed skin if precautions are not taken.

precautionary/preparedness actions...

dress in layers including a hat, face mask, and gloves if you must
go outside.

keep pets indoors as much as possible.

make sure outdoor animals have a warm, dry shelter, food, and
unfrozen water.

make frequent checks on vulnerable family, friends, and neighbors.
ensure portable heaters are used correctly. do not use generators or
grills inside.
