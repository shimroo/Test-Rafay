 welcome to 3 disciplines racing

 at 3 disciplines racing, we’ve been producing world-class triathlons, duathlons, and endurance events for over 25 years. with more than 1,300 events hosted in 25 states and over 300,000 athletes served, we are one of the most experienced event management companies in the nation.
whether you’re an athlete looking for your next challenge, a volunteer ready to lend a hand, or someone looking to elevate their event, 3 disciplines delivers safety, precision, and unforgettable experiences.
with an emphasis on safety, fun, and precision, we’ve built a reputation for delivering some of the best events in the country.
what you can expect from 3 disciplines
professionally managed races, unmatched attention to detail, and a community-driven, athlete first experience.
