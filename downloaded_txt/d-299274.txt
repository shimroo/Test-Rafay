we have been involved in the south padre island real estate market since 1978 and started furcron property management and furcron,inc., realtors® in 1987. we specialize in sales, vacation rentals and property management of complexes. we pride ourselves in having the cleanest and best maintained vacation rental units on the island. most of our units are non-smoking and most have a second tv! we hope we can help to make your stay on south padre island the best it can be with units on the beach, bay, inner island or anywhere you want to be!  feel free to contact us any time.
