water & sewer utility notice

sparta township’s preschool program will be opening registration soon.				read on...




solid waste & recycling notice 

2025 solid waste utility bills are being mailed				read on...




sparta institute international conference 2026 

sparta, new jersey, was selected by the sparta institute of greece as the site of an international conference of municipalities named after the greek city.  together with our greek sister city, sparta nj is forming a joint committee to organize the event.				read on...




sparta open space and recreation plan public survey 

the land conservancy of new jersey is updating sparta township's open space and recreation plan. in order to identify goals and recommendation for the plan, we are gathering input from the community.				read on...




calling all sparta families with roots in american history!
