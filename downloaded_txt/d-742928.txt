   welcome to beaverwood farm 


   established 1987


   a craftsman is only as good as his tools and beaverwood farm uses the best. designed with carriages in mind, the 98 acre farm consists of a range of facilities. riding enthusiasts will love beaverwood's training-level cross-country course, dressage ring and stadium jumping course. scenic trails, quiet roads and extensive carriage storage will appeal to pleasure drivers.
