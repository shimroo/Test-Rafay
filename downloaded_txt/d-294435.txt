we’re flipping the script on the traditional gala and bringing you a night of inspiration, impact and a ton of fun. get ready to connect with passionate supporters, influential community leaders and like-minded philanthropists who are all united in one mission: ending breast cancer for good. 
at party in pink, you’ll get the inside scoop on the incredible work komen is doing and why our mission is more urgent than ever. from komen’s patient care center, which provides vital support to anyone facing breast cancer, to our network of navigators helping thousands of people each year – you’ll see firsthand how your support is making a difference.
