village of lombard newsstay up-to-datefor the latest updates and breaking news, follow us on social media or visit our social media feed below.
 






  
 
 















police chief wirsing announces retirement 


police chief tom wirsing has announced his retirement, effective march 3rd. he will be succeeded by deputy chief joe grage.						read on...







village offices open on presidents' day 


village offices will be open for regular business hours on monday, february 17. garbage and recycling collection will not be delayed by the holiday.						read on...







lombard police super bowl enforcement results 


lombard police announce the results of their recent super bowl enforcement campaign.						read on...







2025 national community survey for lombard 


the village is conducting a resident survey to gather feedback on village services and overall quality of life. residents selected are strongly encouraged to complete the survey, as their feedback will directly influence village planning efforts.						read on...







lombard blood drives collect 70 pints 


this national blood donor month, the village of lombard is thanking donors for their life-saving blood donations at the village's winter blood drive and other local community blood drives.						read on...







protect yourself from scams 


the lombard police department is warning residents to stay alert after recent cases of scams affecting members of our community.						read on...







limit time outdoors during severe cold 


when dangerously cold temperatures strike, residents are urged to limit time outdoors for themselves and their pets, as well as to check in on friends and neighbors who may need assistance.						read on...







prevent carbon monoxide poisoning 


to help prevent co poisoning, the lombard fire department recommends installing co alarms on every level of the home if your home has fuel-burning appliances or an attached garage.						read on...







lombard awarded achievement for excellence in financial reporting 


the village of lombard has been awarded achievement for excellence in financial reporting for its fiscal year 2023 annual comprehensive financial report.						read on...







winter tree pruning locations and schedule 


the village's winter tree pruning program is underway and will continue through april 2025.						read on...







prevent thefts with the 9 p.m. routine 


as we welcome the new year, the lombard police department is encouraging residents to adopt a nightly 9 p.m. routine to help reduce thefts and vehicle break-ins.						read on...







2025 water and sewer rates & online payments 


in 2025, the village's water and sewer rate will increase by $0.64 per 1,000 gallons of water.						read on...







lombard police share internet sale safety tips 


lombard police encourage residents to use the police station as an exchange location to safely complete internet sale transactions.						read on...







seasonal parking of recreational vehicles 


as winter approaches, the village of lombard reminds residents of important guidelines for parking recreational vehicles (rvs).						read on...
