everything youth hockey should be




the chicago hawks and phantoms are dedicated to the athletic development, personal growth and sportsmanship of youth hockey players in illinois. we encourage the development of our players as teammates, as well as individuals, teaching them valuable lessons that can be applied long after they leave the rink. as the largest youth hockey clubs in the state of illinois, the chicago hawks hockey club is committed to the ongoing development and promotion of competitive youth amateur hockey.
