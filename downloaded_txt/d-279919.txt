as 
                  a former ice capader, pro instructor and events director for 
                  rollerblade in the new york 
                  area i realized the public needed a website dedicated to finding 
                  trained profesional skaters
                  who were not only qualified but also insured. with that in mind, 
                  i started skatinglessons.com. 
                  we are listing only professionals with skating and insurance 
                  credentials. it is impossible for us
                  to check on the validity and the current status of the information 
                  gathered on each professional listed, but all the information 
                  provided can be checked out before you hire them. on the links 
                  page we have "hot" links to all the organizations pros belong 
                  to. a quick call to any organization will help you identify 
                  a member in good standing.
