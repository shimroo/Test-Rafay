sunday worship servicesundays, 10:00 amwelcome to montecito covenant church!montecito covenant church is a people of god, called and committed to jesus christ as savior and lord, living by faith under the authority of the word of god through the power of the holy spirit. our purpose is to love and glorify god by worshiping him in spirit and truth; growing in christ-like maturity; caring for one another in love; sharing the good news of christ’s love with the world in word and deed.we invite anyone and everyone to attend our 10:00 am sunday gatherings as we worship god through song, teaching and prayer.
