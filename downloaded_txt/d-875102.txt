riverfest 2025 will be held on sunday, june 1st in beautiful donahue memorial park on the banks of the hudson river. this will be our 26th year! festival hours are from 11 am - 6 pm.catch a shuttle bus from cornwall central middle school or one of several clearly marked bus stops along the route, or walk down to the park. vehicular access to the park is restricted to pass holders and holders of valid handicap placards or plates and will controlled by cohpd at the top of dock hill road . parking is extremely limited.we're looking forward to greeting old and new friends for a fantastic day on the banks of the magnificent hudson river.we are currently accepting vendor applications for riverfest 2025. click here for a vendor application.feel free to email chairman@river-fest.com for more information. please remember that all committee members are volunteers. we will reply as soon as is practical.we look forward to seeing everyone in donahue park on june 1st!
