we fearlessly clear the path for overlooked students to get into the best colleges with the best scholarships.we envision a world where genius, leadership, and excellence are cultivated in the most overlooked students in our community.














expanding minds · igniting potential










the problem

there are the high-performing but underserved students in our community who are overwhelmed by the lack of support in our education system. too many are trudging the path to college alone.




see more of the problem











our solution

by giving driven yet overlooked students the long-term support, compassionate guidance, and academic rigor they need to be competitive, we show them just how far their intellectual engines can take them.




see more of our solution




























since 2017, we have seen students secure amazing futures when they commit to our 3-year academic program. 


























75% of our graduates 
have been accepted into top tier universities.  


























100% of our graduates 
are offered admissions to 4-year colleges with 70% achieving full-ride scholarship support.  others ensure 70-90% scholarship coverage.  
















path to college fellows apply at double the national average rate
