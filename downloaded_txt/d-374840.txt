welcome to terwilliger plazavibrant 62+ senior living in sw portlandterwilliger plaza is a single-site, nonprofit continuing care retirement community (ccrc), offering a continuum of care that includes independent living, assisted living with 24/7 on-site licensed nurses, and memory support services designed to meet the evolving needs of members.































what sets us apartfrom lifelong learners to hikers, musicians to art collectors, the environmentally conscious to the civically minded—we are a community of active, progressive and engaged individuals. 



























we are committednonprofitestablished in 1962, we are a stand-alone, independent, nonprofit, 501(c)(3) organization. 



























we are involved self-governance boardthe majority of our board is comprised of resident members who affect policy and change right where they live.



























we are endorsed accreditedwe are the only sagecare platinum credentialed ccrc in oregon and one of only two with a carf accreditation. 














person-centered care we offer continuing care programs and personalized services for our members in independent living, assisted living and those needing higher levels of support. our approach prioritizes maintaining an integrated connection with the terwilliger plaza community, regardless of the level of care required.
