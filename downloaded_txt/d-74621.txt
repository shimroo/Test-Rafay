since our founding in 1973, in a city where storefront theaters and global icons thrive side by side, chicago opera theater has grown from a grassroots, community-based company to a national leader in an increasingly vibrant, diverse, and forward-looking art form.


















          meet our new general director...
        











meet our new general director...










        meet our new general director... 
      




































we believe opera is a living art form.chicago  opera theater is a company laser-focused on living its values of:expanding the tradition of opera as a living art form and supporting artists who are defining the future of opera;deepening appreciation for the breadth and depth of operatic storytelling by producing works from throughout history that are new to chicago audiences;increasing access to opera for audiences of all ages;and following through on commitments to equity and access – behind the scenes, on the stage, and in the audience.   





































































chicago opera theater is a proud member of opera america.pro bono employment representation provided by faegre drinker biddle & reath llp.
