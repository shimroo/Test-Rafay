about us


monmouth mobile home park can give people a sense of community that people desire and have a place to call “home”. located in the beautiful and family-friendly south brunswick, new jersey, which is midway between princeton and new brunswick, we welcome everyone with open arms as this is the place where families and residents, are able to live and prosper together.
