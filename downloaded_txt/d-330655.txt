durant main street historic downtown



welcome to the durant main street historic district, where charm meets community! our mission as a main street program is all about revitalizing historic downtown durant through preservation, economic growth, and fostering a vibrant sense of togetherness. this bustling hub is the heart of our community, brimming with rich culture and arts that attract both locals and visitors alike. stroll through our unique shops, savor delicious dinning, and enjoy lively entertainment. durant main street is where locally owned businesses thrive, history is cherished, and friends and neighbors come together to live their best lives. join us in celebrating all that makes durant special!
