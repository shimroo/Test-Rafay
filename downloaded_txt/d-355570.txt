as the charlotte eagles, we are



playing a game to change the world.



























we are passionate about soccer and people. across our programs, we offer each of our players the opportunity to develop as an athlete at their unique ability level. our coaches are highly skilled in their field and strive to intentionally know each player and encourage them in ways that go far beyond the game of soccer.

our mission




charlotte eagles is a gospel-centered ministry that introduces people to jesus christ, teaches them to follow him, and empowers them as leaders through the global environment of soccer.



our vision




our vision is to see lives transformed through teams that motivate people to follow christ.
