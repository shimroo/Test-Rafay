coastal yacht servicessince 1977, we have been serving satisfied customers in the southeastern united states.  coastal yacht services is a fully mobile marine service and maintenance company, serving both recreational and commercial clients.  we have extensive experience with all types of vessels, from small fishing boats to multi-million dollar, custom built motor yachts.our services include, but are not limited to: a/c service and maintenance, head service, washing and waxing, gel coat repair, detailing, teak and varnish, captain services, mechanical, bottom cleaning, interior cleaning, and more.we also sell and service products from great companies like dometic, sea recovery, lifeline batteries and rydlyme.we are fully licensed and insured, and we are big enough to serve you but small enough to know you!
