the upeace experience

created in pursuance of un general assembly resolution 35/55 in 1980, upeace has been training leaders for peace for the past four decades. it is a unique global academic institution with over 6,000 alumni hailing from more than 120 nations working for peace on a global scale.

what makes upeace unique?

upeace is the only institution within the united nations system mandated exclusively for peace education. upeace's unique attributes lie in its specialized un mandate, global community, interdisciplinary curriculum, commitment to practical learning, and emphasis on sustainable development. find our more about the people that make up our unique and diverse community.
