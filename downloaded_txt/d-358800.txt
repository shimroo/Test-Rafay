student abc















          what we do
      



at student accounting, billing, and cashier services—sometimes referred to as the bursar's office, the campus business office, or the cashier's office—we help you resolve any financial issues that you might encounter with your student account. we oversee charges and credits related to tuition, fees, housing, dining, payments, financial aid credits, tuition remission, and student health insurance plan (ship) premiums for each of your semesters at rutgers. view our annual finance reports to learn about the  highlights and process improvements taking place in student abc.




  learn more 













          contact us
      



one stop is an integrated and coordinated cross-functional service in the areas of financial aid, student accounts, and registration. if you are a current student rutgers university student, please log in to submit your inquiry.




  submit your inquiry 













          online term bill (otb)
      



review the student account checklist, view your term bill, adjust your credits, and pay any outstanding balances to ensure that you have completed all necessary steps before your semester begins.
