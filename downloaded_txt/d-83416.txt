our mission 



getting medicare right 



the medicare rights center works to ensure access to affordable health care for older adults and people with disabilities through counseling and advocacy, educational programs, and public policy initiatives. 











 counseling & advocacy  our expert counselors provide clear answers and step-by-step assistance to take the guesswork out of health insurance decision-making. learn more 







 education  our clear, unbiased resources are trusted by millions of consumers, caregivers, and professionals to provide important medicare information. learn more
