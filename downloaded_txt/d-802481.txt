welcome to indian run

  










 

indian run golf club is a strategic design with a good blend of holes. the course features reachable par 5’s, a variety of par 4’s and testing par 3’s. green-side and fairway bunkers combined with several water hazards provide the challenge to this 18-hole championship course. with four sets of tees ranging from 5,028 yards to 6,808 yards, indian run is fun for players of all abilities.



































tour
take a tour of the course. we’re happy to show you around.


























pro shop
indian run golf club has a full service pro shop for all your golf needs. we stock a variety of balls, towels, tees, gloves and apparel. 


























banquets
our elegant banquet room can accommodate up to 200 guests at any time of the year, with indian run’s experienced staff taking care of the details.
