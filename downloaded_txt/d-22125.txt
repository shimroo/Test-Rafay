  about ramsey solutions




        
    progress is possible
    

  

hitting rock bottom can ignite a transformation, and dave ramsey’s story is proof. after losing everything to debt, he rebuilt his life by learning to manage money the right way. for over 30 years, he’s taught others to do the same through talk shows, live events, books, courses, coaching and more. today, ramsey solutions continues to help people rebuild their finances with our foundational approach—small steps leading to big wins.
