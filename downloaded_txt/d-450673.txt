press release: village of cottage grove board votes to place referendum on april ballot 


the village board voted in favor of placing a referendum question on the april 1 ballot. residents will be asked whether or not to support an increase in the annual tax levy to cover the cost of constructing and operating the village’s first library.						read on...







public information meeting for police station - february 27, 5:30 p.m. 


there will be a public information meeting regarding the new police station on thursday, february 27, from 5:30 p.m. to 7 p.m. at village hall.						read on...







deputy parks & recreation director trista taylor awarded wpra young professional of the year 


trista taylor, deputy parks & recreation director, was awarded the wisconsin park and recreation association’s (wpra) young professional of the year award.						read on...







service provider change - pellitteri for trash & recycling collection 


the village will have a new trash and recycling collection provider (pellitteri) for residential properties effective january 1, 2025.						read on...







where do i put my trash and recycling carts? 


trash and recycling carts must be curbside by 7 a.m. on your collection day. carts should be placed at the end of your driveway or on the grass terrace.						read on...







alternate side parking: december 1 - march 15 


alternate side parking regulations will be in effect on village streets from december 1 to march 15.						read on...







dog licenses fee schedule 


the village board approved an updated dog license fee schedule at their meeting on november 18.  dog licenses are now available to purchase at village hall.						read on...
