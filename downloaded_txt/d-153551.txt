carlos fiesta welcomes you to baja mexico for november 2010! baja expo is the best place to plan your next trip to mexico's spectacular baja peninsula. and fall 2010 is a great time to visit! average daytime temperatures in august run from the 80's to the 90's and the ocean temperatures are absolutely perfect! the food and the people are warm, and fun awaits you at every turn.  
carlos offers you over 200 unique pages of information on baja. and he continues to visit baja every month and type away at his keyboard every night (after his day job) to bring you valuable information for baja california and baja california sur, mexico.
baja expo includes hundreds of links to other baja-related web sites to provide baja fans with even more information and photographs of america's favorite south of the border playground....baja!
