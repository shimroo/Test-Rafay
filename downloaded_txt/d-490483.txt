putnam county fish & game association 
welcome to the putnam county fish & game association. 
founded in 1932, putnam county fish & game association is a private, non-profit, sporting organization based in putnam county, ny. 

putnam county fish & game association has a rifle range, pistol range, trap range, air gun ranges, archery practice areas, a beautiful 30 station 3d archery course with well maintained trails and targets, and seasonal hunting.
