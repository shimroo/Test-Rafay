kealia resort


area: south maui

on one of maui's most spectacular white-sand beaches, oceanfront kealia resort is a peaceful retreat in sunny south maui. the kealia resort is on the northernmost point of kihei, making it less crowded and also convenient to access activities island-wide. you can contact destination maui's on-site office at kealia at 1-800-265-0686 for personal assistance.


 






























































each kealia vacation rental unit is furnished in an upscale, tropical decor and has all the conveniences of home. located just steps from the beach, each unit features amazing views, a private lanai, a fully equipped kitchen, air conditioning, a washer and dryer, and free wifi. choose either a studio, one-bedroom suite or two-bedroom suite, with either a full or partial ocean view or deluxe oceanfront accommodations.  
 

looking for electric charging stations during your stay? find public ev charging closest to this resort and other island locations using this link: https://chargehub.com/map/#/en/map
a longer minimum stay may be required for some holiday periods.  we may accept direct bookings for less than the minimum stay if shorter openings appear on our calendar.  contact us at vacationrentals@destinationmaui.net for assistance.
