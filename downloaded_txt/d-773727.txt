the legacy continues
the lutgert companies has been creating new experiences in luxury living for its clients and business partners for more than 60 years. the company’s commitment to quality, elegance and service remains as solid today as it was in 1964 — when raymond l. lutgert broke ground on the development of a 760-acre parcel bordering a pristine piece of naples, florida, beachfront. over the past six decades, the lutgert companies has set the standard for the creative and environmentally responsible development of coastal and mountain properties. the lutgert family’s vision and passion for excellence runs three generations deep. that vision and passion continue to produce extraordinary experiences.
