the hotel indigo® london kensington is situated in the heart of the royal borough of kensington and chelsea, our london hotel blends beautifully into this boutique neighbourhood. we’re also located close to the earl’s court tube station, placing all of london’s destinations and major transport hubs like heathrow (lhr) and london city airports (lcy) within easy reach.

faqs for hotel indigo london - kensington



does hotel indigo london - kensington have a pool?


no, hotel indigo london - kensington does not have a pool.




can i bring my pet to hotel indigo london - kensington?


pets are welcome at hotel indigo london - kensington. there is a pet deposit per stay of 35 gbp . our pet policy: please advise the hotel upon confirmation if you are planning to bring your pet with you. for our detailed pet policy please refer to our website or contact the hotel directly. please note certain pets are not permitted to stay at our hotel.




can i cancel my booking at hotel indigo london - kensington for a full refund?


always check the hotel's cancellation policy before booking a rate. room cancellation is subject to rate booked at time of reservation and cancellation policy varies by rate.




is there a complimentary shuttle available at hotel indigo london - kensington?


no, there is not a complimentary local area shuttle available at hotel indigo london - kensington.




what are the check-in and check-out times for hotel indigo london - kensington?


check-in at hotel indigo london - kensington is from 3:00 pm, and check-out time is 11:00 am. contact the hotel directly for options available for early check-in or late check-out.




does hotel indigo london - kensington offer free wi-fi?


yes, the hotel indigo london - kensington offers free wifi!
