stevens marine
since 1971
stevens marine is an authorized alumaweld, kingfisher, smokercraft, sunchaser, mercury outboard, and ez loader trailer dealership serving the greater portland, oregon area with two locations to serve you. we are proud to carry a large selection of new and pre-owned inventory. when you are ready to purchase a new boat, used boat, outboard motor or boat trailer, our friendly and knowledgeable sales, financing, service and parts staff are prepared to make sure your experience is outstanding, from assisting you while you're making your choice to ongoing maintenance and customization needs. at stevens marine, we value the opportunity to create a long-term relationship with our customers, and we do that by giving you the best customer service available.
