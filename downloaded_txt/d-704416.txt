									a legacy. a destination.								



royal palm place: where 14 acres of prime real estate host a vibrant mix of over 20 top-tier eateries, chic boutiques, and upscale living spaces. nestled amidst serene landscapes and stunning architecture, it’s the epitome of a refined, laid-back lifestyle.

shopping
&  fine art 



designer boutiques to fine jewelers, art galleries, and more cater to every occasion and everyday needs at royal palm place; explore the array of shopping options featuring alene too, swimland, vianna brasil, devon’s diamonds & decor, california closets, whitaker studio & leon fine art gallery, among others, offering sparkling treasures and incomparable style. 






view all shopping & fine art→ 





























dining 



									from al fresco cafes to vibrant chop houses and bistros, take a tour of the global flavors of royal palm place, where you’ll find many of your favorite destinations including chops lobster bar, lemongrass, gary racks farmhouse kitchen, another broken egg cafe, 3 natives, sushi by boo, the standard, cote france cafe, yakitori sake house, hijinks sports bar, and so much more, each offering a unique culinary experience.								






view all dining→ 





































specialty 



									as a lifestyle destination, royal palm place features an array of fitness studios, wellness spas, podcast studios, and luxury real estate offices, including pod populi, douglas elliman, compass realty, edward jones investments, spin district, ultimate cryo, and more, each contributing to a diverse and upscale experience.								






view all specialty→ 




































live where you love. love where you live. 



the residences of royal palm place, set within the heart of downtown boca raton’s meandering streets and walking paths, feature 1, 2, and 3 bedroom floorplans along with two-story penthouses; this includes amenities like a rooftop sparkling pool and sundeck, concierge service, a residents’ fitness studio, and more, making royal palm place the ultimate address to live, work, and play.
