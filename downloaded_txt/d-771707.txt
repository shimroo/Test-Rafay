     welcome to classic honda
orlando-based classic honda is a part of the holler-classic automotive group, which has been delivering quality dealership services in the area since 1938. our customer base extends beyond the orlando community into the surrounding cities of apopka, winter garden, windermere, and clermont.


about us
at classic honda, we base our business around the principles of our founder, william e. "bill" holler. to him, the most important focus of the business was always customer relationships. as a group, we are proud to say that we have upheld this culture over the years and our motto is still "never forget your customer — never let your customer forget you!"
when classic honda opened its doors in orlando in 1978, we were fortunate to build our business on such a solid foundation. we are happy to say that we have succeeded in creating a professional and pleasant environment for our customers. we also strive to show our gratitude for the support we have received from the various communities where we do business. we support a long list of charities, which include the orlando fire department, international community school, children's miracle network, clean the world, and many more.


excellent service
our honda certified service technicians are committed to treating every customer as a vip. they are trained to assist you with a wide range of services, whether your vehicle needs routine maintenance or complex mechanical repairs. we offer a fully stocked honda parts center, which carries genuine honda parts, and a knowledgeable parts team who will gladly answer any queries you may have regarding replacement parts.
be sure to keep an eye out for our service and parts specials, as we offer huge discounts. to view a list of our current specials, please select the service & parts tab above.


exciting inventory
our inventory includes a wide range of new honda vehicles as well as pre-owned cars of various makes and models. our top sellers include the honda civic, which is available in either a sedan or hatchback body style. featuring zesty handling, a comfortable interior, advanced standard driver-assist features, and two energetic four-cylinder engine options, the civic offers a lot of bang for your buck.
if you have a large family, you can't go wrong with the honda odyssey. this minivan is a super practical and family-friendly vehicle. some of the main features of the odyssey include versatile seating configurations, plenty of safety features, a robust engine, and innovative technology. to view a comprehensive list of the vehicles we offer, please visit our new and pre-owned inventory pages where you can browse through our inventory at your leisure.


if you need professional advice regarding the financing of your vehicle, you can speak to our helpful sales team who will assist you in choosing the best plan for your unique needs. in short, here at classic honda, we strive to provide our customers with a comprehensive, professional, and friendly dealership experience. we look forward to meeting you and building a trusting and long-lasting relationship. plus, as part of the holler-classic family of dealerships we have access to a wide range of inventory, including additional inventory at our sister honda orlando store.
