dawg celebrates 40th anniversary!
















in 1985 a group of agility enthusiasts formed one of the first agility clubs in the us.  our mission is to promote the sport through training, competitions, and demonstrations. 


























uki trial feb 28, 2025 usdaa trial march 1-2, 2025



we are accepting entries for both shows at this time.   entries close february 21, 2025.   dave grubel will be judging all three days.  we hope to see you at myers park!   














dawg hosts veterans parade at akc trial on march 8



please come join us for dawg’s veterans parade honoring our senior dog agility competitors.  the deadline for the parade entry is february 21, 2025.   you can find all the details about the event and how to enter on our akc march 7-9 show premium.  the show  will be held at the myers park & event center!
