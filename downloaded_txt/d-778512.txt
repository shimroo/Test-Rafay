        welcome to wellton      

located 29 miles east of yuma, arizona, the town of wellton is a center for business, services, recreation, and a leisurely lifestyle. we are home to 3,197 full-time residents and countless winter and retired residents. surrounded by the gila mountains and local farms, the gorgeous sunrises each morning, the warmth, and sun-filled days, it is no wonder that folks have been coming to wellton to rest and enjoy the town’s quiet setting. you’ll discover our friendly town is a safe haven for you and your family.
