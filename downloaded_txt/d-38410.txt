economic development







consider the city of lancaster if you are growing or moving your business.


the city of lancaster offers creative solutions for those looking to build and grow a business. we have lower cost of doing business than many municipalities in the region, and we also have the great infrastructure already in place. there are several industrial parks in lancaster that are shovel ready for new businesses.
