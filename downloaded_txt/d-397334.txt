supporting young women and their children since 1982




    explore our services
  



























jessie's - the june callwood centre for young women is a drop-in multi-service resource centre dedicated to supporting pregnant and parenting individuals who are 21 years old or under at intake. our centre provides services to these young women and their children until they reach the age of 24.




    about us
  



























services















counselling & case management
every participant accessing jessie’s services is assigned a counsellor who is available for individual counselling, information, and advocacy.

















education
we support participants in meeting their educational needs by earning compulsory and elective high school credits in a full-day semestered program onsite at jessie’s.

















food program
we provide meals to all participants and their children daily as well as a monthly food connect program.

















health program
we provide health counselling, referrals, and a weekly health clinic with a family doctor.

















housing
we assist those who are homeless or at risk for homelessness to access and maintain housing.

















parenting & groups
our parenting and prenatal groups are a wonderful place to come and discuss infant and child development, and develop parenting skills.

















practical support
we provide free clothes, diapers and other supplies.

















youth leadership
jessie’s trains young parents to share their experiences with various audiences to dispel myths about being a young parent and provide education about healthy sexuality.
