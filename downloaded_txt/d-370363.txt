st. george's episcopal church welcomes you





the mission of st. george's episcopal church is to worship god and share our faith through word, action, and service.sunday services10:30 amholy eucharist, rite ii(both in person and online)see our worship page.    parish office hours:  monday: noon - 6 pm, tuesday: 9 am - 1 pm, wednesday: 2 pm - 6 pm, thursday: noon - 6 pm                                                                 call ahead if needed: 512-454-2523


   



come visit us!4301 north i-35 austin, tx 78722from i-35 northbound: from the lower deck of i-35, take exit 327a toward airport / 51st st. move to the far right lane on the frontage road, and turn right onto either ardenwood or crestwood roads.from i-35 southbound:take exit 237a toward airport / 38th-1/2 street. continue on the south i-35 frontage road in the center lane. make a u-turn just after the train tracks (before the traffic light) to get on the northbound frontage road. move to the far right lane and turn right onto either ardenwood or crestwood roads. visitor parking is available on ardenwood in the first driveway on the left.st. george's campus:the wright-giles house (church office) and kleberg hall are located on ardenwood rd. st. george's episcopal school is located on crestwood rd. the church can be accessed from either side.
