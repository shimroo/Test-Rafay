hanover powersports
serving hanover, new jersey
at hanover powersports, we understand your time is valuable. our entire staff of trained professionals are here to help you with all your powersports needs from parts, accessories and apparel to our award winning service department and factory trained technicians offering excellent service. we've been making it easy to do business since 1982 and we'll make it easy for you.

our dealership carries a full line of yamaha, ktm, honda®, stihl®, ariens, ssr, husqvarna, yamaha bicycles & pre-owned inventory. we are conveniently located in the heart of morris county, new jersey, minutes from morristown, livingston, madison, chatham, florham park, and millburn, right by the home depot plaza. come visit us today at 210 route 10 west in east hanover, nj, contact us online or call (973) 428-1735 to learn more about all hanover powersports can offer!
