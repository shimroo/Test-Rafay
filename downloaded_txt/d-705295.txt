we organise school trips around the world...
here at nst, we have over 55 years of experience in designing school trips for primary and secondary school pupils. we provide school trips to over 100 destinations throughout the uk, europe, and beyond including america.
we can tailor your educational school trips to suit any subject, including history, english, modern foreign languages, art & design and geography. our team understands the importance of designing school trips that are not only educational but also engaging and exciting for your school class. 
the benefits of booking a school trip with nst 
our educational travel experts know that organising school travel, accommodation and activities for an entire class can be a challenging experience for teachers. whatever your focus, we can plan bespoke, curriculum-linked school trip itineraries to meet the needs of your class. our priority is to make your school trip an unforgettable experience for both pupils and teachers alike. 
having worked with thousands of primary and secondary school groups over the years, our team at nst relies on this experience to support schools before, during and after their school trip - and take the hassle out of the teacher's hands.
get in touch today to find out how we could help you plan and design an engaging, educational school trip of a lifetime.
