crawfish pre-sale 


as part of our mardi gras event, spicy tails will be have 2lb. crawfish plates with corn and potatoes for $20. pre-orders close on february 27 at midnight! a limited number of plates will be available the day of the event at an increased price of $25.			additional info...







mardi gras cajun celebration 


join us for a family-friendly mardi gras celebration of cajun culture. enjoy a lively and festive atmosphere with traditional music, delicious cuisine and crafts and entertainment. laissez les bons temps rouler!			additional info...







make a difference in rowlett – apply for city boards by february 28! 


want to make a difference? the board of adjustment and senior advisory board are seeking dedicated residents to help guide our community’s future. volunteer a few hours each month and make an impact! applications are open through february 28—apply today!			additional info...







city of rowlett announces deborah sorensen as new city secretary 


with nearly two decades of municipal government experience, ms. sorensen brings a wealth of knowledge and expertise to the community.						read on...







2025 special events - save the dates 


save the dates for our 2025 calendar, jam packed with events for all!						read on...







city of rowlett announces renissa wade as a new assistant city manager 


city of rowlett is excited to announce renissa m. wade as the new assistant city manager. beginning january 21, 2025, ms. wade will oversee various city departments, contribute to strategic planning, and work closely with the city’s leadership team.			additional info...







downtown rowlett adopted strategic plan 


after much anticipation in october 2024, city council adopted and finalized the downtown strategic plan.			additional info...

notice of adopted 2024 tax rate 


the city of rowlett adopted a tax rate that will raise more taxes for maintenance and operations than last year's tax rate. the tax rate will be raised by 2.24% and will raise taxes for maintenance and operations on a $100,000 home by approximately $0.00.						read on...







annual drinking water quality report 


rowlett's water is extensively tested to ensure the system consistently maintains a “superior” rating. this is the highest rating for water quality issued by the state of texas!			additional info...

rowlett vision & mission
vision
a well-planned lakeside community of quality neighborhoods, distinctive amenities, diverse employment, and cultural charm.
rowlett: the place to live, work, and play.

mission
the city of rowlett provides innovative citizen-centered services that exceed the expectations of our residents, businesses, and guests.
our purpose is to serve.



 




a focus on economic developmentsituated just 30 miles east of dallas on the shores of lake ray hubbard, rowlett is attractive to businesses of all types. with a major turnpike, light rail system, and access to award-winning healthcare, rowlett is on the water and on the move. read on...
