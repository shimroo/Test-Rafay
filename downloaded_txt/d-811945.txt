sang-froid definition
 
sang-froidcomes from the latin sanguis frigidus (cold blood) and means cool under pressure. sang-froid is both our motto and our goal.

mission
 
 our mission the sang-froid riding club is a non-profitorganization dedicated to the sport of motorcycle riding and racing. headquartered in portland, oregon, sfrc was formed to promote skillful motorcycle riding and to bring creative and alternative motorcycle events to the portland community.
