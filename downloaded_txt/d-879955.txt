pfeiffer & son, ltd. is a houston-based, family-owned electrical contractor serving industrial, commercial and government customers in the greater houston area. we have a long and successful history of providing a unique combination of general and specialized electrical services. for five generations, our family has built a reputation of quality work, fairness, reliability, and honesty.  this approach to business has been valued by our customers and has enabled the company to grow and prosper to what it is today.
