    who we are

edgar morris in southern california is a skin care manufacturer offering scientifically advanced skin care products, specifically formulated for people of color. mr. edgar morris, our founder, understood what was needed when it came to skin care. he had suffered from severe acne and skin discoloration. but, thanks to the skin care wisdom he received in france, he not only revived his soft and radiant skin, but also established his famous beverly hills skin care clinic aptly named after him in the 1980’s. we have been in business for more than 40 years and with his specially formulated products are ready to help you, whatever your skin care problems are.
