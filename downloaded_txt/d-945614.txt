industries we serve

select agents delivered to researchers
with a strong network of biological substance trained, hazmat endorsed cdl drivers – we can pick up your category a and either fly or drive it to the receiving laboratory. completely transparent every step of the way. we can even provide barcode labels to go with your paperwork to be scanned when we pick up and when we deliver. usa couriers is cdc and dot vetted and approved. we maintain a solid security plan for every shipment – to ensure the safety of the transport.

forgotten passport conveyed to passenger
you aren’t alone and the solution is only one call away. our same day delivery courier will pick up your passport from your home or designated address and fly it to you via our first flight out™ service. you may pick it up at the airport or we will deliver it to your hotel. this is a door to door, same day delivery service for packages and more – so you can get on your way quickly.

medical devices hand carried 
in the balance between health and illness, usa couriers puts time on your side. real time solutions for life. couriers are hipaa and privacy trained, utilizing the us veterans administrations training portal. they are also biological transport and clean up trained. select agent couriers are hazmat endorsed cdl drivers with extensive biohazard transport and clean up training. every day we move medication, surgical supplies, biological specimens and associated materials. we understand the meaning of rush and the life and death balance many patients and their medical providers find themselves in. usa couriers offers a variety of same day, nationwide delivery services for medical and pharmaceutical professionals.

entertainment transported
regardless of whether you are a hollywood production company or a talented hopeful, a sports team or advertising agency –important, one-of-a kind deliveries receive white glove treatment, door-to-door.  the challenges of production location and delivery windows are quickly evaluated – creating the best itinerary and mode of transportation available via land, commercial air or charter.

government 
cage: 3hc17 certifications:  wbe, wosb time sensitive shipments transported daily using our fleet of cars, vans, trucks, first flight out™ service, charter flights and on board couriers. secure chain of custody is built into every move with transparency and integrity. being the solution our government’s needs is very important to us, even going so far as to be cdc and dot vetted & approved for the transport of select agents. so while we help you meet your percentages, we hope to keep your business because we provide dedication, value and great service.

financial and bank couriers 
financial and bank couriers deliver highly valuable documents and packages. these selected same day couriers are fully vetted because their responsibilities are greater than a normal courier. couriers make sure materials are picked up and delivered to the right locations, and into the right hands, including id checks if requested. services for banks, credit unions, brokerage firms and loan centers including delivery of deposits, inter-office communications and daily activity to processing centers as well as scheduled cash deliveries. our same day couriers are unarmed and dressed in plain clothes in order to avoid drawing attention.

convention shows 
usa couriers specializes in trade show shipping and exhibition booth logistics. to move your materials and products to trade shows and conventions on time and safely, then we’ll deliver it to the next show or back to your offices – anywhere in the us, puerto rico, us virgin islands and canada. so you can focus on marketing, sales and perfecting your presentation, we’ll handle delivering your booth, banners and displays to the show. we’ll assist you on the best shipping method, proper case markings, documentation and all other crucial information necessary to ensure trouble-free movement of cargo. 

rescue logistics
regardless of what needs saving, once supplied with a tracking or pro number, we dispatch the right courier to rescue the product or medication to store and deliver it to the final recipient as scheduled. we can even schedule it. if it needs refrigeration our team will ensure it refrigerated or temp checked. we recommend your shipment carry a data logger or temperature gauge so our team can ensure the temps remain perfect. we call it rescue logistics. everything from temperature controlled medication to pos equipment – the correct vehicle and professional courier will retrieve the package from will call deliver it.
america’s #1 nationwide courier service
need an important package delivered anywhere within the united states, on time and within your budget? when you need the best national courier service on the market, you can rely on the usa couriers to get the job done. we specialize in a range of unusual, sensitive packages, from forgotten passports to bio-hazardous materials, and strive to become the usa’s most trusted national delivery service, all of them near you.
same day nationwide deliveries by air or land
when it comes to outstanding delivery, our top priorities are timely and convenient service. we will drive right to your home, workplace, or institution to pick up your packages personally. after that, we will use the best possible method with our delivery services to ensure that your package gets to its intended destination within a day. for smaller packages up to 90 pounds, our first-flight out services allow us to pinpoint the earliest available flight from a range of commercial and cargo flights. this allows your package to be delivered from the east coast to the west coast, or anywhere in between, within eight hours.
for passenger flights, or cargo requiring an additional level of care, we provide special charter flights with an emphasis of security, safety, and discretion. if air travel isn’t an option, we provide local and cross-country trucking services with a fleet of specialized vehicles, ensuring successful delivery of each and every one of your items. usa couriers also offers urgent medical and select agent deliveries.
