welcome to soundview property managment, llc 
soundview property management, llc is a full-service property management company based in the picturesque city of gig harbor, washington and servicing the surrounding pierce county areas, as well as port orchard, kitsap county. 
founded in 2012, and with 40 years of combined experience managing, renting, and maintaining properties, we are well-versed in the art of this continuously more complicated and sophisticated field. as a boutique property management company, we provide a more personalized, hands-on approach to our clients. 
please take a few moments to look over our website.  to learn more about our services visit the "more" tab, above. if you're looking for a home, visit the "rentals" tab to see what properties are currently available for lease.  we look forward to hearing from you soon!
