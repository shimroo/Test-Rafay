we rock grand nationals in chokecherry canyon
the unique and diverse terrain in and around the farmington area is a playground for outdoor enthusiasts from all over.







lake farmington 
lake farmington offers tons of outdoor experiences. come to fish or enjoy the water on a non-motorized watercraft, swim at  the beach or get some exercise exploring the shoreline.







four corners balloon rally
over 20 balloons soared through the skies when the four corners balloon rally returned to farmington september 2022.








pinon hills golf course
piñon hills is a legend in the desert golf landscape. what makes us legendary? simply, we are affordable, friendly, fun to play, and hassle free…plus it’s easy to make a tee time and find a place to stay in farmington, new mexico. 









retire farmington 
farmington, nm is a place of outdoor adventure, vitality & active living.
