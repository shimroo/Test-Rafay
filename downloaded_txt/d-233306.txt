help shape the future of our community by sharing your thoughts! join us in determining how to best use our community development block grant (cdbg) funds for the coming years by taking a survey or attending a meeting.			additional info...







rialto pipeline rehabilitation project – shutdown coming soon 


starting february 12th through the 25th, the metropolitan water district will conduct essential maintenance on the rialto pipeline. while service will not be disrupted, we urge chino residents to conserve water.						read on...







street sweeping 


chino’s street sweeping program is managed by waste management, which oversees the contractor, sweeping corp of america. this program aims to keep chino’s streets clean and reduce debris, improving our community’s cleanliness and environmental health....			additional info...
