project canoelearn more





through the power of the outdoors, we help youth transform their lives.we offer therapeutic and educational outdoor programming to ontario youth ages 13 to 18 in both wilderness and urban environments.our focus is on youth who might otherwise miss out on the experience of outdoor adventure as a result of systemic or mental health barriers they face in their lives.project canoe is a registered charity established in 1977 and has served over 4000 youth.
