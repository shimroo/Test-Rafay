passionate about providing resources that help people connect with jesus 







































welcome to authentic media!we are passionate about providing resources that help people connect with jesus. we recognise that we are all god’s children and want to reach people wherever they are on their faith journey. our range of books and bibles for both adults and children are designed to bring people closer to god over a whole lifetime, helping them to mature in their faith. we want people to grow spiritually, connect with christ and be open to what god may be doing in their lives.it is our prayer that our titles will inspire, challenge and encourage faith, bring hope where there is despair, reassurance where there is doubt, and an experience of god’s love, peace and joy.our titles are available from all good bookshops and online retailers.
