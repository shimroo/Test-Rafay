victory christian schools



since 1963, thousands of sacramento-area students have developed understanding, character, and spiritual life at victory.























































































at victory christian schools, we are an evangelical, non-denominational christian school system dedicated to educating tk-12 students.however, this description only scratches the surface. our faculty’s compassion and expertise, our coaches’ dedication to athletic excellence, and the christ-centered conversations that come to life in our classrooms and around our campus truly set us apart.this is what education looks like when the gospel is our top priority, and it meets and exceeds national learning standards.discover the victory christian schools difference for you and your student.

what to expect
