get help


















 
dotcio's it services and support center (itssc) provides a centralized location for self-serve help through the use of an active knowledge base, featuring commonly requested content and documentation of known issues, as well as an online ticketing system to submit a request for it assistance.
in addition, the help desk team is available during help desk posted hours for phone and in-person support. the help desk is located on the main floor of the voorhees computing center.
