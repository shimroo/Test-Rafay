welcome to the official website of clinton city, utah. clinton is a vibrant utah community of over 23,500 residents who enjoy a high quality of life in a safe, family oriented rural atmosphere.  clinton is located just north of salt lake city and southwest of ogden; nestled between the wasatch mountains and the great salt lake.  clinton city offers the finest in police and fire protection, justice court, professional office staff,  highly regarded recreation programs, and a hard-working public works department.emergency notification signup/updateclinton city code
