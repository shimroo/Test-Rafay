celebrating 50 years of inspiring innovators.welcome to the bakken museum




    learn more
  














about the bakken museumsince 1975, the bakken museum has been inspiring a passion for innovation by exploring the potential for science, technology, and the humanities to make the world a better place. located on the west shore of bde maka ska in minneapolis, the museum features interactive exhibits for all ages and exceptional stem education programs. make movies in the spark exhibit, meet the mother of science fiction in mary & her monster, and test out some of ben franklin's favorite experiments in the electricity party room.

featured exhibits













spark





play music on the bakkenspiel, make stop-motion movies at the animation station, and connect with human stories in personal prostheses 2.0.




    learn more
  













ben franklin's electricity party





benjamin franklin’s curiosity led him to explore many novel ideas and devices of his time. join the party and try out some electric parlor tricks!




    learn more
  













mary & her monster





who is mary shelley and why is her novel important today? explore the science, technology, and culture that influenced the mother of science fiction.




    learn more
