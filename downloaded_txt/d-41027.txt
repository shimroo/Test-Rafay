village of grayslake heritage center and museum 


the village of grayslake heritage center and museum is a state of the art history museum that features multiple exhibition galleries, a classroom, a community room, archives and collections storage.  visit the heritage center website today!			heritage center and museum website







grayslake village center 


visit the new grayslake village center website. grayslake's village center is located in the heart of the village. it includes the historic downtown with its antique lighting, historic storefronts, and small businesses with a customer-friendly atmosphere.			grayslake village center website







esper a. petersen foundation family aquatic center 


located in the heart of grayslake, the grayslake aquatic center sits nestled on five and a half acres located near il route 83, making it accessible in minutes from surrounding communities. for more information,  visit the aquatic center website today!			aquatic center website







bike trails 


grayslake was one of the first communities in the chicago region to create a comprehensive development plan for trails, bike paths, and sidewalks..			learn more







community garden 


the grayslake community garden is located on the east side of library lane across from the library lane senior residences and grayslake public library. garden plots are available on a first-come, first-served basis.			learn more
