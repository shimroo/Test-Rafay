welcome to trinity beach state schoolat trinity beach we offer a distinctive learning environment. we believe in engaging children in learning experiences that foster their independence, creativity and the connectedness needed in today’s world and our global future. we recognise the individuality and varied abilities of our students and provide every opportunity for them to find their passion, take risks, strive to improve, succeed and learn from any set-backs. set on a 7 hectare property in the heart of trinity beach, our school offers students access to a range of specialist academic programs and extension co-curricular activities – including sport, instrumental music and language studies.
our commitment is to create a learning environment in which each child can thrive and become an  independent, creative and connected learner. it is through these solid foundations that unlimited potentials will emerge. trinity beach state school provides an education that prepares our students for life. we are a  community of learners, committed to innovation and lead by a united vision for professional excellence.
