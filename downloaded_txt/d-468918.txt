transformative mental health services




our low-cost therapy services are a lifeline to anyone in need. for over 35 years, the lomi psychotherapy clinic has provided accessible mental health care for our community.

welcome to lomi psychotherapy clinic



life is filled with challenges, and during times of struggle, a knowledgeable psychotherapist can provide invaluable support in navigating waves of grief, anxiety, trauma, and depression. whether these struggles persist throughout our lives or arise unexpectedly, it is important to find someone who can genuinely listen, offer compassion, and assist in developing new skills to address these difficulties. through careful guidance, we can uncover fresh approaches to confronting long-standing patterns, newfound anxieties, and grief. moreover, we can rediscover balance, regain a sense of hope, and embrace a revitalized experience of life.regardless of whether your struggles stem from recent trauma or events from the past, therapists can help restore a sense of safety. they possess the tools to guide individuals in processing past trauma, enabling them to find equilibrium and relief. additionally, therapists are equipped to address negative thinking patterns and navigate challenging emotions. through these means, we can ultimately achieve an affirming sense of peace, acceptance, and joy, paving the way for a new chapter of life.














if you are in a mental health crisis, text or call 988.



about lomi















lomi is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization with a mission to promote the health and well-being of the diverse community of sonoma county by providing accessible mental health care and innovative education.

innovative education















the lomi psychotherapy clinic also provides innovative education as a training center and supervision site for new therapists in the field. lomi has been a leader in offering an integrative approach to mental health services since 1970.
