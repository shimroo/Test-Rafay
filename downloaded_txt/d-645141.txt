overview





dap foam offers the industry’s most complete line of expanding spray foam products for use in and around the home. touch 'n foam professional's lineup of professional gun foams, one-component canisters and two-component foam systems can eliminate air leaks and drafts, seal gaps and cracks and insulate against the elements to make homes comfortable, quiet, cozy and more energy efficient all year round.





usage




uses and applications
								a solution for every project
							




general gaps & cracks
fill and seal gaps, cracks, holes and joints in and around homes to provide airtight insulation that blocks drafts, moisture and pests. formulated for fire-block applications and colored orange for easy identification by inspectors.



window & door sealing
provide airtight insulation around windows and doors to block airflow and increase energy efficiency, without warping or bowing frames or jambs.



cavity fill
wood stud walls above grade or in basements typically provide numerous entry points for air and moisture infiltration.  touch ‘n foam professional spray foam insulates stud wall cavities up to a 2 inch fill to create a barrier that is virtually impermeable.



rim joists
basements are a common area where unwanted air flow, moisture and pests enter a home. touch ‘n foam professional's line of expanding foam sealants includes solutions for sealing gaps where sill plates and concrete foundations meet and for insulting rim and band joist areas.
