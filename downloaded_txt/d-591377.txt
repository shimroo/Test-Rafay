about us
nripulse is an atlanta (georgia, us) based monthly newspaper and portal that caters to the needs and interests of the indian-american community in particular, and other members of the growing south asian population in and around georgia.
nri pulse has been recognized by the limca book of records, 2010 edition, as the first newspaper to be published and edited by a non-resident indian woman in the world!contact us: contact@nripulse.com
