welcome to holiday pet hotel!




our dog and cat boarding feature luxury vacation suites, and we even offer boarding services for guinea pigs, other pocket pets, and exotic pets. we’re also thrilled to have an amazing dog daycare program where your precious pup can spend the day with their pals rather than being lonely on the sofa while you’re at work.
if you are looking for pet boarding and dog daycare in encinitas, you are in the right place! whether your pet is with us for a few hours or several days, we promise they will be in excellent hands while in our care.






































who we are




we have been caring for pets for more than 40 years, and we pride ourselves on providing exceptional care and customer service to everyone who enters our facility. we are the oldest and most cost-effective boarding facility in north county and the only one that features private indoor and outdoor rooms for our canine guests.
