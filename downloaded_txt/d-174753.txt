county happenings


view activities, meetings, & events
 






  
 
 

















carlisle borough mayor presents commissioners 275th anniversary proclamation
mayor sean shultz kicked off the carlisle ice art fest with a proclamation presentation to cumberland county commissioners in honor of the county's 275th anniversary. 









representative nate davidson district office opens
cumberland county attended representative nate davidson's district office open house and spoke with residents about the human services we provide.









cumberland county sheriff's office k-9 thor retires
cumberland county wishes k-9 thor a happy retirement after seven years of dedicated service. thor is a belgian malinois, and is specialized in narcotics detection.









junior leadership cumberland hosts elected officials panel
cumberland county commissioner neiderer and judge brewbaker were honored to speak with our future leaders as part of junior leadership cumberland's elected officials panel!









cumberland county celebrates 275th anniversary
commissioners, county employees, and other dignitaries joined in the county's celebration marking 275 years of generations in the making.
