development
innovative designs that both streamline the shooting of shows and bring the latest technologies to the pyrotechnics industry are at the heart of pyromaster’s vision.the saying, “it’s never been done that way before.” will never stop us.  more…
 
maximum performance
our products fit all price ranges for maximum performance. experienced pyrotechnicians from over 20 companies, large and small, are the main influence of our development. we develop products that adapt to our customers' needs. great equipment has to perform in the field yet be easy to handle for set up and tear down. our equipment development requires a constant goal of reducing labor time for field usage & maintenance and provides decades of reliable performance.
 
reliable design
our products focus on durability, compact size for shipping and user friendly operation and setup. the equipment has been shipped all over the world and has performed well every time.once in the field it is comforting that the systems can rebound when there are issues. whether it be multiple back up power supplies for firing systems or four-point locking points on our assurance racks, we always design for redundant methods, leaving no weak link in the chain of performance. our design motto is pyro-tuff, user friendly at a reasonable price.
