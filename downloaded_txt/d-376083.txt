welcome to rvhotline canada, the rv trader classifieds sale site
rv hotline canada is an rv trader classifieds sale site where you can find thousands of rvs for sale; travel trailers, tent trailers, motorhomes, campers, fifth wheels and truck campers - from the top rv dealers in canada. we list both new and used trailers by popular rv manufacturers like airstream, winnebago, jayco, forest river rv, keystone rv and many more.
for almost 20 years we've been at the forefront of rv trader advertising and lead-generation for canadian dealers, and helping families find their rv of choice with ease and simplicity.
browse new & used rv trailers for sale
we work in conjunction with the rvda of canada for the purpose of joining buyers with sellers by listing their inventory of rvs for sale. we have a huge inventory of new and used rv trailers and motorhomes from dealers across all provinces, and we make it easy to contact dealers, to help facilitate the research and sales process.
we list provincial rv rental services
for those who would rather rent an rv you can browse the section of our site dedicated to rv rentals which will help you determine if the rv lifestyle is for you.
we support buying rvs from local dealers
we are advocates of the canadian rv industry and passionately support buying local rvs from rv dealers. if you are looking to purchase and finance an rv, read our buy local, buy canadian page where we outline the advantages of buying from a dealership within 50 kilometers of your home.
