collections






										gvt/pgvt
									
elegant designs on glazed porcelain body


										the gvt/pgvt collection combines the high technical performance of extra-thick products with a sophisticated aesthetic inspired by the modernity of concrete, the timeless appeal of stone and the natural warmth of wood. 
									

explore collection















										wall tile
									
digital designs on glazed ceramic body


									   wall tiles enhance the decor of the space with their glorious style. the wall tiles collection is designed to uplift the aura of a space by decorating the walls with unique designs, attractive shades and alluring aesthetics. 
									

explore collection








										double charge 
									
natural sand art on non glazed porcelain body


									   the double charge collection is stylish, sturdy and elegant. these tiles are easy to install and maintain due to their features like extreme durability, scratch-resistance, anti-scratch and anti-stain among many others. 
									

explore collection
















										parking tile
									
rustic designs on glazed ceramic heavy duty body


									   the parking tiles collection is here to make the outdoor space fun and beautiful with its uncanny attractiveness and sophisticated style. they are specifically designed to suit outdoor spaces like parking areas, garages or parks.
