westlake community run earns recognition for promoting health and wellness 


the city of westlake recreation department is proud to announce that it has won 3rd place in the health & wellness programs and events category at the 2024 annual awards of excellence, presented by the ohio parks and recreation association (opra).						read on...







westlake police department bids farewell to lt. brian pasti after 36 years of service 


the westlake police department is honoring the retirement of lt. brian pasti, who has officially retired after an impressive 36 years of service.						read on...







successful save by westlake's "are you ok?" program 


the city of westlake's "are you ok?" program proved its effectiveness again with a successful save today. after a senior citizen failed to answer her daily call, the westlake police department (wpd) was immediately alerted.						read on...







exciting trail improvements coming to westlake! 


westlake is enhancing its parks with major trail improvements at clague park and bradley road nature park! thanks to community support, these upgrades will provide safer and more accessible trails, connecting key areas.						read on...
