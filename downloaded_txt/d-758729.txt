about kojo

kojo is a five-piece band combining elements of jazz, funk and soul to produce an uplifting and inspiring sound. unrivalled in musicality, the repertoire combines catchy originals with ingenious kojo reworks of popular songs of yesteryear. in its two year history, it has established numerous prestigious residencies in venues around the north west, and is now one of the busiest outfits performing in the area.
