island activities
if you’re on the hunt for fun and adventure, st. croix is the island for you! snorkel amongst colorful coral reefs, kayak in bioluminescent bays, sail along the coast as the sun sets, or book a tasty food tour.

adult-only experience
our resort is an adult-only oasis on the quiet east end of the island, explicitly designed for people wanting a relaxed zen-like atmosphere away from the hustle and bustle.
explore amenities







know before you go
before traveling to st. croix, it is important that you are fully informed of all rules and regulations. please visit our helpful faqs page to read through the island's guidelines for entry and rules to follow during your stay.
during certain times of the year, the island and our beach experiences large amounts of seaweed, which is unavoidable. the resort staff regularly clean the beach, but the beach cannot be cleaned during turtle nesting season.
view faqs







no passports needed
u.s. guests don't need to worry about bringing along passports  as st. croix is part of the u.s. virgin islands. simply bring along your government-issued id!
