          exploring the frontiers of the brain and mind
      


the cmbn's mision is to explore the brain’s structure and function through excellence in research and graduate training. the center is home to fourteen research laboratories whose interests span molecular, cellular, systems, cognitive, and computational neuroscience, and hosts over twenty ph.d. students in the graduate program in neuroscience.




















          phd program in neuroscience
      


the center offers a ph.d. in neuroscience through the graduate program in neuroscience (gpn). 
students perform original research, using cutting-edge techniques that span every level of brain function - from molecules to behavior and computation.
all students receive tuition and a generous stipend, regardless of nationality. masters's degrees are not offered.
