about usseattle swing dance club (ssdc) is the longest running dance club since 1965 and local nonprofit organization that provides west coast swing (wcs) aka modern swing workshops, free beginner lessons, social dances, and competitions in west seattle. join us every first and third sunday!



what to expectwe host modern swing seattle dance events for club members and guests:every first and third sunday at alki masonic hall (4736 40th ave sw, seattle, wa 98116)annual convention easter swing at hyatt regency bellevueworkshop weekends



our eventswe offer free wcs seattle beginner classes too! to see what’s on our schedule, please use the list below or browse our larger community calendar for all the modern swing events in seattle’s surrounding areas.
