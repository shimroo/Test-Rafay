congregation beth tikvah 



carlisle, pennsylvania














reform jewish congregation.















intimate communityfamily oriented - children included in all activities    sunday school sessions bar/bat mitzvahsfriday night services (lay led)high holy day services in partnership with dickinson college hillelcongregational cemetery in carlisleinterfaith families welcome zoom sessions available



congregation beth tikvah is a welcoming, intimate jewish community in carlisle.  affiliated with the union for reform judaism the congregation meets at the milton b. asbell center for jewish life on the campus of dickinson college.
