our history
opened in 1994, the players club course was the latest addition to the foxfire golf club family.  located in scenic lockbourne, ohio,  the players club at foxfire boasts 18 holes of championship golf and 5 tee boxes for all skill levels.  the course remains family owned and operated to this day.
