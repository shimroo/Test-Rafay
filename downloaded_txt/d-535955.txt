welcome to tutta bella
buon appetito! we're tutta bella. born in naples. raised in seattle. we believe in doing italian food the right way, using simple, high quality ingredients to create beautiful flavors that honor our neapolitan roots and celebrate our vibrant puget sound surroundings.our story







our food
our food is simple, flavorful and it warms the soul. we make everything by hand using one common ingredient throughout…love.
