welcome to the mlsc
loughborough's mathematics learning support centre (mlsc) provides comprehensive assistance for students dealing with new and challenging mathematics and statistics throughout their university journey. 





the mlsc, located in room 0.20 of the schofield building, is available to students as a private study space during term time, from 9am to 5pm, monday to friday. dedicated staff also offer in person tutoring both within the centre and in pilkington library. we also offer remote tutoring.



in person and remote tutoring

dedicated tutors are available via a drop-in service in the schofield centre 11am to 1pm and 2pm to 4pm daily
they will also be based in the pilkington library on tuesdays and thursdays from 2pm-4pm.
we also offer remote tutoring sessions from 11am to 1pm and 3pm to 5pm daily.
