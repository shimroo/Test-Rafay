imagine monroeimagine monroe: a lively center surrounded by nature. a place of beauty and goodwill.our parks, waterways, and environment are healthy and accessible for everyone to enjoy. our historic downtown and business districts are thriving and full of locally owned businesses and locally sourced products. we can find everything we need with regional connections and with a variety of choices for work, housing, dining, shopping, arts, and activities.friendly and responsive, we strengthen connections through gathering spaces, events, services, and community-centered infrastructure - creating a safe place for all.in monroe, everyone feels at home and everyone feels they belong.
