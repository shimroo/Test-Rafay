sanibel & captiva island real estate

sanibel and captiva islands are not just a tropical paradise for vacationers; they are also ideal places to call home. the close-knit community values tranquility and peace, creating a serene environment for residents. surrounded by miles of scenic bike trails and nature preserves, our neighborhoods also boast world-famous white sand beaches.equally important to us are the birds, wildlife, and plant life that share our island community. we take pride in protecting our natural habitat for future generations, ensuring that this beautiful ecosystem remains a cherished home for all.david schuldenfrei has been a dedicated realtor in the area since 1976, bringing over 46 years of experience to meet your real estate needs. with beach properties always in high demand, whether you’re seeking an investment, a vacation getaway, or a permanent residence, sanibel and captiva islands are a must-see!
