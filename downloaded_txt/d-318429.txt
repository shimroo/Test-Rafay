warren freyer aia, leed ap, established freyer collaborative architects (fca) in 1991 focusing on a collaborative design approach where functional and aesthetic needs of the client are explored. through years of refinement and evolution, the fca studio has become truly well versed in making a mark on the urban environment while speaking the local vernacular. the studio's work has been featured in numerous publications and periodicals, in addition to receiving awards of excellence from the new york city art commission in conjunction with the american institute of architects.
