attention customers 




				dear ford midlothian customers: 

 

we want to take a moment to thank you for your loyalty and support over the years at tasca ford of midlothian. it has been our privilege to serve you, and we are incredibly grateful for the relationships we’ve built along the way.

while ford has made the decision to close this location effective january 31st, this is not a goodbye—just a change in where we serve you. our commitment to providing you with the best sales and service experience remains stronger than ever, and we would love the opportunity to continue meeting your automotive needs here at arlington heights ford!

 

though our midlothian location is closing, our team is still here for you. whether you’re looking for a new vehicle, need service, or just want to stop by and say hello, we welcome you with open arms at our other locations.

thank you for being part of our tasca ford family. we appreciate your business, your trust, and the opportunity to continue serving you in the years ahead. if you have any questions or need assistance, please don’t hesitate to reach out—we’re always happy to help.

 

we look forward to seeing you soon!

 

the tasca ford of midlothian team
