did you know?



in 2017, comicazi won the will eisner spirit of comics retailer award.visionary comics creator will eisner (the spirit, contract with god) approached comic-con international in the mid-1990s to create an award that recognized the incredible contributions of retailers to the comic book industry. he wanted to stress the crucial bond between creators, publishers and retailers in getting the books into the hands of the public. comic-con created the will eisner spirit of comics retailer award to honor those retailers in the united states and around the world.we give our heartfelt thanks to our amazing customers, friends and family for making comicazi the special place it is today.
