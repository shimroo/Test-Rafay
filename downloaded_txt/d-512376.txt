nationwide historiansour society has over 400,000 members, with some 9,000 new members joining each year in 970 chapters nationwide.




    find a chapter
  



we are an american honor society for undergraduate and graduate students and professors of history. 
















    discover more
  















learn & growshare your work, learn from other historians, and make connections at one of our conferences.




    get involved
  

















promoting the study of historywe are a professional society whose mission is to promote the study of history through encouraging research, good teaching, publication, and the exchange of learning and ideas among historians. we seek to bring students and teachers together for intellectual and social exchanges, which promote and assist historical research and publication by our members in a variety of ways.
















make history with usour members get to participate in regional conferences, biennial conventions, and special scholarship opportunities. you can become part of our 400,000-strong membership and connect with like-minded history buffs.











































support studentseach year we give out tens of thousands of dollars of grants, awards, and scholarships to support our members. consider making a charitable donation to help us expand our efforts.
