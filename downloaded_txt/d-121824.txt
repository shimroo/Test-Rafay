 pacific inn
surrounded by natural northwest wonders
welcome to the pacific inn! our accommodations bring you all the comforts of home but wrapped up in the beauty that is the olympic peninsula of washington state. we are located in the heart of forks just off of highway 101 (forks ave) and main street. a top highlight for our visitors is the vast hiking, fishing, hunting, and beachcombing options nearby. explore the lush hoh rain forest on the west side of the olympic peninsula or a serene ocean view at la push or rialto beach. this 2,000 square mile area is bordered on the north and west by over 100 miles of saltwater shores, and to the south and east by alpine meadows and rain forest valleys. venture out to hot springs and waterfalls as well, to top off your visit.
here at the pacific inn, it is our number one priority to provide our guests with some of the best lodging, deals, and quality service while you are visiting the wonderful town of forks. when you first walk in, you will be blown away by our hometown charm and greeted by some of the best hospitality professionals in the business. for those looking for apartment-style accommodations, our executive suite comes with a full kitchen, in-unit laundry, and a separate living room. our goal is to assist you in any way that we can while you are staying with us. after check-in, you can drop your belongings off in your room and explore the town, utilize any of our onsite amenities, or maybe just hit the hay on our comfortable mattresses!
we hope that you will join us at the pacific inn. whether you are here on business, seeking the great outdoors of washington, or even visiting our local area for its place in pop culture – we are committed to providing an excellent guest experience. book a room with us today and get the most out of your trip to forks.
