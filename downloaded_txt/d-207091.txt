fire protection and hydrant maintenance 

contra costa water district has more than 5,000 fire hydrants in our system—and we add new ones every year. our crews work hard to ensure they’re always ready when needed.				learn more about hydrant maintenance.







ccwd's 2024 annual report is now available! 

our annual report highlights our continued efforts to secure reliable water for today and tomorrow. this year’s report reflects the progress we’ve made in modernizing infrastructure, diversifying water supply, and strengthening our customer engagement.		view ccwd's 2024 annual report.







ccwd board ratifies los vaqueros reservoir jpa dissolution 

contra costa water district board took the final action needed to ratify its decision to end the los vaqueros reservoir expansion project and dissolve the partnership overseeing it.				learn more about this decision.
