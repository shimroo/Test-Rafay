leadership in motion



first equity group, inc. has a three-decade track-record leading successful investment transactions in the aerospace & defense, transportation and automotive sectors, both as a principal and advisor. founded in 1985 as first equity development, inc., first equity remains focused on serving the aerospace and defense industry.  first equity currently owns first aviation services inc., a global leader in maintenance, repair and overhaul serving the aerospace industry. past investments include national airmotive corporation (aircraft engine repair and overhaul), aerospace products international (logistics and distribution services), imtek (direct marketing, printing  and location intelligence), skip barber racing school (automotive education and entertainment), and dart aerospace (helicopter mission equipment).
