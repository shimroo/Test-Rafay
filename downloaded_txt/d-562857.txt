eligible permits can now be processed and issued on the same day, eliminating delays and allowing residents to get started on their projects more quickly.						read on...







improving the city permitting process to better serve you 


			posted
							on february 11, 2025


we’ve added additional capacity at the city hall permit counter to address public works-related questions. this means more availability to get the comprehensive assistance you need.						read on...







city council approves utility base rate fees for vacant properties 


			posted
							on december 19, 2024


under the approved ordinance, vacant properties will be charged the minimum base rate for each utility available, regardless of use. this will ensure that all properties with access to city utilities contribute fairly to their operations and maintenance.						read on...







winter operating hours for city public restrooms 


			posted
							on november 21, 2024


city public restrooms are now operating on a winter schedule (open daily from 8 a.m. to 5 p.m.). these facilities will be closed on thanksgiving day, christmas day, and new year’s day. portland loos remain open 24 hours a day, seven days a week.						read on...







city of port angeles to pause glass recycling at all transfer stations starting october 19, 2024 


			posted
							on october 14, 2024


?the city of port angeles, like other municipalities in the region, is adjusting to the recent closure of the ardagh glass plant. as a result of this closure, the city is temporarily pausing glass recycling beginning october 19 and until further notice.						read on...
