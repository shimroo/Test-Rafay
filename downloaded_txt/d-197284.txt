processing times
heavy volume is slowing our response time to cases sent by mail.
the following transactions require at least 2 weeks of processing time if bonds and/or treasurydirect accounts are in your name.

cashing paper savings bonds where you are named on the bonds, and you send in the bonds with your request
unlocking your treasurydirect account or updating your banking information within that account
converting your paper savings bonds into electronic format (in a treasurydirect account)

requests to search for lost, stolen or missing savings bonds require at least 4 months to process.
other paper savings bond transactions you are authorized to handle, but not in your name require at least 6 weeks to process.
other treasurydirect requests, including trusts, may require 6 months or more to process.
