we are one spirit



spiritual education for change makers



























one spirit learning alliance is a transformative community for interspiritual learning, leading the charge to co-create a world guided by reverence for the sacred connection between all life and a commitment to its flourishing.




    about us
  



“if we are to survive and grow in our world, we must transform ourselves through love and acceptance for all teachings, all people, and all cultures, everywhere. one spirit is a leader in this work.” — rev. cory c.






































one spirit is looking to expand our horizons to meet the needs of today’s healers, change agents, and spiritual practitioners. we invite proposals that:build upon and enhance our existing programs.introduce innovative offerings aligned with our mission.explore new structures or business models to increase relevance, impact, and sustainability.submit a proposal by march 1, 2025 and contribute to a shared vision of one spirit’s future.




    learn more
  














our offerings






seminary

one spirit interfaith and interspiritual seminary is a 2-year ordination program focused on developing core spirituality, exploring the foundations of interfaith and interspiritual ministry, and learning how to respond and show up in spiritual service to others.




seminary







interspiritual companioning & counseling

the certificate in interspiritual companioning & counseling is a 9.5-month program for seminarians, social workers, therapists, and people-centered professionals who feel called to support clients in their spiritual life.




interspiritual companioning







thanatology

the certificate in integrative thanatology with the art of dying institute at one spirit is a 5-month program designed to deepen understanding of the death and dying experience and develop skills in compassionate presence, end-of-life planning, and cross-cultural rituals.




thanatology







events

we offer a variety of educational workshops and community offerings, each fostering a culture of interspiritual discovery, deep connection, and sacred service.

our approach



one spirit offers a heartfelt teaching style that is experiential, rigorous, and personally transformative. exploring our experiences and relationships, we expand our hearts, foster our truest nature, and naturally bring this evolving spirit into the collective consciousness. our approach explores integral, interspiritual, and developmental perspectives to inspire our community to live engaged lives of authenticity, integrity, celebration, and compassionate service. in maintaining our integrity, we keep a strong code of ethics.
