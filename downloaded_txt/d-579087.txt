        about us      

welcome to the town of middlebury, connecticut. in middlebury, our residents know and appreciate small town living at its finest. people are drawn to this town for its beauty, friendliness, and rural warmth. the board of selectman is committed to its residents because they are the heart of our municipality. in addition, the board of selectmen is continuously dedicated to maintaining the high standard of living our residents have always cherished. all of these efforts ensure our hometown to be a premier connecticut hamlet.
