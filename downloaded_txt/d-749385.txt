message from the chief of police
 






  
 




on behalf of the men and women of the hampton police department, welcome to our website and thank you for taking the time to learn about our organization.  we encourage people to explore our website to learn about the programs we provide as well as our mission, vision, and goals of the agency.  the hampton police department is recognized as one of the premier law enforcement agencies in the state of new hampshire.  we are committed to working with our community to prevent crime and help solve community problems.  our agency consists of 39 full time officers, 20+ part time officers, and 10 civilian staff members.  our employees make tremendous sacrifices to help the department achieve our goals and is the strength of our department.  the town of hampton has a year-round population of approximately 16,000 residents.  we experience a large increase in population to upwards of 135,000 during the summer season as many people like to visit our beautiful beaches and shops.  the hampton police strives to provide the best level of service we can for our community.  we encourage our community members to reach out with ideas or thoughts on how we can improve on our services.  i am honored to be a part of this department as well as this community and will strive to meet the challenges we face today as well as prepare for the future of this department.  alexander j. renochief of police
