 articulight has launched the advertiser lpl 3i range of multi-image projectors

								fair lawn, nj, usa - based on the success of its led single image projectors, articulight, inc. has recently launched the multi-image (3 -7 images) advertiser lpl 3i range . 
								it is designed for outdoor and indoor use and is targeted to replace high energy consumption and low lamp life halogen and metal halide sources. 
								the advertiser lpl 3i range is an image projector using long life, low wattage, high power white led cob (chip on board) technology with the selection of 40w to 200w power sources. 
								it  has a smart heat management system that keeps the projector and its various sub-assemblies cool ensuring reliability and durability. 
								the advertiser lpl 3i range uses gobos (metal, glass or dichroic) for image projection. 
								the series is designed to address a wide range of applications in the architectural, entertainment, display and retail markets and to be used outdoors on facades, sidewalks, just to name a few. 
								the advertiser lpl 3i range is compact, lightweight and cost effective.
							  

for more info:  http://articulight.com/gp/advertiserlpl3i/index.html.
