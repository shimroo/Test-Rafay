the tarantula gallery


birdspiderscom merged with birdspidersch – all the wonderful images created, collected, and curated over decades of theraphosid research by arachnologist rick c. west find their new home on this page. enjoy going through hundreds of tarantula pictures of well-known and incredibly rare species – some of them only displayed on this site. if you want regular updates on new additions, consider subscribing to our free newsletter on the website.
