world of brass  uk suppliers of door furniture, door handles and knobs, locks, window furniture, cabinet handles, architectural hardware & ironmongery





our web site was launched back in 2002, when we became one of the first online suppliers of brass door handles, door knobs and door furniture in the uk. every item is selected for its quality and value for money price. we never compromise quality for cheaper prices and are confident in our belief that we can offer some of the highest quality products at the most competitive prices including:
door furniture
from the interior to exterior front door furniture, we have a complete range of door fittings and accessories, including letter plates and door knockers for your front door to hinges and door bolts and much more. in addition to our range of brass door furniture we have a large range chrome door furniture, antique black iron and stainless steel door furniture and hardware.
door handles
from the traditional type brass door handles or designer chrome door handles to 316 grade stainless steel door lever handles on round rose, we have hundreds of door handles to choose from in brass, chrome, black antique, bronze and stainless steel.
door knobs
for those who prefer door knobs to handles we stock a variety of mortice door knobs. in addition to rim door knob sets, knobs on backplate, centre door knobs and knobs with locks.
door locks & latches
its important to get the right sort of lock or latch for your door handles or knobs. we stock a range of locks and security products, including mortice door latches, bathroom locks, british standard sash locks and high security euro cylinders to compliment the range of door handles and knobs.
window furniture & fittings
for casement windows or sash windows we have a complete range of window furniture and fittings in brass, chrome, bronze, black iron and stainless steel; including window stays, fasteners, sash lifts and handles.
cabinet handles & knobs
for that final touch to your kitchen doors, or bathroom cabinet we stock a range of cupboard door handles and knobs in both traditional styles and contemporary designs in brass, chrome, satin nickel, crystal glass and stainless steel.
electrical switches
our victorian, georgian or modern flat plate light switches, dimmers and sockets produced in high quaity solid brass will help brighten your home.
bathroom fittings & accessories
to compliment any bathroom we have a number of bathroom fittings ranges in chrome and nickel.
