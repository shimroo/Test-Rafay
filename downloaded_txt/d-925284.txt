the 2025 pacific musical society & foundation annual competition is now open for submissions! interested applicants should review the entry information and submit an application by january 20, 2025.














the pacific musical society & foundation is a charitable organization dedicated to fostering the art of classical music and ensuring the success of young classical musicians in the san francisco bay area. founded in 1910, we are the region’s oldest active musical organization.through your financial support, the pacific musical society & foundation directly sponsors talented young musicians through our annual competition, while working to increase interest in and viability of classical music in the bay area and beyond. you can read more about our mission here, and visit our winners showcase to see performances by the talented young winners of our 2023 competition.
