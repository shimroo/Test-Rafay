design services

by taking advantage of our complimentary design services, you can rest assured that your design preferences will be fully understood and incorporated into creating a beautiful, functional space. our designers are here to assist you whether you’re shopping for a lamp, redecorating a room, or designing an entire home.











carpet

weisshouse has cultivated a vast and unique broadloom collection that is unrivaled within the city of pittsburgh. we offer a large range of wool and synthetic carpets; whether looking for a stair runner, wall-to-wall carpeting, or custom cut and bound broadloom rugs, we have the carpet to suit your interior. our carpet inventory extends beyond the selection on our website. visit our showroom to browse our entire collection and schedule an installation.











installation & delivery

our dedicated installation and delivery teams can make your interior come to life quickly and stress-free. we receive, store, and deliver with our white glove service, including on-site assembly. we are fully experienced at installing carpets with expert craftsmanship and care throughout the processes. with years of experience designing and transforming spaces, our team ensures that every detail is considered.











window treatments

let the sun shine in (or not)! the right window treatments can make or break a room. weisshouse offers an extensive selection of draperies, shades, blinds, and shutters to elevate your interior decor and enhance your living space.contact our design team to schedule a measure and find the ideal solution for your windows.
