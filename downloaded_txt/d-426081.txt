community sponsors



















































inspiring creativity since 1987! come join us at the 40th annual camp creative12 - 16 january 2026



















































camp creative is where like-minded people come together to learn, make & discover the very best workshops led by artists, designers & musicians from across our nation.



held in the picturesque town of bellingen new south wales, camp creative offers a diverse range of over 80 courses to choose from. whether you're a novice looking to explore your artistic side or a seasoned artist seeking new challenges, camp creative will ignite your creativity and kickstart the new year on a vibrant note.




    learn more
  












countdown to camp creative 2026





































connectcamp creative offers a vibrant and social week filled with endless opportunities to meet like-minded people and form new friendships! you’ll be able to reconnect with old friends from previous camp creative workshops or forge new friendships!















discoverembrace the excitement of delving into uncharted territory, where every brushstroke or dance step leads to a fresh expression of yourself. explore, learn, and grow at camp creative, where the journey of discovering new artistic skills is as rewarding as the final masterpiece.















createunleashing your creativity is like unlocking a door to a world of endless possibilities. so go ahead, pick up that paintbrush, grab that hammer, or step onto that dance floor. let your creativity flow and watch as magic unfolds before your eyes.
