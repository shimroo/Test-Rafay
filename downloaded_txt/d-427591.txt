writing








meet with a writing consultant we assist writers at all levels of experience, across all majors. students can meet with trained writing consultants who offer friendly critiques of written work. 









 




how we help
located in boatwright memorial library, peer writing consultants offer writing assistance and support to all students on campus. visit our team during scheduled drop-in hours, work with a course-embedded writing consultant, or explore our online resources.
