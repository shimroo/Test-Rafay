the home of real wood furniture: serving the metro dc area: northern virginia - nova - maryland - washington, dc - district of columbia
real wood furniture of chantilly, virginia features factory finished, finished-to-order, and unfinished furniture for every room of your home or office in shaker, modern, contemporary, traditional, mission, amish and barn wood styles.  we have standard and build to order options.  we build in pine, maple, oak, cherry, alder, reclaimed lumber and parawood.  select from living room, bedroom, dining room, entertainment, home office, wall units, library, home theater and youth and children furniture with real wood construction and no particle board.  the best furniture value to be found in northern virginia, maryland and washington dc. complement your furniture selection from a tasteful assortment of accessories to personalize your home or office.
