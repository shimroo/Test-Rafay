what are cookies?
                “cookies” are data files that are placed on your device or computer and often include an anonymous unique identifier. for more information about cookies, and how to disable cookies, visit http://www.allaboutcookies.org.
how we use cookies?
                essential cookies are needed to make sure that the site functions properly for each user.  they are needed to keep users logged in and to ensure they can access certain features of the site.
                perfomance cookies are used to measure how much and how often a user visits our site.  this gives us a better sense of user engagement.  we collect information such as the most visited pages by the different types of users.  google analytics is a program that we use to accomplish this.
                functional cookies are used to ensure that the site functions as expected and that the user has a problem free experience.

trial
when a real estate agent signs up for an account, they receive a 14 day free trial upgrade to a premium account.  all upgraded features are shown in green on an agent's profile.  the trial account includes all basic and premium features listed below.

basic
after the 14 day trial period, real estate agents will default to a basic account.  basic accounts are free and include all of the following features:

create and customize your profile.
link to all of your social media accounts and websites.
share your profile on other media platforms.
we provide you with your own qr code to quickly share your profile with others.
choose 4 specializations to improve search results.
get basic profile visitor statistics.
receive client and industry recommendations.
provide recommendations for other agents and industry professionals.
use the agent board to make announcements.
join a real estate team.
add other real estate agents, mortgage brokers, financial planners, accountants, appriasers and more to your contact list and build your network across the country.
invite colleagues to join your network.
provide introductions to and receive introductions from your contacts.
visit agent profiles to see mutual contacts.
full access to the agent store.
all of these features are completely free!


premium
agents can upgrade to a premium account by purchasing a monthly subscription through the agent store and will have access to all of the following upgraded features:

all basic account features included.
get access to enhanced visitor profile statistics, see users that have viewed your profile and added you to their contact lists.
have clients request a call directly from your profile.  manage call back requests.
search score and rankings, see how you compare to other agents locally and nationally.
receive an additional 2 specialization slots.
receive a complimentary fca card for offline networking.
receive discounts at local businesses through our perks program.



upgrade now



verified
agents can become a verified first class agent by purchasing an annual subscription through the agent store and completing the verification process. verified accounts are fully upgraded and give an agent access to all first class agents features including:

all basic and premium account features included.
move up in search results, verified agents are always listed ahead of unverified agents in all search results.
verified first class agent status and badge on profile.
verified achievement section on profile.
create and manage a real estate team.
contribute to your team's verified statistics.
revceive 3 additional specialization slot. (1 additional slot if upgrading from premium)
