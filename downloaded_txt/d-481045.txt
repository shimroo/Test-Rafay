      welcome to the bear center    


the berkeley evaluation and assessment research (bear) center designs and delivers educational assessment instruments and performs research in assessment, psychometrics, and program evaluation.  the bear center is a research unit at the berkeley school of education.we collaborate with researchers in universities across the united states and abroad to develop methods and tools for constructing, managing, administering, and analyzing assessments.
