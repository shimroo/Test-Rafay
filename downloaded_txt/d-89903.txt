chist-era science in your own language



apply for funding in automatic translation of scientific knowledge to overcome language and cultural barriers.
you must be based at a uk research organisation eligible for epsrc funding. project consortia must have at least three eligible partner countries.

open

mathematical sciences postdoctoral fellowship



these are personal, career development focused awards for the mathematical sciences.
you must have either:

a phd
more than four years’ experience in a relevant field by the start of your fellowship

candidates with more research experience may prefer to apply for our open fellowship.
