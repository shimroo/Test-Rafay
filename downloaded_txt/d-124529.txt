sacramento’slgbtq+ library & community hub



the lavender library is a volunteer-run lgbtq+ library, archive, and community space in sacramento with queer books, films, archives, zines, and more. we host book clubs, support groups, open mic nights, craft markets, and other community events year-round.















we are 100% community supported and operated



we rely on community members like you to help us keep the lights on, make rent, purchase new books, digitalize our archive, and pay for operating expenses like this website.please consider becoming a donor to keep this vital lgbtq+ space open in our community now and for years to come.

queer stories are powerful and our voices can never be silenced.we are proud to keep lgbtq+ history books, archives, contemporary literature, and other radical materials on our shelves.
