welcome to dominex natural foods


               for over two and a half decades, dominex has maintained its position as the frontrunner in frozen, specialty eggplant products. whether you're a vegetarian seeking culinary inspiration or simply aiming to incorporate more vegetables into your meals, our lineup promises flavor, affordability, and adaptability. from eggplant cutlets to eggplant fries, dominex offers an array of eggplant options. we supply to retail, foodservice, club, and ingredient sectors. utilizing fresh eggplant sourced year-round, we blend it with a tantalizing herbs, spices, breading, or batter mere days after harvest.
