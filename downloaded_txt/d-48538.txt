about lakewood centerthere's so much to discover
located in the heart of lakewood, california, lakewood center features a collection of unparalleled shopping, restaurants, and entertainment options. lakewood center is home to many of today’s top brands with several shops, department stores, and dining selections. shopping includes macy's, costco, target, burlington, h&m, foot locker, and hollister. dining options include stonefire grill, luna grill, outback steakhouse, george's greek cafe, and california pizza kitchen, or you can have fun at round 1 bowling & amusement or catch a movie at starlight cinema.
