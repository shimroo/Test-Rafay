international board of functional neurology














impacting clinical care through education and clinical excellence



ibfn is an independent testing organization providing and facilitating standards to maintain the highest level of education and training. advanced education in both childhood neurodevelopmental disorders and functional neurology empowers professionals with advanced clinical skills and technologies.
