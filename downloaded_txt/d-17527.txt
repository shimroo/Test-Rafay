welcome to the



























english language theatre in the hague!



the aatg is an english-language amateur theatre group based in the hague, with members from all over south holland. membership is open to all nationalities, adults and children alike, with or without theatre skills or experience. whether you like acting, singing, dancing, choreography, set design, painting, backstage crew, lights, sound, costume, or make-up, there is a spot for you! see our “sign up” page to find out how to join.
