newsletter 




 




 our january play note covers the importance of free play for children, highlighting its role in fostering creativity, self-regulation, and problem-solving. we profile dr. joe frost, a pioneering researcher in play and playground design. our play news features a highlight on how play transforms youth sports and enhances adult friendships.  







report 





 






 “the power of play” report distills decades of research. 







webinar 




 




what does play really mean, and how can it boost our creativity and well-being?join us for an engaging conversation between dr. stuart brown, play expert and founder of the national institute for play, and brendan boyle, toy inventor, founder of ideo play lab, and professor at stanford university. 











podcast 




 








each month, we share a podcast detailing our play notes in the form of a conversation. january’s podcast covers the importance of play for children.
