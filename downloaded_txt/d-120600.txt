about liquid nails®
the liquid nails brand is widely recognized as the leader in construction adhesives, offering a comprehensive range of high-quality products that are specifically formulated to deliver exceptional performance for almost any construction project. our trusted construction adhesives have become an essential tool for builders and diy enthusiasts alike, providing them with the confidence that every project will be completed with precision and durability.

liquid nails products can be found at most major hardware stores, building centers, lumber yards, home centers, and mass merchants throughout the united states, as well as in select international markets. with our unwavering commitment to excellence, liquid nails has become the go-to brand for professionals and homeowners who want each project is done once. done right.
