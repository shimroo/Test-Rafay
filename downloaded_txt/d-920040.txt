welcome to atomiza 




we are a wholesale supplier of car wash, car valeting and detailing products of many years in the uk and europe. our products are also utilised in the industrial and domestic cleaning supplies market as well as sprayers for industrial and agricultural purposes. our products are largely non branded to enable resale without the usual easy trail to source.
we are also an established and experienced supplier for a number of quality manufacturers, with extensive stocks of products and spares.
