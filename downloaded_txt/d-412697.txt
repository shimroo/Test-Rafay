we connect people to nature and work to preserve the environment. we are a chapter of the national audubon society; we are based in southwestern westchester county, ny. we offer guided field trips; nature walks; feeder and hawk watches, educational programs. at lenoir nature preserve, yonkers, we have a butterfly and hummingbird garden. we are an all-volunteer organization. all our walks and programs are free, thanks to our supporters. consider becoming one, by donating via paypal (use this link or the green button below).
