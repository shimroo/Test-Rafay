deur – woodcarvings from south tyrol
the name of the demetz – deur family has been synonymous with the arts and crafts since generations. with its high-quality products, attention to detail and elegant designs, the demetz - deur master craftsman’s workshop has become one of the most important woodcarving companies in val gardena. you will find a wide selection of religious and secular art objects, gifts and collectibles, angels in various designs and a special nativity scene exhibition throughout the whole year.
