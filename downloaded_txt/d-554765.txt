cullman.com was started in 1996 
            by randolph millar and jay fuller as an online news magazine for 
            cullman alabama.  the idea was way ahead of its time. since 
            that date many new organizations have taken that role and the 
            cullman.com website remained dormant.  mr. fuller revived the 
            website on may 1 2020 during the coronavirus crisis to promote local 
            business.
