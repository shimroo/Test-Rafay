fy2026 town manager's recommended budget 


town manager andrew flanagan's recommended fiscal year 2026 budget & financial plan has been released and is now available to view online.			






andover high school improvement project public forums 


the permanent town building advisory committee will host a series of three public forums regarding the proposed andover high school improvement project. forums are scheduled for january 30, february 13, and february 20.			additional info...







participatory capital budgeting program 


nine resident-submitted projects have been selected for the latest round of the participatory capital budgeting program have been selected. the deadline to submit a proposal for the next round of the program is march 10, 2025.			additional info...







overnight demolition activities on portions of i-495 over route 28 


massdot will be conducting overnight demolition work on portions of the 495 bridge over route 28 near the andover/lawrence line beginning sunday, january 5. work will take place between 10:00 pm and 5:00 am nightly, sunday through thursday.			






automated trash & recycling collection faq 


starting july 1, 2025, andover will transition to automated trash and recycling collection. this guide answers commonly asked questions about the new system, including how it works, and what changes to expect.			additional info...







town manager's recommended cip fy2026-fy2030 


town manager andrew flanagan has released the recommended capital improvement program (cip) for fiscal years 2026-2030. click here to view the document.			view cip...







snowplow contractors wanted 


the town of andover is looking for skilled, qualified, and experienced drivers with their own truck/equipment to provide plowing services for the winter of 2024 - 2025.
