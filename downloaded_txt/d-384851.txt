why should you use handshake?

get hired: find and apply for jobs and internships
get discovered: be seen by thousands of employers looking to hire mason talent
get expert help: access career-building resources and advice from university career services
get connected: pick from hundreds of recruitment events hosted by employers each month

need career advice?
our advisors offer tailored support to help you achieve your career goals, navigate challenges, and make informed decisions about your future. make an appointment for personalized guidance on career exploration, resume building, interview preparation, and job search strategies.
