welcome 
        to edukits.cagateway to the heritage community foundations collection 
        of multimedia learning resources. custom designed to make use of the power 
        of new technology and the world wide web, edukits.ca is a public 
        learning portal and dynamic assemblage of educational resources.
the heritage community foundation, drawing on its websites 
        found at albertasource.cathe future alberta online encyclopediahas 
        created lesson plans, activities, games and interactive content to capture 
        students' imaginations and promote hands-on, interactive learning. 
edukits.ca is a key component 
        of the alberta heritage portal, which the foundation is developing to 
        address the needs of students, teachers and parents for an authoritative 
        source for both formal and informal learning.

        de lalberta dont la fondation développe pour adresser les besoins des
