we are "créateurs de convivialité"
pernod ricard is the world leader in premium international wines and spirits. our iconic brands embody our passion for brand building and human bonds.


























convivialité is the heart of everything we do, from our vineyards and farms to distilleries, production sites, retail outlets and offices around the world. we are committed to offering products and experiences which surprise and delight, and to ensuring a respectful, diverse and sustainable future filled with positive human connections for all.
