our locationdfwprc stands proud as one of the largest pug rescue organizations across the united states, extending its compassionate reach beyond dfw to embrace areas in texas, colorado, new mexico, louisiana and oklahoma. together, we are making a difference, one pug at a time.


























about ussince its inception in 1996, dfw pug rescue club, inc. has dedicated itself to rescuing thousands of pugs, securing them loving homes regardless of their age or health status. our commitment to their well-being means veterinary care constitutes our largest ongoing financial need, consuming at least 94% of our annual budget. by joining us, you become part of a two-decade-strong legacy of love and rescue, making a lasting difference in the lives of these charming animals.




    learn more about dfwprc
  











































our partnerswe are incredibly grateful for the support and collaboration of our valued partners who share our dedication to animal welfare. through shared values and a common goal, our partnerships strengthen our efforts and amplify our impact, allowing us to reach more pugs and make a greater difference in their lives.
