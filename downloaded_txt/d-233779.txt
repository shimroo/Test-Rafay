poquoson news










city offices closed for presidents' day 2/17/25 

all non-emergency city offices will be closed monday, february 17 in observance of presidents' day.				read on...







recyling dumpster behind city hall removed 

the recycling dumpster located behind city hall is no longer available and has been removed due to repeated issues with garbage being placed in the dumpster, which was unacceptable to the recycling service contractor.				read on...











strategic plan adopted 2/10/25 

city council adopted a strategic plan at their meeting on february 10, 2025. the strategic plan includes a vision statement, mission statement, four strategic iniatives, and two additional areas of focus.				read on...
