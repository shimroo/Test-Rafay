wildlife management international (wmi) is a wildlife-based company, located in darwin, in the northern territory of australia.it has operated continually since 1978, and undertakes a broad range of activities. wmi have authored numerous books, countless reports, and over two hundred academic publications. wmi staff make regular appearances in the media nationally and internationally.



































wildlife agriculture 

advice and training on humane and sustainable farming and harvesting practices, technology and resource management











education & training 

design, delivery and presentation of training and education packages to clients of all sizes around the world.











filming & media 

over 40 years experience providing film and media services, getting film and documentary crews up close with crocodiles.











consulting 

consultants on wildlife conservation, sustainability, trade, resource management, policy review and development, and human-wildlife conflict.











wildlife tourism 

operators of crocodylus park, research and education centre in darwin nt, australia and providers of wildlife and eco-tourism services.











research 

world class academic and scientific wildlife and tourism services.
