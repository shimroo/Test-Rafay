about rio blanco county, coloradowelcome:the board of county commissioners of rio blanco county, colorado welcome you to our website.  nestled in the heart of northwestern colorado, rio blanco county is a scenic gateway to some of the state’s most breathtaking natural landscapes and outdoor recreational activities. spanning nearly 3,200 square miles, rio blanco is home to thriving communities, rich history, and vast opportunities for adventure.a county rich in historyestablished in 1889, rio blanco county’s name translates to “white river” in spanish, reflecting the region’s stunning waterways and valleys. the area is steeped in the pioneering spirit of the old west and boasts a rich cultural heritage. the towns of meeker and rangely serve as the county’s primary communities, each offering unique experiences that embody the charm and resilience of the rural american west.meeker: known for its historic buildings and world-class hunting, meeker is a place where traditions run deep.rangely: a hub for energy production and outdoor exploration, rangely offers everything from off-roading trails to fossil hunting.natural beauty & outdoor adventurerio blanco county is a paradise for outdoor enthusiasts. whether you enjoy hunting, fishing, camping, hiking, or simply soaking in the awe-inspiring landscapes, you’ll find countless ways to explore our vast wilderness areas.white river national forest: one of the crown jewels of rio blanco county, this vast forest offers atv trails, hiking trails, wildlife viewing, and serene rivers.flat tops wilderness: ideal for hiking, camping, and fishing, this is one of colorado's premier wilderness areas.rangely’s rock crawling park: a world-renowned destination for off-road vehicle enthusiasts.community and economythe rio blanco county community thrives on a balanced mix of industry and small-town living. our economy is driven by agriculture, energy production, and tourism. residents of rio blanco county enjoy a high quality of life, with close-knit communities, excellent schools, and local businesses that form the backbone of the county's economy.key industries:agriculture: rio blanco’s fertile lands support livestock and crop farming.energy: as a key player in colorado’s energy industry, the county is home to oil, gas, and renewable energy production.tourism: from hunting lodges to outdoor adventure, tourism is a growing sector of the local economy.living in rio blanco countyrio blanco county is a place where families can thrive in a safe, welcoming, and supportive environment. whether you’re looking to settle down or just passing through, our communities offer modern amenities with the friendly touch of small-town life.visit usexplore our wide-open spaces, vibrant communities, and unique culture. rio blanco county, colorado, is more than just a place on the map—it’s a place where adventure and community come together. accessibility statementat rio blanco county, we are committed to ensuring that
