welcome to the 2024/25 season



we have something for everyone, from the “music curious” to the “music maven.”we celebrate the power of live music to bring unity, provide a sense of home, and take you on incredible journeys beyond your day-to-day stressors.

in the community















youth concerts are awesome!eugene symphony annually welcomes thousands of elementary students to our daytime youth concerts, opening students' eyes, ears, and imaginations to the incredible world of symphonic music. youth concerts are presented at the hult center for the performing arts located in downtown eugene, oregon.
