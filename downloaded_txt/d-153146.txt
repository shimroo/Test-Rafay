our story
welcome



san jose hd®
your local san jose, california harley-davidson® dealership
our dealership is one of the largest in northern california with over $3,500,000 of harley-davidson® inventory and nearly 30,000 square feet designed to serve your wants and needs. our staff includes a group of motorcycling enthusiasts with years of experience. we want to be your dealer and will go the extra mile to satisfy you. so if you’re looking for san francisco bay area harley® dealers, you’ve come to the right place!

while you’re searching for a harley-davidson® for sale, shop all of our new harley-davidson® motorcycles for sale and our used harley-davidson® motorcycles. we’ve got over 100 harley-davidson® motorcycles in stock and ready to ride home. we also showcase all harley-davidson® models that are available for special order.

need more than one of our motorcycles? at san jose harley-davidson®, we’re a full-service dealership. this means that we can provide competitive motorcycle financing, a factory-trained and certified service department, a fully stocked and on-site parts department and so much more. we also offer additional services such as harley® promotions, test rides, specific model requests and a trade-in evaluation, to name a few!
