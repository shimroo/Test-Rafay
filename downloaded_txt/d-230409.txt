where everyone is welcome 



campbell river is central vancouver island’s gateway to epic outdoor adventures. located on the beautiful west coast of british columbia, campbell river offers a dynamic range of immersive activities all year round. get to know the real campbell river on a memorable visit with your loved ones. be prepared for unforgettable experiences with the community, nature, and wildlife.campbell river is nestled within the territory of the liǧʷiłdax̌ʷ peoples: the we wai kai, wei wai kum, and kwiakah nations⁠. this land is also home to the ayajuthem-speaking people of the homalco, klahoose and k’omoks first nations. 



 



 







 



















 







places to see 



meet our iconic local landmarks 



experience the natural wonders and iconic landmarks that make campbell river a top outdoor destination on vancouver island, british columbia.the options for outdoor adventures are sure to satisfy the desires of everyone who visits. hike the rugged backcountry of strathcona provincial park, or stroll the meandering shorelines of popular sandy beaches. whether you choose to watch the sunset from canada’s first saltwater fishing pier, or cross the elk falls suspension bridge for the very first time, you’ll find no shortage of sites to add to your travel itinerary. 






see more























things to do 



find your niche here in the river 



prepare for extraordinary adventures when you visit campbell river.go salmon fishing in the spectacular discovery passage, or cold water diving in the breathtaking depths of seymour narrows. ride through the exhilarating snowden forest bike trails, and dine out at our diverse array of local restaurants and pubs. there’s something for everyone here in campbell river. 






do more











 



















 







plan your holiday 



where we are & how to get here 



with its stunning natural beauty and abundance of outdoor pursuits, campbell river is a must-see destination for any traveler visiting vancouver island. located almost right in the middle of vancouver island’s east coast, campbell river is a 3 hour drive north of british columbia’s capital city, victoria. the city is also a 3 hour drive south of the most northern tip of the island, port hardy. you can arrive by land, air, or sea, with all options offering a scenic start to a memorable trip. 






start planning























experience campbell river 



campbell river is a picturesque coastal city located on the east coast of vancouver island in british columbia, canada. known as the “salmon capital of the world,” campbell river is a popular destination for fishing enthusiasts, but it also offers a wide variety of outdoor activities for travellers including cold water diving, hiking local trails, and experiencing nature close to home.
