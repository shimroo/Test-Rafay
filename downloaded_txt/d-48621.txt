how to claim cme

the aans is accredited by the accreditation council for continuing medical education (accme) to provide continuing medical education for physicians.
the aans designates this live activity for a maximum of 26.0 ama pra category 1 credits™. physicians should claim only the credit commensurate with the extent of their participation in the activity.


by claiming your cme, you demonstrate your dedication to staying updated with the latest advancements in neurosurgery. to claim your cme, simply log into your aans account, navigate to the ‘cme credits’ section, and follow the instructions to record your participation in the annual meeting. remember, your pursuit of knowledge makes a difference in the field of neurosurgery!

advocacy

through advocacy, policy development and public relations, the aans/cns washington committee and washington office work to defend and protect the ability of neurosurgeons to practice medicine freely and help to ensure the continued advancement of the specialty of neurological surgery





about the washington office
the washington office has played a fundamental role in a number of health policy developments, including advocating for adequate reimbursement, medical liability reform, quality improvement reporting requirements and relief from government regulations.
