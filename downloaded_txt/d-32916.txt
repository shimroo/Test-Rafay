life & work connections connects benefits-eligible staff and faculty with resources, tools, and education to support their total health: physical, mental, emotional, financial, and social.  our services are voluntary and confidential.














“we're committed to the well-being of university of arizona employees – our most important resource. when we support an employee’s total wellness, we live our core values and strengthen our ability to succeed as an institution.”
— helena rodrigues, vice president and chief human resources officer
