welcome to the radio control combat association!
the mission of the rcca is to promote rc streamer combat competition by providing information and technical knowledge while facilitating communication between fellow model aviation enthusiasts.  
as a special interest group of the ama, the rcca provides competition rule recommendations to the ama that are fair, economical, and practical.
