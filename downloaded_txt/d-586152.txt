crosswind homes is a southeastern wisconsin based residential home building company offering high quality construction and craftsmanship at an exceptional value. our quality, integrity and attention to detail has earned us a reputation as a premier home builder specializing in the complete construction of new, single family homes and two family condominium communities.

 
crosswind homes has been creating homes and condominiums in southeastern wisconsin with exceptional quality and value for over 20 years.  our commitment to quality, integrity and attention to detail has earned us a reputation as a premier home builder.
a crosswind homes home or condominium is built with your needs in mind. our homes have creative floor plans that provide good flow, openness, large room size with large windows and amenities to fit your lifestyle. by using the highest quality materials along with some of the finest contractors, you can be assured that your home or condominium will be built to exceed your expectations.

crosswind homes is known for:

on schedule completion
final product exceeding expectations
honesty, integrity, and attention to detail

exceptional quality and value
