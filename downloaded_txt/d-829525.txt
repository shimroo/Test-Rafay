iq test





iq, or intelligence
quotient, is a general measurement of an individual’s capacity to
think and reason.  find out what your iq level is in 35 minutes.
this iq test was prepared by a psychologist and this test will enable you to
compare your iq level with the iq level of the average population. find out
your iq now!

eq test





eq, or emotional
quotient, is the capacity of a person to maximize his/ her personal
traits and social skills.  this eq test aims to evaluate your emotional
intelligence.  and you’d be able to compare your eq with the eq level of
the rest of the population. find out your eq now!
