    ride with us
  
























formed to inspire and encourage interest and to increase participation of mounted games in the united states, our mission is to increase the number of riders involved, enhance the image of games as a spectator sport, and continually improving the quality and quantity of our competitions, while keeping safety and fairness a top priority. usmga aims to provide a unique niche in equine sports by keeping games financially accessible to the general public.
