4 bars, 3 levels, 2 stages… 1 legendary entertainment complex.
 





the pickle barrel nightclub is killington’s largest and most exciting entertainment venue. centrally located on the killington road, the pickle barrel has 3 levels of dynamic entertainment space, performance stages, dance floors and bars housed within one of the area’s most storied buildings. the pickle barrel has been a favorite party spot and gathering place since the 1960s, and today its legendary stage is home to the top names in music. from iconic rock legends, to emerging artists, the live music you want is right here at the pickle barrel in killington, vermont. learn more about the pickle barrel’s colorful history … 



the pickle barrel is a 21 and over club.everyone must show proper age identification in order to enter.



directionsthe pickle barrel nightclub is conveniently located midway up the killington road, right next to jax food & games.google map us: 1741 killington road, killington vt 05751
