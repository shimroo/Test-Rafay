   our mission:  know your client.as designers, our objective is simply to focus on the clients' visions on how they want to live; not to impose our personal style upon them.ron alose design group has been in business for more than 29 years, providing design services to new england and south florida, as well as the west coast.  ron is well-known in the design industry, and has been in the business for over 30 years.  ron alose design group specializes in residential, commercial, and hospitality design services, and has won the title as best designer in newport, by newport life magazine.ron alose design group offers expertise in residential space planning, architectural design and interior decoration. our staff can assist with the most modest to most extensive construction, renovation or decoration project. our resources include hard surface building materials, tile, lighting, as well as an extensive fabric, wallpaper and furniture library. we have acquired direct accounts with numerous suppliers, which allows us to pass savings to our clients on many direct purchase items.
