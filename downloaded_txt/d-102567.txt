about la belle elaine’s



la belle elaine’s, nestled in the heart of seattle, boasts the city's most exquisite collection of wedding dresses, jewelry, and accessories. for over 40 years, our expert stylists have been dedicated to helping brides discover their perfect wedding gown. we offer an extensive selection of classic and modern bridal gowns, with prices starting at $1,500, ensuring elegance for every bride. proudly part of the seattle wedding community, la belle elaine’s has sponsored the seattle wedding show for the past 25 years, showcasing our commitment to making your special day truly unforgettable.
