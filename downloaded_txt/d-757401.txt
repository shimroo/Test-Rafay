shalom y’all














jewish students of all backgrounds come to nc hillel to make friends in a warm, accepting environment and to explore what being jewish means to them. for some students, it’s the first time they have a jewish role model. for others, it’s the first time they’re exploring their judaism on their own terms.hillel @ nc state, nc hillel link, and unc chapel hill hillel allow us to serve our students dynamically from the coast to the mountains. click any of the links below to learn more about one of the campus hillels that make up nc hillel.
