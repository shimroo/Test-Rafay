db shoes trade for women & men
db shoes ltd, established in 1920 specialises in the design and supply of wide and deep fitting footwear. we are constantly expanding our range of wide and deep fitting shoes and offer styles for many occasions and activities, sourced from partner factories around the globe.at db shoes ltd we understand that people are unique...and so are their feet. we offer seven fittings - from a high street e fitting up to 8e. with over 400 styles and colours, we have the largest collection of wide fitting shoes available in the uk.
