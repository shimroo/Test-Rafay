equinox has moved! 



for over 25 years, five-time james beard award nominee chef todd gray, and his wife ellen kassoff have been committed to serving their distinct interpretation of mid-atlantic, regional, and seasonal cuisine. equinox’s hyper-local devotion to their community, their producers, their customers, and their employees have defined equinox as one of dc’s most progressive fine dining establishments.




    our story
  
















be our guest. 



 since 1999, equinox has been one of washington dc’s premier catering resources, servicing weddings, corporate events, and everything else under the sun and moon.
