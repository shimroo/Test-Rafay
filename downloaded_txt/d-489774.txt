for over 60 years, our chamber of commerce has served comfort and surrounding communities like boerne, center point, fredericksburg, kerrville, and san antonio. comfort, an unincorporated area under kendall county, precinct 4, relies on our chamber to plan and execute community events. our office also doubles as a visitor center, providing resources for visitors to explore comfort and the region.we compile the chamber membership & community guide, offering information about comfort, kendall county, business members, and community organizations. additionally, we produce a local shopping guide, maps and a downtown comfort historic buildings walking tour.
