honor those who served, enrich our community, and deliver exceptional experiences.















    learn more about our 50% off event special
  



the war memorialwe are your gathering place, your town square, your cultural center, your art museum, your concert venue, your place for special events, your place on the lake to just be.we are an experiential space unlike any other in michigan, where art and music live, where laughter and love are shared, where memories are made, and debates are heard. we are a place that honors american heroism and celebrates everything that makes our country a more perfect union. to schedule a guided tour, contact betsy alexander at balexander@warmemorial.org
