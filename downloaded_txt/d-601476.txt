we buy records

we purchase collections big and small as well as rarer individual records. soul, funk, disco, jazz, latin, brazil, afro, house and reggae, vinyl only. lp's, 12's and 7 inches are all of interest. we pay 40-60% cash or give 50-70% trade value of what we will sell for if the price is over 100 sek for us to sell. the more expensive the record, the higher the percentage. for cheap records under 100 sek we pay a lower percentage. we will travel worldwide and give a 10% finders fee for any tips on collections we end up buying.

email us at info@recordmania.se and lars@recordmania.se

vinylskivor köpes

vi köper lp-skivor, ep:s, maxis och singlar inom speciellt soul, jazz, reggae, vissa rock/progg-skivor etc. vi betalar 40–60% kontant av vad vi säljer skivorna för av de skivor som vi säljer för över 100 kr. de billigare skivorna betalar vi mindre procentuellt för. ring eller maila vad ni har!
