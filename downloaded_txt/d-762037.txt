  apartment housing,
community-minded living
 
riverton community housing offers many different housing options, both apartments and townhomes, close to the university of minnesota east and west bank campuses. riverton has been a leader in providing affordable, quality housing to both students and non-students in the dinkytown, seward, marcy holmes and southeast como neighborhoods of minneapolis.we provide our residents the unique opportunity to live in residences operating under the cooperative principles, which means members can be involved with the decision making process for their co-op. click about us to learn more, or visit our communities to explore our housing options!
