lowest transaction fees and setup costs

                                straightforward pricing with no catches or gimmicks. we seek to provide the lowest transaction fees and setup costs so that the money raised by your organization is being optimized towards your mission. to do so, we also provide the option to pass the transaction fee onto the donor so your organization is not affected, and keeps the full dollar amount of a donation. so what does this mean? free fundraising!
                            

3.5% +$0.30 flat transaction fee for credit/debit
$1 flat transaction fee for e-checks
free tool to upload checks manually
no monthly/set-up fees for basic customization
