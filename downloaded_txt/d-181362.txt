ari hest is a grammy nominated singer/songwriter who began his career as an independent artist in 2001. he built a loyal following touring the college circuit extensively between 2000 and 2003, after which he released two albums on columbia records, someone to tell (2004) and the break-in (2007) all the while touring both in the u.s. and abroad with artists such as martin sexton, suzanne vega, and ani difranco.
