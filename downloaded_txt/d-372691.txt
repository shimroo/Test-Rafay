southern honda® powersports is your honda powerhouse dealer! pretty cool, right? we certainly think so. we pride ourselves on having incredibly low prices and an unbeatable selection with over 100,000 square feet of inventory. price, selection, and transparency are how we built our name. no fees. no nonsense. just a friendly, out-the-door price and a smooth buying process.

because we have some of the best prices and an impressive collection of vehicles, southern honda® has become a destination for riders from georgia, alabama, kentucky, north carolina, south carolina, ohio, and florida. whether you're a gold wing rider from new york or a farmer who needs a pioneer to get around on your land, we’ve got you covered! and if you’re coming in from out of town, let us help you find all the great riding spots around east ridge. come let us show you why we’re unbeatable.

unless otherwise indicated, major unit prices on southernhonda.com are for base model/standard-color motorcycles, scooters, atvs, and muvs. optional accessories, colors, and finishes (i.e. camo, flames, custom stripes, pearl coats, etc.) may be higher. all major unit prices on southernhonda.com include all applicable honda® incentives. prices are valid for cash and finance, but no credit cards for unit purchases, please.
