about us
cedars motors has been a global car trader for more than 30 years, with headquarters in miami, florida usa and offices around the world.
we supply all makes and models from the us and other countries with 100% free export tax.





our global logistic services offer a quick and safe delivery in any country with reasonable prices. we handle every aspect of the delivery, including:


 


global maritime freight reservations



clearing customs



delivery at port



freight loading at port



delivery of transit containers



customs export documentation



maritime insurance
