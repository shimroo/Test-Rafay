ending domestic violence. creating hope. 














transition house is a leader in the field in addressing domestic violence through intervention and prevention. we serve people of all ages and backgrounds. we work toward social equity and system change to end the perpetuation of harm and violence. with deep roots in cambridge, massachusetts for over 45 years, our model is embraced as a shared responsibility and an ongoing priority to end domestic violence in our community . 













get help.we offer many resources if you or someone you know is experiencing domestic violence. learn more here —>if you need immediate assistance, you can call our hotline that operates 24/7 at 617-661-7203













donate.we would not be able to do the work that we do without the generous support of people like you. visit our donate page to learn more about the different ways to support survivors. donate —>













our community work.we believe in partnerships and collaboration with others in our community to end domestic violence and help those impacted by violence. our relationships within cambridge and beyond are key to our work. learn more about what we’re doing here —>














40 stories is a project that we launched in 2016 to end domestic violence and to deepen the understanding of the work that we do. click any image below to be directed to our library of growing stories.
