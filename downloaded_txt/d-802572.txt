get involved 















the pspa hosts activities that support day and boarding parents, as well as students, throughout the year: parent receptions and informal gatherings, both in person and onlineclass and student activities including annual casino night and prom receptionsparent education speakers, in person and live-streamedcampus closet – the home of official phillips academy clothing and gear

shop at the campus closet 



we invite you to shop at campus closet for official phillips academy clothing and gear, both online and in person at our shop in george washington hall. 100% of the profits go toward funding pspa student, parent, and school activities.




    shop online
  










































the mission of the pspa (parents of students of phillips academy) is to build and maintain a community of parents and guardians of phillips academy students.  we also partner with faculty and staff  to supplement and support student activities.
