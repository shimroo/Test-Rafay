traffic management acquires cargo transit of north carolina









top-rated customer service
effective september 1, 2019, traffic management, inc. purchased cargo transit, inc. in an effort to consolidate services and continue providing great customer service to all of cargo transit’s customers.
cargo transit is passionate about helping its customers operate supply chains more effectively and efficiently and they have put tremendous efforts and resources in place to support their commitment. the foundation of the company is its people. team members operate as one cohesive organization with a single-minded focus on customer service. they take immense pride in the strengths and successes team members bring to the organization through the freight solutions offered to customers.
traffic management, centrally located in the twin cities, has been helping businesses move their freight for 25 years and has a great reputation as being a very customer service driven company. cargo transit is also known for its outstanding customer service and their ethical business acumen so we are very excited to be working together.







traffic management, inc.
traffic management is a full-service logistics company shipping a few boxes to full truckloads anywhere in the world. your transportation partners helping you navigate through the world of shipping. we provide freight moving services to large and small businesses, including trucking, shipping, consulting, warehousing, distribution, and import/export.
