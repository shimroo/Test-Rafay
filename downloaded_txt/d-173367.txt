about hbs sa



the sa was established to represent students and act on their behalf to enrich and broaden the hbs experience. the sa operates student activities and services, acts on student concerns, and acts as a liaison between students, faculty, staff, administration, alumni, and the entire university community. the sa is incorporated as a 501(c)(3) non-profit organization.
