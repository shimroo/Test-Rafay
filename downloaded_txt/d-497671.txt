attention:  parking meters are now active 


meters are active monday through saturday from 9am to 6pm. parking for 2 hours $1 an hour. see link below for more information.						read on...







city hall closed monday  in observance of president's day 


city hall will be closed on monday, february 17th, in observance of president's day.  we will reopen tuesday, february 18th at 8:00 a.m.						read on...







motor vehicle excise tax bills due soon 


reminder, motor vehicle excise tax bills are due on monday, march 5, 2025. last year a number of undeliverable bills were to residents in multi-family properties, if you did not receive a bill, please contact the tax collector at 978-630-4008.						read on...







reminder:  2025 dog licenses are due 


2025 dog licenses are now available and due by march 31st. 
city clerk’s office – room 121 – 978.630.4058. if you no longer have a dog that was previously licensed or you've moved, please let us know!						read on...

open burning season 


open air burning permits for yard clean-ups are now available from the fire department. state regulations allow burning from january 15th to may 1st. application and payment for burning permits are now done online!						read on...
