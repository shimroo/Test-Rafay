welcome to 3a marine service
a fixture on the southern end of boston harbor, 3a marine service has been getting massachusetts  boaters out on the water for over 50 years. we are known and respected for our straightforward advice and timely, professional service. whether a recreational boater, a water-based commercial operation, or a public safety agency, all of our customers know they can trust us to maximize their time on the water.

we cover the south shore, boston, north shore, cape cod, and the greater boston area. we are authorized dealers for robalo, parker, and four winns boats, and for evinrude, yamaha, and tohatsu outboards. we also sell tidewater trailers.

we deliver professional-grade service, delivered by our factory-certified technicians. our retail store stocks a large inventory of parts and accessories. 3a marine service is also a u.s. marine industry (mraa) 5-star certified dealer.

whether you are browsing our website, speaking with us over the phone, or visiting in person - welcome.
