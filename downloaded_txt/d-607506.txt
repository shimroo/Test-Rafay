about us

bathroom boyz, is an online store based in pretoria,
we stock most types of shower spares and showers, we also make parts.
you can shop online from the convenience of your home and we will deliver or courier your parcel to you.





delivery

delivery is done via the courier guy
courier guy is r 100 in gauteng and r 110 elsewhere in sa it takes 2 – 3 days (price for small items but larger items will also be sent via this option)
all shower spare orders are dispatched the next working day and will only be dispatched once payment clears.
framed shower doors are subject to a lead time of 5-7 business days.
frameless shower doors are subject to a lead time of 7-15 business days.
shower screens are subject to a lead time of 5-7 business days.
toilet seats are subject to 3-5 business days lead time.
