    what's new 
  














j's art studio has been teaching fun and creative art classes in the north dallas, richardson, and plano area for over 20 years! we encourage our students to explore a variety of different art media while teaching technical skills in acrylic, oil and watercolor painting, drawing, clay and sculpture, as well as sewing, charcoal, and much more. experienced and nurturing teachers in small classes help the artist realize their true creative potential! we teach kids art classes, adult are classes, art parties, summer camps, and winter camps. beginners to advanced students are welcome!


























our classes follow the school semester.















we provide everything the students need from paint brushes to paper mache!















we offer classes for ages 5 up to teens & adults. each class is tailored to students skill level. 














grow their creative confidenceeach creative activity prompt is an opportunity for your child to embrace a new challenge. through hands-on learning, your child is more likely to remain engaged and to build upon their creative problem-solving skills. 
















birthday partieswe offer birthday parties that will be both memorable and fun! with a variety of projects your birthday kid can choose from, everyone will be able to exercise their creative side while celebrating. we provide everything except the cupcakes! come in and let’s talk about your kid’s big day!
