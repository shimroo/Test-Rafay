






the nineteenth century












access to the free online catalogue to the nineteenth century microfiche collection is no longer available through proquest, effective january 13, 2022.
marc records for titles in the microfiche collection, which include serial numbers directing users to the correct microfiche, may be accessed online via the british librarys register of preservation surrogates.
catalogue records are also available as part of c19: the nineteenth century index, to users at subscribing institutions. here they are combined with records from nineteenth-century short title catalogue (nstc) to provide exhaustive coverage of nineteenth-century monographs and other books.
