welcome to rick case honda
davie, fl
welcome to rick case honda powersports in davie, florida. we specialize in new and pre-owned motorcycles, atvs,scooters, side by sides, and lawn and garden equipment from honda and honda power equipment. be sure to visit our factory promotions page for current money-saving specials or check out our articles from rick case honda® powersports to learn more about powersports.

looking for parts, gear and clothing? we carry accessories from fox racing, joe rocket, shoei, and many more. try our easy online shop by vehicle feature for parts that fit your machine. we also offer oem replacement parts. need help finding something in particular? contact us today.

our full service center specializes in repair and maintenance on the brands we carry using genuine honda parts. contact us today to set an appointment!
