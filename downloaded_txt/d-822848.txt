about tramar industries





s&m machinery is now part of tramar industries. tramar industries is the leading independent machinery dealer of surplus used cnc industrial machine tools in the united states. tramar acquired s&m machinery sales to provide customers with even more options for buying and selling used cnc equipment.
by partnering through an acquisition, tramar and s&m expanded our inventory to include thousands of machine tools, including cnc vertical and cnc horizontal machining centers, cnc turning centers, cnc and manual boring mills, edm’s, bridge mills, drill/tap machines, knee mills, lathes, saws, and various toolroom/support equipment available for sale online.
tramar industries has been buying and selling used metalworking machinery for over 35 years and has experience in sales to the united states, canada, and mexico, as well as europe, asia, and south america.
we deal with the most well-known and reliable cnc machine tool brands, including okuma, okk, makino, mazak, fadal, haas, mori seiki, doosan, and many others.
make tramar industries your first call when buying or selling surplus cnc machinery.
