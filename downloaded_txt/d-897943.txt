contact us











rowin is a privately owned belgian company based in heist-op-den-berg (near antwerp) which started up in 1978. we develop and produce pe/eva vacuum hoses according to strict quality and environmental standards.the procedure is unique. it is a specially developed production system for flexible hoses, based on a heat-melting shape, with no welding and no gluing.this advanced technology results in new, better, and less expensive products. rowin develops its own production machines and is an expert in corrugated vacuum hoses. most thermoplastic materials, from very hard to soft and flexible resins, can be applied.

rowin is a privately owned belgian company based in heist-op-den-berg (near antwerp) which started up in 1978. we develop and produce pe/eva vacuum hoses according to strict quality and environmental standards.the procedure is unique. it is a specially developed production system for flexible hoses, based on a heat-melting shape, with no welding and no gluing.this advanced technology results in new, better, and less expensive products. rowin develops its own production machines and is an expert in corrugated vacuum hoses. most thermoplastic materials, from very hard to soft and flexible resins, can be applied. 







 
























ideal for industrial use 



rowin hoses are extensively used for all kinds of industrial applications: car wash, commercial and industrial vacuum cleaners, and central vac-systems,…the durable plastic material can be used in rough environments.the hoses can be handled easily because of their flexibility.the hoses are an economical solution for many technical ducting problems (discharge hose, transport of air, gasses, fluids, granules,…)
