free transportation to osu



at university village, we put your education first. that is why we offer a bus service to the ohio state university campus that runs 90 hours per week, seven days a week. our fleet of seven charter buses ensures our residents get to campus on time. additional perks of this service are community sponsored trips to easton shopping center, mad river ski resort, amc movie theatre, columbus zoo and zoombezi bay, and rides to campus during osu football games.
