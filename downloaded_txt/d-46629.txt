canadian mist





canadian whisky
in 1967, canadian mist was born on the shores of georgian bay, ontario, home to some of the freshest water in north america. using top-quality grains harvested locally in canada, mellowed white oak barrels, and that pristine water, we created an easygoing, classic whisky made well to be enjoyed by all.
