welcome to the knifemakers’ guild!the knifemakers’ guild was established in 1970. the purposes of the guild were, and continue to be: to promote custom knives and knife makers, to assist the knife maker technically, to encourage ethical and professional business conduct, and to sponsor an annual business meeting and knife show.members meet once a year to conduct guild business, elect officers and display their work at the show. hundreds of collectors, sportsmen and cutlery enthusiasts from around the world attend the annual show to view and purchase knives and to meet their favorite artists and craftsmen.
