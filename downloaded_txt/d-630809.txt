june 28, 2024

antique power photo contest 2024

june 28, 2024/



michael adkins










2024 photo contest!: for our second year of the antique power photo contest, we will continue to keep it simple and have a general theme. just as we do in antique power, we welcome photos of your own taking of any antique or classic internal combustion tractor from the early 1900s to the early 1980s. the tractor model and background can be of your choosing. the tractor can be posed in the field, in front of a historical barn or structure, or in a picturesque setting. remember to highlight the tractor so that it stands out from whatever background setting you choose. we really want you to show off your tractors!read more
