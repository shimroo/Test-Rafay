about walkers marine 



welcome to walker's marine. 



walker’s marine is a full-service and top-ranked florida boat dealer. we are your authorized southwest florida dealer for new boat makes including tiara yachts, hcb yachts, pursuit, regal, maverick, cobia, hewes, pathfinder, and harris pontoon boats. we have been proudly serving boaters for over 30 years. by combining exceptional products with outstanding service, we are committed to help you discover the superior lifestyle you deserve.
