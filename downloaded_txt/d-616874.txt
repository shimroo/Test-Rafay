electrical sales associates began in march 1992 with only four team members. three decades later, electrical sales associates continues to be a growing agency.   our team is committed to representing several manufacturers in the electrical and lighting industry for washington, oregon, alaska, and northern idaho.

esa is your premier northwest manufacturer representative agency.  esa represents both lighting and electrical manufacturers. we pride ourselves with “excellent service always.”  esa is a full-service agency.  we offer layouts and complete submittal packages.  our team is second to none with unparalleled customer service and product knowledge.  pictured to the left is our display trailer used for training and distributor shows. 














 mission statementit is our mission to provide our customers with unparalleled service and product knowledge that exceeds all expectations. our business is built on integrity. our customers make it possible for us to be and stay in business. we continually look for ways to improve our quality and add value to our services. we do so by improving ourselves as a team.
