margaret keane's art















 

keen look, inc. keen look, inc. has been privileged to market the works of margaret keane for over 40 years, we join with her countless friends and admirers in mourning her passing.
we continue to work with her daughter, jane, in providing, in her memory, her art work available as greeting cards, double matted cards, posters and refrigerator magnets.
