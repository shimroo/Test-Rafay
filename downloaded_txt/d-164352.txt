nationwide flowers online
founded in kansas city, mo, kcflorist.com was quickly recognized by forbes and other business publications as a 
                                    leader in providing a convenient, economical, and secure way to send flowers online, while still offering the personal service of a real 
                                    local florist. early innovations, such as nationwide delivery brought kcflorist to national prominence, and its simple, no-nonsense 
                                    approach to flower sending brought customers from near and far. today, kcflorist has become one of the most trusted nationwide 
                                    florists and continues to specialize in deliveries throughout kansas, missouri, the u.s.a. and canada too. our gifts are individually 
                                    designed and locally delivered by real florists just like us. our commitment to you is simple, to provide beautiful fresh flowers,
