home













our blog was created for those who love to travel. with or without money – it does not matter. and not even so: we sincerely believe that everyone can travel with money, but the idea of maximum budget is close to us much more.
we write mostly about how you can save money somewhere and on something. our subscriber is a sophisticated reader. hitchhiking, couchsurfing, volunteering – all these little things our subscribers know for a long time, and europe is traveled around, as well as most of the countries in southeast asia.
of course, we also write about europe, and about expensive travels: our audience is very diverse. we will write about everything except tours and turkish all-inclusives.

travel with pleasure


we have a completely free format, and the style of writing is interesting, informative and understandable.
interesting is about lightness, a sense of humor, and uniqueness. and all points are equally important. the article can be very useful and unique, but if it is not interesting or difficult to read, the uniqueness of the text, no one will appreciate, and vice versa.
informative – is about useful links, marks on the map, photos, screenshots, tips from personal experience, etc. all this makes the article more useful.
understandable is about how the text is perceived by readers. our articles have no lexical stamps and no water.
