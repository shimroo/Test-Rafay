our roots run deep.














our love branches out across the united states and around the globe.we are a diocese of rooted, missional, and compassionate disciples of jesus who share what we have with the world around us.


























"seguimos el llamado de jesús de ir y hacer discípulos de todos los pueblos, ya sea al otro lado de la calle o al otro lado de las fronteras internacionales''



rvdmo. juan alberto moralesnoveno obispo de la diócesis de quincy



"we follow jesus' call to go and make disciples of all peoples, whether across the street or across international borders."



rt. rev. juan alberto morales ninth bishop of the diocese of quincy.


























rooted



our roots run deep in our faith in jesus christ and in the teachings and traditions of the historic church.




    find a church
  


























missional



we follow jesus’ call to go and make disciples of all peoples, whether across the street or across international borders.




    join us
  


























compassionate



in all we do, we strive to do in the love of jesus, having hearts like jesus, and treating others with the compassion jesus has for all people.
