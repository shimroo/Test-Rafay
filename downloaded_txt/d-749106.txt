welcome to fimor north america 



formerly harkness industries 



 fimor north america manufactures and distributes a complete range of polyurethane cast distribution products, custom molding manufacturing, and warehouses fimor le mans squeegee parts and sheets for distribution from our facility located in cheshire, ct.    formerly known as harkness industries, harkness became a part of fimor in 2017.  the cheshire, ct manufacturing site has helped thousands of customers in north america develop quality driven parts in quantities ranging from one unique piece to thousands of custom molded parts since 1962. with its network of qualified industrial plastics distributors, fimor north america is well represented across the united states, canada, many central and south american countries, and parts of asia
