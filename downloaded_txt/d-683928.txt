learn how to engage, present, and persuade. gain a strong foundation in the liberal arts while learning to gather evidence, think critically, evaluate sources, and communicate your findings.

the school of communication develops and enhances understanding of communication processes and their effects on individuals, organizations, culture, and society. the school meets its mission through discovery, learning, and engagement focused on creation, distribution, and interpretation of messages through media systems. we create and share knowledge about the interaction of messages and media within historical, economic, technological, and cultural environments.


the school of communication offers five undergraduate majors: advertising, communication, multimedia journalism, public relations, and sports media and analytics. these majors lead to a b.a. in communication.
