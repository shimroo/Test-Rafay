our mission
the windy city rollers is a nonprofit organization that strives to promote athleticism and fraternity among its members and seeks to foster professional, personal and athletic advancement for all its members. whenever possible, the organization shall support and promote its members in all their pursuits both on and off the track. we strive to advance and grow our organization from within, cultivating the blatant and latent talents of all members.learn more
