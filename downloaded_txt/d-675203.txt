antenna products






since 1947, antenna products has been a leading designer and manufacturer of antenna systems for military, government and commercial use.  widely regarded as the world leading provider of high-powered high frequency (hf) broadband antenna, apc has successfully delivered antennas for notable projects such as haarp, hfgcs, nato brass and most recently, australia’s project phoenix.  in addition, apc is one of the primary suppliers of both navaid and comms antennas to the faa.iso 9001:2015 certified, apc operates its vertically integrated design, manufacturing and testing operations out of its 100,000+ square facilities in mineral wells, tx. 













expertise and history				










 




 




antenna products  (in conjunction with prime contractor bae advanced technologies) designed and built the antenna system for the world’s largest antenna array — the haarp array in anchorage, alaska.  video credits:  producer/editor/shooter: harun mehmedinovic  music: richard lacy & sarah macdonald 













antenna products






since 1947, antenna products has been engineering and assembling antenna systems and receiving wire frameworks for military and safeguard, government, avionics, transportation, and remote foundation industry fragments.  we’ve been part of this industry longer than any antenna manufacturers, and because of this, we have been leaders of industry for 72 years.antenna products manufactures vertically integrated receptor antennas with practically all innovative work, designing, testing, assembling and generation, and technical support housed inside our 100,000+ square foot office in mineral wells, texas.
