squeek’s youtube channelsqueek’s video collection is a fun and amazing journey through time — from a video christmas card with the philadelphia 76ers to how to move a piano to west coast ragtime festivals. we think it’s time to share these long-hidden gems with the world along with her more current recordings. we will continue to postread more
squeek at the bucketsqueek plays regularly at the bucket of blood saloon in virginia city. most months you can find her there friday through monday. her performance dates and times vary, so be sure to take a look at her public appearances calendar before you make the trek up to the historic mining town.
