contact the chamber  welcomeabout the chamber the wahpeton breckenridge area chamber of commerce is a non-profit organization of the wahpeton nd and breckenridge mn area business community. it connects business, industry, and professional individuals to improve the economy and build a more vibrant and prosperous community. the strength of the chamber lies in its membership. collectively we can accomplish what no one could do individually.if you are seeking exposure, creative ways to reach new customers/market your business, or position your company as a community leader and contribute to the growth and development of our communities being a chamber member is a cost effective choice for you!  we would love to help you find the right opportunities for your business to achieve the
