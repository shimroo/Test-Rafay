delivering excellence since 1995 






a full service experience 



 at event rentals, we strive to be the unanimous & desired provider of event equipment & event service within the communities we call home.  we exist to provide exceptional service to those who plan events, host parties, and attend events. our focus is excellence each day – in customer service, in inventory quality, and in overall value.   we provide event equipment rentals ranging from tents, chairs, tables & linens, to catering equipment, lighting, staging, & dance floors.  no event is too big or too small. we are a family owned and operated business that truly cares about you and the success of your event. we want you to have the most successful event possible, from beginning to end. we look forward to serving you & your next event!
