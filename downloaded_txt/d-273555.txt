city of la verne adopts updated general plan 

the city of la verne unanimously adopted its updated general plan, guiding growth, sustainability, and development while reflecting community priorities and state law.				read on...











grand reopening of las flores park playground showcases safety and accessibility upgrades 

la verne celebrates the reopening of las flores park playground with safety, accessibility, and modern upgrades. new features include impact-safe surfaces, shade, and inclusive ramps. now open!				read on...











la verne city council unanimously supports proposition 36 

the city council voted unanimously to support proposition 36, known as the homelessness, drug addiction, and theft reduction act. this measure was introduced in response to the significant rise in homelessness, drug addiction, and theft in california.				read on...











niagara cares and the city of la verne team up to break ground at las flores park 

niagara cares joined la verne city officials to celebrate the groundbreaking of upgrades to las flores park. the park received a $75,000 grant through the 2023 perk your park project to create more vibrant and accessible public playground.				read on...
