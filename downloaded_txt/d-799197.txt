about pep-c



the peninsula emergency preparedness coalition (pep-c) is an all-volunteer 501(c)3 washington nonprofit corporation founded in 1993. pep-c promotes personal, neighborhood and business resilience and self-sufficiency for at least 21 days in preparation for natural disasters and man-made disruptions. pep-c collaborates with local, state and federal agencies to disseminate pertinent information through literature and speakers and to facilitate community training and exercises.learn more.
