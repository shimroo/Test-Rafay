connecticut

trailers & powersports

connecticut trailers is a family-owned, full-service, premier trailer dealer serving greater new england and beyond since 1997. we double the manufacturer's warranty on all new trailers, giving you peace of mind when purchasing from us. we also assure you the best quality product for your hard-earned dollars. we are an exclusive seller of natm-compliant manufacturers. as one of the northeast's largest inventory holders, we are proud to offer industry-leading brands.

our unique fabrications service can help you get the upgrade you want with the trailer you currently own. of course, if you'd prefer to trade it in, we can help with that, too! our convenient on-site dmv registration and dot inspections simplify your buying experience while our dedicated parts and service team keep your trailer or rv running smoothly. we also now offer golf carts for sale or for rental. we're committed to giving our customers the best experience possible with the convenience you deserve.
