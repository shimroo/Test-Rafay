ready to grow your future?
explore our horticulture classes at our new, state-of-the-art facilities! whether
                           you're passionate about landscape architecture, organic gardening, or floral design,
                           we have something for you.class schedule




free college for students!
southwestern college’s promise program offers up to two years of enrollment completely
                           free. and you can join our super financial aid workshops where staff will help you
                           complete your fafsa or dream act applications. contact the financial aid office to
                           find out if you qualify today!financial aid




interested in careers in the fitness and wellness industry?
our fitness specialist program is designed to train and qualify students to function
                           as entry-level, intermediate and advance group exercise leaders and personal trainers.
                           take your first step with us today!class schedule




become a certified yoga teacher!
join our yoga teacher training program and earn your certification in just one semester!
                           learn in a supportive cohort model and take your yoga journey to the next level.class schedule




black history month flag raising
be part of a momentous occasion as we raise the black history month flag together.
                           join us on february 11, 2025 at the corner of east h street and otay lakes road for
                           a ceremonial flag raising. experience the pride and unity of our community as we honor
                           our heritage and future.events calendar
