2025 entertainment




















parade
tuesday, july 22                                     this opening day favorite kicks-off in painesville city and travels west down mentor avenue to the fair. with over 200 units, this a must see event!


















dirt night motor mayhem
wednesday, july 23 will be a night of unforgettable action in the grandstands. there will be half car racing, a rough truck course, figure 8 racing and an autocross track.  bring the entire family to see the dirt night motor mayhem!
 


















moto-x racing

thursday, july 24 rogers racing promotions returns with this event featuring the area’s best on-site built course. this race is part of the 2024 ohio motocross association (oma) faircross series. count on some serious competition from more than 150 entries.  registration starts at 4 pm/6 pm practice/7 pm racing!


























koi drag racing

dirt track drag racing returns again this year but will be held on friday, july 26. you will enjoy this side-by-side, fast paced, open class racing on a very tight track. registration and practice starting at 5 pm/eliminations at 7 pm.


















truck and tractor pulls

cs pulling promotions will bring the truck and tractor pulls to the lake county fair on saturday, july 26 featuring 410 super stock trucks, light limited super stock tractors, street diesel trucks, pro stock trucks and modified tractors. event starts at 6:30, so don't be late.


















demo derby

sunday, july 27,  derby dog returns with the lake county demo derby. featuring motor swap full-size, street stock full-size, street stock compact, windshield, full-size van, plus a kid’s power wheels class.  sponsored by excalibur auto body. rules and info at www.derbydogdemo.com.


























jungle terry

lake county fair favorite jungle terry presents his own popular exotic version of entertainment with 15 free on-grounds shows throughout the week. sponsored by richard k. greaves, cpa


















farm bureau – merhar insurance agency entertainment tent
this year’s line-up is not to be missed. enjoy an event every evening. there is something for everyone to enjoy! click on the link for the entertainment tent schedule.


















all-american pig races
don't miss the fun and excitement of the all-american pig races at this year's lake county fair.  this will be a free on grounds show held multiple times per day on the main midway.  get up close to this family friendly event and be part of the fun.  sponsored by tour lake county and the lake county commissioners.


















whiz bang science show
entertaining and engaging  demonstrations, unique stories and understandable explanations of amazing and baffling scientific concepts will be performed on the fair midway beginning with an extended show on kids day, july 25, cintinuing through the duration of the fair.
