old city’s favorite neighborhood bar with new orleans-inspired cuisinethe kitchen offers philadelphia’s finest food (including vegan options) and is specialized in southern, cajun, and creole cuisines. with 22 rotating craft drafts, a wide selection of craft bottles, wine, classic cocktails and an extensive selection of brown spirits, the khyber pass pub is offering a fun and elevated bar program in addition to the food elements. the back bar was crafted by german woodworkers and used in the centennial celebration in 1876 prior to its use at khyber pass. the upstairs has six rotating crafts with a full bar and a large space for dancing, live music, art shows, private parties and more.
