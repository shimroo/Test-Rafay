joke of the day



three turtles



three turtles 
                    decided to have a cup of coffee. just as they got into the
                    cafe, it started to rain. the biggest turtle said to the 
                    smallest one, " go
                    home and get the umbrella."
                    the little turtle replied, "i will, if you don't drink 
                    my coffee."
                    "we won't," the other two promised.
                    two years later the big turtle said to the middle 
                    turtle, "well, i guess he
                    isn't coming back, so we might as well drink his coffee."
                    
                    just then a voice called from outside the door, "if you 
                    do, i won't go."
