about us  
theresa’s country feed & pet is the largest family-owned pet store in all of southern california. we have pet food for all types of pets as well as all kinds of supplies at low prices. you are going to find more than 30,000 different items at our store, which makes it easy to find something for the most fickle of pets. additionally, our staff is trained to answer any questions you have about the products we sell, what would be the best options for your specific pet, or any of the different services we offer.contact us 
today to learn more about our store or to sign up for any of the different pet care services we offer. we look forward to becoming your favorite pet store throughout ventura & los angeles county.


  


   
welcome to theresa's country feed & pet! since 1983, we have been building our reputation throughout ventura county as the place to go when you want honest customer service professionals backed with excellent product knowledge and very competitive prices. we take the time to educate and train our
staff
to be the best at what they do, which allows them to more effectively meet you and your pet’s needs. this doesn’t just include offering you great prices on pet supplies, but also being your source for many pet care services you require.  when you come and visit our stores, you are going to discover one of the largest inventories of brand-name pet foods and supplies in the area.
