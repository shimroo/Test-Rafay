our family invites you to visit alto vineyardssouthern illinois’ oldest, most celebrated winery

































“wine is what we do best, and it’s what we are all about.” 
-paul renzaglia



   






















 alto vineyards’ grapes have deep roots in tradition, family, and community. following our first vintage in 1988, we founded the rebirth of the grape growing and winemaking industry in southern illinois. we serve our finely-crafted wines with artful events and entertainment at two spacious and vibrant tasting rooms in alto pass and champaign. we have amassed over 1000 state, national, and international awards and are currently offering 22 premier wines.come for the wine, stay for everything else! join us for a unique journey and memorable experiences among the vines.

































guy renzaglia came to southern illinois and recreated his italian homeland by starting the first vineyard and winery in the beautiful shawnee hills.



 

“one man’s dream becomes an illinois tradition”
