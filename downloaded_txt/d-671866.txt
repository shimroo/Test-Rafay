in memory of steven salomon elbaumsteven elbaum joined the madison board in march 2019 serving on the brooklyn advisory board, on the board of directors (2019-2025) and most recently as chairman of the board (2023-2025). his impact on our organization was profound, setting a standard of excellence and compassion that will forever be woven into the fabric of our institution.alongside his wife, sister, and brother-in-law, steven co-founded the elbaum family clubhouse, aimed at providing support for youth in brownsville, brooklyn—his childhood neighborhood. the elbaum family clubhouse is a testament to steven’s commitment to helping others and ensuring that the next generation had opportunities to succeed.

celebrating nearly 140 years of helping young people build strong character and realize their full potential as responsible citizens and leaders















our clubs are strategically located in neighborhoods with need throughout new york city. 93% of members reported that they “feel safe from harm at this club."



safe place















our caring youth development professionals serve as positive role models to our members. 95% of members feel that "the adults at this club care about me."
