welcome.










datura online

datura online is your studio for belly dance, offering a variety of classes, talks, and films covering everything from technique to cultural context, all taught by passionate and experienced instructors.live stream schedule











in-depth training

the 8 elements of belly dance is a comprehensive training approach blending technique and theory, to help cultivate personal growth, mastery, and innovation in belly dance​​.learn more.











studio datura

studio datura, established in 2010 in portland, oregon, offers a warm and welcoming space for belly dance and movement arts, specializing in fusion and raqs sharqi, with a strong community focus.studio calendar











events

workshops offer dancers a chance to dive deeply into rachel’s techniques, creative processes, and unique approach to belly dance, reaching students around the world. see scheduled events
