carmart – your local used car dealer in morayfield


here at carmart, we’ve been providing morayfield and surrounding areas with quality used cars for over 28 years. we’re proud to be a family owned and operated business that understands the importance of genuine, friendly customer service.
at carmart, we specialise in used 4×4’s and commercial vehicles, but also stock a wide range of vehicle makes and models across our two dealership locations. our used car yards’ stock over 100 quality used cars from stylish hatchbacks and sedans, to durable 4×4’s and utes.  it’s important to the team at carmart that our valued customers drive away in a used vehicle that is perfectly suited to their needs, lifestyle and budget.
the team at carmart enjoy forming long lasting relationships with all customers that walk in our morayfield used car dealership. that’s why we offer a range of services to help customers throughout the entire buying process and with the ongoing care of their vehicle.
if you’ve found your perfect second hand car, we’ll help you drive it home sooner with our range of finance, insurance and extended warranty solutions tailored to suit your needs. we’ll help you take care of your vehicle maintenance in the years to come at our morayfield vehicle service centre. if you have a car you’d like to sell, we also offer all our customers a fair and accurate trade-in value to help you save money on your purchase.
if you have any questions about our quality used cars or range of services, please contact a member of the carmart team today, or drop by our morayfield used car dealership to view our range.
