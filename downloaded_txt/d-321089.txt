      our strategic projects
  















                            careers in stem
                        


                            explore in-demand roles and learn about the career opportunities you can unlock with a unsw science degree.
                        




















                            what is a scientist?
                        


                            what is a scientist? is challenging common misconceptions by resetting the lens on what a scientist’s career, journey, and background looks like.
                        




















                            equity diversity & inclusion
                        


                            we believe diversity and inclusion are essential to our success, helping to produce robust, credible and pioneering science of global impact and train the next generation of scientists.
                        




















                            research impact
                        


                            through creative collaborations with leading research institutes, our scientists create knowledge for the benefit of the planet & develop innovative solutions to the world’s most daunting challenges.
                        




















                            sustainability
                        


                            we’re committed to leading the community in sustainable research practices.
                        




















                            student opportunities
                        


                            we offer a range of student opportunities that extend beyond the lab and lecture theatre.
                        




















                            research centres & institutes
                        


                            unsw science research centres support the generation of new ideas and advance science through their work in areas including clean water, renewable energy, human health and a sustainable environment.
                        




















                            unsw institute for climate risk & response
                        


                            empowering business, government and society to address the risks and opportunities of climate change through interdisciplinary research, partnerships and education.
