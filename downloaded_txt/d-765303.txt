welcome to seel’s outboard in charleston, south carolina​ 



seels outboard, inc. is a family owned and operated business. established in 1964 and incorporated in 1974 – we are celebrating 50 plus years in the boating industry. we invite you to visit our dealership showrooms in charleston and mount pleasant, south carolina. view our wide range of boat models on display – your boating adventures are endless in possibilities with a boat from seel’s outboard.
