about malco theatres

malco theatres, inc. is a prominent movie theater chain in the united states, known for its rich history and commitment to providing an exceptional cinematic experience. established in 1915, the company has grown to become a leading player in the entertainment industry, with a reputation for quality service and innovation.

    founded: 1915
    founder: malco theatres was established by the lightman family, starting with a single theater in memphis, tennessee.
    early years: the company's early focus was on providing affordable entertainment to local communities. over the decades, malco expanded its operations and became known for its modern facilities and customer-focused approach.
    growth: by the mid-20th century, malco theatres had grown significantly, opening numerous locations across the southeastern united states.

operations

    locations: malco theatres operates over 37 locations across multiple states, including tennessee, arkansas, mississippi, kentucky, louisiana and missouri.
    theaters: the company boasts a diverse range of theaters, from traditional multiplexes to luxury cinemas featuring the latest in projection and sound technology.
    features: many malco locations offer amenities such as comfortable seating, digital projection, and enhanced sound systems to enhance the moviegoing experience.
    community involvement: malco is deeply invested in the communities it serves, often engaging in local events and supporting charitable causes.

unique features

    luxury and comfort: malco theatres is known for its luxurious seating options, including recliners and spacious seating arrangements, designed to provide maximum comfort.
    advanced technology: the chain is committed to staying at the forefront of cinema technology, with many theaters featuring the latest in digital projection and sound systems.
    food and beverage: malco offers a wide range of food and beverage options, from traditional popcorn and soda to gourmet items like burgers and specialty coffees.
    exclusive screenings: the company frequently hosts special events, including early screenings, film festivals, and community events.

innovation and future outlook

    digital transition: malco theatres has embraced digital projection and sound, enhancing the overall viewing experience and ensuring high-quality presentations.
    expansion plans: the company continues to explore opportunities for growth, with plans to expand its footprint and offer even more advanced theater experiences in new locations.
    sustainability: malco is committed to sustainability and is implementing eco-friendly practices across its theaters, including energy-efficient lighting and waste reduction programs.
