art festivals



arts festivals are a great place to shop for unique items, meet artists, and grab a bite to eat while listening to live entertainment. there’s truly something for everyone! participate in larac's  50+ year tradition of fine arts festivals.




    upcoming festivals
  

















larac’s artist retreat at wiawaka - may 30th - june 1st, 2025



larac’s artist retreat at wiawaka returns in spring 2025!  visual artists, performing artists, composers, writers and anyone working in the arts can come and stay for the weekend to refresh and restore their artistic practice, either actively creating work or in quiet introspection, inspired by wiawaka’s beautiful setting on lake george.for more info on this amazing opportunity, accommodations, fee, activities and how to register, click artist retreat button below.
