please join us and our community of parents, teachers, businesses, and local residents as we ensure that every child has an enriching and well-rounded educational experience in the menlo park city school district. with your donations, we can continue to enable excellence in our schools by investing in arts and science, innovation and technology, guidance and wellness, and in our teachers and their professional development.  want to get involved? volunteer!















donatemake your donation today to support teachers and innovative curriculum at encinal, laurel, oak knoll and hillview. 















what we fundwhen we support the foundation, we enable excellence in our schools.















become a business or realtor partnerour local business community is a vital part of the mpaef and our school district.














what’s happening now












every month, mpaef partners with a local restaurant on a special dining promotion. yummy food + supporting our schools = win, win!














mpaef partners with local businesses to provide a variety of shopping promotions throughout the school year that benefit you and our schools. happy shopping!














our annual spring auction party will take place at hillview middle school on saturday, march 15, 2025 followed by the online auction from march 18-21, 2025.
