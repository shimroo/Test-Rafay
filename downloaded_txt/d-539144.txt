permit center
the building and planning department responsibilities include permit approvals, plan review, building inspections, comprehensive plan updates, and enforcement of the city's building, planning and zoning codes.




council agenda packets
the buckley city council consists of seven members who are directly elected by citizens to four-year terms. the city council is the legislative and policy making body of the city.

police department
in case of emergency dial 911 the buckley police department provides a high level of police services to the citizens of buckley. the department is committed to a community policing philosophy, which is achieved through continued training and education.
