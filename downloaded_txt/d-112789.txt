the santa cruz art league 














one of california’s most significant and historic arts organizations, the santa cruz art league inspires creativity and community through vibrant visual and performing arts.




    our vision
  



come visit us!office hours: wednesday-saturday, 11am-5pmgallery hours: wednesday-saturday 12pm-5pm**gallery hours subject to change if closed for installation. view our gallery calendar here.

exhibitions



movement: a moment in timenationwide photography exhibitionjanuary 17, 2025 – february 22, 2025 juried by local photographer devi pride, this exhibition is inspired by the magic of motion in our everyday lives. fullness emptinesssusie wilson, soo choi, and heejin leemarch 7, 2025 – april 12, 2025 fullness & emptiness is the rhythm of life; a heartbeat is the sound of it, the tides are the creation of it, human experience is the living of it, and art is the translation of it.

become a member



scal members enjoy discounted classes, higher commission rates, access to our exclusive member’s exhibition, and many other perks. becoming a member of our community helps us continue to provide a space for local artists to come together.




    learn more about membership
  




    join our mailing list
  















contribute



donate or volunteer with us to support your local arts community! the santa cruz arts league has always been a community-centered organization, and we rely on the generosity of our friends and members to continue providing a space for our county’s incredible artists.
