college of arts and letters










welcome
welcome to the college of arts and letters at california state university, san bernardino!
we are a dynamic and caring community of students, faculty, and staff members united by a passion for the arts and humanities. our college inspires students and prepares them for the rapidly changing cultural and employment environment of the 21st century. our classes are taught by faculty who are experts in their fields, providing students with tools for success in a world that values written and oral communication skills, analysis, critical thinking, and creativity.
