new beginnings and legacies continued.









































































































































ferio tego® is never to arrive, but rather to voyage, making brief stops along the way, recognizing achievements and failures alike; and, with humility and thoughtfulness, adjusting course if necessary, to continue the journey.




    our brands
  














ferio tego is a premium cigar and accessories company launched in 2021 by michael herklots and brendon scott.  in addition to their flagship ferio tego® brand, the company also owns the timeless® collection, metropolitan® selection, and epoca® brands of premium cigars as well as the ancora® accessories brand.  ferio tego’s cigars are manufactured by hand in the dominican republic, nicaragua, and honduras.




    our story
  
















ferio tego is choosing to dare; to take risks and follow dreams, rather than to settle. we continue a legacy of honor in all dealings. 




    our team
  
















 ferio tego is neither winning nor losing but trying and doing. it is not pass or fail, but rather the work that remains to be done.




    news & releases
  
















ferio tego® is having faith and trust in oneself and others, to do the right thing even though it may be harder, knowing the outcome will be true and pure.
