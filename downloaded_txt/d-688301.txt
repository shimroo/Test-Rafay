hijacker 5th wheel hitches




demco acquired the complete hijacker product line of fifth wheel hitches from victoria industries in 2011. victoria industries ltd. designed and manufactured trailer hitches and other recreational vehicle accessories for north american and international markets for 41 years.
victoria industries was known for its hijacker brand of hydraulic jacks, fifth-wheel hitches, under carriage receivers and camper steps.
demco kept the hijacker brand, adding to its own line of rv and marine towing products. the company diversified into the rv market after making a name for itself in the agricultural sector with field sprayers and grain-handling equipment.
the line of hitches included: the revolutionary autoslide, ums underbox mounting system, ultra slider 5th wheel hitch, ultra slide, and gooseneck 5th wheel hitches.
