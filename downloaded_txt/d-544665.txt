what started out as a small community grocery store and gathering spot in leiper's fork, tennessee, grew into what the puckett's brand is today. with locations throughout tennessee and alabama, puckett's home-grown recipes, southern hospitality, and amazing live music performances have earned us many accolades throughout the years. puckett's has been named one of tennessee’s top 5 local restaurants in southern living’s the south’s best awards and one of the 10 best barbecue restaurants in the u.s. in travelawaits best of travel awards. y’all come visit us soon!
