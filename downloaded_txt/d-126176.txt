town center tif #2 lawsuit will go to trial 


the village was hopeful negotiations with school districts 34 and 94 would produce a compromise and finally put this divisive matter behind us. unfortunately, this optimism was misplaced.						read on...






town center redevelopment 


town center development has been held up over 1186 days because of the lawsuit filed by school district 34. visit our dedicated webpage for the latest information on town center development, winfield tif #2 and the lawsuit.			additional info...






paperless billing now available 


the village of winfield is pleased to announce that we have added a new paperless billing solution. customers can now choose to receive their monthly bills via email.						read on...






online payment options 


the village has online payment options administered through accessmygov.com which allows residents to pay utility bills, police fines and parking permit renewals online using major credit cards and electronic checks.  a fee will apply.						read on...

need help? dial 211 in dupage county 


dupage county has launched 211, a free, confidential information and referral service that provides a central access point to local health and human service 24 hours a day, 365 days a year. learn more at 211dupage.gov or call 211.			





watch village board meetings 


watch the village board meetings live at 7 pm on comcast channel 10 and at&t u-verse channel 99 on the first and third thursdays of each month. meetings are replayed on alternate thursdays and can also be viewed in the media center on this website.			additional info...
