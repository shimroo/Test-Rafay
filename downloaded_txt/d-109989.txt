manitoba schools’ science symposium



the manitoba schools’ science symposium (msss) is the largest annual science event held for students throughout manitoba. msss is held during the month of april at the university of manitoba and is open to all students from grades 4 to 12.



























join us from april 11-13, 2025 for the 54th annual msss fair. we are excited to share that we’ll back at the max bell centre, university of manitoba.
