                    a full-service provider for government agencies and contractors                

sikich is a leader in providing specialized services for governmental agencies and programs, as well as commercial clients. we support government accountability and assist our clients in ensuring taxpayer money and data are properly handled. by validating and questioning our clients’ documentation, we help to establish that funds and systems are being used appropriately and purposefully.
our work requires equal amounts of precision and passion as we strive to contribute to an efficient government that is best able to serve its people.

                    section 508 compliance                

the rehabilitation act of 1978 was amended in 1998 to require federal agencies to make electronic and information technology (eit) accessible to people with disabilities. under section 508, agencies must provide disabled people access to information comparable to that available to others. sikich cpa llc will provide our federal clients with deliverables, including draft and final audit reports, in an electronic format that is accessible to people with disabilities via screen readers or voice-recognition software. the eit standard can be found at: https://www.section508.gov/
