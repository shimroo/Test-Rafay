  mission statement

the rockford chamber of commerce (rcc) is a private, nonprofit, membership-supported organization. by consolidating resources from several hundred businesses large and small, the chamber becomes an efficient and cost-effective resource for serving business members. we are comprised of businesses, organizations  and individuals working collectively to build a strong economy and improve the quality of life in our community.   vision statement

the chamber supports and advocates the growth of member businesses through member programs and collaboration with government, school and community organizations.
  "the rockford chamber of commerce is always there to support local small businesses. they are open to new ideas and are always striving to serve us better."
