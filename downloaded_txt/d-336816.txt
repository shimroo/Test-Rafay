cooperation for service, not profit.
welcome to the amalgamated houses. our website is intended for our cooperators and former cooperators, people applying for membership and those thinking of applying, for historians and students of the cooperative movement, and for everyone interested in this historic and living cooperative community.
founded in 1927, amalgamated is the oldest limited equity housing cooperative in the united states. we still provide affordable housing and a strong community for almost 1500 moderate-income families. democratically governed, we remain committed to cooperative principles. community activities are available for all ages and a wide range of interests.

-abraham e. kazan, founder, president, 1927-1967 manager, 1927-1957 “the father of cooperative housing in the united states”
