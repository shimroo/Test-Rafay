as always our full menu of cakes and flavors are available when ordering 5 business days in advance. please call 615.354.9555 to place special orders.

   

shipping:only bundt cakes can be shipped. two day shipping is required for all orders. due to carrier restrictions, orders can only be shipped tuesday and wednesday for arrival on thursday or friday. cakes can be shipped overnight on thursday for a friday delivery. we take precaution in packing your cakes so that they will arrive in the best possible manner. cakes shipped in warmer weather will require cooling in the refrigerator before removing from interior packaging. 

 

allergy disclaimer: 

 

while many of our products do not contain nuts, we are not a nut free bakery.  products may come into contact with tree nuts or peanuts.
