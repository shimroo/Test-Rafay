if you are looking for a place to call home, please contact our city office.701-322-4343 monday - friday 9am - 1pm central time
mcville has a municipal 9-hole grass-green golf course. our paved bike path leads to the mcville dam located on the east edge of the city. here you can fish, swim, camp and small-hp boat as well as enjoy a 9-hole frisbee golf course. we have manicured baseball and football fields with a running track around the football field.
our community has well-lit, paved streets and a business district boasting several businesses to meet your needs.
 
mcville is an equal opportunity provider.
