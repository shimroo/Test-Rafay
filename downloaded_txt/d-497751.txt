advanced radiology imaging centeras the largest imaging provider in the state and one of the largest in the country, advanced radiology offers a complete range of imaging services including digital 3d mammography, x-ray, mri/mra,ct/cta, pet/ct, nuclear medicine, ultrasound, dxa for bone density measurement and more. you will find us in one of the many convenient outpatient locations in anne arundel, baltimore, baltimore city, carroll, harford and howard counties and in six area hospitals; greater baltimore medical center (gbmc), and university of maryland baltimore washington medical center (bwmc), harford memorial hospital, st. joseph medical center, upper chesapeake medical center.all referrals and most insurances accepted. foreign language interpretation services available upon request. all locations are acr and radsite accredited.
