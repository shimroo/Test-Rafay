welcome to the wayne pike building industry association!

we’re dedicated to supporting families and professionals in the building industry. our goal is to be your trusted resource for all things related to building and remodeling in our community.
building or remodeling?
before you choose a contractor, reach out to the wayne pike bia! we’re here to help you make informed decisions with confidence. as a non-profit organization, we proudly represent over 150 local professionals in the building industry. we’re also part of the pennsylvania builders association and the national association of home builders.
let us help you find the right contractor to create a wonderful space for your family!
