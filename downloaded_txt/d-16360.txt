economic development up-date october 


new projects break ground, a large renewable project in east kern heads to a hearing and nearly major traffic projects receive funding.						read on...







city projects win a pair of asce awards 


valley park and the 2023 grind and overlay project received outstanding awards from the american society of civil engineers.						read on...

partnering with other local law enforcement agencies, parole/probation checks and active warrant searches led to seven arrests.						read on...
