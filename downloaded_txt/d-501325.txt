washington dc apartments in the historic neighborhood of mclean gardens
 
welcome to your new home located in washington, dc, just minutes from historical landmarks such as the national cathedral. vaughan place apartments and townhomes feature modern kitchens with stainless steel appliances, in unit washer/dryer, and private patios with scenic views of the surrounding greenery. your pet friendly community right outside cathedral heights includes a spacious outdoor pool, 24-hour fitness center, and convenient on-site retailers - all within steps from the local newark street dog park.










































brand-new kitchen and baths
brand-new homes feature upgraded finishes, stainless steel appliances, soft-close cabinetry, in-home washers & dryers, and vinyl wood flooring. move into your perfectly upgraded home today!

your premier townhome
designed to pull rich sunlight into your living spaces, these townhomes feature a great entertaining space with large bay windows, sky lights, walk-in closets, upstairs laundry, and more. between the greenery of spring and the array of color in the fall, your new home brings you the true beauty of d.c.
