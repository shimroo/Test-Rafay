about us









2023 annual report

			the department of pediatrics at the university of miami miller school of medicine is dedicated to discovering new ways to diagnose and treat children of all ages and to ensure their optimal health and well-being. our department continues to be committed to the relentless pursuit of inclusive excellence in clinical care, education, research, and advocacy. explore our department's vibrant vision in the 2023 annual report, which highlights numerous national awards and honors, our outstanding clinical services, superb educational activities, prestigious nih grants, publications, patient heroes, and physician heroes.
