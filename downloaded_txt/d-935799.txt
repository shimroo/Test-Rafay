hickory smoked turkeys & hams
since 1962


celebrating 62 years in business - 1962-2024 a little dairyette on the corner of hwy 29 and hwy 82 opened as the original burge's selling barbeque, burgers and ice cream, and the business has been a success story ever since. burges now offers two arkansas dining locations, the original location in lewisville, as well as a location in little rock. each location offers a vintage-style experience, a delicious, fresh to order full menu, and products to go. besides their restaurants, burges is famous for their smoked turkeys and smoked hams, available to purchase online and direct from the lewisville distribution center. burges was honored to be recognized for their long-standing achievements with the induction into the arkansas food hall of fame in 2018.
