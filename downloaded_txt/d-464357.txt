“the crossroads of community and commerce”







“established in 1967 richfield is a beautiful village with low utility rates, low taxes, high home values, a rich community life with abundant recreational opportunities, excellent services and first-rate fire and police protections.  as your mayor, i will use my skills, education and experience to lead our village with fiscal discipline, operational vigilance and civility.  i am committed to providing excellent services and thoughtful economic development. i encourage you to experience all we have to offer from our many parks and trails to our outstanding restaurants.  my door is open to discussing your concerns, so please don’t hesitate to contact me.”"whether you are a resident or visitor to this site, i hope you will find it helpful as you navigate through the information provided.  i am truly honored to call the village of richfield my home.  i invite you to discover why the village of richfield is frequently voted the number one place to live in northeast ohio."- mayor michael wheeler
