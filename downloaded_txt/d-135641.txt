our story
welcome



savannah h-d®
your local savannah, georgia harley-davidson® dealership
savannah h-d® has been in business since 1994 at the gateway blvd location. today we are in our new facility at 1 fort argyle road. visit us at our savannah h-d® dealership, just a short ride away from statesboro, macon, and hinesville, georgia! owner ric peterson has been a harley-davidson® enthusiast for most of his life and takes great pride in bringing harley-davidson® products and service to other riders.

our other location, savannah h-d® on river street opened in 1997

the entire staff is dedicated to providing the best customer service possible. our new, larger facility at savannah h-d® enables us to enhance the harley-davidson® experience. visit or call us 7 days a week for all your riding needs!
