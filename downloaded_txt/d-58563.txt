about queens centerthere's so much to discover
discover the allure of queens center in elmhurst, ny, renowned as one of the nation's most thriving shopping destinations. this iconic four-story enclosed mall, nestled in the heart of queens borough, beckons with a captivating array of premier shopping, exquisite dining, and entertainment offerings. from beloved brands like abercrombie kids, apple, adidas, jd sports, macy's, pandora, sephora, victoria’s secret, and zara to delightful dining experiences at chick-fil-a, shake shack, and the cheesecake factory, queens center promises an unparalleled shopping and leisure experience. conveniently located at the crossroads of queens boulevard, the long island expressway, and woodhaven boulevard, this super-regional mall stands as a beacon of accessibility, seamlessly connected to various modes of transportation for an effortlessly enjoyable visit.
