welcome to our philadelphia hotel



welcome to our hotel in philadelphia, perfectly located near the liberty bell, philadelphia convention center, and award-winning restaurants. enjoy the avenue of the arts theatre district, featuring the academy of music, walnut street theatre, and forrest theater. whether for business or leisure, our midtown hotel ensures a convenient, clean, and comfortable stay.
explore the national constitution center & museum, the philadelphia museum of art, and the franklin institute. history buffs will love the liberty bell’s powerful inscription in person.
for conferences, the pennsylvania convention center is just four blocks away. visitors for medical reasons appreciate our proximity to thomas jefferson university hospital, university of pennsylvania, wills eye hospital, children’s hospital of philadelphia, and penn presbyterian medical center.
sports fans can walk a block to septa's broad street line for easy access to lincoln financial field, wells fargo center, and citizens bank park for eagles, 76ers, flyers, and phillies games. nearby colleges include temple university, drexel university, thomas jefferson university, and the university of pennsylvania.
unwind in our outdoor rooftop pool, 24-hour fitness center, and start your day with complimentary hot breakfast. rooms feature a microwave, mini-fridge, keurig, and 50 inch adjustable tv. enjoy comfort and convenience at the holiday inn express philadelphia - midtown.

faqs for holiday inn express philadelphia-midtown



does holiday inn express philadelphia-midtown have a pool?


yes, holiday inn express philadelphia-midtown has an outdoor pool.




how much is parking at holiday inn express philadelphia-midtown?


at holiday inn express philadelphia-midtown, self-parking is available for a daily rate of 39 usd .




can i cancel my booking at holiday inn express philadelphia-midtown for a full refund?


always check the hotel's cancellation policy before booking a rate. room cancellation is subject to rate booked at time of reservation and cancellation policy varies by rate.




is there free breakfast available at holiday inn express philadelphia-midtown?


breakfast is free with your stay at the holiday inn express philadelphia-midtown.




when is breakfast at holiday inn express philadelphia-midtown?


breakfast at holiday inn express philadelphia-midtown is available from 6:30 am to 10:30 am.




is there a complimentary shuttle available at holiday inn express philadelphia-midtown?


no, there is not a complimentary local area shuttle available at holiday inn express philadelphia-midtown.




what are the check-in and check-out times for holiday inn express philadelphia-midtown?


check-in at holiday inn express philadelphia-midtown is from 3:00 pm, and check-out time is 11:00 am. contact the hotel directly for options available for early check-in or late check-out.




does holiday inn express philadelphia-midtown offer free wi-fi?


yes, the holiday inn express philadelphia-midtown offers free wifi!
