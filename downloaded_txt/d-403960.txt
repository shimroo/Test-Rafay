residential & commercial cleaning services




    check out our reviews
  



coos carpet care serves coos bay, north bend, bandon, lakeside, reedsport, coquille, myrtle point, and beyond.


























carpet cleaning








            coos carpet care's steam cleaning (hot water extraction) method ensures a deep and thorough clean, leaving your carpets looking and feeling fresh. 
          









say goodbye to stubborn stains with our specialized treatments for urine and coffee stains.  additionally, our fiber protection service helps prolong the life of your carpets, keeping them looking their best for longer.



















area rug cleaning (in shop)








            at coos carpet care, we know that each area rug is unique. our controlled environment is ideal for deep cleaning and quick drying. 
          









if your rug has been affected by pet accidents, our urine removal treatment will effectively eliminate any lingering odors. take advantage of our free pick-up & delivery service. or you can drop off & pick up your area rug yourself and enjoy a 10% discount.



















upholstery cleaning & leather cleaning








            coos carpet care’s cleaning services include furniture pieces like chairs, sectionals, recliners, sofas, and ottomans.
          









upholstery: our cleaning process is designed to tackle tough issues like removing pet hair and lingering body oils.  leather: leather furniture requires special care. from moisturizing to protection, we help your pieces stay supple and beautiful for years to come.








    contact us for pricing and scheduling
  















tile and grout cleaning & vinyl cleaning








            at coos carpet care, our services include ceramic and porcelain tile cleaning, as well as vct and lvp vinyl cleaning.
          









we remove tough grease stains and seal grout for lasting protection.  our vinyl coating refreshes floors, making them shiny and and look like new.







































































in-shop area rug cleaning















each area rug is unique. coos carpet care’s controlled environment is the best option for deep cleaning and quick drying your area rug.we offer free pick-up & delivery. or you can drop off & pick up your area rug yourself for a 10% discount.
