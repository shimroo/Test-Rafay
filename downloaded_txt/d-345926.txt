about membercard
a community-minded loyalty program
the membercard provides a mutually beneficial program for nonprofit organizations seeking effective fundraising solutions, consumers wanting to spend less on the activities they love, and merchants looking to increase sales. founded in 1990, the success of membercard is built upon its unique network of high-quality offers from local, regional and national brands across north america.
