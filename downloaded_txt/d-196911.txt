sailactionlong island's sailboat and kayak shop   





long island's sailboat and kayak shopfor over 40 years, joe farrell and sailaction inc. have been taking care of the sailing needs of long island sailors. at sailaction, we specialize in sailboats and kayaks from 8 to 26 feet. customer service and fair prices have been the cornerstone of our business from the start. for those of you who have been our customers, we thank you. for those of you who have not, call us or stop by.
