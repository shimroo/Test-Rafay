 minimally invasive spine surgery

  cervical disc herniation
disc-preserving cervical disc surgery, cervical disc herniation surgery ; anterior microforaminotomy, minimally invasive, disc-preserving, functional cervical disc surgery  
percutaneous anterior cervical discectomy
endoscopic posterior cervical foraminotomy

   cervical stenosis, opll
cervical stenosis surgery without bone fusion ; minimally invasive cervical stenosis surgery (the jho procedure for cervical stenosis): spinal cord decompression via anterior foraminotomy for spondylotic cervical stenosis, or ossified posterior longitudinal ligament (opll)

   thoracic disc herniation
endoscopic transpedicular thoracic disc surgery, thoracic disc herniation surgery ; minimally invasive thoracic disc surgery: endoscopic transpedicular thoracic discectomy 

   lumbar disc herniation
endoscopic lumbar disc surgery, lumbar disc herniation surgery;  minimally invasive lumbar disc surgery : endoscopic lumbar discectomy: low back surgery

   lumbar stenosis 
endoscopic lumbar stenosis surgery ; minimally invasive lumbar stenosis surgery: endoscopic decompression via microforaminotomy for lumbar stenosis

   spinal cord tumors
minimally invasive spinal tumor surgery ; minimally invasive spinal cord tumor surgery: anterolateral or posterolateral approach for spinal cord tumors, endoscopic spinal cord tumor surgery 

   occipital neuralgia
c-2 decompression for occipital neuralgia surgery ; novel surgical treatment for occipital neuralgia or headaches

   spinal instability surgery
minimally invasive endoscopic spinal fusion surgery, spinal instability surgery ; minimally invasive  spinal reconstruction surgery

   chiari malformation
endoscopic chiari decompression, chiari malformation surgery

   hyperhidrosis surgery
endoscopic transthoracic sympathectomy, hyperhidrosis surgery;  endoscopic t2 sympathetic ganglionectomy

   minimally invasive brain surgery 

   pituitary tumors, pituitary adenomas, cushing's disease
endoscopic pituitary surgery through a nostril, pituitary surgery ;  minimally invasive endoscopic pituitary tumor surgery through a nostril:  for pituitary adenomas, prolactinomas, acromegaly, and cushing's disease

   acoustic neuromas
minimally invasive retrosigmoid approach for acoustic neuroma surgery

   meningiomas
endoscopic endonasal meningioma surgery ; minimally invasive endoscopic endonasal surgery or minimally invasive transcranial surgery

   craniopharyngiomas
craniopharyngioma surgery ; endoscopic endonasal removal of craniopharyngiomas through a nostril without a skin incision or eye-brow skullbase mini-craniotomy

   pineal tumors
endoscopic pineal surgery, pineal tumor surgery ; minimally invasive endoscopic approaches to the pineal tumors via a transventricular or a supracerebellar approach

   skull base tumors
endoscopic endonasal skull base surgery ; endoscopic skull base surgery through a nostril: minimally invasive skull base brain tumor surgery without a skin incision
minimally invasive transcranial skull base surgery

   chordomas
endoscopic endonasal chordoma surgery ; endoscopic endonasal removal of chordomas via a nostril without a skin incision

   arachnoid cysts
endoscopic arachnoid cyst surgery ; endoscopic fenestration of cranial arachnoid cysts

   colloid cysts
endoscopic colloid cyst surgery ; minimally invasive endoscopic approach for total tumor removal

   hydrocephalus
 hydrocephalus surgery, endoscopic third ventriculostomy,

   trigeminal neuralgia
trigeminal neuralgia surgery, microvascular decompression surgery

   hemifacial spasm
hemifacial spasm surgery ; minimally invasive endoscopic microvascular decompression surgery for hemifacial spasm

   vertigo, tinnitus
 vertigo, tinnitus surgery ; microvascular decompression of viii
cranial nerve

   glossopharyngeal neuralgia
glossopharyngeal neuralgia surgery ; microvascular decompression
of the glossopharyngeal nerve

   spasmodic torticollis
spasmodic torticollis surgery ; endoscopic microvascular decompression for spasmodic torticollis

   cerebral aneurysms
aneurysm surgery ; eyebrow incision skull base surgery for
cerebral aneurysms
