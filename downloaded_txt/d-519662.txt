mahopac marina is a
full-service marina owned and operated since 1969 by the melchner family and located on beautiful
lake mahopac in putnam county, new york. here on the lake we provide boat slips, gas pumping and a full retail store stocked with everything you need for a fun day on the lake.

  sales - when you arrive, you will find an amazing inventory of new and used boats, outboard motors, covers, lifejackets and more. there is always something going on here including a yearly indoor boat show. the mahopac marina staff is always dreaming up new events and exciting deals. visit us or call to spend some time with our crew.¬†

  service - we also service our customers on all the surrounding lakes as well as the hudson river and long island sound. our state-of-the-art maintenance shop can repair nearly every make and model you can think of. if you are looking to make some upgrades or some new customization, you have come to the right place. our techs have years of experience and we proudly perform hundreds of upgrades a year. ¬†
