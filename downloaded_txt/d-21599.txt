top spanish courses.

    at don quijote we help you prepare for your global future. we combine personalized spanish courses with intensive cultural immersion experiences that will transform your aspirations into reality.
  








intensive spanish 20
our intensive 20 spanish program consists of 20 weekly classes and is perfect for students who are eager to learn spanish in a short period of time without sacrificing the opportunity to fully immerse themselves in the local culture.






summer camps
through our don quijote camps division, we provide a truly unique summer experience for children aged 5 to 18. eight top camp locations in spain are the ideal setting for 60% spanish students to meet 40% international students and share a transformational and cross-cultural experience.






dele exam preparation
we offer specialized courses to prepare you for the official dele exam in spain and latin america so that you can get your dele certification.
