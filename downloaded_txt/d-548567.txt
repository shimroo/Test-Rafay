soleiman appraisal is a full-service commercial and residential real estate appraisal company serving the states of new york, new jersey, connecticut and florida.
we work with homeowners seeking appraisals for property tax reduction, purchase, refinancing, divorce, and other circumstances.
for commercial customers we provide a wide range of services for a full array of property types: apartment, office, industrial, retail and r&d; buildings, residential sub-divisions, shopping centers, hospitals, marinas, mixed portfolios, same-use portfolios and a multitude of other property types.
our appraisers live in the areas they evaluate, so you always benefit from in-depth, local knowledge. this ensures the most accurate appraisals…by your neighborhood experts.
