our mission:















“to be the destination for anyone seeking comfort in their journey, whether walking, running, or just getting through the day.”



charlotte running company specializes in running and walking gear and is dedicated to ensuring every customer finds the perfect shoe. we have four retail stores in the charlotte area. whether you're training for an upcoming event, preparing for a race, or just looking for comfortable everyday footwear, come visit us!
