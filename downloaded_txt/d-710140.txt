experience music, history, and nature at the sembrich



visit the sembrich for a unique cultural experience on the shores of lake george! experience world-class music during our annual summer festival, discover the incredible life of polish soprano marcella sembrich (1858-1935) and the “golden age of opera” at our museum, and enjoy the beautiful natural surroundings and scenic views from our pristine wooded grounds. 


























music



celebrating 100 years of marcella sembrich’s musical legacy on lake george, the 2024 festival boasts a spectacular line-up of today’s acclaimed concert artists and features works by composers in sembrich’s own artistic circle. we also partner with other institutions to bring additional programming to our region, including screenings of the met: live in hd, presented at the hyde collection in glens falls, ny. 




    summer festival
  




    the met: live in hd
  


























history



experience the golden age of opera at the sembrich’s museum, the former lakeside teaching studio of internationally renowned soprano marcella sembrich (1858-1935). our unique collection highlights sembrich’s distinguished operatic career and musical legacy. we also offer a variety of programs, articles, and multi-media presentations online.




    online programs
  




    exhibitions
  


























nature



stroll along the shoreline paths of the sembrich’s historic lakeside grounds and enjoy the pristine, natural beauty of lake george amidst the towering pines. the sembrich property is listed on the national register of historic places.
