about us



america’s leading kitchen designers choose to work with great northern cabinetry because we have been a trusted partner since 1972. with our broad offering, generous selection of personalized options, attention to form, fit, function and appearance, our products will help transform an ordinary house into an extraordinary home. by working with one of our dealers, your cabinetry will be designed to fit your style, crafted with precision and delivered with certainty, all at an affordable price. distinctive - we are proud to offer a wide array of premium materials, modifications and accessories, built to exacting standards to create a style all your own.dependable - our reputation for outstanding quality, speedy delivery and promised limited lifetime warranty means you can complete your project with ease.trusted - you can have peace of mind working with us because of our 70 year history building the business around meeting the needs of the dealer and clientele. every homeowner has a vision for their living space. with our vast network of authorized dealers and their experienced designers, your vision will come to life and be managed by professionals who are creative, caring, and responsive.  to find a designer near you, please use our find a dealer button.choosing the right dealer is important to you – and  to us.  by carefully selecting the best dealers in each market we serve, we are a reliable partner who brings innovative solutions to challenging problems.  if you are a dealer who is interested in learning what a partnership with great northern cabinetry can do for your business, please fill out the form on the become a dealer page.
