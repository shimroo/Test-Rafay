the old opera house is a live theatre house located in the heart of charles town, west virginia. the theatre presents six main stage productions, a summer family production, a one-act play festival, and a ballet every year.the old opera house arts centre teaches classes and summer camps in acting, dance (ballet, jazz, tap, hip-hop, lyrical, and broadway-style dance), and scenic art & design.
