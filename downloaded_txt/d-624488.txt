kendall art glass studios is a spacious hot glass blowing facility located in takilma of southwest oregon. 

 
in this natural setting nationally recognized glass artist dave kendall creates one of a kind functional 
art glass in a full array of vibrant colors using 2000 year old traditional techniques. 


the dance that is glassblowing involves elements of passion, fluidity, drama, spontanaeity, choreography, temperature 
and timing all working together. the hot glass seems to have a life of it's own: creating a piece to completion is an 
immensely rewarding experience bounded by a narrow time window as the glass cools.
