you belong here



rockharbor is a community of worshipping, praying, biblically-grounded, spirit-led, imperfect, authentic followers of jesus. our life together is shaped by three values: encounter, formation, and mission.we encounter the powerful presence of god as we worship, pray, and read the scriptures; we are formed in christ's image through learning together, sharing our lives, and engaging in rhythms of intentional spiritual practices; and we give ourselves away to join jesus in his mission here in orange county and around the world.
