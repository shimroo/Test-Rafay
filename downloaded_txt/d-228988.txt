special election - gas utility sale 


waukee voters will decide if the city-owned gas utility can be sold to another utility provider through a special election on march 4, 2025. city council has heard from residents and evaluated benefits and risks of continuing to operate the utility.						read on...







how to manage your pond or basin 


do you manage a pond on your property or in your neighborhood? join our free information session on wednesday, february 19. iowa stormwater education program and waukee stormwater staff will teach you what you need to know about caring for your basin.						read on...







join citizens academy 


twenty citizens will get hands-on, working knowledge of the waukee police department in its citizens academy. meet the officers, learn defense tactics, take a trip to the shooting range and more in this free program. classes run thursdays, 2/27-5/1.						read on...







waukee named best local community to live in 


waukee was voted the best local community to live in for the 2025 best of des moines awards from cityview magazine.						read on...







now hiring 100+ seasonal jobs 


if you’re looking for a summer job or internship, check out a wide range for options at waukee.org/jobs. roles range from 10 to 40+ hours weekly with wages ranging from $11-21 per hour, depending on position.						read on...
