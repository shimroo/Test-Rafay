about the fcbc
the chief purpose of the fairfield county business club is to bring together individuals interested in personal advancement for the betterment of their business, through promotional methods. this is achieved by exchanging ideas and information on business outside the club, which can benefit all members.
another purpose of fcbc is to provide an opportunity for all members to benefit from the social activities included in affiliations as members of the club.
in order to further the above goals, the fairfield county business club of danbury, connecticut has been meeting each week for over 30 years.
