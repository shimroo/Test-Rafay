 united stone 





 this site united stone has been launched with an innovative approach to improve the position of iran's natural stone in the construction industry.
the goal of our team is to provide the customer with mass and accurate products according to the type and size of stone in a short period of time with a new and advanced method and using modern equipment.
the goal is "to make the world aware of the wonders that lie in the heart of iran's nature" 















edge processing  we can add your desired edge style to your stones to match your countertop or steps design.















processing  what if you can add a different appearance to the same stone and match it better with your requests?
















stone size  not every size is good for all places. we cut stones to your needed sizes with millimeter-scaled tolerance.
