public safety memorial day 


mayor kautz signed a proclamation making february 18th public safety memorial day in burnsville. see how we are honoring our fallen one year after their heroic acts.			additional info...







new playgrounds in burnsville! 


we're working to replace eight playgrounds around town. we'd love to hear from our neighborhood experts about the proposed plans. join our recreation staff on february 19 to ask questions and weigh in on the options.			details







beautiful gardens do beautiful things! 


join the city of burnsville and dakota county soil and water conservation district
for landscaping for clean water courses. topics include the benefits of rain gardens, native plant gardens and shoreline restoration projects.			learn more







mental health and self care 


join our police department to learn healthy coping mechanisms for a healthy lifestyle. we will also cover when to seek assistance for yourself, friends or family members who may be struggling.			additional info...
