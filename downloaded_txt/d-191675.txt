welcome to olivette
“in the center of it all,” the city of olivette is a suburban community in the center of st. louis county. incorporated in 1930, olivette is full of charm and local pride.









nominations open for the olivette business awards 
the economic development committee is seeking nominations for the olivette  business awards. submit your nominations today and help celebrate the achievements of businesses that make olivette a great place to live and work!









commission vacancies 
the city council is seeking applicants to fill three commission vacancies.









coffee with the mayor
join us for coffee with the mayor every second wednesday of the month at five oaks on warson.









five oaks on warson
