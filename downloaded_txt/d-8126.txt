  custom range hoods,  custom countertops and  custom kitchen sinks
    
handcrafted metal offers the finest  custom range hoods,  custom counter tops,  custom bartops,  custom kitchen sinks,  and  custom farmhouse sinks  available.  our award winning   copper range hoods  and  zinc range hoods  grace the finest kitchens.  add hand made straps and artistic patinas for rich, authentic metal accents.  copper countertops    and a matching  copper bartop  and copper kitchen sinks  are perfect for rich brown tones.  use zinc countertops  and a  zinc bartop  for neutral tones and old world charm.  choose our stunning stainless steel range hoods, stainless steel countertops, stainless steel sinks,  and  stainless steel bartops  for a clean modern theme.
all products are handcrafted in usa from the finest metals.
