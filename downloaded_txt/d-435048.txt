built ford tough livestock complex at expo square
tulsa, oklahoma


the national reining breeders classic began with a dream in the fall of 1997, and through the support of the industry, has grown to become the second-largest reining show in the world and the most successful stallion incentive program in reining history. now known as the million dollar show, the nrbc has paid out nearly $28 million in 26 years.
