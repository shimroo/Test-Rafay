				buy, sell, and appraise
            

              bret leifer numismatics (bln) is a full-service rare coin firm. we offer investors, and collectors expert advice and guidance in the selection of high-quality u.s. rare coins. we do appraisals for estates. --- we buy coins and collections.---
              by adding rare coins to your overall portfolio, you increase diversification and can provide a hedge against inflation and the uncertainties of paper assets.
              bret leifer was a financial planner and public accountant with degrees in accounting and finance. one primary goal is to protect your wealth while we strive to increase it. if you can have some fun while doing it, all the better. we want to help you create a wonderful coin collection.
