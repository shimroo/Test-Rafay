environmentreducing emissions, transitioning to renewable energy, and embedding sustainability into product design.















laborpromoting equity with global equal pay policies, de&i initiatives, and grassroots employee engagement like impact day.















ethics & human rightsstrengthening governance with global policies on modern slavery, data security, and whistleblower protection. 















sustainable procurementpartnering with suppliers to enforce esg standards, reduce emissions, and address social risks.
