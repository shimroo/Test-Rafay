simply splendid living



carnegie east house is a not-for-profit organization that offers a sophisticated approach to enriched housing for senior living. we invite you to explore our spacious private residences, beautifully designed amenities, curated culinary experiences, and concierge care.  




    explore our residences
  














new york's premier senior living community



where care and community come together



since 2002, carnegie east house has been providing affordable enriched housing for older adults in the heart of the upper east side in manhattan, new york. it is an active community where residents enjoy the freedom of independent senior living and the convenience of hotel-like amenities, including full dining services, housekeeping, laundry, personal care, and social worker support, and a robust program of dynamic activities. as a not-for-profit enriched housing provider, the carnegie east house community offers a haven where residents can celebrate the experience of aging with dignity and grace.


























life at carnegie east house



the joy of living



a comfortable and fulfilling lifestyle is at the core of the carnegie east house experience. our residents and staff are both friends and family, making each day a joyous one filled with fine dining, interesting activities, and the care and support to lead an independent life.




    explore the lifestyle
  


























amenities



comfort + convenience



an abundance of on-site amenities — from a spacious landscaped terrace garden to a well-stocked, curated library — encourage you to live each day to the fullest.




    explore all amenities
  














living choices










long-term stays

carnegie east house offers affordable long-term stays, providing a welcoming environment with spacious apartments, delicious dining, and engaging activities tailored to residents’ needs. our compassionate staff creates a supportive atmosphere where residents thrive, becoming cherished members of our extended family.











short-term stays

now offering short-term stays, we provide primary caregivers a much-needed break while ensuring loved ones receive exceptional support. families trust us during vacations, business trips, or whenever caregivers need to recharge. our fully furnished apartments offer comfort and convenience, catering to individuals transitioning after hospitalization or rehab, or anyone wanting to test the experience of a new home. with compassionate and personalized services, caregivers return refreshed, while individuals explore our community without commitment.
































matched with care



tailored care



quality of care is the cornerstone of our commitment, reflecting our unwavering dedication to ensuring your well-being and satisfaction. our person-centered approach recognizes and respects your unique needs and preferences.




    see our approach
  






















elevated dining

delicious doesn’t begin to describe the meals prepared by our wonderful chef. restaurant-style quality and service, with specialized diets available, you’ll enjoy a daily choice of full meals at breakfast, lunch, afternoon tea and dinner.

services

to deliver on our commitment to our residents’ overall well-being, we offer a wide range of physical, mental, and social services designed to provide holistic care and a supportive living environment.
