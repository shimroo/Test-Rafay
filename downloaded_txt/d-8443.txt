david sylvian
v a r i o u s


nov. 16: started work on: david sylvian price guide. added a stack of sylvian rarities just in to shop. all sylvian items sent out with an exclusive set of sylvian postcards.

in my experience, the ex-japan frontman is the most collectable of any artist, period. certainly from a financial viewpoint. beyond bowie, prince, beatles et al. it might have something to do with the fact that his editions are released in far more limited numbers than the other artists but boy the artwork for all his releases is just jaw-droppingly beautiful.
