golf to end als

                                    want to organize a golf tournament to help end als? whether you have a personal connection to 
									als, or you just want to support a great cause, by championing a driving for life tournament, 
									you can enjoy the game you love while raising funds for groundbreaking als research.
                                











                            driving for life is one of the longest running fundraising campaigns to support als research through the game 
							of golf. thanks to the support of the golf community, driving for life has raised more than $7 million for the 
							als therapy development institute (als tdi), 
							but we need your help to end als. learn more
