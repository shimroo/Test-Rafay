located in the charming village of warwick, ny, “yesterdays” has been a family favorite since 1984. yesterdays irish pub & restaurant which opened in 1984 (originally located on main street for almost 40 years), is now located in its brand new building — same owner, same chef, and same classic irish and american recipes — at 16 elm street next to the railroad tracks between west street and the wawayanda creek which runs through the center of the village.• yesterdays features a fifty-foot bar, a family-style dining area, semi-private dining tables for family parties, and a catering room for meetings, events, gatherings, and parties. • yesterdays has four large tvs along the bar, viewable from many of the dining tables, which is great for sports-minded youngsters of all ages.• yesterday’s walls are covered with decades of memories. • yesterdays has sunny or shaded outdoor decks for dining.• yesterdays has plenty of free parking for its patrons.• yesterdays has 20 beers on tap. international, domestic, and local.• yesterdays is open seven days a week. including monday!
