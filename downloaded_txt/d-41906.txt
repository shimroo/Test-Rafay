join us as we celebrate the 25th year of walk kansas! registration will open march 4, 2025.
walk kansas is a k-state research and extension (ksre) health initiative that promotes personal health and well-being by helping participants adopt healthful lifestyle habits. while primarily a team-based program, you can also go solo! walk kansas will help you:

be more active with support from friends and family
make healthier nutrition and beverage choices
learn helpful techniques to reduce stress, sleep well, and improve overall well-being

learn more in this sound living interview with sharolyn jackson, state leader of walk kansas.
