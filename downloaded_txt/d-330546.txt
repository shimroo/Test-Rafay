northeast ohio catholic magazine


 

as the official magazine of the catholic diocese of cleveland, northeast ohio catholic is a vital instrument of inspiration and formation through personal faith stories and columns.this publication encourages nearly 700,000 catholics in the eight-county diocese to live a more faith-filled life. it is mailed bi-monthly and free of charge to over 240,000 households within the catholic diocese of cleveland.click here to submit an address update for our mailed edition.
