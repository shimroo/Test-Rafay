about
servicing downtown toronto and surrounding areas, our office is conveniently located between college and wellesley on the yonge subway between yorkville and the financial district . our doctors are available 7 days a week during regular office hours, and outside regular hours by appointment. we will work with you to diagnose and treat your ailments providing the care you need and offer a range of holistic services including chiropractic, acupuncture, custom orthotics, massage, nutritional consultation, shiatsu and reflexology.
