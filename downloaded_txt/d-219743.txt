welcome to richland hills


richland hills is dedicated to the responsible use of public resources and progressive delivery of community focused services, while valuing our small town quality of life.
 






  
 
 













state of the city 

during the state of the city meeting on thursday, feb. 20, from 6 to 8 p.m. find out about local economic conditions, the progress of capital projects and city finances.				read on...







new water/wastewater rate 

effective january 1, 2025, the city of richland hills has updated water and wastewater rates to better meet the needs of our community.				read on...







citizen survey 

in february 2023, the city of richland hills conducted a citizen survey to better understand community priorities, quality of life and satisfaction with city services. now, we’re following up to measure progress and gather your input on what matters most.				read on...
