 2025 annual ranger muster  the us army ranger association invites you to attend this year’s annual ranger muster (arm).  this year’s arm will occur from june 23-27, 2028, and will be hosted at the marriott hotel in downtown columbus, georgia. events will take place throughout the columbus-ft. moore area.   during the week of the arm 2025, the 75th ranger regiment and artb command teams have tentative times for each function/activity:  rhof board: 25 april (1000 est) rhof dinner: 24 june (1730 est social hour, 1830 est dinner) rhof ceremony: 25 june (1300 est)

ranger basesrangers form an instant bond with one another, no matter what era they served.  usara’s ranger bases are an extension of this bond.  ranger bases are groups of rangers who meet regularly and have formalized within usara in order to receive support from the association.   we believe you’ll find that gathering with fellow rangers will be the highlight of your month.
