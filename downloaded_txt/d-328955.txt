stay at the palace hotel in paignton 











set in beautiful grounds, this hotel in paignton is situated in a desirable location on paignton’s esplanade and directly opposite paignton seafront, with the town centre only a short walk away.the hotel offers secure parking, efficient and professional service and tastefully decorated en-suite bedrooms, many with sea views. our leisure facilities include a fully equipped gym, indoor heated pool and beauty treatment rooms and are open to all our guests to enjoy. an extensive food and wine menu will provide the perfect dining experience with a mouth-watering selection of dishes and desserts.we also have many special breaks and holiday offers to ensure your stay in paignton is a memorable one.
