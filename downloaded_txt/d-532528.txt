new cylinderized u-phos® phosphine fumigant in the u.s. market!

degesch america, inc. is proud to introduce u-phos® phosphine fumigant to the u.s. market. this cylinderized fumigant has been approved for use by the u.s. epa and is currently in the approval process in several states.
key features of u-phos® phosphine fumigant include:
• cost effective & reliable
• alternative to methyl bromide
• alternative for aluminum & magnesium phosphide
• gas apps hi-flo dispensing equipment
• customer support
press release          learn more
for more information about u-phos® phosphine fumigant and to inquire about product availability, please contact us at info@degeschamerica.com or 540-234-9281.
