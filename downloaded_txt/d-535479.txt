how do we do it?

at domenico’s, we take pride in everything we do and it shows in not only the food and service but also in the longevity of our employees. from our kitchen team to our servers, bar tenders and hostesses; the  majority have been serving our guests needs from 5 to 26 years.













 
mark arcobasso
owner & head chef




our family tree

owner, mark arcobasso, comes from a restaurant oriented family in st. louis, where his father, uncles, brothers and sisters have owned and operated restaurants since 1970. he got his start at his parents restaurant domenico’s in florissant and began cooking at age 12 and almost 50 years later still plays the role of chef.
