about bridgeford house b & b



at bridgeford house bed & breakfast, you will enjoy outstanding service, and sleep in a quaint and immaculately clean suite or penthouse. each suite (or the penthouse) is well stocked with amenities that will make your stay a wonderful experience… one that you will remember for years to come! additionally, each morning during your visit you will be invited to a hot and hearty breakfast served in our dining room.   there, you will have an opportunity to share that breakfast with our other guests.  past visitors often fondly remember treasured friendships that were formed at our breakfast table.  we hope that you will create fond memories at bridgeford house bed & breakfast, too!
