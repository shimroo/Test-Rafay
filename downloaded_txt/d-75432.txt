inflated balloon delivery
balloon monkey have a huge selection of helium balloons in all shapes, sizes and styles. you can find that one perfect single balloon, or create a whole balloon bouquet!
all of our balloons are delivered fully inflated, so that you get the full wow factor when the box is opened, and the balloons gently float out. each balloon is weighted and has its string set to a specific length, so when you open your parcel, the balloons form a perfect display!
you can select a specific date for delivery, or for smaller orders, you may be able to choose our free delivery option, and have your balloons delivered by post. whichever you choose, your parcel will be tracked so you can keep an eye on it as it makes its way to any uk address of your choice.




send inflated balloon gifts
filled balloons are an incredible gift - and with the personalised designs available with balloon monkey, you can give something really special. our balloons also make brilliant decorations, with many party organisers putting together beautiful balloon bouquets for wedding venues, hen parties and big family celebrations.
we've got the very best range of balloons to order online, whether you're sending a balloon through the post to a friend, or having a surprise delivered to your spouse at the office. with so many styles and designs to choose from, you're bound to find the best option right here.
and as well as our balloon gifts, you can add extra presents to your order, like sweets, chocolates or even cuddly toys and cake! at balloon monkey we know how to make every special occasion even more memorable, with a balloon gift that's delivered right to your door.












never release balloons into the sky
we follow the european balloon and party council code of best practice. balloon monkey has a policy of never selling balloons that we know would be used for a balloon release. please always cut balloons with a pair of scissors and dispose of spent balloons in a bin.
