at camp hebron, we are cultivating a sanctuary where people  can connect with god, nature, and each other.















camp hebron is a christian camp and conference center located in halifax, pa. nestled along the appalachian trail just north of harrisburg, we have 340 acres of wooded sanctuary. come with your church family to retreat in our peaceful mountain setting, send your children to one of our action-packed summer camps, or bring your rv for a weekend family getaway in our campground. we offer everyone a chance to get away and be refreshed.
