real estate built on relationshipsfounded in 1980, holder properties is a privately held, full-service real estate investment and development company. whether it’s property investment, development, asset or property management, our shared success with our partners is built on the foundation of relationships—with trust, passion, commitment, and integrity as our greatest building blocks. but it’s never just about the deal, the land, or even the building. 














holder creates environments where tenants, residents, and students can thrive. because for more than four decades, it’s always been about people.
