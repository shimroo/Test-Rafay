the shepherd’s flock animal sanctuary is committed to providing rescue, rehabilitation, adoption and sanctuary to homeless, abused, sick and neglected domestic, farm and wild animals, particularly those with special needs.the shepherds’ flock is a no-kill, non-profit organization founded on the principle that every animal has intrinsic value and should be treated with respect and dignity regardless of species, breed, age, health or emotional needs.100% of all donations are directed to the care of our animals.we welcome your help in carrying out our mission.
