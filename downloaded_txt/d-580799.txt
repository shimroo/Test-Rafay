surplus sales hours and location
surplus sales is open to the public and campus clients monday through friday from 10 am to 3:30 pm; walk-ins are welcome. for online auction winners and online reservation orders, please contact us or schedule an appointment here. the surplus warehouse is located at 7835 trade st. san diego ca, 92121 behind ste. 100 (northeast corner of the building). parking is free for clients and is available near the gate entrance. we will be closing at 1:30p on wed. 2/19 for mandatory all staff meeting, and closing early on 2/27 for training 2:30pm pst.
