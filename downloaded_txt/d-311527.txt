wickenburg: open for business
since 1931, the wickenburg chamber of commerce has been dedicated to serving the local business community and promoting tourism in wickenburg.whether planning a weekend visit or looking to relocate to the wickenburg area, chamber staff can connect you with the resources you need to get started.the chamber office is open monday through thursday, 9:00am to 5:00pm.
