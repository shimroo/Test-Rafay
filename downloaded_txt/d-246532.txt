private swim & tennis club















welcome to halecrest park, costa mesa’s favorite family-oriented social club. with tennis, swimming, and social activities, halecrest is a year-round private club also offering indoor and outdoor rental venues to the public.  halecrest park creates the perfect space for timeless events. our space is designed to work for your special occasions. join us to celebrate life with family and friends.
