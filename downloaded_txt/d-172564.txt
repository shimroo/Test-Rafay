hengehold trucks has the largest selection of pre-owned commercial vehicles for sale in northern california. from cargo vans to box vans to pickup trucks, we have a range of vehicles to meet your transportation needs.
we are a family owned and operated business that has been selling and renting quality vehicles since 1948. we have no commissioned sales people. each of our vehicles is carefully selected, thoroughly inspected, and sold with the utmost confidence.
customers of hengehold trucks include universities, government agencies, businesses, and individuals. because of our high standard of quality, we are proud to have such a high number of return customers every year.
please browse our inventory of vans and trucks for sale and rent. if you have any questions, or would like to make a reservation, please contact us today. we look forward to helping you find the vehicle that meets your needs.
