about













canadian worship band, starfield, has spent the last 20 years partnering with churches and ministries, leading students and congregations in passionate and energetic worship. they released their self-titled major label debut in 2004, followed by "beauty in the broken" in 2006, "i will go" in 2008, "the saving one" in 2010 and most recently "the kingdom" in 2012. starfield-penned songs like "filled with your glory", "reign in us", and "the saving one" are sung in church services every weekend all over the world. starfield is the most-awarded and top-selling christian band in canada.
