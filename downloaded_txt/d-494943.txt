welcome to heritage pines!
you've worked hard building a career and raising a family.

now it's your turn. now, the fun begins! 

heritage pines is a unique, active adult 55+ community that was the first of its kind in north carolina, and continues to offer more amenities to enhance your life experiences.

heritage pines offers you the opportunity to spend the best years of your life in a home and community where you can enjoy the most that life has to offer in a vibrant neighborhood with like-minded adults.
