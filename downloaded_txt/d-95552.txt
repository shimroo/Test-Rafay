welcome to the city of north miami beach



 
 
 






 
 








 
 
 





strategically located midway between miami and ft. lauderdale international airports and adjacent to the golden glades interchange where i-95, florida's turnpike, and the palmetto expressway meet, north miami beach is the ideal location to live, work, learn, play and enjoy. check out the progress we’ve made over the past 5 years and the future plans for the city of north miami beach.
