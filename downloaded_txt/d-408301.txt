mosquito madness: tire pick-up program 


monmouth county mosquito control personnel will come to your property and pick up as many as 10 car tires without rims. program runs from 2/1 - 3/31. residents must call to set up pick-up. click through for more info...						read on...

township snow reminder 


ordinance 17-4.5 requires all residents to remove snow and ice from sidewalks within 12 hours of daylight after a snowfall. please help us ensure that our school aged children can safely walk to school and get to their bus stops. thank you for your help!						read on...
