welcome to the idaho mavericks baseball organization














discover the heart behind idaho mavericks baseball



idaho mavericks baseball is based in hailey, and we are dedicated to not only teaching the game of baseball but cultivating lifelong passion and character on and off the field. join us in building enthusiasts for life!




    learn more
  


























help us get to cooperstown!it all began with an idea. to take a team of dedicated baseball players and get them to where baseball was founded, cooperstown! this is a unique experience for 12u players, to develop a deeper appreciation for the tradition and why baseball is america’s favorite pastime. this week-long experience also develops fundamentals of baseball.  your donation will help with registration fees. it all goes to the kids.
