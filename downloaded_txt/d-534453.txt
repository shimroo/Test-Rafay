discover the latest in voices of hope: winter 2024 editionthe winter 2024 issue of voices of hope is here! this edition shines a spotlight on the exciting launch of the rotary mobile medical clinic, expanding vital healthcare services to families and children across santa clara county. it also celebrates the incredible legacy of retiring ceo gregory kepferle, who was honored with two prestigious lifetime achievement awards.
