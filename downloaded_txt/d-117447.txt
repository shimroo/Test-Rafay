community newsfollow our updates
 






  
 
 















new leaf park renamed griffin park to honor public service of local family 


a city of jackson park has been renamed to recognize a local family for their service to the community. the city council recently voted to change the name of new leaf park to griffin park, honoring michael griffin and his sons martin and john.						read on...







city of jackson welcomes new police chief 


at the tuesday, feb. 11 city council meeting, christopher simpson was sworn in as chief of the jackson police department.						read on...







new trail will connect ella sharp park and mlk equality trail 


this new addition will further increase pedestrian access and connect the city's largest public park to our main pedestrian trail.						read on...







responding to winter weather in jackson 


the dpw targets major streets and emergency snow routes first, then clears neighborhood streets. a map on the city website shows the routes they take.						read on...







jackson city council meeting feb. 11, 2025 


the feb. 11 city council meeting can be viewed through the city's youtube page.			additional info...







winter 2025 city of jackson newsletter 


the official city newsletter covers the latest news from the city of jackson and highlights what's happening in the community.			additional info...







local law enforcement accepting applications for 2025 citizen police academy 


jackson police officers and sheriff deputies are partnering on this annual program. the academy is aimed at residents wanting to gain firsthand knowledge of the daily operations and procedures of local law enforcement.						read on...







celebrating black history month in jackson 


learn about the people, places and artifacts that have shaped black history in the city of jackson in our special black history website section.			additional info...







program helps jackson police identify people with communication difficulties 


the take me home program is designed to assist officers in the identification of people with autism, alzheimer’s, dementia, down syndrome or any other condition that makes it difficult to communicate.						read on...







tour homes completed through city's 100 homes program 


video tours of the program's most popular home types are available through the city's website.						read on...







see improvements underway at city center in downtown jackson 


renovations at this former masonic temple include an incubator kitchen, food service training center and event space. a video on this page gives an inside look at the progress.						read on...







cooper street in downtown jackson closed for bridge replacement 


the closure began jan. 13. this project from the michigan department of transportation is expected to last through oct. 2025.			additional info...
