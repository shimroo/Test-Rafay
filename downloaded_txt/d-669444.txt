about michael ives


                                                
                         for over 50 years, i have created my art using acrylics and watercolors, sculpting wood folk art and creating videos of my work and life events. in recent years i have created my art using an ipad.
 i reside in coronado, california and lanikai, hawai'i; kiteboarding, swimming, playing piano and creating my art. 
 all artwork is copyrighted by michael ives 2025.
