online dating for widows and widowers in the us


at widows & widowers us, it is our number one goal to help you connect with other widows and widowers in a space designed just for you. we believe in friendship first - helping you to forge bonds with those who relate to what you have been through and to help you find that special someone. today, online dating has become the ideal platform for widows and widowers seeking to start a new relationship.










chatting online in a safe and familiar setting gives you the opportunity to get to know others gradually. in your own time, you can decide whether you want to take things further. sharing your current aspirations and hopes for the future can put you on the path to meeting others who want the same things. it is in this way you can get used to the idea of having someone new in your life.




our easy-to-use platform allows you to instantly connect with other widows and widowers based in towns and cities throughout the us. whether at home or on the move, you can start your dating journey knowing you are in the right place to navigate your way to a new horizon. for widows and widowers seeking to find love again, we are here to make the process a safe and fulfilling one.

finding love again after losing a partner is something that only those with direct experience can truly understand. you're not only missing the love and romance you once shared, but the friendship and the reassurance of being part of a couple. if you've enjoyed a happy and fulfilling marriage, it can be natural to compare your previous partner with someone new. however, dating after loss can be seen as a new start, opening the way towards a fulfilling and lasting relationship with its own unique qualities.
at widows & widowers us, we understand the importance of going at your own pace and meeting others who can genuinely relate to your loss. we don't bombard you with unnecessary notifications; instead, we focus on delivering quality matches that align with your values. if you would like to learn more about our service, please visit our faq section, or alternatively, get in touch! we also have our community guidelines and safety page to ensure you get the most out of our service.
