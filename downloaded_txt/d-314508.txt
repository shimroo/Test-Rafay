designer’s statement

                        generation gems is a jewelry collection unlike
                        anything i’ve worked on before. one that
                        explores the cultural weight and meaning of the
                        gemstones in the jewelry.
                    

                        i partnered with my dream team designer, lapidary and craftsman jewelers to create a collection
                        featuring genuine, ethically sourced gemstones.
                    

                        every texture, curve and angle holds meaning in
                        the design. each piece tells a single story within the
                        collection’s book-meant to be re-told and passed
                        down for generations to come. 
                    

                        generation gems is meant to let you fall in love
                        with jewelry-true jewelry-all over again.
                    













generation gems is able to responsibly source our stones from all over the world. for the last four decades our family has cultivated relationships directly with the mine owners.
