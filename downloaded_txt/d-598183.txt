china vending equipments limited has many years experiences in vending machine business. our goal is to introduce the china-made vending machines and other coin-operated products to international markets. currently, we can offer various vending machines (such as snack/soda machines, coffee machines, gumball/candy machines, toy crane machines and condom/tissue machines), body scales, kiddie rides, amusement machines, redemption machines and other coin-operated equipments.
our products are manufactured under the requirements of relevant international quality standards. all products will be 100% tested by our q/c persons before shipments. we are dedicated to benefit our customers by minimizing their cost with reasonable prices and maximizing their profits. we will do what we can to create the best win-win cooperation with all of our customers. we adhere to principle of "quality first, credit first", providing reliable quality and perfect service for worldwide customers. we welcome domestic and international customers.
