commercial refrigeration suppliers, refrigeration freezers are one of the uk's leading suppliers of commercial refrigeration equipment. refrigeration freezers was founded as part of the foodmaster refrigeration group in the early 1970's, as freezers were the 'latest craze' and families bought them in bulk to save time and money.
since then we have gone on to be one of the most reputable refrigeration freezer suppliers for the hospitality industry with high-quality commercial storage freezers, display fridges and catering equipment being supplied and installed to reputable, bars, clubs, restaurants, pubs, delicatessens & convenience stores throughout uk.
