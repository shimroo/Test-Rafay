we are open for walk-in business including licensing, animal permits, and dog park passes. the public can come in to see our animals, but you will need a current, approved application in order to interact with them. this is being done by appointment only. please explore our website to see the animals that are currently available for adoption. if you are interested in adopting, we have applications at the shelter, or you can fill out the online version here: adoption applicationplease call ahead if you need to bring an animal into the shelter.
