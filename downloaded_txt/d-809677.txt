school of business & management
empowering the next generation of changemakers, innovators, and leaders to shape the future.














are you ready to make a difference?  at the school of business and management, you'll gain the skills, insights, and global perspective to lead with purpose and drive change where it matters most.


  located in the heart of london between the city and canary wharf

 
our programmes teach you to think beyond textbooks, management fads and memorised formulas for success – where the focus is on social justice, sustainability, and good governance to create tomorrow’s responsible leaders.
 
we are proud to be an aacsb-accredited business school and the most inclusive university in the prestigious russell group. 
 
our school is home to five specialised departments - accounting and financial management, business analytics and applied economics,  business and society, marketing, and people and organisations, - that drive forward impactful research and responsible management education with a global perspective.
 
join us—and start building a future you can be proud of.
