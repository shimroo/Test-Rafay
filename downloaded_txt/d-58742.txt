colombia médica is a peer-reviewed, open-access scientific journal covering various medical and health sciences topics. the advantages of publishing in colombia médica are:

free publication of all types of articles and free publication of color images.
constructive peer review
easy and fast online submission of manuscripts.
open access journal available in pubmed central, redalyc, and scielo).
indexed by pubmed, embase, scopus, and web of science
