find your inspiration.no matter the size of your yard, you have room for a garden! at planters by design we specialize in container gardening which will provide four seasons of color to any property. we also provide a garden design service with a special interest in small gardens that feature color throughout the year.  we want our clients to love their gardens in the fall as much as they love them in the spring, while keeping in mind that a winter garden should have it’s  own beauty as well. and if we can add in native plants to both pots and landscapes that will attract those pollinators, we will do it!every container and every garden should be unique to the client and the setting. and every garden should bring joy to those who view them. we love designing with plants and enjoy sharing this passion.
























 services 

 












    planters
  



tiny gardens in a pot…you provide the container or we can find one for you to fit your site. we will create a unique design with your home and yard in mind. 















    garden design
  



creating beautiful spaces to enjoy...make the most of your outdoor space by having us design a garden that is beautiful spring through fall.














    maintenance
  



keep your investment looking good…our monthly maintenance program ensures that we will keep your planters looking their best throughout the seasons.
