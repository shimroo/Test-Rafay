want to test your radio live on the air?



we are a licensed limited coast station. if you need a radio check give us a call. we maintain a watch on vhf channel 16 during business hours. our call sign is ktd560.you can also test your radios dsc (digital selective calling) functions by sending a call (during regular bussiness hours) to our mmsi # 003660528. the best way to do this is to send a "position request" and our station will automatically respond. the response will show on your vhf and our position will automatically appear on your connected chart plotter.our station is operational only during business hours.
