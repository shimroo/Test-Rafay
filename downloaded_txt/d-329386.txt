hilton niagara falls
overlooking the falls
connected to fallsview casino and olg stage

with rooms overlooking both the american and canadian horseshoe falls, and directly connected to fallsview casino resort and olg stage, hilton niagara falls/fallsview hotel & suites is the perfect niagara falls getaway destination. this full-service hotel features three signature restaurants, a fallsview breakfast dining experience, and nightlife lounges in the heart of niagara's entertainment district.hilton niagara falls is designed with our guests’ comfort and convenience in mind, with a variety of modern room options including rooms equipped with jacuzzi tubs, high-definition tvs, and fireplaces. whether it's a weekend getaway or an extended vacation, you can’t go wrong when making hilton niagara falls/fallsview hotel and suites your destination of choice when visiting niagara falls.
















fireworks over the fallsthe 2024 falls fireworks summer series will kick off on friday, may 17 (victoria day long weekend) and run every night at 10:00 pm* until monday, october 14th (canadian thanksgiving).enjoy the display from any hilton niagara falls guest room with a u.s. falls view, canadian & u.s. falls view, or premium canadian & u.s. falls view, or dine at watermark fallsview dining.* weather permitting
