turning metals into motion










precision casting and superalloy production excellence

doncasters is a leading international manufacturer of specialist superalloys and high-precision alloy components made for the most demanding conditions.
we excel in turning metals into motion, working in alloys and manufacturing complex product designs to offer an unparalleled range of solutions for high performance and specialist applications. we employ nearly 3000 people, at 20 locations in europe, asia and america.
with technical expertise spanning nearly 250 years, through our advanced manufacturing facilities, we serve the world’s leading oems in aerospace & defence, industrial gas turbine and mobility markets, whilst also delivering for medical and other specialist industries.
