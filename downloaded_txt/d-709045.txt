who we are
welcome to   copperton lane. we carry an eclectic variety of collectibles and   antiques to accent your home from pre-victorian to mid-century modern:           antique kitchen and household copper, fine glassware, kitchen           collectibles, pottery, figurines, china, linens, textiles,    primitives, toleware, old toys, personal accessories, children's feeding   dishes, and         much more. 
we   are collectors too, and strive to give you the highest level of           customer service and attention, whether you purchase a $5 hankie or a           $500 antique. we love repeat customers, and will do our utmost to   make         you want to visit copperton lane again and again. step in   and browse         awhile!
