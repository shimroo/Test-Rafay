about the series
what if you knew - really knew - that you came from another world?  
this is the premise of the graphic novel series the star runner chronicles, which follows the adventures of aurora palmer, a normal teenager with unpredictable powers linked to her mysterious origins.
it is an exciting series with a positive, forward-thinking vision of how far we can go when we develop the best that’s in us. aurora’s journey is our journey: to understand who we are, where we come from, and what is our ultimate destiny.
