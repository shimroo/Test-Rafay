village vehicle sticker purchases 

residents who have not purchased their annual vehicle sticker are encouraged to do so as soon as possible to avoid being ticketed.				read on...




submit green award nominations by march 14 

the environmental and forestry commission invites residents and local businesses to participate in its annual green awards program, which recognizes sustainable and environmentally-friendly properties...				read on...








submit spring tree planting requests by march 25 

each year, the village of winnetka plants about 200 trees in parkway areas. trees up to 2 ½” trunk diameter size are planted at no cost to residents.				read on...




please shovel sidewalks after mild snow events 

in order to prevent ice buildup on the sidewalks, residents are asked to remove snow from the sidewalks in front of their homes after slushy events or smaller snowstorms.				read on...
