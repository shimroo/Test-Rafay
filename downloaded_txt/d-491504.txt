finding properties
with over 1,500 estate agents on mallorca, it is hard to keep track. you cannot be familiar with every estate agent and owner; therefore, you could easily miss out on many top properties or good deals.

        vivamallorca gives you the best properties on the island at first sight, whether directly from the owner or from a competent estate agent.

        you can search everything that is available as a guest free of charge, and view useful reports, remember favourites, save notes on a property, and send yourself or friends links to interesting properties.

        as a registered user, you can also manage your saved properties at any time and create specific search requests based on your individual criteria, so that you are automatically informed about new offers as frequently as you want.

        all of these services are available to you costless.


offering properties
as a property provider, you think very carefully about how to invest your marketing budget in the best possible way. instead of ads with flat monthly fees, vivamallorca offers the most efficient ad campaign of all.

        you only pay for each successful customer contact, more precisely, when your report is specifically selected and viewed by a customer.

        vivamallorca gives you the best attainable access to your target group without any wasted expenditure and you only pay for actual successes. with your property management options, you have your entire property portfolio under control. you can create and manage all of the details, texts or images and edit them at any time. the stats let you see exactly when and which of your reports was viewed. you then only pay a small fee starting at 0.10 euro per customer contact or report view.

        this means that you can make the most of your marketing budget to present your properties in the most attractive way and so gain new customers.


rent, buy, live
we are not estate agents; we are a marketplace.

        vivamallorca offers you all of the properties on mallorca and the balearic islands from the best providers at a glance.

        whether you're looking for a property to buy, a long-term rental or a holiday rental, or if you are looking for a plot of land, a flat, a penthouse, a villa, a finca or a business, here is where you can find basically everything.

        and it is completely free of charge.
