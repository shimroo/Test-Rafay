welcome to k&w cycle
welcome to k&w cycle, where the variety of powersports products is second to none. in the entire detroit, mi metropolitan area, including rochester, sterling heights, romeo, macomb, and troy, you won't find a more accommodating atmosphere than at k&w cycle in shelby township, mi. there isn't a friendlier or more knowledgeable staff than ours. 

we're happy to help you find either the perfect recreational vehicle or the parts you've been looking for. check out our online catalogs to see what we have; and if you need help in making your selection, call or stop in-we're always ready to help!

look to us for all your riding fun. our selection of atvs and recreational vehicles are designed to accommodate everyone from the farmer, rancher, or outdoorsman to the weekend warrior and avid sports rider. our friendly, knowledgeable staff; quick, dependable service department; and affordable prices all come together to make your shopping experience one you'll remember. you'll be sure to find what you're looking for, with names like  e-z-go, kawasaki, ski-doo, can-am, yamaha, polaris®, slingshot®, and sea-doo all in stock. we carry a wide selection of snowmobiles, utvs, side x sides, atvs, street bikes, dirt bikes, cruisers, sport bikes, pwcs, jet skis, and golf carts. let k&w cycle be part of your choice for work or fun.

we know individuality is important, which is why we pay close attention to the interests of each customer. from the minute you walk through the door, meeting your needs is our top priority. founded not only on excellent customer service, but also on the principles of hard work and a focused attitude, k&w cycle has a long list of satisfied customers. call or stop by, you'll be glad you did.

we are proud to be a michigan based, owned, and operated company since 1963.
