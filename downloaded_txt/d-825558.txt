        welcome to chesterfield      

the town of chesterfield is located along the connecticut river in the southwestern corner of new hampshire. chesterfield is approximately 65 miles from concord, the state capital. it is a rural community, which is situated between the region’s two largest commercial centers, keene, nh, and brattleboro, vt.
