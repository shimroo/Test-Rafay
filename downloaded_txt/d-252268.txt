member club of the usa volleyball and the new england region volleyball association (nerva)the coastal volleyball club is a direct member of usa volleyball and a board member of nerva. we follow all policy guidelines of usa volleyball including safesport, court house background checks, coach education and playing in only usa / nerva sanctioned events. players and parents should expect high standards from a volleyball club and we believe that usa volleyball and nerva strive to attain these standards.we are a member club of the new england region of usa volleyball. as a player you may stop any unwanted contact from a club representative by simply asking (either verbally or in writing) that all contact cease. any player believing a club representative of any new england region volleyball club has been intimidating, harassing, or acted inappropriately in any manner of contact or recruiting should contact the new england region at commissioner@nevolleyball.org
