recycling, trash collections suspended for feb. 18 and 19 

the city of fayetteville’s recycling and trash collection (rtc) services are suspended for tuesday, feb. 18, and wednesday, feb. 19, because of road conditions caused by inclement weather.				read on...




2025 invasive plant ‘bounty’ giveaway april 10-11 

this spring, the city of fayetteville will hold its seventh annual invasive plant “bounty” program, in which the city replaces residents’ invasive plants with a native tree or shrub – for free.				read on...




fayetteville fire department to use ‘e-moto’ bikes for trail response 

the fayetteville fire department has developed a new team that will use two fully electronic dirt bikes, or “e-moto” bikes, to respond to calls on and along trails in fayetteville.				read on...
