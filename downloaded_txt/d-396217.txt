shirley muldowney, an american top gas, funny car, and match racer is a trailblazer and an inspiration to many in automotive sports. she shattered gender barriers by becoming the first woman to receive a license from the national hot rod association to drive a top fuel dragster. with her passion, perseverance, and dedication, she won 18 nhra national events and became the first person to win two and three of drag racing‚Äôs most prestigious titles, top fuel, back-to-back. her impressive achievements have earned her a well-deserved place in the automotive hall of fame, the motorsports hall of fame of america, and the international motorsports hall of fame.

career highlights















1975
the american auto racing writers and broadcasters association named shirley the first woman to be part of their 10-person auto racing all-american team.











1976
drag news top fuel named shirley the driver of the year, and she was voted into the auto racing all-american team for the second time.











1977
shirley became the first woman to win drag racing's most prestigious title, the nhra winston world points championship.











1980
shirley won the nhra winston world points championship for the second time. 











1981
shirley won the ahra world championship and was voted to the auto racing all-american team for the fifth time.











1982
shirley secured the nhra winston points championship, becoming the first person to claim drag racing's most prestigious title three times. she was voted into the auto racing all-american team for the fifth time.











1990
the motorsports hall of fame of america welcomed shirley as an inductee. 











2001
the national hot rod association ranked shirley #5 in the 50th anniversary list of top 50 drivers, 1951-2000.











2004
shirley was inducted into the international motorsports hall of fame.











2005
shirley was inducted into the automotive hall of fame.
