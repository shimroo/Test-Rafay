car seat checks wednesday, march 5 

have your child's car seat installation checked for free at the east peoria fire department's car seat check event from 1 - 4 p.m. wednesday, march 5 at the central fire station, 201 w washington street.		additional info...




take steps to prevent frozen pipes 

frozen pipes can be an unwelcome and potentially expensive occurrence when central illinois has extremely cold temperatures and wind chills. running a thin stream of water in a faucet can help prevent costly plumbing repairs.		additional info...




city offers two warming centers 

residents needing a place to warm up can go to two east peoria facilities during normal business hours: the east peoria civic plaza at 401 w washington street and eastside centre at 1 eastside drive.				read on...
