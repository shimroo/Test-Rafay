maritime london | an overview maritime london is the industry-led body representing maritime professional services in the uk. funded by companies and organisations from a wide range of disciplines, maritime london works to ensure that the uk remains a world-beating location to base maritime related business and to conduct maritime trade.
maritime london’s raison d’être is to:

inform members of the latest relevant developments within government and the market
provide a vehicle to convene and discuss issues pertinent to the industry
promote the unparalleled expertise and capacity that exists on the london market
ultimately act as an advocate in relevant fora on behalf of the membership and the wider sector.
