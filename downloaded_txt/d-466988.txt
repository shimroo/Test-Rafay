welcome to trophy house
it’s time to order your end of the year awards. isn’t it time to add monthly and annual brass plates to your perpetual plaque? or is it time to order a new perpetual plaque to honor your top performers? call us, send an email or just stop by and let us show you the latest styles and ideas in perpetual plaques. popular styles include solid walnut and hawaiian koa wood plaques with laser engraving, or the ever popular engraved brass plates applied to a walnut or koa plaque. we have a wide selection of sizes and styles to accommodate any budget. let us include your corporate logo on your next perpetual plaque.
