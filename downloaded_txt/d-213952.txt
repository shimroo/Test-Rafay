our practice areas



 divorce cases. we work with the most difficult cases when there is a contested divorce with no understanding about how spouses will divide property, who will take care of children and so on. for an uncontested divorce, we often recommend using one of the online divorce services. diy divorce review provided the complete guide to these services.


 our experience. our attorneys possess broad, diverse, and successful experience in all areas of medical malpractice, auto accidents, personal injury and wrongful death law. we also can help get a car dealer license.
 motorcycle accidents motorcyclists have less physical protection than drivers of larger vehicles, catastrophic injuries and fatalities are much more common. know your rights.


 truck accidents. we work with experienced accident investigators to collect evidence from the scene of the accident, interview eyewitnesses, and review a driver’s logbook and driving record.
 auto accidents. while many of our cases settle out of court with satisfactory financial rewards for the client, it’s only because the defense knows we can take it to trial effectively. check out nerdydriver for more info about driver’s license.


 premise liability. building owners are responsible for alerting the public to unsafe conditions. the existence of signage and protective measures are a key factor in determining whether an owner is negligent.
 medical malpractice. if a doctor fails to meet the standard of care, and a misdiagnosis or surgical error results in injury or death to the patent, the caregiver can be held legally and financially liable.


 nursing home injuries. this neglect can result in various forms of injury or death and is caused by any type of physical, psychological, cognitive, or financial harm suffered by a nursing home resident.
 injured on the job. whatever the injury, getting hurt as a result of your job can reduce your mobility, your ability to care for your family and your ability to earn a living. know your rights.
