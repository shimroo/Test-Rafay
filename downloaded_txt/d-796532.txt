hotel in monteverdeonly a 2 minutes walk from the center of santa elena, monteverde, hotel arco iris lodge sits in a private realm of its own. the property spreads over 2.5 hectares of lushly planted hillside and is backed by protected forest.immaculately maintained stone pathways coil through the groomed gardens where you can watch a great variety of birds and other animals. spread out on the property are twenty handsome, stone-and-hardwood cabins.our hotelamenities and services at our hotelroomswe offer a variety of lodging optionsmonteverde cloud forestmonteverde, with its unique tropical flora and fauna, is one of the the best known and most visited areas in costa rica. the cloud forest reserve extends along the cordillera de tilaran at an altitude between 800 and 1,800 m, on the watershed between the caribbean and pacific. the mountains of monteverde are often shrouded in mists, creating a fairytale wonderland replete with mosses, ferns, orchids and bromeliads. the thickets shelter an amazing variety of animals. around 400 species of birds are found in monteverde, including the enigmatic quetzal.monteverdetours and activitiesarco iris lodge
