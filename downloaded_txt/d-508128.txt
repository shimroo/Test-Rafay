company profileabout us









the company was established 1980 with the factory located in tainan, taiwan. we are a specialist manufacturer of p.v.c folding doors, louver doors, interior doors and wall panels.
high quality, innovative colors, satisfactory service. export markets include europe, south-east asia, center & south america, middle-east australia and japan.
