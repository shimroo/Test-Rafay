the pennsylvania nut growers association was formed in 1932 for the promotion of interest in hardy nut bearing trees, their products, and their culture. we are a non-profit group dedicated to assisting local professionals, hobbyists, and students in growing higher quality nut trees. our members benefit through a wide range of opportunities, including tree grafting workshops, demonstrations, farm tours, and newsletter articles as well as access to a network of experienced members. interested in joining?




    learn more

eventseach year we participate in the pennsylvania farm show, and in the summer members gather for a picnic. we also host grafting workshops to share skills with newer growers and micro-nurseries.
