over two decades of design
with over two decades of design work under our belt, thom filicia inc. is one of today’s most influential and respected interior design firms. based in new york city, our projects include residential, hospitality and commercial interiors & architecture all over the world.



learn more





























our philosophy
we believe that interiors should tell your authentic story. they should express where you’ve been, where you are, and where you’re going. we take pride in helping our clients live life more beautifully and would love to work with you!



get in touch



















 






















thom filicia home collection
the thom filicia home collection was born from thom’s desire to create home furnishings that are exceptional, accessible and designed for the way we live today. his collections capture classic simplicity, while introducing unique modern detailing that is both timely and timeless. 



view home collection






 











sedgwick & brattle
sedgwick & brattle is thom’s first lifestyle showroom. named from the cross streets that he grew up on, s&b offers products from the thom filicia home collection as well as a curated collection of items that speak to the thom filicia aesthetic. visit us at the new york design center in nyc or shop online today!
