our story
welcome



mcguire harley-davidson®
your local pacheco, california harley-davidson® dealership
mcguire harley-davidson® is owned and operated by motorcycle enthusiasts. our goal is to provide the highest level of customer service possible. our reputation stems from a professional and courteous sales staff that believe in a no pressure buying experience. riding a motorcycle comes with a sense of freedom that you just can’t get driving a car. we are passionate about our sport and our business. we don’t have to ride, we are compelled to ride. all of our employees share that passion and they all ride. we fulfill dreams and strive to give you the best experience possible when it comes to purchasing your harley-davidson® motorcycle.
