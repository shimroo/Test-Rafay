nestled nestled between san diego and los angeles, this dreamy seaside hideaway is a lush oasis amid the sprawl of socal. down here, things are different. it’s surf, sun and no stress. no schedules. strictly pleasure. in laguna, we are free to be exactly as we are.















a  place for winter rest & summer play



construction is underway at our historic hotel and we can’t wait to share it with you when it’s complete. in the meantime enjoy a delicious meal at one of our open restaurants, or have a drink with us at our fully stocked bar that over looks the ocean.
