see, feel, and perform well with the right light for healthcare spaces and complex environments.








with a history of producing innovative lighting for healthcare and specialty applications, alkco has been defined as a leader in quality of light since the beginning. alkco's sturdy portfolio of solutions illuminate mri rooms, patient rooms, surgical suites, cleanrooms and more, with options to tailor light using industry-leading technologies and lighting controls designed to support well-being and efficiency. today, alkco continues to strive to help you see well, feel well, and perform well. ​

patient-centric indoor lighting solutions for the modern healthcare facility



lighting plays an important role in healthcare, improving both the patient experience and the staff’s ability to provide care.
 
designed to meet the specialized needs of inpatient and outpatient facilities, our luminaires prioritize patient comfort and aesthetics without sacrificing functionality or efficiency. alkco offers lighting for healthcare and cleanroom applications that support the wellbeing of patients and healthcare providers that care for them.
 
explore alkco products




























lighting for medical and scientific spaces in healthcare

 alkco lighting solutions work with led lighting technologies and controls to help reduce costs by saving energy. the latest led technologies from signify, like accurender color rendering technology and ledalite natureconnect natural lighting system, support wellbeing and provide a nurturing atmosphere for patients. connected systems like interact and dynalite can create a dynamic work environment for staff and improve the operational efficiency of facilities.
