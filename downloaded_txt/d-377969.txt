welcome to roll-n-roaster - celebrating 54 years!








when in sheepshead bay, brooklyn, a trip to roll-n-roaster is a must. our top quality roast beef is slowly roasted throughout the day and prepared to your liking; rare, medium, or well. we slice it thinly and pile it high on our own freshly baked rolls, topped off with natural pan gravy. we have more than just roast beef sandwiches, for those looking for some variety! through our years of excellent customer service, we have become a brooklyn community staple!
