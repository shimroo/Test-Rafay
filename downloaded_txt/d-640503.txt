about the cincinnati paralegal association




the cincinnati paralegal association (cpa) welcomes paralegals in all of its many forms and title variations.  

we also have membership categories for:
 former and retired paralegals
law firms (administrative assistants/legal assistants/legal secretaries, office/h.r. managers, attorneys, i.t./ediscovery/litigation team, marketing, etc.)
education (colleges/universities offering paralegal/legal studies, professors and students of paralegal/legal studies) 
vendors in the legal space
companies with legal staff
government and court officials and staff. 


the cpa offers professional and social opportunities for in-person and online networking and career support, monthly free continuing legal education credits for members, a job board, a monthly newsletter, mentor-mentee opportunities, social clubs, a national paralegal day celebration, formal annual meeting dinner, and annual holiday party.  

join today.

the cpa is  an ohio nonprofit corporation committed to the professional development of the individual paralegal and the paralegal profession, the establishment and support of professional standards, and the advancement of paralegals as an integral and distinctive partner in the delivery of legal services.
