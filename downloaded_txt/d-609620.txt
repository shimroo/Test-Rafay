come spend your summer at cua!



welcome to the catholic university of america's summer sessions page! here in the heart of our nation we offer a variety of enriching programs designed to challenge young minds. held on our 176-acre beautiful campus we provide students with the opportunity to explore their passions, develop new skills, and experience college life in a vibrant environment. whether your child is interested in math, writing, game design, or storytelling our summer camps offer a unique and memorable experience that blends learning with fun. join us this summer at the catholic university of america and see what we have to offer!

















admission
whether you are already enrolled as a catholic university student, visiting from another university, an international visitor, a pre-college student, or a working professional, we will help you find all the information you need to plan and participate in our summer sessions.
