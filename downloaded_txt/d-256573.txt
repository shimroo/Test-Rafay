southern indiana is soin
welcome to soin—our corner of southern indiana, where the midwest meets the south and country lanes connect with city streets. we’re located a mile north of louisville, kentucky, and look forward to you visiting our friendly cities and towns where you will be treated to scenic views of the ohio river, rolling hills and picturesque farms.
