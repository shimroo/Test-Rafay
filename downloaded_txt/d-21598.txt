        the online bookshop for maps, travel books & holiday guides  	  

maps worldwide are the uk’s longest established online map & travel bookshop having offered worldwide delivery of maps, atlases, travel books, nautical guides and other map & travel goods since 1998.
in addition to the extensive range of maps, charts, atlases & travel guides we also offer a range of map-related gifts - including many personalised items.
we also offer bespoke on-demand digital and printed map services. this allows us to create custom wall maps and site maps for planning applications, for example. if you want to know more about this service, please get in touch.
