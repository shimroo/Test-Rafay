download my goodlettsville, the city's new mobile app, for timely information and alerts 		



hold local government access in the palm of your hand, and receive alerts with the my goodlettsville app. you can also choose to receive texts, calls and emails.						read on...







goodlettsville embarks on an update to its comprehensive land use plan 		



the city is asking residents to complete a short survey to guide growth and development decisions over the coming decades.						read on...







goodlettsville pd now accepting applications for the 2025 citizens police academy 		



the cpa is designed to give citizens a better understanding about law enforcement and how police officers work within the community.						read on...







homeschool day at mansker's station and the bowen house 		



homeschool day at mansker's station and the bowen house is open to any size homeschool group, from individual families to large homeschool cooperatives.  homeschool day is offered one day per month.						read on...







heritage day 		



heritage day at mansker's station and the bowen house is a popular annual event which takes place on three consecutive days in april to accommodate the overwhelming response of guests who want to experience life on the frontier and on the plantation.						read on...







kick off the holiday season with the city of goodlettsville's hometown holidays events 		



all are invited to goodlettsville's tree lighting ceremony, candy cane village reveal, santa's holiday party, christmas parade, yulefest and more!						read on...







keep goodlettsville beautiful is participating in “no trash november" 		



residents are encouraged to participate in cleanups, adopt-a-street, the use of seeclickfix, and other cleanup efforts.						read on...







goodlettsville’s baker selected as 2024 tennessee city recorder of the year 		



this award was presented by the tennessee association of municipal clerks and recorders (tamcar)						read on...







help to shape the future of parks & recreation by participating in a survey 		



we value your input in the update of the parks and recreation master plan to ensure we are meeting the needs of residents.						read on...







city of goodlettsville to host free household waste collection event 		



the goodlettsville public works convenience drop-off and recycling center is the drop-off site.						read on...







goodfest in the park is set for saturday, october 26, 2024 at moss-wright park 		



with a new name and branding, goodfest in the park combines goodfest & fall market with halloween in the park						read on...







goodlettsville’s high receives 2024 assistant city manager of the year award 		



this award is presented by the tennessee city management association on even numbered years.						read on...







tax freeze and tax relief registration event for senior citizens 		



representatives from davidson & sumner county trustees offices will be at goodlettsville city hall on september 19 and 20 registering senior adults						read on...







construction to begin on the city of goodlettsville’s main street enhancement project 		



will improve lanes and intersections, landscaping, lighting, pedestrian access, and address safety and congestion.						read on...







goodlettsville to kick-off beautification award for local businesses and neighborhoods 		



nominate a business or neighbor for keeping their landscaping and yards looking good!						read on...







have you picked up your copy of the city of goodlettsville's new magazine, good vibes? 		



the city of goodlettsville's new magazine, good vibes,  shares inspiring stories that make up our vibrant community.						read on...







goodlettsville parks and recreation to host movies in the park this summer! 		



join us for a cinematic experience under the stars at peay park. the fun starts at 6:30pm, and the movie starts at sundown.						read on...







city of goodlettsville’s independence day festivities announced for july 3rd 		



main activities kick off at 4:00pm at beautiful moss-wright park, and fireworks go off at 9:00pm.						read on...







city of goodlettsville to host a community cleanup day march 23, 2024 		



keep goodlettsville beautiful, as an affiliate of keep tennessee beautiful, is taking part in the great american cleanup.						read on...







goodlettsville celebrates tennessee tree day this spring 		



goodlettsville public works is the local pick-up site for trees on tree day hosted by the tennessee environmental council.						read on...
