john a. jensen iii ’01 

executive director of alumni and career services


p:
540-458-8593


e:
jensenj@wlu.edu


john was appointed executive director of alumni and career services at washington and lee university in july 2022. in this role, he leads a combined team consisting of the offices of alumni engagement, career and professional development, and lifelong learning. working together, his team engages and supports students, alumni and parents throughout their w&l experience, from the first year on campus to graduation and beyond.























hillary cocke

director of alumni engagement


p:
540-458-8551


e:
hcocke@wlu.edu


hillary is the director of alumni engagement at washington and lee university, where she leads the alumni engagement team, overseeing chapter programs and on-campus alumni events. she joined w&l at the end of 2024, bringing extensive experience in alumni relations and development.hillary earned a bachelor’s degree in economics and american studies from the university of virginia and a master of science in social work from the university of tennessee, knoxville. before joining w&l, she worked in alumni relations and development at the university of virginia and the university of richmond.
