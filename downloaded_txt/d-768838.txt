about our nyc marina
are you tired to being bounced around on the hudson?…come and check out the smooth as glass waters off the morris canal in liberty harbor marina. we have dockage for a day, a week, or year-round and we offer a convenient payment plan that doesn’t drain your wallet before the season even starts. at liberty harbor marina, we service individuals looking for a convenient and reliable jersey city and nyc marina, and we offer all the services that a nyc marina should provide plus more.

harbor amenities

the liberty harbor marina offers the calmest waters in the area while being conveniently located.  our key location can provide almost instant access to new york & manhattan through via the path train, ferry and hudson bergen light rail. come visit and you’ll see why our marina is a cut above the competition.
