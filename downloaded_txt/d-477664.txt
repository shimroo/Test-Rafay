welcome to imperial rv center
welcome to imperial rv center. we are an rv dealer serving customers around des moines and the surrounding areas and helping families build memories that will last a lifetime. we have new and used rvs for sale, as well as service, parts, and accessories for your rv.

whether you are looking for a new or used rv for sale, we have a large selection of quality products at competitive prices. we offer travel trailers, toy haulers, folding campers, and 5th wheels from leading brands, such as alpine avalanche, arcadia, carbon, hideout, outback, passport, raptor, sprinter, prowler, wildwood and wildwood heritage glen. we provide a custom starter pack and an instructional class about how to use your rv to help you prepare for your adventure on the road with confidence.

in addition to new and used rv sales, we carry a large selection of parts and accessories for your rv. we offer a wide variety of quality products for hitch and tow, inside your rv, outside your rv, and rv maintenance products, including accessories, appliances, cleaners, freshwater products, hardware, lighting, sanitation, sealants, and much more.

please browse our website to learn more about imperial rv, view our online inventory of new and used rvs for sale, send requests for parts and accessories, and check out web specials. for more information or answers to your questions, call imperial rv or visit our rv dealership to see our inventory in person.
