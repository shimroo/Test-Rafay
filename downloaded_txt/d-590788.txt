ukri metascience ai early career fellowships



the uk metascience unit will fund a cohort of early career fellows to build our understanding of how the growing adoption of ai is changing the research landscape, including what epistemic, metascientific, ethical and socioeconomic questions this raises, and how governments, industry, and funding organisations should respond.




opportunity status: 
open


funders: 

economic and social research council (esrc), arts and humanities research council (ahrc), biotechnology and biological sciences research council (bbsrc), engineering and physical sciences research council (epsrc), medical research council (mrc), natural environment research council (nerc), science and technology facilities council (stfc) 


co-funders: 
department for science, innovation and technology (dsit), alfred p. sloan foundation

ukri creating opportunities: rethinking economic (in)activity



apply for funding to identify ways of supporting economic activity in places experiencing high rates of ill-health, disability, and informal care in the uk.
you must be based at a uk research organisation eligible for ukri funding.




opportunity status: 
open

	uk research and innovation, arts and humanities research council (ahrc), biotechnology and biological sciences research council (bbsrc), economic and social research council (esrc), engineering and physical sciences research council (epsrc), innovate uk, medical research council (mrc), natural environment research council (nerc)
