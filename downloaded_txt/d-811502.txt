lucky box has been a wholesale provider of fine jewelry packaging, jewelry boxes, jewelry displays, shopping bags, jewelry supplies, pouches, gift boxes, and watch boxes since 1996. due to our large in-stock availability, we can ship products directly to our clients with minimal wait time. by procuring global partnerships with jewelry supply factories across asia, lucky box has always been able to provide the best prices to its clients. 






custom hot stamp printing
do you want to personalize your order?  lucky box offers premier hot stamping services that allow you to customize your jewelry boxes and accessories. we hot stamp every product in-house to ensure the integrity of our prints and to produce faster shipping times. we can print any logo, color, or design. simply send us a picture illustrating your stamping needs and we will take care of the rest! we will even provide you with a wide array of foil colors, shapes, and sizes to help you choose the most appropriate look for your jewelry supplies.
