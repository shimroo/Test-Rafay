what : xlsgen lets you read, write, calculate, display and print excel spreadsheets without using excel. it beats excel in speed by orders of magnitude. virtually any programming environment can be used including c/c++, vb, java, c#/vb.net, python, delphi, rust and so on thanks to its com-based programming interface. xlsgen generates excel files for excel 97, 2000, xp, 2003, 2007, 2010, 2013 and 2016. xlsgen also exports your spreadsheets as pdf, odf, xps, html, csv and xml.who : xlsgen is developed by arst design, an independent software vendor.
