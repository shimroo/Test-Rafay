designing your dream engagement ring
online or in-person
creating your own custom engagement ring is a simple process. design online or in person at our seattle or bellevue showroom. customize from our collection of jewelry or upload your own inspiration images. your joseph jewelry designer will create a 3d model you can customize until your design is perfect. 
get started















design
work with a designer online or in store at our bellevue or seattle showroom to solidify your design with a free sketch and price quote. we can customize an existing piece, combine different rings, or start an entirely new design.














3d model
review stunning 3d renderings of your ring and request changes until it's perfect. visualize your design from every angle and ensure it exceeds your expectations.














production
our master jewelers meticulously craft your one-of-a-kind ring in our bellevue workshop. each piece is hand-crafted with exceptional quality and meticulous attention to detail, creating a lasting heirloom.

custom hand engraving
elevate your ring with the timeless beauty of hand engraving. our bellevue-based master engraver personalizes your piece with exquisite details, transforming it into a one-of-a-kind treasure. choose from a curated collection of patterns or collaborate on a custom design to express your individuality.

jewelry repair & restoration
give your beloved jewelry a second chance to shine. our skilled artisans meticulously restore and repair heirlooms, antique pieces, and everyday favorites. from resizing a well-loved ring to replacing lost gemstones, we handle every detail with expertise and care. your cherished piece will be brought back to its original brilliance, ready to be treasured for generations to come.
