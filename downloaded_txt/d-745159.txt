welcome to bloomfield! it is my great honor to serve as mayor for the township of bloomfield, and i am pleased to welcome you to our official township website. whether you are a lifelong resident, a newcomer, or just visiting, i hope you’ll find valuable resources for staying informed and engaged with our wonderful community. bloomfield is a vibrant and growing community, offering an exceptional environment for families and businesses alike. our township boasts beautiful parks, thriving shopping, dining, and entertainment options, and a welcoming, diverse community. as mayor, i am committed to fostering transparency, trust, and community engagement. moving forward, we are prioritizing initiatives like “clean and strong bloomfield” to improve safety, infrastructure, and quality of life for all. we’re also investing in sustainable practices, supporting local businesses, and enhancing essential services to prepare our town for the future. this website serves as a tool to keep you informed and engaged in what’s happening in our township. you can access information about local programs, services, and events, as well as stay up to date on the latest news from bloomfield’s departments. we encourage you to take advantage of interactive features that allow you to report issues like potholes or streetlight outages, and sign up for email notifications to receive important updates from the township. in the coming months, you may notice a number of changes and upgrades to this website, in an effort to further improve accessibility and user experience. i encourage you to follow the bloomfield township facebook page, and take an active role in our community. whether it’s by volunteering, attending a township council meeting, or simply reaching out to share your ideas, there are countless ways to get involved and help make bloomfield an even better place to call home. thank you again for visiting our website, i hope you’ll visit often for the latest news and updates. if you have any questions, suggestions, or concerns, please don’t hesitate to reach out to my office or any other dedicated township staff. we are here to serve you, and we value your input. sincerely, jenny mundell mayor of bloomfield
