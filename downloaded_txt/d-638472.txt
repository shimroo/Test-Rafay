welcome to norman's
 


award winning fine dining restaurant in orlandoindulge in award-winning fine dining that treats you to a selection of culinary and mixology experiences at norman's. dine indoors in an elegant space that complements the rich flavors of our seasonal menus developed using chef van aken's “new world cuisine”.sit back and relish alfresco dining, creative cocktails, and balmy florida breezes on our covered outdoor patio. celebrate a special occasion or event with our selection of alcoves, and private dining spaces. sip selections from our glass-enclosed wine cellar offering an extensive and diverse list of varietals. norman's was recently named the best restaurant in orlando, fl, in the 2024 orlando magazine readers' choice dining awards. please join us for wonderful fusion cuisine and enjoy a memorable experience worth coming back for!










our food
norman's orlando offers “new world cuisine,” featuring local ingredients and a fusion of cultures, textures, and colors that will please your palate and soul. our menu selection offers lots of diverse dishes crafted by our talented team and the famous restaurateur, chef, and author norman van aken.
