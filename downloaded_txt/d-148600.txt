solutions for every situation





















            life and health risk
          
you need a partner with the financial strength, global reach, and local insight to help you manage change in life and health reinsurance, and the intense client focus to make the most of your in-force book. rga stands with you, working together to enhance your existing product portfolios, expand into new business segments, meet regulatory requirements, manage capital more efficiently, and more. 

learn more
































            financial solutions
          
reinsurance is one of the most flexible risk and financial management tools. it should be considered alongside traditional capital instruments and corporate actions. 

rga pioneered use of reinsurance as a financial management and portfolio-optimizing tool, and we remain a leading provider of such solutions worldwide.


learn more
































            product creation
          
at rga, product development isn’t a transaction; it’s a commitment to creating new opportunities together and ensuring their success. 

whether targeting new segments, insuring new risks, innovating in new channels, or assimilating the latest technologies, rga has experience bringing the best insurance products to life. 


learn more
































            underwriting
          
we are relentless in our commitment to developing underwriting insights and innovations to empower insurers.  our combination of global mortality expertise and local insight is entirely unique. rga has amassed one of the largest mortality databases in the world, and we combine this insight with deep knowledge of local market drivers – from data privacy and protection regulations to consumer expectations. 


































            claims
          
claims management has always been complex, and never more so than today. every insurer’s reputation rests on the ability to pay the right claim at the right time, every time. 
rga is distinguished by more than the depth of our technical expertise; we are known for the value we place on forming stable, long-term partnerships and our demonstrated commitment to protecting the honest policyholder. 


learn more
































            data solutions
          
data and analytics can reveal remarkable insights – from untapped market opportunities to shifting consumer behaviors. but it takes the right people to translate potential into value. rga has the experience and insight to put data to work. partner with rga to responsibly combine technology, data, and expertise. 

learn more
