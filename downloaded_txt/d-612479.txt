about me





bong leal


bong aka mangguhit is an
advertising art director on his
day job creating mainstream
advertisement consisting print/event/tactical/retail ad
and tv commercials, he helps
illustrates storyboards, do animatics
on imovie and creates visuals and
do some creative design stuffs
such as packaging, brochures
and the likes. he always carries
a sketchbook and pen, sketching
things, stranger people and friends
and families. it's a past time to him
and a creative therapy. outside of
his day job, he tries to create his
own comics on his free time and
also collaborates with a friend writer.
