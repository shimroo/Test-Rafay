new arrivals!the latest ranges  fom liberty london are so varied, and includes beautiful florals,  large decorator prints, and the very special range from  author and illustrator brian wildsmith.and then we have these  stunning decorator prints…cushions coming up!!the honeysuckle design has special memories for me of my grandma…she had a brilliant honeysuckle plant and i planted a cutting from that vine at all the homes i lived in.i have always been a lover of childrens picture books, and have collected many over the years, so these gorgeous fabrics designed from brian wildsmith illustrations are just so special.  











brian wildsmith designs  











 







 







 







 











stitch in time opened in naracoorte south australia in 1992. i have always loved fabric and sewing, and creating beautiful things.this evolved into a second shop in adelaide and then a final move to victoria.my love of beautiful fabric is a part of my life, and liberty of london fabric has been a long held passion. i now create a large range of  hand crafted liberty products.these include ladies and gentlemen’s handkerchiefs, eye pillows, scented drawer sachets, gorgeous little purses, wheat bags and padded coat hangers..
