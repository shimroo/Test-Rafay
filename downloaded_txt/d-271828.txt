welcome quilters






when you think of appliqué, think of us! we specialize in hawaiian quilt patterns, 2 fabric appliqué quilt patterns as well as appliqué quilt border and table runner patterns. check out our instructional dvds, you can learn everything you need to know about needleturn applique from nancy lee chong. she has been teaching for 30 years and you can bring her into your home with private lessons. we carry aurifil 50 wt thread along with a selection of aurifil 28wt hand quilting thread. our fabrics are high quality printed and dyed, perfect for your appliqué needs. they have been selected because they work best with our patterns, making your selection process that much easier. don't forget we have janice's hand-dyed fabrics as well. our quilt kits can be paired with our patterns as well as a selection of laser cut quilt kits.
