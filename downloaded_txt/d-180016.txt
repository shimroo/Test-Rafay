buy dresses online & in-person at peaches boutique -  chicago’s prom dress store & the largest prom boutique in the usa
discover your perfect dress at peaches boutique, the largest prom and formal dresses for teens destination in the usa, both online and in our chicago store. since our inception in 1984, we've transitioned from a mom and pop store offering women's designer clothing to becoming not just the premier prom dress store in chicago but a top destination for those looking to buy dresses online. peaches boutique stands out among online dress shops with a selection that is unmatched in variety and accessibility.about the storeour expansive brick-and-mortar location spans over 20,000 square feet, showcasing more than 30,000 dresses at any given time, and features 45 fitting rooms for an unparalleled shopping experience. we proudly stock leading designer dresses, including sherri hill, jovani, jasz, and morilee, catering to every size from 000-32w. for those looking to buy a dress online, our website offers easy navigation through 4,500 unique styles across a broad spectrum of prices, colors, and designers, ensuring you find your dream dress without compromise.fast shippingwe understand the excitement and urgency that comes with dress shopping, especially when that dress is a formal dress for a teen. that's why in-stock items are ready to be shipped the next business day to any location worldwide, making us the go-to teen online dress store for fast, reliable service.shop formal dresses for teensdive into the latest trends and explore the unique collections from our online dress shop. whether you're searching for the perfect prom dress or a stunning formal gown, peaches boutique makes it easier than ever to find exactly what you're looking for with the convenience of shopping from anywhere.
