about us

















our work 



saving lives is what we do! 



prevention is the core of our mission. science has shown that drugs and alcohol interfere with the developing adolescent brain. programs like ours work to boost protective factors and eliminate or reduce risk factors for substance abuse. 



the brent shapiro foundation has successfully created the only drug prevention program called “brent’s club,” an evidence-based and incentive program for youths. we are supported by donors worldwide who are concerned about the substance abuse epidemic. 










 




							brent’s club						


						brent’s club is an evidence-based drug prevention program for youth, focusing on promoting healthy lifestyles & rewarding positive behavior.					













 




							support network						


						our donor support network is a community of compassionate individuals and organizations dedicated to combating the substance abuse epidemic.

our work 



what we do to help! 










 




							youth support						


						create hands-on drug awareness and prevention programs at select boys and girls clubs known as brent’s club.					













 




							drug prevention						


						conquer alcohol and drug dependence by turning the fear, grief, and helplessness caused by this disease into awareness, compassion, and support.					













 




							parental support						


						we support parents in communicating with their children and other parents about alcohol and drugs.
