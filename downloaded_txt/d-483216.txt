high-bandwidth digital content protection (hdcp)welcome to the world of digital entertainment enabled by hdcp technologies. high-bandwidth digital content protection (hdcp) technologies protect high-value digital motion pictures, television programs and audio against unauthorized interception and copying between a digital set top box or digital video recorder and a digital tv or pc. hdcp is a specification developed by intel corporation to protect digital entertainment across the digital interfaces.
