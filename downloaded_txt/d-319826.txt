about una-usa midpeninsulauna-usa midpeninsula is the midpeninsula chapter of the una-usa, a movement of americans who believe that our interests and values can best be advanced by standing with the planet’s only truly universal institution: the united nations. we stand up for the un so the un can keep standing up for the world.
