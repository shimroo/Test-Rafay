serving classics with a twist



american bistro cuisine, with mediterranean influences.



recognized by locals as one of the top restaurants in aptos and santa cruz county for over 32 years, bittersweet bistro offers an american bistro cuisine-style dinner menu with mediterranean influences. dinner service from 3 pm until closing inside or outside under our heated patio.

now open on tuesdays



exciting news! starting november 12th, we’re expanding our hours and opening on tuesdays! more opportunities to enjoy your favorites and indulge in something new. see you soon!


























in the heart of aptosnestled in the picturesque town of aptos, we invite you to indulge in a cozy yet sophisticated dining experience.offering dinner service from tuesday - sunday, starting at 3 pm until the evening winds down usually around 9 pm. our beloved locale is your go-to for an enchanting evening under the stars or a vibrant afternoon on the patio. whether it's a romantic dinner, a family gathering, or simply enjoying the gentle aptos breeze, every visit promises to be a memorable part of your week.don't miss out on savoring the moment with us, where every meal is a celebration of refined tastes and community warmth.
