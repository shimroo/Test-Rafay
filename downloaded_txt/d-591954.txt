		escape from new york




when the clock strikes summer, the race is on to the largest water park in the tri-state area. this world-class oasis of 96 tree-covered acres is closer than you’d expect and more fun than you can imagine. kick back or kick it up on over 20 uniquely-themed water attractions like high-speed slides and relaxing pools. from the octopus pool in kiddie cove to the microbrews at riverview, splish splash lets you get as close to, or as far from, your family as you want to be this summer.
