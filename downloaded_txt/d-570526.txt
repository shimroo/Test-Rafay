located within a one hour drive of boston, providence, newport, and the towns of southeastern massachusetts, tiverton four corners host cozy shops nestled into a quaint 18th century new england village. 















shops & galleriesenjoy the fresh air as you walk the few steps between shops and galleries to peek in windows, meet artists at work and purchase quality goods of all kinds in an unhurried, friendly and charming atmosphere.















food & diningwhether you need a gourmet cheese & charcuterie platter,  a picnic lunch, pastries, handmade ice cream or a relaxing sit down meal.  we've got what you need.  stop and visit our award winning food purveyors. 















professional serviceswant to stay the night in a quaint but modern refurbished 19th century school house?  we have those lovely accommodations as well and if need be, a real estate company.
