“the switzerland of maine”














welcome to jackman.



take a deep breath. smell the pine. hear the sounds of nature. taste the freshest spring water in maine.















this is jackman, and this is what samuel holden found here in 1819, which has retained its timeless appeal today. revel in the great northern outdoors. be alone with your thoughts or socialize at our few places to dine and entertain. jackman - moose river region chamber of commerce invites you to come and experience the enchanting allure of jackman, where stunning natural landscapes, endless recreational opportunities, and serene tranquility await you.
