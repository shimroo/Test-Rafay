your trusted battery specialists
total battery has been canada’s trusted battery specialist for over 30 years. we stock thousands of batteries and power solutions from the smallest of watch batteries to the largest of locomotive batteries or grid tie systems. our team of battery specialists can locate hard-to-find cells or make in-house custom packs for mission critical applications. wherever you live or your business operates, we provide worldwide shipping on single orders or large volume orders.





buy online or in-store
total battery offers two convenient ways to shop. visit one of our 6 locations or buy online from the convenience of your home or business.





world wide shipping
our diversity in shipping couriers allows us to ship all over the world on time and at competitive pricing. contact us to get a shipping quote for your order today!





custom power solutions
our modification centres are able to assemble and build all types of custom battery packs for all types of chemistries. we can also re-build discontinued battery types as well.





battery specialists at your service
making a purchase based upon accurate information can not only save you money but can assist you in earning it. our battery specialists are here to help you!
