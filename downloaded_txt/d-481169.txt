why us?

 

outdoorsplaying paintball outside can be a great way to enjoy the outdoors while getting some exercise. matt's outback paintball offers a great outdoor experience with plenty of room to run around and play.



team buildingpaintball can be a great team building experience for a group of people. it can help people to work together in a fun and competitive environment. it can also be a great way to bond with people who share a common interest.



family fungetting together with the family is always great for bonding and making memories, so why not make it even more fun by playing a game of paintball together? matt's outback paintball is the perfect place for a family outing, as it offers plenty of space for everyone to play, as well as a safe and welcoming environment.
