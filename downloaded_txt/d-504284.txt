austin wildlife rescue rehabilitates and releases orphaned, injured, and sick wild animals and educates the public to coexist with wildlife.

rehabilitation center111 elbow bendelgin, tx 78621



contact us﻿512-472-9453 we are unable to pick up or transport animals



hours of operation9am - 4pm monday - sunday

 

*animals we cannot accept: domestic animals, adult deer (no spots), muscovy ducks, monk parakeets, feral hogs and other non-native species. click here to find out why
