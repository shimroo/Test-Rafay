celebrate our "jewel of the hills" community 


on february 16, 1912, a group of pioneering californians gathered east of san diego and formed a new community. then largely rural and nestled in hills surrounding highlands overlooking the coast, they eventually called this town la mesa.						read on...







green business ribbon cutting event 


join us for a green business ribbon cutting event on monday, february 17 at 4 pm in front of city hall on allison ave. our city hall building became the 8th business in la mesa and the 1st city hall in the san diego region to achieve this certification.						read on...







litter-free la mesa - waite drive 


join i love a clean san diego and the city of la mesa for a litter cleanup on waite drive! we will divert litter from our watersheds and keep it from traveling to the pacific ocean. saturday, february 22, 9 am - 11 am. register at cleansd.org/events.						read on...







ev charging station survey 


the city of la mesa is seeking the community's input on current electric vehicle charging station needs! share your challenges and successes with ev charging in la mesa and preferences for high-priority locations. the survey is open to all residents.						read on...
