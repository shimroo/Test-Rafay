welcome
welcome to jfk construction, inc. providing quality home remodeling since 1979. our many years of service to charlotte and the surrounding communities have made us a local favorite.  we owe it to homeowners like you!  you have welcomed us into your home to deliver professional service and quality projects for your comfort and enjoyment.
our projects have included all kinds of home additions and renovations.  below is a partial list of some of these projects:

master bedroom suites
room additions including 2nd story additions
custom kitchen and bath renovations
front entrance remodels
basement renovations
attic renovations
sunrooms, porches, decks and patios

we hope you will tour our company online and enjoy the beautiful photos in our gallery, the kind words from our customers and the remodeling tips and information.  please feel free to share with us any suggestions or improvements as we are always looking for ways to better serve our remodeling family.
if you have questions about a remodeling project, just let us know.  you can phone us or send a simple request using our online form on the “contact us” page. it will be our pleasure to assist you in any way possible.
