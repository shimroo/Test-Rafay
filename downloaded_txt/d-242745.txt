the mrs world pageant was established in 1984, by david and elaine marmel. it is the first international beauty contest solely for married women. it provides a platform for married contestants to showcase not just their beauty, but also their intellect and community outreach efforts.  this year the pageant celebrated their 40th and ruby anniversary at the westgate resort and casino in las vegas, nevada.in a landmark achievement that has resonated around the world, 33 year old tshego gaelae made history as the first black woman to win the title and the second to bring to title home to her beautiful south africa.  tshego balances her roles as a devoted mother, wife, attorney and model, she expressed her overwhelming joy shortly after her victory by a “thank you to god”. tshego’s success is a testament to the ideals celebrated by the pageant, where diversity and empowerment take center stage.
