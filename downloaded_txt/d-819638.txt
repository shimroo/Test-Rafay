discover the vibrant world of art in southwest oklahoma!



at the leslie powell gallery, we are passionate about promoting creativity, supporting local and regional artists, and enriching our community through the power of fine art. whether you're an art enthusiast or a budding artist, you'll find a welcoming space to indulge your passion.explore, appreciate, and be inspired.welcome to the leslie powell gallery.

explore,appreciate,be inspired



join us in celebrating local and regional artists right here in lawton, oklahoma.embark on a journey of visual delight and cultural enrichment through our visual art exhibitions, live poetry readings, and other community events—all free of charge and open to the public.
