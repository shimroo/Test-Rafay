m&d supply: trusted local hardware store 



m&d supply is a third-generation hardware store, founded in 1967. m&d prides itself on customer service and stays true to its slogan,“where good help is easy to find.”based on our long term experience and customer relation, we can assure you top most hardware supplies with 100% quality assurance on our products. our product area mainly covers both residential & commercial tools:electrical suppliesplumbing suppliespainting toolsbuilding materialsgardening equipmenthome improvement essentialsin addition to customer service, m&d supply is known for stocking hard-to-find items and its long-standing partnership with ace hardware. throughout the decades, despite “big box” stores and internet competition, m&d has continued to grow throughout southeast texas.currently, m&d supply operates nine locations, with a roster of more than 260 employees, eager to serve our residential and commercial customers.thank you for supporting your local m&d supply!
