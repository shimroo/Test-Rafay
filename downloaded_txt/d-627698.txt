an arlington hotel near dc attractions



set in the vibrant ballston neighborhood, three blocks from the metro, our arlington hotel places you near the most popular washington, dc area attractions. our location off i-66 makes us ideal for business and leisure travelers offering access to many local companies, shopping at the ballston quarter, and iconic dc museums and landmarks, including the white house. 
holiday inn® arlington at ballston features comfortable rooms and suites with 32-inch tvs, keurig coffeemakers, and refrigerators. you'll love our hotel amenities, including free wi-fi, 5,000 square feet of meeting space, a fitness center, and a seasonal outdoor pool. enjoy local comfort food for breakfast and dinner at the silver leaf restaurant, where kids eat free.

celebrate world pride in dc with us

celebrate the 50th anniversary of pride in dc, may 17–june 8! stay near parades, festivals, and events. enjoy modern rooms, and an inclusive atmosphere. book now to experience this historic celebration in the nation’s capital!


                                        learn more
                                    





















cherry blossom season

experience the beauty of cherry blossom season in washington, dc! stay at our conveniently located hotel and enjoy easy access to the national mall, tidal basin, and vibrant cherry blossom displays. book your room now to witness the stunning blooms and participate in festival activities. it’s the perfect time to explore dc’s springtime charm!




















near the sights of arlington and dc

located three blocks from the ballston-mu metro (orange/silver line), our hotel invites you to experience our city. we are close to shopping, dining, and top arlington attractions like ballston quarter, kettler capitals iceplex, and the pentagon memorial. the hotel is across the river from washington, dc, the national mall, white house, and more.




















arlington's business hotel

we're just blocks from many companies and government agencies, including accenture, management sciences for health, willis towers watson, the nature conservancy, aes, marymount, fdic seidman center, and the office of naval research. stay on track with work or fire off a presentation in our business center with a printer and a copier.

exclusive government hotel rates

take advantage of special government rates when traveling to washington, dc. enjoy discounted stays at our hotel near government offices, federal buildings, and iconic landmarks. book now for comfortable accommodations, prime locations, and exclusive savings designed for government employees.

faqs for holiday inn arlington at ballston



does holiday inn arlington at ballston have a pool?


yes, holiday inn arlington at ballston has an outdoor pool.




how much is parking at holiday inn arlington at ballston?


at holiday inn arlington at ballston, self-parking is available for a daily rate of 20 usd .




can i bring my pet to holiday inn arlington at ballston?


no, pets are not allowed at holiday inn arlington at ballston.




can i cancel my booking at holiday inn arlington at ballston for a full refund?


always check the hotel's cancellation policy before booking a rate. room cancellation is subject to rate booked at time of reservation and cancellation policy varies by rate.




is there a complimentary shuttle available at holiday inn arlington at ballston?


no, there is not a complimentary local area shuttle available at holiday inn arlington at ballston.




what are the check-in and check-out times for holiday inn arlington at ballston?


check-in at holiday inn arlington at ballston is from 3:00 pm, and check-out time is 12:00 pm. contact the hotel directly for options available for early check-in or late check-out.




does holiday inn arlington at ballston offer free wi-fi?


yes, the holiday inn arlington at ballston offers free wifi!
