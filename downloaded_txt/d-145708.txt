the nova scotia nature trust works to safeguard lands, waters, plants and animals in mi’kma’ki, the ancestral and unceded lands of the mi’kmaq. we recognize the mi’kmaq as past, present, and future caretakers of these lands and we are committed to a path of reconciliation. read more about our journey toward reconciliation...
