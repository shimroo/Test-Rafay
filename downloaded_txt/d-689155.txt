the freedom museum tells the courageous stories of american men and women who served on the battlefront and the home front.



our museum’s mission is to preserve & retell these stories — documenting our local veterans & their valor, exploring their contributions to history, & evoking a renewed sense of american pride.
























we can do it! 
our museum relies on donations to run our educational programs, speaker series, museum tours, and preservation efforts. by making a donation today, you’re providing children and adults the opportunity to relive history, honor our local heroes, and preserve their memory. 



donate now






















“the freedom museum is poised on the crossroads of history, past and present.  we are also located at the crossroads of center and west streets, directly in the heart of downtown manassas.  the freedom museum memorializes the sacrifices made by our local veterans and their contribution to the preservation of our liberty, our american way of life and our freedoms that continue to endure to this day.”

— mr. thomas hueg, past president, freedom museum
