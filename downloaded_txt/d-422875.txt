history 


nestled in the sierra nevada mountains, mariposa county is rich in history, culture and natural beauty. from early native people to gold-seeking settlers to today’s tourists, inhabitants and visitors have shaped a legacy of discovery and transformation.			






living here 


as the home of yosemite national park, mariposa county offers stunning natural beauty and world-class recreational opportunities. with no incorporated cities (or stop lights), the county's 17,000 residents enjoy the slow pace of life.			






things to do 


today, our county is known for friendly citizens who greet or play host to the large number of visitors who come from around the world. we are also known for our festivals and fun spirit which are enjoyed by visitors and locals, alike.
