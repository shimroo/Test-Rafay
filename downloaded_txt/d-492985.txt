home

the southern region, cfa’s region 7, consists of maryland, virginia, district of columbia, north carolina, south carolina, georgia, florida, alabama, eastern tennessee and southern kentucky. the cat fanciers’ association is the world’s largest registry of pedigreed cats. here cat lovers, breeders and exhibitors share southern hospitality as warm as the weather, as well as enthusiasm for our cats — from the beautiful pedigreed cats, the fun of household pet classes, the excitement of agility runs, and the grace and beauty that each cat brings to the show hall and to our hearts!
