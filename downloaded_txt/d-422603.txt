about fortune drums: custom snare drums and drum-sets since 1983. repairs, refurbishing and re-edging since 1968.the fortune drums concept represents modern advances in drum construction coupled with traditional voicing and finishing practices which have been lost to mass production. here you will find instruments that are one of a kind pieces that have never been built before!
