nationally recognized as the largest continuously operating open-air arts and crafts market in the country, portland saturday market is proud to provide a marketplace for local small businesses creating handmade arts, crafts, and foods from the pacific northwest. new vendor applications are welcome anytime! 



become a vendor















with questions or issues filling out your application, please contact us via email at info@saturdaymarket.org or phone at (503) 222-6072.
