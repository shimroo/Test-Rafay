redeem your gift







lets get started

we’ve made the process to redeem your gift certificate a breeze.  first, locate your redemption code. 
					
the redemption code is your gift certificate number and pin. this information can be found inside your printed card on the right-hand side. if you have received an ecyg (electronic choose-your-gift®), you’ll find the redemption code on the lower portion of the digital certificate attached to your email.  each certificate has a unique redemption code.
once you have located this information enter the certificate number and pin in the boxes below.
after entering your certificate number and pin, you will be able to view the selection of brand name product in your gift level. once you choose the item you wish to receive you will be prompted to provide your shipping information, and that’s all there is to it!  
we are confident that you will enjoy the item you selected with your gift certificate. happy shopping!
