welcome to harlingen medical center
welcome to harlingen medical center, a nationally recognized, award-winning hospital dedicated to clinical excellence and enhancing the patient experience.
named one of the nation’s 50 top cardiovascular hospitals by fortune and ibm watson health, we have also been recognized as one of america’s 100 best hospitals™ for orthopedic surgery by becker’s spine review. our commitment to patient safety has earned us an “a” rating from the leapfrog group and a gold plus award from the american stroke association.
harlingen medical center has received numerous accolades from healthgrades for excellence in various procedures, including gallbladder removal, appendectomy, vaginal and c-section deliveries, gynecologic surgeries, coronary interventions, heart attack treatment, pacemaker procedures, total knee replacement, hip fracture treatment, and spinal fusion.
we are dedicated to providing innovative, high-quality care close to home. using state-of-the-art equipment, advanced technology, and evidence-based practices, we ensure our patients receive the most sophisticated care available.
