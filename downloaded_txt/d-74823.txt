festival international de louisiane




    april 23 -27, 2025
  














the largest international music festival in the u.s. each april, downtown lafayette comes alive for a 5-day cultural celebration. festival international boasts top-notch music from around the world, gourmet food, handcrafted artworks and so much more! people of all ages come from near and far to experience festival’s magic. no ticket is required for our event. show your support by purchasing merchandise and on-site beverages. where else can you enjoy authentic asian, cajun or greek food, while listening to an acclaimed african band, as beautifully costumed belgium stilt walkers dance by?! come experience the world of festival with us! see you april 23-27, 2025. 














it’s going to be a great yeartake a look at what’s in store for festival international 2025! explore the music lineup, grab your pin and poster, and get ready to groove with us for our 39th year.

our vibeour cultural celebration is known around the globe for its bold and diverse music, food, art & unforgettable experiences. the 5-day festival transforms lafayette, louisiana into a colorful entertainment mecca with multiple stages throughout downtown. we are a non-profit organization that proudly attracts visitors from up to 48 states and 26 countries during a typical year. aside from making a massive economical impact, festival provides 300,000+ visitors with a chance to explore the bold culture and creativity of acadiana, while also enjoying diverse cultures. as part of festival’s mission, this event also strengthens the francophone and international communities’ ties to lafayette. attendees can expect to hear great french music from france, africa, belgium, louisiana and beyond. we love experiencing our roots with visitors and locals! as always, support local businesses & artists with us at festival international de louisiane!
