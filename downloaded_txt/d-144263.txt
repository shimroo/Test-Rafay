president's day 


attention belleville residents: in recognition of president's day, city offices will be closed on mon., feb. 17, 2025.  given that the landfill will be open on the holiday, trash pickup will adhere to the normal schedule the week of feb. 17-21.						read on...







letter to residents of belleville from jason poole, dir. of public works, regarding snow removal 


the city’s street dept. tries to ensure that streets remain clear of ice and snow. businesses and residents are asked to do their part as well, and to be aware of factors affecting snow removal. read on to view letter from director jason poole.						read on...

60th annual mayor's prayer breakfast 


we are pleased to announce that registration for the 60th annual belleville mayor’s prayer breakfast is now open. this always anticipated event occurs on wednesday, february 26, 2025, at bellecourt manor, from 7:30 am – 9:00 am.						read on...

cities in harmony 


save the date for the 2nd annual cities in harmony free family event on saturday, april 26, 2025 from 11:00 a.m. - 2:00 p.m. it will be a memorable day for our metro east region and surrounding communities!
