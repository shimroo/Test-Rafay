chapter vision



northeastern oklahoma ashrae will be the regional leader in the arts and sciences of heating, ventilation, air conditioning and refrigeration; will be the leading source of technical and educational information for northeaster oklahoma; will be the primary provider of opportunity for professional growth for chapter members.
