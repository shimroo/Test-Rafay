what makes our jade jewelry so unique 



the finest quality jade 



we hand select only the highest quality jade in manufacturing necklaces, bangles, bracelets, earrings, rings and pendants. our jade craftsman are among the most talented in the industry. the majority are 3rd and 4th generation jade carvers and take great pride in their work. this combination of experience, talent and pride guarantees that you are getting the finest quality jade jewelry. 



discounted jade prices 



since we have our own factories in asia, this alleviates the middleman and suppliers, and since we are a direct importer, this allows us to sell our jade jewelry to you the customer at great discounted prices. we are direct manufacturers, importers and designers of fine quality jade. 



jade specialists since 1985 



since 1985 we have we have grown our family business to being the most trusted name in genuine fine quality jade jewelry. our mission has always been to provide all our valued customers the very best customer service before and even after the sale. now after 30 years, we are still a family business, and take pride in the same honorable principles we started with. 



jade items in stock and ready to ship from our u.s. location 



all orders are shipped within 24-48 hours from our u.s. location to all u.s. locations and most locations around the world. our low flat shipping rate applies whether you are buying a single item or many items, the shipping rate stays the same. 



the best jade available 



the quality asian jade is found in burma and northern china. whether you are looking for fine quality jade bangles, or fine quality jade pendants, or fine quality jade necklaces, or fine quality jade bracelets, or fine quality jade earrings, or fine quality jade rings, you can be assured that you will receive only the finest quality jade jewelry here at oriental jade jewelry. 



whether it’s a gift for someone special, or a treat for yourself, buy the best jade here at orientaljadejewelry.com
