private property debris removal (ppdr) program extended through february 2025 

the application process for the private property debris removal (ppdr) program has been extended through february 2025. the program is free to qualified property owners who complete an application. visit black mtn. library wed.-sat. 8a-5p to apply.				read on...






public works announces return of regular scout route sanitation collection beginning mon., jan. 20th 

scout routes and backdoor stop sanitation collection will resume their pre-hurricane regular routes on monday, january 20th, and residents will no longer need to bring their trash to the nearest main road.				read on...
