mission & vision statement
 






  
 
 





missionto enhance the archuleta county community through excellent service and accountability with maximum transparency.



 
 





visionto sustain a community rich in natural beauty where families, individuals and businesses can thrive.



 
 
 





core values



 
 
 





teamworkwe work collaboratively within our organization and the community to provide services to create effective solutions and achieve shared goals.



 
 





servicewe are committed to delivering a high-quality experience, every time, through committed customer service.



 
 





accountabilitywe hold ourselves to high standards and act with transparency, responsibility, and integrity.



 
 





balancewe support the whole employee to foster enthusiastic, dedicated, and established staff.
