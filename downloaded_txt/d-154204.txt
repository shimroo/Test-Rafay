precision electronic services has been a source for quality industrial electronic repair and servo motor repair since 1985. we have the experience, expertise, equipment and highly trained staff that’s needed to ensure your electronic control repair and servo motor repair are handled properly.

controls. when it comes to ac drive repair and dc drive repair, the services we provide are among the best in the business. all ac drives and dc drives repaired by our company are load tested and burned in to ensure your repaired product will function properly when reinstalled in your system. the technicians here at pes have been factory trained to repair many of the top brands of drives including yaskawa, danfoss, reliance, magnetek and others.
