23 february 2025 – epiphany 7c (cocu 15c) 

grace
(genesis 45:3-11)
when bad things strike us
we look for someone to blame:
but joseph forgave.
by grace, forgiving
and through forgiving, healing,
being reconciled.
grace fuels our living
the mystery we name god:
it’s all about grace.
    jeff shrowder, 2025
