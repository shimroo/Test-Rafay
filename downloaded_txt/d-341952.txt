georgetown lombardi is part of georgetown university medical center, an internationally recognized academic health and science center with a four-part mission of research, teaching, service and patient care. our renowned physicians and nurses provide care through a partnership with medstar health, including medstar georgetown university hospital, a u.s. news top 50-ranked hospital for cancer care.
