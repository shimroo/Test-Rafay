every occasion  
the party bus is a fun and exciting way to celebrate, nightclub tours, hens and bucks nights, bus parties, birthdays and social events, debs and formals, pub crawls and winery tours. we also provide services for bus transfers.
whatever the occasion, we  ensure our bus services can get you there quickly and safely! 
 


 
 
 


 
fully accredited 
come and experience it for yourself.  the party bus is fully accredited with the  department of infrastructure, director of public transport and is a member of the  wedding car association of victoria.
