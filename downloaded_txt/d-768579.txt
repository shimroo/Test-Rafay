about the stroke service

find out about the history, mission, and numerous awards of the stroke service. we are proud to be massachusetts's first comprehensive stroke center, recognized for the highest level of stroke care.








comprehensive stroke center

massachusetts general hospital is a certified advanced comprehensive stroke center, capable of treating the most complex stroke cases.








new england regional coordinating center (nercc)

the nercc is a collaboration of sites encompassing 13 of the top academic medical centers in new england, with mass general as coordinating site, in participation with nih strokenet.
