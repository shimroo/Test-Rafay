the bicycle placesince 1972, the bicycle place has served all your bicycling needs.  we offer a full line of bicycles from del sol bicycles, haro bikes, masi bicycles and seven cycles.  we have a fully trained repair staff to service all your bicycle needs from flat tire repair to a full bicycle overhaul.  we also stock the accessories you need for your bike ride, from water bottles to inner tubes to tires and multi-tools.  come visit us today and see how we can help you with your cycling needs.
