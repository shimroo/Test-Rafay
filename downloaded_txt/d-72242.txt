how we work















capacity building and education



we provide comprehensive training, political education, and opportunities for knowledge exchange to foster growth and impact. we focus on political maturity, organisational development, and key topics such as climate crisis, social justice, security, and democracy.








            work plans
          









annual work plan is our leading series of non-formal education educational events, consisting of various forms of activity, such as summer camp, seminars, and local actions.  2024 work plan2023 work plan.








            study sessions
          









study session is annual international educational event organised together with council of europe’s youth department. we look closely at a specific political issue (eg. economics in 2024, security in 2023, urban policy in 2022) and together with participants reducing relevant documents as toolkits or manifestos. study session 2024.



















network



we connect and bring together progressive youth across eastern europe from prague to baku and from skopje to riga. 








            general assembly
          









general assembly is the highest decision-making body of our network. it elects executive committee which leads the network throughout the year. ga adopts the most important documents, such as statutes, political platform and activity/financial plan. it is also a place for the annual meeting of all network members to bring together all the perspectives. 








            regional work
          









we work in three regions: balkans, south caucasus and centre-east europe, facilitating enhanced exchange between activists from the specific region and focusing more on its particular issues. we also bring together activists facing similar problems by organising cross-regional meetings, eg. for the political parties' youth wings, activists from restrictive regimes.



















eastern european perspective



we bring eastern european perspective in the european (young) green and progressive movement by providing space to eastern european activists to talk about their issues and using our platform to advocate for them. 








            digital events
          









our digital events are the space for spread the information and discuss the political developments in eastern europe. our most notably series is electionvision, where we talked about the recent elections in our region. 








            statements
          









statements are the official political positions issued by executive committee advocating for the progressive agenda in the context of recent political developments.
