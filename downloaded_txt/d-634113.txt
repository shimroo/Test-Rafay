about us

since its humble beginnings in may'95, red eagle shipping agencies pvt. ltd. also
                                known
                                as resa, has emerged as a leading provider of freight forwarding services. with its
                                headquarters in new delhi, resa has established its own offices in major indian cities
                                and
                                boasts a vast global network of offices and professional agents. our mission is to
                                simplify your
                                shipping experience by offering a range of services, including international freight
                                forwarding,
                                consulting, and break bulk services.
