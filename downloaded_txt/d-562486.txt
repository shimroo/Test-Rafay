classy option gifts
at classy option, personalized gifts are our specialty.  we always consult with our customers to find the best gifts.  we can put together some very unique arrangements, or we can find a gift to suit your particular needs.  
	      we have experience in shopping for and arranging gifts for customers, employees, visitors, and vip's.  we can also put together special arrangements for birthdays, weddings, and other special occasions such as valentine's day or mother's day.  let us help you with your gift giving needs.  check out samples of our gift baskets by visiting our pictures page.
classy option vintage collectibles
we have a great collection of vintage clothing, china, jewelry, pottery, glass and other special items for sale.  we have over 1000 items on our site at this time, and we will be adding more.  look for vintage jewelry designer names such as sherman, coro, kramer, hattie carnegie, and silver jewelry such as sterling charm bracelets and vintage mexico silver.  we also have some gorgeous and luscious vintage lingerie available.  we also have a great many pieces of china on our pages and many more to come, including many gorgeous and very special tea cups and saucers.  also, we have murano and chalet glass, and  pottery including roseville, hull, and more!
