making staten island a better place to live, work, and shop. we are staten island’s oldest and most respected business organization, and provide a suite of services for businesses of all sizes. 




    join us today
  














founded in 1895, the staten island chamber of commerce has served businesses on staten island for more than 125 years. our mission is to improve the economic climate and expand business opportunities in staten island.
