aarp smart driver course: march 6 & 7 

the roselle police department, in cooperation with the american association of retired persons (aarp) is hosting a two-day smart driver course. the cost is $20 for aarp members or $25 for non-members.		learn more and register

the rsca polish committee will award a $1,000 scholarship to one graduating senior from lake park high school. email your application and essay by monday, april 21 to linda mcdermott at lmcdermott47@msn.com. contact roselle sister cities for details.		download application
