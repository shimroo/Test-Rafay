discover the beauty of opera at opera in the heights! 



welcome to our 2024/25 season! we are excited to present gaetano donizetti's moving opera lucia di lammermoor, rachel portman's the little prince based on the beloved children's book by antoine de saint-exupéry, a co-world premiere of lucidity by laura kaminsky and david cote, and gioacchino rossini's lively l'italiana in algeri. each of these works highlights the journeys of strong women. join us for another memorable year of opera.
