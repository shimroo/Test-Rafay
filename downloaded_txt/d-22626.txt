in this section






 
 
memorial hospital west is located in pembroke pines, fl and offers a range of healthcare services in a patient- and family-centered care environment, including one of the busiest adult emergency care departments in broward county.
we are also home to one of south florida's rapidly growing graduate medical education program.

valet parking





complimentary valet parking is available at the following entrances. keys will only be released to those individuals with a valid valet tag.

main hospital

daily: 6 am – 10 pm (after 10 pm, keys will be left with security at the main lobby)

medical office building

monday–friday: 8 am – 5:30 pm (after 5:30 pm, keys will be left with security at the main lobby). closed on holidays.

emergency room

daily: 7 am – 11:30 pm (after 11:30 pm, keys will be left with security at the main lobby)

imaging services

monday–friday: 6 am – 9 pm (after 9 pm, keys will be left with security at the main lobby). closed on weekends and holidays. 






visitor parking





our visitor parking garage is free and available 24/7. it can be accessed from the flamingo road hospital entrance or nw 4th street. if you are visiting the emergency department, parking is available in the parking lot across from the emergency department entrance.
