organizationthe lafayette police department is divided into four divisions, each one being responsible for different duties. the divisions include: patrol, investigations, special operations, and administrative services. 



 
 









































						1

						swat 








  
 




specialty unitsthe lafayette police department prides itself in being a "full service" police agency. many officers work in what are called "specialty units" and have all received advanced training in their specialized area. specialty units include: swat, mobile field force, crime scene investigation, crisis intervention, honor guard, k-9 unit, and so much more!

community outreachthe mission of lpd's community outreach programs is to bring the police department and the community together to facilitate a better understanding of what the department does through the organizing of events.

the lafayette police training center is the host site for many of the trainings conducted for lafayette pd and nation-wide agencies. the state of the art facility includes a crossfit gym, physical tactics room, firearms simulator, and fully-equipped classrooms.
