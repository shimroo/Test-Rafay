commerce.connection.community.

 

the chamber is here for you! whether you’re a neighborhood business owner or a northcenter resident, we exist to help grow your business and strengthen our community.
























visit our neighborhood.
there’s a little something for everyone in the northcenter. besides diverse dining, lively bars, and unique shops, the chamber also hosts a packed calendar of family-friendly events to connect you with the community.



events






 











join the chamber.
active participation in your local chamber is a great way to make valuable business contacts, get to know your neighbors, and learn about the happenings in northcenter.



become a member



















"joining the chamber is one of the best ways to get your business in front of the community. if you become involved, you're going to get a lot out of it, no matter what your business is".— bill abplanalp, neighborhood boys & girls club
