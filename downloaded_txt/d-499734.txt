office of financial assistance














a villanova university degree is among the best investments you'll ever make. the office of financial assistance is here to offer guidance as students and their families manage the financial aid process and make informed decisions about their educational investment. empowering your educational journey at villanova, we aim to support you, fostering your ability to succeed and thrive, serve, and ignite change.

scholarships and grants



in general, scholarships are given based on merit that is defined by the scholarship program, and grants are given based on demonstrated financial need. a student is considered for federal, state and villanova need-based grants simply by applying for financial assistance from villanova university. some merit-based scholarships require an application process while others do not. in the spectrum of educational funding, scholarships and grants are the "free money,” which do not need to be repaid.
