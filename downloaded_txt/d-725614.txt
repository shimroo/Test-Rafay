courier express was founded in 1985 in atlanta, georgia, with a focus on providing timely, dependable courier service. in july 1990, william "bill" messerly acquired ownership. bill and his sons, jack and jim, quickly established a clear, unified vision for the company: to provide superior service at a fair market price. that simple formula, along with a carefully planned expansion and the ability to build on each success, has spurred steady growth. today, courier express is one of the largest courier companies in the nation.
