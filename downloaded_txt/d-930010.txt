in a splendid historic building from the 1500s, the ancient biblioteca del littorio, amidst the suggestive atmospheres of the ancient via del corso, hotel regno is born in the heart of rome. every day we strive to guarantee that our guests enjoy an exclusive stay, creating unique experiences for unforgettable memories. benvenuti a casa.













                    hotel regno                



hotel regno encapsulates two fundamental pillars: the exclusive italian style and the city's impressive layers of history.the pleasant, elegant and tasteful design of its spaces combine the most modern hospitality technologies with zero environmental impact. from balconies and terraces overlooking the picturesque rooftops of rome to the penthouse suite, the building is a virtuous example of a sixteenth-century palace redesigned as a modern, prestigious hotel.
