our mission is to nurture community connections by providing opportunities for creative expression, educational programs, and events in a historic setting. cornerstone center for the arts is more than an exciting blend of arts programming for anyone interested in his or her well-being. we are also known for hosting community events, performances, proms, and wedding receptions. 



cornerstone, where art and community come together.

supporting arts education and community.










































arts education
cornerstone center for the arts offers arts programming in a variety of areas, including dance, visual arts, music, theatre, fitness, workshops, camps, and much more! whether you already have some experience or are looking to learn a new skill, you are sure to find exactly what you're looking for here at cornerstone.















event venue rentals
our historic location offers several beautiful venues for any kind of event, from wedding ceremonies and receptions to birthday parties and fundraisers with up to 450 guests. cornerstone center for the arts is the perfect place to celebrate all of your events!
