measurement & control solutions for operational excellence

 



 instrumetrics engineering is a leading israeli company within the field of industrial automation process instrumentation & control. we provide solutions for sensing, control, and monitoring of pressure, temperature, level, flow, vibration, gas & liquid analyzers for most common process control environments. we supply test equipment & calibrators, sensors & transmitters, signal conditioners & indicators, data acquisition, and loggers, for the control of process gases and fluids.
