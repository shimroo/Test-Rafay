 + about us


tennessee’s premier powersports dealer


authorized honda and polaris powersport dealership


if you’re looking for the biggest selection of atvs, dirt bikes, motorbikes, and more, then you’ve come to the right place. interstate 69 motorsports is proud to be an authorized honda and polaris®  powersports dealership in union city, tn. we’ve got a wide selection of new  and pre-owned  models on almost eight acres of land. whether you want the newest scooter or utility vehicle on the lot or are in the market for a used motorcycle or street bike, we can help you find your dream ride.




maintenance and service specialists


at interstate 69 motorsports, our service department  is second to none. we’re here to ensure your atv,  motorcycle/scooter,  or utv  runs at top performance. our factory-certified and trained master technicians diagnose and repair any issues on the spot. from winterizing your vehicle to suspension tuning, we can do it all. our parts department will help you find those sought-after honda and polaris parts as well. if we don’t have it in stock, we’ll quickly order it for you. 




the benefits of shopping at interstate 69 motorsports


the best thing about shopping with interstate 69 motorsports is that we treat you like family. people come from all over to purchase their rides from us because we have the best deals, the greatest customer service, and a vast selection of vehicles in stock. unlike some powersports businesses, we never charge fees for crate setups or doc fees. you’ll enjoy browsing our inventory in a no-pressure atmosphere. if you have questions about one of our vehicles, our experts will happily answer them. 




huge selection of powersports 


from hondas to harleys, we have one of the largest selections in tennessee. our experienced sales team will work with you to find the perfect vehicle for your needs. we live and breathe powersports vehicles, so you can trust you’re in good hands with us. we’ll understand the differences between the excitement of a recreational utv, the adrenaline of a street motorcycle, and the performance aspect of a side-by-side.  




contact interstate 69 motorsports today


our vast selection of new and pre-owned powersports vehicles means you can find something perfect for everyone in the family. we’re excited to provide the highest quality of service to memphis, nashville, paducah, jackson, dyersburg, and surrounding areas. best of all, you become family once you walk through our doors. long after you purchase your favorite new atv or motorbike, we’ll keep in contact to ensure you’re happy and everything is running smoothly. contact interstate 69 motorsports  today or stop by our showroom to see the latest and greatest vehicles.
