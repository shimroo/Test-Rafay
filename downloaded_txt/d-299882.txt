get started























parker buildings creates custom designed plans and kits for building pole barns, out buildings, barns, shops, garages, arenas, rv storage, warehouses, mini storage buildings and accessory buildings based on your individual needs 

























 









pole structures 



we provide quality materials for pole barn building structures, whether it is for standard buildings or custom type buildings. these buildings can be used for different purposes, from agricultural, residential, commercial or larger type buildings that can be used as a warehouse. 













 









post frame buildings 



post frame buildings continue to be economical compared to other types of construction. this alternative is allowing our customers to build their dream building, and in most instances, allowing them to have a larger building. 













 









do-it-yourself 



we provide buildings to the do-it-yourself market, by providing material and construction plans to help during the construction process. the material can be delivered to the building site or the customer can choose to pick up their material from our facility.
