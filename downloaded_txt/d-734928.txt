dreamteammoneycomment:  here is something that i didn't expect to find in hyip and i applaud their ingenuity!  definitely an original concept -- payday loans and/or invest.
you can apply for a loan and if approved receive the funds to liberty reserve.  if you are an investor your funds are being used for the loans.  if anyone is planning or interested in taking out  a loan, i suggest that they read the terms closely -- i believe liberty-loan may be an intermediary and therefore your loan may actually be offered by someone other than liberty-loan.  so, if liberty-loan should cease to exist you still may have a loan which would still need to be legally repaid.  note that you must be a us citizen to get the loans through them.   food for thought:   if the loans are actually offered by a 3rd party are our funds actually being used to make the loans?  now you see why i would still treat it like any other hyip -- even though the concept is certainly refreshing and quite plausible.
