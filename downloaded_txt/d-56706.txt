developing lifelong excellencefounded in 1988, carolina crown, inc is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit youth performing arts organization based out of fort mill, sc. annually, we directly impact thousands of young people through two active programs and three live events, fostering music education, performance opportunity, and leadership development.

less money. more music.the one-stop-shop for all your marching band needs: we sell winds, percussion, guard, and field gear at competitive prices. we offer new products as well as used ones. don’t forget to pick up your crown drum corps gear!

oct 18, 2024





bandbeat 2024: clayton hs and d.w. daniel hs crowned grand champions



oct 18, 2024




on saturday, october 5, bands from across the carolinas competed at the annual bandbeat marching band championships hosted by carolina crown and the catawba ridge hs band boosters. clayton hs claimed the 2024 silver division grand championship (1a & 2a), while d.w. daniel hs secured the gold division grand championship (3a, 4a, & 5a). these awards were given to the highest scoring band in each division, regardless of class.

crown welcomes clif walker & joe hobbs to creative team



sep 3, 2024




carolina crown is excited to welcome clif walker and joe hobbs to teamcrown! they will join reid atkinson, michael klesch, jeff sacktig, and other members of the creative team and will focus on percussion design and orchestration for the upcoming season. clif and joe are accomplished musicians and educators who bring a wealth of experience to the team from across the marching arts.
