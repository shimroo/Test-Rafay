our mission / about us


supporting minnesota's hop grower community.


formed in march 2013, the minnesota hop growers association (mhga) was established by growers, researchers, and other persons interested in minnesota hop production.  

mhga is operated exclusively for charitable, scientific, and educational purposes, as defined in section 501(c)(5) of the internal revenue code.
