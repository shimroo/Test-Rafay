downtown refresh community survey 


a survey for the “downtown refresh” initiative will open from february 7 to march 7, 2025. the results will be compiled and presented at the “downtown refresh town hall meeting” on tuesday, march 11, 2025, in city council chambers from 5-7 pm.						read on...







ei ceramics breaks ground on $18 million renovation in middletown 


ei ceramics broke ground in january on its renovation of the former ak steel research facility						read on...







middletown releases winter 2024/25 middletown now newsletter 


middletown communications has released the winter 2024/25 middletown now newsletter, as well as a new survey!						read on...







middletown now newsletter survey 


middletown has released three issues of the middletown now newsletter and would like to get community feedback. please take some time to review the three newsletters and answer a short eight question survey to help us make them better in the future!						read on...







middletown to install road signs honoring j.d. vance 


middletown public works will be installing road signs honoring middletown native vice president j.d. vance.						read on...







middletown city events announces 2025 events calendar 


middletown city events has announces its 2025 calendar, bringing back many favorites from last year while expanding to reach more people and bring out more fun to middletown!						read on...







middletown city council votes to support student musicians at middletown high school 


the middletown city council votes to support student musicians at middletown high school with a $10,000 donation to the mmac.						read on...







middletown city council elevates ashley combs to acting city manager 


the city of middletown city council is elevating ashley combs to the position of acting city manager, effective january 1, 2025.						read on...







middletown to donate $10,000 to middletown high school band trip to inauguration 


the city of middletown announces that it will donate $10,000 to the middletown high school marching band after the band was invited to perform in the inauguration parade in washington d.c. by one of its alums, vice president-elect j.d. vance.						read on...







lead service line information 


the city of middletown releases additional information and resources regarding the notification of possible lead service lines across middletown.						read on...







‘a very merry middletown’ holiday festivities set to launch nov. 15th, featuring ice skating, lights 


a very merry middletown begins on nov. 15 with the opening of the holiday whopla ice rink.						read on...







ground breaks on new robert "sonny" hill community center project 


a groundbreaking ceremony was held on sept. 9, 2024 for the new renovation and expansion project of the robert "sonny" hill community center						read on...







atrium family ymca, middletown break ground on monsoon bay aquatic center 


atrium family ymca and the city of middletown held a groundbreaking ceremony at the ymca facility for the new monsoon bay aquatic center						read on...







middletown releases fall 2024 edition of middletown now 


the fall 2024 edition of the middletown now newsletter is out now!						read on...
