welcome!
welcome to carolina mountain woodturners (cmw), a non-profit club dedicated to woodturning as both a craft and art form. cmw is an educational chapter of the american association of woodturners aaw. if you are new to cmw, start exploring from the about us menu.  if you are a current member, login or explore the links below.

carolina mountain woodturners is a not-for-profit club with a mission to increase interest in woodturning as a craft and art form. monthly live demonstrations are held at the folk art center on the blue ridge parkway near asheville, north carolina. additionally, members can enroll in hands-on classes and open turning days at our fully equipped turning learning center.
we invite you to visit us at our next monthly demonstration.
