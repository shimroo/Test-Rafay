medical support services online training courses

		cbt direct offers the most effective training to prepare you to become certified in the medical
		support services field as a medical coder, medical biller, or medical transcriptionist.

		cbt directs online, computer-based courses, which are current and the most relevant available, were
		developed in partnership with associated professional organizations. this training incorporates
		cbt directs proven, four-step learning process, that ensures the highest rate of information retention for
		each student: presentation, demonstration, guidance, and independent practice,

		medical support services is one the fastest growing professions in the u.s. according to the
		bureau of labor statistics (bls), a division of the u.s department of labor, more than half of
		the 30 fastest growing jobs through the year 2014, are in health care, including 7 of the top 10!
		as this field expands, it needs more individuals who can accurately document all the technical
		information for doctors, hospitals, clinics, other medical facilities, and, insurance companies.

		with positions available in a variety of medical facilities, and with insurance companies, this
		specialized field offers job security and good salaries. some of these professionals may work
		from their homes. those who earn their certifications are even better prepared for future
		advancement.


		medical support services is based upon the vital process that begins when a patient visits a
		physician. the cycle ends with the archiving of the patients documents.

		many students combine the medical coder with the medical biller training and certification. to be
		an even more well-rounded professional, some combine all three areas of knowledge.
