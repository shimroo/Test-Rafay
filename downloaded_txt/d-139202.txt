transportation news 


sun prairie receives a silver bicycle friendly community award, metro transit ridership data is available, and opportunities to provide public comment on bike wayfinding plan and greater madison mpo rtp and tip amendments.						read on...







*cares program expands service to sun prairie* 


the city of sun prairie is excited to announce the expansion of services from the madison fire department’s community alternative response emergency services (cares) program to the community, beginning monday, february 3. click the graphic for more info!						read on...







sustainable technology series 


as part of the city's earth month 2025, the "neighborhood knowledge" series will highlight local adoption of sustainable technologies. we’re seeking residents using solar panels, heat pumps, evs, or similar solutions to share their experiences.			additional info...
