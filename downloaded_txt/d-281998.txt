chief's message


the cobb county police department is a professional law enforcement organization that strives for the highest standard of excellence. the men and women of this department recognize the inherent worth of all people while also being alert to issues that may impact our communities. our actions and relationship with the community are guided by an internal sense of honesty and accountability. we work daily to show honor, integrity, courage, commitment, respect and professionalism to all people.
