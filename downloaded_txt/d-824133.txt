ways to give


share this page













giving


                        your gift provides hope to adults and children with cancer here and around the world.
                        
                    






memorial and tribute gifts


there are many meaningful ways to honor or remember your loved one and support dana-farber and the jimmy fund's mission to conquer cancer. 









naming and funding opportunities


                        make a gift that links your name, or that of a friend, colleague, or loved one, with the compassionate care and cutting-edge research at dana-farber. naming and funding opportunities are also available for corporations and foundations.
                        
                    






corporate engagement


                        learn how your company can build brand awareness while supporting cancer care and research.
                        
                    








foundation giving


                        public and private foundation support is critical to dana-farber’s 
lifesaving mission. it provides seed funding for high-risk/high-reward 
research, ensures that innovative patient care programs are sustained, 
and furthers educational outreach efforts here and around the world.
                        
                    






bequests and planned gifts


                        leave a lasting legacy to dana-farber and the jimmy fund with a planned
 gift tailored to fit your financial goals while helping cancer patients
 worldwide.
                        
                    








shop


                        show your support by purchasing items such as greeting cards, party favors, and unique symbolic gifts from dana-farber and the jimmy fund. they make great gifts for friends and family.
                        
                    






giving day


on may 22, we celebrated dana-farber cancer institute and the jimmy fund’s annual giving day. this special day was dedicated to bringing our community together to support our mission to defy cancer.









cars


                        donate your vehicle (car, truck, boat, plane, and beyond) to dana-farber cancer institute and the jimmy fund through cars (charitable adult rides & services), our vehicle donation program provider.
