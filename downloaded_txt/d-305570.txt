welcome to clearwater lake!
this website is your gateway to clearwater lake and the 
surrounding areas. the lake association and members are 
dedicated to advancing tourism and promoting educational 
and social activities at clearwater lake and the surrounding 
areas.  anyone who has enjoyed a day at the lake, 
participated in some of the activities around the lake, or 
dropped a fishing line in the water knows how special this 
place can be.
