 maps/gis















click the map above to learn more about your community and services around your property. enter your address into the bensenville community portal search box to find the following:    property summary    government representatives    zoning informationwhat is gis? geographic information systems (gis) allows users to access all types of data on one map 
 






 

 construction















typical projects include major road resurfacing, parking lot improvements, sewer and water line replacements, and the ongoing upkeep and replacement of curbs and sidewalks.gis / construction mapsthe village utilizes many avenues to keep citizens aware of construction projects. as a given project start date nears, residents who live in areas most affected by a particular project receive a written alert, including start times and estimated completion dates, and a telephone number to call for the latest information. more construction information.
