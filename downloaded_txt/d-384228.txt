elvis in memphis















“elvis in memphis,” a poignant love song that marks a significant milestone in pat's storied career. this track is a testament to green’s enduring influence in the genre and his ability to evolve artistically. the song, written in collaboration with grammy-winning songwriter brett james and featuring haunting background vocals by walt wilkins, is poised to become a new classic in the realm of texas country music love songs. the single, “elvis in memphis,” produced by sean giddings, tells a captivating story of love, set against the backdrop of the iconic city that elvis presley called home. it’s a song that transcends time, blending traditional country music elements with modern storytelling. the song emerges from a powerhouse collaboration between pat green, brett james, and walt wilkins, three luminaries of the country music world. brett james, known for penning hits for some of the biggest names in music, brings his masterful songwriting to the table, while walt wilkins’ background vocals add a layer of depth and soul to the track, enriching the listener's experience. the collaboration between these three artists has resulted in a track that is both nostalgic and fresh, appealing to long-time fans of country music and newcomers alike.
