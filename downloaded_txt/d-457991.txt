the ontario regiment rcac museum operates the largest collection of operational historic military vehicles in north america.we are looking for volunteers; to drive, maintain and restore vehicles in our collection.if you have an interest in the living history of armoured fighting vehicles this is the place to be.for more information visit our website ontrmuseum.ca or complete an online application to become a museum volunteer at membership@ontrmuseum.ca
