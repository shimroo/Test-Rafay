dupage county stormwater management accepting input on water quality program 


dupage county stormwater management is seeking public input on its countywide water quality program from february 5 through february 19, 2025.						read on...






important notice: utility billing changes now live 


the village of villa park has transitioned to a new payment system, bs&a, replacing xpress bill pay. bs&a allows you to view bills, make payments, manage autopay, and sign up for paperless billing.						read on...






public comments open for comprehensive plan 


we are developing a new comprehensive plan that will act as a roadmap for growth and development over the next 20 years. your input is vital in shaping this plan—join us and share your vision! public comments are open now through march 14.						read on...
