mountain heritage day






mountain heritage day concert rescheduled for march 23, 2025
  
western carolina university is excited to announce the rescheduled mountain heritage
                  day 50th anniversary evening concert, now set for sunday, march 23, at the liston
                  b. ramsey regional activity center. doors will open at 4 p.m.
  
this special event will feature performances by renowned bluegrass bands old crow
                  medicine show, lindsay lou, and sister sadie. originally slated for both daytime and
                  evening slots, these talented artists will unite for one unforgettable concert, celebrating
                  the rich mountain heritage and the resilience of western north carolina in the aftermath
                  of hurricane helene.
  
the concert is free and open to the public, with no tickets required.
for those who reserved tickets for the september concert that was canceled due to
                  the storm, please note that those tickets are no longer needed.
  
** only clear bags will be permitted in the ramsey center for this event
  
mountain heritage day 2025: information regarding vendor applications, sponsorships
                  and more will be announced in the coming weeks. visit our website and facebook page
                  for all updates!
we are excited to see everyone in september!
