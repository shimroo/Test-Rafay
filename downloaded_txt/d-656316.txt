we are trinity farms

 

ours is a family operation just like so many of yours. currently we have 3 generations that plan and work side by side on a daily basis. although the cattle are our true passion, we also raise high quality kittitas valley timothy and alfalfa hay that is exported throughout the pacific rim. today with mike and paulette at the helm, robb and debbie and their 4 grown children also share in the experience that is trinity farms. our goal is to build a sustainable family business that will continue to provide the superior product and experience for which we have come to be known.
