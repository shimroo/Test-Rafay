residential fire in twin falls county claims one life 


at 8:54 a.m. on wednesday, jan. 29 the city of twin falls emergency communications center received a call from a twin falls county sheriff deputy reporting a house fire on ford place in twin falls county.						read on...







ride tft adds new payment methods to ride service 


starting saturday, feb. 1, ride tft passengers will be able to pay for rides with cash through the ride tft app, and passengers will no longer be required to call the dispatch phone number for scheduling.						read on...







city of twin falls winter road maintenance and how citizens can help 


with snow beginning to fall, the city of twin falls would like to remind citizens about its winter road maintenance program and to ask for the public’s help to ensure that crews can safely clear streets when significant snow arrives.						read on...







shoshone falls park & dierkes lake park closed for road construction 


shoshone falls grade at the intersections with the dierkes lake access road, as well as access to shoshone falls park, will be closed beginning friday, oct. 4 in preparation for reconstructing sections of the roadway.						read on...







stay safe and connected: update your cell phone service address and e911 addresses today!

twin falls urban renewal agency seeks applicant for board 


the urban renewal agency of the city of twin falls is seeking applications for one upcoming vacancy on the board of commissioners. residents living in the city of twin falls are encouraged to apply.						read on...







recreational vehicle septic dump facility closed for winter season 


the city of twin falls parks and recreation department has closed and winterized the recreational vehicle septic dump for the winter season. the rv dump site will remain closed for the winter season and reopen in april.						read on...







twin falls police serve search warrants at 3 massage parlors 


on jan. 7, twin falls police officers, with assistance from the twin falls county sheriff’s office, jerome county sheriff’s office and idaho state police, executed simultaneous search warrants at 3 massage parlors in the city of twin falls.						read on...
