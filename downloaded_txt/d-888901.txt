welcome to kidsgames





kidsgames sports
kidsgames sports (formerly known as lp sports)  offers children ages 4 through 6th grade the opportunity to participate in recreational sports leagues in a  fun environment.  a partnership with local churches and communities, the main purpose of kidsgames sports is to utilize sports as a tool to share the gospel of christ with players and their families as well as to encourage all participants to take the next step on their spiritual journey. 
learn more
























kidsgames camp
often called the best kid-thing on earth, kidsgames camp is a 4-day event for every kid, aged 4-12 (as of 9/1/24), to have the best time of their summer.  offering both sport and non-sport electives or activities, kidsgames kids learn, laugh, and have a blast.  check out our video for a glimpse into a day of kidsgames camp.  you won't want to miss out!!
