radnet northern californiaat radnet, our mission is to provide exceptional radiology services that exceed the expectations of patients and referring physicians. we offer some of the best imaging services available in northern california with sites conveniently located through contra costa county, silicon valley and the east bay.we are proud to serve the communities of northern california and thank you for allowing us to become one of the region’s leading provider of medical imaging services.our board-certified radiologists have sub-specialty training and knowledge required to interpret 3t mri, ct, 1.5t mri, mammography, pet/ct, x-ray, ultrasound and other medical imaging studies. our experience helps referring physicians make diagnoses necessary to provide appropriate and timely medical treatment.
