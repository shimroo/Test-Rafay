knr arabians is a small privately-owned arabian breeding farm located northwest of hampton, iowa.    owners kelly and russ reynolds have been breeding arabian and half-arabian horses for over 20 years.   they currently own the multi-champion stallion, bey shahn.



the farm has grown over the years to include llamas, miniature donkeys, miniature horses, a miniature mule, and much more.    we also feature the new black and tan "euro" jack russell " hunt terrier puppies and have many pics of them and their adventures.
