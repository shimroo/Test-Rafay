at the international institute of metro detroit we are:



dedicated to transforming the lives of low-income immigrants, refugees and u.s. born residents through innovative solutions to advancing economic mobility.
















check out our latest project:warrendale community integration project. in 2018, iimd and its partner the islamic center of detroit initiated an inclusive community development project, the warrendale community integration project [wcip]. the project is part of the the kresge foundation’s kip: d initiative. iimd is very grateful for the partnership with the kresge foundation and the partnership with the warrendale community. you can access the full report through the link below.
