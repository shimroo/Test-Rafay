about caribee 



welcome to caribee boat sales & marina. 





best boats + best people + best service = the ultimate boating experience
let’s get you out on the water . . . chasing adventure, finding peace, and making joy-filled memories here in paradise.




best location.




conveniently located at 81500 overseas highway in the heart of the florida keys on islamorada island. our location has earned the florida epa clean marina and clean marine retailer designations, making caribee one of the most environmentally responsible marine operations in south florida.




best storage.




our 7-days-a-week storage operation is outfitted with new racks, the latest forklifts, and experienced operators to assure that your boat is safe, secure, and ready to go when you are.




best ship store.




we are stocked to supply both the everyday and less common needs of boaters and fishermen here in the keys. if we don’t have it, we will get it promptly, usually overnight.
