saint john of the cross catholic schoolst. john of the cross catholic school represents an educational ministry of st. john of the cross catholic parish. the school is affiliated with the roman catholic diocese of san diego, california.st. john of the cross catholic school is a comprehensive preschool, elementary, and middle school accredited by the western catholic education association.

why st. john of the cross?













st. john of the cross catholic school is an award winning school that prides itself on it's catholic identity. our school has an excellent academic curriculum and offers a wide variety of on-campus activities and sports teams. we believe in providing a safe, nurturing, and faith-filled environment for every student, teacher, and parent.



tour of the school













we are located at 8175 lemon grove way in lemon grove, ca. please contact our school office during business hours to schedule a tour of our school. our school office is open monday - thursday from 7:30 a.m. to 3:30 p.m. and fridays from 7:30 a.m. to 2:30 p.m. (the first friday of the month until 12:30 p.m.). you can reach us via phone at 619-466-8624 or email our school secretary at mguerrero@stjohncross.org.
