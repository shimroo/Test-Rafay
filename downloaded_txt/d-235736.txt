about us
specialists in the making of facsimiles of the most beautiful, rare and difficult-to-reproduce works. established in 1980, facsimile editions’ very first facsimile was one of the largest and most complex ever published. in the ensuing years we have continuously pushed the boundaries in terms of detail, materials, authenticity and intricacy.
about usfacsimile creation spurs the development of innovative new processes and materials. our unique paper is the closest to real parchment ever made. we pioneered the reproduction of raised gold illumination without embossing, were the first to reproduce a scribe’s pricking and our bindings reflect an extraordinary level of realism. every facsimile is the product of research, development and intense attention to detail.

facsimiles
leading museums, libraries and private collectors commission work from us. whether a single copy of a 13th century manuscript, tiny fragments of papyrus, a scroll on copper, or an intricate contemporary manuscript with a thousand-piece marquetry binding, all our projects are completed with the same dedication to excellence.
commissioned projectsfacsimile editions creates custom-made, exhibition-quality facsimiles for museums and libraries, allowing them to display rare manuscripts without risking the originals. meticulously replicating every detail, including gilding and binding, these facsimiles offer an authentic experience while significantly reducing costs and logistical challenges for safe, affordable exhibitions.
