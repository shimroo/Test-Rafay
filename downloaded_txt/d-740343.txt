about hebrewletters sl


                                                
                        when i began drawing the hebrew letters, i fell in love with them.  the lettersbegan appearing in my meditations.  the baal shem tov teaches that eachletter is a room of god.  indeed, each letter is a room, a room full of lifeand light - wisdom and wonder.  each letter is alive and has its own personality.the aleph gives me hugs.  i love to sit in the beit when i meditate.  the hey provides me with a path and the vav is full of humor and humility.  theseletters are thousands of years old and have an energy all their own.  an oldkabbalistic text, sepher yitzerah, states that the letters are mystical paths, or paths of wonder.  each letter speaks differently to each of us.  choosethe letter that vibrates for you.  take it for your own.  draw it.  meditate on it.
