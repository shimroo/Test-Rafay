welcome to chatlee boats
family owned and operated in sanford, north carolina since 1967, chatlee boat & marine is the south's premier boat dealer. our success and reputation were built on our customers' satisfaction. as our mission states, we treat every customer as we would expect to be treated, acting with dignity, trust and integrity.

robbie & jeff yow are the owners of chatlee boat & marine. the yow family has owned and operated chatlee since 1967 in sanford, nc. even with 45 employees working under them, these two brothers are on site 6 days a week to make sure operations run smooth.

chatlee boats provides superior customer service and has an entire team that consists of professional sales members, finance experts, service technicians and a knowledgeable parts and accessories staff to support you, our valued customer. we pride ourselves on having well-trained experts that strive for customer satisfaction. come in and visit our friendly, reliable and experienced staff.
