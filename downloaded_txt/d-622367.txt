fpi member product highlight: @ecoproducts  is expanding its innovative new product line created to make it easier for consumers, composters and the entire foodservice value chain to differentiate between compostable and non-compostable products. the new veridiantm line now features new compostable cups, lids and cutlery. veridian, which already included a variety of compostable clamshells, cups, lids, cutlery, bowls and containers, is designed to help customers meet new requirements in washington and colorado. these laws require certain compostable products to be labeled in ways that make them “readily and easily identifiable” as compostable. learn more about these and other innovative products at www.ecoproducts.com. #greatpackaging
