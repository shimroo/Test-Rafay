90 years serving indianawith over 90 years of experience in the field, lee company inc. offers equipment and school furniture. from bleachers, cabinetry, lockers, visual displays, auditorium seating, to media centers and more, lee company works with high-quality vendors to bring you the best options for your k-12 and higher education, sports facilities, office, municipality, manufacturing facility, or medical office needs. our territory managers, designers, and project managers will share their expertise to bring the best design and look for your space.














products and solutions



customized for your needs















athletic equipmentas experts in the field, lee company inc. is prepared to assist you with the updating and customization of your gymnasium. our talented team can complete the outfitting of your gymnasium with basketball and volleyball systems, custom wall pads, player seats, scorer’s tables, divider curtains, lockers, entry mats and more.















student furniturethe furniture in your school is where your students do everything, so make sure it’s the very best! offering everything from standard desks and chairs to media centers and activity tables, we offer products through our vendors that will suit the needs of any school, student and teacher.















indoor/outdoor bleachersdoes your spectator seating leave something to be desired? give us a call for assistance in elevating your school team’s fan experience with updated seating solutions for all indoor/outdoor sports like basketball, baseball, softball, football, soccer and track. we offer stationary and portable options to make sure no matter your event, your fans are comfortable.















lockersclassic school lockers are an asset you can keep in your school for decades. whether you are looking to install in your hallways, athletic or team rooms, we have multiple locker solutions for your hallway and athletic locker needs. our lockers are easy to maintain and are available in custom sizes, styles, security features, and finishes.
