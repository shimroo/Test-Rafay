scottish society of artists




we are the society of scottish artists.




we’re the largest artist-led organisation in scotland. we lead a diverse and ambitious programme and invite artists based anywhere in the world to join us.


join today






open call | ssa engramme artist exchange 2025




the society of scottish artists is delighted to be offering an exciting international exhibition opportunity in collaboration with engramme.
ssa members based in scotland working in printmaking are invited to apply for this prestigious award of a solo exhibition in quebec, canada which will take place in winter 2025/2026.
application opens: friday 14 february 2025
deadline: thursday 20 march 2025, 9am
 
image: cynthia dinan-mitchell, flora resiliente, 2024. engramme, quebec, canada. photo: marion gotti

the land | ssa at the dick institute, kilmarnock




the ssa is pleased to be collaborating with the dick institute, kilmarnock to present an exhibition titled ‘the land’. the exhibition includes selected works from over 40 ssa members, which will be displayed alongside work from invited artist calum wallis.
the exhibition will take place at the dick institute from saturday 15th march – 7 june 2025.
 
 
image: calum wallis, ‘place to scramble’, courtesy of the artist.
