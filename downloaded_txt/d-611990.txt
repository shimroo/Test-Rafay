meet osq’s artistic director & conductor, dr. jin byun                                                                   



jin byun, dma is an esteemed figure in the music community, currently serving as the artistic director and conductor of the oratorio society of queens (osq). with a deep passion for choral music, dr. byun brings extensive experience as a conductor, educator, and professional singer to his role with the osq.said dr. byun, “i’m honored and humbled to join the oratorio society of queens as its artistic direct or and conductor. i look forward to collaborating with the talented members of the choir, creating transformative musical experiences, and contributing tothe vibrant arts scene in queens.” (read more)

























everyone in our chorus has a voice that is heard













osq is a community chorus. osq’s doors are open to everyone who wants to join in the joy and congeniality of singing choral masterworks.  our mission is to engage, educate and uplift the queens community by presenting musical programs that span the repertoire from great choral masterpieces to popular all-time favorites.   (to learn more about osq, click here)
