about us
what is cub scouts? what do cub scouts do? and what is the mission of the program? the answer is actually pretty simple. cub scouts is a program within the boy scouts of america that serves youth from 6 – 11 years old (with some locations piloting a program for 5 year old’s call lions). the cub scouts program aims to prepare youth to grow into self-reliant and dependable adults.
