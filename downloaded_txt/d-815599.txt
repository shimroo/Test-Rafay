welcome to



pine meadows 















golf complex














nestled in northern lebanon county, pennsylvania, pine meadows seamlessly integrates with the picturesque landscape and rolling farmland. from the lush greens to the strategically placed hazards, each round promises a memorable experience. the natural setting among wetlands, bent grass fairways, and kentucky blue grass roughs creates a stunning and serene environment and the challenging contours add an exciting element for golfers looking to test their skills.the addition of paved cart paths and spectacular bridges over the swatara creek provides not only convenience but also a memorable experience as players traverse the course. golfers appreciate the thoughtful design and beauty of the course.


























escape, explore, and enjoy



your day will brighten the moment you turn off of route 343 on to pine meadow road and the outside world melts away as you take in the majesty of pine meadows.  enjoy wonderful meals and cold drinks with your family and friends at the wetlands restaurant & lounge on the expansive deck and patio.host your golf outing at pine meadows golf complex, or join our exciting weekly team matches to connect with friends and fellow golf enthusiasts throughout the year and enjoy the camaraderie of the game.
