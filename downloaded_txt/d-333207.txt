esmeralda resort
an exceptional setting for an exceptional experience
experience the intimate and private ambiance of orient bay. wonderfully located on one of the most beautiful beaches of saint martin, the hotel esmeralda resort **** is a true haven of peace.
opened in 1991, the hotel esmeralda has, over the years, retained its soul, its authenticity, and its sense of hospitality, so appreciated by its guests, while combining comfort and well-being.


nestled in an exquisite tropical garden situated on 3 hectares of land where bougainvillea, hibiscus and coconut palms flourish in abundance, the hotel’s 46 rooms and suites are spread across 14 luxurious villas. each has its own private swimming pool, so you can enjoy plenty of refreshing relaxation. the colonial-inspired architecture and the well-chosen hues add considerably to the charm of the place. the ocean is only a few steps away and nature is at your fingertips...

rooms
the hotel esmeralda resort offers 50 rooms and suites spread across our 14 colourful villas, decorated in tropical shades of turquoise, yellow, pink and lavender.
 
each of our villas has 3 or 4 ground floor bedrooms and/or suites and private, shaded terraces surrounded by lush greenery and richly hued blooms, with direct access to the swimming pool.
all our categories of accommodation can be rented individually or are available in several combinations which can be customised as you wish.
