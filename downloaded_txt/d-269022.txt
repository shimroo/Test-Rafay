we’re everywhere you want to be. 
for over 35 years, park marine boat sales & service has been the #1 boat dealer for new & used boats in atlanta and north georgia. with multiple locations throughout kennesaw, gainesville, lake lanier and lake allatoona, we’re conveniently located everywhere you want to be. as the only boat dealer with service locations on both lake lanier (at port royale marina) and lake allatoona (at park marina and victoria harbour), we have the unique ability to provide service where it matters most – on the water.

a family owned and operated business for over 40 years, we’ve built relationships with generations of boaters and adventurers who share our passion and enthusiasm for being out on the water. we treat each customer, each sale, and each relationship with the highest level of attention, taking great pride in the friendships and loyal customers we have been blessed to earn and maintain. we’re able to do this because of our great team – we would not be where we are today without the dedication and talent of our associates – and we believe we attract the best.
as georgia’s number 1 boat dealer for new & used boats, we’re proud to offer: 

the largest and highest quality inventory of new and used boats 
yamaha jet boats (#1 dealer in the southeast for over 5 years), yamaha waverunners, tracker fishing boats, g3 boats, sun tracker pontoons, and crownline 
top of the market trade in values, options for selling your boat, and boat brokerage programs 
flexible and attractive financing options 
new and used trailers, boating accessories, and watersports gear 
knowledgeable sales associates ready to assist you in finding the boat of your dreams 
factory authorized service at any of our service center locations (in kennesaw or gainesville), on lake lanier (at port royale marina), and on lake allatoona (at park marina or victoria harbour marina)
