your portal to the high fashion worldm&i models helps new and aspiring talent through development, local work connections, and placement opportunities with national and international agencies.our mission is to empower the next generation of talent in the professional world while maintaining an inclusive, supportive, & nurturing environment. we pride ourselves in having over 35 years of experience with scouting, developing, and establishing talent to succeed!





our talent is world class.







































 












local agencym&i models operates as the premier talent agency serving the wichita market and its surrounding areas. we specialize in booking talent for photo shoots, tv commercials, runway shows, promotional modeling, and more. our focus is on delivering high-quality, well-developed, and professional talent to meet the diverse needs of our local clientele.some of our local clients include dillard’s, wichita bridal expo, rainbow’s united, eric fisher, spirit aerosystems, meddy’s, visit wichita, tanganyika, kansas children’s service league, ymca, don hattan, healing waters, bose aviation, kansas magazine, lyndon’s & much more!



 

national/international placementm&i models serves as a mother agency, nurturing models and talent with the potential for representation in larger markets. we connect our talent with top agencies worldwide, placing them with some of the most renowned and prestigious agencies in the industry.
