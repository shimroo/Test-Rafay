curbside e-waste 


submit your request online by contact lrs directly. electronics must be curbside by 6:00 am. limit of seven items per request, with a maximum of two tvs or monitors. ensure requests are submitted by 6:00 pm on wednesday, april 16, 2025.						read on...







comprehensive plan 


help shape the future of north riverside! participate in our comprehensive land use project and share your thoughts through the community survey. your input is crucial in guiding the development and growth of our village. click here to get involved!			additional info...







facebook page consolidation 


in our efforts to streamline communication and better serve our community, we will be merging most of our separate department facebook pages into one consolidated page.						read on...







stickers on sale now 


purchase your 2024/25 vehicle sticker today!  new stickers must be displayed on all vehicles by october 1, 2024.  after september 30th a late fee is applied.						read on...







united against hate, cities and villages collaborate to protect our communities in times of strife 


as suburban cities and villages across the region, we stand united behind a core shared value: the unequivocal condemnation of all acts of hate. during these times of strife, we are more committed than ever to safeguarding our communities.						read on...
