about us











jones edmunds is a florida-based multidisciplinary engineering firm providing environmental and infrastructure solutions since 1974. 







our core values of integrity, knowledge, and service are what drive our comprehensive approach. with these values as our foundation, our work will create generations of impact in the communities we serve.
