about your tacoma store 
bassett home furnishings tacoma is conveniently located just off i-5 at exit 137. as tacoma's design destination since 2000, bassett home furnishings makes your custom home design easy, whether updating a condo or furnishing a home. bassett's welcoming showroom offers custom indoor and outdoor furniture and beautiful finishing touches like quality lamps, rugs, and mirrors. relax and explore bassett's handmade american solid maple and oak benchmade furniture collection at your own pace. find inspiration in bassett's modern, mid-modern, casual, and traditional-style room arrangements. our talented design consultants are available to offer expert guidance to bring just the right touch to your south sound home. with over 700 fabrics in our custom design center, you will find something to love. stop in at bassett home furnishings tacoma today to find your own style.










































complimentary design help
whether you know exactly what you want, or you don't know where to begin, our talented design consultants make sure you get exactly what you want. maybe it's just one piece, or a whole room, either way we're here to help. and we'll make sure you enjoy every step along the way.  we'll even come out to your home and help with measurements, space planning, and learning more about your style.
want personal time? book a virtual or in-store appointment. but you can always just drop in.
