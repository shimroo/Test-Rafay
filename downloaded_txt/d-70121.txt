welcome!holy trinity is a place for everyone — prodigal or pious, questioning or convinced, world-weary or well-set — who is curious about christ jesus and his life-changing vision and values.as one of our favorite hymns says, “this is the lord’s house, home of all his people, school for the faithful, refuge for the sinner, rest for the pilgrim, haven for the weary — all find a welcome!”and you will too!

upcoming events















sunday eucharist i
sundays · 8 a.m.main churchjoin in simple, spoken-word worship in traditional english without music.











coffee hour i
sundays · 9 a.m.lillycrop roomjoin your fellow early bird worshipers for some holy trinity hospitality.











sunday school
sundays · 9:15 a.m.parish building · second floorfor your kids, we’ve got godly play with children ages four to five or faith formation with older youngsters.











lectionary study
sundays · 9:15 a.m.parish libraryexplore the bible readings assigned for worship in church on that day.











sunday eucharist ii
sundays · 10:30 a.m.main churchjoin in contemporary english worship with organ, choir, and music.











coffee hour ii
sundays · 12 noonparish hallfollow up your midday worship with snacks, drinks, and friends old and new.











wednesday bible study
wednesdays · 11 a.m.parish librarydelve into midday study of a book of the bible with other seekers.











healing eucharist
wednesdays · 12:15 p.m.main churchjoin in lunch hour prayers for healing in body, mind, and spirit.











centering prayer
thursdays · 12 noonchildren’s chapelfind stillness and spiritual focus in an hour of quiet meditative prayer.











seniors and friends supper
last fridays of the month · 5 p.m.parish hallenjoy food and fellowship with friends old and new.











family game night
last fridays of the month · 6 p.m.parish hallenjoy fun and games for all ages until 9 p.m. during artwalk gainesville.
