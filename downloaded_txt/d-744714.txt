see what’s new…










































monthly business after hours networking mixers
business after hours networking mixers are held on the third thursday of every month at various member businesses. these mixers are a wonderful opportunity to network with fellow business owners and community leaders, make valuable connections, and cultivate a community of like-minded individuals who work, shop, and play in tuolumne county. plus, there’s appetizers, drinks, and a raffle at each mixer! 



upcoming mixers






 











government affairs committee
our government affairs committee meets on the first monday of every month at the chamber office. government affairs committee meetings provide an opportunity for community leaders to come together and discuss what is going on in their respective industries, ask each other questions, and maintain a strong pulse on our community activity. the chamber advocates for it's members and acts as a voice for the business community to provide valuable input to these leaders as to what businesses need, and get answers to your questions.



learn more






 











town hall luncheons
town hall luncheons provide an opportunity for members to enjoy lunch together while hearing from influential community leaders in regards to many topics that have an impact on our business community.
