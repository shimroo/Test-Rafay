when sharyn sowell isn’t making something, she’s planning what will be next. trained as a jewelry artist, sharyn now works in a variety of mediums. known for scissor cut paper, calligraphy, and a variety of other mediums, her work celebrates the  beauty of nature and humanity. “i believe the earth is crammed with miracles,” says sharyn, “and my art is a joyful thank you.”
