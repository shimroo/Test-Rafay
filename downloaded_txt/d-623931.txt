welcome to bob’s bunz…











for many years bob’s bunz offerings have been as much a part of your florida keys experience as basking in the sun or relaxing under a palm tree!
in decades past bob’s bunz featured  over 100 famous items on the regular menu, as well as “daily specials”. 
you could always satisfy your sweet tooth with one of our delectable bakery treats, featuring a wide variety of muffins, cookies, pies and of course our famous cinnamon bunz and sticky bunz… after all, they are the “best bunz in town”!
bob’s bunz offerings have gone thru many expressions over the years. stay tuned for the next chapter of bob’s bunz returning soon to the fabulous florida keys!
