“an incredible phenomenon, rendered with the precision of a mechanic and the vision of a poet.”














richard wawro was one of the most significant savant artists of the 20th century, celebrated for his detailed and vibrant landscape drawings. using the unusual medium of wax oil crayons, he produced striking images characterised by their intense depth and color, earning him widespread acclaim. notable owners of richard wawro originals included lady margaret thatcher and pope john paul ii.
