wednesday, august 15, 2012












2012 audition info

hammerkatz nyu wants you!

·      do you love writing and
performing sketch comedy?

·      do you want to meet new
people? 

·      do you just love bits???



then
you should audition for hammerkatz, nyu’s premiere sketch comedy group!



how do i audition? everyone in hammerkatz
joins as a writer and
performer, so to be eligible you must complete both parts of the audition.



part 1, performance audition

attend
one of our performance
auditions.

·        
thursday,
september 6th at 8:30pm at kimmel 802

·        
friday,
september 7th at 8:30pm at kimmel 802

·        
saturday,
september 8th at 2pm at kimmel 802



part 2, writing sample

email
a writing sample to hammerkatznyu@gmail.com
by 9pm on september 8th! what’s can be in a writing sample? below are some suggestions. you only need to include one of these examples, but include more if you think that will better represent you as a writer and what you think is funny. 

·        
sketches!
one or a few, 1-5 pages each.

·        
a
short/funny play or script! no more than 7 pages.

·        
a
list of ideas for sketches!



other things you should know
about auditions:

·        
performance
auditions can take a while so be prepared to stay a couple of hours. aka bring
a book or sweet convo skills.

·        
we
provide you with scripts at our performance auditions, which you will perform
in groups. no preparation necessary and no memorization required! 

·        
don’t
sweat having a super fancy or extensive writing sample, we just want to see
what you think is funny!

·        
email
hammerkatznyu@gmail.com if you
have any other questions. or check out http://hammerkatznyu.blogspot.com/!



hope to see
you there!



xoxo,

hammerkatz nyu

hammerkatz wants you!  audition information





hammerkatz is looking for new recruits! if you are interested in being part of the sketch comedy army, send a sample sketch* to hammerkatznyu@gmail.com before september 10th, and then come to one of our performance auditions which will be held september on thursday the 8th 7-11 pm, friday the 9th 6:30-11:30 pm, and saturday the 10th 2-6:30 pm!  we can't wait to meet you! *if you've never written a sketch before, then send us a funny scene or short script or even just some ideas for sketches- we just wanna see how you write and what your sense of humor is!
