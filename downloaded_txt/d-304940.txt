welcome to eastern dental
we offer comprehensive dental services including general and cosmetic dentistry, orthodontics, periodontics, endodontics, teeth whitening, oral surgery and many more. we also offer emergency appointments in all offices. our team of dental professionals will be with you every step along the way during your appointment. we provide you with the comfort and support you need to make your appointment as easy as possible.

why eastern dental?
simple, because your smile is our top priority.
eastern dental has been providing new jersey quality care and healthy smiles for over 40 years. we offer personalized and comprehensive treatment based on your own individual needs. we provide full-service dentistry for the whole family including general dentistry, cosmetic dentistry and orthodontics.
best of all eastern dental is an affordable high-quality dental practice. no insurance no problem, we offer a custom program for you.
