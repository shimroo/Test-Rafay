we equip individuals, groups, and congregations with resources and information for the hands-on work of peace rooted in justice in their local contexts. 



we equip…
















    liturgy resources
  



we gather peacemakers from churches, nonprofits, ministry partners, and individuals who are committed to transforming our societies through a diversity of initiatives and approaches.















we gather…



we mobilize individuals, groups, and congregations in response to pressing concerns, serving over 260 pastors and activists from the caribbean, latin america, africa, and europe with funds for education, material expenses, and tools for awareness of justice issues and organizing.
