scientific information related to  chestnut breeding and science.
updates – find up-to-date scientific information related to chestnut breeding and science.
the american chestnut foundation (tacf) is a non-profit organization dedicated to restoring the american chestnut (castanea dentata) tree to its native forests through a research program implemented through collaborations between professional and citizen scientists. using the 3bur approach (breeding, biocontrol and biotechnology united for restoration), tacf hopes to create a tree that is disease-resistant tree which retains as many american chestnut traits as possible.
pennsylvania chapter of the american chestnut foundation – information on the pennsylvania breeding program.
