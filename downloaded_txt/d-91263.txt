art knives and custom-made knives atfred eisen leather & art knives damascus, folders, art knifemakers,american blade society members and masters“art knives for the sophisticated collector”







roger bergh carved knife





for years fred eisen has collected knives and made custom leather knife sheaths for fine bladesmiths and collectors from around the world.we have decided to include some of these fine, custom art knives in our shop and our web site which are available to you, our customers.







dew hara copper/seido knife







robert appleby knife​







rob wayman damascus knife





we have knives by some of the finest blade-smiths in this country and the world.because many of these knives are one-of-a-kind, a particular knife may not be available, but we may be able to have the artist make a similar knife.







wes crawford skinning knife    





we feature an ongoing and ever-changing collection of knives.to buy any of the knives on this site, click on the secure buy now button.please call fred eisen at 215-862-5988 from 11-6pm eastern time, except for thanksgiving and christmas.
