the rosa one brain system
a $1.05-million gift left in the wills of the late clarence and marjorie anderson will bring groundbreaking technology to the qeii for patients living with uncontrolled epilepsy.  the rosa one brain system, an advanced surgical robot and the first of its kind in atlantic canada, will empower surgeons to pinpoint the source of seizures deep within the brain, potentially offering patients a cure. 💜

a $1.05-million gift left in the wills of the late clarence and marjorie anderson will bring groundbreaking technology to the qeii for patients living with uncontrolled epilepsy.  the rosa one brain system, an advanced surgical robot and the first of its kind in atlantic canada, will empower surgeons to pinpoint the source of seizures deep within the brain, potentially offering patients a cure. 💜 








the best research brings the best patient care
thanks to the generosity of qeii foundation donors, the qeii was the first in canada to perform a patient procedure with the mazor x spinal robot, and is the only health centre in atlantic canada with this cutting-edge technology! 🏥⚙️  this advanced robotics technology enhances precision — meaning shorter hospital stays, faster recoveries, and fewer revision surgeries,  all of which have a transformational impact on patient care and wait times. 💜
