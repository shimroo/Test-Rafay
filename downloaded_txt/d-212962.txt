township spotlights
 






  
 














municipal offices closed on monday, 2/17 in observance of presidents day 


municipal offices will be closed on monday, 2/17 in observance of presidents day.			






middletown township committee to host regular meeting on tuesday, 2/18 at town hall 


the middletown township committee regular meeting on tuesday, february 18, 2025 at 7:00 pm will be open to the public and broadcast through civicclerk. the meeting will take place inside the courtroom at town hall.						read on...







middletown township announces wellness wheels program 


the township announced middletown wellness wheels, a unique program that will be funded through a portion of the opioid settlement funds to provide free local transportation to healthcare services for residents who meet the criteria.						read on...







summer camp 2025 


summer camp registration opens monday, march 3rd at 10am for residents on recdesk! be prepared - we will sell out!						read on...







kids fishing derby 


catch the big fish and claim the prize! we're competing for the longest fish at garvey's pond. medals for top winners in each age range. raffle prizes throughout event.						read on...







eggstravaganza 


join us for a family fun day of spring activities, bounce house, doodle dude and miss fish, petting zoo, photos with the bunny and more! event is indoors/outdoors. no egg hunt here.						read on...







mystery egg hunt 


the hunt is on in our parks! join us for a mystery egg hunt! what is the mystery? you know the time and date, but not the location! our mystery locations will be revealed at 4:30pm the same day on facebook.						read on...







pitch, hit & run competition 


middletown recreation and middletown youth athletic association present mlb pitch, hit & run. major league baseball® pitch hit & run is a free event that showcases baseball and softball skills, while earning a chance to compete at a local mlb stadium!						read on...







town wide garage sale 


shop 'til you drop or spring clean your house at the annual town-wide garage sale happening saturday, may 17th and sunday, may 18th. free for shoppers, $8 per seller to be included on the official listing.						read on...







njaw plans for routine maintenance at its swimming river & jumping brook water treatment plants 


nj american water will temporarily change the water treatment process at its swimming river water treatment plant in colts neck and jumping brook water treatment plant in neptune. the maintenance will begin the week of february 17, 2025 through mid-april.						read on...







connect with the township to help correct concerns 


middletown township is now utilizing the “see-click-fix” platform so residents can easily contact the township to report any concerns. the online reporting tool is called connect & correct. simply submit your request and track it through completion.						read on...







middletown day at the new jersey devils 


cheer on jersey's team during middletown night! tickets include a postgame on-ice shoot from the blue line experience. all children receive a devils swag-bag! reduced parking and concession vouchers optional.						read on...
