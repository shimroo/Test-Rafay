public safety










the facilities and public safety team provide a wide range of critical services to create, operate, and sustain a safe and functional physical environment for illinois tech students, faculty, staff, and visitors. this environment comprises four campuses, more than 60 buildings, and 3.4 million square feet of floor space on 140 acres. locations include the university’s historic mies campus in chicago’s bronzeville community, the high-rise conviser law center in the west loop, the institute for food safety and health at moffett campus in bedford park, illinois, and the rice campus in wheaton, illinois.you can find contact information for the services and departments within facilities and public safety on our contact us page. please call, email, or stop by to speak with us. we are here to serve you, and welcome your feedback.bruce wattsvice president for administrationaccess card and parking services websitebuilding monitors listingsevent servicesfacilities facebook pagehawkworks (submit a work request)iit alert registrationplanning, design, and constructionpublic safety facebook pagekaplan institute websitepublic safety websitesustainability websitesustainability facebook page
