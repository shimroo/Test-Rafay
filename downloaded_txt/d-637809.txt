 sunday services






connection service


9:00am- 10:00ammeet in the sanctuary for contemporary praise and worship.
typical dress: casual, jeans or khakis and collared shirts/nice blouses are most common.
   




traditional service


11:00am - 12:00pmmeet in the sanctuary for a service incorporating hymns and liturgy. a traditional adult choir leads worship with organ accompaniment.
typical dress: traditional-casual sunday dress. button down shirt and slacks to khakis and golf shirt(men), dress pants and blouses(women).
