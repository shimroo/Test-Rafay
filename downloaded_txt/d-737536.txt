national airport hotel with a free shuttle



enjoy a convenient location and comforts of home at the holiday inn® national airport. our full-service hotel is located less than 1 mile from reagan national airport and offers a free airport shuttle to and from dca. unwind in newly-refreshed guest rooms with top amenities, perfect for leisure and business travelers alike.just minutes from our nation's capital, guests enjoy our close proximity to the crystal city metro for easy access to the pentagon, smithsonian institution and washington, d.c. monuments. explore vibrant national landing, one of the area's top shopping and dining destinations, or one of arlington's many landmarks including arlington national cemetery. spend the day sightseeing in old town alexandria with unmatched views of the potomac, or catch a show at the birchmere.we're close to northern virginia's top companies including amazon hq2, ibm, boeing, lockheed martin, booz allen hamilton, raytheon, northrop grumman and more. government contract and military travelers are always welcome. we offer a 24-hour business center and flexible meeting facilities. complimentary high-speed internet is available in all guest rooms and public spaces.keep up with your workout routine in our extensive fitness center featuring cardio and strength equipment. for dining, our hotel features several options to satisfy your cravings. join us in fresh kitchen for breakfast, o'malley's for dinner & drinks, or grab a snack from our 24-hour lobby market.

start your day at fresh kitchen

enjoy a bountiful and healthy breakfast from fresh kitchen, which offers an à la carte menu during the week and a buffet with hot and continental items on weekends.


                                        learn more
                                    





















drinks and dining at o'malley's pub

perfect for happy hour or dinner, o'malley's offers a mix of chef-inspired dishes, classic and craft cocktails and a well-curated beer list with local selections.


                                        learn more
                                    





















complimentary airport shuttle

offering service to and from reagan national airport from 4:30 a.m. to 11:00 p.m. daily. the shuttle departs every 30 minutes and stops for drop off and pickup at designated "hotel shuttle" areas at each terminal. service does not need to be reserved as it runs on an automatic schedule. additional rideshare services are available at the airport.


                                        learn more
                                    





















meetings 

offering five distinct meeting rooms with a combined 5,000 square feet of conference and event space, we can accommodate meetings, banquets, and corporate events of any size. let our experienced meeting planners and creative culinary artists plan every detail from coffee service to audio-visual equipment set-up.

weddings & special events

celebrate life's most momentous occasions at the holiday inn national airport. whether you're planning a wedding, a milestone birthday, a family or military reunion, our first-class catering team will walk you through every detail of your special day. our ideal location allows you to host up to 200 people, just minutes away from washington, dc.

faqs for holiday inn national airport/crystal city



does holiday inn national airport/crystal city have a pool?


no, holiday inn national airport/crystal city does not have a pool.




how much is parking at holiday inn national airport/crystal city?


at holiday inn national airport/crystal city, self-parking is available for a daily rate of 38 usd .




can i bring my pet to holiday inn national airport/crystal city?


no, pets are not allowed at holiday inn national airport/crystal city.




can i cancel my booking at holiday inn national airport/crystal city for a full refund?


always check the hotel's cancellation policy before booking a rate. room cancellation is subject to rate booked at time of reservation and cancellation policy varies by rate.




is there a complimentary shuttle available at holiday inn national airport/crystal city?


no, there is not a complimentary local area shuttle available at holiday inn national airport/crystal city.




what are the check-in and check-out times for holiday inn national airport/crystal city?


check-in at holiday inn national airport/crystal city is from 4:00 pm, and check-out time is 12:00 pm. contact the hotel directly for options available for early check-in or late check-out.




does holiday inn national airport/crystal city offer free wi-fi?


yes, the holiday inn national airport/crystal city offers free wifi!
