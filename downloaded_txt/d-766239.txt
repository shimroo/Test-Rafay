alta vista chiropractic & massage clinic offers integrated care for all
ottawa chiropractor dr. thanh le, dr. rob laquerre, and their professional team offer fully-integrated holistic care to people of all ages and conditions. from the founding of alta vista chiropractic & massage clinic in 1967, our practice has been dedicated to addressing the body as a whole. rather than focusing solely on presenting symptoms, we work to identify and resolve the underlying problem.
in addition to chiropractic care our practice utilizes several other natural therapies to help people move closer to complete health. learn more about our compassionate, qualified practice. we hope to get to know you soon!

languages aplenty
the alta vista chiropractic & massage clinic community is friendly, welcoming, and diverse. not only do we stay open late and offer extended hours, but our practice can communicate in seven different languages. we are truly invested in helping as many people as we can reach optimal wellness and hope to universally share the benefits whole health.




our achievements
we pride ourselves on providing honest, straightforward care. our knowledge and expertise continue to allow us to help countless men and women in and around ottawa. dr. laquerre has been caring for his community since graduating in 1998. throughout his years of service, dr. laquerre has received the following accreditations:
top 5 chiropractors in ottawa – metro magazine & chiropractor of the year – ontario chiropractic society
in addition to local recognition, we also serve members of the national hockey league (nhl), canadian football league (cfl), and ontario hockey league (ohl). we also care for olympians and top-tier golfers. our ongoing commitment to quality care and the innovative use of emerging techniques and technologies allow us to provide unmatched care to all those in need.



we are here when you need us
alta vista chiropractic & massage clinic houses three chiropractors, a physiotherapist and two massage therapists. we are also home to two physical therapists, a certified athletic therapist (cat), and several other qualified health care practitioners. whatever your needs - we are equipped to help you live a happier, healthier life.
today is the day to take back your health. contact us for more information about our practice. we are glad to address any questions or concerns you might have.
