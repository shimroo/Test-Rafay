carle illinois college of medicine

improving quality care for patients is not just something we say, it’s what we do. at the carle illinois college of medicine clinic, medical students are guided by experienced carle physicians, allowing us to care for patients while training and inspiring the next generation of physicians.to make an appointment with the college clinic, call (217) 365-6200.

research at carle
 
carle’s research enterprise, the stephens family clinical research institute, enables new discoveries with real-world applications and allows us to develop best practices that create a higher quality of healthcare.
