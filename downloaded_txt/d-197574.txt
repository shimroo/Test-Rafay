we provide essential academic services

the office of the university registrar oversees academic records for all currently enrolled and former fsu students. in our day-to-day operations, we provide support services for the entire university community, as well as a wide variety of data analysis and reporting. please consult our office with any questions concerning registration, class locations, meeting times, errors in registration records, registration cancellation, or grade issues. we look forward to assisting you with these aspects of your university experience!
