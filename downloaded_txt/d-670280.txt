trail favorites
if you were asked to pick your favorite trail, which one would it be? here is our list.


outdoor gem: alabama
from the ocean to the mountains, there are endless escapades waiting in the wilds, making alabama one of the best (and most diverse states) for outdoors recreation.


outdoor gem: manitoba, canada
with a landscape extending from the northern borders of south dakota and minnesota to the arctic environs of hudson bay, manitoba’s natural wonders come in many forms.


take it off-road
trail running doesn't require pricy gear or specialized skills. if you own a pair of running shoes and you're willing to get dirty, you've just joined the least-exclusive club in the outdoor sports world.
