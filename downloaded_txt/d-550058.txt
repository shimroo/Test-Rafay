health management careers



healthcare is a dynamic and growing field with a variety of opportunities and challenges. we hope this resource guide will give you a better understanding of the rewarding profession of healthcare management.
this resource is for:

baccalaureate students interested in pursuing an undergraduate or a graduate degree in healthcare management.
mid-careerists who are currently working in other fields and want to transition into a career in healthcare management.
high school students searching for information about a career in healthcare management.
