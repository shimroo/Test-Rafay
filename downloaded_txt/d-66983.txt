who we are
jobs with justice of east tennessee (jwjet) is a coalition of labor unions, faith groups and community organizations working together to advance economic justice in knoxville and surrounding communities. we are part of a larger network of local coalitions affiliated with national jobs with justice.we believe that all people deserve:the right to earn a living wagethe right to health care and a secure retirementthe right to a safe and healthy environmentthe right to be fully employedthe right to work free from discriminationthe right to organize, bargain, and act collectively to achieve these rights.
