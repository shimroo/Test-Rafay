memorial hospital pembroke is located in pembroke pines, fl and and offers a range of healthcare services in a patient- and family-centered care environment.
express care – schedule and appointment 
express care is reserved for minor
injuries and/or illnesses. we staff this treatment
area with a licensed physician assistant or nurse
practitioner, registered nurse, paramedic and
emergency care technician. they work directly
with our board-certified emergency room
physicians to meet your healthcare needs
in a timely manner.
schedule an express care appointment
for any life-threatening conditions please call 911 or go to the nearest emergency room immediately.




campus information




cafeteria





located on the first floor, our cafeteria is open daily for visitors and staff dining.
breakfast
monday-sunday: 7 am - 9:30 am (hot)
saturday-sunday and holidays: 7 am - 9:30 am (grill is closed)
lunch
monday-friday: 11 am - 2:30 pm (hot and grill)
saturday-sunday and holidays: 11:30 am - 2 pm (hot only)
dinner
monday-friday: 4:30 pm - 7 pm (hot and grill)
saturday-sunday and holidays: 5 pm - 7 pm (hot only)
other dining options
online food ordering for guests on the get app
the get app is your one-stop shop for ordering food from our cafeteria.
order now 






valet parking





complimentary valet parking is available from 7:30 am – 5 pm. after 5 pm, keys will be left with security at the main lobby. keys will only be released to those individuals with a valid valet tag.                                






visitor parking





parking is available in the parking lot adjacent to the hospital. if you are visiting the emergency department, parking is available in the parking lot across from the emergency department entrance.
