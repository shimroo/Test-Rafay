fresno truck center in fresno, ca

fresno truck center is your full-service freightliner®, western star®, rizon, and battle motors commercial truck dealer. we have been serving businesses in the fresno community for 95 years with all their transportation needs. no matter the class of vocational truck, our fresno location is fully stocked with new and used options that will deliver top-tier performance. it's no wonder why so many come to us to find the highest-quality trucks for sale in fresno!

once you have your truck, we make sure to help you every step of the way through your truck's life cycle. from parts to service to body shop needs, we are dedicated to keeping your truck on the road and its productivity high! we stock over $2 million in parts and boast the largest number of factory-trained and certified technicians in the central valley.

some of our services include semi/commercial truck repair, motorhome repair, and body shop repairs. visit us for all your repair needs including bit inspections and preventative maintenance services. we also would like to mention our in-house semi truck financing and lee financial services that we offer in fresno! if you've decided you want to finance your truck, you're not alone. we help customers with financing too!

browse through our pages and learn what else makes us great, but we would love to see you in person. stop by our fresno truck dealership and allow us to serve you today!

our extended hours of operation and certified technicians are ready to assist you. fresno truck center is proud to be a member of the california trucking association and has been rated an a+ by the better business bureau.

we at fresno truck center are part of the oasis service network. we are here to provide you with premier service and support for your motorhome!
