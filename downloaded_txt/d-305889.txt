chicago’s premier undergraduate research symposiumthe chicago area undergraduate research symposium (caurs) is one of the largest annual undergraduate research conferences in the country, bringing together hundreds of undergraduate students in the chicagoland area from all academic disciplines to present their research. through poster presentations, oral presentations, and a unique gathering of like-minded and hard-working students, attendees experience the impressive breadth and depth of research being conducted by their peers and have the opportunity to network with other undergraduates, graduate students, faculty, and representatives from industry and graduate and professional schools.
