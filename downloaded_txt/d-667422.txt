   welcome to brj & associates in the greater cleveland area 
dr. barry jaffe 
has been practicing since 1970. we offer outstanding patient care 
for children and adults. we use advanced technology and a full range of affordable dental and orthodontic treatment. each patient is treated with the utmost care! we want to be your family dentist.we are located in parma heights in greenbriar square at 6285 pearl road, suite 11.


   

 



   voted one of the best dentists in america this distinction was bestowed upon him by woodward/white inc., an independent research firm. the survey begins with a nomination pool consisting of dentists recommended by the leaders of several major dental organizations and department chairs at leading dental schools. dr. jaffe was then selected as one of the best dentists in america by a vote of his peers as published in northern ohio live, august 2006.
