advocating for our clients since 1982.choosing a lawyer can be one of the most important decisions you make. with over 38 years of legal experience, todd w. goodman brings compassion, knowledge, and insight to his clients in order to assist them through the often overwhelming legal system. mr. goodman has represented clients throughout california, with an emphasis on los angeles county, ventura county, santa barbara county, and orange county. the majority of his work involves personal injury victims who require their rights be protected and are looking for someone they can trust to bring their matter to a successful conclusion. his office also handles collections for medical professionals, corporate/llc formations, and a variety of general legal matters.with an office in thousand oaks, ca, he is centrally located for all of southern california. call now for an appointment and a free legal consultation. he is available 24 hours and will be happy to meet you at your home or a location convenient to you.
