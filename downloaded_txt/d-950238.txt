birmingham’s best location for celebration……since 1997!gabrella manor is an extraordinary wedding venue, with a rich history of entertaining, timeless elegance, and enchanting events. the natural landscape and vintage architecture provide captivating backdrops for very special events. events at gabrella manor are orchestrated by experienced  planning and event staff, who understand that every couple’s vision is unique; they go above and beyond to fulfill those dreams. the various spaces of the estate and home work well for celebrations both large and small.














    history















the bundy house (c. 1938) spanish colonial revival; with terra cotta roof; turreted entry way with patios and terraces. interior  features large rooms designed for entertaining, murals and stained glass windows throughout, a grand staircase, arches and columns, tile flooring and artistic tile scenes in fireplaces.”               originally designed by a dallas architect commissioned by the first owner, robert c. bundy; the estate is approximately five acres. the bundy family was well respected throughout birmingham and was known for entertaining guests and visiting dignitaries to the city.today the house is a restored birmingham landmark with the current owners, brenda jones and steve johnson, re-naming it gabrella manor in honor of her grandmother. the house sits at the edge of the roebuck springs historic district.—excerpted from application to the national register of historic places, october, 1998

the                         owners















brenda jones and steve johnson are the resident owners of gabrella manor.  they were married at gabrella manor in december 1997 (the first wedding there!) and enjoy sharing this grand historic home with others for their own special events.  both are native to alabama.
