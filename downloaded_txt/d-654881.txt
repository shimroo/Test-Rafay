explore dinosaurs at ʻimiloa: february 7 - may 4, 2025‘imiloa is thrilled to host expedition dinosaur: into the deep, a groundbreaking traveling exhibit that explores the fascinating world of prehistoric oceans and their inhabitants. from february 7 through may 4, 2025, visitors will embark on a thrilling journey into earth’s ancient past, encountering marine reptiles, dinosaurs, and the dynamic ecosystems that shaped our planet.hours of operationtuesday - thursday: 9:00 a.m.-5:00 p.m. friday: 12:00p.m. - 7:30p.m. saturday & sunday: 9:00a.m. - 5:00p.m. 600 ʻimiloa place at the university of hawaiʻi at hilo
