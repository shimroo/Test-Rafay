shop brands



at blinds 2go, we offer the largest range of made to measure blinds and curtains in the uk. with speedy delivery, free samples, and incredible value for money, update your home for less today. whether you're looking for the perfect window dressing for your bathroom and kitchen or bedroom or living room, we have you covered.
find your perfect window dressing
browse our wooden, shutter and venetian blinds that combine elegance and practicality, with robust slats made from real wood, faux wood or aluminium. our roller blinds, vertical blinds and roman blinds offer gorgeous style too, with a plethora of shades and fabrics to peruse alongside your choice of lining. featuring patterns from william morris in collaboration with the v&a, emma bridgewater, harlequin, clarissa hulse and more brands, we have window blinds to go in every space.
there's also pleated blinds that help you improve energy efficiency all year round with a honeycomb fabric, blackout blinds that stop the light in its tracks, as well as skylight blinds that offer a perfect fit at your roof window.
innovations in shading
for extra functionality, our electric upgrade offers innovative shading via the touch of our tuiss smartview app, allowing you to set it and forget it from anywhere in your home. you can also enjoy easier installation with our no drill upgrades, replacing brackets with a click or twist mechanism. for luxurious outdoor shading that adds instant appeal to your garden and patio area, look no further than our new range of retractable awnings.
amazing accessories
are you searching for that perfect finishing touch for your home? here at blinds2go, we offer cushions, including scatter cushions, sofa cushions and throw pillows in various colours and patterns.
we're here to help
our friendly customer services team are here to help with any enquiries, from ordering your window dressings to installation. with our online measuring guides and fitting guides too, you'll have your made to measure blinds fitted in no time. feeling unsure about measuring? add the blinds 2 go suresize measurement guarantee to your order for a replacement if your measurements are wrong. t&cs apply. still unsure on the right blind for you? browse through our bespoke buyers guides so you know you've made the right decision.
delivery across the whole of the uk
we deliver across the whole of the uk, meaning it's easy to find blinds near you, plus we provide free delivery on all orders over £249. with our fast express dispatch on selected styles too, it's never been more convenient to order your blinds online.

amazing guarantees

styles for every room

free fabric samples

perfect fit for any window
whether you have bay windows, tall windows, or french doors, our blinds fit beautifully.
