surge football club
surge soccer club promotes youth soccer in san marcos and wimberley, texas and surrounding areas, including martindale, driftwood and maxwell.  the new five mile dam field complex has enhanced our ability to fulfill our mission.
surge soccer club is a texas non-profit, tax exempt organization formed to promote, foster and advance the cause of soccer for youth at all levels of play within its territory.  it shall be the mission surge soccer club to educate, provide administrative service, promote, and stimulate interest in the game of soccer, with one purpose in mind:  to ensure that each player has an enjoyable soccer experience while moving toward his or her personal goal.
