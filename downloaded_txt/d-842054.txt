human resources































human


resources












the office of human resources is responsible for recruiting, retaining and recognizing the faculty and staff members who are part of creating villanova’s vibrant campus community and are key to advancing the university’s mission. hr manages the university’s comprehensive benefits  and compensation program, professional development opportunities as well as initiatives supporting villanova’s commitment to work/life balance. community is at the core of the villanova experience and the hr team is dedicated to strengthening the fabric of that community through its support services and resources.
