the mission of the division of student affairs is to cultivate an inclusive environment to advance students’ holistic development through intentional learning and transformative engagement.  whether students are developing leadership skills, competing in club sports, attending student activities, participating in a registered student organization, or attending a student conduct hearing, they are learning and engaging in ways to enhance and support their educational goals.
we are firmly committed to building meaningful student experiences that are transformative and engaging to foster the growth and development of students so they can enhance their academic experience and become their best selves.
check out our pages to learn more about the division of student affairs departments, critical initiatives, and programs.





who we are




students first
our student-centered approach affords us the opportunity to serve our students with excellence and provide superior collegiate experience.


intentional engagement
it is our priority to provide equitable, and inclusive programming for our students that will allow them to find their place and purpose in order to thrive in their local and global communities.


holistic development
we seek the comprehensive growth of our students, intellectually, socially, and emotionally.






mission statement: the division of student affairs cultivates an inclusive environment to advance students’ holistic development through intentional learning and transformative engagement.
