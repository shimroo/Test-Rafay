man arrested for breaking into house and stealing car 

hingham, ma – a weymouth man was arrested monday morning after he allegedly broke into a home and stole a car from the garage. officers found the man inside the stolen car in a parking lot with drugs in his possession.				read on...




road closures for annual christmas in square event 

there will be road closures with detour routes today (december 6) for the annual "christmas in the square" event in downtown hingham. detour routes will be posted.				read on...




email scam using fake church account 

hingham, ma - a resident reported she was almost scammed by a fake email account claiming to be from a hingham church seeking gift cards. before she actually purchased them, the resident called the actual church to verify and learned it was a scam.				read on...




driver arrested after hitting pedestrian in crosswalk 

hingham, ma - a hingham woman was arrested for alleged repeat drunk driving after hitting a pedestrian in a crosswalk thursday night. the pedestrian was taken to the hospital with non-life threatening injuries.				read on...




license to carry (ltc) update for hingham residents 

hingham, ma - the state firearms licensing portal has opened for applications to be submitted online through the link below. this will be an option only for those looking to renew their ltcs for hingham residents.				read on...
