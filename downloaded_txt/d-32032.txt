welcome to hillel at the university of arizona!we are here to help you build a vibrant, diverse, meaningful, and empowered jewish community at uarizona. wherever you are on your jewish journey, we welcome you. this is a place where you—a uarizona undergraduate or graduate student—can explore identity, build leadership skills, and engage in jewish life on your terms. or just chill in our air conditioned space (cozy vibes in the beautiful sonoran autumn and winter).
