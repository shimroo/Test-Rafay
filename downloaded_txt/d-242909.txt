about us
central ohio youth baseball league provides a competitive and educational baseball experience for the youth of central ohio and beyond for ages 7u to 14u.  through our league and tournaments over the last 15 years, we have raised over $430,000 that has been donated to the ohio state university – james cancer center, nationwide childrens hospital for pediatric cancer research as well as the onoursleeves campaign for under the new “the kids mental health foundation”
