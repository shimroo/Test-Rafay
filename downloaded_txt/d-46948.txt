accolades
la casa pasta has been an award-winning restaurant since 1978. it has been a staple in the community providing authentic italian cuisine over the years with its homemade pasta, seafood creations, and veal specialties. over the years they have been recognized in numerous publications and received numerous awards for their cooking locally from the news journal reader's choice awards and delaware today's best of section."benvenuti in italia!"
