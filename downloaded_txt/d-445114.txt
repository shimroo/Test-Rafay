welcome to redford township
come visit us at the redford town hall complex, home to the police department, 17th district court, marquee, and some township services.







visit the marquee
the marquee is host to a number of events year-round. visit us for oktoberfest, the summer farmer's market, or a number of other seasonal events.







volunteer with us
many of the township's events, including festivals and elections, need volunteers to keep things running smoothly.







parks & facilities
redford township is home to more than a dozen parks. visit the splash pad in the summer, reserve a space for a gathering, or enjoy our outdoor programming. visit leisure services for more information.

welcome to redford townshipredford township is a dynamic community of 49,504 people located on about 11.25 square miles. our residents are active and engaged in government, civic and neighborhood groups, and we bring metropolitan resources to a tight-knit community.redford is home to more than a dozen parks. visit any of them to enjoy playgrounds, ballparks, walking paths and other amenities, enjoy the splash pad in the summer months, or reserve a park space for a family gathering. the township prides itself on offering a wealth of programming. through our leisure services department, residents can enroll in adult softball or join any number of other programs. our festivals committee spends the year planning for the summer festival, oktoberfest, and other holidays and seasonal events. there's something for everyone in redford.
