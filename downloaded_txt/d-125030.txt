ems and sheriff’s office partner to improve cardiac arrest response 


wayne county ems and the wayne county sheriff’s office have teamed up to reduce response times for cardiac arrests. the county recently supported the purchase of 20 automated external defibrillators (aeds) to be placed in sheriff’s office vehicles.						read more...







wayne county property revaluation 


property revaluation ensures fair, accurate property values based on market conditions, as required by nc law. revaluation does not determine your tax bill—the board of commissioners will set the tax rate separately. learn more about the process here.			additional info...







low-income energy assistance 


households in wayne county containing a person aged 60 or more or someone receiving disability benefits and services through the nc division of aging and adult services can apply for help covering the cost of heating their homes starting december 2, 2024.						read more...
