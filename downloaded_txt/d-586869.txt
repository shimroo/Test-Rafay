weather alert


...extreme cold watch remains in effect from tuesday evening through
thursday morning...

* what...dangerously cold wind chills as low as 10 below zero
possible.

* where...portions of north and central texas.

* when...from tuesday evening through thursday morning.

* impacts...the dangerously cold wind chills as low as 10 below zero
could result in hypothermia or frostbite on exposed skin if
precautions are not taken.

precautionary/preparedness actions...

dress in layers including a hat, face mask, and gloves if you must
go outside.

keep pets indoors as much as possible.

make sure outdoor animals have a warm, dry shelter, food, and
unfrozen water.

make frequent checks on vulnerable family, friends, and neighbors.
ensure portable heaters are used correctly. do not use generators or
grills inside.
