welcome to birch ridge resort,a fishing resort on leech lake in northern minnesota. we offer weekly cabin rentals, along with boats, rv and tent sites, fish cleaning service, gas, bait, tackle, and ice. the lodge provides pizza, ice cream, snacks, beer, set-ups (byob), wireless internet, a jukebox, pool table and clothing. peacefully secluded on the north end of sucker bay, birch ridge is just 20 miles from the closest town, enhancing the genuine serene wilderness.
