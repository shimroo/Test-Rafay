about fastoys racing
welcome to your powersports destination!

experience the thrill of powersports like never before at our premier montana dealership. whether you're an off-road enthusiast, a road warrior, or a water sports adventurer, we have the perfect machine to fuel your passion. our vast selection of top-of-the-line boats, atvs, motorcycles, snowmobiles, personal watercraft, and more is matched only by our unwavering commitment to customer satisfaction. with a history dating back to 1998, we've proudly served montana and the flathead valley, including kalispell, whitefish, columbia falls, bigfork, somers and lakeside becoming your trusted source for all things marine and powersports. our expert team is ready to guide you through your journey, whether you're a seasoned rider or new to the world of boating and powersports. from sales and financing to parts and service, we're your one-stop-shop for all your marine and powersports needs. our new 20,000-square-foot state-of-the-art showroom showcases an extensive collection of top-tier boats, motorcycles, utvs, side-by-side (sxs) atvs, jet skis, snowmobiles, scooters, ebikes and more. we sell quality products from kawasaki, arctic cat, beta, timbersled, suzuki, segway, honda, fantic, nautique, crazyebikes and triton trailers.
