a poolside hotel tiki bar at key largo marina



key largo hotel steps from marina adventures 
ideally situated next to key largo resorts marina, our key largo hotel places you in the perfect position to experience the endless florida keys fun. whether on vacation or a team retreat, soak up the tropics at our holiday inn® key largo hotel, one of the top florida keys hotels.
key largo has everything you need to make your vacation unforgettable. spend your days oceanside beneath the florida sun. take a snorkeling and scuba diving adventure or a romantic sunset cruise, all just minutes away from one of the top key largo resorts. spend the day at the john pennekamp coral reef state park or get even closer to marine life by swimming with the dolphins at dolphin plus. there are so many things to do in key largo.
a 55-minute drive south from the bustle of miami, our key largo resort's event facilities offer a quiet, relaxed atmosphere, ideal for corporate gatherings. host your next business event at one of the five function rooms, and relax afterward at our 2,000 sq. ft. tiki hut pavilion. a 24-hour business center offers more support, and free wired and wi-fi access is provided throughout the property.
complete with every perk from on-site dining and a poolside tiki bar to two heated, outdoor pools and a 24-hour fitness center, the accommodations at our florida keys hotel ensure a memorable travel experience. it's an island paradise from the moment you arrive.

faqs for holiday inn key largo



does holiday inn key largo have a pool?


yes, holiday inn key largo has an outdoor pool.




how much is parking at holiday inn key largo?


complimentary parking is available at holiday inn key largo for hotel guests.




can i bring my pet to holiday inn key largo?


pets are welcome at holiday inn key largo. our pet policy: dogs allowed, max 2 per room, 25 pounds and under. service animals welcome.




can i cancel my booking at holiday inn key largo for a full refund?


always check the hotel's cancellation policy before booking a rate. room cancellation is subject to rate booked at time of reservation and cancellation policy varies by rate.




is there a complimentary shuttle available at holiday inn key largo?


no, there is not a complimentary local area shuttle available at holiday inn key largo.




what are the check-in and check-out times for holiday inn key largo?


check-in at holiday inn key largo is from 3:00 pm, and check-out time is 11:00 am. contact the hotel directly for options available for early check-in or late check-out.




does holiday inn key largo offer free wi-fi?


yes, the holiday inn key largo offers free wifi!
