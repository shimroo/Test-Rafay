rebecca walker



writer • producer • speaker • teacher

  







 

i write and produce stories that transform the global conversation about selfhood, power, and the evolution of the human family.


















































    more
  






 

i challenge individuals and institutions to deconstruct cultural and political narratives, cultivate understanding and empathy across difference, and imagine a future story that supports us all.

 

 


























i guide people and organizations to release old, limiting stories about who they are, and write new narratives that support their aspirations to live in alignment with their true feelings, beliefs, and values.

 









i speak at universities, consult with companies, and advise organizations on a range of topics, including diversity, equity, inclusion, and belonging.
