about the bhc

with the generous support of donors, the business history conference awards scholars with various prizes, fellowships, and grants. records of award recipients are available on our website. selection of recipients is the duty of various prize committees composed of bhc members.
the governance of the bhc is shaped by its members, whose service on committees enables the society to achieve its mission of encouraging all aspects of research, writing, and teaching about business history and about the environment in which businesses operate.Â
