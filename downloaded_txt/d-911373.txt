《跑跑卡丁车》夺旗模式生存手册放出　　《跑跑卡丁车》的夺旗模式可谓是目前为主三大模式当中最火爆、最刺激，同时那种淋漓尽致的双快感也是最为直观的！不过，想要在枪林弹雨的赛场拼死捍卫手中象征荣誉的旗帜可是需要一点策略和技巧的，本次《跑跑卡丁车》官网视频专区放出两段夺旗模式教学视频，传授你争我夺之中的奥妙！　　教学视频分为个人赛和组队赛两段，其中个人赛视频充分展现了夺旗模式中回避的精髓！视频中的车手在抢到锦旗之后，不断以冲刺、转向、漂移甩开身后的对手，而且之后更是巧妙的跑到了对手的身后，令对手难以采取有效的攻击！在利用精妙的驾驶技术之余，还巧妙的运用各种道具，不但用来防护对手的攻击，更是充分发挥“进攻是最好的防守”这一理论，向对手发动猛攻抢占了先机！

thursday, june 7, 2007







dow jones, weighing offer, extends severance plan





by cecile daurat and leon lazaroffjune 7 (bloomberg) -- dow jones & co., the newspaper publisher considering a $5 billion offer from rupert murdoch, made 135 managers eligible for a severance plan if the company is sold. the managers are eligible for a year's pay if they are fired within two years of a change of control, joining 25 senior company executives who are covered for as much as two years, spokeswoman linda dunbar said today in an interview. the changes are meant to keep employees from leaving and focus them on their work while dow jones, publisher of the wall street journal, considers a potential sale, the company said today in a regulatory filing. murdoch's $60-a-share offer for dow jones was made public may 1. the company said may 31 it will consider offers from murdoch's news corp. and other potential buyers. ``they're getting their decks in order for a deal, and i think it's going to murdoch,'' said richard dorfman, managing director of richard alan inc., a new york-based investment firm focusing on media companies. ``they're saying, `anybody who has a problem with that, here is your parachute.''' the company also is offering a minimum 12 weeks of severance to eligible employees if they are fired in a change of control, new york-based dow jones said in the filing. about 850 employees qualify, dunbar said. dow jones also adjusted time periods for bonuses tied to performance goals in a change of control, it said in the filing.
