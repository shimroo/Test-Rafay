about the school of pharmacy and health professions

lessmore links


 




master the art and science of whole-person care. whether you are interested in preventing and managing diseases or improving patient wellness, we offer multiple industry-leading paths to choose from at the school of pharmacy and health professions. here, students in our occupational therapy, pharmacy and physical therapy programs gain hands-on experience and learn to care for patients in mind, body and spirit.  our affiliations with healthcare organizations around the country give students access to healthcare professionals who are leaders both in their professions and the community. giving back is at the core of a creighton education.
