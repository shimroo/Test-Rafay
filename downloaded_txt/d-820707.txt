meet judy

looking for a realtor® with business, sales, marketing, and management experience? a realtor® who listens, cares, and communicates?
 look no further…
as a full-time professional realtor®, i am dedicated to meeting the real estate needs of my clients, both buyers and sellers.  i look forward to working with my clients helping them through the exciting and sometimes stressful process of purchasing or selling real estate; whether it is the first time buyer, the seller looking to “move-up” or the senior looking to “right-size” their home… all are important to me.  with my prior business experience and over 25 years as a realtor®, i know how to work diligently, making the process as easy as possible.  my clients benefit from my experience and can-do attitude.
