meet the photographer
hi, my name is tammy. i have been taking pictures since i was a little girl. photography has always been a huge interest of mine. growing up, i remember always having a camera in my hands. i loved taking pictures of anything and everything! i started with point and shoot cameras, then i moved up to film slr cameras, and finally i’ve been using digital slr cameras for about 20 years. the picture to the right is of my family and i. we love to be active and adventurous and see new sites! we are at arches national park in this photo. washington state is where my family and i reside, and we wouldn’t want to live anywhere else! thank you for visiting our website!
