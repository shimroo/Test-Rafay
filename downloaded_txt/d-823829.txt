 about us

 since 1994, discount home warehouse has worked to preserve and pass on our architectural history.  dhw was established to re-purpose the re-useable building materials and unique architectural pieces recovered from the vast number of homes being demolished or extensively remodeled in the dallas/ft. worth metroplex. now this valuable salvage is integrated into new building or design projects rather than headed to overflowing landfills.
