spring clean-up dates announced 


the village and flood brothers disposal are pleased to offer spring clean-up as an opportunity for residents to get rid of unwanted items.						read on...







fy2025-26 budget workshop meetings 


the village board will hold the fy2025-26 budget workshops on tuesday, february 11th, at 6:00 p.m. and saturday, march 15th, at 9:00 a.m.						read on...







maplewood property information 


information on the maplewood property redevelopment can be found on the community development projects webpage.			community development projects webpage







traffic alert - rt. 14 near e. main street 


enbridge, a utility company, will be performing maintenance work along rt. 14 near e. main street starting february 3rd.						read on...







reminder - overnight parking restrictions in effect 


the village of cary reminds residents that from december 1 - march 31, the village's winter parking restrictions are in effect.

during this time, on-street parking is not allowed between the hours of 1:00am - 6:00am.						read on...

if you are in the mood for food, check out our handy interactive restaurant map of all the great places serving up delicious dishes in cary!						read on...
