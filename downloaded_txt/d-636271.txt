welcome to wahoo enterprises!










wahoo enterprises is a small mom-and-pop shop that opened in march 2000. we started out selling office supplies and now, over 20 years later, sell fishing supplies, hunting gear, ammunition, camo clothing, electronics, computer accessories, ink cartridges, and some archery supplies. we also provide bookkeeping services and virtual assistant services.
we are located at 820 – 1st avenue in mcbride, british columbia, canada. if you would like to call us, you can reach us at (250) 569-0320.
our store hours are tuesday to saturday, 10:00 a.m. to 4:00 p.m. closed sundays, mondays, and statutory holidays.
for new products and specials, check out our facebook page at https://www.facebook.com/wahooenterprises
