why choose us
there's no place like perris




 

 

 

 

 







hollywood’s choice

skydive perris is the west coast’s largest and most trusted skydiving center and the closest to hollywood. a listers, vips, and influencers choose us, and more skydiving scenes are filmed here than anywhere else.

learn more 

obsessed with safety

meet our general manager, dan brodsky-chenfeld – a world record holder and 6-time skydiving world champion with more than 30,000 jumps. we lead from a zero tolerance culture of safety.

learn more 

onsite restaurant & bar

watch skydivers while enjoying delicious food and ice-cold drinks. the world-famous bombshelter is open all day, every day for indoor and poolside dining and hosts a full calendar of live music and fun events.

see the menu 

onsite wind tunnel

skydive perris was among the first dropzones to incorporate an indoor skydiving simulator into operations, and we remain one of the world’s only dzs with an onsite wind tunnel! ages 3+ welcome – bring the whole family!

learn more 

aircraft

we utilize the industry’s safest and most reliable aircraft, including the only rear-exit tailgate jump ships in cali. all of our twin turbine planes are meticulously monitored and maintained by our full-time aircraft mechanics.

learn more

							our dz location						 close to the action
