welcome to hyland hills hockey association!




our community hockey association is proud to offer various programs for boys and girls of all skill levels, including intro-to-hockey programs, recreational teams and competitive travel teams. hyland hills jr hockey association's mission is to provide a safe, fun, and positive environment for young athletes to learn the game of ice hockey, develop their skills, and build lasting friendships. the association is committed to promoting sportsmanship, teamwork, and respect on and off the ice.
