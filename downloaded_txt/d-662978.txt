 the hendrick i. lott house is a living time capsule of gotham history located in marine park, brooklyn, new york city.

  

the lott house is currently closed to public access. click here to read about the upcoming restoration and renovation.the hendrick i. lott house is closed to the public until further notice. friends of the lott house will continue to offer outdoor and online programming. we will provide updates on our website and social media accounts. if you have any questions, please direct them to the friends of the lott house via email to: flh@lotthouse.org.
