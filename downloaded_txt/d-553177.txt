pacific beach, washingtonwashington’s best kept secret



just a 90-minute drive from olympia, pacific beach is a haven for relaxation and adventure. with its unique blend of sandy beaches, state parks, and plentiful department of natural resources (dnr) land, there’s something here for everyone.year-round beach funthanks to the pacific ocean’s tempering effects, our beaches offer year-round excitement. escape the low, overcast skies of inland with sunny winter days, or cool off from summer heat with our gentle sea breezes. whether it's the thrilling sight of winter storms or the serene summer sunsets, our coast is a perfect backdrop for your next getaway.activities for all seasonsexplore and play: walk the expansive sands, fly a kite, or just listen to the soothing surf.adventure awaits: depending on the season, join in on clam digging, crabbing, and fishing.
