consciously crafted uniform apparelwhat is standard 100 by oeko-tex®?
standard 100 by oeko-tex® is one of the world’s best-known labels for textiles tested for harmful
substances. it stands for customer confidence and product safety.
to establish oeko-tex® standard 100 certification, every component of a cobmex® sweater, including the yarn, thread, and buttons, has been tested ensuring that it is safe from a human ecological perspective.
