township of union spotlights







	scroll below to find our latest events in union and stay up-to-date! 
 






  
 
 















black history month long celebration 


in celebration of black history month 2025, the township of union is proud to host a month-long lineup of events.			additional info...







small business workshop 


join us and the njeda on thur, february 20, at 9am as we feature njeda's financial resources and programs for local businesses. this workshop will take place at nova’s coffee shop & restaurant.
registration is required.			additional info...







union skate night 


get ready for some thrilling excitement this winter!  
mark your calendars for our epic skate night on saturday, march 8th, at the union sports arena.			additional info...







union county st. patrick's day parade 


join us as we celebrate st. patrick's day with the annual union county saint patrick’s day parade, which will be held on saturday, march 15, in the township of union.			additional info...







networking for a cause 


we proudly host the annual networking for a cause to benefit layla dangiolillo, a brave 9-year-old student diagnosed with leukemia/lymphoma. as she battles this illness from home, her family faces additional challenges.			additional info...







job board 


find your perfect job match with our latest job board. search or post a job or internship opportunity today.			additional info...







no knock registry 


click on the link below to apply for the "no-knock registry." once filled please email the form to clerks@uniontownship.com.			additional info...







a milestone - 250 years in the making 


on july 4, 2026, our nation will commemorate the 250th anniversary of the founding of the united states. the journey toward this historic milestone is an opportunity to pause & reflect on our nation’s past and look ahead to the future.			additional info...
