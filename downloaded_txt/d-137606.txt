our programs



¡soy unica! ¡soy latina! rally

the  rally is a one-day event for latinas ages 7-17.  its purpose is to encourage latinas to be proud of their ethnic identity and cultural heritage.the latinas unidas board, rally committee, volunteers are present and active to serve as role models to the young latinas.

read more

reconocimiento awards

the reconocimiento awards acknowledge the accomplishments and contribution to the community of latinas in the following categories:

professional achievement
inspirational leadership
volunteer service
youth latina leadership

latina unidas scholarship

eligibility for the scholarship includes, but is not limited to:be a latina age 25 or overenrollment in a formal training, higher education, or certification programbe a resident of one of these counties: genesee, livingston, monroe, ontario, yates, orleans, or wayne hold a high school diploma or general equivalency diploma   
 
join latinas unidas

as a registered member you help latinas unidas grow, and be a respected and credible organization in the community. by way of your membership support you help us with our programs.

our mission

the mission of latina unidas is to foster opportunities that will promote unity, cultural identity and the presence of the latina woman in the community; to encourage and motivate women to be self-confident to assume leadership roles to positively impact society.
