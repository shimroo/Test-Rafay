commonly asked questions:
what is the process of buying a used car through autotrek?

buying used vehicles through autotrek is an easy and effortless process. it starts with deciding on the type of vehicle that best fits your needs. from there, you can browse through our online inventory and filter them by make, model, year, body styles, and price range. you can compare all your cars at once from our online portal. once you select a vehicle, you can request a no-obligation carfax report — this will provide an accurate history of the car along with any applicable manufacturer incentives or any applicable epa mileage ratings. 
then the next step is to speak with one of their experienced auto trek representatives who will guide you through their loan and financing options best suited to your budget. to finish up the buying process all paperwork — from purchase contracts to loan documents — is easily done online or via in our littleton dealership. all in all, buying a used car through autotrek should be a stress-free and memorable experience!

how do i sell my car through autotrek?

selling a car through autotrek couldn't be easier. all you have to do is get a very fair offer from us by entering your car's make and model, actual mileage, and details about any accidents or damage to the vehicle. we'll show you recent sale prices for comparable cars in your area and give you a free no-obligation offer. if you decide to accept it, all that's left to do is book an appointment and drop off your car. we will inspect the paperwork and the vehicle itself, then pay you in cash on the spot — it's that easy! so why wait? get yourself an offer now and see how much money you could get for your car right away!

what are the benefits of going through a used car dealership?

when looking to purchase a used car, going through a dealership can have its advantages. not only do dealerships typically have a wide range of cars available, but they will also often provide advice and guidance in finding the best car to fit your specific needs. you can also sign up to begin receiving price alerts on your saved cars. additionally, many dealers also offer deals like a premium package with their service department, as well as financing options that private sellers may not offer. finally, when shopping for used cars at dealerships, customers can rest assured that all vehicles on the lot have undergone extensive safety inspections - minimizing risk and ensuring quality assurance when making such an important purchase.
