camps and programs










summer camps

one-week day and overnight sports broadcasting camps in 13 cities across the country for sports fanatics ages 10-18.











advanced workshop

three-day intensified workshop at mnyk studios in manayunk, pa for ages 15+ looking for a more advanced program.











virtual programs

virtual summer and winter camps, virtual activities throughout the year, private lessons and events, and more.











parent child weekend

spend a fun-filled weekend with your family in philadelphia, pa honing all aspects of sports broadcasting!
