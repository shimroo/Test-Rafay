lightscape track directable is an ultra-minimalist linear luminaire equipped with a micro-prismatic lens (ugr <19) for efficient illumination along the entire length of the luminaire. adjustable lightscape track allows it to be tilted from vertical to 90° to illuminate vertical walls and objects, making it ideal for illuminating vertical walls in retail areas, galleries and exhibitions.
