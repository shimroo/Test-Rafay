beethoven lives upstairs



sunday, september 29, 2024 | 4:00pm




    get tickets
  



symphony and theatre together! join us as the northbrook symphony magically weaves more than 25 excerpts from beethoven’s most beautiful works into a dramatic performance where actors reveal true incidents from the composer’s life. this exciting performance is a single 50 minute act, recommended for audiences age six and up.
