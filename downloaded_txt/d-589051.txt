the heart of niagara

experience niagara's favourite neighbourhood

 
you've got to eat here!we've brought a little taste of ireland to niagara falls. doc magilligan's is your neighbourhood local for any occasion.  
you've got to see this event!from summerfest, restaurant week, live music to the winter festival of lights, there’s always something happening #onthelane    
you've got to have fun here!ride the waves and slides in our 25,000 square foot indoor waterpark with a retractable roof for year-round fun!  
you've got to stay here!americana waterpark resort & spa offers luxurious deluxe accommodations, relaxing spa, indoor waterpark, on-site family restaurant and conference centre.  
you've got to shop here!with canada one outlets and 30+ boutiques and shops, lundy’s lane is a shoppers delight.  
seeing is believing!watch our latest videos to see what's happening in the heart of niagara. lundy's lane, niagara falls, canada
