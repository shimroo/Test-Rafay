why shop here?
welcome to chuck hutton toyota, conveniently located in memphis, tn. we proudly serve southaven, whitehaven and the goodman road area by providing our customers with the best in new toyota models, quality used vehicles, exceptional auto repair and car service, and high-end oem car parts. as the only locally-owned car dealership in memphis, it's our mission to treat our customers and community with care and respect. 
 
because of this, not only have we been awarded the 2016 toyota president's award, but we are also heavily involved in charities throughout the area, like the elvis presley 5k, fast & furriest 5k and distracted driving 5k. when the time comes for you to purchase a vehicle or have your own serviced, look no further than chuck hutton toyota, a family-owned dealership committed to our community.
 

new toyota models for sale
