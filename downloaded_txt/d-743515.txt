libby deep search



libby offers the best reading experience for all ages and has the same great content you loved in the overdrive app! get in-app support with any questions you may have. download it and see for yourself: https://www.overdrive.com/apps/libby/.
features:
• never miss a chance to borrow that book you’ve been waiting for. libby sends push notifications, so you’ll never miss out.
• just want to see books for kids? are you only into audiobooks? with the libby app, you can set preferences to see only the content you’re interested in.
• read while you drive. the libby app offers support for apple carplay and android auto to listen to your audiobooks.
