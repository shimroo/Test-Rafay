we’re no kill and fear free certified.



at cherryland humane society, we’re a no-kill animal shelter dedicated to providing a safe haven for animals in need. our fear free certification ensures that all our furry friends receive compassionate, low-stress care, prioritizing their emotional well-being. with a commitment to saving and improving the lives of countless animals, we invite you to support our cause and bring home your new best friend.  learn more about us →
