your neighborhood shouldn’t limit your health, happiness, or economic outcomes.




    learn more
  












 

with an action orientation to creating connected, resilient, thriving communities impact employs a multidimensional approach to systems change investing in both people and place over time. impact’s work is grounded in the creation of environmental, emotional, and interpersonal safety with the consideration of significant adversity in individual, family, and community histories. targeted mechanisms of change include:elevation of community leadership and community self-determination.investment in programs and activities that foster greater social cohesion and collective efficacy.increased economic prosperity though the creation of pathways to careers and wealth creation. stable and sustainable housing.creative transformation of deteriorating public spaces and structures to improve quality of neighborhood life.innovation though adaptation of evidence-based interventions.comprehensive planning and implementation.recognition and utilization of the “power-with” partners to achieve goals.our missionwe are a community action organization mobilizing people and resources to create connected, resilient, thriving communities.

 












 what we do 
























jobs
while we can help anyone looking for employment, we have special programs focused on those who are re-entering the workforce after incarceration.



learn more





























homes
much of our housing efforts are focused on the goal of ending veteran homelessness, with hundreds of beds for veterans and their families.



learn more





























community
we work on building a stronger community through a trauma-informed approach that meets people where they are.



learn more



















impact loan fundsince 2001, the loan fund has provided financing support to surrounding neighborhoods, working closely with other lenders to attract businesses, support redevelopment, and finance existing business growth to create more local jobs.
