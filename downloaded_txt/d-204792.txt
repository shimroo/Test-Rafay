closed in observance of presidents' day 


in observance of presidents' day, the borough of madison municipal offices will be closed on monday, february 17, 2025.			






payroll data 


the madison borough council is committed to openness and accountability in governance. we’re pleased to announce that the 2024 payroll data and a five-year gross pay study are now available!			explore the reports...







borough newsroom

water meter upgrades 


in an effort to provide you with improved water service and accurate billing, the borough of madison water department is upgrading its meter reading system. the upgrade will include the installation of new reading equipment and a water meter.			additional info...







online construction portal 


with the use of the portal, there is no longer a need to travel to the municipal building, or make phone calls to department offices. instead, you can find all of the resources you need online, including requesting inspections.						read on...
