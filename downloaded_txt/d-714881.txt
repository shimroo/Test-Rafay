champion screw machine engineering

multiple spindle automatics replacement parts, tooling, attachments & rebuilding




 pmpa – next gen video
champion screw machine is a member of the precision machine products association. kevin coffman was featured in a promotional video for the pmpa next gen committee. the video was created to show high school students that a career in manufacturing is a exciting career option.

 
 new – spring loaded chain tensioner
champion has developed a solution to problems with broken chains that drive threading on 1-1/4 ra6 thru 2-5/8 rb8 national acme automatic screw machines.  the chain tensioner gets mounted in the attachment bore 2nd position on a 6 spindle and 3rd position on an 8 spindle machine. benefits – extends web chain life – shock  read more…

 
 modern cam & tool
champion is pleased to announce the acquisition of modern cam & tool. modern is a precision cam manufacturing company located in taylor, michigan and has been engineering and manufacturing oem cams since 1971. champion has been supplying the metal turning industry since 1945 and modern has been a supplier to champion for over 40 years.  read more…

 
 champion featured in production machining magazine
champion screw machine was featured in the july 2015 issue of production machining magazine…. champion screw machine engineering was founded in detroit, michigan, in 1945 by brothers john and b.j. mascow. both left their jobs with modern collet and machine company to create a business to supply replacement parts and machinery to the screw machine industry using an inventory of surplus  read more…
