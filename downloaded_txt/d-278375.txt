we actively support local charities and organizations, and bazzy himself is a well-known figure throughout the state -- plenty of residents tune in every week to listen to one of his various podcasts or radio shows. it's easy to feel like you're part of something greater when you shop at shults ford!







what are the shults ford locations?



we have three ford dealerships in pennsylvania, as well as one just across the border in west virginia. we share inventory between our stores, and we'll move our vehicles around to where you need them. so, if you'd like to see a particular new ford f-150 trim or used bronco model at the dealership closest to you, you'll find it ready and waiting. we offer the following locations:

• shults ford wexford at 10401 perry highway in wexford, pa
• shults ford harmarville at 1300 gulf lab rd in pittsburgh, pa
• shults ford south at 900 regis ave in west mifflin, pa
• shults ford wheeling at 95 robinson dr in triadelphia, wv
