zilica limited is a  successful uk company selling a range of video over ip solutions.
zilica  started selling specialised integrated circuits for video applications in 1998, however due to customer demand a new wholly owned subsidiary of zilica limited was set up in 2006 called antrica.
antrica manufacture and sell video over ip encoders, decoders and network cameras.
zilica limited no longer sells integrated circuits but invests in new electronics companies & business opportunities
