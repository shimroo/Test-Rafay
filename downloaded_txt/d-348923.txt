military science & leadership








military science & leadership the military science and leadership program at the university of richmond, the home of the reserve officers’ training corps, or rotc, is dedicated to commissioning high caliber and diverse future officer leadership for the u.s. army from the university of richmond and its partnership schools.

master sgt. david w. johnson to retire after 20 years of dedicated service
the university of richmond spider battalion will say goodbye to master sgt david w. johnson as he retires in december 2024.  msgt johnson served his country for twenty years in the unites states army. we thank him for his many years of dedicated service and will cherish the leadership and guidance he bestowed upon the cadets within our program.
