on december
			24 2012, in the town of webster ny,  a lunatic set his house
			on fire at 5:30 in the morning then hid on a small hill a few
			yards away and waited for the first responders to arrive to
			extinguish the flames.  he open fire on them and killed two
			firemen wounding two others using a semi-automatic bushmaster
			rifle.  a webster police officer, mark reed, who had
			responded to the scene grabbed his rifle from the patrol car, hid
			behind a nearby house and returned fire at the gunman who
			immediately stopped shooting and retreated from his position,
			being chased then by officer reed and other webster police
			officers.  without the quick actions of officer reed the
			gunman would have killed many more firemen with his semi-automatic
			bushmaster weapon, a shotgun and a handgun which he had in his
			possession.  i have nominated officer reed for the
			presidential medal of freedom, the highest honor a civilian can
			receive in the usa.  please click on this link and sign the
			petition to president obama to give this hero his medal.
