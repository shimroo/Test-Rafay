about us
carved from 265 pristine acres just a short drive from i-83, hunt valley country club's family-friendly ambience, remarkable amenities and impeccable service have earned it recognition among maryland's finest private non-equity golf and lifestyle clubs. from our 18 holes of championship golf to our comprehensive tennis center to the pool complex, gorgeous clubhouse and year-round schedule of members-only social events, hunt valley country club was designed to exceed members' needs and expectations at every turn.
