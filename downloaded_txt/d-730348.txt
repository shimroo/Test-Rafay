welcome to galaxe - working with pride!galaxe is a xerox recognized independent employee resource group that exists to aid in achieving company diversity and inclusion objectives.  galaxe is for employees who are, and who support gay, lesbian, bisexual, or transgender persons. the group aims to increase visibility of its members within   xerox and beyond and provide a connection between its membership and xerox as   well as with other lgbt organizations. be yourself. everywhere!     safe space | professional development | advocacy | community service






























at xerox, pride means acceptance. not only for who you are, but who you want to be.— charlotte walker barnes, channel business manager -diversity, inclusion & belonging advocate.










































































i am grateful to work at a company that is a supporter of and advocate for the lgbtq+ community. xerox recognizes that a company is most successful when each employee feels respected and safe to bring their whole self to work. that’s one of the many reasons it’s consistently rated one of the #hrc’s “best places to work for lgbt equality.”— michelle waites, vice president, legal for carear a xerox company.
















































 





























“supporting the rights of lbgti workers and customers is not only the right thing to do, it’s simply good business.”— suzan morno-wade, global hr executive
