community



we’re honored to be named dream community by the home builders association of lake & sumter counties for 2025. from the moment you enter this private, gated country club community, you’re welcomed by peaceful, park-like surroundings, tree-lined roads, and beautiful green spaces. harbor hills embodies the art of possibility—a place where every home tells a story, and every lifestyle finds a place. whether you’re drawn to luxury or serenity, harbor hills offers endless possibilities for living well.





































it’s your lifestyle



individual personalized service where you are greeted by name and your server pours your favorite drink as you walk in the room. its stylish lifestyle combined with uncompromising quality and unmistakable value makes this an irresistible opportunity in active country club living.
