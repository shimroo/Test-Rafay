city spotlights
 






  
 
 















traffic controls on traffic way bridge 


starting february 6th, traffic controls will be in place on traffic way bridge as we begin preparations for the traffic way bridge replacement project.						read on...







save the date: meet the machines event – a celebration of community and connection in arroyo grande 


the city of arroyo grande is thrilled to invite residents and visitors alike to the 4th annual meet the ma-chines event, a signature family-friendly celebration showcasing the vehicles and machines that build, protect, and serve our vibrant community						read on...







village redwood tree light calendar 


thanks to the partnership between the arroyo grande rotary club and the greater pismo beach rotary club, this redwood tree shines brightly year-round in celebration of various local and national events.			see the full calendar







apply to serve on an advisory body 


the city's following advisory bodies have vacancies: tourism business improvement district advisory board, architectural review committee, downtown parking advisory board, and community service grant program committee. apply today!			additional info...







watermain replacement on cornwall avenue 


the watermain on cornwall avenue is being replaced. work is expected to be completed by the end of april 2025. some traffic impacts are anticipated.						read on...







traffic way bridge replacement project - largest project in ag history 


the project will improve public safety, update the bridge to meet current standards, and remove potential scour and maintenance concerns with a single-span bridge which will span over arroyo grande creek. project completion is expected in 2025.			additional info...







measure e-24 sales tax next steps 


following the approval of measure e-24 by the voters of arroyo grande, the city is now moving for-ward with next steps to implement the new local sales tax.			additional info...







new state law: ab 413 - now in effect! 


ab 413 prohibits parking within 20 feet of the approach side of any marked or unmarked crosswalk.						read on...







winter-spring 2025 activity guide 


check out the city's new winter-spring 2025 activity guide! stay connected and active, and join us to learn and grow while making new friends and connections within the community.			click here to view the full activity guide







plan arroyo grande - general plan update 


we would like to learn more about your experiences in arroyo grande. what are your favorite places in the city? how do you envision arroyo grande looking 20 years from now?						read on...
