    philadelphia society for the preservation of landmarks

bringing our homes to you for 94 years















philalandmarks invites you to experience history through our four 18th century historic house museums in the philadelphia region.



visit our house museums, join our 94-year-old preservation society or host a private or corporate event in a unique historic setting
















tour our houses















philalandmarks is a 94 year old nonprofit organization that preserves and presents four extraordinary historic house museums integral to our nation’s founding and to philadelphia history.
