inside our world



we seek to be a just, diverse, vibrant, and worshipping spiritual community through the pursuit of social justice, creative expression, and sacred exploration of its own protestant church roots and the wisdom of other traditions.

“judson approaches faith not as a monolithic or unchanging set of beliefs but rather as a continual process of action, revelation and exploration.”















—rev. micah bucey
