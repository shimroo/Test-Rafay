about us



the wooden sign company is a woodworking studio and sign shop catering to those who desire that one of a kind sign. we are signmakers, woodworkers, graphic designers, sculptors, painters, typographers and industrial designers offering unparalleled design and fabrication services, exceptional workmanship and competitive pricing for signs, carvings and retail fixtures. combining old world craftsmanship and state of the art technology, we create an end product that is attractive, durable and budget conscious. while our specialty is dimensional signage, we also offer gold leaf signs as well as sandblasted, hand carved and cnc cut signage using wood, high density urethane(hdu) and a variety of other mediums. from small house signs to architectural monuments signs, we bring our passion for creating exceptional work to each and every project we do. let's present your business to the public with the class it deserves! thanks for stopping in and enjoy the site.
