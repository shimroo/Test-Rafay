who we are






forum furniture is one of the leading manufacturers of furniture in the region with over a quarter of a century of experience in furniture manufacturing. the company’s 7000 square meters factory is built on a 15,000 square meters land and can operate 24-7 ensuring deadlines are always met.

established in 1977 forum furniture began as an office furniture manufacturing company based in amman, jordan, successfully supplying jordan and the region with sturdy and contemporary wood and steel office furniture.

the company expanded its production line to include school furniture, auditorium seating, shelving units, as well as partitions in various colors and styles.

forum furniture was the first furniture company in jordan to acquire the iso 9001 certification in 1997. the certificate was later upgraded to become iso 9001:2000. this certification helps insure that forum furniture products meet the strictest quality assurance specifications.
