neal pollack is the author of 11 bestselling books of fiction and nonfiction. his most recent book is pothead. he is the editor-in-chief of book and film globe.a contributor to every english-language magazine and website except for the new yorker.a three-time jeopardy champion.the host of the audible originals documentary podcast extra credit.the lead singer of the neal pollack invasion, and a certified yoga instructor.he lives in austin, texas, seemingly against his will.
