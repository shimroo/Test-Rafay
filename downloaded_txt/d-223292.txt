who we are



pearls supports youth identifying as girls in 5th-12th grade through weekly groups at schools and community sites throughout the school year. in group, pearls focuses on developing core skills for their personal and professional growth, opportunities to prepare girls for college and career life, and many more enriching activities.learn more



























be inspired, make friends, and thrive



we’re a girl-only, safe space supporting girls 5th-12th grade in striving towards their goals. girls can access our programming at schools or community sites in and around milwaukee or at our office on martin luther king jr. drive.

pearls programs



our leadership compass® in addition to our pearls promise are the core of pearls programming. we empower girls to lead the lives they dream of. 



become a leader















our college & career readiness (ccr) program provides college and career exploration opportunities to all girls and specialized support to juniors and seniors. ccr programming empowers girls to step into their lives after high school with purpose and drive.
