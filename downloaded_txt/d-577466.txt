welcome to the home of terenzo bozzone
terenzo bozzone has been in the sport of triathlon for over 15 years and has become more than an athlete. a role model to youth, aspiring and professional athletes. considered as one of the most approachable athletes in the sport who holds many titles that include being a 5 x world champion, 4 x ironman champion and 33 x international half ironman titles.
