why you should join the mml the mopar mailing list  (mml) is a car club that celebrates all things chrysler corporation. we are enthusiastic about our late model daily drivers, work trucks, off road jeeps, classic muscle cars, and antiques.
our primary communication is through our email list but we are active on social media and we document our exploits here.
