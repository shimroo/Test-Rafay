the community church in plano texas



at meadows baptist church, we have a deep commitment to loving all ages. as one of the great baptist churches in plano texas, we believe in the value of every individual, regardless of their stage in life. from the youngest members to the older generations, we strive to create an inclusive and nurturing environment where everyone feels valued and empowered. our goal is to call and empower each life stage to follow christ and live biblically, equipping and supporting them on their spiritual journey. whether you're a child, a teenager, a young adult, or a senior, we want to walk alongside you as you grow in your faith and understanding of god's word.

meadows baptist church, located at 3001 los rios blvd, plano, texas, is a welcoming community dedicated to empowering all generations to follow jesus christ and live biblically. our mission is to create an inclusive and nurturing environment where individuals of all ages can grow in their faith and understanding of god's word. we believe in the value of every individual, from the youngest members to the older generations, and strive to support each life stage on their spiritual journey.our sunday worship service is a time for the entire church family to come together in praise and worship, experiencing the presence of god and the fellowship of believers. we also offer various programs and activities throughout the week to engage and support our members, including bible studies, prayer meetings, and community outreach initiatives.at meadows baptist church, we highly treasure the holy bible as god's revelation of himself and his eternal purpose. we hold the common faith which is revealed in the bible and is common to all genuine believers. as members of the body of christ, we are committed to practicing the oneness of the body with all christians in plano and beyond. we are in fellowship with over 3,500 local churches worldwide to express the one body of christ.our church is led by a dedicated team of pastors and leaders who are passionate about guiding and nurturing the spiritual growth of our congregation. we believe in the power of prayer and the importance of community, and we strive to create a supportive and loving environment where everyone can feel at home.in addition to our regular worship services and programs, we host special events and activities throughout the year to further engage and inspire our members. these events provide opportunities for fellowship, learning, and spiritual growth, and are designed to meet the diverse needs and interests of our congregation.we invite you to join us at meadows baptist church and experience the warmth and fellowship of our church family. whether you are new to the area, seeking a church home, or simply looking to deepen your faith, we welcome you with open arms.
