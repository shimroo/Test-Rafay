iowa city hospice
you have a choice for hospice care. most hospices in our area are for-profit corporations. as a community-based nonprofit hospice organization, iowa city hospice prioritizes:

offering compassionate care to anyone in our community facing serious illness and end-of-life conditions
putting patients over profits
never turning anyone away from hospice care due to inability to pay
local leadership that is answerable to the community that created us
investing in our staff, patient services, and the sustainability of nonprofit hospice care

if these priorities sound good to you, please ask for iowa city hospice by name.
