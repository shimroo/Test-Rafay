initiatives of the ccedc











agconnect
building the capacity of farmers and their supply-chain partners through investment in business training, and workforce.
















economic development partners initiative
creating tax ratable commercial and industrial properties and permanent, full-time sustainable jobs.
















health care connect
addressing the workforce and business development needs within the local healthcare delivery system.
















hireone
developing key strategies for successfully connecting job seekers to hiring employers.
















innovative technology action group
regional technology leaders identifying common challenges and advancing real solutions.
















i2n
supporting start up businesses in life sciences, energy, and information technology.
















manufacturing alliance of chester and delaware counties
promotes manufacturing industry growth through workforce and economic development services.
















seedcopa
a ccedc affiliate program specializing in sba 504, 7a, and usda financing for small businesses.
















smart energy initiative
providing comprehensive workforce and business development services to partnering energy organizations.
