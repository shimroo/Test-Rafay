trans am buyer's guide


hoods


vis racing


oem-style carbon fiber hood


vis racing is known for their fantastic carbon parts and this particular 
hood
is a fantastic upgrade for those who are looking to add some distinctive style and reduce the weight of their trans am. the trans am/firebird is known
for its heavy front end and pulling a few pounds off the front axle will help the nimbleness and turn-in characteristics of the car. in addition, these
hoods are competitively priced and for the sale price of $584.00 each, it would be hard to find a better alternative:

http://www.andysautosport.com/pontiac/1993_2001_firebird/exterior/hoods/carbon_fiber_hoods/vis_racing/visr01020963.html





wheels


american racing


classic 200s wheels - polished


american racing's best known for their multitudes of different sporty wheels for a variety of cars, but they often look best on good ol' american
muscle. this particular
rim
 is a harkening back to simpler times and they really do make for an eye-catching treat. thankfully, these 
wheels
 are on sale at the moment for a very reasonable $332.50 each via andy's auto sport: 

http://www.andysautosport.com/custom_wheels/pontiac/1993_2001_firebird/group_detail.html?submodel=93-02_firebird_non-transam&upc=amrw00156597








high flow catalytic converter 


magnaflow


direct-fit catalytic converter - passenger side


magnaflow brings some of their decades of research and development to this refined 
catalytic converter
. their fit and finish are specifically designed to ensure the easiest installation possible. when modifying your exhaust system, an aftermarket
catalytic converter is recommended to ensure maximum flow and long-lasting, cleanly exhaust. available at andy's auto sport for $402.03 with free
shipping:

http://www.andysautosport.com/pontiac/1993_2001_firebird/performance/exhaust/catalytic_converters/magnaflow/mgfl00001185.html








clutch kits


hays


performance clutch kit - diaphragm (11" diameter)


hays designs their clutches to be as hassle-free as possible. built to factory specifications, installation is easy as pie and this    clutch kit features a heavy-duty cover to minimize flex and ensure strength
and stability, and is assembled with a ductile iron pressure ring and high cone belleville spring. the hays street/strip diaphragm pressure plate offers
light pedal pressure and a quick, clean release for precise gear changes with positive engagement to handle the horsepower of your trans am. on sale at
andy's auto sport for only $521.96 each:

http://www.andysautosport.com/pontiac/1993_2001_firebird/drivetrain/clutch_kits/hays/haye00000080.html






bumpers


sherman


front rebar


for those who have been in a front end collision and need to replace theirbumpers, rebar or any other relevant    parts ought to inspect the collection of oem-style parts from sherman auto.
this rebar, built to the same standards and specifications as the oem piece, can expedite your repair process while saving you some money at the same time.
available at andy's auto sport for $38.05 each:

http://www.andysautosport.com/pontiac/1982_1992_firebird/exterior/bumpers/factory_bumpers/sherman/sher00215684.html






hoods


extreme dimensions


ws-6 style fiberglass hood


extreme dimension's line of fiberglass hoods brings style and performance to
anybody who chooses to adorn their car with one. not only are they lighter than the stock piece, but they have gaping intakes to allow for better airflow
into the engine, keeping it cooler and potentially generating more power. these parts from
extreme dimensions are so light, in fact, that the usage of hood pins is strongly recommended, all of which are available at andy's auto sport for very low
prices:

http://www.andysautosport.com/pontiac/1982_1992_firebird/exterior/hoods/fiberglass_hoods/extreme_dimensions/extr00132310.html






steering wheels


grant


corsa gt steering wheel 13.75" diameter


grant's corsa steering wheels are an affordable way to add some custom
touches to your trans am's interior and improve driving ergonomics. with quality leather covering most of the wheel, your hands will thank you. in
addition, the well-designed shape caters well to the act of hard driving. this beautiful wheel is available at andy's auto sport for a reasonable $248.89
each:

http://www.andysautosport.com/pontiac/1993_2001_firebird/interior/steering_wheels/steering_wheel/grant/grnt00000004.html









headers


slp 


headers - long tube (with y-pipe and high-flow cats)


slp's headers are made from high-quality 409 stainless steel and are designed to
improve power and torque in the mid-to-high-range of the rpm. note: these slp long tube headers won't fit the automatic, convertible trans ams. available
at andy's auto sport for a reasonable $1,420.22 each:

http://www.andysautosport.com/pontiac/1993_2001_firebird/performance/headers/slp/slpa00205163.html






nitrous oxide kits


nitrous express


nitrous system - gasoline (200-500 hp with 10 lb. bottle)


the nitrous express direct port nitrous kits are for the crowd who have already
modified their engines with high strength internal engine parts. these systems utilize the patented nitrous express "piranha" nozzle! available standard
with high tech distribution blocks, and optional "next generation" rail technology. the nitrous system comes complete with nitrous bottle, stainless bottle
brackets, stainless hard lines, nozzles, jetting, and all necessary electrical components. this system requires the drilling and tapping of the intake.
available at andy's auto sport for $1,128.43:

http://www.andysautosport.com/pontiac/1993_2001_firebird/performance/nitrous_oxide/nitrous_oxide_kits/nitrous_express/ntex00001228.html
