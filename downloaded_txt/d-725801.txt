about monster parties trade monster parties trade supplies both consumers and  the leisure industry with personalised party bags, personalised activity bags, personalised retail activity packs, wedding bizzy bags, personalised stationery products and marketing material. we provide all the ancillary products you need for parties such as tableware, balloons, decorations, etc.




 




about monster parties trade monster parties trade supplies both consumers and the leisure industry with personalised party bags, personalised activity bags, personalised retail activity packs, wedding bizzy bags, personalised stationery products and marketing material. we provide all the ancillary products you need for parties such as tableware, balloons, decorations, etc.





monster parties trade offers the best prices for wholesale and retail quantities of a range of the most popular party supplies.  whatever the celebration we have themed party supplies, decorations, banners, balloons, party bags etc.  we can also offer personalised activity packs, stationery, and banners which we design and print in-house.
