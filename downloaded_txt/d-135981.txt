mobility & parking services

the division of mobility and parking services is committed to providing safe, equitable and predictable mobility and parking options for all residents, guests and visitors in the city of columbus. we aim to increase mobility choices, manage congestion, and facilitate access to goods and services in a city experiencing enormous growth. click here(pdf, 1mb) to learn more about us.
