the public policy center (ppc)
the public policy center within the college of social sciences at the university of hawai'i at mānoa brought together expertise, commitment, and a strong desire to enhance the quality of community life through teaching, research, civic engagement, and interdisciplinary work toward the development and analysis of sound public policies.
 
the public policy center is no longer in operation.
for any questions, please contact the college of social sciences.
