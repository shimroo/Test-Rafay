acrylic decor for every purpose

kitchen & dining
our wide range of options of water pitchers, fruit bowls, tongs, versatile serving trays serve as attractive and durable kitchen and dining essentials. our acrylic drinkware options are especially popular for events and parties due to their elevated glass-like look while being nearly unbreakable. 


bath & vanity
we believe our unique, eye-catching acrylic creations will flip any ordinary looking space into something more stylish. style up any bathroom and vanity spaces with our quality and long-lasting acrylic products-from sleek perfume trays and jewelry organizers to modern-styled soap dispensers and waste baskets.


home & office
personalizing a work or home office space comes easy with our high-quality acrylic products. elevate any office with professional looking desk supplies like tape dispensers, waste baskets, pen cups, paper weights and more. we’re confident customers will love the unmatched clarity and impeccable styles to elevate their workspaces.
