“just one of the kids”
















what is hoosier burn camp?hoosier burn camp is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization committed to providing life-changing experiences for young people who have suffered the physical and emotional trauma associated with their burn injury.our annual summer camp and monthly events create experiences for burn survivors where they can be just one of the kids™ in a safe and supportive environment.
