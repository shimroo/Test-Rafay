tax notice







city of athens has proposed a tax rate that will raise more taxes for maintenance and operations (m&o) than last year’s tax rate. the tax rate will effectively be raised by 10.37 percent and will raise taxes for m&o on a $100,000 home by approximately $11.95. for more information regarding the fy 2025 proposed budget and tax rate visit the “financial transparency” page of our website.
