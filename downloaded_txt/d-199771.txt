meet the real new york city.
see the hidden sides of the city. hear the real new york stories. experience it for free.



request a greeter 





























find the little places for big memories.
stroll above manhattan streets on the high line. poke into the happening shops of brooklyn. savor the ethnic murals and delicious foods of the bronx or queens. ferry your way past lady liberty to a neighborhood in staten island.  spend two to four hours getting the insider scoop from volunteers who actually live here. we’re not professional tour guides. we’re the people of new york. proudly representing our city. and sharing its joys.
