welcome to the ag 450 farm

the ag 450 farm mission is to be a practical educational resource for iowa state university and the department of agricultural education and studies demonstrating quality and sustainable production agriculture in the midwest. the farm's serves as a hands-on learning laboratory for students enrolled in ageds 450.
