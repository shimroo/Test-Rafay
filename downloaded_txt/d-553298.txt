explore top vancouver attractions



just steps away from yaletown bars and restaurants, robson street shopping, and the entertainment district, holiday inn & suites vancouver downtown offers a comfortable stay. enjoy a stroll along the vancouver seawall or take a ferry across false creek to granville island market. our prime location provides easy access to sporting events, the cruise terminal, and the vancouver art gallery.
host gatherings in our meeting rooms, stay active in our fitness centre, and relax in our indoor pool. savour delicious meals at our onsite restaurant, pamper yourself in our spa, and stay connected with free wi-fi.
combining a prime location with newly renovated accommodations, top-notch amenities, and excellent service, we ensure your stay is unforgettable. we acknowledge that our hotel operates on the traditional, ancestral, and unceded territory of the musqueam, squamish, and tsleil-waututh nations. we are grateful to live and work on these lands and recognize the importance of honoring indigenous peoples' histories, cultures, and contributions.

faqs for holiday inn & suites vancouver downtown



does holiday inn & suites vancouver downtown have a pool?


yes, holiday inn & suites vancouver downtown has an indoor pool.




how much is parking at holiday inn & suites vancouver downtown?


at holiday inn & suites vancouver downtown, self-parking is available for a daily rate of 40 cad .




can i bring my pet to holiday inn & suites vancouver downtown?


no, pets are not allowed at holiday inn & suites vancouver downtown.




can i cancel my booking at holiday inn & suites vancouver downtown for a full refund?


always check the hotel's cancellation policy before booking a rate. room cancellation is subject to rate booked at time of reservation and cancellation policy varies by rate.




when is breakfast at holiday inn & suites vancouver downtown?


on weekends, breakfast is available from 7:00 am to 11:00 am.




is there a complimentary shuttle available at holiday inn & suites vancouver downtown?


no, there is not a complimentary local area shuttle available at holiday inn & suites vancouver downtown.




what are the check-in and check-out times for holiday inn & suites vancouver downtown?


check-in at holiday inn & suites vancouver downtown is from 3:00 pm, and check-out time is 11:00 am. contact the hotel directly for options available for early check-in or late check-out.




does holiday inn & suites vancouver downtown offer free wi-fi?


yes, the holiday inn & suites vancouver downtown offers free wifi!
