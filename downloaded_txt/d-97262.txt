holly springs prepares for winter weather 

the town of holly springs is preparing for possible winter weather, which could cause icy or snowy streets beginning wednesday. garbage, recycling, and yard waste collection is adjusted. town facilities are closed on wednesday.				read on...







town council discusses future of holly springs at annual retreat 

your mayor and town council members are spending three days this week discussing topics that are important to the future of holly springs during their annual retreat.				read on...











new app gives easy access to fitness center, parks & recreation 

get the most from parks & recreation with the holly springs recpass app! app users enjoy easy access to the hunt fitness center with this digital pass, convenient registration for town programs, a calendar with upcoming events, and lots more.		read more...







town of holly springs upgraded to highest aaa credit status from moody’s ratings 

the town of holly springs was upgraded to a aaa credit rating last week, the highest financial distinction possible from moody’s ratings. the rating was also assigned to the voter approved parks and recreation and transportation bonds debt issuance.				read on...











battalion chief byars named n.c. firefighter of the year 

holly springs battalion chief hunter byars has been named north carolina firefighter of the year by the n.c. veterans of foreign wars organization.				read on...







celebrate black history month in holly springs with art, movies & more 

february is black history month. opportunities to celebrate include free movies, an art gallery and black history showcase, a cooking class, a black expressions poetry event, and more!				read on...











local civic nonprofit grant program applications open 

are you involved in a civic nonprofit group that serves holly springs residents? applications for the 2025 local civic nonprofit grant program are open through march 7.				read on...







applications open for tree advisory, parks & rec committees 

help shape the future of holly springs; consider applying to serve on the holly springs tree advisory committee or parks & recreation advisory committee!				read on...
