welcome to 1st place roofing – your trusted roofing expert
as a leading owens corning preferred contractor, 1st place roofing stands at the forefront of delivering exceptional roofing services, rooted in expertise and a commitment to excellence. we are a proud veteran-owned business, bringing discipline, integrity, and a superior level of dedication to each project we undertake. our team specializes in a comprehensive range of roofing services designed to address every need, from storm damage roof replacement to routine maintenance and installations.
expertise in storm, hail, and wind damage roof replacement
weather can be unpredictable, and its impacts on your roof can be severe. at 1st place roofing, we specialize in storm damage roof replacement, hail damage roof replacement, and wind damage roof replacement. our rapid response team is ready to assess and fix any damage, ensuring your property is protected against future weather events. with our emergency roof replacementservices, you can rest assured that help is only a call away, twenty-four hours a day, seven days a week.


comprehensive roof inspection and maintenance
 
maintaining the health of your roof is essential for the longevity and safety of your home or commercial property. our roof inspection services are thorough and detailed, aiming to identify potential issues before they lead to significant damage. regular maintenance performed by our experienced roofing contractors can extend the life of your roof and prevent costly replacements down the line.
 
seamless roof insurance claims assistance
 
navigating the complexities of roof insurance claims can be a daunting task for property owners. at 1st place roofing, we offer roof insurance claims assistance to guide you through the process smoothly and efficiently. our expertise ensures that you receive the maximum benefit from your claim, making the restoration process as stress-free as possible.
 
get your free roof estimate today
 
whether you’re facing urgent replacement needs or considering a new roof installation,1st place roofing provides free roof estimates to help you make informed decisions. our experienced roofing contractors will evaluate your needs and offer transparent, competitive pricing for our high-quality services. trust us to deliver unmatched workmanship and customer service from start to finish.
 
why choose 1st place roofing?
 
choosing 1st place roofing means selecting a partner dedicated to providing top-notch roofing solutions that stand the test of time. as an owens corning preferred contractor and a veteran-owned business, we embody the values of integrity, quality, and reliability in everything we do. our extensive experience in handling all types of roofing projects, coupled with our commitment to customer satisfaction, makes us the first choice for homeowners and businesses alike. contact us today to experience the distinguished service that sets us apart.
