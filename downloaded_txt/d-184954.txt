welcome to lake jocassee,  
jocassee gorges, cashiers, sapphire, toxaway, & 
surrounding carolina areas
lake jocassee consisting of 7,656 acres was created by damming the
tributaries of the keowee river by duke power. since its waters come directly
from cold, clear, protected appalachian streams, the lake remains cold and clear
throughout the year. it is an excellent area for scuba diving, swimming, and
paddling, as well as fishing.  lake keowee, just below the dam, is one of our 
areas premiere destinations for south carolina mountain real estate, including 
two of the areas largest lakefront communities. please enjoy the site, check out our
photo's, and please make reservations with our lodging facilities.
