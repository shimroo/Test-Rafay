        welcome to north caldwell      

since the beginning in the early part of the eighteenth century, north caldwell has served as a fine example of the charm and simple pleasures of suburban life. north caldwell is a haven that is the "green jewel" of essex county with beautiful homes on its hilly, wooded roads. our residents demonstrate their commitment and love of the community through involvement with organizations, as well as membership on various boards and committees.
