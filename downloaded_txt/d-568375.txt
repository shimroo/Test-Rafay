welcome to stentor - the leading manufacturer of student stringed orchestral instruments worldwide. stentor violins, violas, cellos and basses are recommended by teachers 
and music services everywhere. see our stentor orchestral strings section for details of all stentor instruments and find out how we ensure the quality of our violins, violas, cellos and double basses. 
stentor instruments are available from music shops in the uk and through our network of distributors in over 30 countries.
we are also the uk's leading independent distributor of musical 
instruments, developing and representing some of the best quality 
musical brands. we supply the music retail trade, providing hundreds of 
music shops in the uk and ireland with a wide range of quality 
instruments, parts and accessories.
