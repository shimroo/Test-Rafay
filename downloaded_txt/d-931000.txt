aviation is in our dna
what started as one man’s passion for flight has grown through four generations to become the southwest’s premier aircraft sales and services provider. founded in 1928 by william p. cutter, cutter aviation has been the leading aviation services provider for the southwestern united states and one of the most well-known brands in general aviation.  as the oldest continually family-owned and operated fbo network in america, cutter aviation has decades of experience of providing the best service for an outstanding value.
