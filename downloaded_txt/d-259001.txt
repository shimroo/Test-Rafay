the soaring seabird

 3 bedrooms
 2.5 bathrooms
 sleeps 6
 yachats
 pet friendly

come experience the beauty of yachats in this incredible home. this property is a two story home that provides exceptional ocean views. there are three bedrooms located on the ground floor. upstairs on the second floor is where you will find the kitchen, dining and living room. the living room has large picture windows allowing gorgeous views. there is a beautiful balcony on the second floor with chairs allowing you to soak in sunsets or the power of the ocean during king tides! there is a hot tub on the ground floor to add extra relaxation to your stay.

oregon coast vacation rentals
ocean odyssey is a vacation rental company located in yachats-waldport, oregon, referred to as “the gem of the oregon coast.” we have quality homes located in yachats, waldport, seal rock, south beach and newport. there are many things to do at the central oregon coast with many events throughout the year. our team is ready to take reservations and to assist with homeowner services seven days a week, 24 hours a day. contact us to find the perfect getaway home at the coast or to turn your property into a rental with ocean odyssey.
