public hearing on solid waste disposal set for march 4 


the brazos county commissioners court will hold a public hearing on march 4, 2025, at 1:30 p.m., to consider ordinances related to prohibiting solid waste disposal in certain areas of the county. citizens may attend and testify at the hearing.			view the full notice and proposed ordinance






ware promoted to emergency management coordinator 


brazos county commissioners have named jason ware as the new county emergency management coordinator (emc).  he has served as the interim emc for the past four months, and as the county’s deputy emc for eight years.						read on...
