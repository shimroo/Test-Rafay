sexual violence shatters lives, wounds communities, and perpetuates injustice. 

 

we envision a world free of sexual violence in which individuals together assure that all human beings are treated with dignity and respect for their physical, emotional, intellectual & spiritual integrity.
























making the world a safer place is possible.
fcasv’s team serves as the state expert on sexual violence issues and best practices for responding to victims and survivors.



learn more






 











we are better together. 
this is a people movement of transformational change. whether you’re most comfortable contributing time to help achieve our advocacy goals, money to help us grow, or energy to make change happen, we need you on our team. 



join us



















 

the florida council against sexual violence is a statewide nonprofit organization committed to victims and survivors of sexual violence and the sexual assault crisis programs that serve them.
