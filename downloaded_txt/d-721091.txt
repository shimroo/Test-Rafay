institutional effectiveness & evaluation
our purpose
the goal of assessment is to use data to make informed decisions about teaching, learning, program delivery, equity, and overall institutional effectiveness. engaging in systematic, integrated, and thoughtful assessment of student learning, the student learning experience, and administrative and support functions helps our campus to ensure a high-quality, equitable experience for all students. oiee is committed to this endeavor and to assisting our faculty and staff in the continuous improvement of their programs and processes.
