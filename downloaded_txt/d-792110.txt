the new york state chess association (nysca) has promoted chess in the empire state for almost 150 years.  holding tournaments and other chess events from montauk to niagara, nysca holds america’s longest-running chess tournament, the new york state championship, along with its longest-running state scholastic tournament and other new york state championship competitions.  membership is available through the officers.
