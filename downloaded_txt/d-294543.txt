stitchingart – machine embroidery designs by cathy parkwelcome to stitchingart! we offer a wide array of stunning machine embroidery designs, crafted to work seamlessly with bernina, husqvarna, pfaff, janome, singer, brother, and other top sewing machine brands. alongside our extensive collection of designs available for purchase, we provide a range of free downloadable designs, allowing you to explore our artistry firsthand. at stitchingart, we are committed to delivering high-quality, artistic, and beautifully detailed designs to elevate your creative projects.our designs come in various formats including art, dst, emb, exp, hus, jef, pes, vip, and vp3, catering to a range of sewing and embroidery projects. explore our diverse categories which include alphabets and monograms, angels, animals, artistic patterns, wall hangings, sewing machine covers, quilts, oriental themes, lifestyle and homewares, florals, cushions, clothing, christmas, children’s themes, birds, and bags.we are confident that you will love the machine embroidery designs we offer. each design is carefully sewn and rigorously tested by cathy herself before being made available for sale, ensuring you receive only the highest quality digitized designs.new machine embroidery designs
