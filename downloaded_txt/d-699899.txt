level of compression





 8-15 mmhg
        
mild graduated compression

          for legs that are achy and/or fatigued or standing and/or
          sitting for long periods of time.
        








 15-20 mmhg
        
moderate graduated compression

          for legs that are standing andior sitting for long periods of
          time, spider veins, minor leg swelling, achy and/or
          fatigued legs, leg discomfort during pregnancy, minor
          varicose veins, and for wear after sclerotherapy.
        








 20-30 mmhg
        
firm graduated compression

          for moderate varicose veins, swelling/edema, long flights or travel, heaviness andor leg fatigue, and for wear after sclerotherapy.
        








 30-40 mmhg
        
extra firm graduated compression

          for severe varicose veins with heaviness, achiness, and fatigue, severe edema, after surgery or sclerotherapy, prophylaxis of thrombosis, prophylaxis/treatment of â€˜complications of varicose veins, etc..
        








 40-50 mmhg
        
strong graduated compression

          for lymphedema, severe chronic venous insufficiency, post phlebitis syndrome, chronic venous problems after surgery, and venous ulcers.
