        the cabarrus economic development corporation (cabarrus edc) makes a measurable impact on individuals and families through strategic partnerships that support local startups, existing businesses, and recruitment of new industry.    we serve as the lead economic development agency for cabarrus county and its municipalities. we offer competitive incentives, a talented workforce, a wealth of available resources, and more to help new and existing businesses thrive in cabarrus county.

                        why cabarrus?                    
while cabarrus county has deep roots in manufacturing that once supported a mighty textile industry, today those roots are producing new growth shoots in automotive, bioscience, and food production. it is a place where businesses of all kinds want to take root and the cabarrus economic development corporation is tending to each of these budding enterprises to give them the best environment in which to grow and thrive. get to know some of these businesses that are featured in this video and the services and programs they have used in our community.
