welcome to print-tech, inc.for almost 40 years, print-tech, inc. has been proudly providing print and related services to the southeastern michigan region, and beyond. our founding mission to be a partner that delivers superior customer service while honoring the budgets and timeframes of our clients remains unchanged, and our entire staff is committed to the process.
we offer digital, offset, and wide format printing, along with mailing and fulfillment services. we’d love the opportunity to collaborate on your next project so feel free to contact us whenever you’re ready to begin. you can use the request an estimate page or call us at 734-996-2345.
we look forward to the beginning of our next relationship!
