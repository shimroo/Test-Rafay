who we are?

we are a family based business run by the directors who have over 40 years combined experience. david came from management  background and hayley has been in real estate since she was 17 years old which is 30 years. has experience in all aspects of real estate. we can assist you with all of your real estate needs and service all areas of adelaide. we have properties in mount barker, glenelg, gawler and all surrounding suburbs



















what we do?

from property sales to property management, and tips on how to maximise your property potential and yield, we are the experts to guide you on your portfolio.



















why choose us?

we are landlords/home owners ourselves and understand the property market. hayley is known for her direct tough straight forward personality who fights for what is right and just. unlike other property managers who will not go the extra mile. 




























real estate appraisal

for over 20 years we has consistently delivered an unrivaled portfolio of properties to people who want a hassle free moving experience in and around the world.















premium quality

we are a “safe agent” and a member of the “my deposits” protected deposit scheme too, all of which gives you complete peace of mind.















friendly customer service

each member of our team is very experienced in the market and has exceptional local knowledge, providing a hassle free moving experience for our clients.
