past, present, future




r.r. donnelley started as a partner at a small printing company in chicago in 1864, but then grew his own company into one of the largest and most trusted service providers in the world.
with growth came expansion and in 2016, lsc communications llc was established to better serve publishers, brands, retailers and merchandisers by offering specialized solutions.
in 2020, atlas holdings acquired lsc communications llc. atlas operates a global family of manufacturing, distribution, service and trading businesses and, having operated in the printing and paper industry for decades, is the ideal partner for lsc. lsc is - and will continue to be - at the forefront of providing the best and most innovative solutions for storytellers around the world.
