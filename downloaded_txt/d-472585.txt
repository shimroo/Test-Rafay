services




make the most of your pet’s care.































      




veterinary services




at peachtree pets, we offer the highest quality veterinary healthcare and a wide range of pet wellness services from basic exams to more serious services like dentistry and surgery. everything is designed to keep your happy and healthy.




learn more
          




























      




dog boarding




overnight guests enjoy premier accommodations to make them feel right at home. our comfortable lodging options are designed to meet the unique needs of any canine, with dedicated rooms for small and senior dogs.




learn more
          




























      




dog daycare




between our 6 outdoor yards and our doggie climbing & sporting equipment, our dog daycare guests have fun all day 100% supervised. peachtree pets is a safe place for your dog to socialize & exercise while you’re away for the day.




learn more
          




























      




dog grooming




whether you choose our full groom or specialty salon services, our experienced groomers will leave your dog looking great. regular grooming keeps your dog looking & feeling fresh, but it also keeps him or her healthy!




learn more
          




























      




dog training




from obedience to puppy classes and even canine sports, our trainers have experience with dogs of all breeds and ages. let our skilled trainers bring the best out of your dog! choose from group or private classes in our dedicated, indoor training room.




learn more
          














































about peachtree pets




our goal here at peachtree pets is to provide the absolute best veterinary care, dog boarding, dog daycare, grooming and training for your pups in south atlanta. call us today to hear more about the beautiful facility, smiling staff, and wagging tails at peachtree pets!
