welcome to animal medical centre of medina!
animal medical centre of medina provides quality veterinary care for dogs, cats, birds, and pocket pets in medina, oh, and the surrounding area. our modern and inviting hospital boasts superb veterinarians and caring support staff.
as a full-service animal hospital, we offer the following:

telemedicine
preventive & wellness care (including vaccinations)
pet dental care
veterinary surgery
diagnostics
stem cell therapy
boarding & daycare
grooming
microchipping
and more veterinary services

please call (330) 722-5076 to schedule an appointment or book an appointment online.
