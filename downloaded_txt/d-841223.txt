patriot signage has produced signs for every united states president since 1992 and over 25% of all current u.s. senators.





since 1991 – political & commercial lawn signs and more
from the white house to the courthouse, candidates and their consultants have enjoyed great success with patriot yard signs. presidents, u.s. senators, house members, state legislators, judges, as well as city, county and township elected officials have all made patriot their first choice among campaign signs. the same is true in the commercial and non-profit fields with some of the nation’s largest and best known firms and organizations choosing us for their signage needs.
patriot signage is an award winning company for its leadership in promoting inclusion of individuals with disabilities.

since 2021 – beautifully printed artwork & photography through our newest division, lanta creative
lanta creative is available to the general public to upload and print photography and artwork on a wide range of materials, but also specializes in corporate programs providing memorable gifts for employee appreciation and/or client retention. the gift of a lanta creative print is an unforgettable keepsake.
