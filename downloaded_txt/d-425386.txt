conserving wildlife through outreach and education.animal ark is a 38 acre non-profit wildlife sanctuary located in the northern nevada foothills with a history as intriguing and exciting as its animal residents... 
















animal ark is closed for the winterjoin us in april for our spring reopening!



animal ark wildlife sanctuary is open seasonally april through november. due to our location, our opening dates are subject to change and based on weather conditions. please contact our guest services for opening dates and confirmation.
