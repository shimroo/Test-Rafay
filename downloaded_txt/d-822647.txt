25+ web hosting promotion price vs renewal price comparison 

web hosting companies like to show a very cheap price tag on their websites but sooner or later you will realize it is almost always for the most dummy plan and at a very looooong contract (usually 36 months, sometimes 48 months!). 
not many people are willing to pay for 36 months and not many people are dummies! they make it very difficult to know the real price until you click deep into their order page or deep into some hidden pages. some like to hide it at the bottom of the page. some never even care to show it …
i did all the dirty works and hunt down all the offer prices and renewal prices for 1 month, 12 months, 24 months and 36 months! you don’t need to do it yourself. i will try to update them as often as i can.
offer prices are shown in bold. they are always for the first payment only! renewal prices are shown in red (think blood). this is what you will need to pay after your honeymoon is over.
