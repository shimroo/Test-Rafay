welcome to the
village of elmwood park
located in cook county, elmwood park is about 10 miles west of the chicago loop and features a variety of parks, coffee shops, bars and lots of great eateries known as “restaurant row”. living in elmwood park has both a resident and urban suburban mix feeling with great public schools, public transportation, thriving small businesses, chains, fun local events & activities such as “the taste of elmwood park” featuring live music and amazing food.
