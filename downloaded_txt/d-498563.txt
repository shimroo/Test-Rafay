the san angelo association of realtors® (saar) empowers member success through consumer and member engagement, professional development and advocacy.




    learn more
  






























the san angelo association of  realtors® was founded in september  1926  as a professional trade association to assist  its members, who are licensed   texas real estate agents, brokers, and affiliates working within the real estate industry.

the san angelo association of realtors® exists to serve its members and defend private property rights for all. the organization promotes real estate professionalism by empowering members to practice ethically and successfully in a supportive environment.
