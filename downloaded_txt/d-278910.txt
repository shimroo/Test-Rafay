welcome to the normal theaterlocated in the heart of uptown normal, illinois, the historic normal theater is an authentically restored 1937 art moderne cinema offering a curated mix of vintage and contemporary films, including:classic cinemaart house filmscult favoritesinternational titlesin addition to our public programming, the normal theater is available for private film screenings, meetings, live performances and other special events. contact us to inquire.
