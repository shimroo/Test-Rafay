the mating gamebitter:sweet's intoxicating and playful debut artist album, the mating game, is a beautiful, 11-track work from shana halligan and kiran shahani (former supreme beings of leisure).songs unfold like little vignettes of romantic interludes and scenes from enchanting films of days gone by. shana's melodically sweet-and-sexy vocals paired with kiran's experimental pop beats and masterful programming make for an album that will stand the test of time. fans of portishead, zero 7, serge gainsbourg, everything but the girl, frou frou and bjork will fall in love with the album's sound.
