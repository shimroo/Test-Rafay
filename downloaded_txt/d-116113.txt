everything you need for your active outdoor lifestyle
if you'd rather be in the mountains right now and you love all the gear, footwear, and apparel that keeps you outside, you've come to the right place.
at campsaver you'll find hundreds of thousands of products that provide casual campers, outdoor enthusiasts, and expert adventurers with the right gear to enjoy a safe, comfortable life outdoors.
founded in 2003 out of a small utah state university apartment in logan, utah we stay true to the outdoor individual looking for the next adventure from a hike down the street to a multi-day backcountry trip we have the gear you need.
we are selective about the brands we carry, and we would never recommend something to you that we wouldn't use ourselves.
if you're new to campsaver, welcome. if you're a veteran campsaver gear junkie, welcome back. we're happy to be your home for outdoor gear.
