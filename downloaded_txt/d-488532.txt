making the differences that matter most



the mission of people services, inc. is to serve the local community, providing the delivery of quality services for people with disabilities.



people services, inc.people services inc. has been successfully providing services in lake county since 1981. people services, inc., is a private, non-for-profit california 501 (c) 3 corporation, organized to meet the individual needs of people with disabilities residing in lake county.people services is overseen by a nine-member, volunteer board of directors. people services networks within the community as an advocate for people with disabilities, focusing on community access, consumer rights, fostering independence, and economic self-sufficiency.an array of services are operated by people services, inc. these services are designed to meet the various needs, wants, and desires expressed by those we serve. we are consistently reviewing, enhancing, and changing our services as our service population expands. we are prepared with positive support strategies to meet any challenges that may arise.follow our links and pages and learn about us. contact us if you have any questions or suggestions. we are looking forward to having you help us make the next step.



making a difference since 1974















with a dream that began in 1974, and a company that was born in 1981, people services inc. has spent almost 50 years dedicated to a concept founded on the principle that people with disabilities were more than "special." people with disabilities are people with dreams, goals, feelings, and love. they are people of great value...just like all others who share in the benefits of our community. through the struggles of keeping pace with needs that change as the quality of life continues to be redefined, the vision has always been clear. the quality of our experience is securely embedded in the successes that are achieved by those who look to us for training, support, and advocacy. click here to learn more about who we are.
