veterans
since 1836, the general land office has offered benefits to texas veterans. today, we honor them through the veterans land board (vlb) with more benefits than any other state. 
reach out to the texas veterans call center: 1-800-252-8387. hours of operation: monday — friday, 7:30 a.m. – 5:30 p.m. ct. email: vlbinfo@glo.texas.gov

texas state veterans homes
we provide affordable long-term nursing care for qualified veterans, spouses, and gold star parents.











                  veterans
    
texas state veterans cemeteries
we maintain five state cemeteries where we offer exceptional committal services. these distinguished resting places are for texans who answered the call to serve our nation.
