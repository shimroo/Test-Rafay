larrymiltonmayorbranson, a fourth class missouri city, operates under the council/administrator form of government. the governing body consists of six aldermen elected from three wards (pdf), and a mayor elected at-large.the mayor and board of aldermen serve two-year
terms, and elections are held each april. the city
administrator is hired by the board of aldermen to be
the chief executive officer of the city.
