our tasting room
we offer seated and bar wine tastings on weekends in our tasting room, located less than ten minutes from downtown san luis obispo. reservations are suggested but we also accommodate walk-ins.















our wines
small production with an emphasis in pinot noir and chardonnay for over 29 years, stephen ross wines have won acclaim throughout the industry.
