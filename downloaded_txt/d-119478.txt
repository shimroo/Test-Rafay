welcome to the king’s family! we are a congregation in langley, british columbia,  canada, with a vision to serve jesus christ whom we worship as lord and  saviour. our desire is to see his government acknowledged in every area  of life and throughout the world over which he rules as king of kings.  we hope you enjoy your visit to this site.we are delighted to invite you to join our worship service on sunday morning at 10:00 am. it is a time when we worship god together as a community. we seek to preach the word of god faithfully each week. the children meet for king’s kid church during the regular service. we are always eager to welcome friends and visitors to our service and enjoy fellowship with one another.our worship service is available on live-stream for those unable to attend in person.
