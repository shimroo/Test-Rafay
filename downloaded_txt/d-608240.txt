 we offer hopewe offer hope to families when they don’t know where else to turn. whether we are addressing a one-time mental health crisis or an ongoing condition, we equip young people and their families with the right tools to understand their challenges, treat their conditions, and gain new skills in order to effectively manage their mental and behavioral health challenges. with increased knowledge and resources, they can realize their full potential.






















youth and families

we work shoulder to shoulder with each individual and their family, providing appropriate levels of treatment, consistency with treatment, and timely changes in levels of care. the stories of our clients and families inspire, motivate and humble us.




learn more











leadership

trillium’s leadership brings dedication, determination, and resilience to our work with youth, families and communities, never relinquishing hope and always striving to realize the potential clients and staff have within. 




learn more











our culture

we draw on our trauma-informed culture and commitment to equity and inclusion to make a difference, work within and not on community and be accountable at all levels and in all places.
