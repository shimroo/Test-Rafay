eating for cancer prevention program 


the middle brook regional health commission will be hosting an eating for cancer prevention program at the warren township court room on march 12th at 1:00pm						read on...







radon tests available in health department 


you can’t see, smell, or taste radon gas. it’s the 2nd leading cause of lung cancer - test your home for free today. you can get a free radon test kit from the warren township health department (there is a limited number of tests).						read on...







warren101 - understanding local government series 


mayor lontai & the warren township committee have announced the launch of the town’s new warren101 series, a series of informational sessions designed to give residents an inside look at the operations and responsibilities of various township departments.						read on...







junior membership program for warren boards & committees 


beginning this year, warren township will welcome junior members to its various boards and committees. local students in grades 8 through 12 are encouraged to apply for these unique opportunities to actively participate in local government.						read on...







town hall closed for presidents day 


warren town hall will be closed on monday, february 17 in observance of presidents day.

we will re-open on ?tuesday, february 18 at 8:30am.						read on...







somerset county- residential 2025 shredding schedule 


somerset county has announced the 2025 operation secure shred schedule!						read on...







warren 101 understanding local government: series 2 police department 


the warren police department will be the next session for warren 101 understanding local government.

the session will take place on wednesday, march 12th at 6:00pm at the police headquarters.						read on...







view the photo submissions for the show off your city photo contest 


the warren township committee would like to extend its gratitude to everyone who participated in the 2025 show off your city photo contest. we received a variety of stunning photographs that beautifully capture the charm and essence of warren township.						read on...
