 since 1947, the midwest city chamber of commerce is proud to serve as the premier advocate of our business community.the chamber values our strong relationship with tinker air force base and continues to host events and programs for our military families. education is a very important focus of the chamber because we know our youth are the future of our community.midwest city is an amazing place to live and visit.many wonderful people work together to promote our city as “a unique, patriotic community and a great place to call home.”
