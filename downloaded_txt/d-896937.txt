providing you with the best sound effects & production music  

sounddogs is a pioneer in providing online sound effects and production music resources. when the internet was barely available outside university and military circles and when post-production houses were still changing over to digital media, sounddogs took the leap forward and began the race to offer the best sound fx and background music to the audio industry, specifically with motion picture editors in mind. it was an incredible gamble that involved early collaborations with major sfx producers like toddao, skywalker, soundelux and soundstorm and with impressive clients like apple, microsoft, sun, avid, and digital river.

sounddogs became an early industry influencer and set the stage for other sound effect suppliers to chase them into the digital universe.

in addition to putting their entire sound effect catalog and production music library online, sounddogs has partnered with sound recordists, sound designers and music composers from all over the world; offering these talented sound professionals a platform to have their sound effects previewed, purchased, downloaded and used by major players in the film, television, and radio industries. sounddogs additional ability to license sound effects and royalty free music on behalf of their partners for commercial products makes sounddogs the ideal platform for industry professionals on both sides of the transaction.

within 5 years of the launch of the company, industry professionals from all over the world were using sounddogs to meet their sound effects and production music needs. when we launched a new version of sounddogs.com in 2016, our goals did not change: provide great customer service and immediate delivery of the best in sound effects and royalty free music imaginable. our customers trust us to help them get their job done so they can focus on what’s most important to them.

in 2019, with the sale of sounddogs to sound ideas, the legacy that sounddogs has built will continue to grow and prosper well into the future so that customers can continue to have access to the best sound effects and royalty free music available.
