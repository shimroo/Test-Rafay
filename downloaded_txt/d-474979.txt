news flash










congratulations to randy flamig, employee of the quarter! 


please join us in congratulating rawlins parks & recreation department recreation supervisor randy flamig on receiving employee of the fourth quarter for 2024! randy is a shining example of what our community needs for a successful recreation department.						read on...







special council guest aliyah 


we had a special guest at city council! aliyah, on the rawlins elementary school student council, opened the council meeting with the pledge! she also joined the city manager and city attorney during the council meeting. thanks for joining us!						read on...







city of rawlins moment of silence 


the city of rawlins extends our sincere condolences to the families, friends, and all affected by the tragic losses of christopher william johnson and quentin j. romero. the city of rawlins observed a moment of silence at council yesterday in remembrance.						read on...







february utility insert 


check out the february utility insert, which includes information on open positions, recreation events in february, and more!						read on...







information about new fema floodplain in rawlins 


the city of rawlins has updated our fema floodplain website at www.rawlinswy.gov/fema with extensive information regarding the proposed floodplain. we will also be mailing a letter next week directly to properties we believe may fall into the floodplain.						read on...







prevent frozen pipes with these tips 


with the cold on it's way, don't forget to take steps now to prevent frozen pipes. it's time to make sure your house and pipes are warm and cozy no matter the temperature outside.						read on...
