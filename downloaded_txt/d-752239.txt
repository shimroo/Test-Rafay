our story




incredible views all around
residents love our views of the new york city skyline. from our higher floors and rooftop deck, you can see all the way to the empire state building and hudson river. and closer to the ground? you’ll look out at tree lined streets and classic new york architecture.











upper west side style
historic architecture. world-renowned cultural institutions. amazing access to central park. it’s no surprise that the upper west side is one of the most coveted neighborhoods in the world. amidst the charm and convenience of 89th street, you’ll find the sagamore, an established apartment community that lays the best of this classic neighborhood at your feet.
