 spend three seasons at the augusta country club













as spring unfolds, our golf course and har-tru tennis courts are available for members to enjoy. our lounge offers beverages and a pub menu, allowing members to relax and reconnect after a long maine winter.













once summer is underway, our golf course, tennis courts, clubhouse and waterfront come alive with tournaments, events and activities. from golf and tennis instruction to summer beach parties, there is fun for all!













as the club winds down for fall, enjoy breathtaking views of vibrant foliage and lake cobbosseecontee. the golf course, tennis courts and clubhouse stay alive as the days grow shorter and the nights cooler.














 let us host your event!our full-service clubhouse boasts a newly renovated dining room, pub and lounge. you’ll discover friendly, attentive staff who are ready to accommodate any type of special event: from weddings to corporate events and anything in between.click here for more information, or contact our events manager at info@augustacountryclub.org.
