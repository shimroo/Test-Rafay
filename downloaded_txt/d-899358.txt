saturday, october 25, 2008



	 
	 marilyn anderson-champion of the arts
	 
    
           marilyn anderson, musical and voice instructor at both cal lutheran university and moorpark college, will be honored with "the champion of the arts" award by the high street theater foundation at a brunch on saturday, november 15, 2008, 11:00 am to 2:00 pm, at the secret garden restaurant in moorpark, ca. we are proud of her achievements, as we know cal lutheran and moorpark college are. we hope that you want to help us celebrate her achievements.the high street theater foundation is operated by citizens who have a passion for the performing arts. organized in 2005, we are dedicated to offer quality performance arts programs for youth by conducting workshops that teach theater arts to youth ages 8 to 18. we are conducting a theater workshop for the youth, (our fifth such workshop) in conjunction with the boys and girls club of moorpark, from the second week in january to the second week in march, 2009. the culmination of the workshop will be a presentation of disney's "cinderella kids" featuring the youths who have participated in the workshop. proceeds from the brunch will help support the workshop. the foundation is a 501 c3 non-profit corporation in the state of california. if you have any questions please call.ralph mauriello; secretaryhigh street theater foundation805 529-7580
