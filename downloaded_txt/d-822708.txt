what we do



                     savedaily is an api-based financial services technology firm 
                  


                     we enable financial and non-financial institutions to deliver leading digital investment savings solutions.
                  























                                 investors
                              


                                 would be investors often cannot attain sound investment offerings suitable for their investment experience.
                              















                                 financial institutions
                              


                                 the mass market segment has been historically difficult for advisors to
                                 transition from an advisor based solution to a technology based one.
                              

























                                 the savedaily solution
                              


                                 the savedaily digital investment savings platform enables institutions to deliver leading innovative savings and investment propositions under their own brand that can be efficiently deployed and maintained.
                              




















offerings


                     flexible savings and investing programs
                  


                     the savedaily platform offers 8 financial services technology modules that can be tailored to various demographic segments.
                  











                        health savings accounts
                     


                        corporations can offer their employees a mechanism to handle medical expenses while providing opportunities for financial growth.
                     










                        credit card overlay programs
                     


                        offerings that allow individuals to save while spending on everyday purchases using their existing credit cards and debit cards.
                     










                        robo-advisor solutions
                     


                        artificial intelligence backed robo-advisor based investment services designed to improve investors digital experiences.
                     










                        technology reporting engine
                     


                        web-based dashboards to monitor investor data points and to integrate all partner service offerings on a 360 degree basis.
                     










                        pension offering
                     


                        branded employer sponsored fully automated and electronic pension offerings with or without advisors.
                     










                        high-net worth
                     


                        access to high-value investment opportunities and discretionary advising services for the largest of private bank clients.
                     










                        mass affluent
                     


                        fully automated and electronic advisory based offerings, suitable for virtually all client asset levels and experience levels.
                     










                        insurance wrapped 
                     


                        insurance based products delivered alongside investment based products for the convenience of all investors.
