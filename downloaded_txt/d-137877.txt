 booking guidelines 








our basic economy fares are non-refundable and non-changeable, and seat assignments are not provided until check-in. these tickets are not eligible for paid or complimentary upgrade options. review the basic economy product details and restrictions on our website. 





 seating and in-flight amenities 








our seat configuration and amenities vary depending on the aircraft type and size. you can read more about our seat products and onboard experience to ensure you have all the information you need for a comfortable journey. 





 baggage policies and fees 








our baggage fees vary based on the travel destination. for domestic travel within the united states, there is a fee for the first checked bag and a fee for the second checked bag. for travel between the united states and canada, the fees are the same. for travel between the united states and various international destinations, fees range depending on the destination.  for more details and assistance, call 1-888-652-3933
