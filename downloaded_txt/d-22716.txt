welcome to santiam christian



we take our mission seriously. here at santiam christian, our primary purpose is to provide a christ-centered academic environment emphasizing academic excellence, christian character, responsible citizenship, and service to others.if our mission could be a great fit for your family, please consider applying now for the 2024-25 school year.

explore sc



santiam christian is a unique educational environment. each of our grade levels provide opportunities for students to thrive in a strong and uplifting community.  every day we strive to balance overall academic rigor with individual accomplishment, christian world-view with cultural influence, and our focus on community and family with the needs of each one.
