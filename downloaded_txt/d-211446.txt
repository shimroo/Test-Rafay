when the sun is blazing, and the summer gets hot, the race is on to water country. located in portsmouth, new hampshire, new england’s largest water park has plenty of fun and adventure for everyone. kick back or kick it up on 18 family-friendly water attractions, including classics like the giant wave pool and adventure river.  from endless splashes at tahiti tree house to sharing your favorite dippin’ dots, water country lets you get as close to your family as you want to be this summer.
