welcome to sebastian riding associates



equine assisted service programs for children and adults



at sra, we improve the quality of lives, one stride at a time through horse and human interactions. we provide an inclusive and nurturing environment for individuals living with varied disabilities to thrive. as a path (professional association of therapeutic horsemanship) center, our certified therapeutic riding instructors with the help of our amazing equine team, develop a program designed specifically to meet the needs and goals of each rider. these needs include physical benefit, cognitive function, social development and emotional wellness. we look forward to determining which of our programs is best suited to meet your needs.

horses and humans



we can’t wait to introduce you to our amazing team! 



what makes our program so special is our professional and caring staff, dedicated volunteers and a barn full of hand-picked, specially trained horses.   our staff includes physical therapists and instructors who bring diverse skills, training and education to the program.  our equine team offers a unique ability to influence their riders in profound ways.  together this team uses creative techniques to help our students be successful.
