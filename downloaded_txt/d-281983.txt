welcome to sherwoods spirit of americawe are santa fe's premier gallery, specializing in native american artifacts and historic americana. we offer the finest examples available from every corner of america - from the northwest coast, eastern woodlands and great basin to the great plains.whether you are a serious, accomplished collector or just starting on your collecting journey, we are sure you will find something of interest to you! we are pleased and proud to share these marvelous collectibles with you.
