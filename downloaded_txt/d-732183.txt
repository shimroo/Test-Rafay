welcome to southern rv
southern rv is a rv dealership serving the greater atlanta area with locations in cumming, ga and mcdonough, ga proudly serving the needs of recreationalists near and far. southern rv offers a large selection fifth wheels, travel trailers, motorhomes and more from great brands like jayco, forest river ,wildwood, rockwood, cruiser rv, scamper, sunset trail, crossroads, zinger, drv, thor, venture rv as well as a selection of certified pre-owned and used rv models. southern rv has full sales, service and parts departments with knowledgeable and experienced staff to accommodate your needs. our service department offers rv repair, paint and body work, generator repair, tire replacement, appliance repair and all forms of rv maintenance.  with 15 bays, a crew of certified rv technicians and over 44 years of experience, we're your local trusted rv service and repair center  come see us monday through saturday or call us at 1-800-633-9940 and let us exceed your rv needs! see for yourself why southern rv is one of the top rv dealers in ga.
