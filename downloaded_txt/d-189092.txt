a leading national voice on diversity, inclusion & talent development in the entertainment & media industries




    learn more about our work
  














the national hispanic foundation for the arts seeks to create a world where latino voices and perspectives are fully represented and celebrated in all aspects of the entertainment, media, and telecommunications industries. we envision a future where latino students, artists, writers, performers, and filmmakers have equal access to opportunities and resources, and where their contributions are recognized and valued as essential to the cultural landscape.Â through our advocacy, education, and talent cultivation, we strive to empower latino students and creators and inspire diverse audiences to embrace the richness and vitality of our community's art and culture.
