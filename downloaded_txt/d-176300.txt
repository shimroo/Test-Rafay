since 1954, laconi has been introducing library staff to new technologies and ideas, best practices, and popular programs for children and adults. laconi is comprised of seven unique sections which offer programs designed to enhance skills and knowledge of library staff in their area of expertise.














upcoming eventslaconi offers a variety of continuing education and networking events, from webinars to field trips -all free to staff whose libraries hold institutional membership. browse events with our calendar or by visiting section pages. we hope to see you at an event soon!

 

























membership directorymembership in laconi by your library supports the work we do. check this list to ensure your library is a member.




    member libraries
  

 

get involvedlaconi is always seeking new members to join a section. consider joining to get valuable committee experience, boost your resume, network, and run the next great educational program for librarians.
