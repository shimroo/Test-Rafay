welcome 
the apex chamber
 
the apex chamber of commerce leads the way for both businesses and communities in the town of apex.
founded in 1958, the chamber has an engaged board of directors, motivated volunteers and a dedicated professional staff poised to implement progressive programs that aid in the profitability of its members, build a better economic community, develop future leaders and enhance small business development and growth.
