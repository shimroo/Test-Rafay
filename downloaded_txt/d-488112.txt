page content​welcome to toronto rehab, a global leader in physical and cognitive rehabilitation and home to 
   the kite research institute. our interdisciplinary teams are dedicated to helping patients recover from injury, illness, or age-related conditions and are passionate about improving the health and well-being of patients and their families. it's our shared commitment to serve our communities, propel our field forward and help patients overcome physical and cognitive limitations, to regain strength, resiliency and independence in their daily lives.toronto rehab is home to five physical sites: bickle centre, lyndhurst centre, rumsey neuro, rumsey cardiac and university centre.
