about pernod ricard new zealandpernod ricard new zealand is one of the country’s leading spirits and champagne companies. we manage a prestigious portfolio of premium to luxury brands, including absolut vodka, malfy gin, chivas regal, jameson irish whiskey, martell cognac, malibu liquor, and g.h.mumm and perrier-jouët champagnes.pernod ricard new zealand works in close partnership with consumers, retailers, wholesalers, hotels, restaurants and bars to provide innovative products that meet the needs of the market.
