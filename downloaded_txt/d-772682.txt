hotels and serviced apartments

                        centre point hotels are the proud legacy of one of thailand's top hospitality chains. 
                        a select portfolio of premier hotels, serviced apartments and luxury residences has been created, 
                        all situated in prime locations, including centre point bangkok, other hotels in bangkok city centre, pattaya and korat. 
                        
                        catering to travellers and longer-term visitors alike, our clients are often searching for a uniquely thai experience 
                        and we strive to give them the experience of a lifetime staying with us. among the plethora of hotels in central bangkok,
                        centre point hotels represent the very top of the tree and have duly earned a long-established reputation for excellence.

our brand


















            accommodation in any centre point thailand hotel or serviced apartment will be spacious and elegant. we like to feature a mixture of traditional thai and contemporary architectural appointments, and each room, whether a hotel in bkk or a luxury serviced hotel apartment in bangkok, comes with a comprehensive range of facilities. 
            
            you can rely on any centre point hotel to stand out among other luxury hotels in bangkok city centre, and we also proudly offer serviced hotel apartments in bangkok. each location apartment offers a range of top-tier facilities, including outdoor salt swimming pools, fitness centres, saunas, children’s playrooms, recreation rooms, internet corners, conference rooms, mini marts, beauty salons, and much more. we genuinely believe that no other bangkok thailand hotels can compete with our standards, facilities, and attention to detail. 
            
            we hope you will visit us soon at one of our luxury locations and find out for yourself. whether you seek an oasis of calm retreat hidden away in the most bustling, vibrant city on the planet or a more traditional beach-based experience, we have a centre point hotel to suit your every need.
