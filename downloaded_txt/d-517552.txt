welcome to ripfest 



























ripfest summer camp
indiana diving academy presents ripfest, the world's premiere diving camp. join us under our olympic coach, john wingfield. it will be a dive camp experience unlike any other. 




                        ripfest summer camp info
                      












recfest summer camp
while ripfest is known for its diving camp and club. this year we will be offering something brand new called recfest. a recreation camp with canoeing, horseback riding, and more. not to mention a summer to remember. 




                        recfest summer camp info
                      












ripfest diving club
ripfest offers clubs and full-time training.areas that we offer club: noblesville, westfield/zionsville, hamilton southeastern, mooresville, carmel, and north central.
