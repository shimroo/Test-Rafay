debary's 2024 hometown heroes 


debary’s hometown heroes program celebrates and remembers men and women who have served or are currently serving in the armed forces and have a special connection to the city. meet debary's 2024 hometown heroes!						read on...







household hazardous waste day is april 12th! 


spring cleaning? safely dispose of hazardous materials during debary's household hazardous waste day! stop by city hall on saturday, april 12, 2025, from 8:00 am to 12:00 pm, to responsibly get rid of hazardous household items.						read on...







tnr program: helping our community cats 


debary’s tnr program helps manage our feral cat population in a humane way. volunteers trap, neuter, vaccinate, and return cats to their colonies, where they’re cared for by residents. want to help? join us in making a difference for our feline friends!						read on...
