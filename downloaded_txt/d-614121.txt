                why choose capital markets insight?




capital markets insight is a trusted, authoritative provider of information on the global bond, loan and equity capital markets, with a specialized focused on new issuance – from rumour to after-market performance.
we deliver market intelligence and analysis through a variety of channels, including print, desktop (both standalone and on workspace) and feed products, as well as frequent events such as roundtables and conferences














solutions



                capital markets insight's three product families




















international financing review (ifr)

leading fixed income, capital markets, and investment banking news and commentary - real time, online, in print, and on workspace.


























lpc

real-time and historical news, comprehensive data, and expert analysis on the global syndicated loan market - online, via interactive databases and in print.


























project finance international (pfi)

news, data, and commentary on the international energy and infrastructure financing markets.
