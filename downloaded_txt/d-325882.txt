city announcements
 






  
 















city offices closed - presidents day 


city offices will be closed on monday, february 17, 2025, in observance of presidents day. trash collection will not be impacted by the holiday.						read on...







city council to consider trash contract extension 


the city is negotiating a five-year extension to the solid waste program with republic services, which is scheduled to be considered for approval at the city council meeting on monday, february 24, 2025.						read on...







city considers 2021 building codes for adoption 


the city of creve coeur is considering adopting the 2021 building code, and the public is encouraged to share feedback on the draft code ordinance during the 90-day review period.						read on...







nominations open for creve coeur business awards 


the creve coeur economic development committee is seeking nominations for the 2025 creve coeur business awards, a program that recognizes creve coeur businesses and organizations for their achievements and commitment to the community.			additional info...







triennial resident survey results now available 


the city of creve coeur conducted its triennial resident survey, mailed to randomly selected households during fall 2024 as part of the city’s continued effort to assess citizen satisfaction with city services and to obtain input on key issues.						read on...







heart to heart 5k/10k run & walk 


join us for the 19th annual heart to heart 5k/10k run/walk! this family-friendly 5k and 10k run will be held at millennium park at 7:30 a.m. on saturday, june 7, 2025.			additional info...







arbor day celebration & art contest 


celebrate arbor day with us on sunday, april 27, 2025, at millennium park with a free sapling giveaway, tree planting demonstration, activities for kids, educational displays, display of art contest entries, and a performance by babaloo music & fun!			additional info...







creve coeur popular annual financial report available for fiscal year 2024 


as part of an ongoing effort to inform the creve coeur community about the state of the city's finances, the city is pleased to present the 2024 popular annual financial report (pafr).						read on...
