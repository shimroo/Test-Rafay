blended learning



"blended learning is about creating a more personalized and differentiated learning experience that meets the needs of every student."
- grant wiggins


"in een blended learning omgeving verschuift de rol van docenten van kennisoverdracht naar begeleiding en het faciliteren van het leerproces. docenten worden meer coaches en mentoren voor studenten, waarbij ze ondersteuning bieden en feedback geven"
- fontys onderwijsontwikkeling


"ai is commonly billed as a tool to personalize learning experiences, but education remains a collective and social endeavor. schools are places where children socialize and learn to live together, and ai should support this process rather than replace it. the technology must be steered by human agency to ensure it serves educational goals effectively."
- unesco
