welcome to mill ridge farm



a lexington, kentucky thoroughbred horse farm and nursery



mill ridge farm, founded by alice chandler in 1962, has a rich history of raising and selling thoroughbred racehorses. today, her legacy shines through in all aspects of the farm, which is led by her son, headley bell, and grandson, price bell. since 2000, mill ridge farm has bred, raised, and/or sold 39 grade 1 winners. learn more here.
