exclusively available, worldwide, by postal subscription and published quarterly in january, april, july and october each year, health & strength contains features from the "golden era" of physical culture and mixes them with current content.health & strength always endeavours to uphold the traditions of physical culture - a sound mind in a healthy body, "sacred thy body even as thy soul"with regular contributions from david gentle (muscleaneous), gino edwards (johnny fitness), matt bembridge (the gym professor), hannah bryant, peter yates and many more.published in the united kingdom.
