journal archives - print issues 2003 through 2017
 


















all print issues of the journal of enviornmental management arizona, beginning with feb/mar 2003 through june/july 2017, are available below for download as a pdf. click on the issue picture to view a larger image of the cover; click on the pdf icon to download the pdf. if you are searching for a particular article or author in a past print issue, note that google has cataloged much of this material and can often help you find the issue you need.

 
 the journal of environmental management arizona ended publication after 15 years as a print-based bi-monthly publication, and one year as an online publication.
i thank our readers, contributors, and advertisers for your many years of participation and support for the journal. this journal website will be maintained for your convenience as an archive of our 15+ years of articles, columns, photos, advertisements, and other content. you can find all past issues on this page below.
												
												this website will also soon host past episodes of our video online program, "environmental management.tv", which aired online in 2010.  a link will be made available here to access these videos. 
feel free to contact me if you have any questions or need any information.thank you!sincerely,jim thrush, m.s., publisher & editor jimthrush@cox.net
