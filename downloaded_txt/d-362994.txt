senior citizen tax assistance & benefits 


for senior citizens who qualify for the missouri "circuit breaker" program, free trash service can be obtained by furnishing city hall with a copy of your 2024 form mo-ptc or form mo-pts tax return.						read on...







missouri american water update 


missouri american water has completed the construction of the new water line extension, and it has been fully inspected and tested.						read on...







allenton bridge permanently closed 


the allenton bridge is permanently closed.
the closure is for safety reasons, as the bridge failed its annual structural inspection performed by the missouri department of transportation.						read on...
