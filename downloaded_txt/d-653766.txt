time spent in a saddle is never wasted
























horseback riding
located minutes from winnipeg, manitoba, canada, miracle ranch is the perfect western setting for group, family, and individual horseback riding fun! come make memories with friends & horses - rain or shine or snow.



book a ride





























riding lessons
our skilled instructors will help boost your confidence while enhancing your english, western or jumping horsemanship skills. learn how to work as a team with your horse in our indoor arena using your seat, weight and rein as natural aids.



schedule riding lesson





























horse boarding
at miracle ranch, we pride ourselves on offering a comfortable environment. all of our horse boarders enjoy individual care, clean horse stalls, and quality feed. our knowledgeable staff focus on providing exceptional care for your horse. you’re in good company when you stay with us.
