nfl way to play
nfl way to play is football's practical development model that applies science, data, video, and peer to peer experience to protect players from unnecessary risk, enhance global development, and foster culture change to attain and preserve on-field excellence at all levels of the game.

play football
the nfl celebrates youth and high school football year-round. through camps, activities, nfl flag and tackle leagues, the play football initiative provides a fun and engaging experience for players of all ages and skill levels, equipping young people with key life skills that are building blocks to success on and off the field.

thinking of starting a youth flag football league? nfl flag makes it easy. with nfl flag, you get dedicated support in setting up your league, the credibility of the country’s most reputable flag football organization, access to free marketing resources to grow your league, and the freedom to set up and run your league according to the needs of your community. you can also participate in nfl flag regional tournaments and championships, attend the annual nfl flag summit for league organizers, and get support from the nfl team and other nfl flag league organizers. 
learn more on the nfl flag website.
