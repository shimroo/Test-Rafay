faq



















            where do i park?
          









the rep does not have a parking facility.  metered parking is available on most nearby city streets and is free after 8pm.  the nearest parking garage is on the corner of hanover and high street (at the end of bow street).  we try to start our shows on time, so please give yourself plenty of time to find parking.click here for full directions and parking details.








            can i bring food/drink into the theatre?
          









the rep has a full bar with concessions in our lobby.  patrons are welcome to enjoy snacks and drinks in the lobby or at their seats in the theatre.








            how do i donate?
          









thank you for considering making a donation to the rep!  you can donate to the rep at the box office or bar.  you can also donate online by clicking here.
