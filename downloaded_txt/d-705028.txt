welcome to the tattler site, home of the current and all past issues of the conclave weekly newsletter! (fyi - the namesake for the site's url is main street, the music/radio industry company that has served as a close partner - and former coordinator - of the conclave.)
the conclave, as you may know, is a non-profit educational organization that has been serving the industry for over 36 years. this site serves as the library for the tattler, a weekly emailed newsletter featuring news and views of the industry, with an emphasis on the comings and goings of the fine folks who serve as the foundation of the conclave learning conference.
plan to be in attendance at the 37th learning conference: july 18-20, 2012 at the doubletree park place hotel in minneapolis! to learn more about the conclave, visit the conclave.
