shark club
at shark club, we’re fans of good food, great drinks, and, of course, sports. from burgers to salads, beer to hand-crafted cocktails, mma to golf, we have something for everyone.
so, whether you’re out to celebrate with friends, on a date, or even watch the game, make shark club your home base.



your safety is our priority, and we want you to be confident that we will welcome everyone safely and responsibly. you can learn more about our commitment to keeping our team and guests safe here.
shark club is open every day for indoor and patio dining, plus pick-up and delivery at select locations. click here to find a location near you and their hours of operation.
