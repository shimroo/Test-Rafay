wednesday, october 08, 2008





new set o' wheels




long time no post. in a bid to avoid the whole jon beast thing to be mean with the old go juice and because i've remembered what trains are for in the last few months, i've joined the pct's bit of the cycle scheme.  so having recieved the requisite voucher yesterday and nipped to picton cycles, i am now the fattest and unfittest cyclist in town.  so if you see a puffing heap of fluorescent yellow it'll be me.

cycled to the station at hunts cross this morning via ducky pond, past a moorhen and heron (both looked down their beaks at me) and arrived there breathing heavily.  onto the train and off at brunswick and onto the bike and into work.  latter bit felt a bit easier.  truth be told i'm suprised how unfit i am.
