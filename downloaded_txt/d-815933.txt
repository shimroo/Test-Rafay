a house of prayer for all people














welcome to the community church of new york unitarian universalist!



we have served as a home for liberal religious community, justice-based values, and freedom of expression in the heart of manhattan since 1825. our mission is to grow as a caring, justice-making, anti-racist, diverse, spiritual community.
















sunday service


























join us for weekly worship services at: 209 madison ave. & 35th st., new york, ny 10016(inside the church of the incarnation) and on our youtube channel sunday at 2:00 pm.click here to view our stream!click here to view this sunday’s order of service!in addition to weekly worship services, we offer an array of weekly and monthly programs, as well as special events.visit our calendar page for more information about programming!
