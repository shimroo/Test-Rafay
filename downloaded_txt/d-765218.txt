restoring our waterways



connected waterways help create complete and functioning ecosystems. our dedication to our local watersheds helps our entire region thrive.

protecting our landscape



we work to ensure smart, sustainable management of natural areas to preserve ecosystems for wildlife, recreation, human health, and local economies.

supporting our communities

huron pines project dashboard



we are committed to conserving and enhancing northern michigan’s natural resources to ensure healthy water, protected places and vibrant communities. the dashboard below showcases our projects and initiatives which support this mission.this interactive tool represents projects we have completed since 2014 or are currently pursuing across the region. use it to explore initiatives in land protection, ecosystem restoration, community engagement and more. projects can be filtered by category, region or year.if you have questions or would like more information about a specific project, please email us at info@huronpines.org.
