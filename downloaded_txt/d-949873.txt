city council meetings

the eveleth city council currently meets the 1st and 3rd tuesday of each month at 2:00 p.m. at the eveleth city hall, council chambers.
*beginning march of 2025, the eveleth city council meetings will be held on the 1st and 3rd mondays of each month at 5:30 p.m. at the eveleth city hall.
