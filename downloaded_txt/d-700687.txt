some essentilal links for troika enthusiasts:


 
 


 
- troikapedia - buy the book 'troika 63-83' published to celebrate the 50th anniversary of the start of troika and written by ben harris and lawrence illsley. it contains details of the troika staff including decorators and their marks. the staff dates are taken from the actual troika company records. they also have a troikapedia facebook page. 


 
 


 
- troika pottery on facebook - a great resource for collectors.  


 
 


 
- troika cafe - a useful forum for enthusiasts.


 
 


 
- troika pottery collector's club - this was created by experts who will verify your troika pots. you can then register items on the web site for free! each one is given a unique registration number. this is crucial  if you wish to sell them later because potential buyers can check  the web site to see if the pot is genuine.
