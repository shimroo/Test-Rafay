beach  education  advocates  for  culture,   health, environment and safety foundation institute b.e.a.c.h.e.s.a 501(c) (3) nonprofit tax-exempt corporation

 

visiona world where naturists are respected for their body-positive culture, reverence for nature and protection of the environment; a world where naturist families are free to recreate without clothing within designated areas on public beaches and lands without fear of arrest or harassment.

 

missionto create designated areas on north american public beaches for safe, well-managed, family friendly clothing-optional recreation supported by on-site naturist beach stewardship organizations in a mutually cooperative partnership with members of government, business, law enforcement and the local community.
