the edgewater inn is charlevoix’s premiere, waterfront condominium-hoteljust a short walk to charlevoix’s finest dining, beaches, shopping and galleries.an ideal locationedgewater inn is home to 60 one- and two-bedroom units for rent. each unit is privately owned and decorated according to the owner’s style and preferences, yet every condo has all the amenities to feel like home. with online booking, you can check out each unit to make the selection that suits your needs.
a short walk from downtown charlevoix, overlooking round lake and the pine river channel, edgewater inn has everything you need for a comfortable stay. enjoy views of the drawbridge, and be close to downtown for restaurants, taprooms, wineries, beaches, shopping, and galleries. it is the perfect weekend getaway or long vacation.
book a lakeview unit with a fireplace and have flowers delivered with our romance package for your wedding night. we are happy to accommodate your entire wedding party.
