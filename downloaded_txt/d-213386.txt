welcome to boats unlimited! we offer a variety of marine products and our selection is second to none. boats unlimited prides itself on having the friendliest and most knowledgeable staff in north carolina. it is our goal to help our customers find the perfect watercraft to fit their lifestyle, along with just the right parts and accessories. check out our showroom to see what products we offer. if you need help in making your selection, feel free to call or stop in—we're always ready to serve you.
boats unlimited is family-owned and operated and has two locations in north carolina. boats unlimited can provide you with the latest and best in marine products to make your outdoor boating experience more enjoyable. we specialize in offering the latest innovations in boating technology. we combine a wide variety of products with a friendly and knowledgeable staff to meet all of your boating needs. our goal is to make boats unlimited your one-stop dealership for sales, service, and fun!
