serving the bay area for over 30 years
we are a custom fabricator of high-end architectural products for commercial and residential use. these products include stairs, railings, and other state-of-the-art high-tech metal products. our team of craftsmen are driven to provide superior workmanship using quality materials and components. we deliver our unique products under any conditions and we will meet even the most difficult dead-lines. we also provide 25 year cslb licensed structural steel fabrication & erection (c51), shop & portable welding (c-60), ornamental and misc steel (c-23) and glazing-glass installation (c-17). all railing, including handicap railing and ramp railing are ada (americans with disabilities act) compliant. we are astm & aws certified and have been in business in the san francisco bay area for over 30 years.
