welcome to all-inclusive by marriott bonvoy®
     





immerse yourself on a vacation like no other. from the pristine beaches of barbados and jamaica to the lush landscapes of mexico and costa rica, all-inclusive by marriott bonvoy® connects you to the most sought-after experiences in the most coveted destinations. 
whether you're exploring with a partner, family, and friends, or on your own, let us cultivate authentic moments that match your unique travel style. at our best all-inclusive resorts, you'll go deeper into your destination while uncovering local culture through food, art, music, and more.

an exceptional adults-only resort experience on antigua's beautiful dickenson bay. enjoy poolside relaxation, hand-mixed cocktails, vibrant shows, and rejuvenating treatments at the royal spa. with royalton fit keeping you energized, every moment at royalton chic antigua is unforgettable. experience paradise with spectacular sunsets and endless celebrations.
