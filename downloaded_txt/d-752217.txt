library spotlights


what's happening at the library
 






  
 
 















citizens invited to ‘avoiding fraud’ forum feb. 26 


on wednesday, feb. 26, from 5:30-7 p.m., local experts will partner with the stillwater public library to present a free forum on common types of fraud and how community members can protect themselves and their assets.						read on...







‘masquerade at the museum’ invites teens to explore art, literature and culture 


the stillwater public library and the osu museum of art (osumoa) are hosting a free “masquerade at the museum” party for teens in grades 6-12 on friday, feb. 28, from 5:30-7:30 p.m.						read on...







‘library lovers readathon’ celebrates reading, books and libraries feb. 14 


the stillwater public library is introducing a new annual valentine’s day celebration for community members who love reading and the library. the “library lovers readathon” features locals reading aloud the book passages they love.						read on...







securely dispose of documents at the library’s annual drive-thru shred event 


the stillwater public library and shred-away shredding are holding their annual drive-thru paper shredding event on saturday, feb. 22, from 9-11 a.m. the service is free, but the library does accept donations to help cover costs.						read on...







library is open, north building closed 


the library is replacing its fire suppression system starting december 16. this project, planned in three phases, will affect the library, its services, and its users for approximately 4-6 months.			additional info...







registration open for dolly parton imagination library! 


dolly parton's imagination library is a book gifting program that mails free, high-quality books to children from birth to age five.						read on...







stay connected! 


subscribe to notify me to receive updates and information about community news and happenings.						read on...
