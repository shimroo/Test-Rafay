    testimonials

   
  "being a member of the chaldean american chamber of commerce has provided me with endless networking opportunities. additionally, the chamber’s advocacy efforts have been instrumental in addressing industry-specific challenges and ensuring our concerns are heard at the local level. the chamber’s dedication to supporting businesses within the chaldean community makes it an essential ally for anyone looking to thrive in this sector."
salam bakkal, 13 greenfield bp gas station


 
  "the chaldean american chamber of commerce in michigan serves as an outstanding model of a remarkable community collaborator. the chamber's commitment to fostering economic development and offering valuable assistance to local businesses is commendable, consistently surpassing expectations in their support for their members. whether it involves arranging educational seminars, hosting networking gatherings, or championing the interests of local businesses, they consistently demonstrate a willingness to go above and beyond. their unwavering dedication to the prosperity and advancement of their members is genuinely motivating."
cam barrett, the kroger co.


 
  "being a member of the chamber has been an amazing experience for our chaldean and neighboring communities. it goes beyond being just an organization; it serves as a vital connection that upholds unwavering integrity and dedication. the chamber's team is truly outstanding, demonstrating excellence in everything they undertake. their dedication to our mission not only motivates but also uplifts our communities to reach new milestones."
zaid elia, elia group


 
  “the chaldean american chamber of commerce is a world class organization benefiting the community and its members.  our organization has continued to grow its involvement every year due to the value provided.  whether it is through networking events, educational opportunities or access to the decision makers in our community, the chaldean american chamber is a go-to organization.”
andy gutman, farbman group


 
  “the chamber has unified and synergized the chaldean business community in such a way that allows the business community to feed off itself and grow at a more rapid pace than ever.  it offers meaningful relationships for its members that they may not have otherwise ever been available, and these opportunities continue to emerge with increased involvement.”
justin hanna, taft law llp


 
  “i am consistently impressed by the quality of resources offered by the chaldean chamber of commerce.  the chamber offers its members and the community a plethora of growth and networking opportunities.  the west bloomfield community is enhanced by having this superior agency providing services to businesses and individuals.”
steven kaplan, j.d., west bloomfield township supervisor (elected)


 
  "as a businessperson in our community, the chaldean american chamber of commerce is invaluable. from the moment we joined, we were welcomed into a network of like-minded entrepreneurs and professionals who genuinely care about each other's career successes. the leadership and commitment to educating members on industry topics supports our growth. the chamber's events have also been key for networking and forging meaningful connections with other businesses and potential clients."
danny marogy, united wholesale mortgage


 
  "the chamber has not only been a huge asset through networking and building relationships in the community, but they also helped resolve a matter which more than paid off my membership for a lifetime! they host excellent events such as the awards dinner and golf outings which i look forward to every year. the staff is wonderful, and they serve a dedicated mission to the community."
matthew loussia, value wholesale


 
  “joining the chaldean american chamber of commerce has been a joy for both myself and for our business. the chamber offers many opportunities to get to know one another, build relationships and connect in our community. as an automotive dealer in oakland county, we have been able to connect with many individuals and businesses on a personal level to provide them with a meaningful and dedicated experience, just as the chamber staff and members have done for us over the years.”
carol peters, lafontaine automotive group


 
  "i am delighted to share my enthusiastic testament of the exceptional efforts carried out by the chaldean chamber of commerce. the chamber actively facilitates and strengthens meaningful connections between business, professionals, and stakeholders. as an active member, i enjoy participating in networking events, educational initiatives, and community outreach."
hon. yasmine i. poles, oakland county circuit judge


 
  “the chaldean american chamber of commerce has been an invaluable asset to the business community and our chaldean brothers and sisters at home and abroad. relationships are the foundation of any successful business or industry, and the chamber is unparalleled in fostering lasting and robust relationships. the countless events, networking opportunities, political advocacy, and charitable works are just some of the areas where the cacc has created value for our community and many others. our organization has benefited immensely, directly and indirectly, from the cacc’s strength. the cacc is a resource during economic uncertainty, a voice for members when fighting legislation that places an undue burden on businesses and promotes economic growth through advocacy. from banks to legislators, auto dealers to tradesmen, the chamber’s diverse network of members is a resource that saves us time and strengthens our ability to do business. this chamber is like no other in the country and is blessed with a team of servant leaders fighting to build business, relationships, and community. it has been and will continue to be an integral part of our organization’s growth and the prosperity of our community.”
sylvester sandiha, pinnacle hospitality


 
  “joining the chaldean american chamber of commerce was pivotal in driving our business’ success. the networking opportunities, events, and support from fellow members have helped us build lasting business relationships. being part of this community has expanded our network and enriched our business with invaluable connections and resources.”
tom shayoka, shayoka capital


 
  "it is truly a pleasure working with the incredible cacc team. as one of the most enterprising chambers in the state, they are professional, welcoming, engaged and their connections run deep."

  “at my first event with the chaldean professional network, the energy was vibrant, and it was fantastic to meet new people and reconnect with familiar faces. it was fun to see that everyone is genuinely interested in collaborating, which makes this networking group that much better. i'm excited to be a part of such a supportive and dynamic community. looking forward to the next one!”
bianca dabish toma, logo clothing shop
