welcome ★ star maui limousine









aloha and welcome to star maui limousine service – hawaii.
we’re maui’s best source for limousine transportation services. star maui limousine can arrange limo transportation for everything from “airport arrival and departure” to your very special “maui wedding”.
wedding service is a star maui limousine specialty

we feature “custom white stretch limo’s” with all the amenities. in addition, we have an in-house concierge to arrange all of your important details. from the formally attired, professional chauffeurs to red carpet and just maui’d signs. a complementary beverage service is always available.

“first-rate” airport services

star maui limousine offers the best value on the island for your money. the perfect solution to a long, strenuous flight. no crowded shuttle buses. no long rental car lines. and for only a few dollars more than a taxi!

try our famous hana safari

enjoy an all day limousine tour through maui’s tropical rain forest,  black sand beaches, waterfalls, ending on to the backside of haleakala, maui’s volcano and… finish with a wine tasting at tedeschi vineyards – this is a absolute “must-do”.

sunset at haleakala

our maui limo service features an exclusive “romantic night”, 10,000 feet high at the top of maui’s famous volcano – haleakala.

star maui limousine – hawaii features some of the lowest package rates on the hawaiian islands. all tours and charters are private and customized to your personal needs. our concierge can help with restaurant reservations, accommodations, tours, hawaiian island info and activities. contact us today for bookings or additional information.
