rallye
since  1994, rallye has been providing atlantic canadians with affordable vehicles, parts and service. over the years we've grown, to selling over 6,000 new and pre-owned vehicles per year, making us one of canada's fastest growing dealer groups. we offer a wide selection of new vehicles. we also carry one of new brunswick's largest pre-owned inventories, delivering automobiles, trucks, vans and suvs to seven provinces across canada.


our newest addition, rallye motoplex & marine, with locations in new brunswick and nova scotia is an authorized bmw motorrad, cfmoto, ducati, indian motorcycle, polaris, royal enfield, slingshot, suzuki, triumph, yamaha and zero motorcycles dealership in dieppe, new brunswick. along with our large powersports selection, rallye motoplex & marine has a growing marine catalog, which includes brands such as four winns, g3 boats, mastercraft, mercury, nauticstar, quicksilver inflatables, suncatcher, volvo penta, and yamaha. plus, you’ll find a full sales, service, and parts department, as well as atlantic canada’s largest apparel department!



serving all of atlantic canada, our state-of-the-art glass repair and collision centers offer courteous and well-trained staff to meet all your auto body repair and vehicle restoration needs.

rallye group offers the best selection of new and pre-owned models across our nine locations, including chrysler-dodge-fiat-jeep-ram, chevrolet-buick-gmc, fix auto dieppe, fix auto moncton, hyundai, infiniti, mitsubishi, nissan, novus glass dieppe, novus glass moncton, rallye motoplex & marine and rallye motoplex nova scotia. explore our inventory and get in touch when you’re ready to get your next ride.
