professional real estate services



















serving a diverse and creative helena since 1997.trimac group llc is helena's premier boutique brokerage, representing property buyers and sellers in helena, montana and the surrounding area. our mission is to provide convenient personal service with the benefit of long term market knowledge. our small team of dedicated professional realtors provide detail oriented service and outside the box ideas to bring buyers and sellers together in the helena market and beyond. our commitment to quality is one of the reasons we have been able to create and maintain long-lasting relationships with our clients over the past 25+ years.

areas of practice 



service for buyers and sellers

 

bringing buyers and sellers together is all about trust. that trust builds lasting relationships based on mutual respect and fairness. buying a home is often the biggest transaction a person will make in their lives. we understand the trust we share with our clients, that’s we connect people with the places they love. 



real estate consultation 

 

you can lean on our experience to develop a market strategy, procure a broker’s opinion of value or learn more about the process of investing in real estate. but whether or not you’re ready to buy or sell, we are here answer your questions.



service to the community

 

our company was formed around the principal of service to the people of helena. over the 25 we’ve been in business our principal of service has expanded to honor the community as a whole. today we work closely with the city of helena and various housing initiatives and other non-profit groups to promote safe and secure housing to every resident.
