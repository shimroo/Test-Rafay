hi y'all, 
thanks a lot for visiting this page. 
here you'll find my humble collection of 
compact discs 
and vinyl records; 
showing for the most part, 
but not exclusively, 
electronic music, 
more or less techno, 
not necessarily danceable, 
definitely remarkable. 
also some zappa & mostly jazz these days...
anything goes, 
enjoy your browsing, wolf.
