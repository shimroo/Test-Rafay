srac is a coalition of animal advocates committed to improving the life and treatment of homeless animals in new york city.view our most recent newsletter 






















 

newslettersubscribe heresrac serves as an invaluable clearinghouse of information about new york city's homeless animals and issues affecting them.newsletter archive














through the generosity of those who care about the plight of our city's homeless animals, we work with our partners to encourage low cost spay/neuter services, promote humane education in our public schools, increase licensing and vaccination compliance, and advocating for companion animals for responsible pet owners.
