here’s what’s going on now!




thank you, webster, for all your support in our bottle & can drives! 



your simple act of donating your empty bottles and cans makes a big difference. not only does this help the environment by promoting recycling, but the proceeds from the returned bottles and cans directly support our band’s activities. your contribution will help us continue to create beautiful music and unforgettable performances. thank you for your generosity and continued support! save up your bottles and cans!



























who makes up webster marching band?



while our student musicians come from webster, ny’s two middle (wilink & spry) and two high schools (schroeder & thomas), we’re based out of webster schroeder high school.click the red pin in the map to get directions to schroeder high school.
