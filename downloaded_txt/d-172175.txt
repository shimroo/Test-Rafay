operating in more than 35 countries, vitalaire stands side by side with its patients, healthcare professionals and hospitals.
with services and solutions for home care, it is possible to guarantee better comfort and quality of life for patients in the continuation of the treatment of chronic diseases at home, in addition to taking care of the distribution of medicinal gases with flexible and personalized monitoring. among vitalaire's expertise are service with qualified professionals, clinics for face-to-face service, customer service for requests and doubts, 24-hour delivery (7 days a week), modern medical equipment, preventive and corrective technical assistance and continuing education program.
