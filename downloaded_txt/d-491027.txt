mcgee lumber company in charlotte nc
premium lumber and milled wood products
since 1934, mcgee lumber company has been a cornerstone in building a premier retail and wholesale lumber supply company. mcgee lumber company supplies only top quality milled wood lumber, boards, planks, siding, flooring, molding, timbers, beams, and tongue and groove products. our premium wood includes clear all heart redwood, clear and knotty western red cedar, clear and knotty douglas fir, clear and knotty southern yellow pine, clear and knotty white oak. #1 grade pressure treated lumber, fire retardant lumber and plywood. #2 grade timbers--western red cedar, douglas fir, white oak and southern yellow pine. we millwork, and cut to size wood products up to 20 feet long.
