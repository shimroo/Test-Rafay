engineering extensionengineering extension serves the citizens of kansas by providing technical assistance, training, information and outreach for the carl r. ice college of engineering.



k-state engineering extension is the independent leader in technical assistance, training and outreach for the carl r. ice college of engineering, disseminating knowledge to citizens of kansas and the nation in service of the land-grant mission.
our expertise focuses on the issues of pollution prevention, environmental compliance, radon and energy.

pollution preventionthe pollution prevention institute promotes sustainability through environmental education and services.learn more energythe kansas energy program provides energy-focused education, grant information and technical assistance.learn more radonengineering extension provides radon training and resources through several programs and services.learn more
