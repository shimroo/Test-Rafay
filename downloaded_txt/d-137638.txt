you matter to unlv
the rebel community is defined by its resilience, strength, and generosity. now more than ever, the division of philanthropy and alumni engagement is grateful for you—alumni, donors, and the community—as we come together to support unlv.
our love for the university unites us, whether we're here to volunteer, donate, attend an event, establish a partnership, or leave a lasting impact. the division of philanthropy and alumni engagement is committed to establishing meaningful connections with unlv alumni, donors, students, and friends. thank you for supporting the unlv community.
