 ontario reining horse association  what are you waiting for?                














the ontario reining horse association (orha) promotes, supports, and encourages excellence in all aspects of the sport of reining in the province of ontario.each year, the orha sanctions multiple reining shows, including nrha-sanctioned events. many orha classes qualify for year-end awards, which are presented at our annual banquet.
