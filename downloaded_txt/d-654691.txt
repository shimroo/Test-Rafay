about us



soho wines is an independently owned retail wine and liquor store located in new york city in the heart of soho.  hours of operation are noon to 8 pm, monday through saturday.  we offer a wide selection of imported and domestic wines and spirits at competitive prices and we provide delivery in manhattan only  on orders over $100.00--we do not ship--minimum 1 case or equivalent (orders less than 1 case--messenger rates--$12.00 minimum charge).  all gift deliveries--$12.00 minimum charge! please note:  due to covid, all orders may be subject to a $5.00 fee.



store policies



    i. discounts     a. spirits: while we maintain extremely competitive bottle prices, state regulations allow us to have special promotions. please inquire.    b. wines:    1. there is usually a 10% discount on a solid case (12 fifths or equivalent).    2. special discounts of approximately 17% are given on certain selected items (12 bottles for the price of 10).    3. on certain items such as specially priced or rare wines, no further discount is allowed  ii. credit sales new york state law permits the use of third party credit cards for purchase of alcoholic beverages in retail stores. soho wines accepts master card, visa, discover and american express. note: store credit is still not permitted. iii. catalogue pricesall prices are subject to change without notice; all items subject to prior sale. please use this catalogue as a general guide--special sale prices are not always reflected, and stock is in constant flux. (items and prices are current as of january 27, 2024 ). in case of typographical errors, store prices will prevail.
