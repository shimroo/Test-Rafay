save the date 



alumni week on the close



we are excited to announce plans for a new alumni week on the close from june 9 to 13, 2025. the week will include a retreat with the rev. dr. luigi gioia, theologian in residence at saint thomas fifth avenue, from june 9 to 11, and the 2025 paddock lectures, delivered by the rev. dr. john j. thatamanil, professor of theology and world religions at union theological seminary, on june 11 and 12. on june 11, there will be a choral evensong and reading of the necrology, and on june 12 we will hold the alumni memorial eucharist with the conferral of the distinguished alumni award.planning is still at an early stage, and we are not yet able to share details on the cost and how to reserve accommodation on the close. we will provide information on this as soon as possible. in the meantime, please save the date!
