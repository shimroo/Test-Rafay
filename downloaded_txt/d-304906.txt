welcomethe uc department of family and community medicine has played a leading role in fellow, resident, and medical student education since 1974. today our department comprises of 57 core faculty physicians and 28 residents who work around the clock to provide
    clinical primary care to roughly 100,000 patients each year while at the same time conduct important research with goals set that it will translate into the cures for tomorrow.
