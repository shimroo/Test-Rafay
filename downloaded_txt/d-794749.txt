a pathway through akron’s heritage


























who we are



preserving the past and keeping akron’s heritage alive















the cascade locks park association safeguards akron's rich canal history by preserving important landmarks from lock 16 to lock 10. visitors of all ages can explore interactive exhibits detailing the region’s canal-era heritage, enjoy local goods, and experience the community's past firsthand.




    learn more
  














plan your visit



explore all that cascade locks park association has to offer















walk along the akron's section of the towpath trail that winds through rich landscapes, explore the mustill store museum where canal-era artifacts bring local history to life, and enjoy interactive exhibits detailing the heritage of lock 15. come experience akron’s past and present in one unforgettable trip!
