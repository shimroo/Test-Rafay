welcome to 



atlantic hospitality 



ah is a leading manufacturer and distributor of hotel bed & window coverings, hotel seating, case goods and furnishings.


our commitment to quality, construction methods and materials will insure that every product we make or distribute will enhance your property  and exceed your expectations.

our hotel draperies, sheers, roller shades and window coverings are manufactured to meet the demanding needs of hospitality applications. we employ the same quality and standards when we manufacture our seating products. whether it be our hotel sofas, sofa sleepers, casual or accent chairs, benches or any of our pieces, we take pride in making each & every product for you, they carry our name ah

we distribute an extensive selection of hotel lighting for your guest rooms, public area and restaurants and our commercial mirrors and hotel artwork add that special touch to bring everything together.

with flooring options such as tile, high traffic vinyl flooring and accent rugs your floors are covered.

as a distributor of hotel linens we offer major institutional brands that manufacture every type of linen product that your property will require.

we look forward to helping you bring your design ideas and wishes to life….atlantic hospitality.
