schiffert health center







schiffert health center

































schiffert health center is virginia tech's on-campus health care center.
our clinical staff of 17 doctors, nurse practitioners, and physicians assistants administer care for a wide range of medical concerns. with a travel clinic, gynecology clinic, nutrition clinic, and allergy and immunization clinic as well as a radiology department, laboratory, and pharmacy, schiffert health center can easily address all of your health care needs.








immunization clinics
to help students get updated and stay on-schedule with their routine immunizations, schiffert health center offers mass vaccination clinics in addition to individual appointments.
