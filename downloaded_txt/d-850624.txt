about hettichwe create the perfect combination of intelligent technology, functionality and design. it is with this claim that we develop and produce a variety of fittings for all sorts of different functions; including drawer box systems, slides, hinges as well as sliding and folding door systems. because good furniture needs good solutions – for the home, working world and quality of life. every day, around 8,600 members of staff take up the challenge of developing intelligent technology for furniture. the home of the family-owned business is in kirchlengern, germany.hettich america, l.p. 4295 hamilton mill road suite 400 buford, georgia  30518 corporate tel.: 800-777-1772 e-mail: info(at)hettichamerica.net
