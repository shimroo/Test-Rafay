about us

                      we are indonesia's leading bamboo-cotton textiles company with an empowering, fun and flexible
                      collaborative teamwork attitude. kolabora was founded in 2014 with only a small group of teams.
                      for several years, kolabora has now overseen as indonesia's leading bamboo-cotton textiles
                      company.
                    




vision & mission
