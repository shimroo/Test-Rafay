established in 2005, wildfibers is a full-service local yarn shop serving the beautiful skagit valley and beyond in the pacific northwest of washington state, nestled between the san juan islands and the cascade mountains and 60 miles north of seattle. whether you are a beginner or a seasoned fiber enthusiast in knit, crochet, or felting, you will find bountiful inspiration, friendly and knowledgeable staff, and quality fibers and supplies. stop in and say hello - we would love to see you!
