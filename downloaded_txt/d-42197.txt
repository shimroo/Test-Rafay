 welcome to the "new"

stitchin' witch embroidery site!

although our name says "embroidery", we're actually a whole lot more and this exciting new site will show you everything we have to offer. of course, we still have our online gift store for "gifts for all occasions", but now you can create and design your own apparel, whether it's tees, polos, caps and more, using your own logo or design.  plus, you can create and design keepsake gifts such as mugs, slates, mouse pads for promotional giveaways or special moments in someone's life.  

try it out and see how creative you can be!

and don't forget to keep checking back with us, as we will be adding more products each week!
