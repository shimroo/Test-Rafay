mcdonough welcomes you


while maintaining its southern charm, the city of mcdonough is a thriving, picturesque community that is the center for activities, art, and commerce for henry county and the southern arc of metro atlanta. the geranium city has something for everyone whether they have come to live, work, shop, worship or play.
