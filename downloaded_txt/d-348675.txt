why franchise with gnc?


the gnc difference
global reach
exclusive brands
one team












                            come and join the winning team.  we have been the industry standard of health and wellness solutions since 1935, partnering with our franchise operators to help them achieve their dreams since 1988. for over two decades, entrepreneur magazine has consistently ranked us the #1 vitamin retail franchise.  it is a storied history and we are just getting started.
                          
















                          in recent years, the global wellness industry has grown nearly twice as fast as the global economy.  we are capitalizing on this demand as we strive to help the entire world live well®. with high brand awareness and unlimited potential for expansion, gnc is poised for both domestic and international growth.
                          
















                              there are many things that set gnc apart, but our world-class product innovation is what makes the gnc customer so loyal to our brand. with dozens of top-selling gnc brands and exclusive product lines, there is no one in the industry that does it better.  
                            
learn more about our exclusive brands here


















                            at gnc we understand that in order for our company to have success we need to help our franchise operators thrive.  we provide the training you need to get your business off the ground and ongoing 24-7 support to guarantee you have all the tools you need to be successful.  thinking about opening another location?  our seasoned real estate team with over 100 years of combined experience is here to help.  looking for some guidance on local marketing strategies?  our marketing team is here to support you.
