isshinryu karate association (usika)
isshinryu karate is a system of karate founded by one of the greatest karate masters of okinawa, (soke) grandmaster tatsuo shimabuku.  this systems is derived from several older classical styles, including okinawan goju-ryu and shorin-ryu karate, with what the master considered to be the best of each.  this web site is intended to serve as an online meeting place and resource for old, new and future members of the association.
