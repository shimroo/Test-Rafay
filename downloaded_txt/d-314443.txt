site purpose
          this site is designed to help rebuild disaster stricken areas as
            fast as possible by providing agencies, companies and individuals
            a source for all jobs and projects in these hard hit areas. we are
            not here to profit off of natural disasters. we simply provide the
            two sides a way to get the information they need quickly and accurately.

site disclaimer
         
we are in no way an employment agency, government agency or any
            type of company associated with the rebuilding efforts under way
            during any disaster. we are simply a website to put the information
            out there for people to use in rebuilding these areas. we will not
            call you or email you regarding any jobs and nobody will ever claim
            to be a representative of cleanupjobs.com. never give out any personal
            information over the internet or phone to anyone claiming to be with
            cleanupjobs.com. all employment listing will remain on the site for
            30 days or until you request removal. all company and volunteer listings
            will remain on the site for 120 days or when asked to remove. all
            volunteer listing will last for 30 days or until asked to be removed.
            any questions should be directed to paul miller at dmi. - pmiller@dudleymiller.com
