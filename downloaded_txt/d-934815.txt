生物資訊




2025-01-13

小裸胸鯙 lesser moray eel
我是小裸胸鯙，顧名思義就是我很迷你啦！我的身體光滑無鱗，顏色呈現淡棕色是為了能和環境融為一體熱帶與亞熱帶海域的珊瑚礁洞穴是我的棲息地我喜歡吃魚，當然偶爾也會吃一些甲殼類換換口味 lesser moray eel​ (gymnothorax minor)i’m lesser moray. as the name implies, i’m tiny. my body is smooth and scaleless. in order to blend into the environment, my color is light brown. i inhabit in coral reef caves in tropical and subtropical seas. i like to eat fishes. sometimes i will eat crustaceans to change the favor.





2025-01-18

斑馬裸胸鯙 zebra moray eel
有人說我像斑馬，也有人說像脆迪酥，但我是隻裸胸鯙啦！黑白條紋相間是我最大的特色，喜歡待在沙泥地質的水域平常很懶都躲在岩礁間隙或洞穴裡，甲殼類是我的最愛的食物不要看我一動也不動，吃飯的時候倒是游很快哦～ zebra moray eel (gymnomuraena zebra)somebody say i look like a horse, and others say i looks like crispy flute, but i’m a moray eel!my biggest feature is the white and black stripes. i like to stay in the sand and muddy waters. i’m lazy and usually hide in the rock crevices or caves. my favorite food are crustaceans.  although i look motionless, i swim quite fast during hunting.





2025-01-18

橫帶海龍 obscure pipefish
我的身體纖長又華麗，像個水下舞者，讓我到哪裡都像在走秀全身覆蓋著的條紋和鱗片，讓我在海底可以輕易偽裝成海藻雖然我游動的速度緩慢，但視力卻很好，喜歡吃小蝦、甲殼類和小型浮游動物而在我們的家族裡，跟海馬一樣是爸爸照顧寶寶們直到孵化哦！ obscure pipefish (phoxocampus diacanthus)my body is slender and gorgeous , like an underwater dancer. wherever i go, it looks like i am on a catwalk. my whole body is covered with stripes and scales that make me blend in with algae at the bottom of the sea. despite swimming slowly, i have a great eyesight. i like to eat small crustaceans, and tiny zooplankton. our family is just like the seahorse that the father will take care of the babies until they are  born.
