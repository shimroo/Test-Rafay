networking opportunities


the chamber hosts several different events to strengthen relationships among local businesses, providing networking opportunities and connections with community leaders. these events also promote business education, keeping our members informed and equipped to tackle the challenges and opportunities in cookeville and putnam county.





business before hours
pre-work meetings where we discuss business and community developments



business after hours
after work meetings where we discuss business and community developments



new member lunch
information/networking for new chamber members and new employees



small business expo
spring/fall events where businesses can showcase their products and services



community coffees
informal and informational networking in algood, baxter, and monterey
