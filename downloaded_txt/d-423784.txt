welcome and mission statement

 

the fundamental principle of the milwaukee rowing club is to provide to the community, the broadest possible access to rowing as a recreational and competitive sport. our mission is to instill the ideals and values of rowing, at an affordable cost. we uphold an environment where athletes are expected to work together to achieve common goals. the club does not discriminate on the basis of race, national origin, religion, color, gender, age, sexual orientation, or residence.
