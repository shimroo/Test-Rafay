benefits of chamber membership

        benefits of chamber membership • 
      
























we serve as a business advocate in city, state, and regional economic development groups.




we provide referrals along with a business directory listing on our website. 




membership increases your visibility as an active community member and demonstrates credibility and professionalism for your brand.




we work to build partnerships and pool resources in ways that increase economic vitality.




members receive promotions on our social media channels, which have a large following. 




we promote various events and advertise our community in local and state tourism guides to drive visitation.




we update our local activities through a monthly newsletter which goes out to all members and subscribers on our website.




we create weekly and monthly itineraries making it easy for you to share and enjoy activities in the ports of discovery.
