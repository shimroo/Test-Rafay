dan adams alongside the original team has put together this incredible hardback book 'read and destroy - skateboarding through a british lens 78-95'. it features 272 jam packed pages filled with iconic images and commentary from the team from the rad archive's.

        welcome to native skate store the uk's leading independent skateboard shop  	  

the newcastle upon tyne based native skate store opened it's doors in february 2003 to cater to the thriving northern skate scene. it's the only specialist 'skater owned' skate store in the newcastle and surrounding areas, and does its best to promote skateboarding by sponsoring local skaters, organising skate jams and hosting skateboarding video premiere's. the experienced staff have a good understanding of the skateboarding as a sport, as well as as the skateboarding market which means the store only carries the best brands and are able to advise you with any questions you may have.... read more ›
