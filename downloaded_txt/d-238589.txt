        moonlight design: the lighting specialists  	  

moonlight design is more than just an online store; we are one of the uk's premier garden and interior lighting consultancies. transforming your home, garden or business with magical, designer lighting displays. we offer comprehensive countrywide design and installation services for both private and corporate clients, and award-winning garden designers and landscape architects. if you need our bespoke design and installation service, please call 0208 925 8639 or have a look at our specific design site, theartoflight.co.
discover where it all began and learn about our lighting journey through the decades. 
founded
founded in 2003 in london by david haslehurst, moonlight design limited has established itself as a premier provider of lighting solutions to both trade and retail customers. over the years, the company has earned a reputation for excellence, offering a wide range of high-quality lighting products that cater to diverse needs and preferences.
early years: 2003 - 2010
moonlight design limited began its journey with a clear vision: illuminating spaces with innovative and stylish lighting solutions. the company started as a small business and focused on building strong relationships with trade clients, ensuring they received the best possible service and products. hunza was the first brand added to moonlight design’s extensive catalogue of brands providing exterior and garden lighting, closely followed by the addition of  konstsmide. the early years were marked by steady growth as the company continually expanded its product range and began attracting a loyal customer base in the retail sector.
expansion and innovation: 2010 - 2015
moonlight design limited embraced new technologies and trends as the demand for sophisticated and energy-efficient lighting grew. this period saw the introduction of led lighting options, which became a significant part of the company's offerings. the commitment to quality and customer satisfaction helped moonlight design stand out in a competitive market, leading to further expansion and the opening of additional sales channels.
it was at this stage collingwood was added to the growing catalogue of leading brands.. the collection featured largely exterior lighting and was popular with trade. however, collingwood was also popular for its interior downlights.  elstead, norlys and other associated brands were soon to follow.
a leading name in lighting: 2015 - present 
moonlight design limited has solidified its position as a leading name in the lighting industry by continuously adapting to the evolving market and prioritising customer needs. the company offers an extensive selection of indoor and outdoor lighting solutions, catering to modern and traditional aesthetics. their products are known for their durability, efficiency, and innovative design.
during the last decade, moonlight design has expanded its offering to include brands like endon, utopia and searchlight, all well-known brands, to provide more interior lights, including chandeliers.
today: amazing designer lighting at fantastic prices
as one of the country's leading online lighting specialists, moonlight design offers the very best designer and lighting brands for trade and retail customers. from renowned outdoor lighting specialists like hunza, collingwood and konstsmide to respected interior brands like endon, searchlight and quintiesse, every area of your home or business is covered with the finest designer lighting styles, with something to suit all budgets.
today, moonlight design limited is recognised for its exceptional customer service, expert advice, and comprehensive product range. serving trade professionals and retail customers, the company remains dedicated to its founding principles of quality, innovation, and reliability.
whether you're looking for aesthetic, ambient lighting or something highly functional for a specific installation, the team at moonlight design is here to help with every stage of your journey, from lighting advice to installation. simply browse our website for the latest designer collections online, including schwung, houseof. or  nuura lighting or call our lighting specialists sales team on 020 8925 8639.
