  基督徒會堂的異象，是向矽谷地區未信主的人傳福音，帶領他們認識耶穌加入教會的大家庭，建立眾聖徒達至基督成長的身量；裝備眾聖徒，承擔教會的事奉；差派並支援他們到普世作主的見證，使一切榮耀歸與神。
chinese church in christ reaches the un-churched in silicon valley to bring them to jesus and fellowship in his family. our goal is to build up the saints to christ-like maturity and equip them for ministry in the church and to send them and support them as witnesses to the world for the glory of god.
