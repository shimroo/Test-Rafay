immersive student opportunities







center for dispute resolution
work with the center for dispute resolution to learn educational tools and resources
                                       for solving conflict in organizations and communities.









cmjf spring showcase
walk the red carpet! we showcase the best student work from the year to the public
                                       at the moxie, springfield's independent theater.









holt v spicer debate forum
join our high-impact, award-winning debate team and compete with major universities
                                       across the nation.









internships
acquire credit for experiential learning while networking with potential employers.









ozarks news brief
discover the inner workings of a television news team through reporting, producing,
                                       or crewing our live streaming news program.









the standard
jump start your career by writing for our award-winning student newspaper.









student organizations
find your people! join a student organization aligned with your interests or create
                                       your own.









student productions
get on set! immerse yourself in the filmmaking process from development to production.









vicki stanton public speaking showcase
show off your public speaking prowess as a competitor in the vspss, or gain event
                                       planning experience and class credit helping behind the scenes.
