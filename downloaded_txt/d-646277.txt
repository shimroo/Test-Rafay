membership
 existing members login using member login (also at the upper right)

new members can join by clicking new member signup
 
existing memberships renew january 1st (new memberships paid october onwards carryover to the next year).

club express will automatically send you an email when your renewal is due. follow the link in that email or click on the renew label at the top of the home page.

if you have paid your annual renewal for this year and are not receiving emails from club express, please first check your email spam folder. if club express emails are appearing there, change your email settings. if you’re not receiving club express emails at all, please contact membership director at membership@aquatutus.org.”
