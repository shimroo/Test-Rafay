providing services to children & youth since 1958














serving montreal’s west island for over 60 years

 

our members are all volunteers who work to provide physical and financial help primarily to childrens' projects and also to seniors.  we raise funds annually through a variety of events and initiatives, including our trademark holiday nuts & cakes sale and our ever popular golf tournament each june.

holiday cheer throughout the year



oct 29, 2024




every year we hold our holiday nuts and cakes campaign from mid-october through to the end of december. this is one of our major fundraisers and contributes greatly to the funds we give out to local non-profit community organizations. through these organizations countless children and youth receive support and services which enhance their lives throughout the year.read more

president’s message
greetings,do you enjoy having fun while making a positive impact in your community? do you enjoy helping children and others in need?if you answered yes, we would love to hear from you!as the president of the kiwanis club of lakeshore montreal, i extend a warm welcome to our website and invite you to learn more about our club. imagine yourself as part of the incredibly good work that is happening in your community and how you can amplify that impact.i hope you find something in common with our club and that you choose to follow your heart by reaching out to us. we would be delighted to get to know you and invite you to learn more about our activities or social events.your involvement will definitely increase our impact and make huge differences in the lives of countless children!please contact me at info@lakeshorekiwanis.ca; (514) 620-9270 or directly at andy.sarrasin@lakeshorekiwanis.ca and (514)-947-2074. i am excited to hear from you.yours in service, andyandy (andrew) sarrasinpresident, kiwanis club of lakeshore montrealmember, kiwanis foundation of lakeshore montreal
