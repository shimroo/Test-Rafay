highland village police investigating shooting incident involving juvenile suspect 


hvpd is actively investigating a shooting incident involving a 17-year-old victim that occurred on jan. 31, at approximately 7:05 p.m. in the parking lot near 3198 justin rd. with the assistance of corinth pd, the juvenile suspect is in custody.						read on...







council approves agreement for fm 407 trail alignment assessment and grant funding strategy project 


council approves agreement for fm 407 trail alignment assessment and grant funding project for trail and amenity plan to provide connections to businesses, schools and parks. market cities selected for compensation and benefit plan comparison.			additional info...







winter weather preparation 


the coldest temperatures of the season are forecasted this week. here are a few tips to help you prevent frozen water pipes and our contact information in the event of an emergency.						read on...







txfallenpd tribute event donation presented; improvements to doubletree ranch park splash pad 


city presented largest txfallenpd tribute event donation to texas police chiefs association foundation. council approved renewal of police substation contract with the shops at highland village and improvements to splash pad at doubletree ranch park.			additional info...







council approves first read of the annual budget; approves amendment allowing patio homes 


a public hearing was held and first read of the fy 24-25 budget was approved. assessed valuations provide a 4.9% increase in property tax revenue. the tax rate decreases from $0.501394 to $0.500273. council approved  zoning change to allow patio homes.			additional info...







council receives update on animal care services; approves contract for sewer line assessment 


police chief provided an update on the highland village animal care facility and services, contract was approved for a sanitary sewer line assessment and residents were informed of upcoming water disinfectant conversion.			additional info...
