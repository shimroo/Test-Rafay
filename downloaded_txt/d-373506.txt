clews & strawbridge of chester county, pennsylvania, welcomes you to our boat and watersports store, where your boating experience and satisfaction is our #1 priority!

no matter what type of watercraft you're looking for, we can satisfy your boating needs. clews & strawbridge is a full-service marine dealer offering boat sales, service, transportation, parts, and storage.

our award-winning boat sales and service facility serves all of eastern pennsylvania as well as delaware, maryland, and new jersey. we carry boats from top-quality manufacturers and offer a wide range of used boats. we also carry boat motors and outboards. come find out why after 40+ years we are still the most well-respected name in the business!
