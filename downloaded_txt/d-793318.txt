why spoor & fisher?


                                we’ve been in africa for 100 years, where brand owners rely on us as their regional hub; their safe pair of hands. with an established network and local relationships in all 59 countries and surrounding islands, we know how to handle the intricacies and anomalies involved in registering and enforcing ip in every african country. 

we provide the same services across the caribbean, a region with a developing ip landscape.
we’re known for combining our technical expertise with legal knowledge and high levels of responsiveness together with a practical approach.
