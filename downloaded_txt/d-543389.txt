town updates
 






  
 












two events to highlight local pow camp history on feb. 12 

all are welcome to attend two events on feb. 12 to pay tribute to the history of wwii prisoner of war camp continental by way of a presentation and the unveiling of sahuarita's first historical marker...				read on...







council approves centralized solid waste collection 

the sahuarita town council has approved a plan to centralize residential solid waste collection services, that will begin on sept. 1, 2025 with a fixed monthly rate of $22.75.				read on...











spd receives traffic enforcement grant 

the police department has been awarded grant funds from the arizona governor’s office of highway safety to help with traffic safety and dui traffic enforcement in the community.				read on...







la villita community center grand opening feb. 27 

the entire community is invited to the grand opening of the newly renovated la villita community center (71 w sahuarita rd) on feb. 27 from 10-11 a.m. with an open house to follow.				read on...
