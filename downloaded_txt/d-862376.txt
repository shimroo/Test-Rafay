	welcome to the





	telugu cultural association (tca) of austin is a non-profit organization 501(c) founded over 33 years ago with an objective to promote, preserve and encourage the rich telugu culture, language, art, music, dance, drama and unique traditions of the telugu speaking people of the greater austin and surrounding areas.

know more







	president message



srinivasa byrapaneni

	president, tca

	with immense gratitude and excitement, i take on the role of president of the telugu cultural association of austin. for years, this association has been a foundation for celebrating our heritage, fostering unity, and creating lasting connections.
