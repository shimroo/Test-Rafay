an introduction
welcome to the snapstream developer community.  this is an interactive community designed around the needs of a community contributor.  whether you are creating a new skin for distribution across the world, a new plugin that will change the way people use one of our products, or just looking to get the most out of your personal media experience, this is the place to get your questions answered.  if you cannot find the answer to your question, make a request for further documentation or create the answer as you learn.
