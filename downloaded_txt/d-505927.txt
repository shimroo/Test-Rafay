preserving history on america's main street:the route 66 association of missourithe route 66 association of missouri was established to preserve, promote, and develop route 66 in the show-me state. it was founded by a group of dedicated volunteers who wanted to ensure that route 66's history, culture, and legacy were preserved. today, members from all over the country and beyond have joined the association.come explore route 66 with us! we are excited to bring you on this journey through missouri’s history and culture as we take you back in time along the mother road. it is an experience like no other!
