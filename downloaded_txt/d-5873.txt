the international association for professional pilates teachers














what is a pma membership?membership to the pilates method alliance provides access to technical resources, training, networking opportunities, and expert guidance. with pma membership, you gain a supportive community and valuable tools that empower you to continuously enhance your skills and stay connected with the latest advancements in the pilates industry.




















student member



for those currently training to be a pilates teacher and are not yet earning an income teaching.




    student membership
  




















teacher member



for pilates teachers, instructors, or anyone who wishes to support the goals of the pma organization.




    teacher membership
  




















corporate member



for businesses looking to build a strong platform to network and connect with pilates studios, teachers, and students.




    corporate membership
