tuesday, may 08, 2012





nascar canadian tire series revs into high gear



bowmanville, ontario (may 8, 2012) – on sunday, may 20 the nascar canadian tire series (ncats) kicks-off the race season at the new canadian tire motorsport park as part of the victoria day speedfest weekend presented by castrol edge. in addition to world class auto racing, the weekend program has something for everyone including camping, fireworks, live bands and so much more. "we're thrilled to be marking yet another milestone in our long history of supporting auto racing in canada this year," said allan macdonald, senior vice-president, automotive.  "canadian tire is excited to host the opening race of the nascar canadian tire series at the newly renamed canadian tire motorsport park. we are pleased to bring canadians the best in canadian auto racing as well as a great entertainment experience for the whole family this victoria day weekend." in addition to the nascar canadian tire series opener, the vortex brake pads 200, and other races taking place during the victoria day speedfest weekend presented by castrol edge, canadian tire motorsport park will have something for the whole family this year: * camping: create a memorable long weekend experience with friends and family in the family camping area or on the general campgrounds at canadian tire motorsport park. * canadian tire fan zone: fans will have the chance to gain access to the canadian tire fan zone where guests can watch the race and receive free snacks and exclusive canadian tire swag. a wristband policy is in effect but guests can obtain one by presenting canadian tire money, a canadian tire options mastercard or a canadian tire receipt at the canadian tire hauler in the paddock area.  * fireworks display: when the sun goes down on saturday, may 19 the sky will be lit by a long weekend fireworks display.  * live music: live bands will be performing saturday evening leading up to the fireworks and on saturday and sunday during the lunch hour.  * carnival games: from 10:00 a.m. to 5:00 p.m. on saturday and sunday, carnival games for the family will be taking place in the paddock area.  * street performers: throughout the weekend street performers will entertain guests in the paddock area.  * win a chance to meet ron fellows: guests can win a chance to meet ron fellows and a 'hot lap' with the racing legend in his camaro zl1  "whether you're a racing fan or just looking for a great place to celebrate the unofficial start to summer, canadian tire motorsport park has something for everyone this may long weekend" said myles brandt, president and general manager of canadian tire motorsport park.  "the victoria day speedfest weekend kicks off a season full of events featuring some of the biggest names in racing and the entertainment industry." as part of further establishing itself as canada's automotive authority, canadian tire continues to foster the growth of motorsports in canada. entering its sixth season, the ncats will host 12 races from antigonish, ns, to vernon, bc. in addition, earlier this year, canadian tire announced that 2011 ncats champion scott steckly will once again be sporting the iconic triangle on the race track. for more information on the nascar canadian tire series please visit www.nascarlocalracing.com.   for more information on the 2012 canadian tire motorsport park calendar of events please visit www.canadiantiremotorsportpark.com.  from ryan chalmers, canadian tire motorsport park

thursday, march 08, 2012





grisdale racing products’ new website a hit among racers; grisdaleracingproducts.com enjoys successful launch



march 8, 2012, dundas, ontario - grisdale racing products recently launched its new website, and the reviews remain positive. grisdaleracingproducts.com replaced the old domain earlier this off-season, and if the winter is any indication the crew is in for a busy spring.“the new site has nearly everything the grassroots racer could need,” said thatcher krupp who has been with the company more than 20 years.”the catalogues are the most functional that i’ve seen, and the site gives us a more interactive relationship with our customers than ever.“the biggest improvement is the amount of new information we are able to share. the site gets updated frequently, and between tech articles and updated news our visitors see something new every time.”seven different catalogues feature updated pricing, and racers can view in detail the different options available to them before calling with their orders. technical articles are updated on a weekly basis covering topics from basic body mounting tips for late model teams to more advanced technical tips on various components.the dundas, ontario, retailer stopped short of online shopping, but the reason is simple.“even though online shopping is extremely popular we want to maintain a personal relationship with our customers,” said owner bill grisdale. “we’ve built our reputation on being knowledgeable and helpful, and we want to make sure we bring in exactly what the customer is looking for.“dave trendell and thatcher do a great job ensuring the customers are treated right. we want to make sure the racer can get any information they need on the website, and then they want to show them the best service they’ll find anywhere.”grisdale racing products is a distributor for american racer tires along with various other brands in the motorsports industry. if you want your car featured on the website, visit our facebook page by searching grisdale racing in facebook or email adam at adamross@grisdale.com .to experience the great personal service give dave or thatcher a call at 800-561-1791 or stop in at 400 brock road in dunas, ontario, just a few kilometres from flamboro speedway.- xxx –contact: adam ross800-561-1791adamross@grisdale.com

wednesday, february 15, 2012





kennington looking for another championship in castrol edge dodge



february 15, 2012. st. thomas, ontario racer dj kennington will once again be at the wheel of the #17 castrol edge dodge competing in the nascar canadian tire series this summer. kennington’s association with castrol is approaching two decades and one of the longest continuous partnerships in canadian racing.following his championship winning season in 2010, kennington scored race victories at mosport international raceway & kawartha speedway last season, but fell just short of another title and was forced to settle for second overall. “we gave it a good run last year but came up just a little short repeating” said kennington. “our guys are all anxious to get back and get back on top because we like finishing first better” he added.having the continuous support of primary sponsor castrol which features the castrol edge brand on the #17 dodge is something that gives kennington and his team much more than the resources to compete at an elite level. “i’ve said this for many years, i simply wouldn’t be where i am and would not have been able to win races and a championship without the backing from castrol” said kennington. “they have welcomed me into their family from the beginning and our relationship continues to grow, nobody’s had better support than me” he stated. the continuation of the castrol partnership with dj kennington also has the company excited. marketing communications manager for wakefield canada, the distributors of castrol edge, marsha mclaughlin said “partnering with dj kennington has been an important part of our marketing program with castrol for nearly two decades. we’re proud of all that dj has accomplished in racing and look forward to another exciting season together helping him edge his way to another championship”. the season opening event for the 2012 nascar canadian tire series will take place saturday may 20th at the canadian tire motorsport park, formerly mosport international raceway where kennington is the defending race winner. again this season fans will be able to watch all of the series races on tsn. check local listings for times. race fans are encouraged to keep up to date with the latest team news in several ways. race fans should bookmark the team website www.djkracing.ca and follow them on twitter, @djkracing and fans are also suggested to join the facebook group djk racing. photo: the #17 castrol edge dodge challenger at auto clearing speedway.photo credit: perry nelson (nascar)
