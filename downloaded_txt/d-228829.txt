we’re glad you’re here!



welcome to the read photography website! we are a location and studio photographer located in cedar rapids, iowa. we specialize in senior photography, portrait photography, sport photography, event photography, and digital art. our skilled team at read has the knowledge and experience to deliver quality photos that become lasting memories.as a three generation family business, our passion for what we do is our foundation and fuel. we also realize that we wouldn’t be where we are today without the support of our community in cedar rapids and surrounding areas, as well as a talented team working alongside our photographers. from consultation to results, the read team works together to turn moments into captured memories that will last for generations.
