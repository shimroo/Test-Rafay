




practical pointers



   
     
    practical pointers, inc.  is incorporated in the state of north carolina as a public service, non-profit organization. the publisher claims no copyright and makes no charge. duplication is freely permitted and encouraged.
     
   every effort is made to ensure accuracy of the abstracts. however, they must not be used as a guide for treatment or diagnosis. the original article must be consulted.  the publication is intended for use by professional health-care workers, especially for those in primary care.
     
    the editor welcomes feedback: content? formatting? errors? corrections? clarifications?  the aim is to make the publication as helpful, interesting, and as accurate as possible
  you may access all the original articles from the www:



www.nejm.org


www.annals.org


www.archinternmed.com




www.jama.com


www.thelancet.com


www.bmj.com
