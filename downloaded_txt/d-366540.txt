antiques, interiors, & more




since 1987, the scott antique market has provided  atlanta and the southwestern united states with a premier antique and interiors shopping destination. though originally at the lakewood fairgrounds, by the mid 1990s it came to encompass the entire 361,000 square feet of the atlanta expo centers and now houses everything from antiques and vintages to specialty interior design elements, custom furniture,  clothing,  art,  and  much  more.shows run the second weekend of every month at the atlanta expo centers and  from  november to march  at  the  ohio  expo  center!
