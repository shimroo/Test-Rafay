the scan16 project

in september 2016 we brought to california the original negatives and slides of the chris walter archive photos that had been stored in the uk for the last 20+ years.
there are thousands of negatives and slides, and all new scans fro this file can be found by searching scan16 or going to scan16.at this time the photos are only available from chris walter and photofeatures.

the archive of veteran music photographer chris walter with a career starting in 1963 with the beatles and continuing for the next 50 years. the music photo library for media use has all our currently available material, limited edition prints of some of the major artists are also available at chriswalterphotography.com, including led zeppelin, jimi hendrix, beatles and more.
