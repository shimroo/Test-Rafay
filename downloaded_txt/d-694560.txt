locknetics

















discover the simplicity of locknetics.
backed by the strength and promise of allegion, locknetics provides electromechanical hardware for access control solutions.
designed to fit the challenges of everyday, on-the-job applications, the locknetics portfolio, with electric strikes, electro-magnetic locks and access control accessories, offers more options with fewer skus, all at a practical price point.
