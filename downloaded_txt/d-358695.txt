inspired by the great pubs of ireland, johnny foley’s is one of san francisco’s favorite watering holes. known for its warm hospitality, stiff drinks, honest food and live entertainment since 1998.

conveniently located in the union square area - close to shopping, theater and moscone convention center - this downtown destination is named after johnny foley, an irish born tenor and musical rebel who blended the best of irish song with a creative spirit that could flourish “only in san francisco”.
