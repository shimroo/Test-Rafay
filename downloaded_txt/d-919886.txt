maryland ready: staying prepared for emergencies


why emergency preparedness is crucial in maryland
as a resident of maryland, it's essential to be prepared for any emergency that may arise. with its location near the atlantic coast and the appalachian mountains, the state is prone to natural disasters such as hurricanes, floods, and earthquakes. additionally, the threat of terrorism and cyber attacks cannot be ignored.
maryland ready, a comprehensive emergency management program, aims to educate and empower residents with the knowledge and resources needed to stay safe during emergencies. by being prepared, you can minimize damage, reduce stress, and ensure your family's well-being.




creating a personal emergency kit for your family
the first step in being prepared is to create a personal emergency kit for your family. this kit should include essential items such as non-perishable food, water, and medications. it's also crucial to have a plan in place for communication and evacuation.
maryland ready provides a comprehensive checklist of items you can include in your kit, including flashlights, batteries, and first aid supplies. by having these essentials readily available, you'll be better equipped to handle any unexpected situation.




staying informed and connected during emergencies
in the event of an emergency, it's crucial to stay informed about the situation. maryland ready provides real-time updates and information on its website and social media channels.
additionally, make sure you have a plan in place for communication with your family members. this can include designating a meeting spot, having a backup power source, and staying connected through alternative means.
