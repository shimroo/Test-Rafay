city clerkthe city clerk’s office assists the taxpayers and residents of the city by providing a variety of diverse services.








assessorthe assessment office has many responsibilities, including determining and maintaining assessments, inventory records, ownership interest, and processing exemptions.








building & codesthe building and codes department issues building permits, conducts code inspections, and coordinates meetings of the planning board and zoning board of appeals.








public worksthe public works department is responsible for the maintenance of all city-owned buildings, grounds, parks, and more.








water & sewerthe water and sewer departments operate and maintain the water filtration plant, the reservoirs, distribution system, waste water treatment plant, and the sewer system.
