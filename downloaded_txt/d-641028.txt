serious camaraderie and funthe figawi brand is world renowned by sailors and non-sailors alikeour influence goes beyond our events and community. for almost 50 years, we have cultivated a loyal audience that spans the globe through our legacy of professionally executed events, passion for our community, and successful relationships with partner brands. we would love for you to join the fun by racing in the event and being part of our epic post-race festivities! 




    learn how to participate
  

 










 

sailing on the seas of changefigawi’s primary mission is to support the sport of sailing, especially our youth sailing initiativefigawi creates amazing events that deliver serious fun and promote camaraderie. through these events, we make a difference by giving proceeds to a variety of causes including veterans, first responders, and youth organizations.  we are dedicating our focus on supporting the next generation of sailors! join our loyal “figawi tribe” and get involved today.
