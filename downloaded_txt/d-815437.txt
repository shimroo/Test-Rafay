our mission & visionthe mission of highlands christian academy is to prepare the next generation for success with a distinctively christ-centered, high-quality education.














welcome to highlands christian academy!•••highlands has been providing a christ-centered education for more than 50 years! our goal has always been to come alongside parents, helping them train their children to know jesus christ, achieve academically to their greatest potential, and develop personal passions for the glory of god.hca offers a quality vpk - 12th grade christian education complemented by outstanding athletics and fine arts programs. we invite you to schedule a tour and come discover how highlands is preparing the next generation for success!
