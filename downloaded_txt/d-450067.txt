view our online guides






general
stay informed on all aspects of veteran life and support with our 'general' category. this section provides a broad overview of news, updates, and comprehensive guides that cater to veterans' diverse needs—from healthcare initiatives to community events and legal advice. whether you're a veteran, a family member, or a supporter, our resources aim to empower and educate on the essentials for navigating post-service life effectively.




finance
dive deep into our 'finance' category tailored specifically for veterans, where we cover essential topics such as va loans, managing personal finances, and investment strategies. this section provides vital information on how to maximize benefits, secure loans for homes and vehicles, and manage financial planning with a focus on stability and growth post-military life.




disability
explore our 'disability' section dedicated to veterans facing disabilities post-service. this category provides comprehensive guides on accessing disability benefits, understanding the claims process, and leveraging support networks designed for disabled veterans. gain insights into maximizing your benefits, navigating legal channels, and connecting with community resources tailored to enhance the quality of life for disabled veterans.






tricare
explore our 'tricare' section dedicated to providing comprehensive information on healthcare services available to veterans and their families. this category covers eligibility, plan options, and detailed guides on how to maximize your healthcare benefits under tricare, ensuring veterans receive the medical support they deserve.




benefits
our 'benefits' category serves as a crucial resource for veterans seeking to understand and access the various benefits offered through the va. from disability compensation to pension plans and housing assistance, this section aims to simplify the complexities of veteran benefits and ensure you are fully aware of all entitlements and how to claim them.




gi bills
delve into our 'gi bills' category for detailed insights into educational benefits available to veterans and their dependents. this section highlights how to effectively use the gi bill for higher education, skills training, and certifications, empowering veterans to achieve their academic and professional goals post-service.



 









  


frequently asked questions


how do i file a disability claim?
to file a disability claim, gather your supporting documents and submit an application through the va. the va form 21-526ez is commonly used for disability compensation claims.learn more and start your claim here


how can i get a replacement dd-214?
request a replacement dd-214 by submitting a standard form 180 (sf-180) to the national personnel records center (nprc) or online through the evetrecs system.request your dd-214 here


what are my education benefits as a veteran?
take advantage of education benefits like the post-9/11 gi bill, montgomery gi bill, and the veteran readiness and employment (vr&e) program.explore your education benefits here


how do i access va health care?
apply for enrollment online, by phone, by mail, or in person at a va health facility to access va health care.apply for va health care here


how do i check the status of my va claim or appeal?
check the status of your va claim or appeal online through the va's ebenefits portal or by calling the va's hotline.check your claim status here


what housing assistance is available for veterans?
access housing assistance programs such as va home loans, the specially adapted housing (sah) grant, and the supportive services for veteran families (ssvf) program.learn about va housing assistance here


how do i apply for a va pension?
veterans meeting age or disability requirements and with low income can apply for a va pension using va form 21p-527ez.apply for a va pension here


what are my employment and training benefits?
access employment and training benefits through programs like the veterans employment and training service (vets) and the va's vocational rehabilitation and employment (vr&e) program.explore employment and training benefits here


how can i find mental health support as a veteran?
access various mental health services, including counseling, therapy, and crisis support through va health facilities or the veterans crisis line.find mental health support here


how do i get help with substance abuse as a veteran?
the va provides substance abuse treatment programs, including detoxification, rehabilitation, and outpatient care through local va health facilities.get help with substance abuse here
