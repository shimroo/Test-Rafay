        				since 1944, ball equipment has been eastern michigan's leading dealer for lawn and powersports equipment specializing in parts, accessories, sales, and service. we have quickly become the "superstore," offering our customers endless options from atv's, motorcycles & snowmobiles to lawn mowers, snow blowers & chainsaws. ball equipment offers a wide range of popular product lines such as polaris, honda, brp can-am & ski-doo, arctic cat, cfmoto, ferris, kubota, exmark, gravely, husqvarna, cub cadet & many others! we are the convenient 1-stop shop to purchase, accessorize, repair, and maintain every piece of equipment in dad's garage.

now with 3 dealership locations richmond, birch run & sandusky michigan, ball equipment provides sales, service and support to the larger surrounding counties in eastern, southern and mid michigan areas such as sanilac, huron, st. clair, macomb, wayne, oakland, livingston, lapeer, genesee & tuscola! do you own something that we don't carry? don't worry, we service just about any outdoor power or powersports equipment and try our best providing universal aftermarket parts that will fit your machine. stop in today or call for availability of in-stock inventory, it's worth the drive!
