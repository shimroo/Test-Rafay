our mission



our mission is to help teens and families find their path to recovery through connection, education, and support. 














our programs










recovery high school

austin’s only recovery high school focuses on creating a safe learning environment that supports the needs of students above and beyond what traditional school does.




learn more











teen peer support

connection and community are vital to supporting early recovery. our teen support group offers daily meet up groups, education, peer coaching and fun social activities.    




learn more











family support

the kids aren’t the only ones who need support. our family group offers one-on-one and group support, family education and a connection to resources to help you. 




learn more
