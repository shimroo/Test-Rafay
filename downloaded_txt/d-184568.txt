welcome to yuba city!
the city of yuba city is located near the feather river at the base of the sutter buttes—known as the smallest mountain range in the world. just 40 miles north of sacramento, yuba city is the agricultural, economic, and social hub of the yuba-sutter region. providing a unique blend of urban amenities and small-town charm, yuba city’s historic downtown is the perfect backdrop for shopping, entertainment and community events






wade kirchner was the mayor in 2022-2023. the current mayor is dave shaw (2024-2025).
