your local bluefield, west virginia harley-davidson® dealership
when you visit us you will see that our mission is to provide you with superior products and service. to build quality relationships with you and others is what we strive for, whether it be out on the road or in the store.

cole harley-davidson® is a family-owned dealership that fosters an environment which makes it easy for you to do business, so when you have to choose a dealership, you choose us. our friendly and responsive staff will take the time to greet you and get to know you, because when you purchase a product backed by the harley-davidson® name, you deserve our full attention.
