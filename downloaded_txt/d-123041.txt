botox faq

 



botox is used to improve the look of mild to severe frown lines for a short time.botox comes from a kind of bacteria. the bacteria can make you very sick. but doctors have found that the chemical in botox can also help treat some health problems. they have been using it safely for many years.
some wrinkles are caused when a muscle contracts (tightens up). botox is injected through the skin into the muscle with a needle. the botox keeps the muscle from contracting. when the muscle can't contract, the wrinkle doesn't show as much.
