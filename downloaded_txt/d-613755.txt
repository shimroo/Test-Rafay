elstree film & tv studiosavailable for all your production needs














welcome to lifecast studio uklifecast ltd is based at elstree film & tv studios – borehamwood













creatures our talented sculpting crew are able to design and make all creatures, great and small. from the mythical to the macabre. your imagination is our creation.













movies & television lifecast has worked on a wide variety of television programmes, adverts, theatre productions and films spanning over many years. we have produced fabricated puppets and costumes, designed and made props, prosthetic character make-ups, gore and medical effects, animatronic creature effects and lifelike babies.













fabrication at lifecast we can recreate anything as a soft prop. from costume to creature, gigantic to miniature, furry to hairless … we can bring inventions to life!
























prosthetics old age, deformity, creature, wounds, and scars are just a few of the possibilities with prosthetics … unrecognisable transformations!













3d scanning our 3d scanning and printing sector of lifecast is known as life3d.













animatronics artificial movement bringing sculpture to life…
























propsat lifecast we can replicate almost everything. stunt weapons can be made for safe and lightweight use, objects can be made larger or smaller!













hire/saleat lifecast, we are able to supply a wide range of bodies, stunt dummies, limbs, organs, blood and guts for film effects. some of these will be stock items that can be bought or hired, or we are of course able to custom make any of the above for a specific order.
