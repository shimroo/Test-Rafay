banff film festival nights!join us for a spellbinding journey into the heart of adventure with the banff film festival, coming to redlands on february 21st and 22nd. prepare to be transported:a world of adventure: dive into a handpicked selection of short films celebrating the indomitable human spirit and the majesty of nature. from pushing limits in rugged utah to scaling icy waterfalls in the arctic, each story invites you to embrace the wild.two nights, endless inspiration: friday night brings adrenaline-packed adventures with trailblazing mountain bikers, daring climbers, and arctic explorers. on saturday, immerse yourself in transformative tales of perseverance, from jamaica’s emerging climbing community to the raw power of victoria falls. each night offers a unique lineup of films that celebrate the extraordinary.

powered by hikers



our community (and if you're reading this, that's you!) is the heart and soul of the san gorgonio wilderness association. support our work—and help out the trails you love—by getting involved. whether you'd like to become a member, help repair trails, advocate for wild places or simply connect with fellow hikers, we have an opportunity for you.

plan your hikes 



hitting the trail is more fun—and safer—if you have all the info you need first. check out our information on trails and current conditions, as well as tips to get you started if you're new to hiking.

a nonprofit community protecting trails



we are dedicated to protecting the san bernardino national forest (southern california), including the san gorgonio wilderness, and to serving the public visiting the forest.
