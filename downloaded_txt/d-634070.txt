        welcome      

welcome to the city of neptune beach — a small, quiet coastal community nestled on the northeast coast of florida between atlantic beach and jacksonville beach. neptune beach has a comfortable, casual and laid-back atmosphere that causes people of all ages to flock here to enjoy the beach.
