hello, we’re globalrx



enhancing lives for two decades through facilitating medicine access.












































































who we are



globalrx was founded in 1996 in response to a need for patients located in countries who did not have access to the new usa approved drugs for patients with hiv.  initially the service was for individual patients with hiv infections however shortly thereafter the service was expanded to include other drugs available in the usa but not in other countries. today, globalrx continues to provide medications to patients, to hospitals, and to specialty importers located around the world.

we can help



our objective is straightforward: to elevate livelihoods by expanding global access to essential medicines.unveiling innovative remedies for entities within the worldwide healthcare framework, dismantling obstacles and enacting initiatives that empower them to:-grant patients in demand the entry to vital medications -showcase the worth of cutting-edge therapeutics -facilitate elevated well-being standards
