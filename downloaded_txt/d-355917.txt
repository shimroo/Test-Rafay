65th annual golden west track & field invitational









june 5 – 8, 2025
cordova high school, rancho cordova, ca
visit the meet info page for detailed meet schedules (tba)

registration is open!
youth    •    high school   •    open / masters

entry deadline is may 26, 11:59pm



2025 usatf pacific open and masters grand prix championships will be contested at the 2025 golden west invitational.


day by day event overview
thursday, june 5
high school hammer will be held on  at the golden state throwers facility at 12050 borden rd. herald, ca 95638. weights and measures will be at 3:30pm and event begins at 4:30pm.


friday, june 6



saturday june 7 (high school, open, masters)
high school athletes are those who are enrolled in high school for the 2024 – 2025 academic school year.

field events start time tba: lj, tj, hj,  shot, discus, open/masters hammer (see june 5th for hs hammer), pole vault, javelin.
afternoon/evening running events start time tba – (for a click here for a tentative detailed list): 100m; 200m; 400m; 800m; 1500m; hs only mile; 2000m steeplechase; 80/100/110 short hurdles; hs 300m ih; open/masters 400m ih.

saturday, june 7 (youth)
youth athletes compete in 2-year age divisions ages 7-14. 

pole vault (11-12, 13-14) will be contested during the day in conjunction with the high school athletes
800m, 1500m (7-8, 9-10, 11-12, 13-14) will be contested on saturday  in the evening in conjunction with the high school athletes

sunday, june 8 (youth)
youth athletes compete in 2-year age divisions ages 7-14. events start at 9 am.

100m, 200m, 400m, lj, shot put, 7-8, 9-10, 11-12, 13-14: 
high jump for 9-10, 11-12, 13-14
discus for 11-12, 13-14
mini-javelin for 7-8, 9-10
aero-javelin (450g) for 11-12
javelin (600g) for 13-14

sunday, june 8 (open, masters)

5k race walk at 8am
