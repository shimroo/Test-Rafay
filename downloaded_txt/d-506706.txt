training
aware offers customized in-house training covering all aspects of competitive and marketing intelligence from how to set up a new ci function, gathering intelligence with primary and secondary research, ci analysis, war-gaming and more….



research
aware's ci/mi research & analysis services are designed to help clients at all stages of the competitive & marketing intelligence process, from the initial stages of identifying what is needed to integrating the results into business strategy.



resources
aware specialises in competitive & marketing intelligence and has the experience and knowledge to answer most ci/mi questions. resources include a guide to ci, case studies, articles and videos, book recommendations and an faq section answering typical questions about ci. 



our story
aware was founded in 1995 by arthur weiss. since 1995, the company has grown and now has an international reputation within the competitive & marketing intelligence community, and are recognised as global experts.
