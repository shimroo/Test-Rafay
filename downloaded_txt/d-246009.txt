fun-n-sun is a family owned and operated boat dealership with locations in hurst and cleburne. we have been serving the north texas boating community since 1989 opening our first store in cleburne.in the dfw area, our hurst location is north east of fort worth, and our cleburne store serves the area southwest of the dfw metroplex. come visit both locations; we look forward to helping you with your marine needs! fun-n-sun has wide variety of boats from pontoon, bay boats, aluminum and our specialty, the high performance bass boats. over the years, we have added many of america's top boat lines to our showroom floors. please take a look inside our manufacturers menu to see the boat lines we proudly offer! we also have a full service department and tackle department to help with all your fishing needs. we have assembled a service team second to none. with a long list of training and certifications, our service staff will get you back on the water as quickly as possible.
